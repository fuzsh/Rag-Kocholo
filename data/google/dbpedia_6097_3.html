What is the movie My Life in Ruins about?
Georgia (Nia Vardalos), a travel guide, feels that her life as no direction. Her kefi, or mojo, is gone. But, as she leads a ragtag group of tourists through her native Greece, she begins to see the world in a new light. Georgia opens the group's eyes to the beauty of an exotic land, and, in the process, rediscovers her kefi.

Is My Life in Ruins a sequel to my Big Fat Greek wedding?
My Life in Ruins is based on a screenplay by principal actress Nia Vardalos and television comedy writer Mike Reiss. Is this a sequel to "My Big Fat Greek Wedding"? No, although Nia Vardalos stars in both My Life in Ruins and My Big Fat Greek Wedding (2002) (2002), the two films are not related .
