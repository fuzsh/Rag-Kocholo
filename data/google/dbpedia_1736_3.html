What is the history of Kalka city?
History. The town takes its name from Kalika Maa the ruling deity . Kalka was acquired by British India from the Princely state of Patiala in 1843 as a stopover and depot for the Simla, the summer capital of the Raj. In 1846, it was transferred to the Shimla district and in 1899 to the Ambala district.
