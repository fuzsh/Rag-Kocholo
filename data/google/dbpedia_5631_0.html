Who bought Priority Records?
EMI bought a 50% stake in Priority in 1996 and the remainder in 1998 . Despite EMI's full ownership, Priority remained an independently managed company until 2001, when its operations were merged with EMI's main US subsidiary, Capitol Records. Priority was absorbed into Capitol Records in 2004 and ceased operations.
