Lotte (Charlotte) Beese was born in Silesia, Germany (now Poland) , in 1903. At the age of 20, after training in stenography, she moved to Dresden for an office job at the Deutsche Werkstätten. While there, she was granted a spot in the institute's weaving workshop, where she learned the fundamentals of the craft.
