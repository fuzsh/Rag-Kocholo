Lesotho was previously the British Crown Colony of Basutoland, but it declared independence from the United Kingdom on 4 October 1966 . It is now a fully sovereign state. The name Lesotho roughly translates to the land of the people who speak Sesotho. Basutoland first received the protection of Britain in 1868.
