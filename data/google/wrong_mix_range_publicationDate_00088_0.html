What did Zamyatin write?
He is most famous for his highly influential and widely imitated 1921 dystopian science fiction novel We , which is set in a futuristic police state. In 1921, We became the first work banned by the Soviet censorship board. Ultimately, Zamyatin arranged for We to be smuggled to the West for publication.
