From 747 to 929 AD, Tudela was the capital city of the Banu Qasi territory , which was a small Muslim state that was independent of the Christian Kingdoms to its North as well as the Muslim empire to its south. Even after the fall of the Banu Qasi, Tudela remained a Muslim city until the Christian invasion of 1119.
