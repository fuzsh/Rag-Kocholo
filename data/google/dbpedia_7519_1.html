How long is a game of Olympic handball?
A handball match lasts for a total of 60 minutes and is divided into two halves of 30 minutes each. If a game is tied, overtime is played. The overtime period consists of two halves, each lasting five minutes.

How long has handball been a sport?
The modern set of rules was published in 1917 by Karl Schelenz, Max Heiser, and Erich Konigh, on 29 October in Berlin, which is seen as the date of birth of the sport. The rules have had several revisions since. The first official handball match was played in 1917 in Germany.
