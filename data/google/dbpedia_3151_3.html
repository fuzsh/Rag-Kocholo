What is the clock of the long now project?
A monument scale mechanical clock, built inside a mountain, designed to keep accurate time for the next ten millennia . The Clock is hundreds of feet tall, engineered to require minimal maintenance, and powered by mechanical energy harvested from sunlight as well as the people that visit it.

What powers the 10,000 year clock?
The chime generator is a mechanical computer that rings ten bells in a different order each day for 10,000 years. The generator is made up of a cascading series of Geneva wheels which create intermittent motion (a bell ring) out of continuous rotation (from the main power system).
