Gila Almagor

Israeli actress
Gila Almagor Agmon is an Israeli actress, film star, and author. In Israel, she is known as "queen of the Israeli cinema and theatre". Wikipedia
Born : 1939 (age 85 years), Petah Tikva, Israel
Spouse : Yaakov Agmon (m. 1963–2020)
Awards : National Jewish Book Award for Children's Literature and Silver Bear for Outstanding Artistic Achievement
Height : 5′ 2″
Parents : Henya Alexandrowitz and Max Alexandrowitz
