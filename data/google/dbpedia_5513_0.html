Diocese of Shirak (Armenian: Շիրակի թեմ Shiraki t'em), is one of the dioceses of the Armenian Apostolic Church covering the northern, central and south-western parts of the Shirak Province of Armenia . It almost covers 4/5th of the province, as the south-eastern town of Artik and its surrounding villages are excluded.
