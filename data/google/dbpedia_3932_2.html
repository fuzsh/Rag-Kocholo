Cornelia Otis Skinner (born May 30, 1901, Chicago, Illinois, U.S.—died July 9, 1979, New York, New York) was an American actress and author who, with satirical wit, wrote light verse, monologues, anecdotes, sketches, and monodramas in which she displayed her versatile and distinctive acting skills.
