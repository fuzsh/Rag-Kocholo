Charles Emmanuel III

Duke of Savoy
Charles Emmanuel III was Duke of Savoy, King of Sardinia and ruler of the Savoyard states from his father's adbication on 3 September 1730 until his death in 1773. He was the paternal grandfather of the last three mainline Kings of Sardinia. Wikipedia
Born : April 27, 1701, Turin, Italy
Died : February 20, 1773 (age 71 years), Turin, Italy
Children : Princess Maria Luisa of Savoy , Prince Benedetto, Duke of Chablais , Princess Eleonora of Savoy , and more
Grandparents : Charles Emmanuel II, Duke of Savoy , Marie Jeanne Baptiste of Savoy-Nemours , Philippe I, Duke of Orléans , and more
Parents : Victor Amadeus II of Savoy and Anne Marie d'Orléans
Spouse : Elisabeth Therese of Lorraine (m. 1737–1741), Polyxena of Hesse-Rotenburg (m. 1724–1735), and Anne Christine of Sulzbach, Princess of Piedmont (m. 1722–1723)
