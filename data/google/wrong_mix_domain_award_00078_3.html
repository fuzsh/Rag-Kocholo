Charles Glover Barkla

British physicist
Charles Glover Barkla FRS FRSE was a British physicist, and the winner of the Nobel Prize in Physics in 1917 for his work in X-ray spectroscopy and related areas in the study of X-rays. Wikipedia
Born : June 7, 1877, Widnes, United Kingdom
Died : October 23, 1944 (age 67 years), Edinburgh, United Kingdom
Education : University of Cambridge , University of Liverpool , King's College, Cambridge , and more
Nationality : British
Known for : X-ray scattering; X-ray spectroscopy; Characteristic X-ray
Awards : Nobel Prize in Physics , Hughes Medal , and Royal Society Bakerian Medal
Partner : Mary Esther Cowell
