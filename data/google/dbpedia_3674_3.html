Where was Brewster McCloud filmed?
The film was originally called Brewster McCloud's (Sexy) Flying Machine. The film was shot on location in Houston, Texas for eight weeks from May 22 to July 15, 1970. The original story was set in New York City but it was decided to set the film in Houston.
