What is the significance of Fifth Avenue in New York?
Fifth Avenue serves as the dividing line for house numbering and west–east streets in Manhattan ; for example, it separates East 59th Street from West 59th Street. Higher-numbered avenues such as Sixth Avenue are to the west of Fifth Avenue, while lower-numbered avenues such as Third Avenue are to the east.
