What is the social system theory developed by Niklas Luhmann?
Social systems are defined by Luhmann not as action but as recursive communication . Modern society is defined as a world system consisting of the sum total of all communication happening at once, and individual function systems (such as the economy, politics, science, love, art, the media, etc.)
