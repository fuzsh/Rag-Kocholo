What did King Vidor do?
King Vidor made 54 narrative films between 1919 and 1959 . Only six of those have been lost to posterity. If we count his documentaries and the short two-reelers he made beginning in 1913, it adds up to some 70 films. So his career in narrative filmmaking covered 46 years.
