Saovabha became the consort of her half-brother King Chulalongkorn sometime in 1878 . She gave him 9 children, 5 would survive to adulthood (one died in infancy), two would eventually become King of Siam. In 1897, Queen Saovabha became the first female Regent of Siam, when her husband went on a tour of Europe.
