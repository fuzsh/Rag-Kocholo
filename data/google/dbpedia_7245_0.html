What is the origin of the music box?
The music box, the first successful device that could play a musical tune, was probably invented in Switzerland around 1770 . Early music boxes were designed to play music using flat metal prongs that were plucked by a revolving metal cylinder. Eventually music box makers developed a flat disc to play the music.
