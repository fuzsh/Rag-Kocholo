What is Fraunces Tavern known for?

Who is Washington saying farewell to a Fraunces Tavern in New York City?
On December 4, 1783, nine days after the last British soldiers left American soil, General George Washington visited Fraunces Tavern. He invited his officers from the Continental Army to the Long Room to thank them for their service, and to bid them farewell.
