Bernard Herrmann

American composer and conductor
Bernard Herrmann was an American composer and conductor best known for his work in composing for films. As a conductor, he championed the music of lesser-known composers. He is widely regarded as one of the greatest film composers. Wikipedia
Born : June 29, 1911, New York, NY
Died : December 24, 1975 (age 64 years), North Hollywood, Los Angeles, CA
Librettist : Lucille Fletcher
Descendants : Dorothy Herrmann and Wendy Herrmann
Awards : Academy Award for Best Music (Original Score) and BAFTA Award for Best Original Score
Nominations : Academy Award for Best Music (Original Score) , BAFTA Award for Best Original Score , Golden Globe Award for Best Original Score , and more
Genre : Film score , Opera , New Age , and more
