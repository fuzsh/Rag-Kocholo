Emilio Fernández

Mexican film director and actor
Emilio "El Indio" Fernández Romo was a Mexican film director, actor and screenwriter. He was one of the most prolific film directors of the Golden Age of Mexican cinema in the 1940s and 1950s. Wikipedia
Born : March 26, 1904, Sabinas, Mexico
Died : August 6, 1986 (age 82 years), Mexico City, Mexico
Children : Jacaranda Fernández and Adela Fernández y Fernández
Parents : Emilio Fernández Garza and María Sara Romo Bustamante
Siblings : Fernando Fernández and Jaime Fernández
Spouse(s) : Gladys Fernández ; Columba Domínguez
