What is the Coptic story of St George?
George was the true model of the young Christian man, and ordered a church to be built over his grave . In Egypt, the Copts call him, "The Prince of Martyrs", and built over 200 churches named after him. Moreover, he is a personal friend of the majority of the Copts, who believe in the power of his prayers for them.
