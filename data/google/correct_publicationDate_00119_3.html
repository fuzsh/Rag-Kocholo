Fiction writer

Alfred Elton van Vogt was an American science fiction writer. His fragmented, bizarre narrative style influenced later science fiction writers, notably Philip K. Dick. Wikipedia
Born : April 26, 1912, Gretna, Canada
Died : January 26, 2000 (age 87years), Hollywood, Los Angeles, CA
Spouse : Lydia van Vogt (m. 19792000) and Edna Mayne Hull (m. 19391975)
Movies : Origins of the 21st Century
Awards : Damon Knight Memorial Grand Master Award , Prometheus Hall of Fame Award , Retro Hugo Award for Best Novel , and more
Genre : Science fiction
