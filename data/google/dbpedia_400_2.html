What are some interesting facts about Tyumen?
Founded in 1586, it is the oldest Russian city in Siberia , located on the site of a Tatar town, Chingi-Tura, founded in the 14th century. A river port, it functions as a transshipment point. Its main industries are metalworking, engineering, shipbuilding, timber products, and chemicals.
