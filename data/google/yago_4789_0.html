Ishirō Honda

Japanese filmmaker
Ishirō Honda was a Japanese filmmaker who directed 46 feature films in a career spanning five decades. Wikipedia
Born : May 7, 1911, Asahi, Yamagata
Died : February 28, 1993 (age 81 years), Setagaya City, Tokyo, Japan
Education : Nihon University
Spouse : Kimi Honda (m. 1937–1993)
Battles/wars : World War II ( Second Sino-Japanese War )
Nominations : Academy Award for Best International Feature Film and Japan Academy Prize for Picture of the Year
