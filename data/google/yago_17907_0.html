The Vyborg Side (Russian: Выборгская сторона, romanized: Vyborgskaya storona) is a 1939 Soviet drama film directed by Grigori Kozintsev and Leonid Trauberg , the final part of trilogy about the life of a young factory worker, Maxim. The film was also released in the United States under the title New Horizons.
