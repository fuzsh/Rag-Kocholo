Can you eat Callicarpa?
The genus Callicarpa is from the Greek calli, meaning “beauty,” and carpos, meaning “fruit.” The fruits are edible to humans , but have an astringent quality and not much flavor, making them somewhat unpalatable raw. Beautyberry jelly, however, is quite tasty.
