Erich Mende was a German politician of the Free Democratic Party and Christian Democratic Union. He was the leader of FDP from 1960 to 1968 and the vice chancellor of Germany from 1963 to 1966. Wikipedia
Born : October 28, 1916, Strzelce Opolskie, Poland
Died : June 5, 1998 (age 81 years), Bonn, Germany
Awards : Knight's Cross of the Iron Cross
Party : Free Democratic Party
Previous offices : Vice-Chancellor of Germany (1963–1966), Federal Minister of Intra-German Relations (1963–1966), and Member of the German Bundestag (1949–1980)
Education : University of Cologne and University of Bonn
