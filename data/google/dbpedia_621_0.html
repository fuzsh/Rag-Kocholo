How many officers are in a cavalry regiment?
Each line cavalry Regiment had usually three Squadrons, normally A, B, and C Squadrons, each commanded by a Major or occasionally a Captain. Each Squadron had 6 officers and 152 men. Squadrons were subdivided into four Troops, usually numbered 1 to 4, commanded by a Lieutenant.
