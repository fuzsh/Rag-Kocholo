What does 576p stand for?
576p is the shorthand name for a video display resolution . The p stands for progressive scan, i.e. non-interlaced, the 576 for a vertical resolution of 576 pixels (the frame rate can be given explicitly after the letter).

What does the number in screen resolution mean?
The number of horizontal and vertical pixels on a display screen . The more pixels, the more information is visible without scrolling. Screen resolutions have a pixel count such as 1600x1200, which means 1,600 horizontal pixels and 1,200 vertical pixels (see high-definition resolutions).
