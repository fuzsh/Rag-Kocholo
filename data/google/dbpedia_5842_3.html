What is the history of the VAFB?
Vandenberg was originally established in 1941 as Camp Cooke near Lompoc, Calif . The camp was an Army training center for armored and infantry troops. Transferred to the Air Force in 1957, Vandenberg began its transformation into a space and missile test facility.

What is Vandenberg Air Force Base famous for?
Established in 1941, Vandenberg Space Force Base is a space launch base, launching spacecraft from the Western Range, and also performs missile testing . The United States Space Force's Space Launch Delta 30 serves as the host delta for the base, equivalent to an Air Force air base wing.
