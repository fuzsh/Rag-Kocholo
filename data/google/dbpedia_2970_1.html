What is the significance of the title The Birthday Party?
The title A Birthday Party is given because Meg, Lulu, McCann, and Goldberg throw Stanley a birthday party even though he claims it is not his birthday . This plays into the theme of the ambiguity because it is not clear when Stanley's birthday is or why two supposed strangers would want to throw him a party.
