The Rock Star and the Mullahs

2003 ‧ Documentary ‧ 1h 40m
The Rock Star and the Mullahs is a 2003 documentary film directed by Ruhi Hamid and Angus MacQueen and by producer Pamela Friedman. The film follows the journey of the South Asian rock music band Junoon and addresses music in Islam. Wikipedia
