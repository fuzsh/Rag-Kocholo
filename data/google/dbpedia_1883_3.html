When did survival of the fittest start?
Spencer introduced the term survival of the fittest in 1864 when he published the book The Principles of Biology. He developed this theory after reading the biological writings of Darwin. In 1869, Darwin himself used the same term when publishing his fifth edition of the book, On the Origin of Species.
