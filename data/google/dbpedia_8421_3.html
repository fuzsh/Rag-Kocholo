Why does Cyprus have two flags?
When Cyprus became an independent state in 1960 a modern flag was designed. The colours are neutral and peaceful to attempt to create neutrality between the rival Turkish and Greek communities . People have been known to fly either the Greek or the Turkish flag alongside the flag of Cyprus.
