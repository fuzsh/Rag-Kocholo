What is the meaning of Sextans star?
Sextans, meaning “sextant,” an instrument used to measure star positions , was created by Johannes Hevelius, a 17th-century Polish astronomer, who is credited with delineating seven constellations recognized today: Canes Venatici, Lacerta, Leo Minor, Lynx, Scutum, Sextans, and Vulpecula.
