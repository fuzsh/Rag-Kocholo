Who is Cardinal Martinez Sistach?
Cardinal Lluís Martínez Sistach, Archbishop emeritus of Barcelona (Spain) , was born on 29 April 1937 in Barcelona, Spain. He was ordained a priest on 17 September 1961 and obtained a doctorate in utroque iure in 1967 from the Pontifical Lateran University.
