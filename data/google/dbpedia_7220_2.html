Who is the specialist in the study of heart?
A cardiologist is a medical doctor who specializes in assessing, diagnosing, and treating problems of the heart and blood vessels. Cardiology is a high-stakes specialty. According to the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention , heart disease is the leading cause of death in the United States.

Who is the specialist in diseases of the heart?
A cardiologist is a physician who's an expert in the care of your heart and blood vessels. They can treat or help you prevent a number of cardiovascular problems. They can also specialize in specific areas, like abnormal heart rhythms, heart failure or heart problems you've had since birth.
