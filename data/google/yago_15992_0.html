Who is the lead actor in the fijian film The Land Has Eyes which was Fiji's official submission for the Academy Award for Best Foreign Language Film in 2004?
The film was shot on Rotuma, homeland of director Vilsoni Hereniko. Lead actress Sapeta Taito is Rotuman, and The Land Has Eyes is her first film appearance. She had, in fact, never seen a film before acting in this one. Rena Owen is best known for playing the lead female role in Once Were Warriors.
