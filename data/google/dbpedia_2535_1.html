Why is Swift Current called Swift Current?
Swift Current Creek The name of the City is derived from the creek that flows through it, and the river into which it flows . The creek originates in the Cypress Hills, changing elevations and winding its way across 150 kilometres of wind-swept prairie, until it empties into the South Saskatchewan River.
