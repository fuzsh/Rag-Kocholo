Formed in Toronto in 1993 , the pop-revival sextet the Carpet Frogs comprised lead vocalist Nick Sinopoli, vocalist/guitarist Greg Godovitz, lead guitarist Steve Jenson, guitarist David Love, bassist Leo Valvassori and drummer Lawrence Greech. Their debut LP Frog Curry was issued in 1995.
