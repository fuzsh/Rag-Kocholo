Vladimír Špidla

Former Prime Minister of the Czech Republic
Vladimír Špidla is a Czech politician who served as the prime minister of the Czech Republic from July 2002 to August 2004 and as European Commissioner for Employment, Social Affairs and Equal Opportunities from November 2004 to February 2010. Wikipedia
Born : 1951 (age 73 years), Prague, Czechia
Previous offices : Prime Minister of the Czech Republic (2002–2004), Minister of Labour and Social Affairs of Czech Republic (1998–2002), First Deputy Prime Minister of the Czech Republic (1998–2002), and more
Spouse : Viktorie Špidlová (m. 1974)
Previous campaigns : Czech Republic senate elections, 2022 - Senate of the Czech Republic - Prague 11 and 2021 Czech legislative election - Chamber of Deputies of the Czech Republic - Prague
Party : Party of European Socialists
Education : Grammar school Budějovická (1970) and Charles University
Height : 5′ 10″
