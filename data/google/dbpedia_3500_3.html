What property does a hard-boiled egg have that a raw one does not?
A hard-boiled egg will spin smoothly ; a raw egg won't. Because the yolk and white aren't set in a raw egg, motion can cause the center of gravity to shift easily, causing the egg to wobble rather than spin. With a hard-boiled egg, everything is set so the egg spins smoothly.
