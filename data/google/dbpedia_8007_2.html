Lung cancer
Summer died on May 17, 2012, aged 63, at her home in Naples, Florida, from lung cancer . Being a nonsmoker, Summer thought that the cancer had been caused by inhaling toxic fumes and dust from the September 11 attacks in New York City; she was in her apartment near Ground Zero when the attacks occurred.
