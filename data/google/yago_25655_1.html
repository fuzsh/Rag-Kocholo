Princess Suvadhana (Thai: สุวัทนา; RTGS: Suwatthana, 15 April 1906 – 10 October 1985) Her Royal Highness Princess Suvadhana She was the royal consort of King Vajiravudh (or Rama VI) of Siam. Her original name was Khrueakaeo Abhayavongsa (Thai: เครือแก้ว อภัยวงศ์; RTGS: Khruea-kaeo Aphaiwong).
