What is Charles Tupper known for?
Sir Charles Tupper, 1st Baronet GCMG, CB, PC, M.D. (July 2, 1821 – October 30, 1915) was a Canadian Father of Confederation who served as the sixth prime minister of Canada from May 1 to July 8, 1896 . As the premier of Nova Scotia from 1864 to 1867, he led Nova Scotia into Confederation.
