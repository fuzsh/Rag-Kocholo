Therese of Saxe-Hildburghausen

Therese Charlotte Luise of Saxony-Hildburghausen was queen of Bavaria as the wife of King Ludwig I. Oktoberfest was created in honour of their wedding and is still celebrated annually on Theresienwiese in Munich. Wikipedia

Children : Princess Mathilde Caroline of Bavaria , Princess Hildegard of Bavaria , Princess Adelgunde of Bavaria, Duchess of Modena , and more
Spouse : Ludwig I of Bavaria (m. 1810–1854)
Grandchildren : Ludwig II of Bavaria , Princess Therese of Bavaria , Ludwig III of Bavaria , and more
