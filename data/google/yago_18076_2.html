Tonia Marketaki

Greek film director and screenwriter
Tonia Marketaki was a Greek film director and screenwriter. She was born in Pireas and spent many of her childhood years in the Zografou district of Athens. Her maternal origins are from Kardamyla, in the island of Chios. Wikipedia
Born : July 28, 1942, Piraeus, Greece
Died : July 26, 1994 (age 51 years)
Education : Institut des hautes études cinématographiques
