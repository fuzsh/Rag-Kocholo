What was the Manchester Museum before?
History. The museum's first collections were assembled by the Manchester Society of Natural History formed in 1821 with the purchase of the collection of John Leigh Philips . The society established a museum in Peter Street, Manchester, on a site later occupied by the Young Men's Christian Association, in 1835.
