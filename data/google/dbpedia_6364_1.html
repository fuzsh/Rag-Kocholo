Robert F. McGowan

American film director
Robert Francis McGowan was an American film director and producer, best known as the senior director of the Our Gang short subjects film series from 1922 until 1933. Wikipedia
Born : July 11, 1882, Denver, CO
Died : January 27, 1955 (age 72 years), Santa Monica, CA
Descendants : Roberta McGowan and Helen McGowan
