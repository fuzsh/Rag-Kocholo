Was Shaw University founded in 1865?
Shaw University is a private historically black university in Raleigh, North Carolina. Founded on December 1, 1865 , Shaw University is the oldest HBCU to begin offering courses in the Southern United States. The school had its origin in the formation of a theological class of freedmen in the Guion Hotel.

What are the historical facts about Shaw University?
In 1873, Shaw opened Estey Hall, the nation's first college building with dormitories and classrooms open to African-American women . Estey Hall still stands as one of the historic buildings on today's campus. Shaw was the South's first university to open a four-year medical school training African-American doctors.
