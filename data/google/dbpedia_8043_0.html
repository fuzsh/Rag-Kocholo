Gary Larson (born August 14, 1950 , Tacoma, Washington, U.S.) is an American cartoonist best known for his single-panel comic series The Far Side, which is renowned for its scientific content and bizarre humour. Larson produced The Far Side for 15 years, from January 1980 until January 1995.
