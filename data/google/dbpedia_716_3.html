What did Sarajevo used to be called?
The first mention of the name Sarajevo was in a 1507 letter written by Firuz Bey. The official name during the 400 years of Ottoman rule was Saraybosna ("Palace of Bosnia") , which remains the city's name in Modern Turkish.
