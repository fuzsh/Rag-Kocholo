What happened to Big Brovaz?
Big Brovaz released their debut album Nu-Flow in 2002, which spawned five top 20 singles including the title track, Favourite Things and Baby Boy. The group were dropped by their label in 2004 after member Flawless was sacked, forcing them to cancel their second album Re-Entry .
