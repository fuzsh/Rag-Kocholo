How many original members are still in Status Quo?
Francis Rossi is the lead guitarist, singer, and songwriter of Status Quo. He is 72 years old and was born in London, England. Alongside Rossi, Rick Parfitt is one of the only original members still in the band . Parfitt is 68 years old and plays guitar and provides backing vocals for the group.
