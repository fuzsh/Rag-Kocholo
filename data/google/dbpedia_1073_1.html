Arthur Wilkinson was born on 3 September 1919 in Ealing, London, England, UK. He was a composer, known for The Limping Man (1953), A Song for Tomorrow (1948) and Melodies for You (1967) . He was married to Dorothy Ann Henning. He died in 1968 in Hammersmith, London, England, UK.
