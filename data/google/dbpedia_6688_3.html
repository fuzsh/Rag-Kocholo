Where was Here Comes the Navy filmed?
According to contemporary sources, this movie was filmed on location at the dirigible field in Sunnyvale, CA, the Naval Training Station in San Diego, CA, the Navy yard at Bremerton, WA, in San Pedro, CA and on the U.S.S. Arizona .
