What is Ivan Shishkin known for?
He became famous for his forest landscapes and was also an outstanding draftsman and a printmaker. Ivan Shishkin owned a dacha in Vyra, south of St Petersburg. There he painted some of his finest landscapes. His works are notable for poetic depiction of seasons in the woods, wild nature, animals and birds.
