The bridge across the Kola Bay is a four-lane road bridge across the Kola Bay in the city of Murmansk. The total length of the bridge is approximately 2.5 kilometers , and its above-water part is 1.6 kilometers. Since opening in 2005, the bridge across the Kola Bay is the longest road bridge beyond the Polar circle.
