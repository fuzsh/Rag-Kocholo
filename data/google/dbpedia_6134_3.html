Why does John Blackthorne speak Portuguese?
The main characters in Shōgun are based on history. By the time the story takes place, the Portuguese had been trading in Japan for over a century and had converted many Japanese to Catholicism. Portuguese was Japan's second language and was how Blackthorne was able to communicate as he struggled to learn Japanese .
