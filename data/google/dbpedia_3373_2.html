William Henry Lewis

Former United States Assistant Attorney General
William Henry Lewis was an African-American pioneer in athletics, law and politics. Born in Virginia to freedmen, he graduated from Amherst College in Massachusetts, where he had been one of the first African-American college football players. Wikipedia
Born : November 28, 1868, Berkley, Norfolk, VA
Died : January 1, 1949 (age 80 years), Boston, MA
Previous offices : United States Assistant Attorney General (1911–1913), Massachusetts State Representative (1901–1903), and Member of the Cambridge Common Council of Massachusetts (1899–1902)
Education : Amherst College , Harvard Law School , and Virginia State University
