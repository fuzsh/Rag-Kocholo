Vilsoni Hereniko

Playwright and film director
Vilsoni Hereniko is a Rotuman playwright, film director and academic. He was the writer and director of Rotuma's first ever feature film, The Land Has Eyes. Wikipedia

Movies : The Land Has Eyes , Mirage , The Chief’s Speech , and more
Spouse : Jeannette Paulson Hereniko (m. 1997)
Nationality : Fijian
