What is the contribution of Robert Horvitz?
Through his studies of the roundworm Caenorhabditis elegans, in 1986, Robert Horvitz identified two of the genes needed for programmed cell death to occur . He later showed that another gene protects against cell death, and also identified genes that regulate how dead cells are removed.
