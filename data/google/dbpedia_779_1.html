Where does the last name Menke come from?
North German and Dutch : from a pet form of an ancient Germanic compound name with the first element man(n) 'man' or magin 'strength, might', + the diminutive suffix -ke. Germanized form of Sorbian Měńk 'burbot' (see Menk ).
