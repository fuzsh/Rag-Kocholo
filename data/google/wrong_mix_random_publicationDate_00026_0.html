Why did Isaac Asimov write about robots?
Isaac Asimov wanted to change the image of the robot --and he did. Asimov wrote nearly 40 robot short stories, in addition to many novels with robots as main characters. He felt robots were like any other technology: they would have built-in safeguards and the only danger would be their masters.
