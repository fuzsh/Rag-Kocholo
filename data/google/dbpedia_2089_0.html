What is GIS longitude?
URL copied. [coordinate systems] The location of a point on the earth's surface, stated as an angular measurement in degrees, minutes, and seconds east or west of the Greenwich prime meridian . All lines of longitude are great circles that intersect the equator and pass through the north and south poles.
