Luis Bayón Herrera

Spanish film director and screenwriter
Luis Bayón Herrera was a Spanish film director and screenwriter who worked in Argentine film of the 1940s and 1950s. He was "one of the most important directors of the golden age of Argentine cinema". Herrera was born in Bilbao, Spain. Wikipedia
Born : September 23, 1889, Bilbao, Spain
Died : March 30, 1956 (age 66 years), Buenos Aires, Argentina
