Does Croatia have two time zones?
Timezones are always calculated by their difference to UTC, the "Universal Time Coordinated". In Croatia there is only a single timezone at UTC+2 . For example, New York is currently at UTC-4 in Eastern Time, so the time difference between New York and Croatia is six hours.
