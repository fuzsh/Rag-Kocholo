John G. Avildsen

American film director
John Guilbert Avildsen was an American film director. He is best known for directing Rocky, which earned him the Academy Award for Best Director. He is also renowned for directing the first three films in The Karate Kid franchise. Wikipedia
Born : December 21, 1935, Oak Park, IL
Died : June 16, 2017 (age 81 years), Cedars-Sinai Medical Center, Los Angeles, CA
Children : Jonathan Avildsen , Ash Avildsen , and Bridget Avildsen
Parents : Clarence John Avildsen and Ivy Avildsen
Spouse : Tracy Brooks Swope (m. 1987–2003) and Marie Olga Maturevich (m. 1964–1979)
Other names : Johnny Avildsen

What is the Formula movie about?
While investigating the death of a friend and fellow cop, Los Angeles police officer Barney Caine (George C. Scott) stumbles across evidence that Nazis created a synthetic alternative to gasoline during World War II. This revelation has the potential to end the established global oil industry, making the formula a very valuable and dangerous piece of information. Eventually, Caine must contend with oil tycoon Adam Steiffel (Marlon Brando), who clearly has his own agenda regarding the formula.
