What is the history of the Dodge Center Minnesota?
History. The Dodge Center area was first settled by pioneers in the 1850s . The town was created by the railroad system and named for its central location within Dodge County, Minnesota, which was named for Henry Dodge, twice the governor of Wisconsin. The first train to reach Dodge Center arrived on July 13, 1866.
