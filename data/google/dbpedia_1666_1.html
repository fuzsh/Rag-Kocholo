When did Korchnoi defect?
Born in Leningrad, Soviet Union (USSR), Korchnoi defected to the Netherlands in 1976 , and resided in Switzerland from 1978, becoming a Swiss citizen. Korchnoi played four matches, three of which were official, against GM Anatoly Karpov. In 1974, Korchnoi lost the Candidates Tournament final to Karpov.
