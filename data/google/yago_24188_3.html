Where was Reshma aur Shera shot?
Equally bold visually and ideologically, this high budget but highly idiosyncratic film, shot in and around Jaisalmer , uses a conventional romantic narrative—a variant on Romeo and Juliet, here transposed to the realm of feuding Rajput clans—in the service of a decidedly unconventional message.
