Where did Roy Acuff grow up?
Dubbed the King of Country Music, Roy Claxton Acuff was born in Maynardville, Tennessee on September 15, 1903. He was born to father, Neil Acuff, who worked as a lawyer, postmaster, and pastor, and mother, Ida Carr, who worked as a homemaker. Roy's father played the fiddle, exposing Roy to music at an early age.
