Former Prime Minister of Cambodia

Born : October 31, 1922, Phnom Penh, Cambodia
Died : October 15, 2012 (age 89 years), Beijing, China

Children : Norodom Sihamoni , Norodom Ranariddh , Norodom Buppha Devi , and more
