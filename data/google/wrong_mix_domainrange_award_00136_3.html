Walther Bothe

German physicist
Walther Wilhelm Georg Bothe was a German nuclear physicist known for the development of coincidence methods to study particle physics. He served in the military during World War I from 1914, and he was a prisoner of war of the Russians, returning... Wikipedia
Born : January 8, 1891, Oranienburg, Germany
Died : February 8, 1957 (age 66 years), Heidelberg, Germany
Known for : Bothe–Geiger coincidence experiment; Coincidence method ; Coincidence circuit
Education : Humboldt University of Berlin (1908–1913) and University of Giessen
Books : Der Physiker und sein Werkzeug , Die Streuung von Elektronen in schrägen Folien , and Der Streufehler bei der Ausmessung von Nebelkammerbahnen im Magnetfeld
Awards : Max Planck Medal and Nobel Prize in Physics
Spouse : Barbara Belowa (m. 1920)
