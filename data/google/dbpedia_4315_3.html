Who was Philip Johnston and what was his idea?
Most scholars credit Philip Johnston with initiating the Code Talker idea. A caucasian who grew up in Leupp, Arizona on the Navajo Reservation east of Flagstaff, he approached the Marine Corps in 1942 after the attack on Pearl Harbor and proposed using Native American languages for combat communications.
