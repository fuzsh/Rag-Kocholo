What is the tone of the Modest Proposal?
In "A Modest Proposal," narrative tone must be separated from authorial tone: the narrator, or the "Proposer," is distinct from Swift himself. The Proposer's tone is earnest, persuasive, and rational ; all of these tonal aspects add to the horror of the proposal.
