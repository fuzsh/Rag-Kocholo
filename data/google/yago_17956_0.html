Why did Terence Hill change his name?
Changed his name in 1967. Hill stated on his official German website that it was made up by film producers who gave him a list with 20 names and 24 hours time to choose one of the names. He picked "Terence Hill" for he liked this name the most and it has his mother's initials whose name was "Hildegard Thieme" .
