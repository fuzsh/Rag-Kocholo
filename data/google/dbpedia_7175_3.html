Krishna Kanta Handique

Scholar
Krishna Kanta Handique was a Sanskrit scholar, an Indologist and philanthropist from Assam. He was a recipient of the civilian honour of the Padma Shri and Padma Bhushan. Wikipedia
Born : July 20, 1898, Jorhat, India
Died : June 7, 1982 (age 83 years), Dibrugarh, India
Awards : Sahitya Akademi Award and Padma Bhushan
Books : Somadeva's Yaśastilaka: Aspects of Jainism, Indian Thought and Culture and Krushnakanta Rachana Sambhar
Education : University of Oxford (1920–1923), University of Calcutta (1917–1919), Sanskrit College and University (1915–1917), and more
Children : Bijoy Krishna Handique
Language : Assamese
