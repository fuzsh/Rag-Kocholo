Who played piano for Tony Bennett?
Tony Bennett and Ralph Sharon began working together in 1957, kicking off a musical partnership that lasted for more than half a century. Sharon is the pianist you hear on many of Bennett's records over the course of his entire career. He also accompanied him live for decades.
