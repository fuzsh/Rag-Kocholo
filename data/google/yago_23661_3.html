Prince George, Duke of Cumberland

Prince consort
Prince George of Denmark and Norway, Duke of Cumberland, was the husband of Anne, Queen of Great Britain. He was the consort of the British monarch from Anne's accession on 8 March 1702 until his death in 1708. Wikipedia
Born : April 2, 1653, Copenhagen Castle, Copenhagen, Denmark

Spouse : Anne, Queen of Great Britain (m. 1683–1708)
