Pedro Pires

Former President of Cape Verde
Pedro de Verona Rodrigues Pires is a Cape Verdean politician who served as Prime Minister of Cape Verde from 1975 to 1991, and later as President from 2001 to 2011. Wikipedia
Born : 1934 (age 90 years), Sao Filipe, Cabo Verde
Education : University of Lisbon
Spouse : Adélcia Barreto
Party : African Party for the Independence of Cape Verde
Previous offices : President of Cape Verde (2001–2011) and Prime Minister of Cape Verde (1975–1991)
Presidential term : March 22, 2001 – September 9, 2011
Date knighted : May 28, 2002
