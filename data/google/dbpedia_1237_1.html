What is the variation of the name Conrad?
Conrad is a masculine name of German origin, meaning “brave counsel.” It is derived from the Old High German word kuoni, which translates to “brave,” and rāt, meaning “counsel.” Conrad has a few worldly variations, including the English spelling Konrad and the Italian Corrado or Corradi .
