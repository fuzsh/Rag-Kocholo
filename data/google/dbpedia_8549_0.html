Hans Schweikart was born on 1 October 1895 in Berlin, Germany. He was a director and actor, known for Fasching (1939), Freed Hands (1939) and Ein Haus voll Liebe (1954). He was married to Carlotta Vetrone, Maria Kraushaar and Käthe Nevil. He died on 1 December 1975 in Munich, Bavaria, West Germany.
