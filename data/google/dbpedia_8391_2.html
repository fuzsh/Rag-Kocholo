What did William Burroughs invent?
Burroughs received a patent in 1888 for his " Calculating Machine ." Burroughs and several St. Louis businessmen formed the American Arithometer Co. to market the machine. The calculating machine, however, proved to be inaccurate in everyday use, so Burroughs received a patent in 1893 for an improved calculating machine.
