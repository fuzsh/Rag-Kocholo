Is Etobicoke now Toronto?
When Etobicoke, along with Scarborough, North York, East York, and York, became part of one large, amalgamated City of Toronto in 1998, “Etobicoke” ceased to exist as a legal entity. But after 200 years of existence, Etobicoke definitely still exists in the hearts and memories of those who call this place “home.”
