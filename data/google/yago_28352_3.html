Peter IV of Aragon

Peter IV, called the Ceremonious, was from 1336 until his death the king of Aragon, Sardinia-Corsica, and Valencia, and count of Barcelona. In 1344, he deposed James III of Majorca and made himself King of Majorca. Wikipedia
Born : September 5, 1319, Balaguer, Spain
Died : January 5, 1387 (age 67 years), Barcelona, Spain
Children : John I of Aragon , Martin of Aragon , Eleanor of Aragon, Queen of Castile , and more
