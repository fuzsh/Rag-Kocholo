Elinor Ostrom

American political scientist
View all
Elinor Claire "Lin" Ostrom was an American political scientist and political economist whose work was associated with New Institutional Economics and the resurgence of political economy. Wikipedia
Born : August 7, 1933, Los Angeles, CA
Died : June 12, 2012 (age 78 years), Bloomington, IN
Education : University of California, Los Angeles (1965) and Beverly Hills High School (1951)
Awards : Nobel Memorial Prize in Economic Sciences and John J. Carty Award for the Advancement of Science
Spouse : Vincent Ostrom (m. 1963–2012)
Parents : Leah Hopkins and Adrian Awan
