Ron Marlenee

Former United States Representative
Ronald Charles Marlenee was a Republican politician who served in the United States House of Representatives from the U.S. state of Montana from January 3, 1977, to January 3, 1993. He represented Montana's 2nd congressional district. Wikipedia
Born : August 8, 1935, Scobey, MT
Died : April 26, 2020 (age 84 years), Bozeman, MT
Party : Republican Party
Previous office : Representative (1977–1993)
Education : Montana State University and University of Montana
