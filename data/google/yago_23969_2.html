What was Martin Lawrence's first stand up?
You So Crazy is a 1994 stand up comedy film directed by Thomas Schlamme and starring Martin Lawrence. It was his first major film and stand-up act. Lawrence wears a black, leather suit in the film similar to the wardrobe worn by Eddie Murphy in his films Delirious and Raw.

What year was Martin Lawrence born?
1965
Lawrence was born on April 16, 1965 , in Frankfurt, Germany, where his father, a U.S. Air Force serviceman, was stationed. The family soon returned to the U.S., settling in Landover, Maryland. After graduating from high school, Lawrence decided to try his luck as a stand-up comic.
