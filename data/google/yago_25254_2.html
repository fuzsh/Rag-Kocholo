Who is Maria of Austria?
Maria of Austria, the daughter of Charles V and Isabella of Portugal , was born in 1528. In 1548 she married her cousin, the Archduke Maximilian. She reigned as empress from 1564 to 1576, and in 1580 returned to Spain, entering the convent of Las Descalzas Reales (Madrid), where she remained until her death in 1603.
