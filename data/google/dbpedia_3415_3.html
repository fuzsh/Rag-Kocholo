What was Lil Jon and the Eastside Boyz first hit?
In 1995, Lil Jon collaborated with Big Sam and Lil' Bo to form a musical rap group: the East Side Boyz. In 1996, the group released their debut single, " Who U Wit? ". The song is credited as bringing the term "crunk" into hip-hop currency. In 1997, the group released their debut album Get Crunk, Who U Wit: Da Album.
