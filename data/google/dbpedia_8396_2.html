What is the legacy of Varahamihira?
Legacy. Varāhamihira gained reputation as the most eminent writer on jyotisha after his death , and his works superseded nearly all the earlier Indian texts in this area. Several later Indian astrologer-astronomers speak highly of him, and acknowledge his works among their main sources.
