Did you mean: Hot Wax is a type of Book.
Hot Wacks is a Canadian book that has been in circulation for over 25 years, featuring reviews of bootleg music releases. Reviews include information such as source, quality, track listing and packaging. It is commonly referred to as the ' Bootleg Bible ' by bootleg collectors.
