What films did Bernard Herrmann compose for?
He wrote the scores for seven Hitchcock films, from The Trouble with Harry (1955) to Marnie (1964) , a period that included Vertigo, North by Northwest, and Psycho. He was also credited as sound consultant on The Birds (1963), as there was no actual music in the film as such, only electronically made bird sounds.
