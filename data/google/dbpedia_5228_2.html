What is the history of Barisal district?
The Barisal region was inhabited by Hindus till 1800 . Later, the influx of Muslims from different areas, conversions and mass emigration of Hindus led to a Muslim majority. At present a number of Christians and Buddhists also live here. In ancient times, a nation called "Chandrabhadra" lived in Barisal.
