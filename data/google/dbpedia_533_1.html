What is the UTC offset?
Offset from Coordinated Universal Time. Offset from Coordinated Universal Time represents the difference in hours and minutes between UTC and the local system time . A negative offset indicates that the time is west of UTC and a positive offset indicates that the time is east of UTC.

What is the UTC offset for GPS time?
Leap seconds are added to UTC periodically to keep it in sync with the earth's rotation. GPS time, however, does not account for leap seconds, meaning there is a constant offset between GPS time and UTC. At the moment, the offset of UTC to GPS is +18sec .
