What is the history of VMO 1?
World War II Marine Observation Squadron 155 (VMO-155) was formed at Marine Corps Air Station Quantico, Virginia on 27 July 1943 . In November of that year they moved out to San Diego, California and on 1 January 1944, they were redesignated Marine Observation Squadron 1 (VMO-1) and deployed to Espiritu Santo.
