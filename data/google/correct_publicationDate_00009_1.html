Cordwainer Smith

American author
Paul Myron Anthony Linebarger, better known by his pen-name Cordwainer Smith, was an American author known for his science fiction works. Linebarger was a US Army officer, a noted East Asia scholar, and an expert in psychological warfare. Wikipedia
Born : July 11, 1913, Milwaukee, WI
Died : August 6, 1966 (age 53 years), Baltimore, MD
Parents : Paul Myron Wentworth Linebarger
Spouse : Genevieve Collins (m. 1950) and Margaret Snow (m. 1936)
Notable works : "Scanners Live in Vain"; Psychological Warfare
