Born : June 27, 1880, Perovo District, Moscow, Russia
Died : January 23, 1952 (age 71 years), Paris, France
Spouse : Grand Duke Michael Alexandrovich of Russia (m. 1912–1918), Vladimir Wulfert (m. 1905), and Sergei Mamontov (m. 1902–1905)
Grandchildren : Pauline Gray and Alexandra Majolier
Children : George Mikhailovich, Count Brasov and Nathalie Mamontov
Place of burial : Passy Cemetery, Paris, France
Parents : Sergei Alexandrovich Sheremetevsky and Julia Vyacheslavovna Sventitskaya
