What is the funk genre of R&B?
Funk music emerged in the late 1960s. African-American musicians created it by combining elements of soul, jazz, and R&B . Funk features strong bass lines, often played by the electric bass guitar, and syncopated beats, where the emphasis is shifted from the strong beats to off beats.
