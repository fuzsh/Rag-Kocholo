What is the method of production of test tube babies?
The process of in vitro fertilization is used in laboratories to create test-tube babies. During this process, the sperm and egg are fertilized, then implanted inside the uterus of the biological mother or surrogate mother. While they are called test-tube babies, they are not developed in test tubes.
