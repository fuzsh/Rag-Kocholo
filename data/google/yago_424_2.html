What does the phrase "grave danger" mean?
Grave danger means extreme risk which could prove fatal . This is not an ordinary danger like scraping your knee, it's more like losing your whole leg or even dying. If you fall off a cliff you are at risk of dying from the fall which would qualify as grave danger.
