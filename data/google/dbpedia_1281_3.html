Raúl Silva Henríquez

Chilean prelate
Raúl Silva Henríquez SDB was a Chilean prelate of the Catholic Church, a cardinal from 1962. He served as Archbishop of Santiago de Chile from 1961 to 1983 and as Bishop of Valparaíso from 1959 to 1961. Wikipedia
Born : September 27, 1907, Talca, Chile
Died : April 9, 1999 (age 91 years), Santiago, Chile
Organizations founded : Universidad Católica Silva Henríquez , Committee of Cooperation for Peace in Chile , and Banco Del Desarrollo
Parents : Ricardo Silva Silva and Mercedes Henríquez Encina
Education : Pontif.Universidad Catolica de Chile (1929) and Salesian Pontifical University
Archdiocese : Santiago de Chile
Created cardinal : 19 March 1962; by Pope John XXIII
