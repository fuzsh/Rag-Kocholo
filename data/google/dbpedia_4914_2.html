Joan Maragall

Poet and journalist
Joan Maragall i Gorina was a Catalan poet, journalist and translator, the foremost member of the modernisme movement in literature. His manuscripts are preserved in the Joan Maragall Archive of Barcelona. Wikipedia
Born : October 10, 1860, Barcelona, Spain
Died : December 20, 1911 (age 51 years), Barcelona, Spain
Children : Jordi Maragall i Noble , Ernest Maragall i Noble , Joan A. Maragall i Noble , and more
Spouse : Clara Noble (m. 1891–1911)
Grandchildren : Pasqual Maragall , Ernest Maragall , Pau Malvido , and more
Influenced by : Jacint Verdaguer , Friedrich Nietzsche , Narcís Oller , and more
