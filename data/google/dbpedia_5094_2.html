What are some interesting facts about Theophile Gautier?
Théophile Gautier was born at Tarbes, one of the most ancient cities of France, in the year 1811 . He came to Paris at a very early age, and studied at the College of Charlemagne, at which place he became acquainted with Gérard de Nerval, with whom, later, he wrote many of his dramatic criticisms.
