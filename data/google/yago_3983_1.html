Charlotte "Lotte" Reiniger was a German film director and the foremost pioneer of silhouette animation. Her best known films are The Adventures of Prince Achmed, from 1926, the oldest surviving feature-length animated film, and Papageno. Wikipedia
Born : June 2, 1899, Berlin, Germany
Died : June 19, 1981 (age 82 years), Dettenhausen, Germany
Parents : Eleonore Lina Wilhelmine Rakette and Carl Reiniger
Spouse : Carl Koch (m. 1921–1963)
Notable work : The Adventures of Prince Achmed (1926); Girl of the Golden West (1942)
