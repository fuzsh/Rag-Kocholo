What is a sopranino voice type?
Sopranino refers to a singing voice that is higher than soprano . It typically refers to a range of about E 4 to E 6 , sometimes extending as high as G 6 . Even a A 6 A sopranino voice type is rare. It is not considered a classical music part, nor a popular name, but would be cast as a high soprano.
