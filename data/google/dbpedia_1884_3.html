What is the meaning of the survival of the fittest?
Also known as “natural selection,” it is a simple statement of the fact that in dangerous circumstances, only those individuals most adapted to their environment survive —and the world, with its limited food supply, fearsome predators, and devastating diseases is always a dangerous place.
