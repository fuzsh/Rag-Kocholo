What is the story behind the song Disco 2000 by Pulp?
Featuring a disco-inspired musical performance, the song was based on Pulp singer Jarvis Cocker's childhood memories of his friend Deborah Bone , who he had "fancied" in his youth but could never impress. "Disco 2000" was released as a single on 27 November 1995 by Island Records, the third from Different Class.
