Who sings the song "Girlfriend"?
"Girlfriend" is a song by Avril Lavigne , released as the lead single from her third studio album, The Best Damn Thing. It was produced by Lukasz Gottwald. "Girlfriend" became Lavigne's biggest hit in the United States, where it became her first number one single in the U.S. Billboard Hot 100.
