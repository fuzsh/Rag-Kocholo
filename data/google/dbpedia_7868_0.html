The catalog of Man's Ruin is vast, including relatively famous bands such as The Hellacopters, Nebula, Kyuss, High on Fire, Entombed, Turbonegro, Queens of the Stone Age and The Sex Pistols , to less known bands such as FuckEmos, Soulpreacher, Angelrot, and Los Cowslingers.

What happened to Man's ruin?
Decline and Fall. The label became defunct after a series of distribution changes and problems , the label having outgrown its original distributor. Another factor was small debts being owed to multiple bands.
