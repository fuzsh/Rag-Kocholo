Where was Schubert born?
Franz Peter Schubert was born in Vienna, Austria on January 31, 1797. Schubert was one of the few major composers of his time to be born and raised in Vienna. Haydn, Mozart, and Beethoven all moved there as adults. During his short lifetime, Vienna was a cosmopolitan city that underwent countless upheavals.
