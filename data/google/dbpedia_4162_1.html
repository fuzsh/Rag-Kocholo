Bellu Cemetery

4.5
(221) Cemetery in Bucharest, Romania
Directions
Website
Șerban Vodă Cemetery is the largest and most famous cemetery in Bucharest, Romania. It is located on a plot of land donated to the local administration by Baron Barbu Bellu. It has been in use since 1858. The cemetery covers 28 hectares and it is a cultural attraction in Bucharest.
