Éric Rochant

French film director and screenwriter
Éric Rochant is a French film director and screenwriter. He is an alumnus of the IDHEC from the generation of Arnaud Desplechin and Noémie Lvovsky. Rochant is of Jewish background. Wikipedia
Born : 1961 (age 63 years), Paris, France
Awards : César Award for Best First Feature Film , César Award for Best Short Film - Fiction , and Louis Delluc Prize for Best Film
Nominations : César Award for Best First Feature Film , César Award for Best Film , César Award for Best Writing , and more
Education : Institut des hautes études cinématographiques and La Fémis
