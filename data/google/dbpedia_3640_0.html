What is the history of Jasdan State?
History. Jasdan state was founded in 1665 when Vika Khachar defeated the Khumans of Kherdi . In 1807, after its ruler Vajsur Khachar came to an agreement with the British and the Gaikwar, it became a British protectorate. Later in the 19th century Jasdan became part of the Kathiawar Agency of the Bombay Presidency.
