Peter Godfrey

English actor and film director
Peter Godfrey was an English actor and film director. Founder of the experimental Gate Theatre Salon in 1925, with his first wife Molly Veness, he staged London's first expressionistic production in the following year. Wikipedia
Born : October 16, 1899, London, United Kingdom
Died : March 4, 1970 (age 70 years), Hollywood, Los Angeles, CA
Spouse : Renee Godfrey (m. 1941–1964) and Molly Veness (m. 1923)
Children : Bobbie Poledouris
Grandchildren : Zoë Poledouris and Alexis Poledouris
