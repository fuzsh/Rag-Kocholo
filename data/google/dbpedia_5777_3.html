Is von a German last name?
The term von ([fɔn]) is used in German surnames either as a nobiliary particle indicating a noble patrilineality, or as a simple preposition used by commoners that means 'of' or 'from'. Nobility directories like the Almanach de Gotha often abbreviate the noble term von to v.

Is von a middle name or part of the last name?
Von is a masculine moniker of German origin. It is a commonly used prefix or term , which you might recognize from various surnames like Von Stein or Von Trapp, and was often used to indicate noble familial ties. It means “from” or “of” and is a short and sweet reminder to your little one about their ancestral roots.
