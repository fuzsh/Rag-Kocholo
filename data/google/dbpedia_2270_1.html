What is the origin of the name Clarissa?
Clarissa is a female given name borrowed from Latin, Italian, and Portuguese , originally denoting a nun of the Roman Catholic Order of St. Clare. It is a combination of St. Clare of Assisi's Latin name Clara (originally meaning "clear" and "bright") and the suffix -issa, equivalent to -ess.
