Atlanta Rhythm Section (ARS) is an American Southern rock band from Doraville, Georgia . Formed in the summer of 1971, the group originally featured vocalist Rodney Justo, guitarist Barry Bailey, bassist Paul Goddard, keyboardist Dean Daughtry and drummer Robert Nix.
