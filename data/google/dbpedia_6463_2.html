Is there a train from Port Adelaide to the city?
Adelaide Metro operates a train from Port Adelaide station to Adelaide station every 30 minutes . Tickets cost $3 and the journey takes 21 min. Alternatively, Adelaide Metro operates a bus from Zone D Port Adelaide Interchange - East side to Stop 1 Port Rd - North side every 20 minutes.
