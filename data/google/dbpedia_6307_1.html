Why were propellers made of wood?
They featured a permanently set blade angle, called fixed-pitch, and were made from layers of wood. They were cheap, easy to manufacture, and light , and were the dominant propeller type for high performance aircraft until the mid-1920s. After that, small general aviation aircraft relied upon them for thrust.
