How did Bridal Veil Oregon get its name?
Namesake. Legend has it that while traveling on the Columbia River a passenger on the sternwheeler, Baily Gatzert, saw Bridal Veil Falls and remarked that it looked like a "delicate, misty bride's veil." As the years went by people began to refer to this spot along the Columbia River Gorge as Bride's Veil, Oregon.
