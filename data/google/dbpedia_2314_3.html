Ellen Dease was born in Naas, County Kildare, Ireland , the youngest of five children born to Oliver and Anne Nugent Dease. Her father was a surgeon at Westmoreland Lock Hospital. Orphaned at a young age, she was raised by her maternal grandmother in Dublin, where she attended a school for young ladies.
