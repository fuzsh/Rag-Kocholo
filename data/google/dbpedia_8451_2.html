What is the history of Ilam?
Etymology. The name Ilam is derived from the Limbu language in which "IL" means twisted and "Lam" means road . Ilam was one of the ten self ruling states of Limbuwan before the reunification of Nepal. Its ruler, King Hangshu Phuba Lingdom of Lingdom dynasty, ruled Ilam as a confederate state of Limbuwan until 1813 AD.
