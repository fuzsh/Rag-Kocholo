What happened to the group Kraftwerk?
Following the release of Electric Café (1986), Flür left the group in 1987, followed by Bartos in 1990 . The band released Tour de France Soundtracks, their latest album of new material, in 2003. Founding member Schneider left in 2008. The band, with new members, has continued to tour under the leadership of Hütter.

Why did Kraftwerk make the Tour de France?
For Kraftwerk, Tour De France was a departure from the technological tone of the two previous albums, The Man-Machine and Computer World. Instead, the song is a joie de vivre celebration of cycling, marking the group's increasing interest in the sport .
