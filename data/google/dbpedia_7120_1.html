Kirti Nagar

4.1
(12,147) Metro station in Delhi, India

The Kirti Nagar metro station is an interchange station between the Blue Line and Green Line of Delhi Metro. It is one of the eastward terminal stations of the Green Line, the other being Inderlok, and was opened as a part of a small 3.41 km branch from this station to Ashok Park Main station on 27 August 2011.
