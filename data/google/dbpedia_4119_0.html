Fatih Terim

Turkish football manager
Fatih Terim is a Turkish professional football manager and former player. He was most recently the manager of Greek Super League club Panathinaikos. During his football career, he played for Ceyhanspor, Adana Demirspor and Galatasaray. Wikipedia
Born : September 4, 1953 (age 70 years), Adana, Türkiye
Dates joined : 1975 ( Turkey national football team ), 1974 ( Galatasaray S.K. ), and 1969 ( Adana Demirspor )
Grandchild : Nil Bahçekapılı
Awards : Milliyet Sports Award for Executive of the Year , Milliyet Sports Award for Manager of the Year , Milliyet Sports Award for Turkish Manager of the Year , and more
Nominations : Milliyet Sports Award for Athlete of the Year , Milliyet Sports Award for Executive of the Year , Milliyet Sports Award for Manager of the Year , and more
Children : Buse Terim and Merve Terim Çetin
Nationality : Turkish
