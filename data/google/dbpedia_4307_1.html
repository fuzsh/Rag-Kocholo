What kind of music is sodagreen?
Sodagreen is a Taiwanese alternative rock band formed in 2001. The members are Wu Tsing-Fong (lead vocalist and songwriter), Cheng Chia-Chun (bass guitar), and Lee Wei (drums). Their music is inspired by folk and indie influences.
