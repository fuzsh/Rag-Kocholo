François Dupeyron

French film director and screenwriter
François Dupeyron was a French film director and screenwriter. He directed 17 films between 1977 and 2015. His film La Chambre des officiers was entered into the 2001 Cannes Film Festival. Wikipedia
Born : August 14, 1950, Tartas, France
Died : February 25, 2016 (age 65 years), Paris, France
Nominations : César Award for Best Short Film - Documentary , César Award for Best Film , César Award for Best First Feature Film , and more
Spouse : Dominique Faysse
