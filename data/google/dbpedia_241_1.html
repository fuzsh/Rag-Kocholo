What is the difference between humidity and humidex?
The humidex is used to measure the perceived temperature taking into account the humidity . This is a value that only really matters when the temperature is above 30 degrees when it starts to get hot. In concrete terms, the humidex varies according to the temperature under cover and the relative humidity.
