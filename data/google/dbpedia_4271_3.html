Jürgen Moser

German-American mathematician
Jürgen Kurt Moser was a German-American mathematician, honored for work spanning over four decades, including Hamiltonian dynamical systems and partial differential equations. Wikipedia
Born : July 4, 1928, Königsberg
Died : December 17, 1999 (age 71 years), Schwerzenbach, Switzerland
Awards : Wolf Prize in Mathematics , George David Birkhoff Prize , James Craig Watson Medal , and more
Parents : Kurt Moser
Doctoral advisor : Franz Rellich ; Carl Ludwig Siegel
Doctoral students : Charles Conley; Håkan Eliasson
