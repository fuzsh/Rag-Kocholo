What is the summary of Naipaul's fiction?
Naipaul's fictions are largely categorised as postcolonial fictions, dealing with the complicated lives of people lost in the crowd, seeking to find a concrete identity and space for themselves . Postcolonial fictions are marked by the description of decolonisation and its after-effects on politics, culture and society.
