Is it spelled starring or staring?
The dictionary says starring means "(of a performance) to have someone as a principal performer." For example: "Hamilton is a musical starring Lin-Manuel Miranda as Alexander Hamilton." Staring means "look fixedly at someone or something with one's eyes wide open."

What does staring mean in acting?
Starring means being the main character in a movie or play . Starring is an adjective that's used when someone's at the center of things, like a concert starring a famous cellist. The Greek word aster became the English word star, which was first used to mean "to be featured in a play" in the 1820s.
