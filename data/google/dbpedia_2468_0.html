How did Hispanic Americans play a part in the US military?
Most Hispanics were integrated into the regular Army or volunteer units, although some served in predominantly Hispanic units with their own officers . Hispanics were especially instrumental in protecting the Southwest against Confederate advances, most notably in California, Arizona and New Mexico.
