What did Prince Sihanouk do during the Vietnam War?
As war raged in Vietnam, Sihanouk sought desperately to preserve Cambodia's neutrality . Alternately taking sides with China and then the United States, he was not successful in preventing the war from spilling over into his country. In 1970, while he was out of the country, a coup took place that ousted him from power.
