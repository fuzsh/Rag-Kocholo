Ann Smith Franklin (October 2, 1696 – April 16, 1763) was an American colonial newspaper printer and publisher. She inherited the business from her husband, James Franklin, brother of Benjamin Franklin . She published the Newport Mercury, printed an almanac series, and printed Rhode Island paper currency.
