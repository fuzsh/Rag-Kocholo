Witold Leszczyński

Polish film director and screenwriter
Witold Leszczyński was a Polish film director and screenwriter. In 1967 he graduated from the National Film School in Łódź. He directed over thirty films between 1959 and 2007. Wikipedia
Born : August 16, 1933, Łódź, Poland
