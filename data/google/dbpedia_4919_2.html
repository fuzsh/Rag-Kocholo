Loukas Papademos

Former Prime Minister of the Hellenic Republic
Lucas Demetrios Papademos is a Greek economist and academic who served as 12th Prime Minister of Greece from November 2011 to May 2012, leading a national unity government in the wake of the Greek debt crisis. Wikipedia
Born : 1947 (age 76 years), Athens, Greece
Education : Massachusetts Institute of Technology (1978) and Athens College
Spouse : Shanna Ingram
Previous offices : Prime Minister of the Hellenic Republic (2011–2012), Vice-President of the European Central Bank (2002–2010), and Governor of the Bank of Greece (1994–2002)
Edited works : Enhancing Monetary Analysis , The European Monetary System in the 1990s , and External Constraints on Macroeconomic Policy
Books : The European Monetary System in the 1990s
