How did Men at Work get their name?
The name Men At Work was thrown into the hat by Colin Hay, and was seconded by Ron Strykert, when a name was required to put on the blackboard outside The Cricketer's Arms Hotel, Richmond . The band built a "grass roots" reputation as a pub rock band.
