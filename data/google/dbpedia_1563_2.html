Charlotte Ida Anna "Lotte" Stam-Beese was a German-Dutch architect, photographer and urban planner who helped with the reconstruction of Rotterdam after World War II. Wikipedia
Born : January 28, 1903, Rokitki, Poland
Died : November 18, 1988 (age 85 years), Krimpen aan den IJssel, Netherlands
Books : Lotte Stam-Beese, 1903-1988: Dessau, Brno, Charkow, Moskou, Amsterdam, Rotterdam
Children : Ariane Stam
