How do you do underwater hockey?
Two teams of six players face off against each other. The puck rests on the bottom of the playing area, and the two teams vie to control the puck and put it into their opponent's goal, which also resides on the bottom of the playing area. The playing area is roughly 25m long by 15m wide and optimally 2.5m deep.
