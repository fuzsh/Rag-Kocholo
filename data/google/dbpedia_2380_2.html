What does the word "questacon" mean?
The name 'Questacon' combines two words— 'quest' meaning 'to discover' and 'con' meaning 'to study' . Despite a modest beginning, by the mid-1980s Questacon had achieved national and international recognition. Today Questacon is Australia's largest science centre and is among the world's leading science centres.

How long do you need at Questacon for adults?
Visitor numbers are limited, and tickets can sell out quickly, especially on weekends and school holidays. We suggest that you allow at least 4 hours to see our exhibitions, café, shop and to explore the surrounding areas of Questacon. We would suggest at least 2 hours if you just wanted to see our exhibitions.
