Robert Altman

American film director and screenwriter
Robert Bernard Altman was an American film director, screenwriter, and producer. He was a five-time nominee of the Academy Award for Best Director and is considered an enduring figure from the New Hollywood era. Wikipedia
Born : February 20, 1925, Kansas City, MO
Died : November 20, 2006 (age 81 years), Cedars-Sinai Medical Center, Los Angeles, CA
Spouse : Kathryn Reed (m. 1959–2006), Lotus Corelli (m. 1954–1957), and LaVonne Elmer (m. 1946–1951)
Children : Robert Reed Altman , Mike Altman , Stephen Altman , and more
Awards : Golden Lion , Academy Honorary Award , Golden Globe Award for Best Director - Motion Picture , and more
Height : 6′ 0″
