How did Napoleon react to Lannes' death?
Handsome, brave, and always dependable, Lannes' death was a crushing blow to Napoleon. His soldiers mourned; Napoleon wept openly . He wrote to Lannes' widow: "I [have lost] the most distinguished general in my army, my companion in arms for sixteen years, and… my best friend."
