Peter Yates

Film director
Peter James Yates was an English film director and producer, known for his versatility and “attention to detail” across a variety of genres. He received nominations for four Academy Awards, three BAFTA Awards, and two Golden Globe Awards. Wikipedia
Born : July 24, 1929, Aldershot, United Kingdom
Died : January 9, 2011 (age 81 years), London, United Kingdom
Descendants : Toby Yates and Miranda Yates
Awards : Golden Globe Award for Best Motion Picture – Musical or Comedy
Nominations : Golden Globe Award for Best Director - Motion Picture , Academy Award for Best Picture , Academy Award for Best Directing , and more
Books : Love Me , First Time , Girl Hunt , and more
Education : Royal Academy of Dramatic Art (1952) and Charterhouse School

What was the movie John and Mary about?
In the morning after a one-night stand, New Yorkers John (Dustin Hoffman) and Mary (Mia Farrow) piece together the night's events, from meeting at a singles bar to arriving back at John's apartment. Spending the day together, the two singles become better acquainted and try to figure out if their encounter will lead to anything serious. During their conversations, both are reminded of previous failed relationships and the emotional baggage they need to overcome.
