What is the significance of Delta S?
Delta S is how we measure entropy, which has to do with the disorder of a system . If delta S is positive it means that the reaction can occur spontaneously as it is an increase in disorder and does not require an input of energy, while a negative delta S means that the reaction is not spontaneous.
