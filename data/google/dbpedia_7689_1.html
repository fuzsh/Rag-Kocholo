What is the history of the 2 17 Australian Infantry Battalion?
The 2/17th Battalion was formed in the late April and early May of 1940 at Ingleburn Army Camp in south-west Sydney . As an infantry Battalion it comprised roughly nine hundred newly recruited New South Welshmen. Of the hundreds who served in the 2/17th Battalion, 188 men died and 573 more were wounded.
