The plane carrying Otis Redding and his band crashed at 3.28.pm into icy waters of Lake Monona near Madison. Redding was killed in the crash along with members from The Bar- Kays, Jimmy King, Ron Caldwell, Phalin Jones and Carl Cunningham . Trumpet player Ben Cauley was the only person to survive the crash.

Who survived the Otis Redding plane crash?
Ben S. Cauley Jr. (October 3, 1947 – September 21, 2015) was an American trumpet player, vocalist, songwriter, and founding member of the Stax recording group the Bar-Kays. He was the only survivor of the 1967 plane crash that claimed the lives of soul singer Otis Redding and four members of the Bar-Kays.
