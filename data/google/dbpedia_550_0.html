Is Zantedeschia poisonous?
Zantedeschia species are also poisonous due to the presence of calcium oxalate crystals in the form of raphides . All parts of the plant are poisonous, typically producing local irritation or a burning sensation in the mouth and occasionally vomiting and diarrhea. However leaves are sometimes cooked and eaten.
