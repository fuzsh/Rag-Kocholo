Hasso Plattner was born in Berlin in 1944 . He moved to the South of Germany shortly before graduating from high school and studied Communications Engineering at the Karlsruhe Institute of Technology (KIT), graduating in 1968. In that same year, he started working as a program developer at IBM Germany in Mannheim.
