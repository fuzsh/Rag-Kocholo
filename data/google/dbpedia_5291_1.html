How old is Stratovarius?
Stratovarius was founded in 1984 by three guys from Helsinki, Finland; drummer and vocalist Tuomo Lassila, bass player John Viherva and guitarist Staffan Strahlman. Before Stratovarius the same line up performed under name Black Water. The music of early Stratovarius was very different from what it is today.
