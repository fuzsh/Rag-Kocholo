Tony Benneworth

Australian cricketer
Anthony John Benneworth was an Australian cricketer who played for Tasmania from 1971 until 1979. He was a right-handed batsman and a right-arm medium-pace bowler. He played in Tasmania's inaugural Sheffield Shield team in 1977–78. Wikipedia

Died : March 10, 2018 (age 67 years), Ansons Bay, Australia
