Matthew McDonough (born March 12, 1969) is an American drummer best known as a member of the heavy metal band Mudvayne . He is the band's original drummer and has appeared and performed on every release by Mudvayne. He was also the original drummer of Audiotopsy since 2015 until his departure in 2021.
