Eduard von Möller (also spelled Moeller; 3 June 1814 – 2 November 1880) was a Prussian politician. He was Oberpräsident of Hesse-Nassau from 1867 to 1871. He later became Oberpräsident of Alsace-Lorraine from 1871 until the role was replaced by the office of Reichsstatthalter in 1879.

What is the significance of Alsace-Lorraine?
The loss of Alsace-Lorraine was a major cause of anti-German feeling in France in the period from 1871 to 1914 . France also suffered economically from the loss of Alsace-Lorraine's valuable iron ore deposits, iron- and steelmaking plants, and other industries to Germany.
