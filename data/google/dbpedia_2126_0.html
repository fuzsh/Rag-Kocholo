What is a 2 Tone music?
Two-tone or 2 tone, also known as ska-rock and ska revival, is a genre of British popular music of the late 1970s and early 1980s that fused traditional Jamaican ska, rocksteady, and reggae music with elements of punk rock and new wave music .

What musical genres came together in the British two tone movement?
2 Tone music was a blend of Jamaican reggae, ska, and British punk music . Jerry Dammers was an important figure in the new musical style. He started a label called 2 Tone Records in 1979 and was the lead singer and songwriter for one of the biggest bands of the 2 Tone movement: The Specials.

What is the genre ska?
Ska (/skɑː/; Jamaican Creole: skia, [skjæ]) is a music genre that originated in Jamaica in the late 1950s and was the precursor to rocksteady and reggae . It combined elements of Caribbean mento and calypso with American jazz and rhythm and blues.
