Did you mean: Where was Juan de Acuña, 2nd Marquess of Casa Fuerte born?
Juan de Acuña was the second Criollo (meaning that he was Spanish but born in the New World) to govern in New Spain . He was born in Lima in 1658, the son of Juan de Acuña and of Margarita Bejarano. At a young age joined the military.
