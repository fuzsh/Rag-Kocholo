Florian Schneider

German musician
Florian Schneider-Esleben was a German musician. He is best known as one of the founding members and leaders of the electronic band Kraftwerk, performing his role with the band until his departure in 2008. Wikipedia
Born : April 7, 1947, French occupation zone in Germany
Died : April 21, 2020 (age 73 years), Düsseldorf, Germany
Music groups : Kraftwerk (1970 – 2008) and Organisation (1969 – 1970)
Parents : Paul Schneider-Esleben
Instrument(s) : Synthesizer; vocals; guitar; flute; saxophone; drums; percussion; violin
