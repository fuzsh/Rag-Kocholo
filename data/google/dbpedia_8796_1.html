What is the capital of Al-Andalus?
Córdoba became the capital of the Caliphate of Córdoba, from which the Umayyad dynasty ruled all of al-Andalus until 1031. Under Umayyad rule, Córdoba was transformed into a world-leading center of education and learning, and by the 10th century it had grown to be the second-largest city in Europe.
