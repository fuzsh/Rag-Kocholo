When was Rygar released?
1986
Rygar is a 1986 side-scrolling platform game created by Tecmo. Originally released for Japanese arcades in June 1986 , the game was subsequently ported to the NES (1987), Commodore 64 (1987), ZX Spectrum (1987), Amstrad CPC (1987), Master System (1988), Atari Lynx (1990), and X68000 (1994).
