What is the primary function of the stratified squamous epithelium in the human body?
The epithelium is called stratified squamous epithelium because it is structured in layers. This epithelium is ideally adapted to parts of the body that are subjected to frequent abrasions, such as the skin. As a result, the primary function of stratified squamous epithelial tissue is abrasion protection .
