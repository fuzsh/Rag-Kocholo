Gregory Benford

American author and astrophysicist
Gregory Benford is an American science fiction author and astrophysicist who is professor emeritus at the department of physics and astronomy at the University of California, Irvine. He is a contributing editor of Reason magazine. Wikipedia
Born : 1941 (age 83 years), Mobile, AL
Short stories : If the Stars Are Gods , A Snark in the Night , Matter's End , and more
Spouse : Elisabeth Malartre (m. 2005) and Joan Abbe (m. 1967–2002)
Awards : Nebula Award for Best Novelette , John W. Campbell Memorial Award for Best Science Fiction Novel , Nebula Award for Best Novel , and more
Children : 2
Genre : Science fiction, hard science fiction
