How do you say have you eaten in Iban?
“ Udah makai? ” is the thing you hear an Iban asks a fellow Iban, be it in a phone conversation or a face-to-face interaction. If you walk on a ruai of a longhouse close to time for a meal, it is very common to hear the udah makai question which simply means “Have you eaten?” in Iban.
