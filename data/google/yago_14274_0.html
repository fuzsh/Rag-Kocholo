Who was the husband of Queen Farida?
Historical note: Queen Farida was born Safinaz Zulficar on 5th September 1921 and died on 17 October 1988. She was the first wife of King Farouk of Egypt and was queen from 1938 - 1948, when her husband divorced her after she had given birth to three daughters but failed to produce a male heir.
