What is the history of Ecole Normale Supérieure?
The school was founded in 1794 during the French Revolution, to provide homogeneous training of high-school teachers in France, but it later closed . The school was subsequently reestablished by Napoleon I as pensionnat normal from 1808 to 1822, before being recreated in 1826 and taking the name École normale in 1830.
