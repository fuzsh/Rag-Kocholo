Bharrat Jagdeo is a Guyanese politician who has been serving as Vice President of Guyana since 2020, in the administration of President Irfaan Ali. He had previously also held the office from 1997 until 1999, during the presidency of Janet Jagan. Wikipedia
Born : 1964 (age 60 years), Unity, Guyana
Spouse : Varshni Jagdeo (m. 1998–2007)
Education : Peoples' Friendship University of Russia
Presidential term : August 11, 1999 – December 3, 2011
Office : Vice-President of Guyana since 2020
Party : People's Progressive Party/Civic
Previous offices : President pro tempore of the Union of South American Nations (2010–2011), President of Guyana (1999–2011), and Prime Minister of Guyana (1999–1999)
