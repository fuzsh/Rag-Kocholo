Vajiravudh

Former King of Thailand
Vajiravudh was the sixth king of Siam from the Chakri dynasty, titled Rama VI. He reigned from 1910 until his death in 1925. King Vajiravudh is best known for his efforts to create and promote Siamese nationalism. Wikipedia
Born : January 1, 1881, The Grand Palace, Bangkok, Thailand
Died : November 26, 1925 (age 44 years), Phra Nakhon, Bangkok, Thailand
Children : Bejaratana
Spouse : Suvadhana (m. 1924–1925), Indrasakdi Sachi (m. 1922–1925), Lakshamilavan (m. 1922), and more
Siblings : Prajadhipok and Abhakara Kiartivongse
Education : Christ Church (1899–1901), Royal Military College, Sandhurst (1898–1898), and University of Oxford
Full name : Phra Bat Somdet Phra Ramathibodi Si Sinthra Maha Vajiravudh Phra Mongkut Klao Chao Yu Hua
