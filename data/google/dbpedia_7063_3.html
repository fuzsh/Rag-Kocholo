Herbert Ross (born May 13, 1927, Brooklyn, New York, U.S.—died October 9, 2001, New York, New York) was an American dancer and film director who made a significant contribution to the world of dance as a choreographer for ballet companies, the stage, and motion pictures before turning to directing motion pictures.
