What is the taifa in Spain history?
A taifa refers to one of many Muslim principalities that existed in Spain in the Middle Ages. The taifas originated when the Caliphate of Cordoba, which ruled most of Spain, fell apart in 1031 . As a result, many taifas sprung up, including the taifas of Seville, Toledo, and Zaragoza.
