Friedrich Hayek

Austrian-British academic
Friedrich August von Hayek CH FBA, often referred to by his initials F. A. Hayek, was an Austrian-British academic, who contributed to economics, political philosophy, psychology, and intellectual history. Wikipedia
Born : May 8, 1899, Vienna, Austria
Died : March 23, 1992 (age 92 years), Freiburg im Breisgau, Germany
Influenced : Milton Friedman , John Maynard Keynes , Murray Rothbard , and more
Education : University of Vienna (1923), University of Vienna (1921), and New York University
Influenced by : Ludwig von Mises , John Maynard Keynes , Adam Smith , and more
Children : Laurence Hayek and Christine von Hayek
