Maha Vajiralongkorn

King of Thailand
Vajiralongkorn is King of Thailand. He is the tenth monarch of Thailand from the Chakri dynasty, styled as Rama X. The only son of King Bhumibol Adulyadej and Queen Sirikit, he was made crown prince by his father in 1972, at the age of 20. Wikipedia
Born : July 28, 1952 (age 72 years), Amphorn Sathan Residential Hall, Bangkok, Thailand
Children : Vacharaesorn Vivacharawongse , Dipangkorn Rasmijoti , Bajrakitiyabha , and more
Spouse : Suthida (m. 2019), Srirasmi Suwadee (m. 2001–2014), Sujarinee Vivacharawongse (m. 1994–1996), and more
Parents : Bhumibol Adulyadej and Sirikit
Education : Command And General Staff College (1977–1978), UNSW Sydney (1972–1976), Royal Military College, Duntroon (1972–1976), and more
Siblings : Maha Chakri Sirindhorn , Chulabhorn , and Ubolratana
Height : 5′ 7″
