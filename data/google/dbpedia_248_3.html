What is the history of Vasaloppet?
The first Vasalopp was carried out as early as 1521 by Gustav Eriksson . During his flight from the Danish king he stopped in Sälen and returned to Mora to lead the uprising against the occupation forces. Gustav Eriksson eventually united the realm and became Sweden's first king, better known under the name Gustav Vasa.
