Márton Keleti

Hungarian screenwriter and film director
Márton Keleti was a Hungarian screenwriter and film director. He directed 50 films between 1937 and 1973. His 1959 film Yesterday was entered into the 1st Moscow International Film Festival. Wikipedia
Born : April 26, 1905, Budapest, Hungary
Died : June 20, 1973 (age 68 years), Budapest, Hungary
Education : Franz Liszt Academy of Music (1924)
Spouse : Olga Eszenyi (m. 1932–1944)
Awards : The second grade of Kossuth Prize and The third grade of Kossuth Prize
