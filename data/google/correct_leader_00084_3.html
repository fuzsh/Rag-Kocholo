When did Iraq become Shia?
The majority of Iraqis did not in fact convert to Shia Islam during the Safavid era. Iraqis converted en masse to Shia Islam in the 17th and 18th centuries due to the fact that the elite of Mesopotamia under the Ottoman was composed of Sunni Arabs. These Sunni elites oppressed the majority of Iraqis in the south.
