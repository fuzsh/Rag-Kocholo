What is the history of Westfield Heritage Village?
History of Westfield They envisioned a unique project which would develop a pioneer village as a non-profit educational institution to preserve and present the 19th century rural life in Upper Canada . Their vision became a reality when Westfield was officially opened to the public on June 15, 1964.
