What is the historical significance of the year 1923 for the believers of Syro-Malabar?
In 1923, the Syro-Malabar hierarchy was organized and unified under Ernakulam as the Metropolitan See, with Augustine Kandathil as the first head and Archbishop of the Church . With this, Syro-Malabar Church became an autonomous sui iuris church within the Catholic Church.
