What is the 56th London Infantry Division?
1st London/56th Division was a first-line Territorial Army formation in September 1939 . It was organized as a Motor Division, that is with only two brigades and supporting arms decreased in proportion, but with integral transport for its infantry. It was reorganised as a standard infantry division in June 1940.
