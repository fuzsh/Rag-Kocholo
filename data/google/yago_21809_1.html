Norodom Sihanouk

Former Prime Minister of Cambodia
Norodom Sihanouk was a member of the Cambodian royal house who led the country as King and Prime Minister. In Cambodia, he is known as Samdech Euv. Wikipedia
Born : October 31, 1922, Phnom Penh, Cambodia
Died : October 15, 2012 (age 89 years), Beijing, China
Date knighted : 1968
Deposed dates : March 3, 1955 and October 7, 2004
Organizations founded : Sangkum , FUNCINPEC , Royal University of Agriculture , and more
Grandchildren : Norodom Chakravuth , Norodom Rattana Devi , Norodom Sihariddh , and more
Grandparents : Sisowath Monivong and Norodom Kanviman Norleak Tevi
