What happened to Revco drug stores?
The chain's stock was traded on the New York Stock Exchange under the ticker symbol RXR. Revco was sold to CVS Pharmacy for $2.8 billion ($4.94 billion in 2023) in February 1997 . When it was sold, the chain had over 2,500 stores.
