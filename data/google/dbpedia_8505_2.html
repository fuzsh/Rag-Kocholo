What is the plot of Andha Yug?
Andha Yug focuses on the last day of the Mahabharata war . The ramparts are in ruins, the city is burning and Kurukshetra is covered with corpses and vultures. The few bewildered survivors of the Kaurava clan are overcome by grief and rage.
