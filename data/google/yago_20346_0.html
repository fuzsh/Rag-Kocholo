A Short Film About John Bolton is a 2003 film written and directed by Neil Gaiman . The film takes the form of a fictional television piece on real-life artist John Bolton (but played in the film by actor John O'Mahony). It was released direct to video, along with several bonus features. July 2003 (U.S.)
