Who founded Sunnyvale?
Martin Murphy, Jr. is credited with founding Sunnyvale, bringing the railroad through his property and helping to establish the Convent of Notre Dame and Santa Clara College. These were the first institutions of higher learning in the area.

How expensive is it to live in Sunnyvale?
Sunnyvale's housing expenses are 377% higher than the national average and the utility prices are 9% higher than the national average. Transportation expenses like bus fares and gas prices are 24% higher than the national average. Sunnyvale has grocery prices that are 43% higher than the national average.
