The Polish National Government of 1863–64 was an underground Polish supreme authority during the January Uprising, a large scale insurrection during the Russian partition of the former territories of the Polish–Lithuanian Commonwealth. It had a collegial form, resided in Warsaw and was headed by Karol Majewski.

What was the January uprising in Warsaw?
The January Uprising 1863 – the Polish battle for national liberation, which lasted from January 1863 to autumn of 1864 on Polish lands that has been a part of Russian partition . The direct cause of the uprising was an extensive plan of forced recruitment of Polish youth into the Imperial Russian Army.
