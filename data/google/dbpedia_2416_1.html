What was the German Condor division?
The Condor Legion (German: Legion Condor) was a unit of military personnel from the air force and army of Nazi Germany's Wehrmacht which served with the Nationalist faction during the Spanish Civil War . The legion developed methods of strategic bombing that were used widely during the Second World War.
