What does buckthorn do to other plants?
Like many non-native shrubs, common buckthorn leafs out early in spring and retains its leaves late into fall, shading out spring wildflowers and tree seedlings. There is some evidence that common buckthorn is allelo- pathic, producing chemicals that inhibit the growth of other species .
