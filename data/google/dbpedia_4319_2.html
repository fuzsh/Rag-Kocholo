What is the history of Vaughn, New Mexico?
Vaughn was established in the early 20th century as a Southern Pacific Railroad town . The town's importance grew when a second railroad, the Eastern Railway of New Mexico (an Atchison, Topeka and Santa Fe line from Belen to Clovis), was completed in 1907 with Vaughn as a division point.
