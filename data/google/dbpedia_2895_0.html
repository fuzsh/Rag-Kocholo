115th St. - Morgan Park

4.0
(1) Train station in Chicago, Illinois
Directions
Website
115th Street–Morgan Park is one of two Metra railroad stations in the Morgan Park neighborhood of Chicago, Illinois, along the Beverly Branch of the Rock Island District Line. It is 14.3 miles from LaSalle Street Station, the northern terminus of the line. In Metra's zone-based fare system, 115th Street is in zone 2.
Address : 1982 W 115th St, Chicago, IL 60643
