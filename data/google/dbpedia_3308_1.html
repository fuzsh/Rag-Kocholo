Emile Ardolino

American television director and film producer
Emile Ardolino was an American television and film director and producer, best known for his work on the films Dirty Dancing and Sister Act. He won an Academy Award for Best Documentary Feature for his film He Makes Me Feel Like Dancin'. Wikipedia
Born : May 9, 1943, Maspeth, New York, NY
Died : November 20, 1993 (age 50 years), Los Angeles, CA
Awards : Academy Award for Best Documentary (Feature) , Independent Spirit Award for Best First Feature , Primetime Creative Arts Emmy Award for Outstanding Children's Program , and more
Parents : Ester Ardolino and Emilio Ardolino
