What country is Chaco in?
Two-thirds of the Gran Chaco, or 130 million acres, are in Argentina . Its massive forests are home to unique vegetation and wildlife, including 3,400 plant and 500 bird species and hundreds of mammals, reptiles, and amphibians. Nine million people live in the Gran Chaco, including several Indigenous communities.
