What is Faiz Ahmad Faiz famous for?
Faiz began his poetry with ghazals and steadily shifted his attention to nazm and is acknowledged a renowned poet of both forms among progressive poets . He is equally admired in India, Pakistan as in the western countries. His ghazals have been translated into several languages.

Was Faiz Ahmed Faiz Punjabi?
Faiz Ahmad Faiz was born to a landed, educated family in Sialkot of pre-partition Punjab on February 13, 1911. He grew up surrounded by literature with a father who was a friend to many writers, including Muhammad Iqbal. His schooling took him to Lahore where he studied Arabic and English literature.
