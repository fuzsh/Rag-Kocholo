What are some interesting facts about Johann Christian Fabricius?
Fabricius, who is still a recognized entomologist throughout the world, described about 10,000 new insect species in his publications , thus becoming one of the most important systematic classifiers in the field. He saw the classification essentially as an aid to a better understanding of the workings of nature.
