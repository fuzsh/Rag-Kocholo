Did Buffalo Bill have a ranch in Nebraska?
The Second Empire-style house was the largest house in North Platte in the late 19th Century. It was Buffalo Bill's home from 1886 to 1913. He raised cattle and purebred stallions on his 4,000 acres of land. A large barn was built in 1887 to house Cody's ranch horses and stallions.
