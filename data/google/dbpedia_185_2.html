What is the title Pasha in the Ottoman Empire?
It was the highest official title of honour in the Ottoman Empire , always used with a proper name, which it followed. It was given to soldiers and high civil officials, not to men of religion, and was purely personal and not hereditary, except in 19th-century Egypt.
