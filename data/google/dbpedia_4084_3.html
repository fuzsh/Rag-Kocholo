What is in a museum exhibit?
Exhibits feature objects, paintings, pictures, or other parts of a museum collection along with labels telling the viewer about what they are looking at and how it connects to the other objects in the exhibit . The label usually has the name of the object, when it was made (if this is known), and a brief description.
