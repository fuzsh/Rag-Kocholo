What did William S. Burroughs experiment?
Burroughs used a literary technique in the 1950s-60s dubbed the ' cut-up technique '. Originally used by the Dadaists of the 1920s, it is a form where a written text is cut up to create an entirely new and unique text. He used this technique in his experimental works, The Nova Trilogy, published in the 60s.
