Where does the surname Marland originate from?
English (Lancashire): habitational name from Marland in Castleton (Lancashire), named from Old English mere 'pool' or (ge)mǣre 'boundary, border' + land 'land' . Dutch: habitational name from Maarland on Oost-Voorne (South Holland), now obsolete, or from Maarland in Eijsden (Dutch Limburg).
