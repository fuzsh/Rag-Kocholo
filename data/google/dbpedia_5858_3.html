Eugenio María de Hostos

Puerto Rican educator
Eugenio María de Hostos y de Bonilla, known as El Gran Ciudadano de las Américas, was a Puerto Rican educator, philosopher, intellectual, lawyer, sociologist, novelist, and Puerto Rican independence advocate. Wikipedia
Born : January 11, 1839, Mayagüez, Puerto Rico
Died : August 11, 1903 (age 64 years), Santo Domingo, Dominican Republic
Education : Universidad Complutense de Madrid
Parents : Eugenio María de Hostos y Rodríguez and María Hilaria de Bonilla y Cintrón
Children : Adolfo de Hostos

What did Eugenio Maria de Hostos do?
