What is low FM?
The FCC has a special class of radio licenses called Low Power FM Radio to create opportunities for more voices to be heard on the radio . The LPFM stations are licensed to operate with 100-watts, which allows them to reach an area with a radius of approximately three and one-half miles.

What is the range of low power FM radio stations?
A: LPFM stations are limited to 100 watts and a service range of 5.6 kilometers (3.5 mile radius) . This does not mean that the station cannot be heard beyond a 3.5 miles radius. It's an approximation of the expected average coverage.
