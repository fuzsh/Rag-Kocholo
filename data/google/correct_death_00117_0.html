What happened to the Polk Place Mansion after Sarah died?
After completing his term as president, James and Sarah moved into the home in spring 1849. Polk died of cholera in the home in mid-June of the same year. His widow Sarah continued to live in Polk Place until her death in 1891, and the home was razed in 1901 .
