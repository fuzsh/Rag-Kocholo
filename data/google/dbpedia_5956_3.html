What is Liberty Hall known for?
The two-storey building was the first meeting hall in Jamaica that was fully owned and operated by blacks. It was the home to plays, concerts, dances, elocution pieces, adult and children's choirs . One famous play, which took place here, was the "Slave Ship", which recaptured the horrors of the Middle Passage.
