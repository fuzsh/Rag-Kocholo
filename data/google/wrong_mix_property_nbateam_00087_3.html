Where did Tyson Chandler grow up?
He grew up on a family farm in Hanford, California, just south of Fresno, California . Chandler began playing basketball at the age of three years on a basket Chandler's grandfather, Cleotis, fixed on a tree. Chandler grew up doing farm work such as milking cows, slopping pigs, and cultivating crops.
