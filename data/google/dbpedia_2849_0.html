Marina Bay MRT station is an underground Mass Rapid Transit interchange station on the North South, Circle and Thomson–East Coast lines in Singapore. Located in the Downtown Core district near Marina Bay, the station serves the Marina One Residences, Marina Bay Suites and the Marina Bay Financial Centre.
