Who is William Condon?
William "Bill" Condon (born October 22, 1955) is an American screenwriter and director . Condon is best known for directing and writing the critically acclaimed films Gods and Monsters, Chicago, Kinsey, and Dreamgirls. In 1998, Condon debuted as a screenwriter in Gods and Monsters, which won him his first Academy Award.
