French poet and journalist
Anatole France was a French poet, journalist, and novelist with several best-sellers. Ironic and skeptical, he was considered in his day the ideal French man of letters. Wikipedia

Died : October 12, 1924 (age 80 years), Saint-Cyr-sur-Loire, France
Grandchild : Lucien Psichari
Children : Suzanne Thibault
Parents : François Noël Thibault and Antoinette Gallas
