Life. John was born in 1316 at Eltham Palace, Kent, the second son of Edward II of England and Isabella of France. On 6 October 1328, at the age of twelve, he was created Earl of Cornwall . Caught in the throes of the war between his father, Edward II, and his mother, Isabella, his growing years were turbulent.
