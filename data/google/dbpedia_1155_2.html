Déjà Vu, is the second studio album by American folk rock group Crosby, Stills & Nash , and their first as a quartet with Neil Young. Released in March 1970 by Atlantic Records, it topped the pop album chart for one week and generated three Top 40 singles: "Woodstock", "Teach Your Children", and "Our House".
