What did David Fisher invent?
On March 14, 1876, Fisher patented the furniture caster . This device was a free turning wheel that could (when combined with a few others) allow heavy items to move around a room on rollers, safely and efficiently. This enabled one person to move large pieces of furniture, allowing other workers to tend to other items.
