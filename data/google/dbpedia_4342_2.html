What was Antonio de Mendoza known for?
Don Antonio de Mendoza was the first viceroy (governor) of Nueva España . He first came to the Americas from Spain on the orders of King Charles V in 1535. Among his entourage was a young, ambitious Francisco Vázquez de Coronado. Mendoza played a pivotal role in the Coronado Entrada.
