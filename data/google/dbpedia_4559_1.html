What is the main problem of the lunar theory?
An analytic solution to the motion of the Moon is usually found by solving a three body problem under the simplifying assumption that the center of mass of the Earth-Moon system rotates on an elliptic orbit around the Sun . This is known as the main problem of lunar theory.
