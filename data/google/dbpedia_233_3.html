Mervyn LeRoy

American film director
Mervyn LeRoy was an American film director, producer and actor. In his youth he played juvenile roles in vaudeville and silent film comedies. During the 1930s, LeRoy was one of the two great practitioners of economical and effective film directing... Wikipedia
Born : October 15, 1900, San Francisco, CA
Died : September 13, 1987 (age 86years), Beverly Hills, CA
Grandchildren : Bridget Warner , Jennifer Warner , Carolyn Warner , and more
Awards : Academy Honorary Award , Golden Globe Cecil B. DeMille Award , Irving G. Thalberg Memorial Award , and more
Nominations : Academy Award for Best Directing , Golden Globe Award for Best Director - Motion Picture , Golden Globe Award for Best Motion Picture Drama , and more
Books : Mervyn LeRoy: Take One
