Who is chairman Bruce Sterling?
Sterling is one of the founders of the cyberpunk movement in science fiction , along with William Gibson, Rudy Rucker, John Shirley, Lewis Shiner, and Pat Cadigan. In addition, he is one of the subgenre's chief ideological promulgators. This has earned him the nickname "Chairman Bruce".
