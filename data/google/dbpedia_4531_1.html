What is the stay-behind strategy?
A stay-behind operation is one where a country emplaces secret operatives or organizations in its own territory, for use in case of a later enemy occupation . The stay-behind operatives would then form the basis of a resistance movement, and act as spies from behind enemy lines.

What is a stay-behind force?
The purpose of a stay-behind force is plainly stated in the name: pre-designated operatives who plan to hide, survive, and eventually operate in the rear area of an advancing enemy . Stay-behind forces can slow advances, buy time, impose costs, create confusion, and psychologically demoralize an invading enemy.
