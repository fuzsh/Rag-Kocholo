Nelson Rockefeller

Former Vice President of the United States
Nelson Aldrich Rockefeller, sometimes referred to by his nickname Rocky, was an American businessman and politician who served as the 41st vice president of the United States from 1974 to 1977 under President Gerald Ford. Wikipedia
Born : July 8, 1908, Bar Harbor, ME
Died : January 26, 1979 (age 70 years), New York, NY
Children : Michael Rockefeller , Mark Rockefeller , Rodman Rockefeller , and more
Previous offices : Vice President of the United States (1974–1977), Governor of New York (1959–1973), United States Deputy Secretary of Health and Human Services (1953–1954), and more
Party : Republican Party
Spouse : Happy Rockefeller (m. 1963–1979) and Mary Rockefeller (m. 1930–1962)
Parents : John D. Rockefeller Jr. and Abby Aldrich Rockefeller
