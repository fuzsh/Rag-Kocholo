Walter Shenson

American film producer and director
Walter Shenson was an American film producer, director and writer, best known for producing the Beatles' films A Hard Day's Night and Help!, as well as the 1959 comedy The Mouse That Roared, starring Peter Sellers. Wikipedia
Born : June 22, 1919, San Francisco, CA
Died : October 17, 2000 (age 81 years), Woodland Hills, Los Angeles, CA
Education : Stanford University
Spouse : Geraldine Shenson
