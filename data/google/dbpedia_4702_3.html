Who is the owner of K1 Speed?
K1 Speed is the premier indoor electric kart racing brand. The family-owned business was founded by David and Susan Danglard in 2003 with the goal of bringing European-style electric kart racing to North America. The platform's 92 kart racing centers span 30 states and 8 countries as of May 2024.
