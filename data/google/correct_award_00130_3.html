Czesław Miłosz

Polish-American poet and prose writer
Czesław Miłosz was a Polish-American poet, prose writer, translator, and diplomat. He primarily wrote his poetry in Polish. Regarded as one of the great poets of the 20th century, he won the 1980 Nobel Prize in Literature. Wikipedia
Born : June 30, 1911, Šeteniai, Lithuania
Died : August 14, 2004 (age 93 years), Kraków, Poland
Spouse : Carol Thigpen (m. 1992–2002) and Janina Miłosz (m. 1956–1986)
Grandchild : Erin Gilbert
Awards : Nobel Prize in Literature , Neustadt International Prize for Literature , Nike Award: Jury Award , and more
