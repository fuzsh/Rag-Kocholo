What is general information about Tuvalu?
Tuvalu is an island country in the west-central Pacific Ocean . It sits about halfway between Australia and Hawaii. Its neighbours include Kiribati, to the north, and Fiji, to the south. It is made up of a chain of 9 small coral islands.

What are the cultural practices of Tuvalu?
Tuvalu has a traditional community system . In this system, each family has its own task, or salanga, to perform for the benefit of everyone, such as fishing, house building or defence. Traditional ceremonies are important and include several dances such as fakaseasea, fakanau and fatele.
