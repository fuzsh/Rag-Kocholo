South Sudan (/suːˈdɑːn, -ˈdæn/ (listen)), officially known as the Republic of South Sudan , is a landlocked country in East-Central Africa. It gained independence from the Republic of the Sudan in 2011, making it the most recent sovereign state with widespread recognition. Its capital and largest city is Juba.
