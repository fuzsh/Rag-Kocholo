Nuclear Rabbit is a California band, started in Novato . The band's music has been called metal, more specifically "avant-garde" metal, but incorporates a variety of other styles, making the classification even more difficult. Known for its unusual sound and the lyrics, as well as bassist Jean Baudin's 11-string bass.
