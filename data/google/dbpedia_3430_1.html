What are the parts of a Chinese pavilion?

What is the history of the Chinese pavilion?
The first pavilion, a prefabricated building, was erected here in 1753 as a birthday present to Queen Lovisa Ulrika . It was built in a Chinese-inspired style which at that time was the height of fashion in Europe. The present building replaced it ten years later.
