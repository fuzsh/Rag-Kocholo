What are the R trains in Barcelona?
Rodalies trains are crowded in the mornings. They start in Barcelona and they extend radially, sometimes even hundreds of kilometers, with stops every 3-5 kilometers. Rodalies is not only used by commuters, but also by locals who want to travel inside Barcelona, if their itinerary coincides with one of their lines.
