What are township and range roads in Saskatchewan?
Township roads and range roads have a definite numbering system which is used across rural municipalities . The numbers help to establish the location of the roads as they exist with relation to the Dominion Land Survey system. Range roads travel in a north and south direction on a line of longitude.
