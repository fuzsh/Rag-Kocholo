John II of Aragon

Member of the House of Trastámara
John II, called the Great or the Faithless, was King of Aragon from 1458 until his death in 1479. As the husband of Queen Blanche I of Navarre, he was King of Navarre from 1425 to 1479. John was also King of Sicily from 1458 to 1468. Wikipedia
Born : June 29, 1398, Medina del Campo, Spain
Died : January 20, 1479 (age 80 years), Barcelona, Spain
Children : Ferdinand II of Aragon , Charles, Prince of Viana , Leonor I of Navarre , and more
Parents : Ferdinand I of Aragon and Eleanor of Alburquerque
Great-grandparents : Peter IV of Aragon , Henry II of Castile , Alfonso XI of Castile , and more
Grandchildren : Juana I of Castile , John, Prince of Asturias , Isabella of Aragon, Queen of Portugal , and more
Spouse : Juana Enríquez (m. 1444–1468) and Blanche I of Navarre (m. 1420–1441)
