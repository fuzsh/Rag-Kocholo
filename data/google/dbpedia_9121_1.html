Why was salt so important during the time of the Mali Empire?
Answer and Explanation: Salt was vitally important to many peoples in West Africa. Salt was difficult to find naturally in the region, and highly needed in local diets since people lost so much salt from sweating in the hot climates . Thus, salt from the Sahara Desert and elsewhere was highly valued as a trade good.
