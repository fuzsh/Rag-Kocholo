1868 Thersites

Asteroid
1868 Thersites is a large Jupiter trojan from the Greek camp, approximately 70 kilometers in diameter. Discovered during the Palomar–Leiden survey at Palomar in 1960, it was later named after the warrior Thersites from Greek mythology. Wikipedia
Discoverers : Tom Gehrels , Ingrid van Houten-Groeneveld , and Cornelis Johannes van Houten
Minor planet category : Jupiter trojan; Greek · background
