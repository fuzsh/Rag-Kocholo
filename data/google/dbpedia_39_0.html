Romain Ferrier (born 24 February 1976) is a retired French footballer who played as a defender. His previous clubs include AS Cannes, Bordeaux, Montpellier HSC and Guingamp. He also played in Europe with Bordeaux and Montpellier and had a spell in Greece with Skoda Xanthi .
