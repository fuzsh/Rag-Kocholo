Where did James Newton Howard grow up?
Howard was born in Los Angeles . He is from a musical family; his grandmother was a violinist. His father was Jewish but he did not want his children to know he was, so he changed his last name from Horowitz to Howard. Howard began studying music as a child, taking classical piano lessons at the age of four.
