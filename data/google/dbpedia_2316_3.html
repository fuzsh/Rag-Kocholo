What is the history of McAuley Catholic High School?
As a school for the children of local Roman Catholics, it was originally a girls private school until the move in the 1970s, when the School became coeducational . The School takes its name from Catherine McAuley, founder of the Sisters of Mercy, the order which ran the School until the late 1980s.

Why is Joplin, MO famous?
