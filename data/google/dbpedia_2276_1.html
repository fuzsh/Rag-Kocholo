Paul Mbiybe Verdzekov

Cameroonian prelate
Paul Mbiybe Verdzekov was a Cameroonian prelate of the Roman Catholic Church. He served as bishop of Bamenda, Cameroon from 1970 until 1982 and as archbishop from 1982 until 2006. Wikipedia
Born : January 22, 1931
Died : January 26, 2010 (age 79 years)
