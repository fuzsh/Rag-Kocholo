Who lives at Claydon House?
Claydon House has been the seat of the Verney family since 1620. The present house is almost entirely 18th-century, having undergone major redevelopment in 1757 and 1771 to rival the nearby manor house at Stowe. Today, only a fragment of the great house is still standing.
