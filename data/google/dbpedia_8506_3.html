Dharamvir Bharati

Poet and author
Dharamvir Bharati was a renowned Hindi poet, author, playwright and a social thinker of India. He was the chief editor of the popular Hindi weekly magazine Dharmayug, from 1960 till 1987. Bharati was awarded the Padma Shree for literature in 1972... Wikipedia
Born : December 25, 1926, Prayagraj, India
Died : September 4, 1997 (age 70 years), Mumbai, India
Parents : Chiranji Lal and Chanda devi
Education : M.A. Hindi , PhD
Notable works : Gunahon Ka Devta (1949, novel); Suraj ka Satwan Ghoda (1952, novel); Andha Yug (1953, play)
Spouse : Kanta Bharti (married 1954) (first wife), Pushpa Bharti (second. wife)
