What was the Tuskegee Airmen distinguished unit citation?
The Tuskegee Airmen achieved a number of successes during World War II. The 332nd Fighter Group was awarded the Distinguished Unit Citation for their actions on March 24, 1945 . On their way to Berlin, Germany, the Red Tails destroyed three German ME-262's.

What did the 332nd do to be awarded a distinguished unit citation?
On March 24, 1945, the 332nd became one of the first Italy-based fighter units to escort B-17s all the way to Berlin and back. Along the way, they met 25 German Me 262 jets. In the ensuing combat, the 332nd shot down three of the eight jets destroyed that day and earned the 332nd a Distinguished Unit Citation.
