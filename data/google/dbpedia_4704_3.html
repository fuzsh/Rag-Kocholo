What is K1 Speed known for?
K1 Speed is an American go-kart racetrack franchise based in Irvine, California. The company was co-founded in 2003 by David Danglard and Susan Danglard, who had previously worked in the fashion industry. That same year, K1 Speed opened their first go-kart track outside San Diego, California.
