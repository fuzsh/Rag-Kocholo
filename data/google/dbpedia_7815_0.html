What happened to the Kingdom of Portugal?
The Portuguese monarchy was abolished in 1910 when a republican revolution overthrew King Manuel II , the last reigning monarch of Portugal. Following the revolution, Portugal became a republic, and the royal family was forced into exile. King Manuel II lived in exile in several European countries until he died in 1932.
