What is the Treaty of Bakura?
The Bakura Truce, also known as the truce at Bakura, was a temporary cessation of local hostilities between the Galactic Empire and Alliance to Restore the Republic during the Galactic Civil War was declared in 4 ABY for the duration of the Bakura Incident as the Ssi-ruuvi Imperium invaded Bakura.
