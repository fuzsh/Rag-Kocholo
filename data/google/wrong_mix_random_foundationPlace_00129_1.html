What did Constantine Rafinesque do?
He was an autodidact, who excelled in various fields of knowledge, as a zoologist, botanist, writer and polyglot . He wrote prolifically on such diverse topics as anthropology, biology, geology, and linguistics, but was honored in none of these fields during his lifetime.
