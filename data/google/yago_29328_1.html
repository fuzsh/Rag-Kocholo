Did you mean: What was Viacheslava of Novgorod relationship to Bolesław IV the Curly?
Around 1137 she was married to Bolesław , son of the Polish Duke Bolesław III Wrymouth. The wedding was probably orchestrated by Bolesław's mother Salomea of Berg, who wanted to secure a Russian alliance against her stepson, the later Władysław II the Exile.
