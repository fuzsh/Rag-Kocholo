Dolores Guinness

German socialite
Dolores Guinness was a German-born "Freiin", socialite, fashion icon and jet set member of the 1950s and 1960s. She has been a member of the International Best Dressed List since 1970. Her mother was the Mexican-born socialite Gloria Guinness. Wikipedia
