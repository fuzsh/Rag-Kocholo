What are some interesting facts about Lucian Freud?
Freud got his first name "Lucian" from his mother in memory of the ancient writer Lucian of Samosata . His family moved to England in 1933, when he was 10 years old, to escape the rise of Nazism. He became a British naturalized citizen in 1939. From 1942 to 1943 he attended Goldsmiths' College, London.
