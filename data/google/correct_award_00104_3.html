Who is Juan Ramón Jiméz?
A prolific Spanish poet, editor, and critic , Juan Ramón Jiménez won the Nobel Prize for literature in 1956. He was born in Moguer i Andalusia, an area that he depicted in Platero y Yo (Platero and I, 1914) a collection of prose poems about a man and his donkey.

What are some interesting facts about Juan Ramon Jimenez?
Juan Ramón Jimenez was educated in a Jesuit boarding school and then studied law in Seville, but he did not finish his studies . Jimenez was a poet, literary critic and editor of a literary magazine. In 1917 he married Zenobia Camprubí, a poet and translator.
