Dombivli

4.0
(20,965) Train station in Dombivli, India
Directions
Dombivli Railway Station, which serves the City of Dombivli, is the all time busiest railway station on the Central line of the Mumbai Suburban Railway network. It consists of 5 platforms where platform 1, 2 and 3 serve for slow locals and 4 and 5 for fast local trains. It has 7 tracks.
