Andrei Kirilenko (born February 18, 1981 in Izhevsk, Russian SFSR, Soviet Union) is a Small forward that formerly played for the Minnesota Timberwolves of the National Basketball Association (NBA). He played in the NBA with the Utah Jazz for 10 seasons and then played in Russia with his original team, CSKA Moscow.
