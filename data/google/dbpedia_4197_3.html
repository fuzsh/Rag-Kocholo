Who owns Nettwerk Music Group?
Nettwerk Music Group is a Canadian music company founded by Terry McBride, Mark Jowett, Ric Arboit and Dan Fraser in 1984. Led by McBride as Chairman & CEO, the company today houses a record label operation, in addition to a music publisher and an artist management operation.
