What is the Nottoway tribe in Virginia?
The Nottoway Indian Tribe of Virginia descends from a significantly larger Nottoway community and culture . Nottoway Indians traditionally lived in dispersed units within communities or towns each with separate leaders. Though similar in name and language, each had a unique internal structure.

What is the history of Nottoway?
Nottoway County was first inhabited by native American Indians of the Iroquoian nation tribe called Nadowa . The Nadowa lived along the County's only river and the name of their tribe became associated with the area they inhabited. This name was Anglicized with the coming of English settlers to 'Nottoway'.
