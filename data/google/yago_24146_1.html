Cleve Moler

American mathematician and computer programmer
View all
Cleve Barry Moler is an American mathematician and computer programmer specializing in numerical analysis. In the mid to late 1970s, he was one of the authors of LINPACK and EISPACK, Fortran libraries for numerical computing. Wikipedia
Place of birth : Salt Lake City, UT
Awards : IEEE Computer Society Awards - Computer Pioneer Award (2012) and IEEE John von Neumann Medal (2014)
Affiliation : MathWorks Inc.
Research interests : Computational Science, Numerical Linear Algebra, and Mathematical Software
Doctoral students : Jack Dongarra; Charles F. Van Loan
Education : California Institute of Technology ; Stanford University
Thesis : Finite difference methods for the eigenvalues of Laplace's operator (1965)
