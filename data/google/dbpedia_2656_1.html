What is the name of Ed Banger Records?
Ed Banger Records (sometimes referred to as Ed Rec ) is a French electronic music record label founded by Pedro Winter in 2003 as a division of Headbangers Entertainment. It focuses on house music, particularly French house, as well as alternative dance, electro, hip hop, nu disco, and synthpop, among other styles.
