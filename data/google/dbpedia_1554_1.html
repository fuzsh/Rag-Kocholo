What was Cozy Powell's biggest hit?
" Dance With The Devil " was his only solo hit in the United States, peaking at No. 49. The track featured Suzi Quatro on bass. Powell's second hit during 1974 was with "The Man in Black", which reached No. 18.

Who did Cozy Powell drum for?
Cozy Powell was one of rock's greatest and most travelled drummers, playing with the likes of Rainbow, Black Sabbath, Peter Green, Brian May, the reunited ELP and countless others . He sadly died in a car accident on April 5, 1999, at the age of 50.
