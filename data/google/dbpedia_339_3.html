Edward LeSaint

American stage actor and film director
Edward LeSaint was an American stage and film actor and director whose career began in the silent era. He acted in over 300 films and directed more than 90. He was sometimes credited as Edward J. Le Saint. Wikipedia
Born : December 13, 1870, Cincinnati, OH
Died : September 10, 1940 (age 69years), Los Angeles, CA
Spouse : Stella LeSaint (m. 19131940)
