Rudy Toth (16 December 1925 – 9 July 2009) was a Canadian composer, arranger, conductor, pianist, and cimbalom player of Czech birth . As a composer he wrote works mainly for television and the radio, working frequently for the Canadian Broadcasting Company for over three decades.
