Is Dolores a real name?
Dolores, also spelled Delores, is a female given name . It is pronounced [ duh-lawr-is, -lohr- ]. The Spanish name Dolores has its roots in the Latin word "dolor," which translates to "pain" or "sorrow." This name is quite common in Spanish-speaking nations and is frequently used as a way to refer to the Virgin Mary.
