Hal Roach

American film producer and director
Harold Eugene "Hal" Roach Sr. was an American film and television producer, director and screenwriter, who was the founder of the namesake Hal Roach Studios. Roach was active in the industry from the 1910s to the 1990s. Wikipedia
Born : January 14, 1892, Elmira, NY
Died : November 2, 1992 (age 100years), Bel Air, Los Angeles, CA
Organization founded : Hal Roach Studios
Production company : Hal Roach Studios
Grandchildren : Addison Randall and Matthew Scott Carpenter
Awards : Academy Honorary Award , Academy Award for Best Short Subject, Comedy , PGA Lifetime Achievement Award in Motion Pictures , and more
Nominations : Academy Award for Best Short Subject, Comedy , Academy Award for Best Short Subject, One-reel , and Retro Hugo for Best Dramatic Presentation, Long Form
