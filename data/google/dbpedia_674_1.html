Duke John Albert of Mecklenburg

Member of the House of Mecklenburg
Duke John Albert of Mecklenburg was a member of the House of Mecklenburg-Schwerin who served as the regent of two states of the German Empire. Wikipedia
Born : December 8, 1857, Schwerin, Germany
Died : February 16, 1920 (age 62 years), Lübstorf, Germany
Spouse : Princess Elisabeth of Stolberg-Rossla (m. 1909) and Princess Elisabeth Sybille of Saxe-Weimar-Eisenach (m. 1886–1908)
Great-grandparents : Frederick Louis, Hereditary Grand Duke of Mecklenburg-Schwerin , Louise of Mecklenburg-Strelitz , Frederick William III of Prussia , and more
Grandparents : Paul Frederick, Grand Duke of Mecklenburg-Schwerin , Alexandrine of Prussia, Grand Duchess of Mecklenburg-Schwerin , and Heinrich LXIII, Prince Reuss of Köstritz
Parents : Frederick Francis II and Princess Augusta Reuss of Köstritz
Awards : Order of the Black Eagle
