Ali Sokol (8 May 1921 - 23 September 1974) was a Kosovar Albanian pulmonologist . He completed his education in the Medrese of Skoplje from 1933 until 1941. He then enrolled in the Faculty of Agriculture at the University of Pisa in Italy.
