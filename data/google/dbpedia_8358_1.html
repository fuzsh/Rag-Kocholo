Zolty Cracker is a Canadian band based out of Vancouver, British Columbia, Canada . The band was named for lead vocalist and guitarist Gilles Zolty; the other members were Annie Wilkinson (bass, accordion, vocals, cello) and Ronald Wayne Adams (drums, percussion, harmonica, vocals).
