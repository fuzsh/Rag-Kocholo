Chulalongkorn

Former King of Thailand
Chulalongkorn, reigning title Phra Chula Chom Klao Chao Yu Hua, was the fifth king of Siam from the Chakri dynasty, titled Rama V. He reigned from 1868 until his death in 1910. Chulalongkorn was born as the son of King Mongkut in 1853. Wikipedia
Born : September 20, 1853, Bangkok, Thailand
Died : October 23, 1910 (age 57 years), Amphorn Sathan Residential Hall, Bangkok, Thailand
Children : Vajiravudh , Prajadhipok , Raphee Phatthanasak , and more
Siblings : Prince Sukhasawasdee , Thavi Thavalyalabh , Saovabha Phongsri , and more
Parents : Mongkut and Debsirindra
Grandchildren : Bhumibol Adulyadej , Ananda Mahidol , Galyani Vadhana , and more
Full name : Phra Bat Somdet Phra Poraminthra Maha Chulalongkorn Phra Chunla Chom Klao Chao Yu Hua
