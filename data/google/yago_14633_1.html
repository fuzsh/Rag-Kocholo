French film director and screenwriter
Maurice Cloche was a French film director, screenwriter, photographer and film producer. Best known for his Oscar-winning film Monsieur Vincent he won a 1948 Special Academy Award for Best Foreign Language Film. Wikipedia
Born : June 17, 1907, Commercy, France
Died : March 20, 1990 (age 82 years), Bordeaux, France
