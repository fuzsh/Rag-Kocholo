Born : February 3, 1874, Allegheny
Died : July 27, 1946 (age 72 years), American Hospital of Paris, Neuilly-sur-Seine, France
Parents : Daniel Stein and Amelia Stein
Siblings : Leo Stein
Education : Harvard University (BA); Johns Hopkins University
Literary movement : Modernist literature
Resting place : Père Lachaise Cemetery
