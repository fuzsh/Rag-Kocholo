Princess Margaret of Denmark

Member of the House of Bourbon-Parma

Children : Prince André of Bourbon-Parma , Anne of Romania , Prince Jacques of Bourbon-Parma , and more
Spouse : Prince René of Bourbon-Parma (m. 1921–1962)
