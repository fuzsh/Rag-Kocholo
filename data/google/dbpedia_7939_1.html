Is Eastern Daylight Time in the summer or winter?
ET is five hours behind UTC in winter, which is called Eastern Standard Time (EST). It is four hours behind UTC during summer Daylight saving time , when it is called Eastern Daylight Time (EDT). The largest city in the Eastern Time Zone is New York, the largest city in the United States.

What dates are Eastern Daylight Time?
On the second Sunday in March, at 2:00 a.m. EST , clocks are advanced to 3:00 a.m. EDT leaving a one-hour gap. On the first Sunday in November, at 2:00 a.m. EDT, clocks are moved back to 1:00 a.m. EST, which results in one hour being duplicated.
