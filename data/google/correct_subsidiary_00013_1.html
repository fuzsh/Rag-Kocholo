New York, New Haven and Hartford Railroad Company, American railroad operating in southern New England and New York. It was absorbed by the Penn Central Transportation Company in 1969 . It was built up from about 125 small railroads, the earliest of which began operation in 1834 as the Hartford and New Haven Railroad.
