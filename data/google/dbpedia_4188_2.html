Mississippi John Hurt

American singer-songwriter and guitarist
John Smith Hurt, known as Mississippi John Hurt, was an American country blues singer, songwriter, and guitarist. Hurt was raised in Avalon, Mississippi and taught himself to play the guitar around the age of nine. Wikipedia
