What is songs of praise usually of God called?
Hymns are religious songs of praise or celebration to God or to a god. Hymns are collected in books called hymn books, hymnals, or hymnaries. The practice of singing and composing hymns is known as hymnody. Someone who writes hymns is called a hymnodist.

What is it called when a song has multiple songs in it?
A medley consists of several different songs that have been rearranged into one continuous work using the original sheet music or score, and involves a completely new performance to record it.
