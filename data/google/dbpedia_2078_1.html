Louis Gerhard De Geer

Former Prime Minister of Sweden
Baron Louis Gerard De Geer af Finspång was a Swedish statesman, lawyer and writer. He was the first Prime Minister of Sweden. Wikipedia
Born : July 18, 1818, Finspång, Sweden
Died : September 24, 1896 (age 78 years), Hanaskogs slott, Hanaskog, Sweden
Previous offices : Prime Minister of Sweden (1876–1880), Minister for Justice of Sweden (1876–1879), Prime Minister for Justice of Sweden (1875–1876), and more
Great grandchildren : Jan De Geer , Carl De Geer , and Louis De Geer
Children : Gerhard Louis De Geer and Gerard De Geer
Parents : Gerhard de Geer and Freiin Henriette Charlotte Lagerstråle
Grandchildren : Sten De Geer and Arvid De Geer
