Is WWV AM or SSB?
The signals broadcast by WWV use double sideband amplitude modulation . The modulation level is 50 percent for the steady tones, 50 percent for the BCD time code, 100 percent for the second pulses and the minute and hour markers, and 75 percent for the voice announcements.

What format does WWV broadcast in?
WWV and WWVH continuously broadcast a binary coded decimal (BCD) time code on a 100-Hz subcarrier . The time code presents UTC information in serial fashion at a rate of 1 pulse per second. The information carried by the time code includes the current minute, hour, and day of year.
