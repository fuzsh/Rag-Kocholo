What did Cole Porter accomplish?
American composer Cole Albert Porter (1891-1964) wrote songs (both words and music) for over 30 stage and film musicals . His best work set standards of sophistication and wit seldom matched in the popular musical theater. Cole Porter was born in Peru, Indiana, on June 9, 1891, the son of a pharmacist.
