"Cry to Me" is a song written by Bert Berns (listed as "Bert Russell") and first recorded by American soul singer Solomon Burke in 1961. Released in 1962, it was Burke's second single to appear in both Billboard magazine's Hot R&B Sides and Hot 100 singles charts.

What happened to Bert Berns?
Bert Berns can't be easily explained in a few words. He was a consummate record man who died at age 38 in 1967 , after seven years in the record business. He left behind a wife, three small children, and many bereaved friends and associates who loved and looked up to him. He also left behind a number of angry enemies.
