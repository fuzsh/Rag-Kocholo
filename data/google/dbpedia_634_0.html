Bryan Forbes

English film director and screenwriter

Born : July 22, 1926, Stratford, London, United Kingdom
Died : May 8, 2013 (age 86 years), Virginia Water, United Kingdom
Organization founded : Lumiere Pictures and Television
Production company : Lumiere Pictures and Television
Grandchildren : Lily Clempson , India Leon , Octavia Leon , and more
Awards : Edgar Award for Best Foreign Film and BAFTA Award for Best British Screenplay
Nominations : BAFTA Award for Outstanding British Film , Academy Award for Writing (Original Screenplay) , BAFTA Award for Best Film , and more
