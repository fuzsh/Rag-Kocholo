Is Funkdoobiest Mexican?
Funkdoobiest is an Latino rap group made in Los Angeles, California. The original members are: Son Doobie (Jason Vasquez), a Puerto Rican rapper, DJ Ralph M (Ralph Medrano), a Mexican DJ, and Tomahawk Funk (Tyrone Pacheco) of the Lakota nation (Native American). The latter left after their second album.
