The Guyanese dollar is the official currency of Guyana. The currency is abbreviated as GYD and is represented by the symbols $ and G$. Banknotes are printed in G$20, G$50, G$100, G$500, G$1,000, and $5,000 denominations while coins are minted in G$1, G$5, G$10, and $100 denominations.
