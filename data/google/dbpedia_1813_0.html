As of the 2011 Bangladesh census, Boalkhali upazila had 44,108 households and a population of 223,125 . 46,730 (20.94%) were under 10 years of age. Boalkhali had an average literacy rate of 58.90%, compared to the national average of 51.8%, and a sex ratio of 1042 females per 1000 males.
