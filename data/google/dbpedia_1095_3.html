Dennis W. Sciama

Physicist
Dennis William Siahou Sciama, FRS was an English physicist who, through his own work and that of his students, played a major role in developing British physics after the Second World War. Wikipedia
Born : November 18, 1926, Manchester, United Kingdom
Died : December 18, 1999 (age 73 years), Oxford, United Kingdom
Education : University of Cambridge and Trinity College
Spouse : Lidia Dina (m. 1959–1999)
