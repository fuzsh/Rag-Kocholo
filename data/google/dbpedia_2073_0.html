1051 - 1107 Mi Fu was a Chinese painter, poet and calligrapher of Sogdian origin, who was born in Taiyuan during the Song dynasty. He became known for his style of painting misty landscapes . This style would be deemed the "Mi Fu" style and involved the use of large wet dots of ink applied with a flat brush.
