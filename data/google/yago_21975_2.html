What is the meaning of love's labour's lost?
All of Shakespeare's plays can be said to be in love with language, but Love's Labor's Lost is literally about language: about words and their definitions, and about how characters from every level of society play with words and tease and tempt each other with flights of rhetoric and literary wit.
