What are strikeouts labeled as?
In the scorebook, a strikeout is denoted by the letter K . A third-strike call on which the batter doesn't swing is denoted with a backward K.

What are strikeouts in stats?
A strikeout is a statistic recorded for both pitchers and batters , and is usually denoted by the letter K, or sometimes by the initialism SO. A "strikeout looking"—in which the batter does not swing and the third strike is called by the umpire—may be denoted by an inverted K (i.e. ꓘ).
