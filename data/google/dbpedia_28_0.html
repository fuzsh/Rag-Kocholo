What happened to the Choctaws?
The first American Indian tribe to be removed to Oklahoma, the Choctaw suffered greatly. Roughly 70,000 people were forced out of the region and at least 3,000 lost their lives on the march . This forced walk is now commemorated as the Trail of Tears.
