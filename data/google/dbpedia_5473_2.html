Which city has the first radio station in West Virginia?
The oldest extant West Virginia radio station is WSAZ (now WRVC) in Huntington , originally licensed in October 1924. Other long-time stations include WWVA of Wheeling, which began broadcasting in December 1926, and WCHS of Charleston, which went on the air as WOBU in September 1927.
