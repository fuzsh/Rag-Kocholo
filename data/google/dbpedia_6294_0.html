Jean Van Den Bosch (5 August 1898 in Brussels – 1 July 1985 in Sint-Jans-Molenbeek) was a cyclist from Belgium. He won the silver medal in the Team Road race and a bronze medal in the Team Pursuit for Men at the 1924 Summer Olympics in Paris.
