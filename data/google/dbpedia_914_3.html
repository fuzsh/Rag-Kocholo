What is the history of Nhill?
The town was first surveyed in 1879, when Noske's Flour Mill was built and by 1880 a general store, drapery, and hotel were built . In 1897 a major tornado destroyed most of Nhill but the historic Post Office, built in 1887, is among many buildings that survived and still features in the town.
