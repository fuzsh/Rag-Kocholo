How many times has The Secret Garden been remade?
The Secret Garden has been adapted into multiple forms, especially since the book entered public domain, but there are four distinct film versions of the novel. The first was made in 1919, during Frances Hodgson Burnett's lifetime. The second was made in 1949 by MGM. The third was made in 1993 by American Zoetrope.
