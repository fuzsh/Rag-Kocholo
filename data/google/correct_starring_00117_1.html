Kim Stanley

American actress
Kim Stanley was an American actress who was primarily active in television and theatre but also had occasional film performances. She began her acting career in theatre and subsequently attended the Actors Studio in New York. Wikipedia
Born : February 11, 1925, Tularosa, NM
Died : August 20, 2001 (age 76 years), Santa Fe, NM
Spouse : Joseph Siegel (m. 1964–1967), Alfred Ryder (m. 1958–1964), Curt Conway (m. 1949–1956), and more
Nominations : Academy Award for Best Actress in a Leading Role , Academy Award for Best Actress in a Supporting Role , Primetime Emmy Award for Outstanding Supporting Actress in a Limited or Anthology Series or Movie , and more
Parents : J.T. Reid and Ann Miller
Siblings : Kenneth Reid
