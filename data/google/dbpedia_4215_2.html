What is the history of Ragdoll productions?
History. Ragdoll Productions was founded on 26 July 1984 by Anne Wood , who produced shows for the ITV network. It was incorporated as Ragdoll Productions (U.K.) Limited in April 1985 and was originally based in Birmingham. The company's first show, Pob's Programme, premiered on Channel 4 later that year.
