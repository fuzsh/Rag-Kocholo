Where did Rutherford B. Hayes get buried?
Spiegel Grove, Fremont
In Spiegel Grove President and Mrs. Hayes are buried within the grounds of Spiegel Grove . A granite tombstone, made from granite quarried from the Hayes homestead in Dummerstown, Vermont, marks their burial site. They were originally laid to rest in nearby Oakwood Cemetery, a public cemetery in Fremont, Ohio.
