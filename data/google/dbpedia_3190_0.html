Why is Ferrari located in Maranello?
Enzo Ferrari moved his car construction operations to Maranello during 1943. He left his initial premises in Modena for a number of reasons. This was wartime, and the law requiring factories to be placed outside built-up areas encouraged Ferrari to look for an alternative site .
