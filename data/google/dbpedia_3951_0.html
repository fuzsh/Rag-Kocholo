What form is country music written in?
AB format has been the song writing format of choice for modern popular music since the 1960s. It is often used in love songs, pop, country, rap and rock music. Unlike the AABA form or AAA, which both highlight the verse, AB format puts emphasis entirely on the chorus.

What type of melody is country music?
At its core, country music is about simple sounds, usually built around a plain melody and 3 chords. The 3 chords most commonly used in country music are the “I”, “IV” and “V7” chords, for example “G”, “C”, and “D7” in the key of G major.
