Enrique Bunbury

Spanish singer-songwriter
Enrique Ortiz de Landázuri Izarduy, best known as Enrique Bunbury, is a Spanish singer and songwriter. He has been described as "by far the most international star of Spanish rock." He was propelled to fame as lead singer of Héroes del Silencio. Wikipedia

Marriage location : Tepoztlán, Mexico

Awards : MTV Europe Music Award for Best Spanish Act and Latin Grammy Award for Best Rock Album
