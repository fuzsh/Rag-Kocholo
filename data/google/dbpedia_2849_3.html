How to get to Marina Bay via MRT?

Via MRT

1
Get on any MRT on the Circle or Downtown Line.
2
Get off at Bayfront MRT station (CE1/DT16). This is the closest MRT station to Marina Bay Sands.
3
Take either Exit C or D for a short walk to arrive at Tower 3 where the entrance to Marina Bay Sands SkyPark is located.
