What is the meaning of Fribourg?
Fribourg (French: [fʁibuʁ]) is the capital of the Swiss canton of Fribourg and district of La Sarine . Located on both sides of the river Saane/Sarine, on the Swiss Plateau, it is a major economic, administrative and educational centre on the cultural border between German-speaking and French-speaking Switzerland.
