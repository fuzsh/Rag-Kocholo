How many games are in the Eerste Divisie?
The competition consists of 20 clubs. The clubs play each other once at home and once away, which means every team will play 38 matches in a season . The champion and the runner up of the Eerste Divisie will receive an automatic promotion to the Eredivisie.

Is Eerste Divisie professional?
Set up in 1956, the Eerste divisie is the second-highest tier of men's professional football in the Netherlands . Named after its sponsor Keuken Kampioen, the league consists of 20 clubs, each playing the other clubs once at home and once away.
