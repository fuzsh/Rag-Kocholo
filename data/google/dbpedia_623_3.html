Is the Nodosaurus a dinosaur?
Nodosaurus, (genus Nodosaurus), armoured dinosaurs found as fossils in North America dating from 95 million to 90 million years ago during the Late Cretaceous Period. A heavy animal about 5.5 metres (18 feet) long, Nodosaurus had a long tail but a very small head and a minuscule brain.
