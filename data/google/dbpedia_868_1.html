What type of incident is a train crash?
A train accident or train wreck is a type of disaster involving two or more trains . Train wrecks often occur as a result of miscommunication, as when a moving train meets another train on the same track, when the wheels of train come off the track or when a boiler explosion occurs.

What was the first railroad accident in 1832?
The first recorded railroad accident in U.S. history happened on July 25, 1832, near Quincy, Massachusetts . Four people, who had been invited to watch stone loads being transported, were thrown from a car on the Granite Railway when a cable snapped. They fell over a 34-foot cliff.
