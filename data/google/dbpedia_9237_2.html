What is the jurisdiction of the archbishops?
Jurisdiction. The jurisdiction of the archbishop is twofold, episcopal and archiepiscopal . The first extends to his own diocese exclusively and comprises the rights and powers of the fullest government of the diocese, clergy and laity, spiritual and temporal, except as restricted by Church law.
