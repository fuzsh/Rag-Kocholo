Who made the little princess movie?
A Little Princess is a 1995 American fantasy drama film directed by Alfonso Cuarón . Loosely based upon the 1905 novel A Little Princess by Frances Hodgson Burnett, the film stars Eleanor Bron, Liam Cunningham, Liesel Matthews, Vanessa Lee Chester, Rusty Schwimmer, Arthur Malet, and Errol Sitahal.
