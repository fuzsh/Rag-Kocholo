Karl Theodor, Duke in Bavaria

Member of the House of Wittelsbach
Karl-Theodor, Duke in Bavaria, was a member of the House of Wittelsbach and a professional oculist. He was the favorite brother of the Empress Elisabeth of Austria, and father of Queen Elisabeth of the Belgians. Wikipedia
Born : August 9, 1839, Castello di Possenhofen, Pöcking, Germany
Died : November 30, 1909 (age 70 years), Kreuth, Germany
Children : Duchess Marie Gabrielle in Bavaria , Duke Ludwig Wilhelm in Bavaria , Duchess Amalie in Bavaria , and more
Siblings : Duchess Sophie Charlotte in Bavaria , Maximilian, Duke in Bavaria , Wilhelm Karl, Duke in Bavaria , and more
Spouse : Infanta Maria Josepha of Portugal (m. 1874) and Princess Sophie of Saxony (m. 1865)
Parents : Princess Ludovika of Bavaria and Duke Maximilian Joseph in Bavaria
Grandchildren : Marie-José of Belgium , King Leopold III , Albrecht, Duke of Bavaria , and more
