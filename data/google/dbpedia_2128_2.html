Is two tone the same as ska?
Compared to 1960s ska, 2 Tone music had faster tempos, fuller instrumentation, and a harder edge . The genre was named after 2 Tone Records, a record label founded by Jerry Dammers of the Specials. In many cases, the reworking of classic ska songs turned the originals into hits again in the United Kingdom.
