Margaret Dumont

American film actress
Margaret Dumont was an American stage and film actress. She is best remembered as the comic foil to the Marx Brothers in seven of their films; Groucho Marx called her "practically the fifth Marx brother." Wikipedia
Born : October 20, 1882, Brooklyn, New York, NY
Died : March 6, 1965 (age 82 years), Hollywood, Los Angeles, CA
Height : 5′ 9″
Spouse : John Moller, Jr. (m. 1910–1918)
Parents : Harriet Anna Harong and William Baker
