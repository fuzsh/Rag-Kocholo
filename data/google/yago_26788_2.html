Julia Agrippina, also referred to as Agrippina the Younger, was Roman empress from AD 49 to 54, the fourth wife and niece of emperor Claudius, and the mother of Nero. Agrippina was one of the most prominent women in the Julio-Claudian dynasty. Wikipedia
Born : November 6, 15 AD, Colonia Claudia Ara Agrippinensium
Died : March 23, 59 AD (age 43 years), Miseno, Italy
Spouse : Claudius (m. 49 AD–54 AD), Gaius Sallustius Passienus Crispus (m. 41 AD), and Gnaeus Domitius Ahenobarbus (m. 28 AD)
Siblings : Caligula , Julia Drusilla , Nero Caesar , and more
Children : Nero Claudius Caesar Augustus Germanicus
Parents : Germanicus Caesar and Agrippina the Elder
