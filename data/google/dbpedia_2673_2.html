T. E. B. Clarke

Screenwriter
Thomas Ernest Bennett "Tibby" Clarke, OBE was a film screenwriter who wrote several of the Ealing Studios comedies. Wikipedia
Born : June 7, 1907, Watford, United Kingdom
Died : February 11, 1989 (age 81 years), Surrey, United Kingdom
Books : Murder at Buckingham Palace , The man who seduced a bank , The Trail of the Serpent , and more
Siblings : Dudley Clarke
Awards : Academy Award for Best Story and Screenplay and International Award for Best Screenplay
Spouse : Joyce Caroline Steele (m. 1932–1983)
