Why is the Bolzano Weierstrass theorem important?
The Bolzano Weierstrass theorem is a key finding of convergence in a finite-dimensional Euclidean space Rn in mathematics, specifically real analysis . It is named after Bernard Bolzano and Karl Weierstrass. According to the theorem, each bounded sequence in Rn has a convergent subsequence.
