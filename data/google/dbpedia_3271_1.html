How long is the Brazilian Serie A season?
During the course of a season (from May to December ) each club plays the others twice (a double round-robin system), once at their home stadium and once at that of their opponents, for a total of 38 games. Teams receive three points for a win and one point for a draw.

How is Brazilian football structured?
The Brazilian football league system is a series of interconnected leagues for football clubs in Brazil. It consists of several independent pyramids, which are the national pyramid and the states pyramids . As these pyramids are independent, clubs usually compete in a state pyramid and a national pyramid.

What is the Brazilian Football league Série C?
The Campeonato Brasileiro Série C is the third tier of the Brazilian football league system . Unlike the first and second divisions, the Série C is not played in a double round robin system, arguably because many participating teams lack the financial conditions to travel long distances.
