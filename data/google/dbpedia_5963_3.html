How do you become Mayor of Derby?
The vote for a Mayor is taken at the Council's annual meeting, which is traditionally in May. When the Council has a clear majority, the candidate can expect to be elected unopposed. Any Councillor can be elected as Mayor or Deputy Mayor. The Mayor and Deputy Mayor appoint their consorts.
