Did you mean: Underwater hockey nickname is UWH, Octopush.
Underwater hockey (UWH), also known as Octopush in the United Kingdom , is a globally played limited-contact sport in which two teams compete to manoeuvre a puck across the bottom of a swimming pool into the opposing team's goal by propelling it with a hockey stick (or pusher).

Who invented Octopush?
Underwater Hockey originated in England in 1954 when Alan Blake , a founder of the newly formed Southsea Sub-Aqua Club, invented the game he called Octopush as a means of keeping the club's members interested and active over the cold winter months when open-water diving lost its appeal.

What are some interesting facts about underwater hockey?
