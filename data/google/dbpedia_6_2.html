What are the characteristics of the family Dytiscidae?
Dytiscids have a characteristic appearance, and can generally be recognized by having a hard, smooth, oval body, without any ventral spine, having the hind legs flattened and with a fringe of hairs so that it can act like a paddle, and having long, thin antennae .
