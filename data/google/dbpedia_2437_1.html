Which four authors have won the Booker Prize twice?
In 2001, Peter Carey won his second Booker Prize for True History of the Kelly Gang, a work now ranked by The Guardian as one of the 100 greatest novels written in English. Carey is one of only four writers to have won the Booker Prize twice, the others being J.M. Coetzee, Hilary Mantel, and Margaret Atwood .
