How did Julia Alvarez write In the Time of the Butterflies?
Although the novel portrays a real situation that occurred in history, Alvarez wrote In the Time of the Butterflies as a fictional story in order to spread awareness of the Mirabal sisters' journey. The novel was a finalist for the National Book Critics Award in 1994.
