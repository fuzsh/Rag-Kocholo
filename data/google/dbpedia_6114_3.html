What is the genre of Gore movies?
Also known as the splatter genre, gore is all about the portrayal of graphic violence . Blood, guts and body trauma are classic elements in gore movies. Films in the gore category rely heavily on special effects to disfigure body parts. New filmmakers can experiment with effects and get creative with theatrical makeup.
