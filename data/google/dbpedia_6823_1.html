What is the function of Lgbtq Theatre?
LGBT theatre (also known as gay theatre, lesbian theatre or queer theatre) is theatre that is based on the lives of gay and lesbian people and gay and lesbian culture . Some LGBT theatre is specifically about the experiences of gay men or lesbian women.
