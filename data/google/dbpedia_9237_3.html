Why is Bremen a free city?
George 1 Louis, the Elector of Hannover (and from 1714, King of Great Britain and Ireland) who in 1715 acquired Bremen-Verden, recognized Bremen as a free city in 1720 . The city was captured in 1806 and then annexed by France in 1810, before it regained independence in 1815.
