What is the story Lorna Doone about?

Where is Lorna Doone based?
The heart of Lorna Doone country is tiny Malmsmead, which lies deep in the valley below County Gate, on Exmoor . In the village itself is Lorna Doone Farm, and nearby Oare House and Oare Church, where R D Blackmore's grandfather was once rector.
