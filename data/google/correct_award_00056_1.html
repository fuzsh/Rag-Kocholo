Giorgos Seferis

Greek poet and diplomat
Giorgos or George Seferis, the pen name of Georgios Seferiadis, was a Greek poet and diplomat. He was one of the most important Greek poets of the 20th century, and a Nobel laureate. Wikipedia
Born : March 13, 1900
Died : September 20, 1971 (age 71 years), Athens, Greece
Awards : Nobel Prize in Literature
Books : Strophe , Six Nights on the Acropolis , George Seferis: Collected Poems , and more
Place of burial : First Cemetery of Athens, Athens, Greece
Education : University of Paris (1918–1925)
Nationality : Greek and Ottoman
