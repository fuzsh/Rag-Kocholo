Does anyone live on Heligoland?
In the middle of the North Sea, north of the German coast, is a small island (well, archipelago really). Inhabited by around 1,100 people , it sits in an area of sea called German Bight. Best of all, it has its own grey seal colony. This island is Helgoland in the German spelling, Heligoland in English.
