What does a mealybug look like?
What do mealybugs look like? Most adult mealybugs are wingless females with oval, segmented bodies covered with wax . Males have wings but are rarely seen. Newly hatched mealybug immatures (nymphs) lack wax and can move around plants but soon settle down and produce a waxy covering.
