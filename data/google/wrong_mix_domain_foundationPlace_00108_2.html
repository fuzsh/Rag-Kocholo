What does the Basel symbol mean?
The Basel emblem, symbolizing a bishop's crozier , dates back to 999 when Basel began being ruled by prince-bishops. The emblem also represents a support or guide (the shepherd's crook that saves straying sheep) and an emblem of authority and administration.
