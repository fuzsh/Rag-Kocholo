George E. Leach

Former Mayor of Minneapolis
George Emerson Leach was an American politician who served as a major general in the United States Army and two-time Republican Mayor of Minneapolis. Wikipedia
Born : July 14, 1876, Cedar Rapids, IA
Died : July 17, 1955 (age 79 years), Los Angeles, CA
Party : Republican Party
Previous offices : Mayor of Minneapolis (1937–1941) and Mayor of Minneapolis (1921–1929)
Education : Central High School
Service/branch : United States Army
Years of service : 1905–1941
