"Rod" White (born March 1, 1977) is an American archer. He competed in the 1996 Olympic Games and the 2000 Olympic Games. In the 1996 team competition he won gold with Justin Huish and Butch Johnson . In 2000 the American team (with Johnson and Vic Wunderle) won the bronze medal.
