Chiksa' and Chahta were brothers and led the two groups. Before the split, the two groups experienced many years of war with a powerful enemy.

Why don t the Choctaws and Chickasaws play as big a part in the story of Georgia history as the Seminoles Creeks and Cherokees?
Georgia's largest tribe was the Choctaw, which had about 25,000 adults and children. They lived in what today is south Mississippi. To the north of the Choctaws were about 2,000 Chickasaws. Neither tribe played an important role in Georgia history because their lands were far to the west of Georgia's frontier .

What were the Creek Seminoles Chickasaw Choctaw and Cherokee known as?
