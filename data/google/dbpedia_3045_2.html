What was the 184th Infantry Regiment in the Civil War?
The 184th Pennsylvania Infantry was organized at Harrisburg, Pennsylvania in May 1864 and mustered in under the command of Colonel John Hubler Stover . The regiment was attached to 1st Brigade, 2nd Division, II Corps, Army of the Potomac. On July 14, 1865, the 184th Pennsylvania Infantry was mustered out of service.
