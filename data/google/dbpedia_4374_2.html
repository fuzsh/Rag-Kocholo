What is the oldest house in Woodburn Oregon?
Settlemier House is a historic house in Woodburn, Oregon, United States. It was built by Jesse H. Settlemier, founder of Woodburn, in 1892. The house is no longer inhabited but is open to the public as a museum and as a location for special events such as weddings.
