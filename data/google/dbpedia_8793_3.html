On 8 July 2008 the Council of the European Union approved Slovakia's application to join the euro area on 1 January 2009. On 1 January 2009 the euro became legal tender in Slovakia. It replaced the Slovak koruna (SKK) at the irrevocably fixed exchange rate of €1 = SKK 30.1260.
