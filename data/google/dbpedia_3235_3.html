How do you calculate the best batting average?
Simple, by extracting the number of not outs from the number of innings played . Lets take an example: Player A has scored 10200 runs in 325 matches but he/she has batted in 300 innings. Player A remained not out in 50 of those innings. So the batting average = 10200 / 300-50 that is 10200/250 = 40.8.
