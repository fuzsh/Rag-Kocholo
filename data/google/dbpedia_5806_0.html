Brentano attended schools in Aschaffenburg and Augsburg. After graduation, he went to Ireland where he became a student at the world famous Trinity College in Dublin from 1861 until 1862. Back in Germany, he attended the universities of Muenster, Munich, Heidelberg, Würzburg, Göttingen, and Berlin .
