What ethnicity is the last name Gilbert?
Gilbert is an English surname with Saxon origins . It is hypothesised to be a patronymic name, deriving from someone who was the son of 'Giselbert', 'Gib' or 'Gilbert'. These particular given names were especially popular in England during the medieval period.
