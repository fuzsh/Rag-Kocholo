When did musical theatre begin to evolve as a specific genre of its own?
Although music has been a part of dramatic presentations since ancient times, modern Western musical theatre emerged during the 19th century , with many structural elements established by the light opera works of Jacques Offenbach in France, Gilbert and Sullivan in Britain and the works of Harrigan and Hart in America.
