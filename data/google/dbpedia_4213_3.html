What is the story of death by a thousand cuts?
Synopsis. Death by a Thousand Cuts investigates the use of slow slicing or lingchi, a form of torture and capital punishment practised in mid- and late-Imperial China from the tenth century until its abolition in 1905 . Lingchi involved repeatedly slicing the convict's flesh beyond the point of death.
