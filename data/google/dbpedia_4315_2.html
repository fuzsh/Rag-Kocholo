Who was Philip Johnston Navajo Code Talkers?
During World War II, the U.S. Marine Corps, in an effort to find quicker and more secure ways to send and receive code enlisted Navajos as "code talkers." Philip Johnston was the initiator of the Marine Corps' program to enlist and train the Navajos as messengers .
