Rome2Rio

Company
Rome2Rio is an Australian online multimodal transport journey planner offering travel services globally. The company is based in Melbourne, Australia, and is owned by the German online travel comparison and booking website Omio. Wikipedia
Headquarters : Melbourne, Australia
Founders : Bernhard Tschirren and Michael Cameron
Date founded : 2010
Owner : Omio
