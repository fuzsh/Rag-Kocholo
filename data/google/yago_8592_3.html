G. W. Pabst

Austrian film director and screenwriter
Georg Wilhelm Pabst was an Austrian film director and screenwriter. He started as an actor and theater director, before becoming one of the most influential German-language filmmakers during the Weimar Republic. Wikipedia
Born : August 27, 1885, Roudnice nad Labem, Czechia
Died : May 29, 1967 (age 81 years), Vienna, Austria
Awards : Golden Medal for Best Director , German Film Award - Honorary Award , International Award for Best Director , and more
Nominations : Gran Premio Internazionale di Venezia per il miglior film
Books : Pandora's box (Lulu)
Children : Michael Pabst and Peter Pabst
