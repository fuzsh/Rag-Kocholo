Why is it called Hungry Horse Dam?
Columbia Falls and Kalispell are located southwest of the dam site. The name "Hungry Horse" comes from the tale of two horses (Tex and Jerry) who had wandered away from their logging sled in the winter of 1900-1901 . The horses struggled in the deep snow for a month, and needed to be nursed back to health.
