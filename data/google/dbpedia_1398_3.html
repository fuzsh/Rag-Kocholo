Robert Redman was head coach of the Huskies football team from 1947-51 , leading his teams to undefeated seasons in 1948 and 1951 and to Pennsylvania Conference championships in 1948, 1949 and 1951. In his four seasons as head coach he completed a record of 38-4, a winning percentage of 90.5 percent.
