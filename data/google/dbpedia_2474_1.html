Jo Van Fleet was born on December 29, 1915 in Oakland, California, USA. She was an actress, known for East of Eden (1955), Cool Hand Luke (1967) and I'll Cry Tomorrow (1955). She was married to William Glenn Bales. She died on June 10, 1996 in Queens, New York City, New York, USA.
