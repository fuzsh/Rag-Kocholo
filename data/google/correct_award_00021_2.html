Max Planck

German theoretical physicist
Max Karl Ernst Ludwig Planck ForMemRS was a German theoretical physicist whose discovery of energy quanta won him the Nobel Prize in Physics in 1918. Wikipedia
Born : April 23, 1858, Kiel, Germany
Died : October 4, 1947 (age 89 years), Göttingen, Germany
Education : Ludwig Maximilian University of Munich (1879), Ludwig Maximilian University of Munich , and Humboldt University of Berlin
Children : Erwin Planck , Hermann Planck , Grete Planck , and more
Spouse : Marga von Hoesslin (m. 1911–1947) and Marie Merck (m. 1887–1909)
Siblings : Otto Planck and Adalbert Planck
Parents : Emma Patzig and J. w. Planck
