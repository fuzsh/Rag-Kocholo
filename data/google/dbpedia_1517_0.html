Where was Poultrygeist filmed?
Shot on 35mm film by long-time Troma cinematographer Brendan C. Flynt, principal photography for Poultrygeist took place during the summer of 2005 at an abandoned McDonald's in the Bailey-Kensington neighborhood of Buffalo, New York . Over 80 crew members and 300 unpaid extras worked on the film.
