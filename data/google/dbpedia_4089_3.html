Why is Charles de Gaulle so famous?
Charles de Gaulle was the president of France from the late 1950s to the late 1960s. He was also a military soldier during both World Wars, which shaped him into the tactical ruler he rose to become . Over the years, his political approaches have been discussed, and they influenced many historical events.
