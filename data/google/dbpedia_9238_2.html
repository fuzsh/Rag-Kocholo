What was the currency in Tibet before China?
Tibet's monetary system was more closely tied to India than to China until the Twentieth Century. The Tangka was the principal unit of account in Tibet until 1912 . The Indian Rupee was equal to 3 Tangka, and 1 Tangka was equal to 6 Annas, though 1 Rupee was equal to 16 Annas.
