Born a German national, Admiral Józef Unrug commanded the Polish coastal defence during Poland's 1939 defensive war against Nazi Germany . Unrug died in Lailly-en-Val, France in 1973, and was buried in Montresor, central France. His and his wife's remains were brought to Poland for the state burial in Gdynia.
