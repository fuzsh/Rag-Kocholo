Wahid Abdallah El Fattal (Arabic: وحيد عبدالله الفتال; born 1 June 1978 ) is a Lebanese football coach and former player who is currently the goalkeeper coach of the Lebanon national team. As a player, El Fattal represented Lebanon in the 2000 AFC Asian Cup.
