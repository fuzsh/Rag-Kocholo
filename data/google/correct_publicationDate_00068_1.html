What does Douglas Adams mean when he says So long and thanks for all the fish?
It is a quotation from Episode Three of The Hitchhiker's Guide to the Galaxy radio series by Douglas Adams. This is a comedy, the phrase, as used there, is to signify that Dolphins are more intelligent than humans . In the story, Earth is destroyed, the dolphins knew this was coming and left the planet.
