Dasgupta (born October 1885, Kushtia, India [now in Bangladesh]—died December 18, 1952, Lucknow, India) was an Indian philosopher noted for his authoritative A History of Indian Philosophy, 5 vol. (1922–55). Dasgupta received master's degrees in Sanskrit and philosophy from Sanskrit College in Calcutta .
