What is the plot of the story a Connecticut Yankee in King Arthur's Court?
It is the story of Hank Morgan, a 19th-century resident of Hartford, Connecticut, who is inexplicably transported back in time to the court of King Arthur . Hank uses his knowledge of modern technology to try to improve the lives of the people he encounters, but his efforts are ultimately unsuccessful.
