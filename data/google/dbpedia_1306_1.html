Who originally sang "Girl, you really got me"?
"You Really Got Me" is a song by English rock band the Kinks , written by frontman Ray Davies. The song, originally performed in a more blues-orientated style, was inspired by artists such as Lead Belly and Big Bill Broonzy. Two versions were recorded, with the second performance used for the final single.
