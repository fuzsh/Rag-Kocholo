What does Escuintla mean?
The name Escuintla, comes from the indigenous language nahuatl «Itzcuintlán», which is a word composed of two words: · «Itzcuintli»: means "dog" · «tlan»: means "abundance" Therefore, Escuintla means " abundance or place of dogs ".
