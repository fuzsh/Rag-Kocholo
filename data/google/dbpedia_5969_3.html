Steve Golin

American film producer
Steven Aaron Golin was an American film and television producer and the founder and CEO of Anonymous Content LLP, a multimedia development, production and talent management company and co-founder and CEO of Propaganda Films. Wikipedia
Born : March 6, 1955, Geneva, NY
Died : April 21, 2019 (age 64 years), Los Angeles, CA
Organizations founded : Anonymous Content and Propaganda Films
Production companies : Anonymous Content and Propaganda Films
Awards : Academy Award for Best Picture , BAFTA Award for Best Film , Gotham Independent Film Tribute Award , and more
Nominations : Academy Award for Best Picture , Primetime Emmy Award for Outstanding Drama Series , BAFTA Award for Best Film , and more
Education : AFI Conservatory (1981) and Tisch School Of The Arts
