What are the major themes of Larkin's poetry?
The most common themes in Philip Larkin's poems include love, death, time, and marriage . Larkin often questions love and holds a deep scepticism towards it. Not only this, but Philip Larkin also questions how long love can truly last. Often in his poems, Larkin explores the inevitability of death.
