What was the 100 hour ground war?
On 24 February 1991, the ground war began. In just 100 hours, the Iraqi Republican Guard retreated and coalition forces liberated Kuwait City . The swift victory came as a surprise to everyone involved. The Iraqi Army was known to be strong and well-trained, but they were no match for the U.S. military and its allies.
