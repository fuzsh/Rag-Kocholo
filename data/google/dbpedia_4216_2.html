Where is Abney and Teal set?
The show, which follows the adventures of two friends, is set on a series of small islands on a lake in a park in an unnamed city . The show was first broadcast on CBeebies in the United Kingdom in September 2011, and is produced by Ragdoll Worldwide, the joint venture of Ragdoll Productions and BBC Worldwide.

What animal is Abney from Abney and Teal?
Abney is a blue, cat-like creature with a jumper, scarf and trousers, while Teal is a girl with pigtails, a dress and Converse-like shoes. Other regular characters include Neep the anthropomorphic turnip, Bop the furry aquatic creature, the seven wooden, comma-shaped Poc-Pocs and accordion-playing Toby Dog.
