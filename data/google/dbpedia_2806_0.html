Makhdoom Bilawal was a sufi saint , philosopher and poet from Sindh, Pakistan. He was born in 1451 AD (856 AH) and died in 1522 AD (929 AH). He is also known as Makhdum Bilal or Bilali Makhdum. He is celebrated as a hero in Sindh for his resistance against the Argun invaders who tried to conquer his homeland.
