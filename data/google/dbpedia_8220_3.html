Where did Kickapoo Joy Juice originate?
Kickapoo Joy Juice is a citrus-flavored soda inspired by Al Capp's comic strip “Li'l Abner,” which appeared in US newspapers from 1934 to 1977. The name, “Kickapoo Joy Juice”, was originally coined as a “volatile brew” in Li'l Abner, an American comic strip.
