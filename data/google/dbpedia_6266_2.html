Why didn't Trotter become a member of the NAACP?
Trotter did not attend the second meeting of the newly formed organization because of his suspicions of the role of white money and leadership in the NAACP ; only one African-American was elected as an officer. Trotter was also concerned about NAACP's lack of strong opposition against Booker T. Washington.
