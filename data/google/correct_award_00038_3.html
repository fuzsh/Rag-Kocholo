Arno Allan Penzias

American physicist
Arno Allan Penzias was an American physicist and radio astronomer. Along with Robert Woodrow Wilson, he discovered the cosmic microwave background radiation, for which he shared the Nobel Prize in Physics in 1978. Wikipedia
Born : 1933, Munich, Germany
Died : January 22, 2024 (age 90 years), San Francisco, CA
Spouse : Anne Pearl Barras (m. 1954)
Education : Columbia University , The City College of New York , and Brooklyn Technical High School
Nationality : American and German
Awards : Nobel Prize in Physics and Henry Draper Medal
Parents : Justine Eisenreich Penzias and Karl Penzias
