What is the meaning of White Fang?
The name means exactly what it says, white fang. The name comes from White Fang's first encounter with humans . When White Fang, as a cub, bares his teeth to the humans in a snarl, they laugh at his puppy attempts at ferocity and comment on his white fangs.
