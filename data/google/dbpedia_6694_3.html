What is the old name for Afghanistan?
The old name of Afghanistan is "Khorasan" or "Ariana." Khorasan is an ancient term that historically referred to a region that included parts of modern-day Afghanistan, Iran, Turkmenistan, and Uzbekistan. The name Ariana has also been used to describe the region, especially in ancient Greek and Roman texts.
