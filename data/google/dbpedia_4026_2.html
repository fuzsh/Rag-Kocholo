Who founded the See of Alexandria?
The Church of Alexandria is an autocephalous, or self-headed, Orthodox Christian Church founded in Alexandria, Egypt, around 49 CE by Saint Mark the Evangelist , one of the Apostles. This makes it one of the Apostolic Sees. A see is a region of power or influence, and as Saint Mark was an apostle, his see is Apostolic.
