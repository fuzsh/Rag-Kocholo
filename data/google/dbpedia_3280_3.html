Vesta M. Roy was a Republican New Hampshire politician. She was the first woman to ever serve as the President of The New Hampshire Senate, Acting Governor, and Governor of New Hampshire. Wikipedia
Born : March 26, 1925, United States
Died : February 9, 2002 (age 76 years), Kenmore, NY
Previous offices : Governor of New Hampshire (1982–1983), Acting Governor of New Hampshire (1982–1983), and New Hampshire State Senator (1978–1986)
Party : Republican Party
Education : Wayne State University
