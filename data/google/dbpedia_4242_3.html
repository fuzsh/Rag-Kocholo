Mealybugs are in the insect family Pseudococcidae , part of the superfamily Coccoidea, which also includes armored scales, soft scales, and cottony cushion scale. Mealybug bodies are distinctly segmented and usually covered with wax. Older individuals may have wax filaments around their body margins.

Mealybugs are insects in the family Pseudococcidae , unarmored scale insects found in moist, warm habitats.
