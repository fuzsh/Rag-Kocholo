What is the book Forty Signs of Rain about?
The focus of the novel is the effects of global warming in the early decades of the 21st century . Its characters are mostly scientists, either involved in biotech research, assisting government members, or doing paperwork at the National Science Foundation (NSF).

What is fifty degrees below?
