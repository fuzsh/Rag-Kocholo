What does the name Coxsackie mean?
The Village of Coxsackie is located on the west side of the Hudson River in Greene County, NY. The name “Coxsackie” comes from an Algonquin Indian word believed to mean “Place of Owls”, “Migrating Geese”, or “Cut Rocks” .
