Where did Sigrid Undset live?
Undset was married to painter Anders Castus Svarstad and had three children. After having divorced her husband, she converted to Catholicism and became a lay Dominican in 1928. Undset opposed the Nazis and spent World War II living in the USA. She later moved to Lillehammer, Norway .
