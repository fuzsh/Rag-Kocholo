Dinah Shore

American singer and actress

Born : February 29, 1916, Winchester, TN
Died : February 24, 1994 (age 77 years), Beverly Hills, CA
Children : Melissa Montgomery-Hime and John David Montgomery
Spouse : Maurice F. Smith (m. 1963–1964) and George Montgomery (m. 1943–1963)
Nationality : American
