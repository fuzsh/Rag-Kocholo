Does Nigeria use West African time?
Nigeria observes West Africa Time (WAT) , which is one hour ahead of Coordinated Universal Time (UTC+01:00), year-round as standard time. Nigeria has never observed daylight saving time. It shares WAT with fourteen other countries in Africa.

What is West Central Africa time zone?
West Africa Time, or WAT, is a time zone used in west-central Africa . West Africa Time is one hour ahead of Coordinated Universal Time (UTC+01:00), which aligns it with Central European Time (CET) during winter, and Western European Summer Time (WEST) / British Summer Time (BST) during summer.
