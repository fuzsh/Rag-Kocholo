What is the Mexican version of cumbia?
Mexican cumbia is similar to other adaptations of Colombian music such as Salvadorian cumbia, Peruvian cumbia or Argentinian cumbia, among others. It is not a unification of a single genre, but a mix of styles that are very diverse and wide ranging, from province to province, from era to era.
