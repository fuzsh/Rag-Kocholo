What was before CP M?
Before CP/M, computer manufacturers designed their operating systems to work only with their own hardware and peripheral equipment . An IBM OS would only work with IBM computers; a Burroughs OS would only work with Burroughs computers, etc. Applications had to be written for each computer's specific OS.
