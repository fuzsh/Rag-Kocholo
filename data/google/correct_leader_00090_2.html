Mikhail Mikhailovich Kasyanov is a Russian politician who served as Prime Minister of Russia from 2000 to 2004. Previously, he had served as First Deputy Prime Minister in 2000 and Minister of Finance from 1999 to 2000. Wikipedia
Born : 1957 (age 66 years), Solntsevo
Party : People's Freedom Party
Education : Moskovskiy Avtomobil'no-Dorozhnyy Gosudarstvennyy Tekhnicheskiy Universitet (1981–1983) and Moskovskiy Avtomobil'no-Dorozhnyy Gosudarstvennyy Tekhnicheskiy Universitet (1974–1976)
Spouse : Irina Kasyanova
Children : Natalya Klinovskaya and Aleksandra Kasyanova
Organizations founded : Anti-War Committee of Russia , Russian People's Democratic Union , and People for Democracy and Justice
Previous offices : Prime Minister of Russia (2000–2004), First Deputy Prime Minister of Russia (2000–2000), and Minister of Finance of Russia (1999–2000)
