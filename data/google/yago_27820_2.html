Louise of the Netherlands

Queen of Sweden
Louise of the Netherlands, also called Lovisa, was Queen of Sweden and Norway from 8 July 1859 until her death in 1871 as the wife of King Charles XV & IV. Wikipedia
Born : August 5, 1828, The Hague, Netherlands
Died : March 30, 1871 (age 42 years), Stockholm, Sweden
Children : Louise of Sweden and Prince Carl Oscar, Duke of Södermanland
Parents : Princess Louise of Prussia and Prince Frederick of the Netherlands
Spouse : Charles XV (m. 1850)
Grandchildren : Haakon VII , Christian X (King of Denmark) , Princess Louise of Denmark , and more
Grandparents : Louise of Mecklenburg-Strelitz , William I of the Netherlands , Frederick William III of Prussia , and more
