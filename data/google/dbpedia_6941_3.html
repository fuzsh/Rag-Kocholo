Why does the fool call Lear Nuncle?
The term ''nuncle'' is a contraction of ''mine uncle'' and used to be used as a term of familiarity. The fact that the Fool uses it to refer to Lear means that he sees him, on some level, as a familial figure . Lear, similarly, seems to see the Fool as his son, to an extent.
