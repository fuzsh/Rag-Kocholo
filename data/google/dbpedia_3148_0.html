Ambient music

Music genre
Ambient music is a genre of music that emphasizes tone and atmosphere over traditional musical structure or rhythm. It is often "peaceful" sounding and lacks composition, beat, and/or structured melody. Wikipedia
Parent genres : Background music , Techno , Dub , and more
Creator : Brian Eno
Cultural origins : 1960s–1970s, United Kingdom, Jamaica (dub music) and Japan
Derivative forms : Biomusic ; chill-out ; downtempo ; IDM; new age ; post-rock ; space music ; trance; trip hop

What genre is ambient music?
Ambient music is a loosely defined musical genre that incorporates elements of a number of different styles - including jazz, electronic music, new age, modern classical music and even noise . It is chiefly identifiable as having an overarching atmospheric context.
