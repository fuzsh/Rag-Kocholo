What is the main idea of the play Love's Labour's Lost?
Love. It is no surprise that the topic of love is central to Love's Labor's Lost. The entire plot of the play revolves around love and various characters' attempts at courtship . Characters swear off love, talk endlessly about it, admit their powerlessness before it, and devote themselves entirely to its pursuit.
