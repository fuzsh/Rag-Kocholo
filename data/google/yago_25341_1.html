Born : June 25, 1894, Alexandria, Egypt
Died : May 29, 1978 (age 83 years), Los Angeles, CA
Children : Princess Fathia of Egypt , Fawzia of Egypt , Farouk I of Egypt , and more
Grandchildren : Fuad II of Egypt , Shahnaz Pahlavi , Princess Farial of Egypt , and more
Spouse : Ahmed Hassanein (m. 1942), Fuad I of Egypt (m. 1919–1936), and Khalil Sabri (m. 1918)
