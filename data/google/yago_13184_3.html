Who made the Americanese Wall cartoon?
"The Americanese Wall" by Raymond O. Evans critiques the harsh immigration restrictions promoted by Alabama congressman John Lawson Burnett. It was published in the humor magazine Puck in March 1916 during the push to pass legislation, sponsored by Burnett, that would eventually become the Immigration Act of 1917.

What did Congressman Burnett want?
After repeated vetoes of such laws, the 1917 Immigration Act, sponsored by Congressman John Lawson Burnett, finally succeeded in imposing a literacy test on immigrants to the U.S. It required immigrants over the age of 16 to be able to read 30-40 words in any language .
