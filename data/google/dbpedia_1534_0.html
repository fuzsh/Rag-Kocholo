Tomasz Budzyński

Polish musician and painter
Tomasz Maciej Budzyński is Polish musician, painter and poet, the lead vocalist of the band Armia. He has worked with bands 2Tm2,3, Budzy i Trupia Czaszka, he was also first vocalist of punk rock band Siekiera. Wikipedia
Born : 1962 (age 61 years), Tarnobrzeg, Poland
Spouse : Natalia Budzyńska
Music groups : Armia (Since 1984), 2Tm2,3 (Since 1996), and Siekiera (1983 – 1984)
