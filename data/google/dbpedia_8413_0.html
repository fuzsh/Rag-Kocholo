What is the meaning of Komatsu?
Komatsu Surname Meaning Japanese: written 小松 ' small pine tree '. It is found mostly in west-central Japan the island of Shikoku and the Ryūkyū Islands; an alternate reading found farther east is Omatsu. Several samurai families of various lineages took this surname from any of various villages of this name.
