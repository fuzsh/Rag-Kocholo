Elena Gerhardt

German mezzo-soprano
Elena Gerhardt was a German mezzo-soprano singer associated with the singing of German classical lieder, of which she was considered one of the great interpreters. She emigrated to London in October 1934. Wikipedia
Born : November 11, 1883, Leipzig, Germany
Died : January 11, 1961 (age 77 years), London, United Kingdom
Education : University of Music and Theatre Leipzig
