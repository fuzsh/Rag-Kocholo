What is the geography of Uruguay?
The country itself has flat plains on its eastern, southern, and western edges . The narrow Atlantic coastal plain is sandy and marshy, occasionally broken by shallow lagoons. The littorals of the Río de la Plata and the Río Uruguay are somewhat broader and merge more gradually into the hilly interior.
