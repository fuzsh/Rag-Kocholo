Udham Singh

Udham Singh was an Indian revolutionary belonging to Ghadar Party and HSRA, best known for assassinating Michael O'Dwyer, the former lieutenant governor of the Punjab in India, on 13 March 1940. Wikipedia
Born : December 26, 1899, Sunam Udham Singh Wala, India
Died : July 31, 1940 (age 40 years), HMP Pentonville, London, United Kingdom
Parents : Sardar Tehal Singh Jammu and Mata Narain Kaur
