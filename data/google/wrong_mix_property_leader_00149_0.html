Mari Alkatiri

Former Prime Minister of Timor-Leste
Mari bin Amude Alkatiri GCIH is a Timorese politician. He was Prime Minister of East Timor from May 2002 until his resignation on 26 June 2006 following weeks of political unrest in the country, and again from September 2017 until May 2018. Wikipedia
Born : 1949 (age 74 years), Dili, Timor-Leste
Party : Fretilin
Previous offices : Prime Minister of Timor-Leste (2017–2018) and Prime Minister of Timor-Leste (2002–2006)
Partner : Marina Alkatiri
Education : Universidade Eduardo Mondlane
Organization founded : Fretilin
Marriage location : Madagascar
