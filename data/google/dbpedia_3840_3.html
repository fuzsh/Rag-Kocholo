What is the history of Eagle Harbor?
Eagle Harbor was originally known as Trueman Point, a river port established in the early 18th century to serve as a shipping point for tobacco plantations located in the Aquasco area . In 1747, it was considered by the Maryland colony as an official tobacco inspection station, but it never realized that designation.
