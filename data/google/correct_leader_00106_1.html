Edith Cresson

Former Prime Minister of France
Édith Cresson is a French politician from the Socialist Party. She served as Prime Minister of France from 1991 to 1992, the first woman to do so and only woman until Élisabeth Borne's appointment in 2022. Wikipedia
Born : 1934 (age 90 years), Boulogne-Billancourt, France
Spouse : Jacques Cresson (m. 1959–2001)
Education : Haut Enseignement Commercial pour les jeunes filles (1954)
Party : Socialist Party
Nationality : French
Descendants : Alexandra Cresson and Nathalie Léger-Cresson
