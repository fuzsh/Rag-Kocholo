What is Hebron, CT famous for?
The east-central Connecticut town of Hebron lies at Tolland County's southern tip. Incorporated in 1708, Hebron had a strong agricultural base and up through the late 1800s boasted manufacturers of charcoal (once a fuel source for other industries), bricks, textiles, paper, and other goods.
