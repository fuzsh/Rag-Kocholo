What grade of concrete is used in bridge construction?
Grade M45 Concrete M45 concrete is a high-strength mix with a compressive strength of 45 N/mm². It is used for critical load-bearing structures, such as bridges and high-rise buildings. The mix proportions for M45 concrete are: 1 part cement, 2 parts sand, 8 parts coarse aggregate, and 0.6 parts water.
