Is Callicarpa poisonous?
It is often said that Callicarpa plants are toxic but this is actually something of a myth. The ripe fruits are not poisonous to humans or pets such as cats and dogs . However, raw berries should only be eaten in small amounts. They are best enjoyed cooked into jams or fermented into beautyberry wine.
