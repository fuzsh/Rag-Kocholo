How can I tell a movie review in English?

Top Tips for writing

1
Start with the film's title.
2
Say the type of film and when it was made.
3
Explain the film's story but don't explain the ending!
4
Give your opinion of the film, e.g. I love the actors in this film.
5
Should people go and watch the film? Say what you think at the end.
