What is the history of Coggeshall?
History. Coggeshall dates back at least to an early Saxon settlement, though the area has been settled since the Mesolithic period . There is evidence of a Roman villa or settlement before then and the town lies on Stane Street, which may have been built on a much earlier track.

What is the history of Bradwell?
Historically Bradwell was part of Hope ecclesiastical parish until 1868 when the Anglican Church of St. Barnabas opened . Samuel Fox, inventor of the Paragon umbrella frame, donated £100 towards the cost of constructing the church. The village grew again in the eighteenth century around lead mining.
