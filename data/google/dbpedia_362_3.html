What is the history of Sprottbrough Doncaster?
History. The place name 'Sprotbrough' is first attested in the Domesday Book of 1086, where it appears as Sproteburg . The name is thought to mean 'Sprot's borough'. Much of the local land was owned by the Fitzwilliam family until the 15th century, when the Copley family emerged as major landowners.
