What was the first newspaper in Quebec?
The Quebec Gazette / Gazette de Québec is Quebec's first newspaper and Canada's second newspaper, after the Halifax Gazette, which was first issued in 1752. Printers Brown and Gilmore would also print the first book published in Quebec, the Catéchisme du Diocese de Sens (Catechism of the Diocese of Sens) in 1765.

What is the main newspaper in Quebec?
Le Journal de Montréal is a daily French-language tabloid newspaper published in Montreal, Quebec, Canada. It has the largest circulation of any newspaper in Quebec and is also the largest French-language daily newspaper in North America.
