What are some interesting facts about Isaac Bashevis' singer?
Isaac Bashevis Singer grew up in a Jewish neighborhood in Warsaw, where the main language was Yiddish . Singer's father, a rabbi, worked in a yeshiva, an Orthodox Jewish school for the study of sacred texts. Singer began studies to become a rabbi himself but decided to devote his life to writing.

Did Isaac Bashevis Singer write in English?
Singer always wrote and published in Yiddish. His novels were serialized in newspapers, which also published his short stories.
