Justin Timberlake

Born on January 31, 1981, Justin Randall Timberlake started his career with the boyband 'N Sync, before embarking on a successful solo journey. As an American singer, songwriter, and actor, he's a force in the entertainment industry. With sales surpassing 88 million records globally, Timberlake has earned numerous accolades, including ten Grammy Awards. Beyond music, his versatile acting has also been recognised, further solidifying his legendary status.
