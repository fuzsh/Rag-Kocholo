Joint Stock Company Rusnano was founded on March 11, 2011, following the re-organization of the state corporation, Russian Corporation of Nanotechnologies, established in 2007. RUSNANO implements state policy for the development of the nanoindustry in Russia, acting as a co-investor in nanotechnology projects, which have substantial economic or social potential.
