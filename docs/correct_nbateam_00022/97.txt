The Los Angeles Clippers began play in 1970. The team originated as the Buffalo Braves but moved to San Diego and became the San Diego Clippers in 1978. In 1984, the team moved to Los Angeles The team plays its home games at the Staples Center. The Clippers play in the Pacific Division with the Golden State Warriors, Los Angeles Lakers, Phoenix Suns and Sacramento Kings. Among their most famous players include Terry Cummings, Blake Griffin, Chris Paul, Lamar Odom and Elton Brand.
