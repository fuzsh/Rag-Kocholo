Gabriella Zanna Vanessa Anstruther-Gough-Calthorpe (born on 8 April 1989), professionally known as Gabriella Wilde or Gabriella Calthorpe, is an English model and actress.

She has appeared in the films St Trinian's 2: The Legend of Fritton's Gold (2009), The Three Musketeers (2011), Carrie (2013), Endless Love (2014), and Wonder Woman 1984 (2020).
