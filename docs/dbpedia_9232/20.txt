Summitville, a hamlet in the "Sullivan County Catskills", was once an important transportation center. This was the highest point reached by the Delaware and Hudson Canal, and an important junction on the New York, Ontario & Western Railway.

Branches to Kingston and Port Jervis left the Cornwall to Oswego main line here. Years of losses came to an end with nearly total abandonment in 1957.
