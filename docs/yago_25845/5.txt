About

Canadian writer who is known for her works in the young adult and "chick-lit" genres. Her publications include Monkey Business, Me vs. Me, and Fishbowl.

Before Fame

After graduating from McGill University, she published her debut work, a romantic novel entitled Milkrun.

Trivia

She served as editor of the Girls' Night In, Girls' Night Out, and Vacations from Hell fiction anthologies.

Family Life

Her mother, Elissa Ambrose, had a career as a romance writer. She married Todd Swidler in 2004; they became parents to daughters Chloe and Anabelle.
