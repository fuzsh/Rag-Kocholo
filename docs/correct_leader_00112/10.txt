Stjepan "Stipe" Mesic (born 24 December 1934) was Prime Minister of SR Croatia from 30 May to 24 August 1990 (succeeding Antun Milovic and preceding Josip Manolic), President of Yugoslavia from 30 June to 6 December 1991 (succeeding Sejdo Bajramovic and preceding Branko Kostic), and President of Croatia from 18 February 2000 to 18 February 2010 (succeeding Franjo Tudman and preceding Ivo Josipovic).
