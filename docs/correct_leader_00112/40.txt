The Ministry of Foreign Affairs and European Integration received late last night, through the Croatian Mission to the European Communities in Brussels, a response by the European Community’s Spokesperson’s Office to the complaint lodged yesterday against the EC Deputy Spokesperson regarding her unacceptable and one-sided comments on the statements by Croatian President Stjepan Mesić

The response reads that the Office of the EC President José Manuel Barroso will be informed about the content the Ministry’s complaint.
