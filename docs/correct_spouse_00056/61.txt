Rose McGowan is off the market. The actress married artist Davey Detail on Saturday in a small ceremony in Los Angeles, People reports.

A rabbi performed the ceremony in front of about 60 guests, according to People. The couple, who have been together since 2012, got engaged in July.

"Hell to the yeah!" McGowan, 40, tweeted Saturday. "I'm a Mrs.! What?!AWESOME! Love to you all!!!!!"

McGowan was previously engaged to director Robert Rodriguez and Marilyn Manson.
