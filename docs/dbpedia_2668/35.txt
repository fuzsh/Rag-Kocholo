The death occurred at Southport last

Sunday, at the age of 91, of one of

Brisbane's pioneers, Miss Jane Ellen

Whiteway. The late Miss Whiteway

came to reside in Southport nine years

ago. Born at Totnes, Devonshire, she

came to Australia in 1866 with her

father, Mr. Thomas Whiteway, of the

famous Whiteway cider family, her

two brothers, the late Mr. Edmund

Whiteway, of Lower Tent Hill, Gatton,

and the late Major William Whiteway,

of Charters Towers, and her youngest

sister, the late Mrs. Charlotte Hall, of

Sandgate, with whom she was associat-

ed in business at Villa Marina for

many years. The funeral, which was

of a private nature, took place on Sun-

day afternoon to the Southport ceme-

tery, the chief mourners being Mr. T.

R. Hall (nephew), Miss Ethel White-

way (niece), and Masters Jack and

William Hall.
