Artists, bands, and comedians appearing at The Paramount can promote their tours and new music releases on the air to Connoisseur Media’s weekly radio listeners on its five stations – WWSK FM, WALK FM, WKJY FM, WBZO FM or WHLI AM, according to a press release.

Slated for completion in May 2015, the studio will be situated in the theater’s storefront at 370 New York Avenue.
