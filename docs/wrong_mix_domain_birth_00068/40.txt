Nas, born Nasir Jones, is a critically acclaimed rapper and songwriter from Queens, New York. Emerging in the early 1990s, he quickly became a prominent figure in the hip-hop scene with his debut album, 'Illmatic,' which is considered one of the greatest hip-hop albums of all time.

Nas's lyrics often delve into the harsh realities of urban life, exploring themes such as the inevitability of death, coping through substances, and the constant struggle for a better life. His work vividly portrays New York street narratives, reflecting the urban survival mentality of those growing up in inner-city environments.
