LightLab is a developer of optical coherence tomography (OCT) for the treatment of cardiovascular disease. LightLab received Food and Drug Administration clearance for the C7-XR imaging system and companion C7 Dragonfly imaging catheter on May 5. With the acquisition of LightLab, St. Jude Medical stated it will offer a portfolio that includes both OCT and fractional flow reserve technology to provide physicians with comprehensive lesion assessment information, ranging from the anatomical images of OCT to the physiological data of fractional flow reserve.
