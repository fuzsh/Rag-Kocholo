Company Overview:

Novar has been delivering savings to multi-site retailers across the country through energy and operational management for more than 20 years. It is one of the largest global energy management firms with capabilities ranging from installing smart building control systems to full solution center support for monitoring and managing those systems effectively.

Novar’s business is focused on the multi-site retail sector. Its installed systems and supporting services are currently used by the nation’s leading retail establishments. Novar delivers the level of performance and cost-effective data management, analysis, and proactive energy management operation strategies that improve business performance.

Novar is part of Honeywell International, a $33 billion diversified technology and manufacturing leader serving customers worldwide with energy management solutions; aerospace products and services; control technologies for buildings, homes, and industry; automotive products; turbochargers; and specialty materials.
