A handy book to learn Kodava language with words in English, Hindi, Kannada, Roman and International Phonetic Alphabets (IPA). Words and their pronunciation in Kodava language have been given in Kannda, English, Hindi and Roman and IPA.

A wide range of words related to relationships, parts of human body, names of vegetables, fruits, spices, animals, etc, numbers, months, days along with greetings, wishings and many more have all been included. A must have for all those who are interested in learning the language.
