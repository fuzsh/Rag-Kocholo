Tasmania was struggling at 3 for 72 in its second innings at tea on day three of the Sheffield Shield match against South Australia at the Adelaide Oval.

The Tigers were still 37 runs away from making the Redbacks bat again, after the home side was all out for 345 after taking first innings points.

Tasmanian skipper George Bailey was on 20 and Dan Marsh 6 at the interval.

Jon Wells, Ed Cowan and Alex Doolan had all been dismissed.
