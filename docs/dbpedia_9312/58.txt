Aaron Ramsey was born on 26 December 1990 in Caerphilly, Gwent, Wales, UK. He is an actor, known for Tony Tango (2015), UEFA Champions League (1992) and Emirates Cup 2013 (2013).

Football player; who has played for Cardiff City (2007-2008, 2011 - Loan), Arsenal (2008-Present), and Nottingham Forest (2010-2011 - Loan). Ramsey has represented Wales since 2008, and is captain of his country.
