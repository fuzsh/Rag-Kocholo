Cadence Design Systems Inc (Cadence) is a provider of electronic system design software and intellectual property (IP). The company’s product and solution portfolio include electronic design automation software, emulation and prototyping hardware, system interconnect and analysis, verification intellectual property and design intellectual property. It offers tool adoption, design systems and flows, design methodologies, custom PDK development and advanced sip technologies, including 3d-ic, transition to advanced process nodes, advanced functional verification, DFM support and validation, and design team ramp-up services. Cadence’s products and services find application in designing and verifying advanced semiconductors, consumer electronics, networking and telecommunications equipment, and computer systems. The company operates through a network of subsidiaries across Asia-Pacific, Europe, the Americas, Middle East, and Africa. Cadence is headquartered in San Jose, California, the US.
