Justin R. Durban is a Composer for Film and Video Games. He was born on December 7th, 1977 in the small town of Madisonville, Kentucky. He majored in Graphic Design and Art studio with a Theatre minor at the University of Kentucky. His work can best be described as "Upliftingly Dark" Cinematic Film Music with Epic Intimacy. Justin moved to Los Angeles California in July, 2005 to further pursue his career in the music and film production industry.
