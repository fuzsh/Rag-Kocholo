In 2009 Lais Ribeiro began to model locally and appeared in an editorial for Elle Brazil that was photographed by Fabio Bartelt.

In 2010 she signed with Women Management New York and made her debut at the fall show for Rag & Bone in New York. She also walked for Vera Wang, Marc Jacobs, Proenza Schouler, Michael Kors and Ralph Lauren.

Also in 2010 she walked for Alberta Ferretti, Versace, Roberto Cavalli, Bottega Veneta and Dolce & Gabbana in Milan, she walked the fall shows for Jean Paul Gaultier, Christian Dior, John Galliano, Givenchy and Hermes in Paris. She appeared in an editorial form Vogue that was photographed by Steven Meisel, she walked the spring shows for Marc Jacobs, tommy Hilfiger, Gucci, Givenchy and Dolce & Gabbana in New York, Paris and Milan and she walked in the Victoria’s Secret fashion Show.

In 2011, Lais Ribeiro appeared in an editorial for Vogue Spain that was photographed by Victor Demarchelier and appeared in an editorial for Vogue Brazil that was photographed by Renam Christofoletti.

In 2012 she was on the covers of Vogue Brazil and Vogue Germany.

In 2013 she appeared in ad campaigns for Tom Ford, Victoria’s Secret Michael Kors and Nine West. She appeared in editorials for Interview magazine, Flaunt and Ponystep magazine, she was on the covers of Wish Report, Harper’s Bazaar Brazil and L’Officiel and walked for Marc by Marc Jacobs, Helmut Lang, Jean Paul Gaultier and others.

In 2014 Lais Ribeiro appeared in ad campaigns Victoria’s Secret and Ellus, she appeared in editorials for Elle, British Vogue and Elle UK, she was on the covers of Elle Brazil, S Moda for El Pais and Harper’s Bazaar Arabia and walked for Victoria’s Secret, Caroline Herrera, jean Paul Gaultier and others.

In 2015 Lais Ribeiro walked for Cushnie et Ochs, Zac Posen and Carolinas Herrera. She was on the cover of Elle Italia and 10 magazine; she appeared in editorials for Elle Italia, Vogue Brazil, Self magazine and GQ Brazil.
