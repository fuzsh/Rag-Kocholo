Organizations are collecting vast amounts of data about their customers, operations, and financial performance. Bellevue University’s online Data Science Foundations certificate of completion prepares you to be in the driver’s seat to analyze and make relevant discoveries from large data sets.

The graduate certificate program introduces you to the vocabulary, programming languages, big data frameworks, statistics and tools commonly used in the process of data science - for any industry. Some background in math, computing, and statistics is helpful, but no experience is required. Data Science Foundations is a graduate-level certificate comprised of courses from the Master’s in Data Science degree program.
