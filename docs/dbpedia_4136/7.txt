Durham University Oriental Museum, Elvet Hill Road, Durham City, County Durham, UK.

The Oriental Museum, opened in 1960, is part of Durham University. It is open to all Monday - Friday 1000hrs - 1700hrs; Saturday, Sunday, and Bank Holidays 1200hrs - 1700hrs.

The collection contains exhibits from China, Japan, South East Asia, The Near and Middle East, as well as Egypt.

• Limestone Stela of the Steward Dedu (1943 BC) Dedu is pictured in the bottom left corner, larger than the rest, to show his importance. His wife is in front of him. They are both receiving offerings from family members. The inscription invokes gods associated with Abydos, asking for blessings on Dedu.
