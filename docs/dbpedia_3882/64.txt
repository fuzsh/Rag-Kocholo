The Reef: High Tide is the sequel to the highly successful animated film “The Reef (2006)”

Unfortunately, being the only hero in town can take its toll, especially when a group of sharks declares that the end of the reef is soon at hand.

The Reef was released by Warner Bros. UK (9th Feb 2007) box office US$2.5 million with 240 prints. Other territories include Brazil (Playarte), Benelux (Belga), Germany (Fox, Atlas), France (Fox), Spain (Manga), Italy (Mediafilm), Greece (Odeon), Iceland (Sena), Portugal (Lusomundo), Scandinavia (Scanbox), Eastern Europe (Modus Vivendi), Israel (I.L. Films), Middle East (ECS Films), Turkey (Pra Films), Indonesia (Queen Films), Thailand (M Pictures), South Africa (Nu Metro) and The Weinstein Co. for North America and the rest of the World.
