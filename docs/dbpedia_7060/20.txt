Gina Torres (born April 25, 1969) is an American actress. She is best known among dorks as Zoe Washburne from Firefly (also starring Adam Baldwin and Alan Tudyk), and has appeared in many other television series, including Hercules: The Legendary Journeys; its spin-off, Xena: Warrior Princess, Alias, Angel, The Shield, and Suits.

Her voice acting credits include various DC Comics roles such as Vixen from Justice League Unlimited, Wonder Woman from DC Universe Online, and Superwoman from Justice League: Crisis on Two Earths and LEGO DC Super-Villains, Ebony Brown from The Boondocks, and Ketsu Onyo from Star Wars Rebels.
