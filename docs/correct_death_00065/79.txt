Gottfried Wilhelm Leibniz

(1646-1716)

German mathematician and philosopher (1646–1716) – Gottfried Wilhelm Leibniz was born in Leipzig (Largest city in Saxony, Germany) on July 1st, 1646 and died in Hanover (Capital city of the German federated state of Lower Saxony) on November 14th, 1716 at the age of 70. Today Gottfried Wilhelm Leibniz would be 378 years old.
