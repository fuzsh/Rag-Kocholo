Drammen Station (Drammen stasjon) is a railway station located in downtown Drammen in Buskerud, Norway. The station is served by the Oslo Commuter Rail to Oslo, Kongsberg and Eidsvoll, regional trains on the Vestfold Line and express trains to Bergen on the Bergen Line and to Kristiansand on the Sørlandet Line.
