The Simpsons is an American animated sitcom which was created by Matt Groening for the Fox Broadcasting Company. The Simpsons is about an American middle class family called the Simpsons, Homer, Marge, Bart, Lisa and Maggie.

The television season of 2019–2020 saw the beginning of the 31st season of the series.

The series has also spawned a successful film adaption.
