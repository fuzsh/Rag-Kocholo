Poppodium Boerderij is located at Amerikaweg 145, 2717 AV Zoetermeer.

Parking

Free parking is available on Amerikaweg. Keep in mind that you have to walk a few minutes, especially in case of well-attended concerts. If there are no more parking spaces available on Amerikaweg, there are still several parking options on the other side of Randstadrail Station Voorweg (parking Ingenieur Veltplein and parking Bakkerhove 3). To get there, drive past Poppodium Boerderij on Amerikaweg and turn left (underneath the railway) towards Meerzichtlaan. Then take the first left onto the Voorweg. After 450m you will find a spacious parking lot just behind the station. If you walk across the station via the stairs, you will arrive at the entrance of Poppodium Boerderij.

Public transport

Poppodium Boerderij is a one-minute walk from Randstadrail stop Voorweg. Traveling to the Boerderij from The Hague region? From The Hague Central Station you can take both Randstadrail 3 and 4 (get off at the Voorweg low stop). Traveling to the Boerderij by public transport from Utrecht/Gouda? Change at Station Driemanspolder from the train to Randstadrail 3 in the direction of The Hague (get off at Voorweg high stop).
