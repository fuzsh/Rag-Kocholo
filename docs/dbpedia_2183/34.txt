🇬🇧 After last year's epic tour alongside the German band Pabst, DZ Deathrays are returning to Europe, and of course, Prague won't be missing out – catch them at Rock Café on October 29th!

Australian dance-punk trio DZ Deathrays will bring their loud innovative riffs and concentrated dose of youthful energy to Prague. As fierce as ever - even after more than a decade on the scene - you can expect to hear their greatest hits such as "The Mess Up" and "Like People" on the maximum volume. Save the date&don't miss out on these legends from the "land down under"!
