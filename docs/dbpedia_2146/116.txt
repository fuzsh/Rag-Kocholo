M

Mimi Winick posted a condolence

I had the privilege of working with Bennett as a teaching assistant and then in a graduate class at VCU. I was their supervisor and professor, but I learned so much from them, too. Most memorably, I circulated a syllabus for our literary theory course that did not have a unit on the significant field of queer theory. Bennett questioned this, and their questioning led me to change the syllabus-- improving the course for everyone. They were a crucial part of that community in other ways, too. I learned even more about Bennett's talent, integrity, and valuable work from the service today. I send my deepest condolences to their families, born and chosen.
