32nd Medical Brigade Unit Crest (Conservamus Personis)

This product is the unit crest for the 32nd Medical Brigade. The unit's motto, "Conservamus Personis," is displayed on the lower scroll, meaning "We Conserve The Personnel." The maroon color signifies the unit's Medical designation. The torch, derived from the rod of Aesculapius, symbolizes knowledge in the Medical profession. Designed for wear on the Army Dress Blue (ASU) Uniform.
