United States Army 32nd Medical Brigade Unit Crest (Conservamus Personis)

Criteria: This item is the unit crest of the 32nd Medical Brigade. The unit's motto, "Conservamus Personis," is emblazoned across the lower scroll and translates as, "We Conserve The Personnel." The color maroon alludes to the unit's Medical designation. The torch represents knowledge and is adapted from the rod of Aesculapius, a common symbol of the Medical profession. This item is made for wear on the Army Dress Blue (ASU) Uniform.

Note: Sold in pairs (i.e., if you order a quantity of 1 you will receive two Unit Crests).
