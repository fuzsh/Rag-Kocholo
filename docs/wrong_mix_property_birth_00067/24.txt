As of February 2015 the Czech Republic Davis Cup team is number 1 in the world, They have a very strong team with players such as Thomas Berdych. Lately Berdych has been very successful in the Davis cup and also in his grand slam matches including the recent Australian open.

The other players in the team consist of Radek Stepanek, Lukas Rosol and Jiri Vesely that are all very strong players.

The Czech Republic may soon be pushed out of this number 1 position if the Australian team manage to beat them in the first round of the world group Davis Cup competition, although this is unlikely.

The team has a history of some embarrassing losses and some great victory's. They have won several titles but they have also come runners up on two occasions in 1975 and 2009, They are the only team apart from the USA to appear in all but 1 world cup season. The Czech Republic players represented Czechoslovakia until 1992 and then the Czech/Slovak Republics in 1993 due to special circumstances

The Czech Republic Davis Cup team are looking very strong and even without Berdych for some of the 2015 season the team is still expected to be successful, The team is looking like a strong competitor for the title for the third time in four years.
