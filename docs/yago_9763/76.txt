Love at Large

Alan Rudolph (director, screenwriter)

Tom Berenger, Elizabeth Perkins, Anne Archer (starring)

N.p. David Blocker Productions, 1990. Vintage studio still photograph from the 1990 film.

Alan Rudolph puts a modernist spin on the hard-boiled detective film in this underrated gem. Starring Tom Berenger, Elizabeth Perkins, Anne Archer and Kate Capshaw and directed by Alan Rudolph.

Shot on location in Oregon.

8 x 10 inches. Very Good plus, with a small stain to the image and a blue manuscript ink notation to the verso.
