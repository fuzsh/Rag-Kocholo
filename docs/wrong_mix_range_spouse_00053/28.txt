“THE GAMBLER with Adam Kaufman” is Boston’s first and only nightly sports betting show, airing live on WRKO in Boston Monday-Thursday 10 p.m. to 12 a.m. A shortened podcast version of the show is posted following each new episode. Veteran Boston sports personality Adam Kaufman has an eye on all the action, hosting this lively program with a collection of contributors and industry experts. Together, they sweat picks, props, and parlays, monitor line movement, and give you the edge to beat the book. Spread the word and lock “THE GAMBLER with Adam Kaufman” into your daily routine!
