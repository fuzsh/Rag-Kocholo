Todd McCaffrey is the New York Times Bestselling author of over forty books, hailed by some as "A Modern Master Of The Craft." He writes both science fiction and fantasy, including eight in the Dragonriders of Pern (R) universe. the six-book Canaris Rift Series, steampunk Steamworld series, the eight-book LA Witch series, the on-going Cody's War series, and the Twin Soul Series in collaboration with the Winner Twins.
