Hayden Christensen is a Canadian actor and producer best known for portraying Anakin Skywalker/Darth Vader in the Star Wars prequel trilogy films Star Wars: Episode II - Attack of the Clones and Star Wars: Episode III - Revenge of the Sith. He has also starred in various films such as Life as a House, Shattered Glass, Awake, Jumper, Takers and First Kill. Most recently, Christensen can be seen reprising his role as Darth Vader in the Disney+ miniseries Obi-Wan Kenobi, which premiered in May 2022.
