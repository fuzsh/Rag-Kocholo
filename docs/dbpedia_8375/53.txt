Hayden Christensen (born April 19, 1981) is a Canadian actor best known for his portrayal of Anakin Skywalker / Darth Vader in the Star Wars franchise. He appeared in the prequel trilogy films, Star Wars: Episode II – Attack of the Clones (2002) and Star Wars: Episode III – Revenge of the Sith (2005), and made a voice cameo in Star Wars: The Rise of Skywalker (2019). Christensen also reprised his role as Anakin Skywalker / Darth Vader in the Disney+ series Obi-Wan Kenobi (2022) and will appear in the Disney+ series Ahsoka (2023). He gained critical acclaim for his performances in films such as Life as a House (2001) and Shattered Glass (2003).
