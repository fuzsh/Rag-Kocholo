“Friends that don’t accept you for who you are, aren’t really your friend. True friends will never try to change you.” I think this quote goes extremely well in the story “The Soul of Caliban” when Amelie tries to tear apart Caliban and Leon’s relationship. Throughout the story “The soul of Caliban” by Emma Lindsay Squier it is obvious that Amelie tried to change Leon’s beliefs and actions for the worse.

An example of allowing someone to change your beliefs or your actions for the worse is when a women, Amelie, came into Leon's life. Emma Lindsay Squier wrote “”That dog! He is a monster! Never again would I feel my baby is safe!”” Right away Amelie is showing her hatred for Caliban. Leon sees and hears that and that puts Caliban in a bad spot with Leon. This shows that Amelie changes Leon by turning him against Caliban.
