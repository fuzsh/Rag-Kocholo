SQUIER, Emma - Lindsay. Children of the Twilight Folk-Tales of Indian Tribes. New York: Cosmopolitan Book Corporation. 1926.

First Edition. The folk-lore of the Native American collected by Miss Squier, traveling from Alaska to Arizona in her quest. A decade later, Squire would write The Dancing Pirate, basis for the early Rita Hayworth motion picture. Great unsigned wrap-around dustjacket art of Indians at war. Near Fine in Very Good dustjacket, moderate shelf wear and two inch closed tear to front panel.
