Richard K Morgan is a British sci-fi and fantasy writer. He is best known for his novel Altered Carbon, which was later adapted into a successful TV series. He has won a number of awards, including the Philip K. Dick award for his novel Market Forces. His other works include Thirteen, Woken Furies, and Black Man. He has also written several short stories which have been published in anthologies and magazines. An advocate of world-building and gritty science fiction, Richard K Morgan continues to craft ambitious stories and explore complex themes.
