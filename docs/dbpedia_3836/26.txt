Dean Joseph Norris (born April 8, 1963) is an American actor known for his roles in various movies and TV shows. He gained widespread recognition for his portrayal of DEA agent Hank Schrader on the AMC series Breaking Bad (2008–2013) and its spin-off Better Call Saul (2020). Norris also played town councilman James 'Big Jim' Rennie on the CBS series Under the Dome (2013–2015) and mob boss Clay 'Uncle Daddy' Husser on the TNT series Claws (2017–2022). With a career spanning over three decades, Norris has appeared in nearly 50 movies and more than 100 TV shows. Some of his notable film credits include Lethal Weapon 2 (1989), Total Recall (1990), Terminator 2: Judgment Day (1991), and The Book of Henry (2017).
