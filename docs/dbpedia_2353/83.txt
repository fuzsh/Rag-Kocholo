Chennakeshava Temple is one of the beautiful ancient temple situated in Belur, Karnataka. This temple was built by the Hoysala Empire King Vishnuvardhana. Belur was an early Hoysala capital which is now situated in the Hassan district of Karnataka state, India. It is 40 km from Hassan city, and 220 km from Bangalore and Sakaleshpur is 50 km away. Sakleshpur is a popular tourist destination and is known for its lush green hills, trekking trails, and scenic beauty. Chennakesava is a form of the Hindu god Vishnu. Belur is very near to Halebidu which has another beautiful temple built during the rule of the Hoysala dynasty. These temple complexes have been proposed to be listed under UNESCO World Heritage Sites.
