RATP Group (RATP) is a state-owned urban public transport operator in France. The company operates, maintains, and develops the existing public transport network such as rail, bus, metro, cable, and tramway networks in Paris and across the world. RATP offers an automatic shuttle service, which connects the Paris Orly airport to the capital. RATP also operates new transport networks such as electric buses, car-sharing, ride-sharing, and self-driving shuttles and offers to transport on-demand services. The company also develops and maintains public transport systems in Algeria, China, Brazil France, India, Italy, Morocco, Saudi Arabia, South Africa, South Korea, Switzerland, the Philippines, the UK, and the US. RATP is headquartered in Paris, France.
