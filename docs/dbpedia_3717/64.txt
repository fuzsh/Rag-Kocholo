David LaChapelle (born March 11, 1963) is an American fine-art and surrealist photographer, music video director and film director from Hartford, Connecticut, USA. He attended the North Carolina School of the Arts and School of Visual Arts in New York City. LaChapelle did a photoshoot with Lady Gaga in 2009 for Rolling Stone magazine and a picture was taken for the cover for The Remix and the Book of Gaga.
