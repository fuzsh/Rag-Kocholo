Born in Hartford, Connecticut, David LaChapelle ([1]) is an American photographer, music video director and film director. In the 1980s, LaChapelle was affiliated with 303 Gallery. While still in high school, Andy Warhol hired LaChapelle as a photographer for Interview. In 2014, LaChapelle exhibited his series, 'Land Scape' in New York, Vienna, London, and Paris. In 2018, LaChapelle exhibited 10 of his series in one exhibition, Good News For Modern Man, in the Groninger Museum (The Netherlands). David LaChapelle's photography has appeared on the covers and pages of magazines including Details, GQ, i-D, The New York Times Magazine, Rolling Stone, The Face, Vanity Fair, Vogue Italia and Vogue Paris.
