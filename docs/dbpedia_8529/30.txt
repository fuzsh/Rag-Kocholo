A film treatment of Marita Conlon-McKenna's acclaimed novel, Under the Hawthorn Tree, which brilliantly recreated the Great Irish Famine of the 1840s through the story of three young survivors.

Produced by Young Irish Filmmakers, and now available for the first time on DVD, it is divided into four chapters for classroom use.

A study guide to this film and the book is available for free download.

THIS IS A REGION 2 DVD.
