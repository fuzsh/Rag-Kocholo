Biography

Marion Lucile Griffin was born on November 26, 1905, in Waycross, Georgia. In 1910, she is living with her parents and grandmother in Waycross, Georgia. [1]

In 1930, she is living in Manhattan, New York. [2]She married Luigi Criscuolo on July 9, 1932, in Manhattan, New York. [3][4]

She then married William Zeckendorf on December 10, 1940, in Palm Beach, Florida. [5]

She died in an airplane crash on March 5, 1968, in West Indies[6] [7]at the age of 62, and was buried in her hometown.[8]
