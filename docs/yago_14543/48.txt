Arvind Desai Ki Ajeeb Dastaan is a Romantic Family Drama Film focusing on the life of the Multimillionaire- Arvind Desai, living his lavish yet complicated life. He dates some other girl and falls in love with others and marries another- his relationship with the family members is too weird and which leads him to various problems. Director & Writer- Saeed Akhtar Mirza, Cast- Dilip Dhawan, Anjali Paigankar
