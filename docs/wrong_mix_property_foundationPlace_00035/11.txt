(MENAFN- UkrinForm) President Volodymyr Zelensky, visiting the Donetsk region, listened to reports on the operational situation and awarded the military personnel serving in this area.

The Head of State announced this on Telegram , Ukrinform reports.

"Donetsk region. 110th, 31st and 47th Separate Mechanized Brigades. I listened to reports on the operational situation and discussed our actions with the Chief of Staff and brigades. I thanked our soldiers and recognized them with awards. Thank you for defending our country, for what you do every day to ensure that Ukrainians have Ukraine," Zelensky said when presenting the awards.

As reported, on June 26, President Volodymyr Zelensky, together with the Commander-in-Chief of the Armed Forces of Ukraine Oleksandr Syrskyi and the new Commander of the Joint Forces of the Armed Forces of Ukraine Andrii Hnatov, visited the Donetsk region.
