British-born Daphne Heard was an accomplished actress who appeared in many Hollywood films throughout her Hollywood career. Heard began her career with a role in the Judy Geeson suspenseful adaptation "Goodbye Gemini" (1970). Later, she acted in "The First Churchills" (PBS, 1970-71) and "Three Sisters" (1970) with Jeanne Watts. Later in her career, Heard acted on "Jude the Obscure" (PBS, 1971-72). Heard passed away in June 1983 at the age of 79.
