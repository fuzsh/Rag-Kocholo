Biography

William Basie is Notable.

William Basie was a New Jerseyan.

Count Basie was an American jazz pianist, organist, bandleader, and composer. [1]

William James Basie was born in 1904 in Red Bank, New Jersey,[2] the son of Harvey L. and Lillian Basie.

He married in Vivian Lee Winn in Jackson, Missouri in 1930.[3]

He married Catherine Morgan in Seattle, Washington in 1942.[citation needed]

He was 79 when he died in Hollywood, Florida on 26 April 1984.[4][5]

William was a Freemason, Wisdom Lodge No. 102, Chicago, Illinois.

Count Basie was inducted into the Prince Hall Freemason, Wisdom Lodge No. 102 in Chicago, Illinois. [6]

He has a memorial with his wife in Pinelawn Memorial Park in East Farmingdale, New York.[7]
