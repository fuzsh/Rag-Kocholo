Richard Wynn, the Chief Executive Officer of Credit Financier Invest’s (CFI) United Kingdom operations, is leaving the brokerage at the end of November, Finance Magnates has learned.

He joined the brokerage in August 2018 when it extended its global operations by opening a new office in London. Before taking the charge of CFI’s UK operations, he was the company’s branch manager.

Wynn has over 30 years of experience in FX and the trading industry.

“We are grateful for Richard's support as we established CFI UK, and we wish him well for the future,” a company spokesperson told Finance Magnates.

An FX Industry Veteran

Prior to joining CFI, Wynn was heading a sports management consultancy and also was a consultant at Sovereign Recruitment Solutions - two of his experiences outside the FX industry.
