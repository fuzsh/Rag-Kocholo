Sir James Francis Garrick, 1875.

Born 10 January 1836 in London and christened James Francis Gowin. Parents James Francis Gowin and Catherine Branson came to Australia and changed their surname to Garrick. Politician and Agent-General. James, like his brother, was articled to a Sydney solicitor. He was admitted to practice in December 1860. He was appointed C.M.G. in 1885 and K.C.M.G. in 1886. Died 12 January 1907, survived by his wife Catherine (nee Cadell), whom he married on 3 January 1865. (Description supplied with photograph.).
