Joan Anton Maragall, gallerist in Sala Parés since 1989 and Galería Trama since its creation in 1991, in Barcelona. President of the galleries association Art Barcelona since 2013 and member of the MACBA General Council since 2015. Between 1983 and 1989 he was Director of the Press Association of Barcelona, the Association of Journalists of Catalonia and the International Press Center of Barcelona. He was also President of the Union of Art Galleries Associations of Spain between 1995 and 1998; President of the Circle of the Liceu between 2002 and 2004 and at present is part of the patronage of different foundations. Graduated by ESADE and with studies in History of Art, he has taught in master courses in Faculties of History and Fine Arts.
