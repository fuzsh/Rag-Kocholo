MFE controls Mediaset S.p.A. and Grupo Audiovisual Mediaset España Comunicación SAU and is the largest shareholder of the German broadcaster ProsiebenSat1. MFE-MEDIAFOREUROPE is listed on the Euronext Milan market operated by Borsa Italiana S.p.A. and on the Spanish Stock Exchanges (Madrid, Barcelona, Bilbao, and Valencia).

Mediaset is the holding company of the Italian companies active in the media and communication sectors. It specialises in free-to-air production and distribution across multiple platforms, as well as in the production of films, multimedia content and advertising sales.

Grupo Audiovisual Mediaset España Comunicación is a wholly owned subsidiary of MFE-MEDIAFOREUROPE following the effectiveness of the cross-border merger by absorption of Mediaset España Comunicación, S.A., as absorbed company, with and into MFE-MEDIAFOREUROPE N.V. It is the leading company in the media sector in Spain. The largest in terms of audience, linear and non-linear broadcasting, market share and advertising revenues, and among the country’s main content creators and producers.
