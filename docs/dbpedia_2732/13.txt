Mediaset Espana Comunicacion SA (Mediaset Espana), a subsidiary of MediaForEurope N.V., is a broadcasting and communication media group. The company broadcasts audiovisual contents through television and internet platforms. It also provides services such as television broadcasting, in-house television programs and film production. Mediaset Espana sells advertising on the television channels and provides services such as audiovisual production, news agency, advertising promotion, OTT and online video advertising. It operates various television channels including Telecinco, Cuatro, Factoria de Ficcion, Boing, Divinity, Energy and BeMad providing broadcast programming services to its customers. The company provides services through its streaming contents platform Mitele which offers TV programs, movies and sports programs. Mediaset Espana is headquartered in Madrid, Spain.
