Gangolli Ramashet Nagaraj, popularly known as Shringar Nagaraj, was an Indian actor, cameraman, and producer in Kannada cinema. He is best known for the 1987 silent film Pushpaka Vimana, which won the National Film Award for Best Popular Film Providing Wholesome Entertainment.

Shringar Nagaraj was born on July 16, 1939, in Bengaluru, Karnataka, India. His birth geographical coordinates are 12° 58’ 19” North latitude and 77° 35’ 37” East longitude, with an altitude of 920 meters above sea level. Shringar Nagaraj passed away at the age of 73 in 2013.

Astrologically, Shringar Nagaraj falls under the Sun sign of Cancer, with Libra as his Ascendant, Cancer as his Moon sign, and Aquarius as his Black Moon Lilith sign.
