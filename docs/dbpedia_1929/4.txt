Sweden win U19 European Championships

Stina Blackstenius inspired Sweden to WU19 EURO glory on Monday beating Spain by 3-1 to take the trophy. Blackstenius scored two first half goals at a time when Spain were dominating possession and set up a late one to seal the game and win Sweden the title.

Blacksenius also claimed for herself the top scorer trophy with 6 goals in four games at the tournament, bringing her tally for the season to 20 in 10 games.
