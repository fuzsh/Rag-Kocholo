The Adventures of Rocky and Bullwinkle Show is a former live stage show at Universal Studios Florida that opened in 1992, based on the cartoon TV series of the same name. The show featured many characters from the TV series including Rocky, Bullwinkle, Boris, Natasha and Snidley Whiplash. The sixteen minute show then permanently closed and got removed in 1993 in Florida and 1996 in Hollywood and got replaced by StarToons.

Cast[]

June Foray as Rocket J. Squirrel, Natasha Fatale

Keith Scott as Bullwinkle J. Moose, Boris Badenov, Dudley Do-Right

Corey Burton as Narrator and Snidely Whiplash
