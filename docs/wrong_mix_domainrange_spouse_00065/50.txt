Actor Stephen Dunham has died, aged 48.

Dunham passed away on September 14 after suffering a heart attack, reports Variety.

The actor is best known for his work on NBC sitcom DAG.

He also appeared on television shows What I Like About You, Just Shoot Me and Hot in Cleveland, as well as in movies like Savages, Get Smart, Monster-in-Law and Catch Me If You Can.

Dunham can also be seen in the upcoming Paranormal Activity 4.

He is survived by wife and actress Alexondra Lee.
