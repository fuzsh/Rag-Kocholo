Stephen Dunham, an actor who appeared in numerous television series, and had regular roles on the comedies Oh, Grow Up and Hot Properties, died September 14, 2012, in Burbank, California. The date of his passing was his 48th birthday.

Dunham, along with his wife, actress Alexondra Lee, whom he met while both were working on Oh, Grow Up, will appear as husband and wife in the upcoming feature film Paranormal Activity 4.

Dunham's other notable TV credits included such series as What I Like About You, DAG, The Bill Engvall Show, True Jackson, VP and Hot in Cleveland.
