Biography

Alexondra Lee is an American actress. She was married to actor Stephen Dunham until his death in September 2012. She worked with him in Paranormal Activity 4.

She was born in Pennsylvania to parents Mae and Harry Lee. Lee studied ballet at the age of four and began dancing with the New York City Ballet at the age of seven with whom she performed in The Nutcracker Suite.

She starred in the short-lived Special Unit 2 as Detective Kate Benson and had a regular role as Callie on Party of Five. In 2006, Lee appeared as one of five corpses recounting the tales of their death on a CSI: Crime Scene Investigation episode entitled “Toe Tags”.

Lee was ranked No. 81 on Stuff Magazine’s 101 “Most Beautiful Women in The World” in 2001. She had a role in Paranormal Activity 4 as Holly Nelson.
