Reservist to be on 'State'

The next edition of the public-TV show The State of Ohio will feature an interview with state Rep. Danny Bubp, R-West Union, who is in Iraq.

Bubp is a colonel in the Marine Corps Reserve and is on his first tour in Iraq. He is serving as a liaison to the provisional governor of Al-Anbar province.

The show will air at 5:30 p.m. Friday on WOSU-TV (Channel 34) and at 12:30 p.m. Sunday on WOUB-TV (Channel 20) in

Athens.

Special to explore rock history

WODB (107.9 FM) will broadcast a 52-hour music special, The History of Rock and Roll, beginning at 6 a.m. Thursday.

The special, which covers rock 'n' roll from its inception in the 1950s through the early 1980s, features interviews, audio clips and a show-ending musical montage.

The program will air from 6 a.m. to 7 p.m. Thursday through Saturday and 11 a.m. to midnight Sunday.

TV sets beat online viewing

While watching television online is growing, 94 percent of adults who subscribe to a cable or satellite service prefer to watch shows on traditional sets rather than online, according to a study from Nielsen Media Research and the Cable & Telecommunications Association for Marketing.
