About Ubisoft:

Ubisoft is a leading producer, publisher and distributor of interactive entertainment products worldwide. Ubisoft has offices in 21 countries and distributes its products in more than 50 countries around the globe. To learn more, please visit www.ubisoftgroup.com

About the Ubisoft studio Blue Byte:

Blue Byte is one of the pioneers of the German gaming industry. The company was founded in 1988 and has since then focussed on the development of entertainment software. Blue Byte created two of the most famous series in the history of PC games in Germany: The Settlers® series and the Battle Isle saga. The Settlers - a highly acclaimed strategic simulation game - sold more than 5 million copies worldwide. In the summer of 2003, the series celebrated its 10th anniversary. Today, Blue Byte is a 100% subsidiary of Ubisoft and, as a development studio, produces the current games in the series The Settlers®. For more information, please visit www.bluebyte.de
