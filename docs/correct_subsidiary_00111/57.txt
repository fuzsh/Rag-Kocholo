Agile Software Corp. (AGIL) on Friday revealed that its stockholders voted to adopt a merger agreement pursuant to which a wholly-owned subsidiary of Oracle Corp. (ORCL) will be merged into the company, making Agile a wholly-owned subsidiary of Oracle. The company noted that over 74% of the Agile shares outstanding were cast in favor of the merger agreement. According to the company, the merger agreement, was initially signed on May 15, 2007, and has already obtained all U.S. and foreign governmental regulatory approvals.

The deal, values Agile at $8.10 per share, and represents a value of $495 million, the company noted.
