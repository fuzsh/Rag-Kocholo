The climate of Zagreb in july is good

The climate in july in Zagreb is wet (with 110mm of rainfall over 6 days). The weather is better from the previous month since in june it receives an average of 135mm of rainfall over 8 days.

The climate good there the month of july. Temperatures are up to 29°C. The seasonal minimum is 21°C. Thus, the mean temperature average in july in Zagreb is 25°C. Note that these seasonal averages are in contrast with those observed in Zagreb in july with a maximum record of 37°C in 2013 and a minimum record of 12°C in 2014. You can hope to have about 12 days with temperatures over 30°C, or 39 of time.

In july in Zagreb, the days on average last 15:22. The sun rises at 04:21 and sunset is at 19:43.
