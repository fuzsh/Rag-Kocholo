Aster is a new single family home development by Coventry Homes currently under construction at 17 Street Northwest & 16 Avenue Northwest, Edmonton. Aster unit sizes start at 2351 square feet.

Aster is an exciting new community in southeast Edmonton, conveniently located just minutes from Anthony Henday Drive. As the community takes shape, its inviting trails, green spaces, and landscaping will reflect its flower-inspired name.
