Aster is a new single-family home community by Pacesetter Homes, currently under construction at 1611 14 Street Northwest, Edmonton. The available units are priced between $489,997 and $739,998, with sizes ranging from 1553 to 2812 square feet. There are 15 quick move-in homes currently for sale.

Named after Alberta's vibrant native flower, Aster is an ideal location for your dream home. This master-planned community features beautifully landscaped green spaces and multi-use trails for all ages. Conveniently located in Southeast Edmonton, Aster provides easy access to shopping, recreation, and schools, making it a perfect choice for families and individuals alike.
