Aster is an exciting new community in southeast Edmonton, conveniently located just minutes from Anthony Henday Drive.

Named after the vibrant flower native to Alberta, Aster is a natural choice for the location of your dream home. This master planned community will offer landscaped green spaces and multi-use trails for your enjoyment. And, when it comes to shopping, recreation and schools, Aster’s convenient location in Southeast Edmonton gives you easy access to everything you need.
