Kahur Abbas (Persian: كهورعباس‎, also Romanized as Kahūr ʿAbbās) is a village in Marz Rural District, Chah Dadkhoda District, Qaleh Ganj County, Kerman Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 36, in 9 families.[1]
