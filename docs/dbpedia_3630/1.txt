Ganj Konar (Persian: گنج كنار‎, also Romanized as Ganj Konār) is a village in Rameshk Rural District, Chah Dadkhoda District, Qaleh Ganj County, Kerman Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 38, in 8 families.[1]
