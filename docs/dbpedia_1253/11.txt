universal media transcoder MediaCoder is a universal batch media transcoder that integrates most popular audio/video codecs and enables you to convert between most audio and video formats without having to install additional codecs. Among other things, it supports conversion for portable devices as well as ripping from audio and video discs and repairing corrupted or partial downloaded video files. MediaCoder also includes MPlayer for video playback. The program is optimized for multi-core processors.MediaCoder is best suited for advanced users that are familiar with media formats and encoding options. 64-bit only Copyright Snapfiles.com
