Vittoria Francesca of Savoy (Maria Vittoria Francesca; 9 February 1690 – 8 July 1766) was a legitimised daughter of Victor Amadeus II of Sardinia, first king of the House of Savoy. Married to the head of a cadet branch of the House of Savoy, she is an ancestor of the kings of Sardinia and of the Savoy kings of Italy.

Marriage[edit]

At the Castle of Moncalieri, on November 7, 1714, Victoria Francisca married Victor Amadeus, Prince of Carignano, son of Emmanuel Philibert, 2nd Prince of Carignano and Maria Angela Catherine d'Este.

Issue[edit]

Prince Joseph Victor Amadeus of Savoy (1716–1716)

Princess Anna Teresa of Savoy (1 November 1717 – 5 April 1745) married Charles de Rohan, Prince of Soubise and had issue.

Prince Louis Victor of Savoy, Prince of Carignan (25 September 1721 – 16 December 1778) married Princess Christine of Hesse-Rotenburg and had issue.

Prince Victor Amadeus of Savoy (1722) died young.

A stillborn daughter (24. March 1729)
