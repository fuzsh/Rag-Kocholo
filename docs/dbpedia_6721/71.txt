SonicWall is an American technology company that specializes in network security and internet content control. It was founded in 1991 and is headquartered in San Jose, California. SonicWall offers a diverse range of security solutions, including firewall, VPN, and email security. The company’s products are designed to keep businesses protected against a variety of threats, such as malware, phishing, and spam. SonicWall’s solutions employ advanced technologies to safeguard networks and data, including deep packet inspection, threat prevention, and cloud-based sandboxing. The company services different industries such as healthcare, education, and government. Its global customer base includes small and medium-sized businesses, enterprises, and service providers. In 2016, SonicWall separated from its parent company Dell Technologies and became an independent cybersecurity company, backed by private equity firm Francisco Partners and hedge fund Elliot Management. The company continues to evolve its offerings and expand its global footprint, with offices in North America, Europe, and Asia.
