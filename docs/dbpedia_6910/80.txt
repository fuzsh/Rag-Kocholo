Bernd Eichinger (April 11, 1949 - January 24, 2011) was a German producer, director and screenwriter who co-produced The NeverEnding Story alongside Dieter Geissler.

Trivia[]

In the 1980s, Eichinger was in a relationship with Jane Seitz, who edited The NeverEnding Story. Seitz committed suicide in 1988.

Eichinger died from a heart attack in 2011 at the age of 61.
