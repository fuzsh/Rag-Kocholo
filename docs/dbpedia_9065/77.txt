Naughty By Nature is an American hip-hop group from East Orange, New Jersey. The group consists of Treach (Anthony Criss), Vin Rock (Vincent Brown), and DJ Kay Gee (Kevan Hall). They are known for their energetic live performances, their signature East Coast hip-hop sound, and their numerous hits, including "O.P.P.," "Hip Hop Hooray," and "All 4 One."

The group's history dates back to the late 1980s when Treach and Vin Rock met at East Orange High School. They began rapping together and eventually formed the group, adding DJ Kay Gee to the lineup. They released their debut album, "Naughty By Nature," in 1991. The album was a critical and commercial success, spawning the hit single "O.P.P." (Other People's Property). The song became a cultural phenomenon, reaching the top 10 on the Billboard Hot 100 and solidifying Naughty By Nature's place in hip-hop history.

Their second album, "1995," was even more successful, reaching platinum status and featuring the hit singles "Hip Hop Hooray" and "All 4 One." "Hip Hop Hooray" became a hip-hop anthem, and "All 4 One" was a smooth ballad that showcased the group's versatility. These two albums established Naughty By Nature as one of the leading hip-hop groups of the 1990s.
