Naughty By Nature is an American hip-hop trio from East Orange, New Jersey, formed in 1986. The group consists of Treach, Vin Rock, and DJ Kay Gee. They gained mainstream success with their hit single 'O.P.P.' in 1991. They are known for their energetic performances and significant contributions to the hip-hop genre.

Their lyrics often celebrate hip-hop culture and emphasize the importance of unity within the community. They frequently address themes of struggling for recognition in the music industry, resonating with many aspiring artists. Over the years, their work has continued to highlight the strength and resilience of the hip-hop community.
