Profile of Kelly Clarkson

Kelly Clarkson is an American singer-songwriter, actress, author and television personality who rose to fame after winning the first season of series American Idol. Over the course of her career, Clarkson has released 8 studio albums and 4 EPs. Some of her most popular singles include "Before Your Love", "Miss Independent", "Breakaway", "Since U Been Gone", "Behind These Hazel Eyes", "Because of You", "Stronger (What Doesn't Kill You)", and "Heartbeat Song".
