The Centerville Public Library is Centerville, Indiana's library for the town of Centerville and surrounding communities. According to their website, it is the mission of the Centerville-Center Township Public Library to provide traditional library services, current information, up-to-date technology, and other materials necessary for the entertainment and lifelong learning of the patrons they serve.
