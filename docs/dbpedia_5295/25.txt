Na’ima B. Robert is a speaker, coach and best-selling author of ‘From My Sisters’ Lips’, as well as over 25 other books that have been translated into over 14 languages. She is the recipient of several literary awards and is a respected author of diverse fiction and the author of Show Up: A Motivational Message for Muslim Women.

She was the founder of SISTERS Magazine and Editor-in Chief for ten years. She has appeared on BBC's Newsnight, GMTV and on several BBC radio stations. Her work has been published on Thrive Global, in The Guardian, The Times and The Telegraph. She has been profiled in The Independent and Stylist Magazine and has been a regular guest on a variety of TV stations.
