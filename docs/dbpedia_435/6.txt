The Captains Regent are indirectly elected by the Grand and General Council and serve a 6-month term. There is no absolute term limit, but Captains Regent must not serve an additional term within three years of the prior term.

All 60 members of the Grand and General Council are directly elected by list proportional representation vote in up to two rounds. All members serve unlimited 5-year terms.

Seats won in last election: 18 out of 60 (10 Libera and 8 PS-PSD)

Seats won in last election: 0 out of 60 (new party)

Gender Quota: On each party or coalition list, candidates of the same gender may not take up more than two-thirds of list entries (Legge 31 gennaio 1996 no. 6, Art. 15.1).
