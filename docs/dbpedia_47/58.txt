Diktel is the headquarters of Khotang District, a part of Sagarmatha Zone, Nepal. At the time of the 1991 Nepal census it had a population of 7,324 persons residing in 1,433 individual households.[1]

The town is located at and has an altitude of 1,530 metres (5,020 ft). It contains a notable jail.[2] During the Civil War the town was attacked by hundreds of Maoists rebels who also broke into Diktel jail and freed inmates[3]
