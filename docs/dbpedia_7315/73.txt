The Renault Group is a French multinational automobile manufacturer that produces a range of automotive vehicles, ranging from: cars, trucks, tractors, tanks, buses/coaches, aircraft and aircraft engines, and autorail vehicles.

Stratasys' Justin Cunningham had the chance to speak with their Additive Manufacturing Process Specialist, Mélanie Chevé, at Formnext 2023 to discuss how additive manufacturing has helped their company improve.
