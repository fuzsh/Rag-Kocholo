What is Ben Tackie's boxing record?

In a professional boxing career spanning 21 years and four months, Ben Tackie fought 44 times with 31 wins and 13 losses.

Tackie retired from professional boxing with a record of 31-13. He was a one-time super-lightweight world title challenger. He had a 40.9% knockouts-to-fights ratio with 18 of his total 44 fights being knockout wins. The only knockout loss in Tackie's career was a 4th round TKO loss to Ramon Alvarez, which was not a world title fight.

His last fight was a 4th round TKO defeat to Ramon Alvarez on October 10, 2015.
