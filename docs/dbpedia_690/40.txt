About Silapathar Na Gaon

According to Census 2011 information the location code or village code of Silapathar Na Gaon village is 289113. Silapathar Na Gaon village is located in Sissibargaon subdivision of Dhemaji district in Assam, India. It is situated 1km away from sub-district headquarter Sissibargaon (tehsildar office) and 39km away from district headquarter Dhemaji. As per 2009 stats, Silapathar is the gram panchayat of Silapathar Na Gaon village.

The total geographical area of village is 82.52 hectares. Silapathar Na Gaon has a total population of 268 peoples, out of which male population is 135 while female population is 133. Literacy rate of silapathar na gaon village is 84.33% out of which 86.67% males and 81.95% females are literate. There are about 61 houses in silapathar na gaon village. Pincode of silapathar na gaon village locality is 787059.

Silapathar is nearest town to silapathar na gaon for all major economic activities, which is approximately 3km away.
