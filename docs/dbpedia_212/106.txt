The Black Crowes, a renowned rock band from the late 1980s, is synonymous with soulful, blues-infused rock and roll.

The Black Crowes were an R&B rock band that broke through in the early 90s with their debut album Shake Your Money Maker. Rolling Stone’s David Fricke described it as “a guitar-party cracker that marries white Southern R&B crunch and Anglo cock-strutting attitude in the beloved early-Seventies manner of the Faces and the Rolling Stones,” and the record went on to sell millions of copies.

The Crowes’ style was stripped-down, classic rock ’n’ roll. They wowed crowds in the US and Europe in 1990 and 1991 as support acts for Aerosmith, Robert Plant, ZZ Top, Heart, and more.
