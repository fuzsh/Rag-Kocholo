Persson’s cabin - Persson's cabin offers two four-bed rooms (Quadruple Rooms) and one Double Room. Amenities include garden furniture and a nice big lawn for the kids. There is also a children's playground and playhouse close by.

Elsa’s Barn - The barn features four Standard Double Rooms where pets are welcome. All room have their own external entrance. There is also a hayloft and family studios furnished with five beds on two levels. On the lower level there is a sofa bed for two persons. On the top level there is a double bed in addition to a single bed and a toilet and shower.

Leksand’s Cabin - This charming cabin has its own patio and two separate Standard Double Rooms. The cabin is perfectly suited for families or groups of friends. Private terrace in the summer.

Activities

For families with young children the lakeside area offers a range of leisure activities, including Leksand Sommarland (amusement park), Orsa Grönklitt Björnpark (bear safari park) and Tomteland (Christmas elf land) in Mora.
