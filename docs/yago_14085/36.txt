Hiten Tejwani's love for his wife Gauri Pradhan knows no bounds. The actor is head over heels in love with his better half and can go to any extent to make her feel special. In fact, Hiten and Gauri are touted to be one of the most adorable couples in the television industry. The couple has been married for over a decade now and they are just going from strength to strength. And they are proud parents to two children - Neevan Tejwani and Katya Tejwani.
