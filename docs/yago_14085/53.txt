Gauri Pradhan Tejwani – Gauri is a former Indian model. She is a television actress who appeared in several popular tv serials during early 2000s. She is married to television actor Hiten Tejwani. Gauri made a comeback with Meri Aashqui Tum Se Hi, once again under Balaji Telefilms. She played a pivotal role of Falguni, lead protogonist Ishani's mother.Gauri is currently playing the role of ‘Anita’ on the show ‘Tu Aashiqui’.
