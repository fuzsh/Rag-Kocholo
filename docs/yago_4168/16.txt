From Brownlow's 'The Parade's Gone By':

"Nalpas liked the next scenario (Les Morts Reviennent-ils? was its original title) and he gave Gance five thousand francs to cover everything-his fee, film stock, sets, costumes, and actors.

"It's not much," said Gance, feeling somewhat alarmed. "We haven't got enough money," replied Nalpas. "It's all I can do for you." Gance completed the film, finally titled Un Drame au Château d'Acre, in five days flat.
