The Adventures of Rocky and Bullwinkle is a 2000 live-action/CGI hybrid film featuring characters of The Rocky and Bullwinkle Show. The film stars Robert De-Niro, Renee Russo, and Piper Pearbo. It is the third Jay Ward feature film, and the first Rocky and Bullwinkle installment to be produced in the 21st century.

Synopsis[]

"Rocky the Flying Squirrel and his erudite friend Bullwinkle J Moose are living in the now desolate Frostbite Falls, barely surviving on diminishing residual checks from re-runs of their old television series. Meanwhile, back in Pottsylvania, Fearless Leader and his notorious spies Boris Badenov and Natasha Fatale have escaped their cartoon existences and are reborn as characters in flesh and blood."
