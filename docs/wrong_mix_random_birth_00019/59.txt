Tasha Wilson Wins AR Milken Award

Kingsland Elementary School | Kingsland, AR | Nov 30, 2018

Arkansas U.S. Senator John Boozman congratulates Tasha Wilson on her Milken Educator Award. "You are someone we can all look up to, admire, and follow your example," he says to the recipient. "You are receiving this honor, but you also represent all of your fellow teachers in Arkansas."

Background, from left: Arkansas Commissioner of Education Johnny Key; recipient Tasha Wilson; and Cleveland County School District Superintendent Craig Dupuy.

In this photo: Tasha Wilson (AR '18)
