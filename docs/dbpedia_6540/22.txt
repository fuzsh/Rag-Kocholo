Most important cities in the region »Cibao«

The region has a west-east extension of about 250 km and over 120 km from north to south.

It consists of the 14 provinces Dajab, Duarte, Espaillat, Hermanas Mirabal, La Vega, Mar, Monse, Monte Cristi, Puerto Plata, S, Saman, Santiago, Santiago Rodr and Valverde.
