Kenneth James Gillespie is currently the Chairman at the White Ribbon Foundation and the Chairman at White Ribbon Australia.

He is also an Independent Non-Executive Director at Senetas Corp.

Ltd.

Mr. Gillespie is a Director at the Australian Drug Foundation, the Australian Strategic Policy Institute, The Foundation for Post-Acute & Long-Term Care Medicine, Inc., and the Anzac Research Institute.

Additionally, he serves as a Director at Airbus Australia Pacific Ltd., Naval Group Australia Pty Ltd., and Three Stables Pty Ltd.

Previously, Mr. Gillespie was the Chairman at XReality Group Ltd.

from 2015 to 2018.

He also held the position of Independent Non-Executive Director at Vitalharvest Freehold Trust.
