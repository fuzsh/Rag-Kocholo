Ken Gillespie is the Head of Sales at Esri Australia, the exclusive local distributor of Esri's GIS software. Ken is also a Trustee at CEDA, a national organization focusing on economic development. Previously, Ken held roles such as Commercial Director at DriveYello, Commercial Manager at nearmap.com, and National Sales Manager at APN News & Media. Ken has a background in sales and management, with experience in diverse industries such as technology, media, and printing.
