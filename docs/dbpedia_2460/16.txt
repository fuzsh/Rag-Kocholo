Kenneth Gillespie biography

Lt. Gen. (Retd.) Kenneth James Gillespie, AC DSC CSM , is Non-Executive Independent Director of Senetas Corporation Limited since April 30, 2013. He retired from the Army in June 2011 after a distinguished 43 year career. He had a career recognized by his appointment as Vice Chief of the Defence Force/Chief of Joint Operations and Chief of Army. He has had considerable overseas experience and developed a network of contacts in the defence, security and diplomatic fields. He is currently the part-time Defence Adviser to the New South Wales Government; a Director of the boards of Australian Aerospace Limited, Indoor Skydive Australia Group (Chairman), the White Ribbon Australia (Chairman), and the Australian Drugs Foundation.
