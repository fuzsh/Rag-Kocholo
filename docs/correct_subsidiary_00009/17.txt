A big day Monday for Siebel Systems: The e-business software maker announced plans to buy a privately held e-commerce software company on the same day it announced a 2-for-1 stock split. After the market closed, Siebel said it would buy OnLink Technologies for 3,706,564 shares of stock. At Monday's closing price of 164 3/16 for Siebel, the deal was worth about $609 million. Siebel said the stock split would be payable Sept. 8 to shareholders of record on Aug. 18. Full story. -- Sergio G. Non, ZDNet Inter@ctive Investor
