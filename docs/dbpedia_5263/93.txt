Pentair is a manufacturing company that delivers smart and sustainable water solutions, empowering its customers “to make the most of life’s essential resources.” The company is made up of more than 11,000 team members spread across six continents and over 20 countries. To join its team, Pentair looks for individuals who align with its values of adaptability, innovation, putting customers first, accountability, integrity, respect, teamwork, and positive energy. In the past, the company has supported flexible work arrangements by hiring for hybrid and 100% remote jobs based throughout the U.S. Past opportunities include flexible product jobs, sales jobs, and technical support jobs, to name a few. Eligible employees also receive access to benefits, like training and development opportunities, reward and recognition programs, health coverage, paid parental leave, disability protection, and more.

Since its foundation in 1966, Pentair has been working to globally deliver water solutions for commercial, residential, industrial, municipal, and agricultural markets, including filtration systems, flow technologies, and pools and spas that provide clean and safe water. In addition, the company provides technical products for residential and business needs. Pentair is also committed to helping support water conservation awareness through the Pentair Foundation, which provides education and resources in the communities the company serves.
