Justin Moore is an American country music singer and songwriter born on March 30, 1984, in Poyen, Arkansas. He moved to Nashville, Tennessee, to pursue his music career and signed with Big Machine Records in 2008. Known for his traditional country sound, some of his major hits include 'Small Town USA' and 'If Heaven Wasn't So Far Away.'

Moore's lyrics often explore themes of unyielding commitment and personal spiritual redemption. His songs frequently reflect on steadfast loyalty and the journey toward personal growth and faith. These themes resonate deeply with his audience, contributing to the authenticity and emotional depth of his music.
