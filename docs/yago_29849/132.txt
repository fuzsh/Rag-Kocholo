Articles English: Ferdinand I of Aragon

Fernando I the Just, the Honest Trastámara of Aragon, King of Aragon, Count of Barcelona, King of Valencia, King of Sardinia and Corsica, King of Majorca, Count of Roussillon, Count of Cerdagne, King of Sicily, was born 27 November 1380 in Medina del Campo, Castile and León, Spain to John I of Castile (1358-1390) and Eleanor of Aragon (1358-1382) and died 2 April 1416 Igualada, Catalonia, Spain of unspecified causes. He married Eleanor de Alburquerque (1374-1435) 1393 JL .
