Andrew Kooman is the writer of the human trafficking thriller She Has A Name, now available on Prime, the historical drama We Are the Body, and is the producer of the series Dream, Breath of Life, and MAiD in Canada which stream on various platforms, including UnveilTV.com.

An official author with the Skit Guys, Andrew regularly makes people laugh in church. Andrew writes a popular weekly newsletter called Things I Wrote Down which you can subscribe to for free at andrewkooman.com.
