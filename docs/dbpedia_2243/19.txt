Under the deal, KazMunaiGaz will transport the natural gas produced by CNPC from the Aktobe oilfield via phase two of the Kazakhstan-China Gas Pipeline, in addition to supplying 5 billion cubic metres of natural gas to the pipeline, as agreed earlier.

Meanwhile, the two companies will jointly develop the Urikhtau gas condensate field.

KazMunaiGaz is expected to export 5 Bcm to 10 Bcm of gas per year to China after meeting demand from Kazakhstan's southern regions, which makes it necessary to build the second phase of the Sino-Kazakhstan Gas Pipeline.
