Timbaland, born Timothy Zachery Mosley, is an American record producer, rapper, and songwriter known for his innovative production work. Emerging in the late 1990s, he has collaborated with a variety of top artists, significantly shaping the sound of modern hip-hop and R&B. Notable hits include 'Cry Me A River' with Justin Timberlake and 'The Way I Are' featuring Keri Hilson.

Timbaland's lyrics often reflect his self-assurance and confidence in his craft, celebrating his achievements within the music industry. His songs also delve into themes of irreparable relationship damage and the sincerity of late apologies, offering a nuanced look at personal and professional rivalries.
