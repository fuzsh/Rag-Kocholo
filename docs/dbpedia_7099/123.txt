About OrangeScape

OrangeScape is a software platform company that has been in the forefront of democratization of IT using cloud services, through its SaaS platform for business process & workflow automation, KiSSFLOW. Gartner and Forrester recently listed OrangeScape as one of the Top 10 Platform-as-a-Service (PaaS) companies. KiSSFLOW is used by 10,000+ customers across 121 countries, including over fifty Fortune 500 companies.

OrangeScape was founded on the belief that simple things should stay simple and complex things should be possible. The company has operations in Mountain View, USA & Chennai, India. OrangeScape was named a Top IT Innovator in India by NASSCOM.
