'Poincaré (geb. 1860) und Zar Nikolaus II. 1868-1918.', 1934. Raymond Poincaré (1860-1934), French statesman who served three times as 58th Prime Minister of France, and as tenth President of France from 1913 to 1920 with Nicholas II (1868-1918), the last Emperor of Russia, ruling from 1 November 1894 until his forced abdication on 15 March 1917. From Die Großen der Weltgelchichte. [Ecktein-Halpaus, Dresden, 1934]. Artist Unknown. (Photo by The Print Collector/Getty Images)
