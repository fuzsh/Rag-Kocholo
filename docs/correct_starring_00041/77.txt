John Paul Cusack (born June 28, 1966) is an American film actor and screenwriter. He is well known for his roles as Lloyd Dobler in Say Anything, as Roy Dillon in Gifters, as Martin Q. Blank in Grosse Pointe Blank, as Vince Larkin in Con Air, as Craig Schwartz in Being John Malkovich, as Rob Gordon in High Fidelity, among many others. Two of his four siblings: Joan Cusack and Ann Cusack are also busy actors. He portrayed Jackson Curtis in the film, 2012.
