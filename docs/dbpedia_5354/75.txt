Donald Grant built quite a career for himself as a vibrant source of comedy, starring in many films throughout his Hollywood career. Grant began his career with roles in "Mistress" (1987-88), "Monster in the Closet" (1987) and the comedy "Surrender" (1987) with Sally Field. Later, he acted in "Dear John" (1988-1992), "L.A. Takedown" (NBC, 1988-89) and "The Fresh Prince of Bel-Air" (1990-96). More recently, Grant worked on the Robert De Niro action picture "15 Minutes" (2001).
