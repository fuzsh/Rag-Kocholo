Biography

Tony Orbison was born in Tennessee.

Tony Orbison died young.

Anthony King Orbison was born on 29 June 1962, in Nashville, Davidson County, Tennessee, United States. His parents were Roy Orbison (age 26) and Claudette Frady (age 20).

Anthony (Tony) died in a fire at his father's home in Hendersonville, Tennessee, on September 14, 1968. His older brother, Roy DeWayne also perished in the blaze, while one brother, Wesley, escaped without harm. Their father had been touring in England and their paternal grandparents, Orbie and NadineOrbison had been caring for the boys in the Hendersonville home. They also escaped the fire. [1]

Anthony King Orbison died on 14 September 1968, in Hendersonville, Sumner, Tennessee, United States, at the age of 6. He was mentioned on a memorial in Woodlawn Memorial Park and Mausoleum, Nashville, Davidson County, Tennessee, United States.[2]
