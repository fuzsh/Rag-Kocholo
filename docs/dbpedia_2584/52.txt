Amr Saleem Kashmiri is a Pakistani actor. He started his film career(as a lead) in super hi Palistani movie "BOL", an Urdu-language social issue drama by Shoaib Mansoor, established a new box office record in Pakistani cinema. In August 2011 BOL was released in the US. Kashmiri's beautifully underplayed performance of Saifi's character received many accolades, including "Best New Talent" award at this year's London Asian Film Festival. His other interests are making music and singing.
