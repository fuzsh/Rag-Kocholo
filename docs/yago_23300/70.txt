She was the Queen consort of Egypt, (May 26, 1919 - April 28, 1936) as the second wife of King Fuad. She was the daughter of Abdu'r-Rahim Pasha Sabri, Minister of Agriculture and Governor of Cairo, by his wife, Tawfika Khanum Sharif. Queen Nazli also was the maternal granddaughter of Major-General Muhammad Sharif Pasha, Prime Minister and Minister for Foreign Affairs. She was also a great-granddaughter of Suleiman Pasha.
