LaGrange is a city located in Harris County, Heard County, and Troup County Georgia. LaGrange has a 2024 population of 32,818. LaGrange is currently growing at a rate of 1.47% annually and its population has increased by 6.15% since the most recent census, which recorded a population of 30,916 in 2020.

The average household income in LaGrange is $67,778 with a poverty rate of 25.42% . The median age in LaGrange is 34.9 years: 32.5 years for males, and 37 years for females.
