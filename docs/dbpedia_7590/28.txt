The LaGrange Troup County Chamber of Commerce is dedicated to serving businesses of all types and sizes in Troup County, Georgia. You won’t find a better place to live, work and play than Troup County and West Central Georgia. Located just one hour south of Atlanta’s Hartsfield-Jackson International Airport, two hours east of Birmingham, AL and four hours from the Atlantic or Gulf Coast, Troup County embraces diversity and exemplifies big city life with a small town atmosphere.
