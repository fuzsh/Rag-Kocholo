DVDA is the musical comedy outfit of South Park (1997) creators Trey Parker (lead vocals, keyboards) and Matt Stone (vocals, drums, bass guitar), and South Park crew members Bruce Howell (guitars), D.A. Young (keyboards, vocals) and Nels Dielman (drums). The band's songs have featured heavily in South Park, as well as numerous other Parker/Stone works such as Team America (2004), Orgazmo (1997) and Die Sportskanonen (1998).
