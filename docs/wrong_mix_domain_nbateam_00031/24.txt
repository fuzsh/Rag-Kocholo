Mike Miller played 17 seasons for 7 teams, including the Grizzlies and Heat. He averaged 10.6 points, 4.2 rebounds and 2.6 assists in 1,032 regular-season games. He won the Rookie of the Year award, 1 Sixth Man of the Year award and 2 NBA championships.
