Bruce Robinson is a filmmaker best known for his black comedy "Withnail and I", the Academy Award nominated "The Killing Fields" screenplay and his take on Hunter S. Thompson's "Rum Diary" starring Johnny Depp. However, Robinson's obsession for the past 10 to 15 years has been Jack the Ripper and he's just released a ripping yarn called "They All Love Jack: Busting the Ripper".
