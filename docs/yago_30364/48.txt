Nancy Carell was born on 19 July 1966 in Cohasset, Massachusetts, USA. She is an actress and writer, known for Jungfrau (40), männlich, sucht... (2005), Auf der Suche nach einem Freund fürs Ende der Welt (2012) and Brautalarm (2011). She has been married to Steve Carell since 5 August 1995. They have two children.
