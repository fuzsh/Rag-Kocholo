About DANILO BARTHEL

Grew up with USC Heidelberg (Germany) junior team.

Made his debut with USC Heidelberg, Pro A, during the 2008-09 season.

Signed for the 2011-12 season by Fraport Skyliners.

Signed for the 2016-17 season by FC Bayern Munich.

Moved to Turkey for the 2020-21 season, signed by Fenerbahce Istanbul.

Played there also the 2021-22 championship.
