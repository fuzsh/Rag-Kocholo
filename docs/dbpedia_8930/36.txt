John William Boone1

M, b. 25 December 1842, d. 1 March 1916

John William Boone was born on 25 December 1842 in Alabama.1

John married Sarah Hester HUNT, daughter of Henry Harrison HUNT and Sarah BURT, on 12 October 1865 in Autauga County, Alabama.1

John William Boone and Sarah Hester HUNT were divorced between 1880 and 1893.1,2

John William Boone died on 1 March 1916 in Autauga County, Alabama, at age 73.1 He was buried in Liberty Baptist Church Cemetery.1
