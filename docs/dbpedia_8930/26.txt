William Joseph Boone, age 94, of Louisville, Kentucky, passed away August 5, 2016. He was born January 22, 1922, in Howardstown, Kentucky, to Charles Henry and Margaret Oâ€™Leary Boone. In addition to his parents, he was preceded in death by his brother, Charles Daniel Boone and his sister, Mary Angela Boone Donahue, and a son, John Paul Boone.

Bill, also fondly known as Billy Joe in his younger years, grew up in the small town of Howardstown, Kentucky on his family's farm.

He was a member of the United States Marine Corps and served in the South Pacific Theater of Operations during World War II.

Upon returning home, he married the love of his life, Betty Jane Wimsett, in New Haven, Kentucky, in 1948.

A devout Catholic, he was an active member of St. Bartholomew Parish for many decades.

He was the foundation of a close-knit, loving family, who remain so to this day. He will never be forgotten by those whose lives he generously touched.

In addition to his parents, he was preceded in death by his brother, Charles Daniel Boone and his sister, Mary Angela Boone Donahue, and a son, John Paul Boone.
