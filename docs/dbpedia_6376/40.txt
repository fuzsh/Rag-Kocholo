George Englund was born on 22 June 1926 in Washington, District of Columbia, USA. He was a producer and director, known for Der häßliche Amerikaner (1963), The Eddie Fisher Show (1957) and Zachariah (1971). He was married to Bonnie Graves and Cloris Leachman. He died on 14 September 2017 in Palm Springs, California, USA.
