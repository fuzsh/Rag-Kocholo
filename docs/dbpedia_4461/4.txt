Berliner Fernsehturm, Germany

Latitude and longitude coordinates are: 52.520645, 13.409779.

Berliner Fernsehturm is a famous TV tower and one of the most known and recognizable landmarks of today's Berlin, the capital city of Germany. It is often called the Berliner Fernsehturm, and the tower is one of the tallest constructions in the city. This tower is almost 12 hundred feet (about 370 meters) high, and it was constructed in the 1960s, still remaining one of the most typical architectural products of East Germany. Currently, Fernsehturm Berlin serves its main function, broadcasting the signal around the area. It is a destination for tourists as it is known as a great observation facility. The tower is open for visitors, who can rise to a height of over 200 meters and enjoy the amazing views with a visibility range of 43 km.

Fernsehturm Berlin can be found in the very central part of the capital city, in the district called Mitte. The tower is located in one of the largest pedestrian squares of Berlin, Alexanderplatz. Right opposite the tower, there is a large railway station, Berlin Alexanderplatz Bahnhof, that can be found. The tower is surrounded by small green areas known as Wasserkaskaden Park, famous for its beautiful fountains. A few other attractions of Berlin are located in the neighborhood. Those are, for example, a beautiful church, Saint Mary Church, as well as Neptunbrunnen, an amazingly beautiful fountain of Neptune. There are also many shops and outlets in the area, which are always filled with shoppers from around the world.
