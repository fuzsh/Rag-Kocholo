Watanabe Natsuna, 渡辺夏菜 (わたなべ なつな) (given name: Natsuna (夏菜), family name: Watanabe (渡辺), stage name: Natsuna (夏菜), also called Natsuna Watanabe, Natuna, Watanabe Natuna, Natuna Watanabe ) (born 23 May 1989, T/B/W/H: 165/83/57/86, sign: Gemini, blood: A) is the actress who plays Kei Kishimoto in the live-action movie. She also appears in the merchandising gravure photobook released for the movie, GANTZ/K.
