Germaine of Foix dating history

Relationships

Germaine of Foix was previously married to Ferdinand, Duke of Calabria (1526 - 1538), Johann of Brandenburg-Ansbach (1519 - 1526) and Ferdinand II of Aragon (1505 - 1516).

Germaine of Foix was in a relationship with Charles V, Holy Roman Emperor (1517 - 1519).
