Grant Chapman (they/he) has appeared as an actor at theaters in NYC and across the country including NY Classical Theatre, Asolo Rep, Alliance Theatre, Trinity Rep, Opera House Arts, Bristol Riverside Theatre, and the Alabama and Pennsylvania Shakespeare Festivals, and has been an acting company member of The Williams Project and the Bread Loaf Acting Ensemble. In addition to Brown/Trinity, Grant has taught acting, voice, and speech for the Atlantic Acting School and the Experimental Theater Wing at NYU Tisch, Barnard College/Columbia University, and Molloy College/CAP21. Grant holds a BFA from the University of Minnesota/Guthrie Theater and an MFA from Brown University/Trinity Rep. www.grantchapman.net
