By HERALD STAFF

NASHUA, N.H. -- Craig Snipp pitched 62/3 innings as the Skyhawks defeated the Nashua Pride, 7-5.

Snipp struck out four and yielded two runs on eight hits.

Yohanny Valera provided some of the Skyhawks' offense, finishing 2-for-4 with three RBIs.

Jorge Moreno homered for Sussex, while Matt Petrusek worked 1 2/3 innings to record the save.

Sussex will play Nashua again tonight before closing the first half of the season with a four-game series against first-place Quebec.
