Digital Integration Incentive

The Digital Integration Incentive aims to increase the market supply of software integrated with seamless tax filing and e-invoicing capabilities*; and drive end-business adoption of these software. This is part of IRAS’ ongoing efforts to facilitate the fulfilment of tax-related obligations through taxpayers’ natural systems.

To qualify for DII, the product need not be a new solution in the market.

The three seamless filing products refer to the submission of Corporate Tax Returns (Form C-S) and GST Returns (GST F5/F8), as well as being a Peppol-ready Solution Provider as approved by the IMDA.
