Argyro Strataki (Greek: Αργυρώ Στρατάκη, born 3 August 1975 in Heraklion) is a Greek former heptathlete. She represented her country at the Olympic Games in 2004 and 2008. She was also a four-time participant at the World Championships in Athletics and a three-time competitor at the European Athletics Championships. Her personal best for the heptathlon is 6235 points – a Greek record.

Her sole major international medal was a silver at the 2005 Mediterranean Games. Strataki won six Greek title in the heptathlon from 1998 to 2006, She was also a five-time winner of the women's pentathlon at the Greek Indoor Championships.
