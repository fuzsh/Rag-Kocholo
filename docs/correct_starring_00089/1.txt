TINA: Guest Appearance (Movie / Video)

Last Action Hero (1993)

Last Action Hero is an action comedy movie, starring Arnold Schwarzenegger as Jack Slater. Tina Turner appears in a short cameo as the mayor of Los Angeles, talking to Schwarzenegger. The movie, directed by John McTiernan and produced by John McTiernan and Arnold Schwarzenegger came to cinemas on June 18, 1993 and was released on home video in 1995.
