CSN International

Helena

The CSN radio station for the Helena area is translator K215CG, on 90.9 MHz, located at the Helena North Hill Electronic Site. The site is shared with several other radio broadcasters, including KHLV, KUHM, K226BI, KVCM, & KHKR.

The new addition to the south side of the building houses five transmitters.

Equipment for two of the stations is mounted in this rack. From the top, STL receiver and transmitter for translator K226BI, which re-transmits the programming of KKGR, satellite receiver and transmitter for translator K215CG, for CSN.

The CSN antenna is a one-bay Jampro "arrow," mounted at the lowest position on the tower.

This satellite dish down-links the CSN programming from Twin Falls.
