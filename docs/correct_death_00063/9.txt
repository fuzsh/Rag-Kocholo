From The Times, August 4, 1923

Warren G Harding is dead, and Calvin Coolidge is President of the United States. Death by apoplexy came instantaneously at San Francisco at 7.30 last night. At 2.47 this morning the Vice-President was sworn in as 30th President, at Plymouth (Vermont), his father, a notary, administering the oath.

After a day of apparent progress towards recovery and an optimistic evening bulletin from the physicians, a stroke of apoplexy brought instant death to President Harding. The first act of those with the President was to notify Mr Coolidge. The new President will come to Washington immediately, reaching here about midnight.
