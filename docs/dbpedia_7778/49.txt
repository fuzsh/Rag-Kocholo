Founded in 1899, the Hackley School is an independent, college-preparatory, day and 5-day boarding school for students from kindergarten through grade 12. The school sits on 285 acres in the Westchester County village of Tarrytown, New York, just 30 minutes from New York City. With a commitment to building community among its 840 students, the Hackley School offers a broad array of arts opportunities, sports teams and clubs in addition to an expansive academic program. A new 115,000 square foot Center for Health and Wellness has recently opened on the campus which includes a fitness center and weight room, swimming pool, indoor track, and teaching kitchen, in addition to other amenities. The school is in the early stages of an institution-wide strategic planning process.
