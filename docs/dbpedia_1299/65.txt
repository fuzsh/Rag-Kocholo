Matt McColm (born 196?) is an American actor and stuntman, and former model. He has modeled for Ralph Lauren early in his career.[1] As an actor, he is best known for portraying Johnny Domino, the lead character in the 1997-1999 television series Night Man and Agent Thompson, the agent who kills Trinity at the climax of the The Matrix Reloaded. He also appeared as Vick Chamberlain, a T-888 Terminator in the television series Terminator: The Sarah Connor Chronicles.

He has a twin brother, Mark, who usually does stand-in for him in several television and movie shows.
