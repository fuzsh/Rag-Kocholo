Matt McColm (born January 31, 1965) is an American actor and stuntman, and former model. He portrayed one the various henchmen working for Thomas Gabriel in Live Free or Die Hard.

Career[]

McColm prevoiusly headlined the short-lived 90s comic book adapted television series "Nightman" playing the title protagonist. McColm also has provided stunt work and/or acted in blockbusters such as The Matrix Reloaded, Cellular, Argo and The Wolverine. Other television roles include the shows "Baywatch" and "Terminator: The Sarah Connor Chronicles".
