The side label on the bottle of Nøgne Ø Skog & Mark informing us it was brewed on June 25, 2014, as batch number 1214. Some of the ingredients are:

... brennesle fra Arilds hage og utedass, urter fra hotellets urtehage, granskudd fra Krokskogen og vårt lokale vann

which means nettle from Arild's garden and outhouse, herbs from the hotel's herb garden, spruce shots from Krokskogen forest and our local water.
