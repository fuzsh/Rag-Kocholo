JACKSON, Billie Gray, 90, of Kettering, Ohio went home to be with the Lord on May 4, 2023. He was born on November 1, 1932 in Overton County, Tennessee to Ruben Elmore and Mildred (Eldridge) Jackson.

Billie was the loving husband of the late Eunia Jackson for 68 years. He was also preceded in death by his parents and sisters, Johnnie May and Rosalee.

Billie leaves behind his sons, Steve (Loretta), Scott (Carla), and Mark (Jeanie). He was the best Papa to Mark jr., Brett (Alexis), Bradley (Jenna), Benjamin (Emily), Christopher, Scott jr., Emily (Brandon), Mandy, and the late Joe. Billie was also the proud great grandfather of 12.

Billie will be remembered as an honest and giving devout Christian by his extended family and many friends.

Family will greet friends Friday, May 12 from 12:00pm-2:00pm at Routsong Funeral Home, 81 N. Main St., Centerville. Funeral Services will begin at 2:00pm at the funeral home. Billie will be laid to rest in Miami Valley Memory Gardens.

In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to CancerFree Kids, 420 W. Loveland Ave., Loveland, OH 45140.
