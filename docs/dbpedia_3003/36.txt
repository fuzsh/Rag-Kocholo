Vanessa directs the office of the Phoenix Hebrew Academy Rebibo Center for Jewish Life in addition to working as Administrative and Facilities Manager including finances and payroll. She takes great pride in assuring all aspects of our organization runs smoothly and efficiently.

She has worked closely with Rabbi David Rebibo for over 17 years and Rabbi Yisroel Isaacs for almost a decade.

She is also famous amongst the Phoenix Hebrew Academy students and alumni for her magical cures which involves asking ailing students “How much water have you had today?”

She was born in Phoenix Arizona and attended Rio Salado College.

When not at work, she enjoys spending time with her husband Tom of 45 years, her two children and two grandchildren.
