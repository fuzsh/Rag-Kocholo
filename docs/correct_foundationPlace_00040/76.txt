Our Mission

To harness the gifts of energy, ideas, time, and money to make meaningful and lasting impacts in communities.

About Vancouver Foundation

Vancouver Foundation is dedicated to creating healthy, vibrant and livable communities across BC. Since 1943, our donors have created 1,800 endowment funds and together we have distributed more than $1 billion to charities. From arts and culture to the environment, health and social development, education, medical research and more, we exist to make meaningful and lasting improvements to communities in BC. For more information, please visit www.vancouverfoundation.ca.
