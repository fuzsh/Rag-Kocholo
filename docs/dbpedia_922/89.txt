St. Andrews House, located in Bronx, NY, offers a unique and thought-provoking study series that delves into the depths of Genesis 37-50, providing fresh insights and answers to challenging questions. Through this study, participants will gain a new understanding of God's redemptive love and sovereign plan for their lives, while uncovering the hidden beauty within Joseph's story and the significance of including his brother Judah in the title of the book.

With a copyright spanning from 2006 to 2014, St. Andrews House, LLC presents an engaging and transformative experience that goes beyond the familiar details of Joseph's life, offering a profound exploration of biblical literature and its relevance to personal growth and spiritual development.
