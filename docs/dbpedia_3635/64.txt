Maria Francisca of Savoy (Marie Françoise Élisabeth; 21 June 1646 – 27 December 1683) was the queen consort o Portugal twice as the wife of two Portuguese kings: Afonso VI of Portugal and Peter II of Portugal. She was a sister of Marie Jeanne of Savoy, Duchess of Savoy an thus aunt of King Victor Amadeus II of Sardinia.

Children[edit]

Infanta Isabel Luísa of Portugal, Princess of Beira (6 January 1669 – 21 October 1690) died from smallpox having never married.
