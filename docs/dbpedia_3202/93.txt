Motoi Sakuraba (桜庭 統) is a Japanese composer and keyboardist. He has contributed to numerous video games, anime series, as well as television dramas and other albums. You can hear his works in games such as Tales, Star Ocean, Mario Golf, Mario Tennis, Golden Sun, and the Dark Souls series.

It was then revealed that he would be doing the musics for Eiyuden Chronicle along with Michiko Naruke and Akira Yamaoka.
