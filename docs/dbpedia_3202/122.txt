Masahiro Sakurai, the head of the game developer Sora, revealed on Thursday the five composers who are scoring the soundtrack for Nintendo's Kid Icarus: Uprising game: Motoi Sakuraba (Tales of games, Star Ocean, Saiyuki), Yūzō Koshiro (Ys, Etrian Odyssey), Yasunori Mitsuda (Chrono Trigger, Inazuma Eleven), Noriyuki Iwadare (Grandia, Lunar, Ace Attorney, Kimikiss pure rouge), and Masafumi Takada (killer7, No More Heroes).

These five composed "Main Theme," "Magna's Theme," "Boss Battle," Seizoku's Theme," and "Training Room," respectively, and Nintendo is streaming samples of these tracks.
