James Newton Howard, born June 9, 1951 in Los Angeles, California, is an American keyboardist and composer. He composes mainly film scores. He has received numerous nominations and awards for his musical work. He has also worked with Elton John and Crosby, Stills & Nash, and produced three solo albums, the last of which, James Newton Howard & Friends in 1984, was a collaboration with the Porcaro brothers and Toto's David Paich.
