BIOGRAPHY

James Newton Howard is an American composer and musician born on November 9, 1951, in Los Angeles, California. He began his career playing piano in Los Angeles jazz clubs before studying classical music at the Music Conservatory of Los Angeles. After graduating, he worked as an arranger and conductor for various pop and rock artists, including Barbra Streisand, Diana Ross, and Elton John. James has also composed music for several films, such as Pretty Woman, The Fugitive, and Batman Begins.
