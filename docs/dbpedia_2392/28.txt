Giuseppe Becchi was born in Florence, Italy, in 26th of September 1977. He received his master laurea in computer engineering at University of Florence in September 2003. His thesis was on “Natural interaction with zoomable interface”.

He has worked at Micc for Mediateca di Palazzo Medici Riccardi project and as SOA architect for IM3I and euTV European projects. Now he is involved in SMST (Social Museum and Smart Tourism) project as developer for the infrastructure layer.

His main interests are in the field of information architecture, web usability and accessibility, cms and multimedia databases.
