Lottie Jo Redd of Cuba, Missouri and formerly of Greenville, Mississippi left from this life on Monday, August 7 at Cuba Manor in Cuba, Missouri at the age of 84 years. Those who are left to treasure her memory and to continue her legacy are her son Charles "Chuck" Redd III (Donna) of New Blaine, Arkansas; daughter, Charlotte Underwood (Ross) of Greenville, Mississippi; 5 grandchildren and 11 great grandchildren. Visitation from 1:00-2:00 p.m. Friday at Boone Funeral Home 905 Main St. Greenville, Mississippi. Graveside service will be held at 2:00 p.m., Friday, August 11 at Greenlawn Memorial Gardens, Greenville, Mississippi. The Mizell Funeral Home of Cuba, Missouri and Boone Funeral Home of Greenville, Mississippi are honored to be serving the Redd family.
