She filed her petition in Los Angeles Angeles Superior Court, citing irreconcilable differences as the reason for the breakup of the marriage, which dates to April 1991. The former couple have been separated since June 2013.

The 58-year-old matriarch of the Kardashian family is asking for joint physical and legal custody of their 17-year-old daughter Kylie. They also have an 18-year-old daughter, Kendall.

Jenner also is asking to maintain possession of her jewelry and all assets she has obtained since the date of separation.

She was formerly married to the late attorney Robert Kardashian, father of her older children Kourtney, Kim, Khloe and Robert Jr.
