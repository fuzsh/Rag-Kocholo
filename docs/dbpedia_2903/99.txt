Direct Line Insurance Group Plc

Direct Line Insurance is listed on the London Stock Exchange under the ticker DLG. Direct Line is a multi-line insurance company based in the United Kingdom. It is the UK's largest motor insurer. The company's brands include Direct Line, Churchill, and Privilege. The company was founded in 1985.
