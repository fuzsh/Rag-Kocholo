Composer and musician David Foster has been married five times. He was previously married to singer B.J. Cook from 1972 to 1981, actress Rebecca Dyer from 1982 to 1986, actress Linda Thompson from 1991 to 2005, model Yolanda Hadid (mom to Gigi and Bella Hadid) from 2011 to 2017, and most recently wed singer and actress Katharine McPhee in 2019.

Singer and actress J.Lo has been married three times. She was previously married to waiter Ojani Noa (1997 to 1998), dancer Cris Judd (2001 to 2003) and singer Marc Anthony (2004 to 2014). She is currently engaged to Alex Rodriguez, and their relationship is the absolute cutest.

Actor Tom Cruise has been married three times. His first wife was actress Mimi Rogers, from 1987 to 1990. He then married actress Nicole Kidman later in 1990, and they separated in 2001. He married actress Katie Holmes in 2006, and they divorced in 2012.

Actress Kate Winslet has been married three times. She was married to assistant director Jim Threapleton from 1998 to 2001; then director Sam Mendes from 2003 to 2010, and in 2012 she wed businessman Ned Rocknroll.

Actress Halle Berry has been married three times. First was baseball player David Justice from 1992 to 1997, followed by musician Eric Benét from 2001 to 2005 and French actor Olivier Martinez from 2013 to 2016.

Actress Eva Longoria has been married three times. She was first married to actor Tyler Christopher from 2002 to 2004, then NBA player Tony Parker from 2007 to 2011. She married her third (and current) husband, businessman José Bastón, in 2016.

Actor Charlie Sheen has been married three times. The first marriage was to model Donna Peele (1995 to 1996) followed by actress and current RHOBH castmember Denise Richards (2002 to 2006), and finally actress Brooke Mueller from 2008 to 2011.

Actress and Broadway star Liza Minnelli (mom of Wizard of Oz actress Judy Garland) was married four times. She was married to musician Peter Allen (1967 to 1974), director Jack Haley Jr. (1974 to 1979), sculptor Mark Gero (1979 to 1992) and producer David Gest (2002 to 2007).

Actress Drew Barrymore has been married three times. She was first married to bartender Jeremy Thomas from 1994 to 1995. Then she was married to actor and comedian Tom Green from 2001 to 2002, and actor Will Kopelman from 2012 to 2016.

Actress Angelina Jolie has been married three times. She was married to actor Jonny Lee Miller from 1996 to 1999, actor Billy Bob Thornton from 2000 to 2003, and of course, super-famous actor Brad Pitt from 2014 to 2016.

Queen of reality TV (and businesswoman!) Kim Kardashian has been married three times. She married music producer Damon Thomas in 2000 (which lasted for four years), followed by a 73-day marriage to NBA player Kris Humphries in 2010. She then married rapper Kanye West (her current hubby) in 2014.

90210 actress Shannen Doherty has been married three times. Her first marriage was to actor Ashley Hamilton (1993 to 1994), then poker player Rick Salomon (2002 to 2003). And she married her current husband, photographer Kurt Iswarienko, in 2011.

Model and actress Pamela Anderson has been married four times. She was married to Motley Crue drummer Tommy Lee from 1994 to 1998 and to musician Kid Rock from 2006 to 2007. Then she married poker player Rick Salomon (who also was married to Shannen Doherty) in Las Vegas in 2007, but had the marriage annulled less than six months later. They remarried in 2014 but divorced months later.

Star Wars actor Harrison Ford has been married three times. He was married to his college girlfriend Mary Marquardt from 1964 to 1979, then married screenwriter Melissa Mathison in 1983 and divorced in 2004. He married actress Calista Flockhart in 2010.

Actress Demi Moore has been married three times. She married singer Freddy Moore in 1980; they were together five years before she married actor Bruce Willis in 1987. They divorced in 2000 and Demi went on to marry actor Ashton Kutcher (who is now married to actress Mila Kunis) in 2005, divorcing in 2013.

Singer Janet Jackson has been married three times. Janet was married to singer James DeBarge from 1984 to 1985; then she married dancer René Elizondo Jr. in 1991 before separating nine years later. She married her third husband, billionaire businessman Wissam Al Mana in 2012. They separated in 2017 but are not officially divorced.

Television host Larry King has been married eight times, but technically had seven wives. His most recent marriage to actress Shawn Southwick ended in 2019. Before he married Shawn, Larry was with businesswoman Julie Alexander from 1989 to 1992, math teacher Sharon Lepore from 1976 to 1983, his high school sweetheart Alene Akins from 1968 to 1971 (and he was previously married to her from 1961 to 1963), Mickey Sutphin from 1964 to 1967, and Annette Kay in 1961. His first wife was Freda Miller; they married in 1952. He had his marriage with her annulled in 1953.
