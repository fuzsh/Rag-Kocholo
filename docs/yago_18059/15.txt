Maria Theresa, Archduchess of Austria (1)

F, #40290, b. 20 March 1762, d. 23 January 1770

Last Edited=7 Nov 2004

Consanguinity Index=2.99%

Maria Theresa, Archduchess of Austria, was born on March 20, 1762 at Vienna, Austria. (1) She was the daughter of Joseph II vor Austria, Holy Roman Emperor, and Maria Isabella di Borbone, Infanta de España. (1)

She died on January 23, 1770 at age 7 in Vienna, Austria. (1) She was buried at Capuchin Church, Vienna, Austria.1

Maria Theresa, Archduchess of Austria, was a member of the House of Habsburg-Lorraine. (1) She was baptised with the name of Maria Theresa Elisabeth Philippine Louise Josephe Johanna. (1) She gained the title of Erzherzogin von Österreich (styled as HI&RH Archduchess of Austria). (1)

Archduchess Maria Theresa of Austria (1762–1770)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Archduchess Maria Theresa of Austria (March 20, 1762 – January 23, 1770) was a daughter of Joseph II, Holy Roman Emperor, and his first wife, Isabella of Parma. She died suddenly at age of seven of pleurisy. She is buried in the Imperial Crypt, like her parents and her stepmother.
