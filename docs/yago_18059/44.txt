Archduchess Theresa of Austria, (Theresa Monika Valerie Elisabeth Ludovika Walburga Anna, (born 9 January 1931 at Schloss Wallsee in Amstetten, Lower Austria, Federal State of Austria) is a member of the Tuscan branch of the House of Habsburg-Lorraine and an Archduchess of Austria and Princess of Bohemia, Hungary, and Tuscany by birth. Through her marriage to Prince Rasso of Bavaria, Theresa is also a member of the House of Wittelsbach and a Princess of Bavaria.

Early Life

Theresa was the second child and eldest daughter of Archduke Theodor Salvator of Austria and his wife Countess Maria Theresia Waldburg Zeil Trauchburg.

Marriage and Issue

Theresa married Prince Rasso of Bavaria, second son and youngest child of Prince Franz and wife Princess Isabella Antonie of Croy, on 17 October 1955 at Schloss Wallsee at Amstetten.

Theresa and Rasso had seven children: Princess Maria-Theresia of Bavaria (born 1956); married Count Tamas Kornis Goncz-Ruszka; Prince Franz-Josef of Bavaria (born 1957), a Benedictine monk in Peramiko, Tanzania, now called Pater Florian, Princess Elisabeth of Bavaria (born 1959); married Count Andreas Kufstein; six children, Prince Wolfgang of Bavaria (born 1960), married Countess Beatrice of Lodron-Laterano and Castelromano; three children, Princess Benedikta of Bavaria (born 1961); Count Rudolf Freyberg-Eisenberg; four children, Prince Christoph of Bavaria (born 1962); married Baroness Gudila Plettenberg; four children. Princess Gisela of Bavaria (born 1964); married Prince Alexander of Saxe-Gessaphe; four children.

House: Habsburg-Lorraine (by birth), Wittelsbach (by marriage)
