BOZEMAN, MT: Oracle said it will acquire RightNow Technologies, a social customer-relationship management service, for $1.5 billion. The deal is expected to close in late 2011 or early 2012.

RightNow, which employs more than 1,000 people worldwide, works with customers including Yahoo, the US Census Bureau, Canon, and Orbitz. RightNow hired Weber Shandwick as its AOR in March.
