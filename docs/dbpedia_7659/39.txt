RICHARD Donner is responsible for directed some of the most popular films.

The late director died on Monday July 5, 2021.

What movies has Richard Donner directed?

Richard Donner was an American director, producer, TV, and occasional comic-book writer.

Throughout his 46-year career in film, he has directed over 80 productions.

Those movies include:

London Affair (1970)

The Omen (1976)

Superman (1978)

Superman II (1980)

Inside Moves (1980)

Ladyhawke (1982)

The Goonies (1985)

Lethal Weapon (1987)

Scrooged (1988)

Lethal Weapon 2 (1989)

Lethal Weapon 3 (1992)

Maverick (1994)

Assassins (1995)

Conspiracy Theory (1997)

Lethal Weapon 4 (1998)

Timeline (2003)

16 Blocks (2006)

When he was not directing movies, he also directed various episodes of TV shows, including, The Twilight Zone, Gilligan's Island, and Mr. Novak.

How did Richard Donner die?

His wife Lauren, 72, confirmed his death with Hollywood trade publications on Monday, July 5.

While she confirmed his passing, there has been no cause of death released.

Since his death has been announced, the entertainment world has been reacting to the sad news.

American director and producer Steven Spielberg released a statement on Monday about Donner that read, "Dick had such a powerful command of his movies, and was so gifted across so many genres. Being in his circle was akin to hanging out with your favorite coach, smartest professor, fiercest motivator, most endearing friend, staunchest ally, and — of course — the greatest Goonie of all. He was all kid. All heart. All the time.”

Carol Kane also talked highly of the director saying he was "a creative guidance counselor in the most nourishing way."

What was Richard Donner's net worth?

According to Celebrity Net Worth, Donner himself had an estimated net worth of $100 million. The website estimates that combined with his wife, American producer Lauren Shuler Donner, they had a combined net wroth of $200 million.

A large part of their net worth comes from his directing of the Lethal Weapon films. The couple also co-executive produced several X-Men movies.

Along with producing, the couple also had money in real estate.

They previously owned a custom-built mansion in Maui with over 160 feet of ocean frontage that sold for $16.5 million in 2016.
