Weather in October

October reels the City of London further into the heart of Autumn. The temperatures go down to a cooler spectrum, varying from 9.6°C (49.3°F) to 15.3°C (59.5°F). The red and golden hues of falling leaves dominate the visual landscape, and the city sparkles under the breathtaking Autumn sunset. As November unfolds, with its diminishing daylight hours, London nestles into the anticipation of Winter's arrival.

Temperature

October in City of London, United Kingdom, heralds a minor drop in average high-temperature, sliding from September's 19.3°C (66.7°F) to a still refreshing 15.3°C (59.5°F). City of London, in the month of October, anticipates a consistent average low-temperature of 9.6°C (49.3°F).

Daylight

In City of London, the average length of the day in October is 10h and 40min.

On the first day of October, sunrise is at 7:01 am and sunset at 6:37 pm BST. On the last day of the month, sunrise is at 6:53 am and sunset at 4:34 pm GMT.

Note: On Sunday, 27. October 2024, at 2:00 am, Daylight Saving Time ends, and the time zone changes from BST to GMT. Daylight Saving Time starts again on Sunday, 30. March 2025, at 1:00 am; consequently, the time zone reverts from GMT to BST.
