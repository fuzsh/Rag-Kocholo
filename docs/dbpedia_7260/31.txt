Evan Jackson Leong, a distinguished director and documentary filmmaker, has recently unveiled his modern crime thriller, SNAKEHEAD. The film delves into the gritty underworld of human smuggling in Chinatown, featuring a talented cast including Shuya Chang, Jade Wu, Yacine Djoumbaye, and Sung Kang. Known for his acclaimed documentary Linsanity, Leong brings his signature storytelling style to SNAKEHEAD, promising to captivate audiences with its exploration of Asian matriarchs' resilience and a unique blend of traditional and contemporary filmmaking techniques.
