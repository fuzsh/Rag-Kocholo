The Merry Wives of Windsor is an opera in three acts by Otto Nicolai (1810-1849) with a German libretto by Salomon Hermann Mosenthal based on the Shakespeare play “The Merry Wives of Windsor”. The opera is a “Singspiel”which contains spoken dialogue between musical numbers.
