'Eugénie-Hortense De Beauharnais - Wife of Louis Bonaparte; Queen of Holland', 1807, (1896). 'The child is Napoleon-Charles, her eldest son, who died in 1807 at the age of five years'. Hortense de Beauharnais (1783-1837, daughter of Napoleon's first wife, Josephine de Beauharnais) and her son Napoléon Louis Charles Bonaparte (1802-1807). Engraving after the painting by François Gérard. From Life of Napoleon Bonaparte, Volume II, by William Milligan Sloane. [The Century Co., Macmillan & Co., Limited, New York & London, 1896]
