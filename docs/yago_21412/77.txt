Eugenie-Hortense De Beauharnais - Wife of Louis Bonaparte; Queen of Holland, 1807, (1896)

Eugenie-Hortense De Beauharnais - Wife of Louis Bonaparte; Queen of Holland, 1807, (1896). The child is Napoleon-Charles, her eldest son, who died in 1807 at the age of five years. Hortense de Beauharnais (1783-1837, daughter of Napoleons first wife, Josephine de Beauharnais) and her son Napoleon Louis Charles Bonaparte (1802-1807). Engraving after the painting by Francois Gerard. From Life of Napoleon Bonaparte, Volume II, by William Milligan Sloane. [The Century Co. Macmillan & Co. Limited, New York & London, 1896].
