Viola Grace Green, 81, of Westminster died suddenly, May 26, 2010 at her residence. Born February 24, 1929 in Phoenix, Maryland, she was the daughter of the late Harry C. Miller and Ella Mae King Miller. Before retiring she was a presser for English American. After retiring she worked for the ARC as a bus Aide. She was a member of the Women of the Moose Chapter 897, American Legion, the Fraternal Order of Eagles 2226 in Littlestown, the Fraternal Order of Eagles in Westmisnter 4378 and a life member the VFW Ladies Auxiliary. She enjoyed bingo, gambling, her Moose Family and spending time with family. Surviving are children and spouses David M. and wife Mary E. Green of Westminster, Sandra L. Ingle, Robin M. and husband Donald Miller of Manchester, Gail A. and husband Wayne E. Nolte of Wesminster, Mark A. and companion Ginny Kinzer, Karen M. Green, all of Westminster. Also surviving are grandchildren, Terry A. Ingle of New Windsor, Bradley D. Green and Steven A. Miller both of Manchester, Justin Kinzer of Westminster , Vicky A. Roberts of Hanover, P.A, three great-grand children Jacob A. Ingle of New Windsor, Payton L. Miller of Manchester and Peaghen J. Green of Manchester.Also surviving is a brother Earl H. Miller of Hampstead. She was predeceased by brothers William Miller, McKinley Miller and a sister Isabelle Jones, and son-in-law Alton Ingle. A funeral service will be held on Saturday May 29, 2010 at 2 P.M with Rev. Kevin Clementson officiating. Interment will follow in John Luther Miller Cemetery. The family will receive friends on Thursday from 7-9, Friday from 3-5 and 7-9, and Saturday from 1 P.M until the time of service. The Women of the Moose Chapter 897 will be conducting a memorial service at 8 P.M on Friday at the Funeral Home. Memorial contributions may be made to the American Heart Association, P.O Box 5216 , Glen Allen, V.A 23058. Online condolences may be made to the family at fletcherfuneralhome.net
