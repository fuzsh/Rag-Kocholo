Etobicoke is a district located withing the limits of the city of Toronto, Ontario, located right between the city center of Toronto and Mississauga. The population of the district is about 350,000 people and local community is diverse and multicultural. Local economy relies on the revenues of some official and governmental organizations, including a large airport. The attractions of Etobicoke include Budapest Park, Ontario Lake shores, local sports clubs, etc.
