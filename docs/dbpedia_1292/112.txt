Edmond T. Gréville was born on 20 June 1906 in Nice, Alpes-Maritimes, France. He was a director and writer, known for Veertig jaren (1938), Insel der Versuchung (1959) and Die unheimlichen Hände des Dr. Orlak (1960). He was married to Vanda Gréville. He died on 26 May 1966 in Nice, Alpes-Maritimes, France.
