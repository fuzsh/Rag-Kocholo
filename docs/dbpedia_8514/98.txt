Liberal Democrat equalities minister Lynne Featherstone has branded the ideas of David Cameron’s closest aides as “hideous” in a tirade that risks causing a Coalition rift.

She attacked the role of Adrian Beecroft, an advisor to the Prime Minister on cutting business regulation.

The hugely influential member of Cameron’s inner circle is thought to have recommended a U-turn on the policies of flexible working hours and shared parental leave.

Her strong words will appease many rank and file Lib Dems that worry that the Coalition is increasingly becoming out of touch with female voters.
