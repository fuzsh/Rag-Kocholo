Jim Webb was born on 9 February 1946 in Saint Joseph, Missouri, USA. He is a writer and producer, known for Rules - Sekunden der Entscheidung (2000), Whiskey River and Prepared to Serve: The U.S. Naval Academy in the Vietnam Era (2003). He has been married to Hong Le Webb since October 2006. They have one child. He was previously married to Jo Ann Krukar and Barbara Samorajczyk.
