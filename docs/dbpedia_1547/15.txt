In this episode the host John Siracusa chats with John Eley, CEO at GoldenSource and fintech investor. GoldenSource is an enterprise data management software company which sells to the financial services industry. Providing data governance and data quality control to create new gold copy data for any domain, including instruments, parties, prices, corporate actions, products, and positions. Some clients are Charles Schwab, Putnam, ASX, Deutsche Bank, HSBC, M&G, and Nordea.

John has been in leading positions at previous companies with successful exits. Today, he leads GoldenSource, is an adjunct professor at Columbia University and invests early in fintech companies.
