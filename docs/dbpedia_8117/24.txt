Sreelekha is an Indian film actress who is known for her work in Bengali cinema. She is best known for her performance in Bappaditya Bandopadhyay's Kantatar, which won her a BFJA Award and an Anandalok Award. She made her debut in Bimal Dey's film Sei Raat. In 1998, she got a breakthrough in Basu Chatterjee's Hothat Brishti (1998), which came out as a major success at the box office. Her notable television series include Trishna, Ei To Jiban, Pratibimba, Bhanga Gorar Khela, Probahini Ei Samay and Bandhan, to name few.

Born on: 30 August, 1975
