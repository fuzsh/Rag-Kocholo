St. Catharines is a large city in Ontario. With the population close to 380,000 people, this city is one of the largest and the most populous in the Niagara region. It is a wonderful vacation spot with numerous parks and excellent recreation zones, old beautiful architecture, monuments and churches, galleries and annual festivals.
