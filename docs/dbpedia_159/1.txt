Indulge in the enchanting landscapes of Myślibórz and Księży Ostrów on this gravel bike route. Covering a distance of 76 kilometers with an ascent of 170 meters, this journey offers a perfect balance between natural beauty and cultural allure. Immerse yourself in the beauty of Lutnia, discover the historical treasures of Myślibórz, explore the charming village of Kazimierz Biskupi, and admire the scenic Księży Ostrów.
