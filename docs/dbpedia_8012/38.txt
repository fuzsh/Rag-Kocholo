320. Volksgrenadier-Division

The 320. Volksgrenadier-Division was formed in October 1944 from the partially formed 588. Volksgrenadier-Division. It saw action in Poland, suffering heavy losses near Cracow, and Czechoslovakia before surrendering to the Soviet forces at the end of the war.
