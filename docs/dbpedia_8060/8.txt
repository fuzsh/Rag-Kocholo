NEW YORK, Aug. 4 (UPI) -- Woody Allen has confirmed the cast of his next film will include Jesse Eisenberg, Blake Lively, Parker Posey, Kristen Stewart, Corey Stoll and Bruce Willis.

Also featuring Jeannie Berlin, Ken Stott, Anna Camp, Stephen Kunken, Sari Lennick and Paul Schneider, the as-yet-untitled movie is to begin shooting this month on location in New York and Los Angeles.
