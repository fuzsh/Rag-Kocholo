About Daniel Wass

Daniel Wass is a Danish professional footballer who plays as a Center Midfielder (CM) for the Brøndby IF in the Danish Superliga. He joined the club in 2022 with a release clause amounting to €14,300,000 and his contract is valid until 2025.
