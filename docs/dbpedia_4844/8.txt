Surname Boubacar

Last name Boubacar (Boubacar , بوبكر , Бубакар ) , all around the world, is a common surname. The surname Boubacar is habitual in Niger, where it is quite a common surname, Libya, and Mauritania (explore the name in all countries). Not in comparison with a country's population, the name is most common in Sub-Saharan Africa, particularly Niger, Mali, and Senegal. More prevalently, Boubacar is the forename as well as the last name.
