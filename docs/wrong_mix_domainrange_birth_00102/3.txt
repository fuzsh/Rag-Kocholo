PORTLAND (WGME)-- Former First Daughter Barbara Bush, daughter of George W. Bush, is welcoming a daughter of her own after giving birth in Maine.

This is the first child for the 39-year-old and her husband Craig Coyne.

The little girl is named Cora Georgia Coyne, and she was born on Monday not far from the family's Walker's Point compound in Kennebunkport.

Former President George W. Bush and his wife Laura say the baby is "healthy and adorable, and we are proud and grateful."
