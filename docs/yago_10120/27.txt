Sophie Amalie of Brunswick-Lüneburg and Frederick III of Denmark were married for 26 years before Frederick III of Denmark died, leaving behind his partner and 8 children.

They had 8 children, Christian (378), Anna Sophie (376), Frederica Amalia (375), Wilhelmine Ernestine (373), Frederick (372), George (371), Ulrika Eleonora (367) and Dorothea (366).

About

Danish Royal Frederick III of Denmark was born on 18th March, 1609 in Haderslevhus Castle, Haderslev, Denmark and passed away on 9th Feb 1670 Copenhagen Castle, Copenhagen, Denmark aged 60. He is most remembered for King of Denmark and Norway from 1648 until his death in 1670. His zodiac sign is Pisces.
