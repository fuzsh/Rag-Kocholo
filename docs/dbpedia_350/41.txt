Terry Flanagan passes a wet summer's evening in a Wicklow field watching men tossing 2lb horseshoes at stakes hammered into the soggy ground.

He discovers that, as well as horseshoes, there's grub, 'gallery' and 'genuine boys'.

Music used: "Morrison's Jig", Brian Parfeniuk
