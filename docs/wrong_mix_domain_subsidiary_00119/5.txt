CommQuest has signed a strategic support center agreement with Hong Kong-based Telecom Technology Centre (TTC). Under the agreement, TTC will support CommQuest customers in handset design, PCB modifications, and user interface software customization. CommQuest will maintain engineers at TTC's Hong Kong facility to accommodate specific customer needs and facilitate the sharing of technical information.

TTC is a telecommunications product developer with a staff of 45 professionals. The company specializes in radio circuit design, telecommunications software, and application-specific IC (ASIC) development.

CommQuest is a wholly-owned subsidiary of IBM. It develops chip sets for various digital communications applications.
