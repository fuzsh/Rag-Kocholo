Marco Ferreri directs a two hour drama, about a guy trying to get into the Vatican to meet the pope, but dealing with everything from checks, followings, getting arrested , meeting officials.

I really dislike being so negative, but this was a massive massive disappointment to me.

He is one of my favourite Italian directors, with his pathos and sick sense of humour.

Doing Kafka however , I don't grasp this one at all, I just want to watch the Danish and Russian takes on the castle again rather than this.

I fully understand this is about bureaucracy, so I missed nothing.

This was torture to sit through, wasting so many good actors on a plodding and comedically inert script…
