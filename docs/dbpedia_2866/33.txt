Adam Matthew Digital is an academic publisher based in the United Kingdom and the United States.

The company specialises in online primary source databases and curated collections for the humanities and social sciences.

We have been working with Adam Matthew for the past 4 years, initially producing promotional literature for their collections of digitised primary source materials from different historical era, then leading on to providing creative support for some of their key digital products including the branding and UI of Quartex, their revolutionary digital collection platform.
