Yuvika Chaudhary is an Indian actress and model. She was born on August 2, 1983, in Baraut, Uttar Pradesh, India. Yuvika gained fame through her appearances in films and reality television shows.

Yuvika Chaudhary started her career as a model and participated in the reality show "Zee Cine Stars Ki Khoj" in 2004. Although she did not win the competition, she received recognition and subsequently entered the entertainment industry.

In 2007, Yuvika made her acting debut in Bollywood with the film "Om Shanti Om," directed by Farah Khan. Although her role was small, she gained attention for her performance in the popular song "Dhoom Taana." Following that, she appeared in films like "Summer 2007" (2008), "Toh Baat Pakki!" (2010), and "Enemmy" (2013).

Yuvika Chaudhary has also made appearances in regional cinema, including Punjabi and Kannada films. Some of her notable Punjabi films include "Yaaran Naal Baharan" (2005) and "Daddy Cool Munde Fool" (2013).

Apart from her film career, Yuvika has participated in reality television shows. She was a contestant on the reality show "Bigg Boss 9" in 2015 and later appeared in the dance reality show "Nach Baliye 9" in 2019 with her husband Prince Narula, whom she met on "Bigg Boss 9" and got married to in 2018.

Yuvika Chaudhary continues to work in the entertainment industry, appearing in films and making guest appearances on television shows.
