The goals from Saturday night’s match in Mar del Plata between Independiente and Racing are right here. Racing claimed the Copa Ciudad de Mar del Plata with a 2-1 win over their Avellaneda rivals, with goals from new signing Claudio Bieler and Matías Cahais giving them the advantage before El Rojo‘s consolation with 8 minutes to go from Federico González. UK readers might like to know that if they want to watch Sunday night’s match in Mendoza between Boca Juniors and River Plate, they can do so on ESPN without a subscription – ESPN are broadcasting the game for free (at the end of a promotional free weekend) on Freeview channel 34. Kick off is at 1:10am UK time.
