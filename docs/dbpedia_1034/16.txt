Melanie Starks is an American actress, singer, music and film producer.

She coordinated the production of Trance-Fusion (2006), The MOFO Project/Object (Deluxe Edition) (2006), The Dub Room Special! (2007) and One Shot Deal (2008). On The Frank Zappa AAAFNRAAA Birthday Bundle (2008) she sang a version of Dumb All Over.

Melanie Starks is included in the list of names in the liner notes of The MOFO Project/Object (2006) album.
