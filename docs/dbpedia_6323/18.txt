An adventure game based off the popular "The Dog" character family. Plays as a dog whose brother falls ill one day. In order to help your brother, you head to an island in search of a legendary flower. The game includes stages in grass planes, snowy fields and even deserts. You can select from a variety of dogs.

Also known as: The Dog Island: The Tale of The One Flower, The Dog Island: Hitsotsuno no Hana no Monogatari
