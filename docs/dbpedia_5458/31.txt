Wksu Fm is Kent State University’s prize winning NPR News and Classical Music public radio station. Serving more than 20 Ohio counties and elements of Western Pennsylvania, Wksu Fm operates in the station’s broadcast center within Kent and via repeater at 89.1 WKSV within Thompson, and via translator stop 95. 7 in Ashland.

Wksu Fm official website address is www.wksu.org
