⛺ Yekaterinburg has a humid continental climate with severe winters, no dry season, warm summers and strong seasonality (Köppen-Geiger classification: Dfb).

⛳ Yekaterinburg is situated in or near the boreal moist forest biome.

🌡️ The annual mean temperature is 0.9 degrees Celsius (33.7 degrees Fahrenheit). See the temperatures page for a monthly breakdown and the fixed scale graph.

🐬 Average monthly temperatures vary by 33 °C (59.4°F). This indicates that the continentality type is continental, subtype truly continental.

☔ Total annual precipitation averages 460 mm (18.1 inches) which is equivalent to 460 Litres/m² (11.28 Gallons/ft²).
