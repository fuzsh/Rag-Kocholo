Deacon C. Roger O'Donnell, passed on from this life on August 10, 2021, at the age of 86.

Deacon O’Donnell was born on May 10, 1935, and ordained a permanent deacon for the Archdiocese of Detroit on October 4, 2003. He served at Church of the Divine Child, Dearborn, from his ordination until his retirement in 2018. Deacon O’Donnell was also a chaplain with Angela Hospice Home Care for multiple years.

Visitation for Deacon O’Donnell will be at Santeiu Funeral Home, 1139 Inkster Road, Garden City, MI 48135 on Wednesday, August 11th from 2 – 7 pm. A rosary will be held at 6:30 pm.

Deacon O’Donnell’s funeral Mass will be held at Church of the Divine Child in Dearborn (1055 N Silvery Ln., Dearborn, MI 48128) on Thursday, August 12th at 10 am, and instate beginning at 9 am.

Kindly pray for the repose of the soul of Deacon C. Roger O’Donnell. Keep him and his family in your prayers.
