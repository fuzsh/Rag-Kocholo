Gro Harlem Brundtland (born 2 April 1939) was Prime Minister of Norway from 4 February to 14 October 1981 (succeeding Odvar Nordli and preceding Kare Willoch), from 9 May 1986 to 16 October 1989 (succeeding Willoch and preceding Jan Syse), and from 3 November 1990 to 25 October 1996 (succeeding Syse and preceding Thorbjorn Jagland). She was a member of the Norwegian Labor Party's moderate wing.
