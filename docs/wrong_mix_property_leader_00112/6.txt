Honorary citizen of Bihać and the former President of Croatia Stjepan Mesić will visit the municipality of Bihać today, where he will meet with the Mayor Emdžad Galijašević and the Chairman of the Municipal Council Roman Jurić.

Mesić will visit the cultural center and hold a lecture on the topic “Croatian Accession to the EU, Opportunities and Benefits for BiH and Bihać Muncipality”.
