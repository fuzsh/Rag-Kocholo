Veritas Christian Academy is an independent, non-denominational, PreK - 12, college preparatory school with an educational culture built on the classical Trivium and historic Biblical Christianity, located in the foothills of the Blue Ridge Mountains in Fletcher, North Carolina in between Hendersonville and Asheville.

Established in 1998, Veritas has committed itself to the highest educational standards. Veritas fulfills its mission through the Classical curriculum and a Christ-centered approach.
