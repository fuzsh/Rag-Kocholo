Guests from Norma Shearer and Irving Thalberg's wedding included many notable names in Hollywood at the time: (From left to right) Film director Jack Conway, a friend of the bride Bernice Ferns, the bride's brother and MGN sound engineer Douglas Shearer, actress Marion Davies, the bride Norma Shearer and her groom Irving Thalberg, the groom's sister Sylvia Thalberg, Louis B Mayer, sisters Edith and Irene Mayer, and MGM director King Vidor.
