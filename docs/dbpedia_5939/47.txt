Torrent Power Ltd (Torrent Power), a subsidiary of Torrent Investments Pvt Ltd, is a power utility company. The company's primary activities encompass power generation, transmission, distribution, and the manufacturing and supply of power cables. Its product portfolio includes coal-based, gas-based, and renewable power plants, as well as power and control cables. It transmits and distributes power to residential, commercial and industrial customers in Ahmedabad, Gandhinagar, Surat and Dahej SEZ as well as Dholera SIR in Gujarat; Bhiwandi in Maharashtra; and in Agra in Uttar Pradesh. It offers highly personalized as well as innovative customer services for better experience and customer satisfaction. It operates through a distribution network that spans across several regions in India, including Gujarat, Maharashtra, Uttar Pradesh, and the Union Territory of Dadra & Nagar Haveli, Daman and Diu. Torrent Power is headquartered in Ahmedabad, Gujarat, India.
