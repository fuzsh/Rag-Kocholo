Mary Lee Kyle Woods, age 63 of Shepherd, Texas, passed away Monday, May 16, 2016. She was born February 24, 1953 in Jasper, Texas to parents Myrtice Jewel Kyle and Jessie Lee Stephens Kyle Hodges who preceded her in death along with her brother, Sandy LeeRoy Kyle.

After graduating from Shepherd High School she received a Bachelor’s degree in Psychology from the University of Houston. She was married and divorced with no children.

Survivors include her brothers, Joe Dennis Kyle and Myrtice Ray “Cootie” Kyle; close friend, Joan Arrendall; numerous cousins, nieces and nephews.

Graveside services will be held at 10:30 a.m., Saturday, June 4, 2016 at Farley Chapel Cemetery, Shepherd, Texas.
