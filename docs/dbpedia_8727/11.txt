J. M. E. McTaggart (1866-1925), c1920. By Roger Eliot Fry (1866-1934). English idealist metaphysician. A fellow and lecturer in philosophy at Trinity College, Cambridge, he was an exponent of the philosophy of Georg Wilhelm Friedrich Hegel and among the most notable of the British idealists. McTaggart is known for "The Unreality of Time" (1908).
