Brewster McCloud (1970)

Directed by Robert Altman.

With Bud Cort, Sally Kellerman, Michael Murphy, Shelley Duvall

Brewster McCloud is a 1970 American black comedy film directed by Robert Altman. The film follows a young recluse who lives in a fallout shelter of the Houston Astrodome, where he is building a pair of wings in order to fly.
