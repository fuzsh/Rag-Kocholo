Michael Klueh (born March 15, 1987) is an American competition swimmer. He is an eight-time medalist (four gold, two silver, two bronze) at the World University Games. Klueh competed for the University of Texas from 2005 to 2009. He is a former Texas 5A state high school record-holder in the 500-yard freestyle.
