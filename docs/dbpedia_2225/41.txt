Arthur J. Wagner OBITUARY

Wagner, Arthur J. passed away peacefully on March 5 due to complications from Covid. Art was born in Pontiac to Arthur and Helen Wagner. Art graduated from St Fredericks school 1961. He then attended Rets electronic school. He started his career in 1963 working for Mcdonnell aircraft, where he worked on the Gemini space Program. He then returned to Michigan from St Louis to work with Vought Corp, working for the us Army on the Lance missile for 14 years. Art finished his career at TRW automotive electronics. He Retired as a supervisor in 1999 from the test engineering lab. Arthur was preceded in death by his Father Arthur Wagner and his Mother Helen Wagner, Sister Patricia and Margaret Wagner. He was also preceded by his brother Raymond Wagner and Danny Wagner. Also his sister in law Lynda Wagner. And 2 nieces Michele Lynn and Margaret Ann. Art is survived by Ray and Cathy Wagner, Jack and Joanne Childers, Lorraine Farnsworth and James and Robert Wagner. And many great nieces and nephews and great great nieces and nephews. Cousin Katherine Kriueff, Pat Smith, Lucille and Sharon. And good friend Billy Holmsworth. Funeral services will be held privately. Arrangements entrusted to COATS FUNERAL HOME- WATERFORD. Interment Mount Hope Catholic Cemetery in Pontiac, Michigan. Art was loved by many and loved his dogs. He will be missed by many.
