Valis: The Fantasm Soldier Collection II (夢幻戦士ヴァリス COLLECTION II, Mugen Senshi Valis COLLECTION II) is a second compilation from the 4th main Valis game, an Sega Genesis and MSX Version of the first game and the SD Deformed of the second game. It was released for the Nintendo Switch on September 9, 2022 in Japan, was developed by D4 Enterprise and published by Eida.
