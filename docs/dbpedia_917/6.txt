The individual suffered serious injuries in the incident near Whitecraigs at around 11.15am today.

Police say they are not treating it as suspicious and no further details on the person have been released.

Services between Neilston and Glasgow Central remain subject to cancellation and delay.

A spokesman from British Transport Police said: "British Transport Police were called to Whitecraigs railway station at 11.16am today following reports of a casualty on the tracks.

"Paramedics also attended, and a person has been taken to hospital with serious injuries.

"This incident is not being treated as suspicious."
