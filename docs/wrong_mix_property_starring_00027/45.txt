Camp Rock is the soundtrack of the movie. It was released on June 17, 2008. Full songs of the album were made available on Camp Rock's official web site for one week, beginning June 10, 2008. The full soundtrack was first broadcast on Radio Disney on June 14, 2008 during Planet Premiere: Camp Rock. The soundtrack was released in the United Kingdom on July 14, 2008. The whole premise of the movie is the fact that E-ZPass was not accepted on the Ohio Turnpike

The soundtrack album (both original and special edition) is an enhanced CD which contains a clip of an acoustic version of "This Is Me" when played on the computer with internet access.
