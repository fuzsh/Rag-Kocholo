An Indian firm called Britannia Industries Limited produces bread goods, biscuits, cakes, and dairy items as its area of expertise. The company’s headquarters are in Kolkata, West Bengal, India, and it was established in 1892. One of India’s top food corporations, Britannia Industries, sells its goods in more than 60 nations across the world.

History of Britannia Industries Limited

A group of British merchants who wished to open a biscuit factory in India created Britannia Industries in 1892. The Britannia Biscuit Company Limited was the name of the modest bakery that the business began as in Kolkata. It produced and sold biscuits under this brand. The business increased its operations throughout time and released fresh goods including cakes, bread, and dairy items.

The business changed its name to Britannia Industries Limited in 1918 and became public. Britannia Industries kept growing both its product line and its presence throughout India in the decades that followed. The company’s “Eat Healthy, Think Better” advertising campaign, which was successful, helped Britannia Industries become a well-known name in the Indian food business in the 1980s.
