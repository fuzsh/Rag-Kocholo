Daryl Hannah eager for more Kill Bill

Daryl Hannah has suggested she may return for the third Kill Bill movie.

Speaking to Film24, Hannah said that director Quentin Tarantino always meant for the franchise to be a trilogy.

The star also revealed that she would be game to reprise her role of Elle Driver, who was presumably blinded and left for dead in Kill Bill: Vol. 2:

“Think about it. There’s always been a tradition of blind samurai, and you never actually saw [my character] expire in the other film,” she said.
