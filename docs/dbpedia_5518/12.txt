Welcome to the Diocese of Honolulu Careers Page

The Roman Catholic Church in the State of Hawaii is comprised of 66 parishes and 32 Catholic schools within the state. The Catholic Church employs approximately 1550 individuals serving in ministry, education, administration and facilities and maintenance. As one family, our purpose is to give witness to Jesus and minster to our children, families, communities and those in need.

We invite you to explore employment opportunities at our various parishes and schools.
