Reichskommissariat Norwegen is a colony of Germany situated in Northern Europe, comprised of the territory of the former Kingdom of Norway. Reichskommissariat Norwegen borders the Kingdom of Sweden and the Republic of Finland to the East, the Barents Sea to the North, and the Norwegian and North Seas to the West.

Reichskommissariat Norwegen is one of two Reichskommissariats to not share National Socialism with Germany. Instead, it's a fascist state with Reichskommissar Josef Terboven as its head.

As of 1962, Reichskommissariat Norwegen is a part of the Einheitspakt. Its main language is Norwegian, although it declares German to be an official language. It uses the Reichsmark as its currency.
