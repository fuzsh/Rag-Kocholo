Boston Public is an American television drama series created by David E. Kelley for Fox which aired from 2000 to 2004.

Premise[]

The show focused on the personal & professional lives of the staff at the fictional Winslow High School in Boston, Massachusetts.

Why It Was Cancelled[]

After "Boston Public" was moved to the Friday night death slot for its fourth season, the ratings declined and it was cancelled after the 13th episode aired on January 30, 2004. The final two episodes aired in March of 2004.
