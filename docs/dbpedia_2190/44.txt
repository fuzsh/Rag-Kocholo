Boston Public was a FOX network drama TV series that was created by David E. Kelley. It first premiered on October 23, 2000.

The cast members throughout the run of series consisted of Chi McBride, Fyvish Finkel, Loretta Devine, Anthony Heald, Jeri Ryan, Jessalyn Gilsig, Nicky Katt, Rashida Jones, Michael Rapaport, Sharon Leal, Natalia Baron, Joey McIntyre & Jon Abrahams.

Initially, the show preceeded "Ally McBeal" on Monday nights and it became popular, receiving critical acclaim for its drama and ethnically diverse cast.

When Fox moved it to the Friday night death slot during its fourth season, viewership went down and on January 30, 2004, it was cancelled after the 13th episode of the season had aired. Production on the show was halted after the 15th episode was completed.

"Boston Public" had aired for 4 seasons and 81 episodes.

Plot[]

Set in Boston, "Boston Public" centers on Winslow High School, a fictional public high school in the Boston Public Schools district.

It features a large ensemble cast and focuses on the work and private lives of the various teachers, students and administrators at the school.
