Who is Daniel Wass?

Daniel Wass is 35-years-old, born on 31 May 1989, and is a right-footed professional football player from Denmark. Born in Gladsaxe, Daniel Wass has also played in Copa del Rey for Valencia, in Primera Division for Atletico Madrid, in Copa del Rey for Celta and in Coupe de France for Thonon Évian. He currently plays as a Defensive Midfielder, Midfielder (Centre) in Superliga for club Brøndby.
