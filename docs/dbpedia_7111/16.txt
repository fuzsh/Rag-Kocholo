EXOR NV (Exor) is a diversified investment holding company, primarily involved in long-term capital appreciation investments in global leading companies. The company's portfolio is largely composed of investments in listed companies such as Ferrari, Stellantis, CNH Industrial, and Philips, which represent a significant portion of its invested asset value. Additionally, Exor has investments in a variety of listed and private companies across different sectors. The company also invests through its wholly owned alternative investment manager, Lingotto, and its venture capital arm, Exor Ventures. Exor operates globally, with investments in various sectors and geographies, including the US, EMEA, and APAC. Exor is headquartered in Amsterdam, the Netherlands.
