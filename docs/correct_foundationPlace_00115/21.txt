Description

Silicon Graphics Computer Systems (also known as Silicon Graphics, Inc, or SGI) was founded in 1982 by Dr. James Clark. Headquartered in Mountain View, SGI manufactures high performance computing technology and is known for both its 3D visualization workstations and its design of graphics software. In the first image, SGI was located at 630 Clyde Avenue. By the time the second and third images were taken in 1995, SGI was located at 211 No. Shoreline Blvd. Mountain View, California.
