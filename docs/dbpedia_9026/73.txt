Maya is an associate with the Firm’s litigation practice. In that role, Maya primarily assists BSM’s dealership clients with regulatory issues, contract review, franchise-related litigation, labor and employment matters, and consumer disputes.

Maya graduated from Florida A&M University College of Law in 2021. While in law school, Maya competed on the Moot Court team, interned with the United States Attorneys Office for the Northern District of Florida, Tallahassee Division, and interned with the Honorable Mark E. Walker, Chief Judge, United States District Court, Northern District of Florida. Upon graduation from law school, Maya served as a law clerk to the Honorable Monte C. Richardson, United States Magistrate Judge, Middle District of Florida, Jacksonville Division.
