ALBANIA Socialist People's Republic (1976-1990) - Albanian lek [ALL]

1 lek = 100 qindarka

In 1976, when the official name of Albania was changed into Socialist People's Republic of Albania there were mostly banknotes in circulation. Only in 1988 a short series of change coins was issued. The new full name of the country was placed only on coins with face values 1 and 2 leke.
