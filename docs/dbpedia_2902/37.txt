Austin is a lively city in Texas full of music, great food and outdoor activities. Amtrak’s Texas Eagle train stops near downtown and the Colorado River, and you’ll need to board this train in one of the cities on the route in order to reach Austin.

Major cities with direct train service to Austin:

Chicago

St. Louis

Little Rock

Dallas

Fort Worth

Austin

San Antonio

El Paso

Tucson

Palm Springs

Los Angeles

To view your trip options, start the ticket booking process on the Amtrak.com homepage. You can view the train schedules and transfer options without buying tickets.
