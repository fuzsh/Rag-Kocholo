Svanemøllen station is an S-train station in the Østerbro district of Copenhagen served by the A, H, B, C, and E services in Copenhagen, Denmark.Svanemøllen station was inaugurated on 15 May 1934 simultaneously with the opening of the Boulevardbanen between Østerport station and Copenhagen Central Station also known Røret .
