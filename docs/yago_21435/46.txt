Wladyslaw I Herman Piast of Poland, Duke of Poland, was born circa 1044 to Casimir I of Poland (1016-1058) and Maria Dobroniega of Kiev (c1011-1087) and died 4 June 1102 of unspecified causes. He married Judith of Bohemia (c1056-1086) 1080 JL . He married Judith of the Holy Roman Empire (1054-c1094) 1089 JL .
