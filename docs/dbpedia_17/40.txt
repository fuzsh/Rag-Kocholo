Chalco de Díaz Covarrubias

We have collected many facts about the city of Chalco de Díaz Covarrubias, as well as pictures, maps or statistics.

So you will learn about this incredible town of 174,704 inhabitants of México.

The town of Chalco de Díaz Covarrubias belongs to the municipality of Chalco (State of México). There are 174,704 inhabitants and it is 2,237 meters altitude. The Magical Town of Tlayacapan is 47 minutes away by car (47 km). Of all the towns in the municipality, it occupies the number #1 in terms of number of inhabitants.
