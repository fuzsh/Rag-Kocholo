Chalco de Díaz Covarrubias, also known as Chalco, is a city located in the eastern part of the State of Mexico, Mexico, approximately 35 kilometers southeast of Mexico City. The city has a subtropical highland climate with average temperatures ranging from 12 to 25 degrees Celsius throughout the year. Chalco has a rich history dating back to the pre-Hispanic period, with evidence of human settlements in the area from as early as 1200 BC. The city played a significant role in the Mexican Revolution and is known for its cultural heritage, including traditional dances, music, and cuisine.
