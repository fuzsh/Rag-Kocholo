Antonio Lopez Pujol, 84, passed away Tuesday, April 15, 2014 at his residence. He was born April 8, 1930 in Barcelona, Spain to Juan Lopez Canovas and Teresa Pujol Furness. He married Josephine Brown on October 6, 1952 in Montrose, Colorado. She preceded him in death on August 17, 2003.

Antonio lived in California for a number of years and in 2000 he moved to Mesquite, Nevada. He enjoyed reading at the library, watching games shows like Wheel of Fortune, and gambling at the Eureka Casino.
