Three Wise Fools is a very odd movie, but the oddness here manages to (for me) end up as being mostly charming. This starts with a group of fairies/pixies telling a story being told about a woman leaving her three suitors to go off with a sudden Irish suitor instead, and a whole bunch of skepticism about if humans exist. Which is an odd take to get to the core of the story, which is a young orphaned girl showing up in the lives of the titular three wise fools to get them to somewhat reform their views.
