OVERVIEW - BRITANNIA INDUSTRIES LTD

Britannia Industries Ltd is a listed public company incorporated on 21 March, 1918. It is classified as a public limited company and is located in Kolkata, West Bengal. It's authorized share capital is INR 50.00 cr and the total paid-up capital is INR 24.09 cr.

Britannia Industries Ltd's operating revenues range is Over INR 500 cr for the financial year ending on 31 March, 2023. It's EBITDA has increased by 31.24 % over the previous year. At the same time, it's book networth has increased by 32.41 %. Other performance and liquidity ratios are available here.
