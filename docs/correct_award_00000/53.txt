Category of Nobel prize Nobel Prize Winner Peace

Jean Henry Dunant was the first recipient of the Nobel Prize in Peace.

He was awarded for "promotion of peace and fraternity among nations"

He founded International Committee of the Red Cross

Literature

Sully Prudhomme was the first recipient of the Nobel Prize in Literature.

He was awarded for his "poetic composition"

Physics

Wilhelm Conrad Rontgen was the first recipient of Nobel Prize in Physics

He was awarded for his "discovery of X-rays".

Medicine

Emil von Behring the first recipient of Nobel Prize in Medicine.

He was awarded for "his work on serum therapy, especially its application against diphtheria".
