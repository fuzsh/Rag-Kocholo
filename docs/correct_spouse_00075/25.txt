Poppy Montgomery has popped out a little guy.

The 42-year-old actress and husband Shawn Sanford welcomed son Gus Monroe Deveraux Sanford on Tuesday, a rep for the “Unforgettable” star told Page Six.

Baby Gus weighed 7 pounds, 4 ounces, and measured 20.5 inches long. He was born in Los Angeles.

This is the couple’s second child together: Montgomery and Sanford, a Microsoft exec, are also parents to daughter Violet Grace, who turns 2 yeas old in April.

Australian-born Montgomery also has a 6-year-old son, Jackson, from her previous relationship with actor Adam Kaufman.
