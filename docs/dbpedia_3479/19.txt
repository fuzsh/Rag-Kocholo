Lottie Williams

b. circa 1892, d. 6 May 1946

Lottie Williams was born circa 1892 in Arkansas.

She and an unknown person obtained a marriage license on 13 March 1914 with Gardner Page acting as bondsman.

She married Mathew Houston Watts, son of Shelby Watts and Mary M. Crabtree, on 15 March 1917 in Crabtree, Van Buren County, Arkansas, with T. J. Middleton, JP, officiating.

Mathew Houston Watts registered for the draft on 12 September 1918 in Van Buren County, Arkansas, while living with his wife Lottie in Crabtree, and self-employed there as a farmer.

Lottie Williams died on 6 May 1946 in White County, Arkansas.

Her husband Mathew Houston Watts became a widower at her death.
