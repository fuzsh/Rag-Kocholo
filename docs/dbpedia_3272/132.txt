In 2024, a new generation of Brazilian wonderkids are capturing the football world’s attention with their remarkable talents and significant potential. These rising stars are making waves in both domestic and international arenas, showcasing the depth of Brazil’s footballing talent.

In this week’s edition of BusinessDay Business of Sports, we take a look at five rising Brazilian stars in 2024:

Endrick Felipe

Club: Palmeiras (joining Real Madrid in July 2024)

Position: Centre-Forward

Age: 17 Years

Market Value: €60 million

Endrick Felipe, known simply as Endrick, is one of the most promising young talents in Brazilian football. His rise to prominence began in the youth ranks of Palmeiras, one of Brazil’s most successful clubs. His outstanding performances at youth levels quickly drew attention from top European clubs, leading to his highly anticipated transfer to Real Madrid. Endrick represents the next generation of Brazilian football talent, with the potential to make a significant impact on the global stage. His move to Real Madrid is a testament to his abilities and the bright future that lies ahead for this young prodigy.

Vitor Roque

Club: Barcelona in 2024

Position: Forward

Age: 19 Years

Market Value: €45 million

Nineteen-year-old Vitor Roque stands out as one of the most exciting young talents in football. His move to Barcelona marks the beginning of a new chapter in his career, where he is expected to develop into a world-class player. The football world will be watching closely as he takes on new challenges and continues to grow his reputation on the global stage.

Estêvão

Club: Palmeiras

Position: Right Winger

Age: 17 Years

Market Value: €30 million

Often compared to Lionel Messi and nicknamed ‘Messinho,’ Estêvão is praised for his technical skills and flair. His performances have made him one of the most exciting young talents in Brazil. The 17-year-old represents the latest in a long line of talented Brazilian footballers poised to make a significant impact on the world stage. His journey from Palmeiras’ youth academy to potential stardom is a story that resonates with fans and analysts alike, as they eagerly await his next steps in professional football.

Luis Guilherme

Club: Palmeiras

Position: Right Winger/Attacking Midfielder

Age: 18 Years

Market Value: €24 million

Luis Guilherme is a highly regarded young forward who has been making significant strides in Brazilian football. His journey began in the youth ranks of Palmeiras, a club known for producing top-tier talent. Guilherme’s rapid development and impressive performances have earmarked him as one of Brazil’s most promising footballers. His creativity and vision on the field have made him a key player for Palmeiras, and his versatility allows him to influence the game significantly from various positions.

Gabriel Moscardo

Club: Corinthians

Position: Defensive Midfielder

Market Value: €15 million

Gabriel Moscardo is one of the most promising young talents in Brazilian football. Born in São Paulo, he has quickly ascended through the ranks of Corinthians’ youth academy, known for producing top-quality players. Moscardo’s development and early performances have caught the attention of scouts and football enthusiasts worldwide. Known for his defensive skills and ball-handling capabilities, he is already a crucial part of Corinthians’ midfield setup at just 18 years old.

The rise of Brazilian wonderkids in 2024 highlights the country’s rich footballing tradition and its ability to produce world-class talent. Players like Endrick, Vitor Roque, Estêvão, Luis Guilherme, and Gabriel Moscardo are at the forefront of this new generation, poised to make significant impacts both domestically and internationally. Their development and performances are a testament to Brazil’s enduring legacy as a football powerhouse.
