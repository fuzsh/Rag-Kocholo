Count Stanisław Kostka Zamoyski (1775&ndash;1856) was a Polish nobleman (szlachcic), politician, landowner, patron of arts.

Stanisław was the 12th Ordynat of Zamość estates. In 1809 he became the chairman of the "Provisional Government" (rzad tymczasowy) of Galicia. He was Senator-Voivode of the Duchy of Warsaw and the Polish (Congress) Kingdom from 1810 until 1831. He founded the Zamoyski Ordynacja Library and was chairman of the "Agricultural Society". He was awarded the Order of the White Eagle on June 16, 1812. In 1822 Stanisław became the chairman of Senate in the Polish (Congress) Kingdom.

He married Princess Zofia Czartoryska on May 20, 1798 in Puławy.
