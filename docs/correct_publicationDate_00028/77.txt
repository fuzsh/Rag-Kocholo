POLYPHEMUS.

Shea, Michael.

[Sauk City, Wisconsin]: Arkham House Publishers, [1987]. Octavo, cloth. First edition. This copy signed by Shea, cover artist Harry O. Morris and foreword author Algis Budrys. Collects seven horror novellas including "The Autopsy" and "The Pearls of the Vampire Queen," a sequence from Nift the Lean, winner of the 1983 World Fantasy Award. "Shea is one of the most visually powerful creators of horror images writing today." - Barron (ed), Horror Literature 4-269. [Reference: Barron (ed), Fantasy and Horror 5-79. ].
