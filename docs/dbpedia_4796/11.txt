Discovery is a 1931 horse that was born in 1931 and raced from 1934 to 1935. He was sired by Display out of the Light Brigade mare Ariadne. He was trained by J. H. Stotler and raced for Alfred G. Vanderbilt, and was bred in Kentucky, United States by Walter J. Salmon. Discovery has raced and won at Churchill Downs, Saratoga, Suffolk Downs. His has a stakes win at the 1935 Merchants' and Citizens' Handicap.
