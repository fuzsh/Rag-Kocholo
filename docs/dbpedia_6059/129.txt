Lily in Love should have been something that I loved. It stars Maggie Smith and Christopher Plummer, and it has a very similar story to Mrs. Doubtfire and Tootsie (which I love). But unfortunately, the film never really took off for me, I was constantly struggling to connect with these two characters and understanding why they were the way they were. I won’t deny that Smith and Plummer have chemistry together, because they do, but the characters that they are playing seem like they never would have been together to begin with.
