Who is Jimmy Fallon?

James Thomas Fallon, born on September 19, 1974, in New York City, U.S., is an American comedian, TV host, and actor. He rose to fame during his tenure on Saturday Night Live from 1998 to 2004, where he co-hosted Weekend Update. Fallon later hosted Late Night with Jimmy Fallon from 2009 to 2014 before taking over The Tonight Show Starring Jimmy Fallon.
