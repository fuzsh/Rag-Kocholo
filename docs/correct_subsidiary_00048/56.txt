Intel Corp. has successfully completed the merger of its subsidiary, Intel Networks, Inc., with and into Shiva Corp., effective as of Feb. 27, 1999. Each issued and outstanding share of Common Stock (other than shares of Common Stock held by Stockholders exercising dissenters' appraisal rights) will be converted into the right to receive $6.00 in cash, without interest. Shiva Corp., a wholly-owned subsidiary of Intel, will be renamed to Intel Network Systems, Inc.
