We are in Anglona, a historical region in the north of Sardinia characterized above all by a hilly morphology and a coast that alternates rocky walls with beaches. In this article I describe a small town that became a municipality only about fifty years ago, which an enormous value from a historical and natural point of view, Santa Maria Coghinas. Before making the itinerary I asked myself, what to see in Santa Maria Coghinas ? The village, the Doria castle, the natural hot waters of the thermal baths and lots of natural beauty in the surroundings
