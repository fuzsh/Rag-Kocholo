President Mohamed Nasheed has sent a message of congratulations to Prime Minister Baburam Bhattarai of Nepal following his election to office.

President Nasheed's message to Prime Minister Bhattarai reads as follows:

Quote:

As you assume office as the Prime Minister of Nepal, the Government and the people of the Maldives join me in conveying our heartfelt congratulations to you. I am confident that under your leadership Nepal will attain greater prosperity and development and continue to play a key role in the affairs of our region.

Nepal and the Maldives have enjoyed friendly relations through the years, and I look forward to work closely with you to further strengthen these ties in the years ahead.

Please accept my best wishes for your good health and well-being, as well as for the continued progress and prosperity of the people of Nepal.

Unquote.
