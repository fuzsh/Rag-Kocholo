Olivia D'Abo Biography (1969?-)

Surname is pronounced "dah-bo"; born January 22, 1969 (some sources cite 1967or 1971), in London, England; immigrated to the United States, c. 1977; daughter of Mike (a singer, musician, lyricist, composer, and radio presenter) and Maggie (a model and actress; maiden name, London) d'Abo; married Patrick Leonard (a composer); children: Oliver William.
