Heber City, Utah

Heber City is a mid-size town located on US Highway 189 just 17 miles south of Park City in the heart of the Heber Valley. Recreate in nearby Deer Creek and Wasatch Mountain State Parks. Visit the Heber Valley Aero Museum or capture stunning photographs as you take a steam engine tour on the Heber Valley Railroad.

Oakley, Utah

Oakley is 17 miles northeast of Park City tucked against the Uinta Mountains and situated along the Weber River. This small community heaves with activity during the annual Rodeo and Fourth of July Celebration and anglers and sight-seers follow the Weber River in search of scenic landscapes and large trout. Enjoy a host of activities in the national forest and Weber Cottonwood Recreation Site.

Midway, Utah

The town of Midway is bordered by Jordanelle, Wasatch Mountain, and Deer Creek State Parks, 18 miles south of Park City. During summer, attend Midway Swiss Days or the Sheepdog Championships. In the wintertime, attack the trails on snowshoes or skinny skis at Soldier Hollow Cross-Country Ski Resort.
