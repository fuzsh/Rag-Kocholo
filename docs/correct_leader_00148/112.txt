Djibouti’s government will intensify its fight against poverty, improve governance and implement renewable-energy projects to help spur economic growth, Prime Minister Dileita Mohamed Dileita said.

Dileita spoke in the capital, Djibouti, on June 19 after meeting David Craig, theWorld Bank’s country representative for Egypt, Yemen and Djibouti, who paid a courtesy call to his office. He didn’t provide further details.
