Early Origins of the Falcone family

The surname Falcone was first found in Catalonia, in northeastern Spain. One of the earliest records of the name was Saint Falcón, who died in 1147.

Migration of the Falcone family

Some of the first settlers of this family name or some of its variants were: Achilla F. H. Falco, who settled in Colorado in 1879; and Michele Falco, who was naturalized in Ashtabula Co., OH sometime between 1875 and 1906.
