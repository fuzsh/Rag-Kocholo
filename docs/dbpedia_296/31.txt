Genesis is an annual professional wrestling pay-per-view promoted by Total Nonstop Action Wrestling. The name was originally used on May 9 2003 for a two-hour DirecTV special that highlighted early moments in TNA’s history. [cite web|url=http://web.petabox.bibalex.org/web/20030621225350/nwatna.com/2003/05/06/01.shtml|title=NWA-TNA Presents Genesis|accessdate=2007-09-16]

Genesis was actually not amongst the original names used by TNA for its first twelve pay-per-view events, but ultimately was added to the rotation as the thirteenth pay-per-view name during a time of name shuffling on the part of TNA Management.

Genesis 2005 took place on November 13 2005 from the iMPACT! Zone in Orlando, Florida.

The event was dedicated to the memory of Eddie Guerrero, who had died earlier that day, although Eddie never wrestled for TNA. During the show, many wrestlers paid tribute to Guerrero by performing gestures and moves used by Guerrero. Christian Cage made his TNA debut at this event.

*Pre-Show match: Shark Boy defeated Nigel McGuinness (5:30)

**Shark Boy pinned McGuinness with a Small Package.

*Pre-Show match: The Diamonds in the Rough (Simon Diamond, David Young and Elix Skipper) defeated The Naturals (Andy Douglas and Chase Stevens) and Lance Hoyt (5:48)

**Skipper pinned Stevens after a Double Side Slam.

*Raven defeated P.J. Polaco (5:59)

**Raven pinned Polaco after a Raven Effect DDT.

*3Live Kru (B.G. James, Ron Killings and Konnan) defeated Team Canada (Eric Young, Bobby Roode and A-1) (w/Kip James as Special Guest Referee) in a Hockey Stick Fight (10:21)

**Killings pinned Young after a Nizzie Legdrop with two hockey sticks laid over Young's crotch.

*Monty Brown defeated Jeff Hardy (8:51)

**Brown pinned Hardy after a Pounce to become #1 contender to the NWA World Heavyweight Championship.

*Christopher Daniels, Samoa Joe, Alex Shelley, and Roderick Strong defeated Chris Sabin, Matt Bentley, Sonjay Dutt and Austin Aries (w/Traci) in an Elimination X Match (23:15)

**Aries pinned Strong after a Brainbuster and 450° splash (12:02)

**Daniels pinned Aries with a Small Package (12:14)

**Shelley forced Dutt to submit with the Border City Stretch (15:08)

**Bentley pinned Shelley after a Superkick (15:49)

**Joe forced Bentley to submit with a Coquina Clutch (20:45)

**Daniels pinned Sabin after the Angel's Wings (23:15)

**After the match, Samoa Joe attacked Daniels and hit him with two Muscle Busters. The second one on a Steel Chair

*Abyss (w/James Mitchell) defeated Sabu in a No Disqualification Match (10:46)

**Abyss pinned Sabu after a Black Hole Slam onto a barbed wire-wrapped chair.

*A.J. Styles defeated Petey Williams (w/A-1) to retain the TNA X Division Championship (18:19)

**Styles pinned Williams after a Super Styles Clash.

*Rhino and Team 3D (Brother Ray and Brother Devon) defeated Jeff Jarrett and America's Most Wanted (Chris Harris and James Storm) (w/Gail Kim) (15:44)

**Devon pinned Storm after a 3D.

**After the match Team Canada came down to attack Team 3D until Christian Cage (who made his debut earlier in the evening with a promo) came and did the Unprettier on Coach D'Amore. Cage then helped Team 3D put Jarrett through a table.

Genesis 2006 took place on November 19 2006 from the iMPACT! Zone in Orlando, Florida. The tagline was "The Revolution will be Televised".

*Pre-Show match: Robert Roode (w/Ms. Brooks) defeated Eric Young (3:50)

**Roode pinned Young using the ropes for leverage.

*Pre-Show match: Eric Young defeated Robert Roode (w/Ms. Brooks) (1:00)

**Young pinned Roode with a roll-up using the ropes for leverage.

**This rematch happened after referee Rudy Charles learned that Roode had cheated in the previous match.

*The Voodoo Kin Mafia (B.G. James and Kip James) defeated Kazarian, Maverick Matt and Johnny Devine (3:39)

**Kip pinned Devine after a Cobra Clutch Slam.

**Kip used the Pedigree on Bentley during the match to continue their "war" with WWE.

*The Naturals (Andy Douglas and Chase Stevens) (w/Shane Douglas) defeated Sonjay Dutt and Jay Lethal (w/Jerry Lynn) (8:30)

**Stevens pinned Dutt after a Sitout powerbomb / Missile dropkick combo.

*Christopher Daniels defeated Chris Sabin to retain the TNA X Division Championship (13:29)

**Daniels pinned Sabin with a roll-up.

*Ron Killings and Lance Hoyt defeated The Paparazzi (Austin Starr and Alex Shelley) (w/Kevin Nash) (11:11)

**Hoyt pinned Shelley with a small package.

*Christian Cage defeated A.J. Styles (15:56)

**Cage pinned Styles after sitting down during a sunset flip attempt by Styles.

*The Latin American Exchange (Homicide and Hernandez) (w/Konnan) defeated America's Most Wanted (Chris Harris and James Storm) (w/Gail Kim) to retain the NWA World Tag Team Championship (9:28)

**Hernandez pinned Storm after Homicide hit Storm with a blowtorch can.

**After the match, Jim Cornette came out and stripped LAX of the Tag Team Titles due to their actions leading up to the PPV - a decision that was later reversed.

*Abyss (w/James Mitchell) defeated Sting by disqualification to win the NWA World Heavyweight Championship (15:30)

**Sting was disqualified after clotheslining referee Rudy Charles.

**Sting lost the title because of TNA's rules that a title can change hands on a disqualification.

*Kurt Angle defeated Samoa Joe (13:42)

**Angle forced Joe to submit to the Ankle Lock.
