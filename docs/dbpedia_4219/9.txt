About

English singer and bassist best known as a member of the indie rock group Noisettes. She has also made guest vocal performances on songs by artists like Matthew Herbert and Annie Lennox. Her vocals are featured on Mark Knight's first album "Feel The Pressure".

Before Fame

She started out as an aspiring actress and danced burlesque with the Lost Vagueness group.

Trivia

She has voiced the character Teal on the British-Canadian animated series The Adventures of Abney & Teal.

Family Life

She has a twin sister named Shorai Shoniwa.
