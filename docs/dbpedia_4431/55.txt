Welcome to Centerville ...

The town of Centerville is located in Wayne County<1>.

The location of Centerville has been provided by the Geographic Names Information System (ie- the GNIS).<2>

While we don't have a date for the founding of Centerville, you might consider that their post office opened in 1818.

From the Census Estimates for 2019, Centerville has a population of 2,583 people<3> (see below for details).

Centerville is 1,000 feet [305 m] above sea level.<4>.

Time Zone: Centerville lies in the Eastern Time Zone (EST/EDT) and observes daylight saving time

The area code for Centerville: (765)

The ZIP code for Centerville: 47330<5>

Population Details ...

Taken from the 2019 Census Estimates, Centerville had an population of 2,583 people. This is an increase of 1.21% since the 2010 Census (or an increase of 6.43% since the 2000 Census).

At the time of the 2010 Census, Centerville had a population of 2,552 people. This makes Centerville the county's 2nd most populous community.

The population of Centerville represents 0.04% of the population of Indiana (which has 6,483,802 people), making it the state's 169th most populous community.

With a 2010 count of 2,552 people, the population of Centerville increased 5.15% from the 2000 Census (which had a count of 2,427 people).

More information can be found on the Census Page for Centerville.
