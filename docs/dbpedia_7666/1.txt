Norberto Santana, Jr. is an award-winning investigative journalist and has served the past 15 years as publisher of the nonprofit newsroom, Voice of OC, in Orange County, California. He has also served as a board member for Investigative Reporters and Editors, the Institute for Nonprofit News, the First Amendment group, CalAware and is a member of the NAHJ Investigative and Data Journalism Task Force. @NorbertoSantana
