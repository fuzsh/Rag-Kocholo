Background

The first single 'Shock To My System' was released in early May 2011 in Ireland.[2] The song was added to radio playlists in Ireland on the week beginning 25 April 2011.Initially, Keep Running(previously titled 'Tokyo') was scheduled to be the first single[3] though this was later released on 26 August 2011.[4][5]

No music video was made for this single.
