Ringwood Secondary College has been welcoming students for 70 years.

Founded in 1954, Ringwood Secondary College is a Year 7 - 12 co-educational College of approximately 1600 students with a strong tradition of academic excellence.

The College offers a broad, comprehensive and progressive academic program that seeks to engage every student. The curriculum provides a wealth of rich learning opportunities and a focus on the 'whole student'. The development of strong and meaningful relationships between students and teachers is a major factor in the achievement of excellent results. We continue to strive to find ways to enhance the aspirations of our students - to challenge themselves to reach their full potential.
