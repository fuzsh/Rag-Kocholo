Wild & Wonderful Outdoors Expo

The Martinsburg Roundhouse is pleased to present the Wild & Wonderful Outdoors Expo featuring vendors for every type of outdoor enthusiast. Application is open to everyone selling goods or services that would be used for outdoor recreation, from hunting and fishing to camping, hiking, biking, canoeing and more! We will also have food vendors, live bands, 50/50, and gun raffles.

Fundraising proceeds support revitalization of the Roundhouse - West Virginia's 16th National Historic Landmark.

All food vendors are expected to have the proper licensing, permits, certifications, registration, and inspections with the appropriate agencies where applicable and display them in a visible location.

City of Martinsburg's Fireworks

On July 4, at 9:30 pm, Zambelli Fireworks will be presenting the City of Martinsburg’s fireworks display from a new location on the hill at the end of E. John Street. The public will not be allowed to access E. John Street past the intersection of Oak Street.

While this is the City's first year at this location, some of the sites that folks viewed a smaller December fireworks display from included: PO Faulkner Park; the Caperton Train Station; Adam Stephen House, various areas around Downtown Martinsburg and the Berkeley 2000 Center. We believe this area will allow for more visibility and allows for the display to be set off on City property. There is not a designated or centralized gathering place for viewing the July 4th display as there are various areas where they can be viewed.

Amani Outdoor Music Jam

We are so excited to be hosting our first ever Outdoor Music Jam. 6 hours of live music on our beautiful, scenic farm accompanied by our awesome beer, cocktails, and pizza! We are starting the day off with Billy & The Curly Brothers, a band based out of West Virginia who specialize in a variety of covers that range from pub songs, folk, country, and classic rock.
