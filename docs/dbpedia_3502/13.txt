''A Special Christmas'' is an album by R&B group SWV that was released in the United States on November 16, 1997 by RCA Records. It is a holiday album featuring both cover versions and original material. ''A Special Christmas'' would be the penultimate album the trio recorded together before they disbanded in 1998. Coko, the group's lead singer, released her first solo album in 1999. - Wikipedia
