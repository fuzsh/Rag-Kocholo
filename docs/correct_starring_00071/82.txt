When fate ends their story abruptly, life gives them a second chance. Sometimes to avenge a grave injustice, and some to reunite with their love from past lives.

Quite a few Bollywood filmmakers have explored the reincarnation theme and its dramatic possibilities to fascinating levels and found success too.

Choreographer turned director Farah Khan is hoping for similar results for her much-publicised and anticipated second release, Om Shanti Om starring Shah Rukh Khan, Deepika Padukone, Shreyas Talpade and Arjun Rampal.

OSO set within the film industry deals with rebirth as well. rediff.com profiles more films on the theme.
