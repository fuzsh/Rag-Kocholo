It may have taken Dr. Neela Rasgotra six seasons to find happiness on ER, but British-born actress Parminder Nagra's real life has seen much joy as of late.

The 33-year-old Bend It Like Beckham star and her husband, celeb photographer James Stenson, welcomed a baby boy last week, her rep confirmed to Us Weekly. "Mother, daddy and baby are all doing great."

Born on May 19, son Kai David Singh Stenson is the first child for the couple, who married in January.
