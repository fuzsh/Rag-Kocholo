Erik Thommy is a 26-year-old midfielder and winger for VfB Stuttgart. Thommy was born on August 20, 1994 in Ulm, Germany, and spent his youth career playing for SV Kleinburen from 2000 to 2002, SSV Ulm 1846 from 2002 to 2009, TSG Thannhausen from 2009 to 2010, and FC Augsburg from 2010 to 2013. His senior career continued at Augsburg, where he played for both FC Augsburg and FC Augsburg II between 2013 and 2018. He played on loan to 1. FC Kaiserslautern from 2015 to 2016 and on loan to Jahn Regensburg from 2016 to 2017. He moved to his current club in January 2018, and scored his first Bundesliga goal on February 24, 2018 against Eintracht Frankfurt.
