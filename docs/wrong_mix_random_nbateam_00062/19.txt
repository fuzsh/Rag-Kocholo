Wipro was founded in 1945.

With a rich history spanning over 75 years, Wipro was established by Mohamed Premji as a vegetable oil manufacturer in Amalner, Maharashtra. Today, it has grown into a diversified conglomerate with a strong presence in IT services, consulting, and outsourcing.

Wipro is headquartered in Bengaluru.

Situated in the heart of India’s Silicon Valley, Wipro’s headquarters are located in Bengaluru, Karnataka. This strategically chosen location allows the company to stay in close proximity to a pool of talented professionals and cutting-edge technology.

Wipro is one of the largest IT services companies globally.

As of 2021, Wipro holds a significant position among the world’s top IT services companies. Its extensive range of services includes software development, system integration, cloud computing, data analytics, and much more.

The company operates in over 60 countries.

Wipro has expanded its operations worldwide, with a strong presence in more than 60 countries. This global footprint enables the company to cater to an extensive client base and deliver innovative solutions on a global scale.

Wipro employs over 200,000 professionals.

With a vast workforce spread across the globe, Wipro takes pride in its diverse and highly skilled team of over 200,000 professionals. Their expertise and dedication contribute to the company’s success and reputation.

Wipro is listed on the Bombay Stock Exchange and the National Stock Exchange of India.

Wipro’s shares are traded on the Bombay Stock Exchange (BSE) and the National Stock Exchange of India (NSE), making it easily accessible to investors in the country.

Wipro has a strong focus on sustainability.

As a responsible corporate citizen, Wipro places great emphasis on sustainability and CSR initiatives. The company actively works towards reducing its carbon footprint, promoting renewable energy, and driving social initiatives for the betterment of society.

Wipro has acquired several global companies.

Throughout its journey, Wipro has pursued a strategy of strategic acquisitions to enhance its capabilities and expand its market reach. Notable acquisitions include Appirio, a cloud consulting firm, and Designit, a global strategic design firm.

Wipro has its own innovation labs.

Wipro has established innovation labs that serve as catalysts for futuristic technological advancements. These labs focus on research and development, emerging technologies, and collaboration with partners and clients.

Wipro has won several prestigious awards.

Over the years, Wipro has been recognized with numerous awards and accolades for its exemplary performance and contribution to the IT industry. These include the Forbes World’s Best Employers, Fortune World’s Most Admired Companies, and the Ethisphere Institute’s World’s Most Ethical Companies.

Wipro is actively involved in corporate social responsibility.

Wipro is committed to making a positive impact on society through its extensive corporate social responsibility (CSR) initiatives. It focuses on education, healthcare, community development, and environmental sustainability.

Wipro continuously invests in research and development.

Recognizing the importance of innovation, Wipro allocates a significant portion of its resources to research and development. This investment enables the company to stay at the forefront of technological advancements and deliver cutting-edge solutions to its clients.

Conclusion

In conclusion, Wipro is a leading global information technology company that has made significant contributions to the industry. With its wide range of services and strong client base, Wipro has established itself as a trusted partner for businesses worldwide. The company’s innovative solutions, commitment to quality, and emphasis on sustainability have set it apart from its competitors. As Wipro continues to grow and evolve, it will undoubtedly continue to make a profound impact in the digital transformation space and beyond.
