Supported by PixelAnt Games during its development, the 2023 sim racing video game, “Forza Motorsport” was crafted by Turn 10 Studios and brought to the gaming community by Xbox Game Studios. On October 10, 2023, it made its debut on Windows and Xbox Series X/S platforms, marking the thirteenth major release in the esteemed Forza series.

The game features elements, including real-time ray tracing on the track, tire degradation, dynamic time of day, enhanced damage model with more detailed effects, paint deterioration, and accumulation of dirt. Additionally, it incorporates effects like “animated track life,” “physically based lighting and volumetric fog effects”, and “a fully procedural cloud system.” The game offers both a single-player career mode and multiple online multiplayer options.
