The Starting Line

In 1999, in Churchvill, Pennsylvania, four friends joined together and started a band called The Starting Line, a pop punk band,for fun and writing and playing music they loved to listen to. Similar bands to the Starting Line are Yellowcard, Hawthorne Heights, A New Found Glory, and Sugarcult. Band Members are:Ken Vasoli: Vocals, BassMatt Watts: GuitarMike Golla: GuitarTom Cryskiewicz: Drums
