Jason Biggs is rumored to star in Marvel's ‘Spider-Man' reboot.

Jason Biggs will probably go down in history for the guy that made love to an apple pie in ‘American Pie', unless he pulls a Bryan Cranston and starts selling crystal meth in a Breaking Bad spinoff. Since the teenage rom-com hit theaters, his acting career has been little less than amazing but, things are looking for Jason Biggs. According to reports, Biggs is going to co-star in the upcoming ‘Spider-Man' reboot.

According to Latino Review, Jason Biggs in talks to be playing the Scorpion alongside Billy Zane. That being said, this is just a rumor and Latino Review has tons of rumors floating around about the new Marvel movie.
