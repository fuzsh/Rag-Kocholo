Biography:

Sumeet Raghavan is an Indian actor renowned for his contributions to film, television, and theater. He is celebrated for his performances in various shows such as Wagle Ki Duniya – Nayi Peedi Naye Kissey, Hudd Kar Di, Sarabhai vs Sarabhai, Sajan Re Jhoot Mat Bolo, Badi Doooor Se Aaye Hai, and Sarabhai vs Sarabhai: Take 2. In cinema, he is recognized for his roles in Holiday (2014) and My Name Is Khan (2010). Beginning his career as a child actor, he portrayed the character of Sudama in B. R. Chopra’s rendition of Mahabharat. Despite his extensive work in television, he earned his first lead role in the Marathi film Sandook in 2015.

Raghavan was born on 22 April 1971 in Mumbai, Maharashtra, India, to a Tamil father named R. Raghavan and a Kannadiga mother named Prema Raghavan. He tied the knot with Chinmayee Surve in 1996.
