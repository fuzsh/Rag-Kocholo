On this weekend edition of "Washington Watch with Tony Perkins" former Southern Baptist Convention president and senior pastor of Cross Church in Springdale, Arkansas, Dr. Ronnie Floyd, guest hosted for Tony. President of Abundant Hope International in Israel, Susan Heagy, joined Dr. Floyd to talk about the historical significance of Tisha B'av, an annual fasting day recognized on the Jewish Calendar. Terry Jeffrey, Editor-In-Chief for CNS News, joined our guest host to discuss the increasing number of Christian refugee admissions under President Trump, and the decreasing number of Muslim refugee admissions. Also, Chairman of the Subcommittee on Homeland Security, Sen. John Boozman (R-Ark.), joined Dr. Floyd to weigh in on President Trump's signing of a new bill placing sanctions on Russia and North Korea.
