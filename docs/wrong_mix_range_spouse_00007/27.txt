Celebrity Face Recognition

Purpose

The ‘Celebrity’ model analyzes images and returns probability scores on the likelihood that the media contains the faces of over 10,000 recognized celebrities.

Intended Use

Any one who needs to identify famous people in their media. This could be used by video and streaming platforms, image hosts, photography applications, and digital asset management.

Limitations

Celebrity faces outside the 10,000 people used to train the model.
