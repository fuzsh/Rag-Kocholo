A surprisingly engaging animated action film, Kevin Munroe's "TMNT" offers what may be the best cinematic take on Eastman and Laird's Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles so far. Spry animation and action beats, along with a solid story, make the film a thoroughly enjoyable look at the sewer-dwelling heroes.

Unfolding in media res in relation to turtle lore, "TMNT" revolves around the four teenaged turtles, separated by life and growth, coming back together to engage in a battle against an ancient evil. That evil involves monstrous beasts and legendary villains, and the young crime fighters must put down their differences to fight for their home turf.
