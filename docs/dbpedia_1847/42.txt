Wall of Honor Level: Air and Space Leader

Honored by:

Mr. John L. Doyle

Captain Doyle was a successful fighter pilot in WWI with 15 victories with 56 & 60 Squadrons. He lost a leg near the end of the war.

He joined the Royal Air Force in 1940 and became a trained instructor and Chief Ground Instructor at several RAF bases.

He was born in 1893 and died in 1974.
