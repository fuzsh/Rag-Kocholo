Rahul Varun (born Rahul Mishra; 5 August 1993)[1][3] is a Bollywood journalist and actor.[4][5] He is best known for playing the role of Mohit Malhotra in the 2020 Hindi-language comedy film Pagleaazam.[6][7][8][9] He is the founder of The Filmy Charcha,[10][11] a Mumbai-based news agency.[12][13][14]
