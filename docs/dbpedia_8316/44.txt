Mohit Malhotra is an Indian Television actor known for his works in Hindi serials. He has done few print commercial ads for Pepsi, Minute Maid, Royal Enfield, Aircel, V-mart etc. He started his television career with MTV show "Splitsvilla 2".

Early Life of Mohit Malhotra

Mohit Malhotra was born on April 4, 1986 in New Delhi. He is a graduate with honors in Management from Delhi University and has also done specialized course in international business from the elite University of California, Berkeley. He later attended Georgetown University, McDonough School of Business to complete his MBA though he left school to pursue a career in acting. His brother is Himannshoo Malhotra who is a popular television actor.
