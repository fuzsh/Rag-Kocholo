Part of epistolary inheritance of the prominent Ukrainian church and state figure, scientist, metropolitan Ilarion (Ohienko) (1882–1972) is contained in a book. Correspondence lights up life and activity and metropolitan Ilarion, and him numerous correspondents – prominent representatives of the Ukrainian orthodox clergy, research workers, writers, publicmen, and others like that. Intended for historians, philologists, researchers of history of Ukrainian Orthodox Church, and also all, who is interested in a native culture and history.

Book in Ukrainian

566 Pages
