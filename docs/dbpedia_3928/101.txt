Recently deceased movie star Debbie Reynolds will be honored by the Stanford Theatre at 221 University Ave. in Palo Alto with a double feature this weekend.

The comedy “The Pleasure of His Company” (1961, co-starring Fred Astaire) will be screened at 7:30 p.m. Friday, Jan. 6, through Sunday, Jan. 8, and 3:40 p.m. on Saturday and Sunday.

“The Catered Affair” (1956), will be shown at 5:45 p.m. and 9:35 p.m. Friday through Sunday.

Reynolds died on Dec. 28, 2016, the day after the death of her daughter, actress Carrie Fisher.
