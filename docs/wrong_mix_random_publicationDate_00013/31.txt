A 40-year-old man is dead after a shooting in Birmingham Sunday evening.

Birmingham Police were called to the 2300 Block of 7th Avenue North just after 6:00 P.M. on a call of a person shot. Officers were then notified of a gun shot victim at Guglielmo Marconi Park in the 600 Block of 25th Street North.

Birmingham Fire and Rescue responded to the scene and transported 40-year-old Urian Dion Woods of Birmingham to UAB Hospital where he later died.
