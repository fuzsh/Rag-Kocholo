Nencki Institute is located in the Ochota district, one of the oldest and the most beautiful districts in Warsaw, 6 km from the F. Chopin Warsaw Airport, and about 4 km from the Warsaw Central railway station. It has been ranked one of the top 3 most attractive districts in Warsaw to live in. The Institute is part of Ochota Campus a big biomedical academic and research campus which encompasses universities, research institutes, the biggest clinical hospital in Poland, as well as start-up companies, and word-class core facilities.
