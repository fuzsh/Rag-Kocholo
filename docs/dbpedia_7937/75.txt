Adah, Pennsylvania is located in the Eastern Time Zone, which means that the time is Eastern Standard Time (EST) during regular time and Eastern Daylight Time (EDT) when daylight savings is in effect.

Eastern Standard Time

When it is Eastern Standard Time, Adah, Pennsylvania is UTC-05:00 which means that it is -5 hours offset from Coordinated Universal Time (UTC).
