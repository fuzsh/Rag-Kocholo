Taaqatwar is a 1989 Indian Bollywood action film released on Jun 16, 1989. The film is directed by David Dhawan, produced by Bobby Raj , music composed by Anu Malik.

It is a story of how Munjal (Played by Anupam Kher) is a self preserved individual who seeks his motive to clear each and every statement. He uses John to play his revenge against each and every motive. He is the man who seeks revenge against Sharma’s family. He then comes to know that he shares some emotional bonding with these Sharma’s tribe, so later he seeks his revenge against Manju.
