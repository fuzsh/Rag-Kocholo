About Paul.

Paul Davis is the owner of the Paul Davis Law Firm, LLC in Lawrence, Kansas. Davis is a lifelong resident of Lawrence, Kansas. He graduated with a Bachelor of Science in Political Science from the University of Kansas in 1995 and with a Juris Doctor from the Washburn University School of Law in 1997.

Davis has been in private practice since 2003. He formerly served as the Legislative and Ethics Counsel for the Kansas Bar Association from 1999-2003. Davis focuses his practice in the areas of estate planning, probate, real estate, and family law. He is a member of the Douglas County Bar Association, Kansas Bar Association, and American Bar Association. Additionally, Davis is a member of the Kansas Bar Association Real Estate, Probate and Trust Section and the American Bar Association General Practice, Solo and Small Firm Section. He also serves as the Vice President of Programming for the Douglas County Estate Planning Council.

Davis has been active in the Lawrence community having served on the board of directors for the Arc of Douglas County, Health Care Access, and Leadership Lawrence. He also served from 2003-2015 in the Kansas House of Representatives, representing the 46th District of east and central Lawrence.

Davis and his wife Stephanie, a psychologist with U.S. Department of Veterans Affairs, have one daughter.
