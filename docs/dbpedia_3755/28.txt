Paul Davis joined Frieden & Forbes as Of Counsel in July of 2020. He graduated with a Bachelor of Science in Political Science

from the University of Kansas in 1995 and with a Juris Doctor from the

Washburn University School of Law in 1997.

Davis has been in private practice since 2003. He formerly served as the

Legislative and Ethics Counsel for the Kansas Bar Association from

1999-2003. Davis focuses his practice in the areas of estate planning,

probate, real estate, and government relations. He is a member of the

Douglas County Bar Association, Kansas Bar Association, and American Bar

Association. Additionally, Davis is a member of the Kansas Bar

Association Real Estate, Probate and Trust Section and the American Bar

Association General Practice, Solo and Small Firm Section. He also

serves as the Vice President of Programming for the Douglas County

Estate Planning Council.

Davis served from 2003-2015 in the Kansas House of Representatives, representing the 46th District of east and central Lawrence. He was the House Minority Leader from 2009-2015.
