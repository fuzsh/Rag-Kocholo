Lilli Palmer was born on May 24, 1914 in Posen, Prussia, Germany [now Poznan, Wielkopolskie, Poland]. She was an actress and writer, known for Anastasia: The Czar's Last Daughter (1956), The Four Poster (1952) and Cloak and Dagger (1946). She was married to Carlos Thompson and Rex Harrison. She died on January 27, 1986 in Los Angeles, California, USA.
