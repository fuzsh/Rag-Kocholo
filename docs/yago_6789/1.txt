CAIRO, May 6--Narriman Sadek, 17-year-old Egyptian commoner, was married to King Farouk and became Queen of Egypt today. Her uncle, Mohammed Aly Sadek Bey, Egyptian Minister to the Netherlands, signed the marriage act at the Koubbeh Royal Palace on the road from suburban Heliopolis to Cairo, while the bride was still at the home of her mother, a modest two-story dwelling in Heliopolis.
