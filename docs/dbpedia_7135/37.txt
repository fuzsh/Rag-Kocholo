The International Airlines Group (IAG) is an Anglo-Spanish multinational airline holding company headquartered in London, England. It is the parent company of British Airways, Iberia, Aer Lingus, Vueling, and Level. IAG was formed in January 2011 after the merger of British Airways and Iberia, and is the sixth-largest airline company in the world by number of passengers carried.

The qualifications and experience required for IAG jobs vary depending on the role. Generally, candidates must possess a relevant tertiary qualification such as a degree or diploma in a relevant field, and have experience in the field. Some roles may also require specific professional qualifications or certifications. Additionally, IAG looks for candidates who demonstrate strong communication and interpersonal skills, the ability to work in a team environment, problem-solving skills, and the ability to work under pressure.

The recruitment process for IAG typically involves the following steps: 1. Submit an application: Applicants can submit their application either online or via post. 2. Initial screening: Your application will be carefully reviewed by an experienced recruitment team. 3. Telephone interview: A telephone interview will be conducted with a member of the recruitment team. 4. Assessment centre: If you are successful in the telephone interview, you will be invited to attend an assessment centre. 5. Final interviews: You may be invited to attend a face-to-face interview with a member of the senior management team. 6. Job offer: If you are successful in the recruitment process, you will be offered a job with IAG.
