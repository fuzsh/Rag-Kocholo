William Rudolph O'Donovan was an American artist who was born in 1844. The Metropolitan Museum of Art featured William Rudolph O'Donovan's work in the past.William Rudolph O'Donovan's work has been offered at auction multiple times, with realized prices ranging from 60 USD to 400 USD, depending on the size and medium of the artwork. Since 2012 the record price for this artist at auction is 400 USD for Virginia Gerson, sold at Heritage Auctions, Dallas in 2015. The artist died in 1920.
