Directions

Liberty Hall is located at 644 Massachusetts Street in downtown Lawrence, Kansas. A map and directions are available here via Google Maps.

Parking

Parking for patrons of Liberty Hall is available at metered spots lining Massachusetts Street, and in hourly lots throughout the downtown Lawrence area (mapped below).

Parking is free after 6pm Monday through Saturday, and is free all day on Sundays.

We recommend giving yourself a little extra time for parking before arriving to a movie or event.
