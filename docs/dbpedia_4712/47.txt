The Score a Pie program is only available for fans in the stadium, on home games when the home team score the first goal in the first period. You'll have the opportunity to Score A Pie at Edmonton Oilers and Calgary Flames games.

iPhone:

Go to Settings and tap Notifications. Go to Settings > Notifications. Choose how you want notifications displayed on the Lock Screen: Select the Pizza 73 app under Notification Style. Tap Allow Notifications.

Android:

Open your device's Settings app. Tap Notifications. App settings. Under "Most recent," find apps that recently sent you notifications. To find more apps, in the dropdown menu, tap All apps. Tap the app. Turn the app's notifications on or off.
