Located in Buffalo, NY, the Bennett School of Innovative Technology is an educational institution that prides itself on fostering innovative thinking and technological skills among its students. With a commitment to preparing students for the rapidly evolving world of technology, Bennett School offers a wide range of courses and programs designed to develop critical thinking, problem-solving, and communication skills. Bennett School's athletics programs are as impressive as its academic offerings. The school's state-of-the-art facilities cater to a variety of sports, providing students with the opportunity to excel in their chosen athletic pursuits. The school is a proud member of the state athletics association, which enables students to compete at a high level against other schools while fostering a sense of teamwork, discipline, and physical fitness. Bennett School's athletics programs not only promote healthy competition but also instill important values such as sportsmanship and perseverance in its students.
