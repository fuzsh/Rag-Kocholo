Bennett High School, located in the town of Bennett, CO, is an educational institution that prides itself on providing a well-rounded experience for its students. With a commitment to excellence, the school's athletics programs are a shining example of dedication and teamwork. From the football field to the basketball court, the Bennett Tigers compete with utmost passion and sportsmanship. Bennett High School is a proud member of the state athletics association and actively participates in various athletic events and competitions. The school's affiliation with the association allows students to showcase their skills and talents while representing their beloved Tigers. With a supportive and dedicated coaching staff, athletes at Bennett High School receive the guidance they need to excel in their respective sports and become well-rounded individuals both on and off the field. The school's commitment to athletic excellence fosters a sense of community and pride among students, faculty, and the loyal Bennett community.
