Chukwudiebere “Chu” Maduabum, sometimes credited as “Chuk”, “Chuck”, or “Chu Chu”, is a Nigerian professional basketball player for the Tokyo United Basketball Club of the B.League. He was drafted 56th overall by the Los Angeles Lakers in the 2011 NBA draft before his rights were traded to the Denver Nuggets.

Chukwudiebere Maduabum was born on March 19, 1991, in Lagos, Lagos, Nigeria. His birth geographical coordinates are 6° 27’ 15” North latitude and 3° 23’ 41” East longitude. Chukwudiebere Maduabum is currently 33 years old.

Astrologically, Chukwudiebere Maduabum falls under the Sun sign of Pisces, with Gemini as his Ascendant, Taurus as his Moon sign, and Sagittarius as his Black Moon Lilith sign.
