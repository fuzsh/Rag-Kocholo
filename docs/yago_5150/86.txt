Jill Hennessy is a Canadian-born actress well known to television audiences for her roles as Claire Kincaid in three seasons of the series Law & Order and as Dr. Jordan Cavanaugh in the series Crossing Jordan. She also appeared in the films RoboCop 3, Dawn Rider (2012) and Most Wanted.
