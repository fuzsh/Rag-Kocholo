Lawrence Muggerud (born January 28, 1968), better known by his stage name DJ Muggs, is an American hip hop record producer and DJ. He began his career as a member of The 7A3, and later went on to join Cypress Hill. He has also produced songs for Funkdoobiest, House of Pain and Ice Cube, among others. He is the leader of the Los Angeles collective Soul Assassins.
