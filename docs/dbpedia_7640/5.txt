Larramie Cortez "Doc" Shaw (born April 24, 1992 in Atlanta, Georgia, United States) is an American actor. He is perhaps best known for his role as Malik Payne, in Tyler Perry's House of Payne, for which he won a Young Artist Award in 2009 for best supporting performance in a TV series. Shaw commenced his acting career by appearing in the film Nobody Loves Me!

Career[]

Shaw began starring in television commercials before appearing in House of Payne. Larramie was a presenter at the 39th annual NAACP Image Awards ceremony. He joined the cast of Disney Channel sitcom The Suite Life on Deck for the show's second season which began airing on August 7, 2009. He plays Marcus Little(lil little) a former Grammy Award winner whose career ended after his record label dumped him. The first time the characters of The Suite Life on Deck series met Marcus, they thought he was in the Witness Protection Program. He left The Suite Life on Deck following the episode "Bon Voyage", to join the new Disney XD Show "Pair of Kings".
