OVERVIEW:

Chevy Truckin was published by McMullen Argus Publishing, a division of Primedia. It provided news and information for the Chevy truck enthusiast. The publishers also produced Truckin'.

The title started with a two times per year schedule, but in its fourth year it went to quarterly.
