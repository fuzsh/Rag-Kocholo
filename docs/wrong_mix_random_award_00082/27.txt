Chris Coghill is an English actor who played Mercedes McQueen's ex-boyfriend Johnny in season 4 of Hollyoaks Later.

Coghill has also appeared in Emmerdale, Coronation Street, Cold Feet, Heartbeat, New Tricks, The Bill, Shameless, Hotel Babylon, Waterloo Road, Holby Blue, The Royal, Blue Murder, EastEnders, Silent Witness, Suspects, Holby City, Endeavour, Line of Duty, Doctors and Casualty.
