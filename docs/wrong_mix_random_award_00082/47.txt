Chris Coghill was an actor most recognized for his memorable roles in dramatic films. Coghill began his career with roles in the Steve Coogan dramedy "24 Hour Party People" (2002) and "Someone Else" (2006). He then acted in "Secret Diary of a Call Girl" (Showtime, 2007-2011) and "Home Fires" (PBS, 2014-). More recently, Coghill acted in the comedic drama "Spike Island" (2015) with Emilia Clarke.
