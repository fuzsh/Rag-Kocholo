Dean Martin was born on June 7, 1917, in Steubenville, Ohio, to an Italian father, Gaetano Alfonso Crocetti (15 dec 1894–29 Aug 1967), and an Italian-American mother, Angela Crocetti (née Barra; 18 Dec 1897–25 Dec 1966).

They were married in Steubenville, Jefferson, Ohio, 25 dec 1914.

His father, who was a barber, was originally from Salino an hamlet of Montesilvano, which no longer exists.

Until 1927 Montesilvano was part of the province of Abruzzo Ulteriore 1, known later as Teramo. Now Montesilvano is part of the province of Pescara.

Gaetano Crocetti was born on 15 dec 1894 to Giovanni Crocetti and Focoso Maria.

Giovanni Crocetti and Focoso Maria were married in Montesilvano on 23 February 1884.

In the marriage act we read that all 2 spouses were foundlings, sons of unknown parents.

Giovanni Crocetti dies in Montesilvano on December 20, 1925
