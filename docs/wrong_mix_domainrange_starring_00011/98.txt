Will Ferrell is an Emmy-winning and Tony-nominated actor, comedian, writer and producer who scored his big break as a cast member on Saturday Night Live. After leaving the show in 2002, Ferrell transitioned to films, starring in hits such as Zoolander, Old School, Elf and Anchorman: The Legend of Ron Burgundy. In 2009, he made his Broadway debut with the one-man show You're Welcome America: A Final Night with George W. Bush. Since leaving SNL, he has returned as a host five times. He was also a producer of the supernatural drama series Motherland: Fort Salem, as well as the 2021 comedy film Barb and Star Go to Vista Del Mar. Ferrell also starred alongside Paul Rudd on the television series The Shrink Next Door. He next stars in the movie Despicable Me 4, out July 2024.
