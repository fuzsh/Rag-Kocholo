The Lovin & Withers Investment House is located at 722 East Beale Street in Kingman, Arizona. The house was built around c. 1914. The house is in the Bungalow/Craftsman style. Lovin & Withers built the house as contractors. They used native stone. The house closely resembles the two houses on Pine Street. The house was a rental property during a growth period.

Today it is office for local lawyer. The house is on the National Register of Historic Places and the number is 86001161.
