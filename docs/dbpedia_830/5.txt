Lovin and Withers Cottages are located at the corner of Eighth and Topeka Streets in Kingman, Arizona. The five cottages were built in 1916 as an investment property, back then rentals were known as investments. The five cottages were added to the National Register of Historical Places in 1986. When you are visiting Kingman and you decide to take pictures of these homes on your own,

please do not disturb the residents. You can take a picture from the street if you want.

Descriptive name of the home is for historical reference only, it is not the residents name.
