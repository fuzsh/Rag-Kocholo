Samsung launched the Galaxy S6 smartphone in India last month starting at Rs. 49,900. We unboxed it recently, here we have the benchmarks of the smartphone. The Galaxy S6 is powered by a Octa-Core 14 nm Exynos 7420 SoC which has four ARM Cortex A57 CPUs clocked at 2.1 GHz and four ARM Cortex A53 CPUs clocked at 1.5GHz. It has 772 MHz Mali T760 MP8 GPU and 3GB RAM LPDDR4 RAM, similar to the S6 Edge. It has a 5.1-inch Quad HD (2560Ã—1440 pixels) Super AMOLED display and runs on Android 5.0.2 (Lollipop).

Quadrant Benchmark

It scored 36141 points and lies behind the S6 Edge in the Quadrant Benchmark.

AnTuTu Benchmark 5

It tops the AnTuTu Benchmark 5 by scoring 69528 points, slightly better than the S6 Edge.

Vellamo 2.0 HTML5

It scored 2903 points in the Vellamo 2 HTML5 browser benchmark.

Vellamo 2.0 Metal

It tops the Vellamo 2 Metal CPU subsystem performance test by scoring 1590 points.

Linpack Single Thread

In the Linpack Single Thread benchmark it clocks 616.42 MFLOPS, similar to the S6 Edge.

Linpack Multi-Thread

It clocked around 777.26 MFLOPS in Linpack Multi-Thread benchmark and lies behind the S6 Edge.

Geekbench 3 Single-Core

It scored 1475 points in the Geekbench 3 Single-Core benchmark.

Geekbench 3 Multi-Core

It topped the Geekbench 3 Multi-Core benchmark by scoring 4782 points, slightly better than the S6 Edge.

NenaMark 2

It managed to clock 59.4fps in the NenaMark 2 benchmark.

GFXBench 2.7 T-Rex (On screen)

It clocked 38fps in the GFXBench 2.7 T-Rex OnScreen benchmark.

GFXBench 3.0 Manhattan (On screen)

It clocked 16fp in the GFXBench 3.0 Manhattan OnScreen benchmark.

3DMark Ice Storm Unlimited

It scored 22243 points in the 3DMark Ice Storm Unlimited GPU benchmark.

Basemark X 1.0 HD (Onscreen)

It clocked around 17.3fps in the Basemark X 1.0 OnScreen benchmark.

Basemark X 1.0 HD (Offscreen)

In the Basemark X 1.0 Offscreen benchmark it clocked around 22.2fps.

Basemark OS II
