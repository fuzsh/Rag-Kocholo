Andrei Kirilenko - Points

Andrei Kirilenko scores a career high 31 points (2004)

On February 17, 2004, Andrei Kirilenko set his career high in points in a NBA game. That day he scored 31 points in Utah's road loss against Miami, 97-85. He also had 3 rebounds, 3 assists, 2 steals and 1 block. He shot 4/5 from two, 5/9 from three, shooting at 64.3% from the field. He also shot 8/8 from the free-throw line.

Andrei Kirilenko grabs a career high 18 rebounds (2006)
