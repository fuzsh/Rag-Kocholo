On January 16, actress Natsuna (31) announced her marriage to a non-celebrity man.

In a written announcement, Natsuna shared, "I was touched by his kindness that gently wrapped around me at all times, his broad perspective toward things, and his flexible thinking. I respected him from the bottom of my heart and became attracted to him." She continued, "From now on, we will support each other to build a family full of laughter."

Hailing from Saitama Prefecture, Natsuna made her debut with the drama 'Gachi Baka!' in 2006. Then in 2009, she changed her stage name from Watanabe Natsuna to Natsuna. Since then, she has been starring in various movies, dramas, and variety programs.
