Freiburg, Baden-Württemberg, Germany

Latitude and longitude coordinates are: 47.997791, 7.842609.

Freiburg is a beautiful old city in Baden-Württemberg, in the south westernmost part of Germany, sitauted right at the border between Germany and France, as well as quite close to the border with Austria. It is quite a large city with the number of residents estimated to be about a quarter of million people. Freiburg is a cultural center and a very popular tourist spot, visited by millions of guests every year. Warm sunny climate, wonderful old architecture, plenty of bars and great places to eat, a large number of various annual events and amazing friendly people make Freiburg attractive to visitors from all around the world.
