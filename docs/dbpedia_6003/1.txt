What’s the climate like in Freiburg im Breisgau?

The current climate in Freiburg im Breisgau features temperatures ranging from a high of 26°C to a low of 6°C throughout the year. The average yearly temperature is around 16°C. At night, temperatures in the colder months average around 0°C and in the warmer months around 15°C.

The highest temperature recorded in Freiburg im Breisgau in recent years was 37°C in July 2015. The lowest daytime temperature recorded was -6°C in January 2017.

On average, Freiburg im Breisgau gets 907 mm of precipitation each year, including rain, snow, and hail. Of this, about 35 cm is snowfall. For comparison, New York gets 833 mm of precipitation each year.
