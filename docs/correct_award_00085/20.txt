Q. Among the following Nobel laureates, who was not a recipient of the Nobel Prize for Peace?

Answer: Sir Winston Churchill

Notes: Winston Churchill received the Nobel Prize in Literature in 1953 for his numerous published works, especially his six-volume set The Second World War. Linus Carl Pauling was awarded the Nobel Prize in Chemistry in 1954 and the Nobel Peace Prize in 1962, making him the only person to be awarded two unshared Nobel Prizes.
