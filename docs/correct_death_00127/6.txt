William Randolph Hearst

(1863-1951)

American newspaper publisher (1863–1951) – William Randolph Hearst was born in San Francisco (Consolidated city and county in California, United States) on April 29th, 1863 and died in Beverly Hills (City in Los Angeles County, California, United States) on August 14th, 1951 at the age of 88. Today William Randolph Hearst would be 161 years old.
