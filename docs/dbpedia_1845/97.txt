John DOYLE

27 High Street, Prescot

Private 25081, Royal Dublin Fusiliers

John Doyle enlisted into the army on 18th August 1914. Itâ€™s possible that he enlisted in Ireland and came to England when the Battalion came to the UK in September 1915

Pte Doyle went to France with his unit on 19th December 1915

He was discharged on 26th September 1918. He was awarded a pension due to a Head Wound and also because his rheumatism had been aggravated by his military service. His pension application shows him living in Prescot.

He was entitled to the Silver War Badge, 1915 Star, British War Medal and Victory Medal
