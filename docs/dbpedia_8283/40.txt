From 1887 - 1973, the St. Louis Car Company manufactured and sold streetcars and other kinds of rolling stock of street and steam railways. In succeeding years automobiles, small airplanes, millitary equipment, and other prototype vehicles were produced. These images are just a few of the thousands that make up the St. Louis Car Company Collection, located at St. Louis Car Company Collection, University Archives, Department of Special Collections, Washington University in St. Louis Libraries Please help us add information to these photos. If you can identify people, places or other details, send your comments to spec@wumail.wustl.edu or register to flickr and comment online. For more information about the St. Louis Car Company Collection, which includes more photographs, see www.library.wustl.edu/units/spec/archives/guides/bysubjec... For information regarding permission to publish these images please contact spec@wumail.wustl.edu
