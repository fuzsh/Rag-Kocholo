Established in 1969 and formerly known as Newcastle Polytechnic, Northumbria University’s international reputation as a leading British university is the result of a distinctive combination of outstanding academic research and teaching, innovative programmes, traditional values and social and cultural diversity.

Through their world-leading research and transformative education, Northumbria University enables people from all backgrounds to succeed and supports businesses and communities in the North East, London and beyond to thrive.

Northumbria University has a strong civic history which they are keen to build upon. They are committed to playing their part in the future of our place by working together as a community with their partners contributing and shaping the region’s economic and societal development.
