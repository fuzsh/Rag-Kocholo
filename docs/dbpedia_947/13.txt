At Northumbria University, it is not where you're from that matters; it is where you want to go and how you get there.

The university is amongst an elite group of institutions worldwide offering Business Degree programmes with dual Association to Advance Collegiate Schools of Business (AACSB) accreditation.

In 2022, Northumbria University was named University of the Year in the prestigious Times Higher Education (THE) Awards, widely recognised as the ""Oscars of higher education"". In 2023, the university was named Research University of the Year and Modern University of the Year by the Daily Mail.

Northumbria University is poised to enrich and empower, to make a remarkable impact on the world and help you get to where you want to be.
