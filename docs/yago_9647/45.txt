Chainsaw Scumfuck is a 1988 horror short directed by and starring Alex Chandon, as well as Ollie Bond, Tom Cox, and Matt Russel.

Young men are terrorized at home by a chainsaw and shears wielding intruder.

Victims[]

Tom - Fingers sawn off with a chainsaw, stabbed and disemboweled with garden shears by an unknown killer.

Dan - Legs sawn off and eviscerated with a chainsaw by an unknown killer.

Olli - Leg chopped off with garden shears by an unknown killer, bleeds to death off-screen.
