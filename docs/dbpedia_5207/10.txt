East London is located at latitude -33.01529 and longitude 27.91162. It is part of Africa and the southern hemisphere.

The geographic coordinate system enables any place in the world to be located using its latitude and longitude. The latitude is the position relative to the equator, specifying the north-south position. The longitude specifies the east-west position measured from a reference meridian (usually the Greenwich Prime Meridian). The latitude and longitude of East London have been calculated based on the geodetic datum WGS84.
