East London is a city in South Africa at latitude 33°01′08.98″ South, longitude 27°53′59.49″ East.

East London is a city in the Eastern Cape province of South Africa. It is the smallest of the major cities in South Africa. The Xhosa name for the city is iMonti. It is most well known as the home for Mercedes-Benz in South Africa where the factory mainly produces the C-Class compact sedan.
