Richard L. Boggs Sr., 83, of New Galilee, passed away Tuesday, April 8, 2008, in Kindred Hospital, Oakdale, Pa.

Born July 26, 1924, in Niles, Ohio, a son of the late Minter Boggs and Elmerita Lambright, he was the owner of Boggs American Service Station since 1959. He was a member of the Darlington Reformed Presbyterian Church, where he served as treasurer; a volunteer fireman and treasurer of the New Galilee Fire Department for many years, and served in the U.S. Navy during World War II.

In addition to his parents, he was preceded in death by his wife, Rose Tomeo Boggs; a sister, Gertrude Mertz, Millvale, Pa., and a brother, Robert Lambright, New Galilee.

Surviving are two sons, Richard L. Boggs Jr., Enon Valley, and Donald A. Boggs, Jacksonville, Fla.; four grandchildren, Michael A. Boggs, Hudson, N.H.; Richard J. Boggs, Darlington; Elizabeth T. Boggs and Minter A. Boggs, both of Enon Valley; a great-granddaughter, Zoee Boggs; two sisters, Elmerita Bergman, Jacksonville, Fla., and Esther Brockman, East Palestine, Ohio, and many nieces and nephews.

Friends will be received today from 2 to 4 and 7 to 9 p.m. in the CAMPBELL'S CHIPPEWA FUNERAL HOME, 2618 Darlington Road, www.campbellfuneralhomes.com. Additional visitation will be held Friday from 10 a.m. until the time of service at 11 a.m. in the Darlington Reformed Presbyterian Church, with his pastor, the Rev. Jeff Noyes, officiating.

Interment will follow in Reformed Presbyterian Church Cemetery, New Galilee, Pa.
