Liz English (April 30, London, England - is an English actress. Her only Disney role was Marie in The Aristocats.

Born in London, England, she was an actress and frequent backup singer for major stars such as Tom Jones and Engelbert Humperdinck. She is also the mother of Louise English, who went on to follow in her mother's footsteps and became an actress.

Appearing in the revue Evening Stars with Benny Hill and alongside Ron Moody in Oliver!, English would make her most prominent singing appearance as Marie in The AristoCats.
