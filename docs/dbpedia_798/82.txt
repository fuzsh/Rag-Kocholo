Annie Taylor share ‘Love Is Blind’

Annie Taylor image via Fabio Martin

Annie Taylor release the debut single from their upcoming album Inner Smile.

Annie Taylor kicks off new album Inner Smile with a raucous new track, ‘Love Is Blind.’ On the track the band successfully combines a classic 80s Pat Benetar sound with a heavier grungey guitar-driven vibe.

‘Love Is Blind’ was released on April 14th. the album Inner Smile is out on July 7th via Taxi Gauche Records.
