Felton Covered Bridge County Park, a picturesque and historic location in the center of Santa Cruz, California, provides a special fusion of outdoor activities, scenic beauty, and nostalgic appeal.

Park Features

Among the facilities available at Felton Covered Bridge County Park are the following:

The San Lorenzo River is crossed by a medieval covered bridge.

Hiking Trails: Beautiful paths for equestrian riding, bicycling, and hiking

Picnic Areas: Places set aside specifically for barbecues and picnics

Clean and well-maintained restrooms and drinking fountains

Parking is plentiful close to the park.

Schedule and Admission Charges

Operating Hours: Daily from 8 a.m. to dusk

Admission Fees: Free of charge; parking costs are extra.

$5 daily parking fees per car

Outside Activities

Outdoor activities at Felton Covered Bridge County Park include the following:

Hiking and Biking: Take a bike ride or stroll along the park's picturesque pathways.

Horseback Riding: Ride a guided horse around the trails of the park.

Enjoy a picnic in the approved locations with your loved ones.

Fishing: In the San Lorenzo River, try your luck at catching fish.

Insider Advice and Techniques

Get There Early: To guarantee parking and avoid the crowds, get to the park early.

Put on cozy shoes: When riding and trekking, put on comfy shoes.

Carry Sunscreen: Take precautions against the sun's intense rays.

Verify the weather: Examine the weather forecast and make plans appropriately.

Reaching That

It is simple to get reach Felton Covered Bridge County Park via automobile or public transportation:

Parking is plentiful close to the park.

Public Transportation: Nearby bus lines are run by Santa Cruz Metro

Recommendations

There are many lodging options in nearby towns to accommodate a variety of spending levels and tastes:

Santa Cruz: opulent lodgings, getaway homes, and resorts

Felton: Comfortable holiday accommodations, camping sites, and bed & breakfasts

Aptos: Low-cost lodging options, including motels and vacation rentals
