Jun 28, 2005 - The Portland Trail Blazers selected Martell Webster in Round 1 with Pick 6 in the 2005 NBA Draft. Jul 2, 2005 - Martell Webster signed a multi-year contract with the Portland Trail Blazers. Jan 15, 2006 - The Portland Trail Blazers assigned Martell Webster to the Fort Worth Flyers of the G League. Feb 4, 2006 - The Portland Trail Blazers recalled Martell Webster from the Fort Worth Flyers of the G League. Oct 30, 2006 - The Portland Trail Blazers exercised their Team Option to extend the contract of Martell Webster. Oct 29, 2007 - The Portland Trail Blazers exercised their Team Option to extend the contract of Martell Webster. Oct 31, 2008 - Martell Webster signed a rookie scale extension with the Portland Trail Blazers. Jun 24, 2010 - Martell Webster was acquired by the Minnesota Timberwolves from the Portland Trail Blazers in exchange for Ryan Gomes and the draft rights to Luke Babbitt. Jul 13, 2012 - The Minnesota Timberwolves placed the contract of Martell Webster on waivers. Jul 15, 2012 - Martell Webster, previously with the Minnesota Timberwolves, became a free agent. Aug 24, 2012 - Martell Webster signed a contract with the Washington Wizards. Jul 1, 2013 - Martell Webster, previously with the Washington Wizards, became a free agent. Jul 10, 2013 - Martell Webster signed a multi-year contract with the Washington Wizards. Nov 30, 2015 - The Washington Wizards placed the contract of Martell Webster on waivers. Dec 2, 2015 - Martell Webster, previously with the Washington Wizards, became a free agent. Sep 22, 2017 - Martell Webster signed a contract with the New Orleans Pelicans. Oct 1, 2017 - The New Orleans Pelicans placed the contract of Martell Webster on waivers.
