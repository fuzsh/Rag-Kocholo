1927-28 Everton FC Statistics

Everton FC of The Football League First Division ended the 1927-28 season with a record of 20 wins, 13 draws and 9 losses for 53 points finishing first in TFL1.

The club scored 102 goals, best in the league. The team yielded 66 goals, fewest in the league. Dixie Dean netted 60 goals for the team, while Tony Weldon, Alex Troup, Dick Forshaw and Ted Critchley each tallied five or more also.
