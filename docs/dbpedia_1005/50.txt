What is the weather like in Rodrigues in September?

Rodrigues is a region in Mauritius. September in Rodrigues generally has warm temperatures, with moderate precipitation.

Temperatures

You can expect warm maximum daytime temperatures of around 24°C and nighttime temperatures around 22°C in Port Mathurin.

Precipitation

September is a moderate rainy month with an average rainfall of around 34 mm in Port Mathurin.

Average sunshine

Meanwhile, Port Mathurin enjoys close to 240 hours of sunlight. This means it's a great month if you prefer bright and sunny days.
