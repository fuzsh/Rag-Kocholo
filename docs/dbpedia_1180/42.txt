Anthony Cornelius Hamilton is an American singer, songwriter, and record producer. Hailing from Charlotte, North Carolina, he signed to Jermaine Dupri's So So Def Recordings prior to the release of his second studio album Comin' from Where I'm From (2003). The album received platinum certification by the Recording Industry Association of America (RIAA) and was preceded by its lead single of the same name and its follow-up, "Charlene". He released his third and fourth albums, Ain't Nobody Worryin' (2005) and The Point of It All (2008) to continued success and gold certification by the RIAA. His fifth and sixth albums, Back to Love (2011) and What I'm Feelin' (2016), were released through RCA Records.
