Aishwarya Rai Bachchan Opens Up About Her Marriage To Abhishek Bachchan

Aishwarya Rai Bachchan and Abhishek Bachchan's 17-year marriage has been a subject of interest. Recently, they attended a wedding together but didn't pose for photos, sparking rumors of tension. However, in a 2016 interview, Aishwarya spoke about their strong bond, saying, "We're equally aware and conscious of embracing each other's side, and today we're one." She emphasized the importance of being mindful and actively participating in each phase of life, including relationships. Aishwarya highlighted that marriage is not just about two individuals coming together but also about uniting two families.
