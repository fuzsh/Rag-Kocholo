Bibi Gayton is a Mexican actress, that became an Icon of the 90’s and sang as well. She is best known for her role on the telenovela Alcanzar Una Estrella II, and 1993 telenovela Dos Mujeres, Un Camino, which was the most watched show of its season. She rose to fame in 1989 as a singer in the group Timbiriche. She married Eduardo Capetillo in 1994, which was also a Mexican icon in the 90’s. He is Mexican actor and singer. They are still currently married with several children.
