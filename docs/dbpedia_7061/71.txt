Gina Torres is an actor, singer, and producer who plays Keeper Yennen in The Legend of Vox Machina.[1]

Background[edit | edit source]

Gina was born and raised in New York City in a Cuban-American family and speaks fluent Spanish. She has had numerous supporting roles in film, television, and video games. [2]
