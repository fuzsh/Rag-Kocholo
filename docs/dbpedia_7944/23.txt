OneRepublic

OneRepublic is a alt/pop rock band formed in Colorado in 2004. Their recent success can be attributed to their hit single "Apologize." The Remix is featured onSuper-Producer Timbaland's album Shock Value. In the past few summers they have been ranked as one of the top bands on myspace.com. The bandconsists of 5 members: * Ryan Tedder- Vocals, guitar, piano * Zach Filkins - Guitar, vocals * Eddie Fisher - Drums, percussion * Brent Kutzle - Bass guitar, cello, vocals * Drew Brown - Guitar "Apologize" went double-platinum and broke the all time record for most airplay of any song in Top 40 history.
