Angela Aki is a Japanese singer-songwriter and pianist known for her heartfelt ballads. Born in Tokushima, Japan, she moved to the United States for high school and college, which influenced her bilingual music style. Angela gained popularity with her major debut single 'Home' and her cover of 'Kiss Me Good-Bye' for the game Final Fantasy XII. She has released several albums and continues to be a prominent figure in Japanese pop and rock music.

Angela's music often delves into deeply emotional experiences, particularly focusing on themes of farewells and the end of relationships. Her lyrics explore the complexities of understanding and moving on from breakups, emphasizing personal growth and resilience. Through her poignant storytelling, she connects with listeners on a profound emotional level.
