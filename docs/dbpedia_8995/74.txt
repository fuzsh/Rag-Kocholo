Biography

James Stroud was born in 1856 to John Stroud and Ann Page.

He worked as a General Labourer and later as a Cement Labourer.

James married Elizabeth Emily Oliver, daughter of Mary Jacova Stent and Robert Oliver in 1878. He and Elizabeth had 13 children in their marriage.

James Stroud died in 1936 age 80.
