Donald Lee "Don" Stroud (born September 1, 1943) is an American actor, musician, and surfer who has appeared in over 100 films and 175 television shows since the 1960s. In the James Bond franchise, he portrayed Colonel Heller in the 1989 film Licence to Kill with Timothy Dalton and Robert Davi. His other notable credits include The Buddy Holly Story (1978), The Amityville Horror (1979) and Django Unchained (2012).
