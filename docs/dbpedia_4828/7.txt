Ridley Scott is the acclaimed British film director responsible for such landmark films such as Alien, Blade Runner, and Black Hawk Down. In addition to directing, he and his late brother Tony Scott formed Scott Free, a production company that was responsible for producing several television series and miniseries including Numb3rs and The Andromeda Strain as well as the 2010 film adaptation of The A-Team.
