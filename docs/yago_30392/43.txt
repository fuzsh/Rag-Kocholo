About

American actress who portrayed Natalie Buchanan on One Life to Live and played Lauren Buccellato in the 2012 film West End.

Before Fame

She made her television debut on Wishbone when she was seventeen years old.

Trivia

She received a Soap Opera Digest Award for Outstanding Newcomer for her role on One Life to Live in 2003.

Family Life

She married Glenn Angelino in 2008.
