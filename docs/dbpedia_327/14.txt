Techtron Systems is a custom electronics company that has been in operation since 1971. They focus on producing cutting-edge products at competitive prices while maintaining high levels of quality and customer service. Techtron Systems optimizes advanced manufacturing methods, continually improves its management system, and provides a safe workplace. With a history of excellence, they have grown to a state-of-the-art manufacturing facility and remain dedicated to testing, proving, and producing components efficiently. Led by President Paul Teel Jr., the company's mission is to provide high-quality products for clients and drive innovation.
