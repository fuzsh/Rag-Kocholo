Techtron Systems is an independent, locally owned, Low Voltage Electronics Integration company located in Gwinnett County, Georgia. We have been doing business in the Atlanta area for over 20 years.

We are licensed and fully insured in the entire state of Georgia. Techtron Systems specializes in the sales, service, installation, and inspection of a wide variety of low voltage electronic systems in Commercial, Residential, and Industrial environments. This includes security alarm systems, fire alarms, speaker systems, intercom systems, access control, gate access, surveillance camera systems, home automation and home theatre systems, and much more.

We understand that every client has diverse and specific needs, and we treat each one with the same diligence and care that we would with our own friends and families. You can rest assured that when you call us, you are working with professionals who care.
