Educating our future about our past

William Holmes McGuffey House and Museum presents an accurate historical representation of several periods of history ranging from the 1830s to the 1880s, suitable for students and groups of any age.

McGuffey House and Museum offers free guided tours to groups of any size. If you have a large number of people, we ask that you schedule your tour at least two weeks in advance.
