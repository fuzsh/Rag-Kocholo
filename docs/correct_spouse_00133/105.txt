Pearl Elizabeth Rice Buckner,77 of 67 Sam Fox Rd.. passed away Sunday June 2, 2013. She was a resident of Buncombe County and daughter of the late Kellie and Elda Greene Rice. She is also preceded in death by her brothers Carl and Richard Rice. Ms. Buckner was a member of Pleasant Hill Baptist Church.

She is survived by daughters, Joyce Fox and husband Sam of Mars Hill, Linda Lumston and husband David of Swannanoa, and Diane Raines of Burnsville; grandchildren, Michael Coates and wife Cindy, David Buckner and wife Carlene, Jason Fox and Tiffany Donaldson; five great grandchildren; a brother Willard Rice and Grace of Asheville.

Memorial service will be held at 5:00 pm Wednesday, June 5, 2013 at Locust Grove Baptist Church. Officiating will be Rev.D. Harry Culbertson, Jr. The family will receive friends Wednesday, June 5, 4:30-5:00 prior to the service at the church.

In lieu of flowers memorials may be made to Care Partners Foundation Memorials, PO Box 25338, Asheville NC 28813.
