TELECOMWORLDWIRE-30 April 2004-Marimba to be acquired by BMC(C)1994-2004 M2 COMMUNICATIONS LTD http://www.m2.com

Enterprise management solutions provider BMC Software Inc (NYSE: BMC) has entered into an agreement to acquire Marimba Inc (Nasdaq: MRBA), a software change and configuration management solutions provider.

Once the transaction is complete BMC will assume outstanding Marimba stock options and convert them into BMC software stock options. A spokesperson for the company said the acquisition will position BMC for future growth and also enable the company to offer a wider range of enterprise solutions.

BMC will pay USD8.25 in cash for each share of Marimba. This gives the acquisition a total value of USD187m.
