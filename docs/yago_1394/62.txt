M. Night Shyamalan is an American director, producer, screenwriter and actor. He completed his first film, Praying with Anger, in 1992. Since then, he has directed numerous films, including The Sixth Sense, Lady in the Water and Split. Shyamalan also serves as the executive producer for the Apple TV+ series Servant. Shyamalan's newest film, Knock at the Cabin, is set to premiere in theaters in February 2023.
