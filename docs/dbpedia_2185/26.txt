Ella Fitzgerald – A Swinging Christmas – Vinyl Record

Ella Wishes You a Swinging Christmas is a classic holiday album by the iconic jazz singer Ella Fitzgerald. Originally released in 1960, the album features Fitzgerald’s soulful and swinging interpretations of various Christmas classics.

Ella Wishes You a Swinging Christmas is a festive and joyous album that captures the spirit of the holiday season with Ella Fitzgerald’s incomparable voice. The tracklist includes beloved Christmas standards such as “Jingle Bells,” “Winter Wonderland,” “Sleigh Ride,” and “Have Yourself a Merry Little Christmas,” among others.

Known for her impeccable vocal technique, Fitzgerald brings warmth, charm, and a touch of swing to each song. The arrangements are lively, and the album as a whole exudes a timeless quality that continues to resonate with listeners during the holiday season.

Ella Fitzgerald’s Ella Wishes You a Swinging Christmas remains a cherished holiday classic, appreciated by fans of jazz and Christmas music alike for its timeless elegance and the unique artistry of one of the greatest jazz vocalists in history.
