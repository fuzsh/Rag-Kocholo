Senegal

Boubacar Sylla is a young Pan-African from Senegal, committed and dedicated to a world of peace and fraternity. Engineer and writer, on the associative level he was President of the Student Conference of the University of Thies in Senegal, then Special Advisor of the Swiss Christian Party and currently in service at the African University. He has solid professional experience in the banking sector. He often publishes chronicles without bias to call for more tolerance, work and love of people.
