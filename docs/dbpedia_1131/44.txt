Who is Valeriy Khoroshkovskyi? Biography, gossip, facts?

Valeriy Ivanovych Khoroshkovsky (Ukrainian: born January 1 1969 in Kiev) is a Ukrainian businessman former head of the Security Service of Ukraine former Minister of Finance and former First Vice Prime Minister.
