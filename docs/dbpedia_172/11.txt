Ezzatabad (Persian: عزت اباد, also Romanized as ‘Ezzatābād; also known as Makān Mastoqol and Īzadābād)[1] is a village in Dizaj Rural District, in the Central District of Khoy County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 1,442, in 229 families.[2]
