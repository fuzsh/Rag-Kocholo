Rebecca Waechter

Johns Island, SC

Rebecca is an active duty Air Force spouse with a B.A. in Art: Painting and Drawing and a M.A. in Professional Counseling. She uses art as a medium to express and help others cope with mental health issues, such as anxiety, depression, and PTSD. Rebecca believes that art can be a powerful tool for healing and advocacy.

She is a nationally and internationally sold artist. Her work was most notably featured in the credits of the fan art episode of the hit FOX animation series Bob's Burgers. In 2022 she had a piece of original artwork and prints travel to the international space station. They arrived and returned via the SpaceX Crew-4 on the Falcon 9 rocket and Dragon capsule.
