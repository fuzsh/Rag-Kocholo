Inukai Tsuyoshi was a Japanese artist who was born in 1855. Inukai Tsuyoshi's work has been offered at auction multiple times, with realized prices ranging from 1 USD to 5,568 USD, depending on the size and medium of the artwork. Since 2015 the record price for this artist at auction is 5,568 USD for CALLIGRAPHY AND FLORA, sold at China Guardian Auctions, Beijing in 2015. The artist died in 1932.

Artist's alternative names: Inukai Bokudo
