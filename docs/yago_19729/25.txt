Family

Marie Caroline was a daughter of Francis II, Holy Roman Emperor, later Francis I of Austria after the dissolution of the Holy Roman Empire, and Maria Teresa of the Two Sicilies, and named after an elder sister who had died in infancy. She was of the House of Habsburg-Lorraine.

Marriage

She was married, on 26 September 1819 in Dresden, Germany to Prince Frederick Augustus of Saxony son of Maximilian, Prince of Saxony, and Princess Caroline of Parma. He was of the House of Wettin. They had no children.
