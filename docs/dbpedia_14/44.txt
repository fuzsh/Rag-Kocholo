Sultan Muzaffar Shah II ibni Almarhum Raja Mahmud (Jawi: سلطان مظفر شاه ٢ ابن المرحوم رج محمود; died 1653) was the tenth Sultan of Perak reigning from 1636 to 1653.[1][2] He was the first Sultan of Perak to come from a different dynasty from the first nine Sultans of Perak.[3] His paternal grandmother, Raja Putri Putih, was the daughter of Sultan Mansur Shah II of Pahang.[citation needed]
