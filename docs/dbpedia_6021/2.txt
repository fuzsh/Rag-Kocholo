WCJZ (105.7 FM, "Classic Rock CJ 105.7") is an American radio station licensed to serve the community of Cannelton, Indiana. The station is owned and operated by Hancock Communications, Inc., doing business as the Cromwell Radio Group, and the station's broadcast license is held by Hancock Communications, Inc.[1]

For the Charlottesville, Virginia radio station that held the call sign WCJZ at 107.5 FM from 2004 to 2007, see WCHV-FM.
