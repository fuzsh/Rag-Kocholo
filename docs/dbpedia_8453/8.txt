Gavkhoni International Wetland, also known as Gavkhoni Swamp, is a wetland in central Iran, a large part of which is located in Isfahan province.

Mand River is one of the most important rivers in Bushehr province, which originates from the mountains of Fars province and after a long route, finally flows into the Persian Gulf.

Tang-e Bostanak is one of the protected areas of Fars province, which is located in the north of this province and at a distance of about 160 km from Shiraz city.

Umm Algorm, known in the local language as Gorm; It is like a crescent with a large area that only a small part of it can be seen and observed.

This gorge is famous as the Beheshte Gomshode (Lost Paradise) due to its great beauty and many enthusiasts enter this area to visit it.
