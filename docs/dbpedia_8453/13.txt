Ilam Province Plains

Ilam has some plains. Here are some of its important ones ; Ilam, Shirvan, Chardavol, and Holeylan. The population density of these cities is so high. Other plains of the Province are; Salehabad, Mehran, Mohsenabad, Nasirian, Dehloran, Mosian, and Abbas Plains. They are considered as alluvial rivers. Most of them have thick clay.

Protected areas and hunting grounds of Ilam Province

Different animal spacious has lived in mountainous districts and Ilam forests. Here are some of its most important ones; goat, Ovis orientalis, sheep, leopard, bear, wild boar, wolf, fox, jackal, rabbit, weasel, squirrel, eagle, falcon, gyps, partridge, See-see Partridge, francolinus, gazelle, etc. there also lived lions and tigers once.

So many birds especially goose, swallow, Barn swallow migrates to warm district of the province.

There are lots of hunting grounds in Ilam. Here are some of them according to their counties;

Ilam hunting grounds; Manasht, Share zil, Qolarang, Sival, Siahku, Bulishalam, Bankul, Qalandar, Gachan, Sivan Bedarreh, Malwh Gun, Mah Panjab, Kabirkuh, Siahkuh Boli, Anarag Boli, Allah Khoda, Zamzamian, Shalian, and Kravan Mounts.

Tangqir Village

It is located in the center of Shirvan and Chardavol County of Ilam. It has mild and dry weather.

Chenarj River passes its east. Khoram Mount is located in southwest and Ban-e Hezar Mount in the northeast of the village. Mansuri Strait is located in the northeast of the village. The mountains, rivers, Mansuri Strait, medicinal plants, wildlife are the village natural tourist attraction.
