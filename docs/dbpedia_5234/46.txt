The postcode in Mexico

The Postal Codes National Catalog is prepared by the Mexican Postal Service and the postal code has 5 digits, depending on the country area. The first two digits identify the state or part of it. In the case of San Juan Comaltepec the Zip Code range of the Oaxaca state is 68 a 71. To send letters or packages to San Juan Comaltepec, Oaxaca you must always use the five-digit postal code.
