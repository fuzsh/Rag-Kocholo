Kwame Brown Biography

Kwame Brown is best known as the number one overall pick in the 2001 NBA Draft. Brown was selected by the Washington Wizards and became the first high school player ever taken with the top selection. He spent the first four seasons of his career with the Wizards. Brown ended up playing for six other NBA franchises. Most of his career, he was utilized as a valuable bench asset. At Glynn Academy, he became a McDonald's All-American before jumping to the NBA. He left as the school's leading rebounder, shot blocker and second leading scorer all-time. As a senior, he was the Georgia high school player of the year.
