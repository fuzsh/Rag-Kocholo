Horizon Christian Academy was approved by the Student and Exchange Visitor Program (SEVP) in the Student and Exchange Visitor Information System (SEVIS) on 5/9/2007. SEVP and SEVIS are administrated by Immigration and Customs Enforcement, the principal investigative arm of the U.S. Department of Homeland Security (DHS).

Horizon Christian Academy's campus is at Cumming, GA. We suggest you review and contact other SEVIS certified schools nearby before apply for the admissions of Horizon Christian Academy.

Horizon Christian Academy is just one of the more than 10,000 SEVIS certified schools authorized to issue I-20 forms for academic students(F1 Visa), vocational students(M1 visa) or exchange visitors(J1 Visa) in United States.

We won't be able to keep all our data current and accurate. Before choosing Horizon Christian Academy to start your study or research, you should ask whether Horizon Christian Academy is still currently in SEVP program and authorized to issue I-20 Forms in SEVIS system. You should also confirm with them the cost and calendar year of Horizon Christian Academy.
