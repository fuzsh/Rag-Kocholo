"We seek to inspire students, enhance learning environments, and aid scholarly research by making important primary sources available to a global audience." -- http://www.amdigital.co.uk/about-us/ (February 2015)

Adam Matthew Publications was founded in 1990 as a micropublisher, based in Raeding, England. Moved to Marlborough, Wiltshire, in 2001. In 2007 the directors of Adam Matthew Publications formed Adam Matthew Digital. In 2012 the company was acquired by The Sage Group, and became an independent subsidiary of SAGE.
