Ladevci, Skradin

Lađevci is located in the City of Skradin, in Šibenik-Knin County at an elevation of 85 meters above sea level and it covers the area of 1.28 km² (0.49 sq. miles). According to the 2011 census report, there were 112 inhabitants. It is predominantly populated by Croats (99%), but also. Nearby places: Krković, Čista Mala, Cicvare, Čista Velika, Međare, Piramatovci, Morpolača, Žažvić, Ždrapanj and Bribir.
