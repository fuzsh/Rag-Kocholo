For too long, language has prevented families from coming together at the movies. Now you can bring your parents, grandparents, and other friends and family members to the movies with you regardless of the language they speak with the TheaterEars app.

TheaterEars is the #1 App for Movies in Spanish at the Movie Theater

TheaterEars is simple and easy to use. The app works on both Android and iPhones and can work with both wired and wireless earbuds.
