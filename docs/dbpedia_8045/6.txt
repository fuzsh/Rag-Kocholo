Maria Amalia Christina Franziska Xaveria Flora Walburga Wettin von Sachsen was born 24 November 1724 in Dresden, Saxony, Germany to Augustus III of Poland (1696-1763) and Maria Josepha von Österreich (1699-1757) and died 27 September 1760 Palacio del Buen Retiro, Madrid, Spain of tuberculosis. She married Carlos III of Spain (1716-1788) 1738 .
