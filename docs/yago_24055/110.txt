Title: Livilla (daughter of Antonia Minor and Drusus) Contributor(s): Hopwood, Bronwyn (author) Publication Date: 2015 DOI: 10.1002/9781444338386.wbeah26304 Handle Link: https://hdl.handle.net/1959.11/17967 Abstract: Livilla (ca. 13 BCE-31 CE), daughter of Drusus and Antonia Minor, was the sister of Germanicus (15 BCE-19 CE) and Claudius (11 BCE-54 CE). She was married to Gaius Caesar in 1 BCE, then Drusus the Younger in 4 CE. With Drusus she produced a daughter and twin boys. In 31 CE Livilla was accused of having helped her lover, the praetorian prefect Aelius Sejanus, poison Drusus in 23 CE. After her death she suffered 'damnatio memoriae'.
