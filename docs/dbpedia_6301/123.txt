American television production company. Originally the television division of 20th Century Studios since 1949, In 1986, the Fox television network was launched, these studios becoming the main content supplier of its shows, including The Late Show, Married...with Children, the Tracey Ullman Show. This latter spun off the studios most famous and longest-lasting show, The Simpsons.

In 2019,the Fox Studios (but not the news or sports channels) were acquired by the Walt Disney Corporation.
