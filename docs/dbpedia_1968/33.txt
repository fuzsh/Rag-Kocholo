Hirotoshi Masui (増井 浩俊, born June 25, 1984 in Yaizu, Shizuoka) is a Japanese professional baseball pitcher for the Hokkaido Nippon-Ham Fighters in Japan's Nippon Professional Baseball.

Masui is a right-handed pitcher with an overhand delivery. As a reliever, he throws a four-seam fastball (tops out at 96 mph) and a forkball as his primary pitches.
