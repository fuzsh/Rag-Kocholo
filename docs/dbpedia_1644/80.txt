Miss Elena Gerhardt, a singer of German songs, who has recently attracted much attention in Germany and England, made her first appearance in America at a recital in Carnegie Hall yesterday afternoon. She soon made it clear that she is an artist of no common fibre, and that by her intelligence and understanding, her musical feeling, she has penetrated deeply into the essence of the German song.
