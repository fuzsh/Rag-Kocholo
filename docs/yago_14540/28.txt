Saeed Akhtar Mirza is an Indian screenwriter and director in Hindi films and television. He is the maker of notable parallel films such as Mohan Joshi Hazir Ho! , Albert Pinto Ko Gussa Kyoon Aata Hai , Salim Langde Pe Mat Ro and Naseem , which won two National Film Awards in 1996.He is director of the popular TV serials Nukkad and Intezaar , along with various documentary films on social welfare and cultural activism. He is also a trustee of ANHAD, a Delhi-based NGO working for communal harmony.
