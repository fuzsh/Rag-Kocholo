Leeds, UK

Latitude and longitude coordinates are: 53.801277, -1.548567.

Leeds is a beautiful and large city that can be found in West Yorkshire, the United Kingdom. It is a historic inland city located approximately halfway between London and Edinburgh, with plenty of fascinating historic landmarks and tourist attractions worth seeing. Leeds is one of the centers of the country's industrial and business sectors. The city has been built on the banks of the River Aire and is located about 55 miles east of the shores of the North Sea. The city is situated 45 miles northeast of Manchester, about 60 miles northeast of Liverpool, about 200 miles north of London, and about 210 miles south of Edinburgh.

Together with the cities of Liverpool, Manchester, Bradford, York, and others, Leeds forms a really large urban and industrial area which is considered to be among the largest in the country. The city is famous as a commercial and financial center of the country, with plenty of financial institutions and business companies located there. Leeds is a historic city which has got the fame of a key tourist attraction of the region, mainly for its amazing old architecture and plenty of cultural events that take place in the city year-round. Points of interest in the city include the Royal Armouries Museum, Leeds City Museum, The Grand Theatre & Opera House Leeds, Leeds Art Gallery, Potternewton Park, Park Square, First Direct Arena, and Thackray Museum of Medicine. The city is also home to lots of educational institutions, such as the University of Leeds and Leeds Beckett University.
