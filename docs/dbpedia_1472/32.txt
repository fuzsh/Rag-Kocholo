Trevor Goddard (b. 14 October 1962, Croydon, Surrey, England, UK - d. 7 June 2003, North Hollywood, California, USA) was an English actor. He was best known for playing Kano in Mortal Kombat and Lt. Cmdr. Mic Brumby in JAG. He boxed professionally before becoming an actor.
