Evangelos Venizelos (Greek Ευάγγελος Βενιζέλος, born 1957) was the previous Greek Minister of Culture. He is a member of the Panhellenic Socialist Movement. In private life, he was an attorney, professor and constitutional scholar.

Despite the shared last name and initials, Evangelos bears no relation to the famous early twentieth-century Greek politician, Eleftherios Venizelos.
