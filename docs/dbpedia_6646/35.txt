Boxing Legends Of The Ring

Chavez 2

Excerpt from Genesis Game Guide by The Scribe

NAME: Boxing Legends of the Ring AUTHOR/VENDOR: Electrobrain RELEASED: 1993 TYPE: Sports (boxing) LANGUAGE: English and Spanish PREMISE: A decent enough boxing sim focusing on legends of the middleweight class - all but a few of who will be unfamiliar to most people. IMPRESSIONS: Good graphics and gameplay, adequate sound. Superior to Foreman for Real in one or two aspects, but not all. VARIATIONS: Chavez 2 (Spanish, 1994 copyright, YACEG variant with Julio Chavez and a different lineup of boxers)
