Kuwait / Information Office / February 13

HE Deputy Prime Minister and Foreign Minister Sheikh Mohammed bin Abdulrahman Al-Thani held Tuesday bilateral meetings with HE Iraqi Foreign Minister Ibrahim Al Jaafari, HE Italian Minister of Foreign Affairs and International Cooperation Angelino Alfano, HE Malaysian Foreign Minister Anifah Aman, HE UK Minister of State for the Middle East Alistair Burt, HE Greek State Minister for Foreign Affairs George Katrougkalos.

HE Deputy Prime Minister and Foreign Minister held the meetings were held on the sideline of the meeting of the International Coalition Against ISIS which was hosted in Kuwait.

The meetings dealt with bilateral relations and means to develop them in addition to the efforts for countering ISIS and enhancing regional peace and stability.
