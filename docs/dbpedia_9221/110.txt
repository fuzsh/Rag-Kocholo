Reckless Kelly is a 4 year old filly and has raced from 2022 to 2024. She was sired by Good Magic out of the Unbridled's Song mare Unbridled Betty. She was trained by Michael E. Gorham and has raced for Steeplechase Farm, and was bred in Kentucky, United States by Fred W. Hertrich III & John D. Fielding. Reckless Kelly has raced and won at Delaware Park, Laurel Park.
