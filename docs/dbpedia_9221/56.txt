Reckless Kelly is an Americana and alternative country band formed in Bend, Oregon, and later based in Austin, Texas. Founded in 1997, the band consists of brothers Willy and Cody Braun, along with other talented musicians. They are known for their energetic live performances and have garnered a dedicated fan base over the years.

Reckless Kelly's music often delves into the journey through life, reflecting on personal growth and the challenges one faces along the way. Their lyrics offer a blend of introspection and resilience, painting a vivid picture of overcoming adversity and evolving as individuals. The band's storytelling resonates deeply with listeners, making their songs both relatable and inspiring.
