Oconee Fall Line Technical College (OFTC) offers 100+ instructional programs leading to associate degrees, diplomas, and technical certificates of credit, which prepare students for careers in the industrial, business, computer, public service, or allied health fields. Our programs prepare Oconee Fall Line Technical College students to enter their first career, obtain the needed skills to change careers or advance in their current employment place.

Local instructors with industry experience offer our students a unique opportunity to learn first-hand from proven expertise in their field. The hands-on experience incorporated into each program ensures our graduates' success, demonstrated by Oconee Fall Line Technical College's workplace warranty.

Our commitment to a caring environment that promotes student success is a value we take very seriously at Oconee Fall Line Technical College. Here, you will find others like you who are interested in excellent, affordable education; nationally accredited programs; and a talented faculty and staff. We offer a friendly, inclusive environment where you can be yourself and feel safe as you learn and grow.

The mission of Oconee Fall Line Technical College, a unit of the Technical College System of Georgia, is to contribute to the economic and workforce development of east-central Georgia through quality technical and continuing education, adult education, and business and industry services. The College offers associate degrees, diplomas, technical certificates of credit, and non-credit certificates in a student-centered learning environment through traditional and distance education delivery modes.
