On October 15, 1924, Duke Adolf Friedrich of Mecklenburg-schwerin remarried the widow of his half-brother Duke John Albert, Princess Elisabeth of Stolberg-Rossla.

Front row: Queen Wilhelmina of The Netherlands, Princess Thyra of Mecklenburg-Schwerin, Princess Woizlawa Feodora of Mecklenburg-Schwerin, Grand Duchess Elisabeth Alexandrine of Oldenburg née Mecklenburg-Schwerin, Princess Ida of Stolberg-Rossla née Reuss of Greiz, Princess Anna of Ysenburg and Büdingen Princess of Lippe.

Middle row: Prince Henry of Mecklenburg-Schwerin Prince Consort of The Netherlands, Princess Juliana of The Netherlands, Grand Duchess Alexandra of Mecklenburg-Schwerin née Hanover, Bride Princess Elisabeth of Stolberg-Rossla and Bridegroom Duke Adolf Friedrich of Mecklenburg-Schwerin, Crown Princess Cecilie of Germany née Mecklenburg-Schwerin, Queen Alexandrine of Denmark née Mecklenburg-Schwerin, Grand Duke Friedrich Franz IV of Mecklenburg-Schwerin.

Back row: Prince Heinrich XXXIII Reuss of Köstritz.
