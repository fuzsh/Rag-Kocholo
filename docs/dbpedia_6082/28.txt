Arizona Sports 98.7, with the call-sign KMVP, is a sports radio station serving Phoenix, Arizona, under the ownership of Bonneville International Corporation. It is affiliated with ESPN and the Arizona Diamondbacks.

It broadcasts ESPN programming and locally produced shows in which the hosts and guests focused particularly on the performance, results, and news about Arizona's most important sporting teams. Its weekly schedule includes commentary talk shows, sports news reports, and sports live coverage. Arizona Sports 98.7 is the flagship station of the Arizona Cardinals, Arizona Diamondbacks, and the Sun Devils.

Arizona Sports 98.7 live streams all over the world to allow all sports fans to listen live to the game coverage and scoreboard analysis of Arizona's sports teams.
