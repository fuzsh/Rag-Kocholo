Adam Barrie (a.k.a. Adam Berry) is an American actor, whose most famous films include Sorority Row (2009), Private Romeo (2011), A Birder's Guide to Everything (2013), and Wake Me When I Leave (2015).

Adam Berry is a film composer whose works have earned Emmy nominations twice. Berry has composed musical scores for “South Park”, “The Penguins of Madagascar” and “Kim Possible” to name only a few. His first Emmy nomination was for his work on “Kim Possible” and his second nomination came in 2010 for “The Penguins of Madagascar”.
