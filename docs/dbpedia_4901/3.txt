On the 2nd and 4th of March 1957 the WA Sporting Car Club hosted at Caversham Raceway hosted 22nd running of the Australian Grand Prix.

The winner of the event after some controversy was declared as Lex Davison in a Ferrari 625 from Stan Jones in a Maserati 250f.

1957 - CAVERSHAM RACEWAY 6 HOUR PRODUCTION CAR RACE 3rd June 1957

Three Wins in a Row

Co drivers Syd Anderson and Syd Taylor (sitting in car) have won the Caversham Six Hour race each year since it was inaugurated in 1955.

Each time they have driven an Austin Healey and each time they have won outright as well as the Sports Car class.
