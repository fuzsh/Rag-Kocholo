Estonia, properly known as the Republic of Estonia, is a country that is located in the Baltic Region of North Europe. It is bordered by the Gulf of Finland in the North, the Baltic Sea in the West, Latvia in the South, and Russia in the East. It also shares a portion of its border in the East with Lake Peipus.
