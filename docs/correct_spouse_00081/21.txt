by The Post-Standard

People reports Desperate Housewives star Eva Longoria is leaving her husband basketball player Tony Parker after three years of marriage.

She filed for divorce today in Los Angeles. Over the years, rumors have circulated that Parker was having an affair. However, Longoria stood by her man and often cheered on the San Antonio Spurs star during his games. Recently, Longoria mentioned that she and her husband plan to have children.

Longoria, 35, and Parker, 27, married in Paris in a castle.
