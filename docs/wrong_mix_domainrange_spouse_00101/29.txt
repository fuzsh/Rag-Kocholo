Actor Stephen Dunham has died on his 48th birthday after suffering a heart attack at his home in California.

The star passed away on Friday at Providence St Joseph's Hospital in Burbank, days after collapsing at his home.

He died on the day he turned 48, according to The Hollywood Reporter.

Dunham appeared in movies such as The Mummy, Catch Me If You Can and Monster-in-Law, while his TV roles included stints in Hot Properties with Sofia Vergara, What I Like About You and DAG.

He also has a role in Oliver Stone's new drama Savages, and he will be seen in upcoming horror movie Paranormal Activity 4 with his actress wife Alexondra Lee.
