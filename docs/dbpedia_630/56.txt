Guitarist & songwriter Eduardo Gudin is well known in Brazil and has written 100+ songs, some co-written w/artists, Ivan Lins & Dori Caymmi. Never in the US before, his performance here w/singer & pianist Naila Gallotta & a 4-6 member band will be his debut appearance, as well as his newly released album of English lyric songs, entitled “REAL SAMBA”.

Genres:

Bossa Nova, Samba, & World Music.
