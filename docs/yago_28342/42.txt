King Gustaf V was born on June 16, 1858 in Drottningholm Castle, Stockholms län, Sweden. He was an actor, known for Blyge Anton (1940), 40 år med kungen (1947) and Ruotsin kuningasparin vierailu (1925). He was married to Queen Victoria of Baden. He died on October 29, 1950 in Drottningholm Castle, Stockholms län, Sweden.
