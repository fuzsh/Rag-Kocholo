Jun 27, 2001 - The Golden State Warriors selected Jason Richardson in Round 1 with Pick 5 in the 2001 NBA Draft. Aug 24, 2001 - Jason Richardson signed a multi-year contract with the Golden State Warriors. Oct 17, 2003 - The Golden State Warriors exercised their Team Option to extend the contract of Jason Richardson. Nov 1, 2004 - Jason Richardson signed a rookie scale extension with the Golden State Warriors. Jun 28, 2007 - Jason Richardson was acquired by the Charlotte Hornets, along with the draft rights to Jermareo Davidson, from the Golden State Warriors in exchange for the draft rights to Brandan Wright. Dec 10, 2008 - Jason Richardson was acquired by the Phoenix Suns, along with Jared Dudley, a 2010 2nd round pick (CHA own) and a TPE, from the Charlotte Hornets in exchange for Raja Bell, Boris Diaw and Sean Singletary. Dec 18, 2010 - Jason Richardson was acquired by the Orlando Magic, along with Earl Clark, Hedo Turkoglu and a TPE, from the Phoenix Suns in exchange for Vince Carter, Marcin Gortat, Mickael Pietrus, a 2011 1st round pick (ORL own) and cash. Jul 1, 2011 - Jason Richardson, previously with the Orlando Magic, became a free agent. Dec 14, 2011 - Jason Richardson signed a multi-year contract with the Orlando Magic. Aug 10, 2012 - Jason Richardson was acquired by the Philadelphia Sixers, along with Andrew Bynum, from the Orlando Magic in exchange for Maurice Harkless, Andre Iguodala, Nikola Vucevic and a conditional 2017 1st round pick (PHL own). Jun 10, 2014 - Jason Richardson exercised a Player Option to extend his contract. Jul 1, 2015 - Jason Richardson, previously with the Philadelphia Sixers, became a free agent. Jul 9, 2015 - The Philadelphia Sixers renounced their free-agent exception rights to Jason Richardson. Aug 18, 2015 - Jason Richardson signed a contract with the Atlanta Hawks. Sep 25, 2015 - The Atlanta Hawks placed the contract of Jason Richardson on waivers. Sep 27, 2015 - Jason Richardson, previously with the Atlanta Hawks, became a free agent.
