Jason Richardson was a professional basketball player, a shooting guard. He was drafted fifth by the Golden State Warriors in 2001 after a successful tenure at Michigan State, with whom he won the 2000 NCAA Championship with. Richardson was a fan favorite, and even won the Slam Dunk contest in 2002. Richardson left the Warriors in 2007 and went on to play for several more teams before his retirement in 2015. This original Jason Richardson Nike jersey, with a few marks from stroage and light wear on the number, is from the earliest days of his professional career when he was with the Warriors.
