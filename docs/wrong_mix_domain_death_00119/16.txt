Biography

Johan Christian Fabricius was born on April 22, 1624 in Tønder. His parents were Carsten Jensen and Gude Frodesdatter Schmidt. He was a 1649 præst i Løjt.

Johan Christian married Anna Villadsdatter on May 27, 1650. Together they had the following children:

1651 Vilhad Johansen Fabricius;

1654 Christian Fabricius;

1656 Caspar Fabricius;

1659 Johan Christian Fabricius;

1662 Andreas Fabricius;

1665 Catharina Gude Johansdatter Fabricius.

He died on July 9, 1686 in Løjt.
