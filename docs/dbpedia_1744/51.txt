Edwin Sydney Stuart (28 December 1853-21 March 1937) was Mayor of Philadelphia (R) from 5 January 1891 to 7 January 1895 (succeeding Edwin Henry Fitler and preceding Charles F. Warwick) and Governor of Pennsylvania from 15 January 1907 to 17 January 1911 (succeeding Samuel W. Pennypacker and preceding John K. Tener).
