U.S. Soldiers and Airmen with the Pennsylvania National Guard's 3rd Weapons of Mass Destruction Civil Support Team, simulates an emergency response situation at Bloomsburg University's Robert B. Redman Stadium, Bloomsburg, Pennsylvania April 11, 2024. To maintain their proficiency, the team must undergo an external evaluation every 18 months where they are evaluated by a team from U.S. Army North. (U.S. Army National Guard video by 2nd Lt. Kate Kramer and Wayne V. Hall)
