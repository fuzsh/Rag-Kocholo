Robert B. Redman Stadium, located on the upper campus of Bloomsburg University, is a prominent feature of the university's expansive 103-acre athletic complex. Originally constructed in February 1974, this state-of-the-art facility replaced Athletic Park in downtown Blooms, offering a modern and dynamic venue for various sporting events.

Situated amidst the picturesque surroundings of Bloomsburg, Pennsylvania, Robert B. Redman Stadium stands as a testament to the university's commitment to athletic excellence. With its rich history and strategic location, this stadium serves as a hub for the Bloomsburg Huskies' athletic programs, providing a premier setting for athletes and spectators alike.
