Uninvited [Edit] [Talk]

Uninvited is a point and click adventure game, developed by ICOM Simulations. Originally a 1986 Macintosh game, it was later ported for the NES and released in Japan in September 1989.

Plot[]

The player's character crashes their car while driving past a sinister old mansion. They realize their sister is missing upon regaining consciousness, and need to enter the house and contend with its ghostly inhabitants to find her.

Gameplay[]

Players interact with the game by using a list of verbs to perform various actions (open, hit, use, etc.) on a display in the corner showing the nearby area.
