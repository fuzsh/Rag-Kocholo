The Barcelona-Cerbère railway, part of a remarkable transportation legacy, was built between 1858 and 1878, a testament to the engineering prowess of its time. In the 20th century, the Barcelona-Cerbère railway underwent significant electrification, modernizing the entire line and enhancing the efficiency of transportation.
