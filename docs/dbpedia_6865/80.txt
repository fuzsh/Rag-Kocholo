Horizon Christian School is a Foundation to Year 12, co-educational Christian School situated in the rural township of Balaklava. They seek to provide students with a quality education, Christ-centred core values and life skills that will provide them with the foundation for future success in all that they pursue. Horizon Christian School aims to develop student’s values and leadership potential in a caring and Christian environment, through the work of committed Christian teachers. They also encourage positive attitudes toward staff, schoolwork and life in general. They foster the development of close associations with parents regarding their child’s progress at school. The school vision is "A Transforming Christian Education For All".

Through the Project, Horizon Christian School aims to develop a greater understanding of inclusive education for our students with disabilities and learning difficulties and to improve our current practises. It is a great opportunity to learn from other schools and experts in this field and to tailor improvements to suit our school setting.
