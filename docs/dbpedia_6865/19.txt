Horizon Christian Schools, located in Tualatin, Oregon, is a well-established educational institution offering a Christian-centered worldview to over 500 students from preschool through 12th grade. Founded in 1981 as a ministry of Horizon Community Church, the school provides a rigorous and well-rounded education without denominational restrictions, emphasizing the development of each student's God-given gifts and talents.

With campuses for both elementary and high school students, Horizon Christian Schools boasts a dedicated faculty committed to Christ-centered principles and a comprehensive curriculum that includes specialist classes in foreign language, fine arts, music, and athletics. The school's unique iSmart program focuses on nurturing students' individual strengths and talents, preparing them to excel academically and in life.
