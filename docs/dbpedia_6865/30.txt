Horizon Christian School in Hood River, OR, has been dedicated to providing quality Christian education since 1977. With a focus on Academic achievement, Biblical truth, and Christian Character development, the school offers small class sizes, individual attention, and a challenging academic program taught by experienced teachers.

Through a curriculum integrated with a biblical worldview, Horizon Christian School aims to help students develop their full potential academically, spiritually, emotionally, and physically. The school emphasizes the importance of building a solid foundation for life by nurturing relationships, fostering individualization, and promoting the integration of God's truth into all aspects of education.
