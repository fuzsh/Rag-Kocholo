Petroglyph Games

Profile

Petroglyph Games is a video game publisher and developer based in Las Vegas, Nevada, United States. The company was formed by the last group of ex- Westwood Studios employees who resigned when Westwood Pacific was shut down by Electronic Arts in 2003.
