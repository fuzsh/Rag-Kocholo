Oando PLC, through its subsidiaries, provides energy solutions in Nigeria and internationally. The company operates through Exploration and Production, and Supply and Trading segments. It explores for and produces oil and gas; and trades in crude oil and petroleum products, including naphtha, gasoline, fuel oil, gas oil, kerosene, and bitumen.
