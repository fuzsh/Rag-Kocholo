SageTV, an Inglewood, CA-based software company, is introducing an updated version (6.2) of its SageTV Media Center for Mac sometime today.

The new software allows Mac users to convert TV recordings to iPod (video), AppleTV and RAZR format. The company also offers a wireless extender, that allows you to connect more than one television set to your computer. The software also enables watching YouTube and Google Video on the big screen television. It has multiple tuner support.
