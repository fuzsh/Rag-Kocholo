Princess Bala is the deuteragonist of DreamWorks' film, Antz. She is Z's love interest and the princess of Z's colony.

History[]

In the film, Bala falls in love with Z and at the end of the film, the two marry and have at least 4 million children together off-screen.Despite being a princess and having the pampered lifestyle, Bala ultimately realized she needed a little adventure and love when she travelled with Z.
