Ian Kamau

Ian Kamau is an artist, writer, designer and city builder who grew up in the St. Lawrence neighbourhood of downtown Toronto. His parents, both pioneering filmmakers, emigrated from Trinidad in 1970.

Paul Nguyen

Paul Nguyen is an award-winning activist and filmmaker from Toronto's Jane-Finch area. In 2004, he created Jane-Finch.com to change negative stereotypes about his community. His grassroots project quickly became a national success story.

John Lorinc

He has written extensively on amalgamation, education, sprawl and other city issues. He is the recipient of two National Magazine Awards for his coverage of urban affairs.
