The French Le Fantasque class destroyers were ordered under the French navel programme of 1930. There were six ships in the class

Displacement: 2569 tons standard and 3200 to 3400 tons full load. Speed: 37 Knots Compliment: 210. Armament: Five 5.5 inch guns, Four 37mm AA guns, Four 13.2mm AA Guns and Nine 21.7 inch Torpedo Tubes, also 50 mines.
