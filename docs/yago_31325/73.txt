Princess Sophie of Bavaria, mother of Franz Joseph I, emperor of Austria; (Sophie Friederike Dorothea Wilhelmine; 27 January 1805 – 28 May 1872), was the daughter of King Maximilian I Joseph of Bavaria and his second wife Caroline of Baden. The identical twin sister of Queen Maria Anna of Saxony, Sophie became Archduchess of Austria by marriage to Archduke Franz Karl of Austria. Her eldest son Franz Joseph reigned as Emperor of Austria and King of Hungary; her second son Maximilian briefly reigned as Emperor of Mexico.
