Francis I of the Two Sicilies (Francesco Gennaro Giuseppe Saverio Giovanni Battista; 19 August 1777 – 8 November 1830) was King of the Two Sicilies from 1825 to 1830 and regent of the Kingdom of Sicily from 1806 to 1814.

First marriage[edit]

On 26 June 1797 he married his distant cousin Maria Clementina of Austria, a daughter of Leopold I, Grand Duke of Tuscany and Maria Luisa of Spain, Maria Luisa was a daughter of Charles III of Spain.

Issue with Hereditary Princess Maria Clementina[edit]

Princess Maria Carolina of the Two Sicilies (5 November 1798 – 17 April 1870) married Charles Ferdinand d'Artois, Duke of Berry and had issue.

Prince Ferdinando Francesco d'Assisi of Naples and Sicily (27 August 1800 – 1 Julie 1801) died in infancy.

Second marriage[edit]

On 6 July 1802, he married Infanta María Isabel of Spain, daughter of Charles IV of Spain and his Italian consort Maria Luisa of Parma.

Issue with Queen María Isabella[edit]

Princess Luisa Carlotta of the Two Sicilies 24 October 1804 – 29 January 1844) married Infante Francisco de Paula of Spain, Duke of Cadiz and had issue.

Maria Christina of the Two Sicilies (27 April 1806 – 22 August 1878) married Ferdinand VII of Spain and had issue.

Ferdinand II of the Two Sicilies (12 January 1810 – 22 May 1859) married twice and had issue.

Prince Carlo Ferdinando of the Two Sicilies, Prince of Capua (10 November 1811 – 22 April 1862) married Penelope Smyth and had issue.

Prince Leopold of the Two Sicilies, Count of Syracuse (22 May 1813 – 4 December 1860) married Maria Vittoria of Savoy but had no issue.

Princess Maria Antonia of the Two Sicilies (19 December 1814 – 7 November 1898) married Leopold II, Grand Duke of Tuscany and had issue.

Prince Antonio of the Two Sicilies, Count of Lecce (23 September 1816 – 12 January 1843) never married but had issue.

Princess Maria Amalia of the Two Sicilies (25 February 1818 – 6 November 1857) married Infante Sebastian of Portugal and Spain but had no issue.

Princess Maria Carolina of the Two Sicilies (29 November 1820 – 14 January 1861) married Infante Carlos of Spain, Count of Montemolin but had no issue.

Teresa Cristina of the Two Sicilies (14 March 1822 - 28 December 1889) married Pedro II of Brazil and had issue.

Prince Louis of the Two Sicilies, Count of Aquila (19 July 1824 – 5 March 1897) married Princess Januária of Brazil but had no issue.

Prince Francis of the Two Sicilies, Count of Trapani married Archduchess Maria Isabella of Austrick and had issue.
