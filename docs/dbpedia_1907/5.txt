Though the late Selena coined the term “techno cumbia,” releasing a single by that very name in 1994, the subgenre flourished in Peru during the mid-to-late ‘90s. Singers like Rossy War and Ruth Karina, as well as boy bands like Skandalo, experimented with adding electric guitars, synths, and drum samples to cumbia rhythms.
