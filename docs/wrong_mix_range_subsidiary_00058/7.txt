ELAN was founded in May 1996 in Hsin-Chu Science-Based Industrial Park. It launched its operation in January 1997. ELAN started as a fabricator of DRAM chips, then later shifted towards designing and developing integrated circuits in July 1997. ELAN became a publicly traded company in April 2000, listing on the Taiwan Stock Exchange (TSE) with the ticker symbol 3478. ELAN Microelectronics Corporation is a subsidiary of ELAN Technology Inc.
