Max Rosenberg (September 13, 1914-June 14, 2004) was a film producer, mostly of horror or supernatural films such as The Curse of Frankenstein (1957), Tales from the Crypt (1972), The Land That Time Forgot (1975), and its sequel, The People That Time Forgot (1977). He also produced a children's film, Lad, a Dog (1962), and director Richard Lester's first film, It's Trad, Dad! (1962).
