About

WVC is a subsidiary of Weigel Broadcasting Co. Weigel is a family-owned media company based in Chicago, IL. The company owns and operates national television networks, as well as local television stations throughout the US.

40+ Million viewers watch Weigel networks and programming in a single week on more than 600+ distribution partners across broadcast, cable, satellite and digital platforms.

Weigel is a leader in the media industry with The "MeTV" Network, the #1 Classic TV network, as well as the “Start TV” Network in association with the CBS Television Stations, the "Movies!" Network in cooperation with the Fox Television Stations, and "H&I" Network and the "DECADES" Network.

Weigel owns television stations in New York, Los Angeles, Chicago, Dallas, Houston, San Francisco, Phoenix, Seattle, Denver, St. Louis, Nashville, Salt Lake City, Hartford, Milwaukee, South Bend and Rockford. Weigel's local stations include CBS, ABC, The CW, MyNet and Telemundo network affiliates.
