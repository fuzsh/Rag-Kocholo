This document provides biographical information and summaries of the presidencies of several leaders of the Philippines: - Emilio Aguinaldo was the first president during the short-lived First Republic from 1899-1901. - Manuel Roxas was the first president of the independent Third Republic from 1946-1948 and focused on rehabilitation after WWII. - Elpidio Quirino was president from 1948-1953 and dealt with issues like the Huk rebellion and economic problems. - Ramon Magsaysay was president from 1953-1957 and made reforms helping the masses while stopping the Huk rebellion. However, he died in a plane crash before finishing his term.
