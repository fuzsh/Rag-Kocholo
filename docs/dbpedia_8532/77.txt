There have been 17 presidents of the Philippines. The list provides brief details on each president, including their terms in office. Emilio Aguinaldo was the first president from 1899-1901. Rodrigo Duterte was the 16th president from 2016-2022. The current president, Ferdinand Marcos Jr., is the son of former president Ferdinand Marcos. He took office on June 30, 2022 as the 17th president of the Philippines.
