About

Russian Royal Grand Duchess Xenia Alexandrovna of Russia was born Xenia Alexandrovna Romanova on 6th April, 1875 in Saint Petersburg, Russia and passed away on 20th Apr 1960 Molesey, United Kingdom aged 85. She is most remembered for Grand Duchess Xenia of Russia. Her zodiac sign is Aries.
