NBC has acquired the crime thriller Wasteland.

Eleventh Hour's Mick Davis will write the script.

Wasteland follows a US Marshal - who in the midst of a massive prison break - is charged with hunting down fugitives with supernatural powers, Deadline reports.

The series will be produced by Sony TV with Mick Davis, Bob Cooper and JJ Jamieson set to executive produce.

A release date for Wasteland has yet to be set.
