Director Luis Bayón Herrera

Actors like Pepito Petray, Enrique García Satur, Iris Portillo, Antonio Maida, Lucy Galián have participated in films in which we find Luis Bayón Herrera as a director and in them they have shown their great artistic gifts.

A director like Luis Bayón Herrera makes his best films in the Romance, Drama genre. These themes are the most used by this director to recreate himself in his artistic works.
