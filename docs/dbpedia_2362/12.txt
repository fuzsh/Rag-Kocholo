She was born to Margaret McLaughlin and John Kearnes in Lansing, OH, December 9, 1937, the youngest sister of Marjorie and Charlotte.

Marlene, a devoted wife and loving mother, spent her leisure time as an accomplished gardener and dedicated dog owner.

Marlene worked and retired from the Wheeling Wonder Bread Bakery, where she met her loving husband, Rudy Toth.

Marlene was preceded in death by her mother, Margaret, father, John, husband, Rudy, and sister, Charlotte. She is missed and remembered by her son, Richard Grandstaff, her devoted sister, Marjorie Coconis, nephews, Michael Coconis and wife, Eve, John Coconis and wife, Tasha, and George Coconis, and in-laws, John and Joanne Toth.

Viewing and funeral will be private.

Please remember Marlene with donations to the Belmont County Animal Rescue League, 45244 National Rd W, St Clairsville, OH, 43590.
