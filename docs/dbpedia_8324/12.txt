Adam Patrick DeVine is an American comedian, writer, producer, actor, creator and voice actor. He is a star and creator of the Comedy Central series "Workaholics" and "Adam DeVine's House Party". He is best known from Pitch Perfect and Modern Family. Adam played the recurring character of Andy Bailey, Joe's nanny and Beth's fianceé.
