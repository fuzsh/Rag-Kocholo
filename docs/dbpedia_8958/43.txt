In Yemen the following currency is used:

The Yemeni rial (YER): this currency is devided in 100 Fils. You can recognise the currency by the symbol ï·¼.

You probably need to change money when traveling to Yemen. You might want to consider not to bring to much money. A credit card usualy is the easiest way to pay abroad. Withdrawing money at a local ATM is an other option. We strongly advise you to check the possibilities and international fees and charges of your bank or credit card company.
