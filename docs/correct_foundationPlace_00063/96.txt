AVG Technologies

About The Developer

The security software company AVG Technologies developed antivirus software and internet security like AVG Antivirus. It was founded in 1991. The developer is Jan Gritzbach and Tomáš Hofer. It is headquartered in Brno, Czech Republic and Amsterdam, Netherlands. The company has been a subsidiary of Avast software in 2016.

Development Operating Systems: Windows, Mac, Android
