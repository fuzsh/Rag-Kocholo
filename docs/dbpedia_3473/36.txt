Routes and distances from Edirne to Şile

Flagma calculates distances Edirne → Şile for car driving; offers the following routes: fastest, economy route 309 km, shortest route 305 km; and determines car and truck driving times. Flagma simultaneously calculates distances between cities for all the routes. The tabulated links show distances from Edirne to other cities of Turkey.

In the table and on the map the roadways Edirne → Şile are shown with line patterns. Each road type has its own line pattern; the brighter and the thicker the pattern of the road from Edirne to Şile is, the higher the class of the road is. The map and the table for the route Edirne → Şile can be printed out in A4 format.

Fuel consumption is calculated as a total amount of fuel consumed on each part of the way with a due account of the road type: freeway, highway,... In the calculation form please indicate the fuel consumption per 100 km for highway driving, according to the specifications of your vehicle.
