Whitworth College

Intercollegiate College of Nursing

Whitworth College is a Private not-for-profit institution with a campus in Spokane, Washington. The institution is approved to offer nursing programs by the Washington Board of Nursing. The Spokane, Washington campus for Whitworth College is located in a Suburb setting, see the map below. Total enrollment for all degrees including nursing programs is 2,415. The student-to-faculty ratio is 10-to-1. In-state tuition and fees were $48,990, while out-of-state tuition and fees were $48,990 for the most recent reporting period.
