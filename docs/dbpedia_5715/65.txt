Bryan Sammis is an American artist who was a member of the band The Neighbourhood. He was the band's first drummer and later left the group in January of 2014. He was replaced by Brandon Fried.

Career[]

Bryan was an original member of The Neighbourhood, coming together with his now former band mates in August of 2011. He left the band in early 2014, being replaced by Brandon Fried. He sometimes goes by the alias of Olivver and would wind up going solo under the name Olivver The Kid.
