Arlene Alda, wife of actor Alan Alda, will be speaking about her book, “Just Kids from the Bronx - Telling It the Way It Was: An Oral History,” at 2 p.m. Sept. 20 at Monroe Temple of Liberal Judaism, 314 Main St., Monroe.

Copies of the book will be available for purchase and to be signed by the author. The event is free and open to the entire community.
