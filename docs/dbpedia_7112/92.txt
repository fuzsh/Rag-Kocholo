History

Alpina Burkard Bovensiepen GmbH, better known as Alpina is a German car manufacturer that manufacturers cars based on BMW cars. Alpina is an official car manufacturer and not an aftermarket tuning manufacturer, there is much confusion about it. Alpina models contains the BMW-prefix, and a B-prefix for petrol and a D-prefix for diesel.

Alpina is founded in 1965 by Burkard Bovensipen and has its headquarters in Buchloe, in thee district of Bavaria, Germany.

Some Alpina models are produced at the same assembly lines as the BMW models.
