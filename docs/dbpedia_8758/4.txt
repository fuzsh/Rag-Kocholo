Horam

Where is Horam?

Horam is located in the county of East Sussex, South East England, three miles south of the town of Heathfield, 12 miles north of the major town of Eastbourne, and 43 miles south-east of London. Horam was historically in the county of Sussex. Horam falls within the district council of Wealden, under the county council of East Sussex. It is in the TN21 postcode district. The post town for Horam is Heathfield.
