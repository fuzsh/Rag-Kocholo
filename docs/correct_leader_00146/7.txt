Prime Minister Madhav Kumar Nepal met with his Bhutanese counterpart Jigme Y Thinley, who arrived in Kathmandu today for one-day visit to attend the 13th day death rituals of late former prime minister Girija Prasad Koirala.

During the meeting held at Prime Minister's Office in Singh Durbar, Nepal urged Thinley to help solve the problem of Bhutanese refugees, that has long marred Nepal, at the soonest.

PM Nepal also met Tamrat Samuel, Director, Asia-Pacific Division of the UN's Department of Political Affairs.

The meet was focused on the issue of further tenure extension of UNMIN and OHCHR-Nepal.
