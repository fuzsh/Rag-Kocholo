Alargan International Real Estate Co. is a Kuwaiti shareholding company headquartered in Salmiyah in Kuwait. It was incorporated in 1994 and it has been listed in Kuwait stock exchange since 2007. Alargan is one of the leading real estate companies in the GCC region as it operates in Kuwait, Saudi Arabia, Bahrain, Oman. The company is engaged in owning and selling lands, shares of real estate companies. It deals with investment funds managements as well. Some of the company's most outstanding projects are Jeyoun and Saar Gate in Bahrain, Hadiya, Movenpick Hotel and Resort, ARGAN Business park in Kuwait, Qurm Gardens and Al Hail Homes in Oman.
