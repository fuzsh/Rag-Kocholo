SURREY, British Columbia - Oct. 4, 2023 - PRLog -- Amrit Singh Rai, the founder of Night and Day Recovery Society, has been acquitted of all charges stemming from his arrest in 2012. The Honourable Mr. Justice Crossin rendered a verdict of Not Guilty, vindicating Mr. Rai after an arduous eight-year legal ordeal.

In 2012, Mr. Rai, while overseeing multiple recovery houses, was found in possession of a small quantity of narcotics during a routine house inspection in one of the residents' rooms. Due to his history of addiction, swift conclusions were drawn by the police and the prosecution, leading to charges against him.

Throughout the entire legal process, Mr. Rai steadfastly maintained his innocence. He mounted a robust defense to prove his innocence and to restore the reputation of his Recovery Society, which has been highly regarded within the recovery community.

Reflecting on his acquittal, Mr. Rai stated, "This trial affirms what I have asserted since the outset of this process: I was innocent of all the charges brought against me. It is gratifying to have my good name restored by this verdict. I remain committed to helping individuals overcome drug and alcohol addiction through my recovery society."

Since its establishment in 2012, Night and Day Recovery Society, under Mr. Rai's leadership, has transformed countless lives. With a proven track record of assisting hundreds of individuals in their recovery journeys, Night and Day has become a beacon of hope and healing for those seeking lasting sobriety.

The residential program at Night and Day provides a supportive and structured environment where individuals can fully engage in the recovery process. They are surrounded by a compassionate and experienced team of addiction specialists, counselors, and peer support staff. Night and Day has consistently guided individuals on a transformative journey, empowering them to reclaim their lives and establish a foundation for a drug-free future.

Through unwavering commitment and the provision of comprehensive and individualized care, Night and Day Recovery Society continues to make a profound impact in the lives of those struggling with addiction.
