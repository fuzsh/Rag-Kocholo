HER MAJESTY QUEEN RAMBHAI BARNI OF SIAM INSPECTING GUARD OF HONOUR FORMED BY FIRST BATTALION SEAFORTH HIGHLANDERS

Description Edited by NAS:

Queen Rambhai Barni inspecting guard of honour formed by the First Battalion Seaforth Highlanders, upon arrival in Singapore. The Queen was escorting the royal ashes of her husband, King Prajadhipok, back to Thailand from United Kingdom.

Covering Date:

18/05/1949
