Viktor Fedorovich Vasin was a Russian painter who was born in 1919. Viktor Fedorovich Vasin's work has been offered at auction multiple times, with realized prices ranging from 56 USD to 1,905 USD, depending on the size and medium of the artwork. Since 2013 the record price for this artist at auction is 1,905 USD for Poultry, sold at Claydon Auctioneers in 2013. The artist died in 1997.

Artist's alternative names: Viktor Fedorovitch Vasin, Viktor Vassine
