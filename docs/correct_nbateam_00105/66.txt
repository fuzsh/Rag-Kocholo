Jul 15, 2004 - Kenyon Martin was acquired by the Denver Nuggets from the New Jersey Nets in exchange for a conditional 2005 1st round pick (PHL own), a conditional 2006 1st round pick (DEN own) and a conditional 2001 1st round pick (LAC own).
