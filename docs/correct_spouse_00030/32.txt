Kristine Hermosa was born on September 9, 1983 in Quezon City, Philippines. She is an actress, known for Nasaan ka Maruja? (2009), All My Life (2004) and Enteng Kabisote: Okay ka fairy ko... The legend (2004). She has been married to Oyo Boy Sotto since January 12, 2011. They have three children. She was previously married to Diether Ocampo.
