Meggitt - About the company

What does Meggitt do?

Manufacturer of components and sub-systems for the aerospace industry. The company conducts its business through three market segments, including aerospace equipment, energy equipment, and defence system. Through its aerospace equipment segment, it offers aircraft braking systems, polymer and composites, thermal systems, and safety systems. Through its sensing system segment, it offers multiple sensors, such as vibrometers, sensors, and ferrocerium piezoceramics. It also offers a ground manoeuvring camera system (GMCS) and a high-definition camera for cabin surveillance.

Which sectors and market segments does Meggitt operate in?

Meggitt serves in the B2B space in the Semiconductors, Aerospace, Maritime and Defense Tech market segments.
