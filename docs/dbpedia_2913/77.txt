Ian Joesbury, President, Meggitt Polymers & Composites and Andrew Mair, Chief Executive, Midlands Aerospace Alliance invite you to come Inside Meggitt with the MAA.

This one-day intensive event incorporating a site visit to a key Meggitt aerospace facility is for a strictly limited group.

Meggitt has embarked on big organisational and operational changes in recent years to modernise its structure, strategy and improve its performance for its own customers -- the world's aerospace prime contractors.

This special day will give aerospace suppliers the cutting-edge knowledge they need to align business strategies and meet the requirements of rapidly evolving global aerospace players like Meggitt.

Meggitt plc

Headquartered in the UK, Meggitt PLC is a global engineering group specialising in extreme environment components and smart sub-systems for aerospace, defence and energy markets. Some 10,000 people are employed across manufacturing facilities in Asia, Europe and North America and regional bases in Brazil, India and the Middle East.

Meggitt's corporate management

Meggitt businesses are supported by an evolving group infrastructure with strong functional leadership. This enables the company to combine talents and technologies, share services and benefit from a range of centrally managed initiatives. From information technology to strategic sourcing and low-cost manufacturing, all are designed to boost the efficiency of Meggitt operations and give customers what they want.

Change at Meggitt Polymers & Composites

Meggitt Polymers & Composites (MPC) designs, engineers and manufactures seals used for multiple purposes all over aircraft structures, aero engines and aircraft systems. As a strategic business unit within Meggitt, MPC is a superb case study in how Meggitt companies are currently being transformed to retain and grow their leading positions as suppliers of specialist technologies in world aerospace markets.
