Jack Parnell was born on 6 August 1923 in London, England, UK. He was an actor and composer, known for Barbra Streisand and Other Musical Instruments (1973), The Arthur Askey Show (1961) and The Benny Hill Show (1957). He was married to Monique Bonneau. He died on 8 August 2010 in Southwold, Suffolk, England, UK.
