Rihanna, whose full name is Robyn Rihanna Fenty, is a Barbadian singer, actress, fashion designer, and businesswoman. Born on February 20, 1988, in Saint Michael, Barbados, Rihanna rose to fame in the early 2000s with her debut single "Pon de Replay." She has since become one of the best-selling music artists of all time, known for her distinctive voice, versatile music styles, and hit songs like "Umbrella," "Diamonds," and "Work." Beyond music, Rihanna has made significant strides in acting and fashion, launching successful brands such as Fenty Beauty and Savage X Fenty.
