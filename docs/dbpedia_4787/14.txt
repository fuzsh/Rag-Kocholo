Berkeley Preparatory School is located at 4811 Kelly Road in Tampa. Four gated entrances with security officers provide access to the 86-acre campus, which features world-class facilities for students in pre-k through twelfth grade.

To schedule a tour, please contact the Admissions Office by calling (813) 885-1673 or emailing admissions@berkeleyprep.org, and check out the Admissions Events page for more information about our “Mornings at Berkeley” events.
