Queen Maria Josepha, Wife of King Augustus III of Poland is a work by Italian artist Pietro Antonio Rotari (1707 - 1762). It's one portrait oil on canvas painting created in 1755. The painting is owned by the museum of Gemäldegalerie Alte Meister - Staatliche Kunstsammlungen Dresden, and is accessible to the general public.
