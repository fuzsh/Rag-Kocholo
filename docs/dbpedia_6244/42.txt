WSBR corporate members enjoy a range of benefits including valuable networking opportunities and direct access to prominent executives and thought leaders from across the space and satellite business spectrum in both the domestic and international communities. The luncheon forums encourage the open exchange of ideas and information, and members are free to pose questions and delve further into timely topics of the day.

Additionally, corporate members are encouraged to play an active role in WSBR’s education initiatives. Participation with the Education Committee is warmly welcomed.

As the primary financial support of the Washington Space Business Roundtable, its corporate sponsors play a crucial role in facilitating both WSBR’s networking activities and its ability to provide quality educational opportunities to the communities WSBR aims to support. Corporate sponsors are the heart of WSBR.
