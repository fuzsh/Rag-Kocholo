About Dombivli Ctl Station

Dombivli Ctl Railway station is in Thane district making it an important railway station in the Indian state of Maharashtra. The station code name of Dombivli Ctl is ‘DCTL’. As part of one of the busiest and populated Indian states, Maharashtra, the Dombivli Ctl railway station is known amongst the top hundred train ticket booking and train traveling stations of the Indian Railway. The total number of trains that pass through Dombivli Ctl (DCTL) junction is 60.
