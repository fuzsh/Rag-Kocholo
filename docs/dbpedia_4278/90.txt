Hermann Schubert (1921-1995) was a German chemist and academic. After completing his studies in chemistry, he earned his doctorate in 1953 and habilitation in 1959 at the Martin-Luther-University Halle-Wittenberg. He became a lecturer in Organic Chemistry and later a professor, leading the Institute of Organic Chemistry. His research focused on liquid-crystalline heterocycles and other organic chemistry topics. Schubert played a significant role in the university's chemistry section during the DDR's higher education reform and lived in Nehlitz, near Halle (Saale).
