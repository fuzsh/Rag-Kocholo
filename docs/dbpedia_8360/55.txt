He is a versatile artist and sings semi-classical forms like Bhajans, Thumris, Gazals, and Natya Sangeet with panache. Recipient of several prestigious awards, Panshikar has made his mark in performances throughout India as well as in Europe, the United States, and the Middle East.

Panshikar was born into a family of Sanskrit scholars and classical musicians. He is the son of the eminent Marathi stage actor and producer Prabhakar Panshikar. Panshikar's aptitude for music was recognized by him and fostered him from an early age. He began his formal study of music in Mumbai at the age of eleven.
