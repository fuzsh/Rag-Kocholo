6:30pm - 8:00pm

Commercial Drive, Vancouver

Free light dinner is served!

Asian Women for Equality offers information, advocacy, and support to promote community and leadership among Asian women. We are feminists working to advance equality, particularly for Asian women. Our origin traces back to our collective response to violent attacks on Asian women in Vancouver.
