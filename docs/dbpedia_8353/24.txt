Pooja Sharma was born on 12 February,1989 in Delhi, India. She is an Indian actress and model. She is known for her role Draupadi in the serial "Mahabharat" (2013) and Parvati / Kaali in the serial "Mahakaali - Anth Hi Aarambh Hai" (2017). Pooja has been interested in acting since childhood. She wanted to become an actress from an early age. Her parents have always supported her.
