What’s the climate like in Leipzig?

The current climate in Leipzig features temperatures ranging from a high of 26°C to a low of 5°C throughout the year. The average yearly temperature is around 16°C. At night, temperatures in the colder months average around 0°C and in the warmer months around 15°C.

The highest temperature recorded in Leipzig in recent years was 38°C in June 2019. The lowest daytime temperature recorded was -10°C in February 2021.

On average, Leipzig gets 564 mm of precipitation each year, including rain, snow, and hail. Of this, about 22 cm is snowfall. For comparison, New York gets 833 mm of precipitation each year.

The best time to visit Leipzig for sunny weather is May through September. During this period, you'll have good temperatures and not too much rain, making it a great time to visit Leipzig. In the other months of the year, there's too much rain or the temperature isn't ideal for a visit if you want sunny and pleasant weather.
