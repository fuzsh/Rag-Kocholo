Tom Clancy's Splinter Cell: Pandora Tomorrow is an action-adventure video game developed by Ubisoft Shanghai and published by Ubisoft Shanghai and Ubisoft Milan, while Ubisoft Montreal, the developer of the original Splinter Cell, was working on Chaos Theory.

Platforms: Microsoft Windows, PlayStation 3, Nintendo GameCube, PlayStation 2, Game Boy Advance, Xbox, Java

Initial release date: March 23, 2004

Genres: Stealth Video Game, Action-Adventure Video Game, Third-person Shooter, Tactical Shooter

Nominations: BAFTA Video Games Award: Online Match

Mode: Multiplayer game

Writer: J.T. Petty

Developers: Ubisoft Shanghai, Ubisoft Montreal, Gameloft, Ubisoft Annecy Studios
