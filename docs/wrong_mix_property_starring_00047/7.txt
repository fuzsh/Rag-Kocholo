Joanne Samuel (b. August 5, 1957) is an Australian actress who remains best known for her role as Jessie Rockatansky the screen wife of Mel Gibson's character Max in the 1979 film Mad Max. In 1997, she played as the school principal, Mrs. Bingle in the Wiggles full length feature film, The Wiggles Movie.
