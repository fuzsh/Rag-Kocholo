Therese of Saxe-Hildburghausen (Therese Charlotte Luise; 8 July 1792 – 26 October 1854) was the consort of King Ludwig I of Bavaria.

Issue[edit]

Maximilian II of Bavaria (28 November 1811 – 10 March 1864) married Princess Marie of Prussia and had issue

Princess Mathilde Caroline of Bavaria August 30 1813 – May 25 1862) married Ludwig III, Grand Duke of Hesse and by Rhine but had no issue.

Otto of Greece (1 June 1815 – 26 July 1867) married Duchess Amalia of Oldenburg but had no issue.

Princess Theodelinde of Bavaria (1816–1817) died young.

Luitpold, Prince Regent of Bavaria (12 March 1821 – 12 December 1912) married Archduchess Auguste Ferdinande of Austria and had issue.

Princess Adelgunde of Bavaria 19 March 1823 – 28 October 1914) married Francis V, Duke of Modena but had no surviving issue.

Princess Hildegard of Bavaria (10 June 1825 – 2 April 1864) married Archduke Albrecht, Duke of Teschen and had issue.

Princess Alexandra of Bavaria (26 August 1826 – 21 September 1875) never married.

Prince Adalbert of Bavaria (19 July 1828 – 21 September 1875) married Infanta Amalia of Spain and had issue.

Titles and styles[edit]

8 Julie 1792 - 23 September 1780 Her Serene Highness Princess Therese of Saxe-Altenburg, Duchess of Saxony.

23 September 1780 - 12 October 1810 Her Serene Highness Princess Therese of Saxe-Hildburghausen, Duchess of Saxony.

12 October 1810 - 13 October 1825 Her Royal Highness The Crown Princess.

13 October 1825 – 20 March 1848 Her Majesty The Queen.
