Meteorological data were collected across three visits to site P3 during COMICS cruise DY086 aboard the RRS Discovery in November and December, 2017. Measurements included: air pressure, temperature and humidity; solar and photosynthetically active radiation at both Port and Starboard sides. Data were provided by the British Oceanographic Data Centre and funded by the National Environment Research Council. BODC advised that the ship's anemometer shows inconsistencies and so data from the instrument were not included.
