Bio:

Alisha Glass (born Alisha Glass in Leland, Michigan, on April 5, 1988) is an American indoor volleyball player who plays setter. She attended Penn State University where she played setter for their volleyball team for four years. After graduating college, Alisha joined the U.S. women’s national team. Alisha helped the team win the gold medal at the 2014 World Championship, in which she was selected the tournament’s Best Setter. She spent the 2015/2016 season playing for Italian club team Imoco Volley Conegliano before being selected to play on the USA Women’s Olympic Volleyball Team in the 2016 Summer Olympics in Rio de Janeiro.

Best Known For:

Alisha is best known for playing setter for the U.S. women’s national volleyball team.
