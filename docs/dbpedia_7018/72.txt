Benjamin Franklin FRS FRSA FRSE was an American polymath and one of the Founding Fathers of the United States. Franklin was a leading writer, printer, political philosopher, politician, Freemason, postmaster, scientist, inventor, humorist, civic activist, statesman, and diplomat.

Born: 17 January 1706, Milk Street, Boston, Massachusetts, United States

Died: 17 April 1790, Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, United States

Vice President: Charles Biddle; Peter Muhlenberg; David Redick

Nicknames: Water-American, The Newton of Electricity

Siblings: James Franklin, Ebenezer Franklin, Jane Mecom
