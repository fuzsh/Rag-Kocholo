Village in West Azerbaijan, Iran

Razi (Persian: رازي, also Romanized as Rāzī)[1] is a village in Qatur Rural District, Qatur District, Khoy County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 753, in 129 families.[2] It contains a border crossing linking it to Kapıköy in Turkey.[3]
