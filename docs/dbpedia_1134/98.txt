Toni Garrido is a Brazilian singer, songwriter, and actor, known for his smooth voice and dynamic stage presence. He gained prominence as the lead vocalist for the reggae band Cidade Negra, blending reggae with Brazilian rhythms and rock influences. Throughout his career, Toni has also pursued solo projects, showcasing his versatility and passion for music.

Toni Garrido's lyrics often explore deep themes such as yearning for companionship, seeking divine guidance, and enduring personal journeys. His songs resonate with listeners who appreciate heartfelt narratives and reflections on life's challenges and triumphs. Recently, his work continues to delve into these profound subjects, maintaining his connection with audiences through relatable and inspiring messages.
