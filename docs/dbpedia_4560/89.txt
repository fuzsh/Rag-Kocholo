JACKSONVILLE, N.C. (Aug. 31, 2017) U.S. Marine Corps MV-22B Ospreys assigned to Marine Medium Tiltrotor Squadron (VMM) 162 (Reinforced), 26th Marine Expeditionary Unit (26th MEU) prepare for take off in support of a Defense Support to Civil Authority mission at Marine Corps Air Station New River, N.C. VMM-162 sent Ospreys to join the 690 Marines from the 26th MEU embarked aboard the amphibious assault ship USS Kearsarge (LHD 3) as part of prudent measures in anticipation of future tasking to support relief efforts in the aftermath of Hurricane Harvey.
