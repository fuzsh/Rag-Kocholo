U.S. Marines with Marine Medium Tiltrotor Squadron VMM 162 (REIN), 26th Marine Expeditionary Unit (MEU) participate in Combined Composite Training Unit Exercise (COMPTUEX) in the Atlantic Ocean .VMM-162 is the Aviation Combat Element of the 26th MEU that provides the Marine Air Ground Task Force (MAGTF) six functions of aviation from assault support, anti-air warfare, offensive air support, electronic warfare, control of aircraft and missiles and air reconnaissance. (U.S. Marine Corps video by Cpl. Juan A. Soto-Delgado)
