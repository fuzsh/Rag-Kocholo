Julie Dreyfus (born 24 January 1966) is a French actress. She is well known in Japan, where she made her TV debut on a French-language lesson program on NHK's educational channel in the late 1980s.

Julie is best known for her appearances in the Quentin Tarantino films Kill Bill Volume 1 and Kill Bill: Volume 2 (2004), in which she played Sofie Fatale. She starred in a similar role in Inglourious Basterds (2009) as Francesca Mondino, as Joseph Goebbels's French translator.

She is fluent in French, Japanese and English.
