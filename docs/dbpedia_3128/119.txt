Olin Patterson is the co-founder and serves on the Board of Directors and is a co-founder of International Surf Lifesaving Association (ISLA). With over ten years of lifeguarding, business, and international development experience, he has been leading teams of ISLA volunteers all over the globe while developing the brand and digital direction of ISLA.

International Surf Lifesaving Association

The International Surf Lifesaving Association is an international organization that advances professional lifesaving development to areas in need around the globe. ISLA aids people in championing the aquatic safety situation in their own coastal communities. This is accomplished through lifeguard training programs, lifeguard exchanges, equipment donations, purchasing connections, and utilizing the latest technology to sustain a global network of lifeguards that share information, techniques, stories, and culture.
