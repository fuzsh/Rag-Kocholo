Where is Dodge Center, Minnesota ?

Dodge Center is located in Dodge County, in southeastern Minnesota, United States. It is situated approximately 20 miles west of Rochester and about 70 miles southeast of Minneapolis. The municipality lies along U.S. Highway 14, providing connections to other regional towns and cities, including Owatonna to the west.
