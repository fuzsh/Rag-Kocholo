Biography

Margo Humphrey was born in 1942 in Oakland, California. She earned a BFA in Painting and Printmaking from the California College of Arts and Crafts and an MFA in Printmaking from Stanford University. She has taught art at numerous colleges and universities including the University of California Santa Cruz, the University of Texas at San Antonio, the San Francisco Art Institute, the School of the Art Institute of Chicago, and the University of Benin. She is currently the Department Head of Printmaking at the University of Maryland in College Park.
