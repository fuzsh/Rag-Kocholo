Set of six folded notecards depicting landmarks of Council Bluffs, Iowa: Historic General Dodge House, Squirrel Cage Jail, Old Bayliss Fountain, Wabash Trace Nature Trail, Council Bluffs Public Library, and the Broadway United Methodist Church. The Council Bluffs Watertower appears on the back of each card with a text description of the photograph on the front. Printed on thick Pearl paper, size 5.5” X 4” (A2). Insides are blank. Envelopes included.
