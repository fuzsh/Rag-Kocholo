This document provides information about the independent British sci-fi film Ex Machina. It was distributed by Universal Pictures with a budget of $15 million. It was produced by independent British company Film4 and had a theatrical release in the UK in January 2015 and the US in March 2015. The film used various locations in Norway and the UK for filming and employed visual effects in post-production to create the robotic features of the main character Ava. It targeted a niche audience of females and those aged 15-25 with interests in technology and sci-fi.
