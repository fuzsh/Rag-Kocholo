Nicky Henson (12 May 1945-16 December 2019[1]) voiced characters in several Big Finish Doctor Who audio stories: Pieter Stubbe in Loups-Garoux, Dick Turpin in The Doomwood Curse and Gregson Grenville in Deimos.

He was previously considered for the role of the DJ in Revelation of the Daleks. (TCH 41)

Personal life[]

He was once married to fellow Big Finish actor Una Stubbs.
