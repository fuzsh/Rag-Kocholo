ST. LOUIS (First Alert 4) – We are partnering with the St. Louis Blues to rebroadcast the clinching game of all four rounds from the 2019 Stanley Cup Championship Run!

KMOV will rebroadcast the games on over-the-air Channel 32 and Charter Spectrum Channel 186. Blues radio broadcaster Chris Kerber will host the day-long broadcast on June 12, which will start at 9 a.m. and run for nine hours until 6 p.m.

Full Broadcast Schedule:

9 a.m. – 11 a.m. Round 1, Game 6 vs. Winnipeg from April 20, 2019

11 a.m. – 1 p.m. Round 2, Game 7 vs. Dallas from May 7, 2019

1 p.m. – 3 p.m. Western Conference Final, Game 6 vs. San Jose from May 21, 2019

3 p.m. – 6 p.m. Stanley Cup Final Game 7 at Boston from June 12, 2019

Players and management will give commentary during the Stanley Cup Final Game 7 rebroadcast. On the radio, 101 ESPN will air Game 7 of the Stanley Cup Final, commercial-free, from 6 to 7:30 p.m. on June 12.

The Blues have designated June 12 as “We Went Blues Day” to commemorate the fifth anniversary of the day the team won the Stanley Cup. Click here for more events the Blues are hosting for “We Went Blues Day.”
