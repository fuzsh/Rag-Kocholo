Ayaka Ozubulu, whose real name is Chigozie Obunike, is a well known Igbo highlife musician. He is cherished by both youthful and elderly people in the eastern part of Nigeria in light of his brilliant tunes.

Practically the entirety of his tunes conveys a rousing story that draws the consideration of his audience members, which settled on them to decision him the King of Highlife Music, despite the fact that he had declined such named.
