LULU ON THE BRIDGE Highly successful American writer PAUL AUSTER (The New York Trilogy, Leviathan) always loved movies. Now LULU ON THE BRIDGE marks his solo debut as a director. In there Academy Award-winning actress, MIRA SORVINO (The Mighty Aphrodite, Mimic), appears as charming Celia Burns in the jazzy and smokey world of Izzy Maurer (Harvey Keitel), a saxophone player. Their extraordinary love affaire gets to an dramatic end, when MIRA SORVINO disappears as LULU.
