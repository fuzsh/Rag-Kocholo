Pentair Plc (Pentair) is a provider of water treatment solutions. It designs, manufactures and markets aquatic systems, filtration and separation, and flow management solutions. Its product portfolio includes pressure vessels, gas recovery solutions, pumps, agricultural spray nozzles, pressure tanks, control valves, activated carbon products, automatic controls, automatic cleaners. Pentair also offers flow technology equipment such as water disposal pumps, water supply pumps, fluid transfer pumps, turbine pumps, and solid handling pumps. Key brands of the company include Aurora, Jung Pumpen, Pentair, Berkeley, Shurflo, Codeline, Kreepy Krauly, Haffmans, X-Flow, Aquatic Eco-Systems and Sta-Rite. It serves food and beverage; oil and gas; industrial; agriculture; residential; and commercial water treatment sectors. The company has operations in the US, Europe, the Middle East and Asia- Pacific. The company is headquartered in London, the UK.
