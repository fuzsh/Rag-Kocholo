Siti Walidah, better known as Nyai Ahmad Dahlan, was a female emancipation figure, wife of Muhammadiyah founder Ahmad Dahlan, and National Hero of Indonesia.

Nyai Ahmad Dahlan was born on January 3, 1872, in Yogyakarta, Yogyakarta, Indonesia. Her birth geographical coordinates are 7° 48’ 5” South latitude and 110° 21’ 53” East longitude. Nyai Ahmad Dahlan passed away at the age of 74 in 1946.

Astrologically, Nyai Ahmad Dahlan falls under the Sun sign of Capricorn, with Aries as her Ascendant, Libra as her Moon sign, and Cancer as her Black Moon Lilith sign.
