Alda also wrote and directed several episodes of the series. He made his directorial film debut with The Four Seasons (1981) and has appeared in films such as Same Time, Next Year (1978), California Suite (1978), and The Seduction of Joe Tynan (1979). Alda received a nomination for the Academy Award for Best Supporting Actor for his role in The Aviator (2004).
