Anne Anne of Bohemia Edit Profile

Anne of Bohemia was the eldest surviving daughter of Wenceslaus II of Bohemia and Poland and his first wife Judith of Habsburg.

Background

Anne was born in 1290 in the Kingdom of Bohemia. Her mother, Judith, died in 1297 when Anne was seven years old. Of her mother"s ten children, only four of them lived to adulthood: Wenceslaus, Anne, Elisabeth and Margaret.

Career

Her siblings included Elisabeth of Bohemia and Wenceslaus III of Bohemia. In 1300 Anne"s widowed father, Wenceslaus, remarried a Polish princess called Elisabeth Richeza from the genus Piast dynasty. Anne"s father then gained the Crown of Poland for the next five years (he died in 1305).

They had a daughter, Agnes of Bohemia.
