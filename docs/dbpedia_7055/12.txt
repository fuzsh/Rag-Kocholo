Located in Madrid, Museo del Traje presents the evolution of clothing from the 16th century to the present day. It displays a large collection, ranging from historical costumes to jewelry, embroidery or rosaries, among many others.

Access its website to enjoy its activities, collections, guided tours, library or concerts: www.culturaydeporte.gob.es/mtraje/inicio.html
