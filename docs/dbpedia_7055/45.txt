8. Museo del Traje

Madrid, Spain

An often overlooked museum that has a surprisingly large collection and beautiful exhibitions.

Since: 1925 (1st exhibition), 2004 (current museum)

Size: 160,000 (pieces & documents)

Collection: historical, contemporary and traditional clothing, jewellery and accessories (with a focus on Spanish dress & designers)

Exhibitions: multiple galleries
