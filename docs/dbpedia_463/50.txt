Oscar Einar Steen (born February 20, 1982 in Stockholm, Sweden) is a professional Swedish ice hockey forward. He has played two seasons in the Swedish Elitserien, the first playing for Färjestads BK and the second for Leksands IF. He's currently playing for Modo Hockey. In 2007 Oscar won the Swedish Championships with Modo Hockey.

His older brother, Calle, has also played in Swedish Elitserien.
