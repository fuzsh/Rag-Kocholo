The Chief of Staff, Army announces the assignment of the following general officers:

Major General Charles H. Swannack, Jr., Commander, Multinational Division (North), Stabilization Force-11, Task Force Eagle, Operation Joint Forge, to Commanding General, 82d Airborne Division, Fort Bragg, North Carolina, with a report date to be determined.

Major General Robert Wilson, Chief, Office of Military Cooperation, Egypt, Cairo, Egypt, to Commanding General, 7th Infantry Division (Light) and Fort Carson, Fort Carson, Colorado, with a report date to be determined.

Major General John R. Wood, Director of Strategy, Plans and Policy, Office of the Deputy Chief of Staff, G-3, United States Army, Washington, DC, to Commanding General, 2d Infantry Division, Eighth United States Army, Korea, with a report date to be determined.

Brigadier General (Promotable) John R. Batiste, Senior Military Assistant to the Deputy Secretary of Defense, Washington, DC, to Commanding General, 1st Infantry Division, United States Army Europe and Seventh Army, Germany, with a report date to be determined.

Brigadier General (Promotable) Eric T. Olson, Commandant of Cadets, United States Military Academy, West Point, New York, to Commanding General, 25th Infantry Division (Light), Schofield Barracks, Hawaii, with a report date to be determined.
