Penn Howard was born and raised in Los Angeles and began his acting career at age five. He is a part of SAG after appearing in national commercials (Heinz, Airbnb, Walmart), short films and print. Penn has been doing theater for several years and his favorite acting class is Marnie Cooper School of Acting because it offers an honest approach that feels genuine to him. He is very athletic playing lacrosse, basketball and tennis but doesn't consider himself sporty. He's a reader, writer, thinker, DnDer, animal lover and straight A student. He is playful, has a great sense of humor and is happy to discuss history or all things animals anytime.
