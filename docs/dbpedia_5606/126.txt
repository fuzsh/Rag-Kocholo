William Howard Penn OBITUARY

HEBRON, Ind. – William Howard Penn Sr., 66, of Hebron, Ind., passed away Friday, April, 1, 2011, at his home.

Bill was born in Columbus, Ohio, on Feb. 10, 1945, to Walter James and Pearl Marie Penn. He was raised in Trenton, N.J., graduated from Steinert High School, and attended Philadelphia College of Bible.

Bill is survived by his wife, Judith Arlene Nirshl Penn; daughters, Judith Arlene Moates of Cahokia, Ill., Michelle Anne (Dave) Corrigan of Kouts, Ind., Kimberly Mae (Doug) Fritts of Portage, Ind., and Dawn Marie (Mike) Eggert of Lowell, Ind.; a son, William H. Penn Jr., currently attending DePaul University, Chicago, Ill.; grandchildren, Jessica Marsalek, Anthony Marsalek and Adam Marsalek, of Dupo, Ill., Michaela Gazdich, Crystal Centifanto, Macqui Corrigan, Beth Corrigan and Josh Corrigan, all of Kouts, Ind., Dyllan Eggert of Lowell, Ind., Christian and Joseph Fritts of Portage, Ind., and Joshua and Jacob Moates of Dupo, Ill.

Bill was preceded in death by his parents.

Bill lived in Fairless Hills, Pa., after marrying, where he was a member of Knights of Columbus Council No. 4215 and St. John Neumann 4th Degree No. 933, and 4th Degree Color Corp. He was also a member of St. Francis Cabrini Catholic Church.

In 1981 he moved to Indiana, where he was employed by American National Can Co. in Hammond, Ind., from which he retired in 1993. Bill was also employed by the Andrean High School maintenance department for eight years. Bill was a member of St. Elizabeth Ann Seton Catholic Church in Valparaiso, Ind., where he served as a Eucharistic Minister, and served as Grand Knight for the Elizabeth Seton Knights of Columbus No. 7473. Bill was a very devoted husband, father and best friend.

A Funeral Mass was held at St. Elizabeth Ann Seton Church in Valparaiso, Ind., on Thursday, April 7, 2011. Services concluded at the church with cremation to follow.

Memorial contributions may be given to William’s family.
