Biography

Howard S. Penn was a son of John T. Penn (b. abt.1831) and Susanna (b. abt. 1828).

Howard had five siblings: Celia, Eliza, Charles, Mary Olevia, and Gulda.

Howard married Martha E. Welsh (1864-1883) on March 5, 1883. She died later that year in giving birth to their son Howard E. Penn (1883-1927).

Howard then married Martha's younger sister Rachel J. Welsh (1867-1922) on December 24, 1886. They had a son Bradley R. Penn (1887-1953).

Howard and other members of his family are buried at the Morgan Chapel United Methodist Cemetery in Carroll County, specifically Howard E. Penn (and wife Grace), Bradley R. Penn (and wife Alice), George William Welsh (and wife Emma), Susan Ellen Welsh of 1871, and Charles Welsh of 1876 (and grandsons Charles Jr. & Wm. Francis).
