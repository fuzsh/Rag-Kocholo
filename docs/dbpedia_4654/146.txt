The legendary robotic doggy, K-9, from the iconic British series Doctor Who will be getting his own television program.

The funny and adorable K-9 appeared in a 1997 serial, The Invisible Enemy, and in a recent spin-off entitled The Sarah Jane Adventures

K-9 announced the 2017 release of a new series dedicated solely to his adventures on his Facebook page.

Very few details are known so far, however, it has been revealed that the creator of K-9, Bob Baker, will be involved at some sort of capacity, and that the Doctor Who villain Omega will also be featured.
