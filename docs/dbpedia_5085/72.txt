Silent Hill is a psychological horror movie directed by Christophe Gans, written by Roger Avary, produced by Samuel Hadida and Don Carmody, based on Konami's 1999 video game of the same name. Starring Radha Mitchell, Laurie Holden, Sean Bean, Deborah Kara Unger, Tanya Allen, Alice Krige, and Jodelle Ferland, it was released on April 21, 2006, in Canada. The film was shot on sound sets and on location in Brantford, Ontario and Hamilton, Ontario in Canada. Additional filming took place at Toronto Film Studios, Alma College, Lister Block, and Farewell Studios.

Where was Silent Hill Filmed?

Silent Hill was filmed in 1119 Sewells Road (Road to Silent Hill), Alma College, Ancient & Accepted Scottish Rite of Freemasonry of Canada, Brampton, Brantford, Burford, Colborne Street (Silent Hill Street), Devil's Punchbowl, Hamilton, Lister Block, St. George, St. Thomas and Winnipeg.
