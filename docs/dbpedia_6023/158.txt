The Nest is a 2020 thriller film written, directed, and produced by Sean Durkin. It stars Jude Law, Carrie Coon, Charlie Shotwell, Oona Roche, and Adeel Akhtar.

It had its world premiere at the Sundance Film Festival on January 26, 2020. and was released on September 18, 2020, by IFC Films.
