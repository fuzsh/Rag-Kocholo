This page contains maps created to enhance your awareness of the soaring environment.

CAUTION – THE USE OF THESE MAPS DOES NOT FULFILL YOUR RESPONSIBILITY TO REFER TO THE APPROPRIATE, CURRENT AND OFFICIAL NAVIGATION CHART! THEY ARE FOR INFORMATIONAL PURPOSES ONLY!

Airspace Maps

These are simple maps marking the airspace around each Utah Soaring Association gliderport. They are designed to print in a larger format of 7″ x 10″, or a smaller postcard size of 3.5″ x 5″. Each map can be printed with or without a light watermark display of the surrounding terrain.
