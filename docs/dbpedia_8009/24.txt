Cory Doctorow is a science fiction author, activist, and journalist. His latest book is Attack Surface, a stand-alone adult sequel to Little Brother. He is also the author of How to Destroy Surveillance Capitalism, nonfiction about conspiracies and monopolies; and of Radicalized and Walkaway, science fiction for adults; a young adult graphic novel called In Real Life; and young adult novels like Homeland, Pirate Cinema, and Little Brother. He maintains a daily blog at Pluralistic.net. He works for the Electronic Frontier Foundation, is an MIT Media Lab research affiliate, a visiting professor of computer science at The Open University, a visiting professor of practice at the University of North Carolina’s School of Library and Information Science, and co-founded the U.K.’s Open Rights Group.
