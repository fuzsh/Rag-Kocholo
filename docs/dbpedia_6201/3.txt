OUR STORY

Mercury, has grown to be one of the leading names in the IT peripherals & Power products industry. Founded in 2021, it began as a distributor of Computer Peripherals and Power-related hardware products globally.

To facilitate our operations, we have dedicated subcontract facilities in China, strategically located offices worldwide and more than 550 global distribution channels. Our current market reach includes Middle East, Africa, CIS, India and Indian Subcontinents.

Our strategic business objective is to consistently develop competitive and innovative products to meet the trend of rapidly increasing customer demands.

At Mercury, our core strengths lie in our talented Marketing and Product Management Team, our strong relationship with vendors and a strong Management Team. Our core assets are our customers, vendors and employees. To strengthen our customer care, we have cutting edge service centres in all major markets providing technical and repair support to our customers.
