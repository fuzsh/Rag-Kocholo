Daniel Clark Roper is a 20th and 21st century Human man. He served in the United States Navy and notably commanded the USS Enterprise (CVN-65).

Biography[]

In 1992, Captain Roper served as the 13th CO of the nuclear-powered aircraft carrier Enterprise. During his command, the vessel underwent a refit at the Newport News shipyard, and on 30 September the vessel was officially transferred to Atlantic Fleet Command in Norfolk, Virginia. During the refit, ship's personnel was reduced to 3,000. (The Official Fan Club Magazine Issue 92: "Operation: Enterprise!")
