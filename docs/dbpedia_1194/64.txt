David Robbins is an American photographer who was born in 1957. Numerous key galleries and museums such as Krakow Witkin Gallery have featured David Robbins's work in the past.David Robbins's work has been offered at auction multiple times, with realized prices ranging from 63 USD to 12,557 USD, depending on the size and medium of the artwork. Since 2004 the record price for this artist at auction is 12,557 USD for Talent, sold at Phillips London in 2014. David Robbins has been featured in articles for South African Art Times, Daily Serving and e-flux.
