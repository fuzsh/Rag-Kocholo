Park Jang-hyun (born June 6, 1989) is a South Korean male ballad singer and the leader of the four-member vocal group Bromance. He gained recognition in 2011 after participating in Mnet's 'Superstar K3', where he received high praise from judge Lee Seung-chul for his performance of 'Regret'. He began his music career in 2013 by participating in the OST of the drama 'The Heirs' and debuted as a singer on July 12, 2016, with Bromance's first EP 'The Action'. The group has made a significant impact through various music programs.
