Important ZIP Code 84032 Information

ZIP Code 84032 is located in the city of Heber City, Utah and covers 328.452 square miles of land area. It is also located within Wasatch County.

While 84032 may be known by these alias names, the USPS advises not to use them when addressing mail to this ZIP Code: Center Creek, Charleston, Hailstone, Heber, Independence and Keetley.

According to the 2020 U.S. Census, there are 24,766 people in 7,485 households. ZIP-Codes.com estimates that the current population is 31,488. It has a population density of 75.4 people per square mile.

84032 is classified as a "non-unique", also known as a Standard ZIP Code. Its primary purpose is for normal street, apartment, and business mailbox delivery. Carriers will deliver mail to this region through vehicles, walking routes, and other means. It may also have PO Boxes located at the USPS facility.

84032 is in the Mountain (GMT -07:00) timezone and does observe daylight saving time. It only has the 435 Area Code providing telephone service.

A CBSA is broken into two sub categories, Metro and Micro, and are detailed in the CBSA Type Field. These are defined by their population densities.
