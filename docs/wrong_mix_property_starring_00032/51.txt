Demi takes on singing wannabe Mitchie again in the Disney Channel musical.

British fans can get a sneak peek of the highly anticipated TV film on the Disney Channel at 8.55am on May 15, which will preview the video for one of the lead tunes, It's On.

The sequel sees competition for Camp Rock from a new music camp, which has opened across the lake and poaches Camp Rock's tutors as well as threatening its existence - before Mitchie steps in and vows to save her beloved camp.

Since its premiere in 2008, Camp Rock has been watched by over 137 million worldwide, according to the channel.
