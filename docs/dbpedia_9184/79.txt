village, former national capital

Village in Trakai district municipality, administrative centre of eldership. There is a railway station, post office, two schools, church, site of former castle mound and monastery.

Supposedly, in 1321 grand duke Gediminas moved the capital of Lithuanian Grand Duchy to Senieji Trakai until 1322.
