Tapulous has signed up another major label for its bespoke iPhone games. Its new deal with UMG will kick off with a Tap Tap Revenge spin-off game for Lady GaGa, which launches today. Others will follow, while UMG tracks will also be made available for download in the upcoming Tap Tap Revenge 3.0 game. The latter is interesting because for the first time Tapulous will be charging for track downloads – $0.50 each.
