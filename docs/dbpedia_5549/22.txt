DESCRIPTION

High Priestess of Soul is the last album Simone released under Philips Records. The songs are accompanied by a large band directed by Hal Mooney.

The album contains popular songs, such as 'Don't You Pay Them No Mind' and African American gospel and folk related songs written by Simone herself, such as 'Take Me to the Water' and 'Come Ye'.

After this album title, Nina Simone was sometimes referred to as 'the high priestess of soul.'
