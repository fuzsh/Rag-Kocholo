Amanda Peet is an American actress known for her versatile performances in various films and television shows. Born on January 11, 1972, she started her career with television roles before making her feature film debut in Animal Room (1995). However, it was her portrayal of Jill St. Claire in The Whole Nine Yards (2000) that gained her wider recognition. Peet has since appeared in a range of films, including Saving Silverman (2001), Something's Gotta Give (2003), and 2012 (2009). In addition to her film work, she has also showcased her acting skills on television shows like Jack & Jill (1999-2001), Studio 60 on the Sunset Strip (2006-2007), Togetherness (2015–2016), Brockmire (2016–2020), and Dirty John (2020).
