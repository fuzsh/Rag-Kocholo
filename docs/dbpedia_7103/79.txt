Software company OrangeScape Technologies has teamed up with

Team Computers, a New Delhi-based IT infrastructure solution provider, to expand

its reach across the nation for its flagship product called Dimension. Suresh

Sambandam, Founder and CEO, OrangeScape Technologies said, "We are very

excited about our tie-up with Team Computers to market our flagship product

Dimension to a much wider customer base in India."

Dimension is OrangeScape's flagship product. Dimension is a

rule-based business application development platform for business analysts,

consultants and domain experts, without writing a single line of code.

Applications are defined (not coded) using a spreadsheet like interface.

The applications are automatically deployed as J2EE

components with ready to use persistence, web like search, workflow and

enterprise portal capabilities.

Ranjan Chopra, Chairman and CEO, Team Computers said,

"Dimension is an excellent fit for our Enterprise Information Portal (EIP)

strategy. We pick and choose code-less technologies as elements of our EIP

offering that leads to business activity monitoring and management, with a

closed feedback loop."

OrangeScape Dimension is one of the first Indian product companies to release

a SAP certified connector product.
