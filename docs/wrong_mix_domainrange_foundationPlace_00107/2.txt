The Bloomfield Educational Foundation (BEF) will host a 2024 Gala, honoring some of Bloomfield’s best. The ceremony will take place on April 24, at Nanina’s in the Park, in Belleville, NJ.

UNICO is an acronym which stands for Unity, Neighborliness, Integrity, Charity and Opportunity. The Bloomfield chapter of UNICO was formed nearly 70 years ago, to promote and enhance the image of Italian Americans, through community service.
