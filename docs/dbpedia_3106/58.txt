Where is Salmon, Idaho ?

Salmon lies in Lemhi County, situated in the central part of Idaho, USA. Nestled within the Rocky Mountains, it is located near the confluence of the Salmon River and the Lemhi River. The town is northwest of Idaho Falls and southeast of Missoula, Montana.
