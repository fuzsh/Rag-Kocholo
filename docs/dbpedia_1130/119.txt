MOSCOW - Ukraine wants to see integration processes within the CIS, particularly in economic terms, Ukrainian First Deputy Prime Minister Valeriy Khoroshkovsky has said.

"We are closely monitoring the processes happening within the CIS, the Eurasian Economic Community, and, of course, we support these integration processes, first and foremost, in economic terms," he said at a briefing in Moscow on Monday after the meeting of the Interstate Council of the Eurasian Economic Community.
