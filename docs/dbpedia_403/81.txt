Weather in Tyumen in February

Temperatures on an average day in Tyumen in February

The average temperature in Tyumen in February for a typical day ranges from a high of 11°F (-12°C) to a low of -4°F (-20°C). Some would describe it as freezing cold and breezy.

For comparison, the hottest month in Tyumen, July, has days with highs of 75°F (24°C) and lows of 56°F (13°C). The coldest month, January has days with highs of 3°F (-16°C) and lows of -11°F (-24°C). This graph shows how an average day looks like in Tyumen in February based on historical data.
