Biography[]

Piers Anthony Dillingham Jacob (born August 6, 1934 in Oxford, England is an English American writer in the science fiction and fantasy genres, publishing under the name Piers Anthony. He is most famous for his long-running novel series set in the fictional realm of Xanth.

Anthony's family immigrated to the United States from Britain when he was six.

He graduated from Goddard College in Vermont in 1956. He became a naturalized U.S. citizen while serving in the United States Army in 1958. After completing a two year stint in military service, he briefly taught school at Admiral Farragut Academy in St. Petersburg, Florida before becoming a fulltime writer.

Many of his books have appeared on the New York Times Best Seller list.

He has claimed that one of his greatest achievements has been to publish a book for every letter of the alphabet, from Anthology to Zombie Lover.
