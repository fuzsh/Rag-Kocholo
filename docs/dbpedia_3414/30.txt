This week, South Central Los Angeles, California emcee veteran WC released his first product in over three years – That’s What I’m Talking About. The five song EP is the Westside Connection/Low Profile member’s first work with New York’s E1 Entertainment, having spent the last five years signed to Ice Cube’s Lench Mob imprint.

The work features fellow west coast veterans Kokane and Tha Dogg Pound. The tracklisting to the December 20 digital release is as follows:

1. What’s Good (feat. Kokane)

2. Stickin To The Script (feat. Tha Dogg Pound & Bad Lucc)

3. That’s What I’m Talking About

4. I’m Official

5. Frontline
