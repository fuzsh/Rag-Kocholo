Welcome to Woodcrest Christian

Passionately Preparing Students to Impact the World for Christ

We proclaim Him, admonishing every man and teaching every man with all wisdom, so that we may present every man complete in Christ.

Colossians 1:28

Located in Riverside, Calif., Woodcrest Christian School is a private school (TK-12) committed to providing a Christ-centered education with numerous opportunities for students to be immersed in the richness of a well-rounded, classic education, focused on God’s eternal Truth. In that effort, we strive to prepare each of our students to confidently face the world ahead of them, prepared to use their God-given talents to demonstrate the love of Christ in word and deed. After more than 70 years, our mission remains to glorify God by pursuing excellence in Christ-centered academics. Discover how Woodcrest Christian can prepare your child for the future and anchor their foundation in Christ.
