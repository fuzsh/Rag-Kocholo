Fedora : Very large (two sheet) Italian Art Nouveau period opera poster by Marcello Dudovich, 1899. This is an advertisement for a production of "Fedora" by Umberto Giordano which was performed for the month of June at the Teatro Verdi.

Dudovich (1878-1962) is recognized as one of Italy's greatest poster artists.
