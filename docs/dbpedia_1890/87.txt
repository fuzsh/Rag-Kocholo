Sarawak is a releasable from Malaysia. It is located in Asia .

Background[]

Sarawak is one of the two Malaysian states located on the island of Borneo. It is situated in the northwest of the island, bordering the Malaysian state of Sabah to the northeast, Indonesia to the south, and Brunei to the north. Sarawak has a population of over 2.7 million people and its capital city is Kuching. The state is known for its diverse culture and rich history, as well as its natural attractions such as national parks, beaches, and rainforests. Sarawak is also a significant producer of oil and gas, and its economy is driven by industries such as agriculture, forestry, and tourism.
