Spanish tennis player David Ferrer has an estimated net worth of $10 million. In October 2013, the Association of Tennis Professionals ranked him third.

In 2013, he was finalist at the French Open. Meanwhile, he reached the semi-finals of the US and Australian Opens twice. He was also a quarter finalists the Wimbledon Open twice. Furthermore, he was a member of the Spain Davis Cup team which won the 2011, 2008 and 2009 Finals.

Though Ferrer doesn't have the powerful strokes which many of his contemporaries possess today, he is still considered as one of the most successful tennis players on all surfaces today, most especially on clay and hard courts. He is known for his consistent determination, baseline play, fitness, and of course, his excellent footspeed.
