Founded in 2009, True Patriot Love Foundation has become Canada's leading organization supporting military members, Veterans and their families. The foundation raises awareness around the challenges related to military service and funds programs that deliver significant impact in the areas of mental and physical health, rehabilitation & recovery from injury, supporting the children and spouses of those who serve, and promoting re-integration into communities. Since inception, True Patriot Love has committing over $28 million in funding to 825 community-based programs across the country, which has helped change the lives of more than 30,000 military members, Veterans and families.
