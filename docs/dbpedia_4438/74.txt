Umicore SA reported earnings results for the full year ended December 31, 2023. For the full year, the company reported sales was EUR 18,265.9 million compared to EUR 25,435.5 million a year ago. Revenue was EUR 18,414.4 million compared to EUR 25,620.1 million a year ago.

Net income was EUR 385.1 million compared to EUR 569.9 million a year ago. Basic earnings per share from continuing operations was EUR 1.6 compared to EUR 2.37 a year ago. Diluted earnings per share from continuing operations was EUR 1.6 compared to EUR 2.37 a year ago.
