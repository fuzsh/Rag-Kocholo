Fifty vividly painted sculptures of cyclists accompanied by two motorcyclists ride their life race, forming the installation Cyclus, a work of art of the Split sculptor and painter Vasko Lipovac (1931.-2006.). Since its first exhibition in Zagreb in 1995, this forty-meter long installation has visited many Croatian and European exhibition spaces and has became one of the most famous and most recognizable works of Croatian Contemporary Art. Its last exhibition took place in London on the occasion of the passage of the Tour de France through the city, and since the year 2000 one part of the installation makes permanent representation in the Olympic Museum in Lausanne. This publication accompanied exhibition in London in Jully 2014.
