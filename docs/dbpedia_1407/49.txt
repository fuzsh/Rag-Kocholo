Why choose studio 7?

Finding the right dance studio can make all the difference. Studio 7 teaches students self-confidence and how to use drive and focus to turn dreams into reality through the art of dance. The qualified faculty offers dance classes in a range of styles, and our programs are designed to fit your special interests, including recreational classes, non-competitive performance tracks, full-curriculum competitive teams, and routes to college and post-college professional dance. Studio 7 fosters a culture of support and inspiration, provides a space for children to develop and dream big, and gives your child a place to feel welcome and a community to belong to.
