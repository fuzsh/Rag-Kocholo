Sanaya Irani, the innocent and reserved Paro of new TV series Rang Rasiya is an Indian actress, host, and a model. She is popular among the youth audience.

She gained immense popularity via TV series Miley Jab Hum Tum as Gunjan Bhusan, Iss Pyar Ko Kya Naam Doon as Khushi Kumari Gupta, and Chhanchhan as Chhanchhan Sarabhai-Borisagar. Sanaya started her acting career on television with with SAB TV’s popular show Left Right Left as Sameera Shroff. She acted in NDTV Imagine’s Radhaa Ki Betiyaan Kuch Kar Dikhayengi as Sanaya. She both participated and hosted in many reality shows including Kaho Na Yaar Hai as participant, Zara Nachke Dikha – Season 2 as host, Meethi Choori No.1 as participant, Movers & Shakers Masala Markey as guest, Star Parivaar Ka Tyohar as guest, Welcome – Baazi Mehmaan Nawazi Ki as participant, Ishq Wala Love as co-host with Karan Grover, Jhalak Dikhhla Jaa (season 6) as guest co-host with Drashti Dhami in Teen Ka Tadka week, and Bigg Boss 7 as guest.

Currently, she is portrayed in TV series Rang Rasiya as Paro, aka Parvati, a very reserved and innocent Rajasthani girl opposite Ashish Sharma as Rudra. Their on-screen chemistry is well-received by the audience.

Sanaya also appeared in various advertisements with big Bollywood stars like Shahrukh Khan (Belmonte), Amitabh Bachchan (Dabur Honey), Priyanka Chopra (Lux), Saif Ali Khan (Lee), and Kareena Kapoor. She also appeared in music videos like Jagjit Singh’s song “Tumko Dekha To Yeh” among others. She also played a minor role in the Bollywood movie Fanaa starring Amir Khan.

Sanaya was born on September 17, 1983 in Mumbai to Parsi parents. She has one brother and one sister who both live in Chicago, U.S. She studied for 7 years in a boarding school in Ooty. She graduated from Sydenham College. She is currently dating Mohit Sehgal, her co-actor from TV series Miley Jab Hum Tum.
