Description

JOSAPAR Joaquim Oliveira S.A. Participações operates as a food products company in Brazil. The company offers soy-based powdered products under the Soy+ brand; olive oils under the Nova Oliva brand; rice under the Nikkoh, Beleza, Tio Mingote, No Ponto, Pedrinho, Bataclan, and Impacto brands; and other products under the Tio João, arroz Tio João, Supra Soy, Meu Biju, and SUPREMO INSUMOS brands. It exports to approximately 40 countries. The company was incorporated in 1973 and is headquartered in Porto Alegre, Brazil. JOSAPAR Joaquim Oliveira S.A. Participações is a subsidiary of Peroli S/A Participações.
