Joe Reilly’s Columbia FM is selling its Full Service Oldies “WHLM” network to Seven Mountains Media for $450,000.

The deal includes two AMs and four translators all running the same programming: 930 WHLM Bloomsburg, 1280 WBWX Berwick, 94.7 W234BH West Hazleton, 96.3 W242BY Berwick, 104.3 W282CO Bloomsburg, and 105.5 W288DF Danville. As part of the acquisition, Columbia FM owner Joe Reilly signed a five year non-compete agreement to own or manage a station within 60 miles.
