For the FM radio station in Bloomsburg, Pennsylvania, United States formerly holding the call sign WHLM-FM from 1981-2001, see WCFT-FM. For the FM radio station in Berwick, Pennsylvania, United States formerly holding the call sign WHLM-FM from 2006-2017, see WMMZ.

WHLM (930 kHz "Pop Radio") is a commercial radio station airing a CHR - Top 40 radio format.[2] It is licensed to Bloomsburg, Pennsylvania, and is owned by Seven Mountains Media. WHLM simulcasts with sister station WBWX 1280 AM in Berwick.
