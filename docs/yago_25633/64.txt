Jacques Doillon (French: [dwajɔ̃]; born 15 March 1944) is a French film director and screenwriter. Some actresses to break through are Fanny Bastien, Sandrine Bonnaire, Judith Godrèche, Marianne Denicourt, Charlotte Gainsbourg and Juliette Binoche.

Early life and education

Doillon was born in Paris and attended the Lycée Voltaire.

Career

His 1989 film The 15 Year Old Girl was entered into the 16th Moscow International Film Festival.
