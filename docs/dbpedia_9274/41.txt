Karl Lawson is Deputy Headteacher at Ysgol John Bright in Llandudno where he has led on a number of school improvement areas, including teaching and learning, professional learning, early career induction and health and well-being, working with colleagues across both Wales and in England to facilitate improvements in all of these. Karl previously held the role of Chair and Chief Examiner for A Level Religious Studies at WJEC for several years and has also authored a number of subject textbooks.
