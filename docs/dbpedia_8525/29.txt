On July 24th, Basler Zeitung published an article about Spirecut: “Spirecut, a Muttenz-based medtech start-up, is revolutionizing hand surgery with a groundbreaking ultrasound-guided technique spearheaded by CEO Frédéric Schuind. Moving away from traditional incision-based methods, Spirecut’s innovation requires a minor puncture to treat common hand disorders such as trigger finger and carpal tunnel syndrome.”
