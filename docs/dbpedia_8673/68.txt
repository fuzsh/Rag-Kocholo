Aoife Mulholland is a musical theatre actress from Salthill, Co Galway. She spent 10 years working in London. Her career took off after she took part in Andrew Lloyd Webber’s reality TV show How Do You Solve a Problem like Maria? It was searching for unknown talent. Although she didn’t win, the exposure catapulted her into a career where she played many lead roles in West End musicals including Chicago and Legally Blonde. She finally got to play Maria in The Sound of Music in the Palladium. She lives in Dundrum, with her husband, Jason Molins, a former captain of the Irish cricket team and their three sons, Max (11), Brody (9) and Zander (7).
