In this Spanish name, the first or paternalsurname is Ledesma and the second or maternal family name is Magaña.

Israel Reyes Ledesma Magaña (16 May 1954 – 27 November 2014) was a Mexican politician from the Institutional Revolutionary Party. From 2009 to 2012 he served as Deputy of the LXI Legislature of the Mexican Congress representing the State of Mexico.[1][2]
