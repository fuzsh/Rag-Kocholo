Sharon Brown has been played over 10 times on NTS, first on 17 August 2014. Sharon Brown's music has been featured on 13 episodes.

Sharon Brown is a singer-songwriter and musician from New York, New York. Brown wrote a song called "The Family Tree" in 1981, Despite receiving club play in the both U.S. and UK, the single was never officially issued. She scored her most successful hit single with her first official release, "I Specialize In Love" in 1982. The song spent three weeks at #2 on the Hot Dance Club Songs chart.
