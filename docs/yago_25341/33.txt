Nazli Sabri and Fuad I of Egypt were married for 16 years. They dated for 4 months after getting together in 1919. After a 12 days engagement they were married on 24th May 1919. 16 years later Fuad I of Egypt died, leaving behind his partner and 5 children.

They had 5 children, Farouk (104), Fawzia (102), Faiza (100), Faika (98) and Fathia (93).

About

Egyptian Royal Nazli Sabri was born Nazli Abdurrahim Sabri on 25th June, 1894 in Alexandria, Egypt and passed away on 29th May 1978 Los Angeles, California, United States aged 83. She is most remembered for Queen of Egypt from 1919 to 1936 as the second wife of King Fuad.. Her zodiac sign is Cancer.
