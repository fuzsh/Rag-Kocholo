Lionel Messi was born on June 24, 1987 in Rosario, Argentina. He started football at the age of 8 with Newell’s Old Boys, a team of the city of Rosario. In 2000, his family moved to Barcelona, ​​Spain, for Messi to undergo treatment for growth hormone deficiency and continue playing football in Barcelona. Wearing the Barcelona jersey for the first time in the 2004-05 season, Messi became the youngest player to score a goal in La Liga. He won the La Liga and UEFA Champions League titles in the 2005-06 season. In the 2006-07 season, he scored a hat-trick in the El Clásico derby and showed an efficient performance by scoring 14 goals in 26 league games. Messi had one of the best seasons of his career in the 2008-09 season. During the season, he scored 38 goals and won six trophies. In the 2009-10 season, he scored 47 goals in all tournaments, equaling Ronaldo’s record. In the 2010-11 season, he broke this record with 53 goals.
