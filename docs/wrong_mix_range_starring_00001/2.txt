Love Actually (2003)

A romantic comedy directed by Richard Curtis, featuring Neeson as a man coping with the loss of his wife amidst interconnected stories during Christmas.

Taken (2008) and Taken 2 (2012)

Neeson stars in both films, portraying Bryan Mills, an ex-CIA officer pursuing human traffickers in the first film and facing repercussions in Istanbul in the sequel.

Cold Pursuit (2019)

A remake of a 2014 Norwegian film, Neeson plays a snowplow driver seeking revenge on the drug cartel responsible for his son's forced overdose.

Non-Stop (2014)

Neeson is U.S. Air Marshal Bill Marks in this mystery thriller set during a transatlantic flight, where he must prevent passenger deaths triggered by extortion.

The Ice Road (2021)

A Netflix original directed by Jonathan Hensleigh, it follows a team of truck drivers delivering cargo to workers in a diamond mine, with a sequel announced for 2023.

The Ballad of Buster Scruggs (2018)

Neeson features in the Coen brothers' anthology film, specifically in the segment "Meal Ticket," portraying an aging impresario facing dwindling audiences.

Chloe (2009)

A mystery thriller starring Neeson, Julianne Moore, and Amanda Seyfried, revolving around a gynecologist's attempt to test her husband's fidelity with unexpected consequences.

A Walk Among the Tombstones (2014)

Neeson plays troubled ex-cop Matthew Scudder in this neo-noir action thriller, hired to uncover the truth behind a drug kingpin's wife's murder.

A Monster Calls (2016)

A dark fantasy directed by J.A. Bayona, where Neeson voices a monster guiding a young boy through challenging circumstances involving illness and family dynamics.

The Next Three Days (2010)

A thriller and remake of a French film, Neeson appears as an ex-con aiding a man's desperate attempt to break his wife out of prison after she is wrongfully convicted.

FAQs:

Which Liam Neeson movie on Netflix is a remake of a 2014 Norwegian film?

"Cold Pursuit" (2019) is a remake of the 2014 Norwegian film "In Order of Disappearance," where Neeson plays a snowplow driver seeking revenge for his son's death.

What is the premise of "Non-Stop" (2014), a thriller starring Liam Neeson on Netflix?

In "Non-Stop," Neeson plays U.S. Air Marshal Bill Marks, facing extortion during a transatlantic flight, where he must prevent passenger deaths linked to a $150 million demand.
