James “Jamie” Martin was born to Brooke English while his father, Tad Martin, was presumed dead. Eventually, Tad returned to Pine Valley and he and Brooke married briefly. But they ultimately divorced while Jamie was still very young. Tad remarried Dixie Cooney, whose son JR was a stepbrother to Jamie for many years and whose ties to Jamie are just as strong as biological ones…
