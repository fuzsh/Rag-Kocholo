Zombie BBQ is a fun shooter for the Nintendo DS, in which you have to move forward by shooting down every living (or dead) creature in your path! Use the great arsenal of weapons at your disposal to do away with the savage hordes of zombies. Shoot, blast, massacre, bombard, and burn your enemies to the bone with a simple touch of your stylus on the console screen. Can you save fairy tale land, or will you let its creatures live unhappily ever after?
