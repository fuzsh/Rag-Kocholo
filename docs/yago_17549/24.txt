Donald and Colleen Healy of Houston have announced October wedding plans for their daughter, Kathleen Patricia Healy, and Dr. Woodson Charles Merrell, a son of Charles and June Merrell of Chatham, N.J.

Ms. Healy, 30 years old, is a senior writer at Allure magazine in New York. She graduated from Columbia University, where her fiance earned his medical degree. Dr. Merrell, 44, is an internist in New York and an instructor of clinical medicine at Columbia University. He is a cum laude graduate of Amherst College.
