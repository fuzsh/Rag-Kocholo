Kathleen Patricia Healy, a senior writer for Allure magazine, and Dr. Woodson Charles Merrell, an internist, both in New York, were married yesterday. The Rev. Gary Fertig, an Episcopal priest, performed the ceremony in St. Thomas Church in New York.

Mrs. Merrell graduated from Colgate University. She is the daughter of Mr. and Mrs. Donald R. Healy of Houston, where her father is the national marketing manager for Shell Oil.

Dr. Merrell is a clinical instructor of internal medicine at Columbia University, from which he received his medical degree. He graduated cum laude from Amherst College. He is a son of June W. Merrell of Chatham, N.J., and the late L. Charles Merrell. The bridegroom's father was the general manager of the treasury department of U.S. Steel, now the USX Corporation, in New York.
