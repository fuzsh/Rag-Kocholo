Ali Walker is Assistant News Editor at POLITICO, where he is responsible for editing content and anchoring the morning news cycle.

He previously worked on POLITICO’s production desk, as deputy production editor.

He got his start in journalism at Fios community newspaper on the Isle of Lewis – his home – in 2012, before going on to work for the Evening Express in Aberdeen, Scotland as deputy chief sub-editor.

When not in the office, Ali can usually be found riding his bike, playing football or inciting food-based riots on Twitter.
