Description

A weary lion rests beside a pool of water in the tall grasses. A verse from Psalms 23 says, Thou preparest a table before me in the presence of mine enemies. Another addition to the Tribe of Judah series, this one is focusing on the fact that the Lion of Judah will prepare a place of rest for us, even in the presence of our enemies.
