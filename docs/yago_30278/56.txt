Articles English: Eleanor of Alburquerque

Eleanor Sanchez Ivrea de Albuquerque, Countess regnant of Albuquerque, Queen consort of Aragorn, was born 1374 in Aldeadávila de la Ribera, Castile and León, Spain to Sancho de Albuquerque (1342-1374) and Beatriz of Portugal (1347-1381) and died 16 December 1435 Medina del Campo, Castile and León, Spain of unspecified causes. She married Fernando I of Aragon (1380-1416) 1393 JL .
