Origin

Zakarian : Armenian: patronymic from the personal name Zakar a short form of Zakaria (see Zacharias). This form of the surname is found mainly in the US. Compare Zakaryan.

Zakaryan : Armenian: variant of Zakarian found mainly in Armenia and Russia.
