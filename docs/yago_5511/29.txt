(CBS) Bryce Dallas Howard and her actor husband Seth Gabel welcomed their first daughter last week.

Bryce's filmmaker father, Ron Howard, announced the news Sunday on Twitter, writing, "Beatrice Jean Howard-Gabel Born Jan 19 2012 8lbs 6oz Bryce & Baby B are spectacular Daddy Seth & brother Theo are beaming ear to ear." He later added, "Beatrice's arrival is hugely exciting for our family Thanks for all the kind tweets folks."

The couple's son Theo is 4 years old.

Bryce, 30, married Gabel in June 2006 after dating for more than five years. The actress recently starred in "50/50" and "The Help."
