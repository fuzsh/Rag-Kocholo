Bad Peterstal-Griesbach

Explore one of the top hiking destinations in the black forest national park region.

Deep valleys, refreshing rivers and stunning views - Bad Peterstal-Griesbach is an idyllic town in the black forest nationalpark region. The town is also famous for its mineral water and family and hiking friendly offers.
