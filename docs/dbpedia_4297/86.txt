Bad Peterstal-Griesbach in the Black Forest

Information in brief

Location: in Northern Black Forest, 30km east of Offenburg

Autobahn A 5 exit at Appenweier (No. 54)

400-1000 m above sea level

2.750 inhabitants

Description

Bad Peterstal-Griesbach is a well-known spa in the upper Rench valley, which is one of the most beautiful side valleys of the river Rhine.

The place is famous for its mineral springs and the mild highland climate. People have been going to the mineral and mud spa for more than 400 years. Bad Peterstal-Griesbach is a fascinating mixture of life, culture, recreation, wellness and spa.
