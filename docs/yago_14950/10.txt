Murphy 771 is a 2006 Irish crime drama. Its based on real life events, a fraud situation involving identity. All Ben Murphy has to do is contact the number in a letter he received telling him that he'll win a lot of money. A man who says he's the banker at a financial Asian institution, tells the recipient of the letter, Mr. Murphy, that the winnings need to be "verified." But Mr. Murphy doesn't make the call. This film's director chose to chronicle his situation, along with other real life identity fraud events into a storyline. The movie is a learning experience for those who choose to watch it.
