Bruce Willis is an American actor known for his versatile career in film and television. Born on March 19, 1955, in Idar-Oberstein, West Germany, Willis rose to fame for his iconic role as John McClane in the “Die Hard” film series. With a career spanning several decades, he has appeared in numerous successful movies, including “Pulp Fiction,” “The Sixth Sense,” and “Armageddon.” Willis is celebrated for his action-hero roles and has received critical acclaim for his performances in various genres.
