Prime Minister of Belarus Sergei Sidorsky will go on a working trip to the Gomel oblast on December 10, BelTA learnt from the Council of Ministers Office. The Prime Minister is expected to visit Kombinat Vostok company where he will be made familiar with the storage and pre-sale preparation of fruits and vegetables before they are sold on the home and foreign markets. Sergei Sidorsky is expected to participate in the unveiling of a new building of the Gomel Oblast Court. A new chocolate products workshop of OAO Spartak is supposed to be opened. The head of government will be made familiar with Spartak products and will consider the implementation of the innovation project “Manufacturing of chocolate and chocolate products”. The Prime Minister will also participate in a session of Gomel oblast administration officials and heads of certain companies located in the oblast.
