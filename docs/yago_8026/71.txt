Gail O’Grady was born on January 23, 1963 in Detroit, Michigan, USA as Gail Ann O’Grady. She is an actress, known for American Dreams (2002), Deuce Bigalow: Male Gigolo (1999) and NYPD Blue (1993). She was previously married to John Stamatakis, Anthony J Pellegrino, Steve Fenton, Severin Wunderman, Jeffrey Byron and Richard Dasko.
