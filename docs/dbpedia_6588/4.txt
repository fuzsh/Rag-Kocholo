Town without pity: Four American GI's, stationed in Germany circa 1960, are accused of attacking a local teenage girl. An Army major is assigned to defend the soldiers. If he's going to prevent their death sentences, he will have to turn the spotlight on the victim who is already traumatized.

Inherit the wind: The classic American drama inspired by the famous Scapes "monkey trial" in which a teacher was prosecuted for teaching Darwin's theory of evolution.
