TiVo Corp (TiVo), a subsidiary of Adeia Inc, is a provider of media and digital entertainment technology products and solutions. The company's offerings include TiVo Stream 4K, TiVo EDGE, TiVo Mini LUX and accessories among others. It provides services to cable, satellite, TV service providers, streaming and digital media portals, studios, broadcasters and networks, music ecosystems, and telecommunications pay TV service providers. TiVo is headquartered in San Jose, California, the US.
