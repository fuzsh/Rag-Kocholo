What does Asya mean?

Asya as a girls' name is of Hebrew and Greek derivation, and the name Asya means "He (God) has favored me; sunrise". Asya is an alternate form of Anne (Hebrew): the English variation of Hannah. Asya is also a variant of Asia (Greek).

How popular is Asya?

Asya is an uncommonly occurring first name for women. Asya is also an uncommon last name for both adults and children. (2000 U.S. DEMOGRAPHICS)
