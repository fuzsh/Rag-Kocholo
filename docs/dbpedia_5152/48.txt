John Marvin Buck, 86 of Douglassville, PA died on Sunday, January 10, 2021, at home.

Born September 20, 1934 he was the son of the late Carl Buck and Victoria (Steiner) Buck.

He was the husband of Ellen J. (Stout) Buck.

He retired in 1996 from Superfresh.

John’s interest where golf, hunting and the Philadelphia Eagles.

He was a member of St. Paul’s Lutheran Church, Douglassville, PA

Surviving along with his wife are sons, David J. husband of Debra Buck of West Chester, PA and Gary A. husband of Teresa Buck of Douglassville, PA, daughter, Laurie E. wife of David Johns of Sagamore, PA, brother, James D. husband of Sandra Buck of Berwick, PA, sister, Sharon widow of Larry Ridall of Wyomissing, PA; 9 grandchildren; 17 great grandchildren.

He was predeceased by brother, Carl Z. Buck.

Services will be held at the convenience of the family.
