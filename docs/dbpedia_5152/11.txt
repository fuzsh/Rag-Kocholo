Biography

Born Joseph was born 29 May 1724 in Norwalk, New London County, Connecticut Colony. [1][2][3]

Marriage He married Catherine/Katherine St. John in 1747. [4]

Died He passed away 28 April 1777 in Sharon, Litchfield County, Connecticut, and is buried there in Hillside Cemetery. Tombstone inscription: Inscription: Died of the small pox in his 53d yr [5][6][7][8]
