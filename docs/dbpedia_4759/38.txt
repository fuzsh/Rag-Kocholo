Kure, Japan, 1950-08-15. Wearing a white hat, the Prime Minister, the Honourable Robert G. Menzies, inspects troops of BCOF Support Company, the 3rd Battalion, The Royal Australian Regiment (3RAR), who are lined up standing at attention on parade. The soldiers accompanying Menzies on the inspection are, left to right: 2/37507 Major Ian Bruce Ferguson, the Officer Commanding Support Company; 3/169 Lieutenant Colonel Floyd Stanley Walsh, Commanding Officer of 3RAR; Lieutenant General H.C.H. ('Red Robbie') Robertson, Commander-in-Chief of BCOF. The soldier standing in the front row at extreme right is 2/45222 Corporal W.R. Ward. The Prime Minister was carrying out the inspection as part an official visit to Australian units serving with BCOF in the Kure area.
