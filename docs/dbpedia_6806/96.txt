Dadasaheb Phalke Award Winners

Dada Saheb Phalke Award Winners: The Dadasaheb Phalke Award is India's highest commendation award given in the field of cinema. This award is presented annually at the National Film Awards by the organisation set up by the Ministry of Information and Broadcasting which is the Directorate of Film. The beneficiary of this award is selected by the committee comprising eminent personalities from the Indian film industry. Dada saheb Phalke is honoured for the "Outstanding contribution to the growth and development of Indian cinema".
