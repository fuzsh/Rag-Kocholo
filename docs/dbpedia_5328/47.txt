Marc Allen is an internationally renowned seminar leader, entrepreneur, author, and composer.

With little business acumen, Allen cofounded New World Library in 1977, and has guided the company, as president and publisher, from a small start-up to its current position as one of the leading independent publishers in the country.

Allen is author of The Magical Path, The Greatest Secret of All, The Millionaire Course, and Visionary Business.

Also a well-known musician and composer, Allen released his first instrumental album, Breathe, in 1978, which has sold nearly 100,000 copies. He subsequnetly released Petals, Quiet Moments, and Solo Flight. His latest release is an orchestral work titled Awakening.

Active in philanthropy as well, Allen, with his wife Aurilene, cofounded the Brazil Hope Foundation to help street kids in Brazil.
