Ludwig III of Bavaria (Ludwig Luitpold Josef Maria Aloys Alfried; 7 January 1845 – 18 October 1921) was the last King of Bavaria, reigning from 1913 to 1918.

Marriage[edit]

On 20 February 1868, at Saint Augustine's Church in Vienna, he married Archduchess Maria Theresa of Austria-Este.

Issue[edit]

Rupprecht, Crown Prince of Bavaria (18 May 1869 – 2 August 1955) married (1) Duchess Marie Gabriele in Bavaria and had issue. Married (2) Princess Antonia of Luxembourg and had issue.

Princess Adelgunde of Bavaria (17 October 1870 – 4 January 1958), married Prince Wilhelm of Hohenzollern-Sigmaringen.

Princess Maria of Bavaria (6 July 1872 – 10 June 1954) married Prince Ferdinand Pius, Duke of Calabria and had issue.

Prince Karl of Bavaria (1 April 1874 – 9 May 1927) died unmarried.

Prince Franz of Bavaria (10 October 1875 – 25 January 1957) married Princess Isabella Antonie of Croÿ and had issue.

Princess Mathilde of Bavaria (17 August 1877 – 6 August 1906), married Prince Ludwig of Saxe-Coburg and Gotha and had issue.

Prince Wolfgang Maria Leopold of Bavaria (2 July 1879 – 31 January 1895) died unmarried.

Princess Hildegarde of Bavaria (5 March 1881 – 2 February 1948) died unmarried.

Princess Notburga of Bavaria (19 March 1883 – 24 March 1883) died in infancy.

Princess Wiltrud of Bavaria (10 November 1884 – 28 March 1975), married Wilhelm, Duke of Urach.

Princess Helmtrud of Bavaria (22 March 1886 – 23 June 1977).

Princess Dietlinde of Bavaria (2 January 1888 – 14 February 1889) died in infancy.

Princess Gundelinde of Bavaria (26 August 1891 – 16 August 1983), married Johann Georg, Count of Preysing-Lichtenegg-Moos.

Titles and styles[edit]

7 January 1845 – 5 November 1913 His Royal Highness Prince Ludwig of Bavaria.

5 November 1913 – 13 November 1918 His Royal Highness The King.
