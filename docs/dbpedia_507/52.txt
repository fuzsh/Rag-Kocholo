The city of the kings was the first capital of Hungary.

Its name is Alba Regia in Latin, but the town is not a Rome one, it was founded

by Géza Hungarian prince.

Székesfehérvár was the place where Hungarian kings were crowned and bured.

Nowadays it is a nice town between Budapest and Balaton lake.

Székesfehérvár is plenty of festivals particulary in August.

You can enjoy the international folk dance festival,Musica Regia,Golden Bull jazz,folk and light music art days and Dragon Boat Festival.
