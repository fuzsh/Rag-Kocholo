Sinopharm Jiangsu Co., Ltd is a core subsidiary of GHW International. It was established in 1998 and jointly established by the Jiangsu Provincial Medicine Administration, China Pharmaceutical Foreign Trade Corporation and Nanjing Hanhe Industrial Co., Ltd. It is an international pharmaceutical company with both import and export and pharmaceutical permits.

The company is committed to the integration of resources in the pharmaceutical industry, providing high-quality products and professional services. After more than 20 years of development, the company has a global procurement and sales service system.
