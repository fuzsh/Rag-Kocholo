Pressmart, a Hyderabad-based e-edition and new media delivery services provider, has entered into a strategic alliance with Summit, an editorial, advertising and digital asset management solution company, to offer a new editorial-linked media delivery platform to their publishing customers. Under the terms of the agreement, Pressmart will integrate its 'eEdition' solution and associated new media delivery services with Summit's 'Editorial Workflow' solution. Additionally, Pressmart will offer its eEdition and mobile newspaper solutions to large-sized and local community English and vernacular publications, Pressmart stated in a press release on Wednesday.
