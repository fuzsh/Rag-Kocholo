3/3/72-HOUSTON: Yoko Ono Lennon(R), wife of former Beatle John Lennon(L), received temporary custody 3/3 of her 8-year-old daughter by a previous marriage, but the little girl is missing. The Lennons enter a custody hearing at a Domestic Relations Court in Houston. The whereabouts of the child, Kyoko, and her father, Anthony D. Cox, have not been known since 12/71 when Cox was released from county jail after refusing to allow Mrs. Lennon to visit the child. (UPI)
