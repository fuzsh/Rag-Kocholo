Election for the constituency of Mid Sussex on 7 May 2015

An election to the 56th Parliament of the United Kingdom for the constituency area of Mid Sussex, held as part of the general election on 7 May 2015.

Conservative hold by Nicholas Soames with a majority of 24,286 - 42.2% - on a turnout of 70.9%.

An electorate of 81,034, having a valid vote count of 57,492 and an invalid vote count of 252.

Result declared at 07:12 on 8 May 2015. Declarations times do not form part of election declarations and should be regarded as indicative rather than official.
