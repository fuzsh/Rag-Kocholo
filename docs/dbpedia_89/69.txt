On Thursday at 12:34 p.m. the National Weather Service issued an updated lake effect snow warning in effect until 7 p.m. for Onondaga and Madison counties.

The weather service states, "Heavy lake effect snow. Additional snow accumulations of up to two inches. Winds gusting as high as 30 mph."

"Plan on slippery road conditions. The hazardous conditions could impact the evening commute," comments the weather service. "If you must travel, keep an extra flashlight, food, and water in your vehicle in case of an emergency. The latest road conditions for the state you are calling from can be obtained by calling 5 1 1."
