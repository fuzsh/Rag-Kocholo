Netviewer GmbH is a leading manufacturer and provider of web conferencing solutions for collaborative work. The company, based in Karlsruhe, Germany, offers a range of products that enable users to view and edit documents jointly over the internet. Netviewer’s innovative products are transforming the way people collaborate and work together, making it easier and more efficient than ever before.

Netviewer Meet: The Power of Online Meetings

Netviewer Meet is a powerful web conferencing solution that allows users to hold meetings over the internet. With Netviewer Meet, users can share documents, presentations, and screens with others in real-time. The solution is perfect for project collaborations, live demos, and training. With its simple and user-friendly interface, Netviewer Meet makes it easy for anyone to participate in online meetings.

Netviewer Support: Helping Customers Anywhere, Anytime

Netviewer Support is a web-based solution that enables companies to provide remote support to their customers anywhere and anytime. With Netviewer Support, companies can connect to a customer’s computer, view the customer’s screen, and fix issues remotely. This solution is ideal for companies with a large customer base that requires support services. Netviewer Support improves customer satisfaction and reduces support costs, making it a must-have for any company.

Netviewer Present: Engage Audiences with Online Meetings

Netviewer Present is an online meeting solution that allows users to engage audiences with their presentations. With Netviewer Present, users can hold online education, training, and informational events. The solution is perfect for webinars to promote sales and customer loyalty, and conventions over the internet. Netviewer Present provides users with the tools they need to deliver engaging and interactive presentations to their audiences.
