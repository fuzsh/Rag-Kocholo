Description

The Technical University of Ostrava (VSB) is a public university located in Ostrava, Czech Republic. It is the largest university in the Moravian-Silesian Region and is known for its focus on engineering, economics, information technology, and mining.

The university was founded in 1849 as the Mining Academy and has since grown to include seven faculties:

Faculty of Mining and Geology

Faculty of Metallurgy and Materials Engineering

Faculty of Mechanical Engineering

Faculty of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science

Faculty of Civil Engineering

Faculty of Economics

Faculty of Information Technology

Here are some of the benefits of studying at the Technical University of Ostrava:

High-quality education: VSB is a well-respected university that offers a strong education in a variety of fields.

Affordable tuition fees: Tuition fees at VSB are very affordable compared to universities in Western Europe and North America.

English-language programs: VSB offers a number of bachelor’s and master’s programs taught entirely in English.

International student support: VSB provides a variety of support services for international students, including help with visas, accommodation, and finding a job.

Great location: Ostrava is a vibrant city with a lot to offer students.

Student Life

The Campus of VSB-TUO houses all kinds of facilities for students, and employees, but also for the general public. Our campus can be a second home for you. The campus of the VSB-the Technical University of Ostrava is one of the largest in Central Europe. Apart from accommodation, it also offers catering services, sports, and cultural facilities, and a number of other services. On this campus, you can also find a post office, student shops, and a free wifi connection for all students. There is also a gallery, library, and PC pavilion within the campus. Students can use the services of a local doctor, a psychologist, or a career consultant. Not only lifelong learning education is possible at VSB-TUO. Diverse sports of sports activities are also offered for international students at the University. VSB-TUO is one of the best-equipped universities in the Czech Republic that offers all foreign sports activities on its campus. The positive approach of the school management allows students to use all sports facilities for compulsory and non-compulsory education, and it provides the Department of Physical Education and Sports (KTVS) with excellent conditions to fulfill its primary aim: to make physical education and sports an integral part of the lifestyle of VSB–TUO students and graduates.

Housing

There are double and triple rooms in the student residences that range from 75-100 euros per month.
