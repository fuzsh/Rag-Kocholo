Ian Mahinmi has announced his retirement from professional basketball at the age of 34 after dealing with various physical problems in recent seasons, announced the official French portal of the NBA.

The center from Rouen (France) came to the competition through the San Antonio Spurs in the 2007-08 season, having previously been selected in 25th place in the 2005 Draft after training in the ranks of Pau Orthez.

After his time in San Antonio he played for the Dallas Mavericks, Indiana Pacers, and ultimately the Washington Wizards where he barely had continuity due to injuries.

NBA champion in 2011 with the aforementioned Mavs, he has career averages of 5,2 points and 4,4 rebounds in 618 official games.
