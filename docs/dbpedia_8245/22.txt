The U.S. District Court for the District of Oregon is a federal court that serves the entire state of Oregon. With courthouses located in Portland, Eugene, Medford, and Pendleton, the District provides legal services and resources to the local community. The Court handles a wide range of cases and is committed to upholding justice and the rule of law.

Recently, Judge Amy Baggio was confirmed as a U.S. District Judge for the District of Oregon, joining the Court in the Portland Division. With a background in criminal defense law, Judge Baggio brings valuable experience and expertise to the bench. The Court also hosts the Ninth Circuit Civics Contest, an annual essay and video contest for high school students, promoting civic education and engagement.
