Petroglyph Games, Inc. is an American video game developer based in Las Vegas, Nevada. It was founded on 1 April 2003 by several former Westwood Studios employees. By 2012, approximately 20% of the Petroglyph Games staff were former Westwood developers.[2]

Petroglyph Games is the development studio behind the 8-Bit series of games and Forged Battalion.
