Robert C. Ford, aka Wall $treet Poet, is a poet and spoken word artist. His poetry has been featured in short films by the Mexican director Octavio Maya Rocha, including Ascending Volumes, inspired by a painting by Mark Kostabi (Guns N' Roses/Use Your Illusion, Ramones/Adios Amigos), and The Call-NYC, featuring a visual artist, a musician and a poet, who attempt to bring light to the darkness of the COVID-19 pandemic through their art.
