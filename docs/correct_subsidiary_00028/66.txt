Manhattan Associates Inc (Manhattan Associates) provides software solutions. The company develops, deploys, services, and maintains software solutions that manage supply chain, inventory, and omni-channel operations. Its solution offerings include software, services, and hardware. These solutions help data exchange, coordinate the actions and communication of participants in supply chain ecosystems, such as manufacturers, distributors, suppliers, transportation providers, trading partners, channels including catalogers, store retailers, and web outlets, and consumers. It also offers omni-channel solutions, inventory solutions and supply chain management solutions for streamlining warehouse management and control procurement to delivery process. Manhattan Associates offers its solutions to manufacturers, retailer, wholesalers, logistics providers and other industries including grocery, food and beverage, medical and pharmaceutical, and trucking. It has business presence in the Americas, Europe, the Middle East and Africa, and Asia-Pacific. Manhattan Associates is headquartered in Atlanta, Georgia, the US.
