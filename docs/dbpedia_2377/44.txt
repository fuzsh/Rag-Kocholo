It is a large centre with more than 200 interactive exhibits relating to science and technology. "The Questacon" opened in 1988 at Ainslie Primary School. Questacon was the first interactive science center in Australia. The center is split up into seven galleries on different floors, accessed by a spiral ramp around the 'drum'. Questacon's shop offers a range of educational science toys, books and teacher's resources. Questacon's cafe is situated in the foyer of the building.
