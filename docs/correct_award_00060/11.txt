Rudyard Kipling is youngest to get Nobel Prize in Literature

short by Sanira Mediratta / 02:06 pm on

English writer Rudyard Kipling, who was 41 years old when he was awarded the Nobel Prize in Literature in 1907 is till date, the youngest winner of the prize. Best known for 'The Jungle Book', he was awarded the prize "in consideration of the power of observation, originality of imagination, virility of ideas and remarkable talent for narration."
