Mishti Chakraborty Bio

Mishti Chakraborty, whose birth name is Indrani Chakraborty, is a popular Indian actress and model hailing from Kolkata, West Bengal. She was born on December 20, 1987. Mishti is well-known for her work in the South Indian film industry, specifically in Telugu and Tamil cinema. Mishti made her acting debut with the Telugu film "Chinnadana Nee Kosam" in 2014 opposite Nithiin. Although the film didn't fare exceptionally well at the box office, Mishti's performance received rave reviews. She was praised for her natural beauty, expressive eyes, and graceful screen presence, earning her the nickname "Mishti Chakraborty - The Beauty Queen." Following her debut, Mishti went on to star in several successful Telugu and Tamil films, including "Columbus" (2015), "Sarabha" (2016), and "Babu Baga Busy" (2017). Her versatile acting skills and ability to portray various characters with ease quickly gained attention in the industry. In addition to her work in the South Indian film industry, Mishti has also appeared in Bollywood movies. She made her Bollywood debut in 2015 with the film "Kaanchi: The Unbreakable" directed by Subhash Ghai. Although the film didn't receive much critical acclaim, Mishti's performance was widely appreciated, solidifying her position as a talented actress. With her captivating looks and impressive acting skills, Mishti Chakraborty has carved a niche for herself in the Indian film industry. She continues to receive praise for her performances and remains a sought-after actress in both the Telugu and Tamil film industries.
