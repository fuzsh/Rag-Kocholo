Mishti (born as Mishti itself, but named in school as "Indrani Chakravarty" from West Bengal, India) is an Indian film actress. She made her Bollywood debut with Subhash Ghai's film Kaanchi: The Unbreakable, Tollywood debut with Nithiin-A. Karunakaran film Chinnadana Nee Kosam. and her Mollywood debut with Jinu Abraham's film Adam Joan
