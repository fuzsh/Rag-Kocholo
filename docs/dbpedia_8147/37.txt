Indrani Chakraborty, better known by her stage name, Mishti, is an Indian actress who predominantly appears in Telugu and Hindi films. Mishti was born on December 20, 1987, in Kolkata. Her mother is a housewife and her father is a construction businessman.

Mishti made her Bollywood debut with the 2014 film, Kaanchi: The Unbreakable. Her Telugu film debut was Chinnadana Nee Kosam (2014). It was around this time that she began to use the name Mishti professionally. Her Malayalam film debut came with the 2017 film Adam Joan.

The second Telugu film Misthi acted in was Columbus opposite Sumanth Ashwin. Mishti returned to Bollywood with Indra Kumar's Great Grand Masti in 2016, which is the third film in the Masti film series.

In 2017, Mishti appeared in the historical film Begum Jaan, alongside Naseeruddin Shah and Vidya Balan. Mishti has appeared in approximately 13 movies since her debut in 2014
