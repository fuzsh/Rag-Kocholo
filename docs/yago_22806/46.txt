Unbound. Slip of paper autographed by Cliff Robertson and Dina Merrill Robertson. The Oscar-winning actor Robertson and actress Merrill, daughter of financier E.F. Hutton and current co-owner of RKO Pictures, were married from 1966 to 1986. Both had long and distinguished careers, and early in their marriage they appeared together as villains on several episodes of TV's camp classic, *Batman*. Approximately 5" x 8". Fine.
