Maria Josepha of Austria

Maria Josepha of Austria (Maria Josepha Benedikta Antonia Theresia Xaveria Philippine, Polish: Maria Józefa; 8 December 1699 – 17 November 1757) was the Queen of Poland and Electress of Saxony by marriage to Augustus III. From 1711 to 1717, she was heiress presumptive to the Habsburg monarchy . Read more on Wikipedia

Maria Josepha of Austria was the wife of Joseph I, Holy Roman Emperor. She was the daughter of Emperor Charles VI and Elisabeth Christine of Brunswick-Wolfenbüttel. She was a member of the House of Habsburg.
