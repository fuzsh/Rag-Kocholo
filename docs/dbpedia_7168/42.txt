Julie Andrews, Blake Edwards (subjects)

N.p. N.p., Circa 1960s. Vintage borderless photograph of actress Julie Andrews and her husband, director Blake Edwards, on the set of "Darling Lili" in 1970.

Edwards and Andrews were married for over four decades, from 1969 until Edwards' death in 2010.

7.75 x 9.25 inches. Near Fine.
