Kyle Elliot Korver (born March 17, 1981) is an American former professional basketball player, who played 17 years in the National Basketball Association. He played as a shooting guard/small forward for multiple teams including the Atlanta Hawks, Utah Jazz, Chicago Bulls, and Atlanta Hawks, where he earned his one and only All-star appearance
