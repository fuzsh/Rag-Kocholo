Woody Harrelson (born July 23, 1961) is an American actor. He is well known for his roles as Mickey Knox in Natural Born Killers, as Larry Flynt in The People vs Larry Flynt, as Carson Wells in No Country for Old Men, as Haymitch Abernathy in The Hunger Games film series, among others. He also had regular television roles as Woody Boyd on Cheers and as Marty Hart on True Detective. He portrayed Charlie Frost in the film 2012.
