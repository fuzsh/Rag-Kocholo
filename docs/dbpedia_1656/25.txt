Known for the elegance and breadth of her playing, Amy Bach performs as a soloist and collaborative pianist in Salt Lake City, Utah.

Her repertoire ranges from Granados and Prokofiev to Boulanger, Psathas, and Dohnanyi. A frequent collaborator for instrumentalists, Amy has accompanied vibes, tuba, and clarinet as well as all string instruments. She has played locally in the US at the Bountiful/Davis Arts Center and abroad in Salles, France at La Balie Candlelight Concerts and in Kent, England in Finchcocks Hall.
