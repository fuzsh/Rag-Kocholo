Charlton Heston was born on 4 October 1923, in Evanston, Cook, Illinois, United States. He married Lydia Marie Clarke on 17 March 1944. He lived in Wilmette, Cook, Illinois, United States in 1940 and New York City, New York, United States in 1950. He registered for military service in 1941. In 1941, his occupation is listed as american actor and political activist. He died on 5 April 2008, in Beverly Hills, Los Angeles, California, United States, at the age of 84, and was buried in Saint Matthew's Episcopal Church Columbarium, Pacific Palisades, Los Angeles, Los Angeles, California, United States.

The Presidential Succession Act is an act establishing the presidential line of succession. This was a precursor for the Twenty-fifth Amendment which outlines what is to happen when a President is killed, dies, or is unable to fulfill the responsibilities of President.
