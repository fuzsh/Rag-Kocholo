Maya the Honey Bee was a German anime that aired on the Nick Jr. morning block. It was based on the classic children's book The Adventures of Maya the Honey Bee by Waldemar Bonsels. The dubbed version for America aired on Nick Jr. between January 1, 1990 and December 31, 1992. It is unknown how many episodes aired on Nick Jr.

Plot[]

The story centers on Maya, an inquisitive, adventurous, and somewhat flighty young honey bee, and her adventures in the forest around her. However, the hive Maya is raised in is divided in two colonies, and when she commits the unforgivable crime of leaving the hive to explore the wide world, she is exiled. And so, Maya befriends other insects and braves dangers with them.
