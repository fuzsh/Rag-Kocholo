Timbs Genealogy

OriginsThe surname 'Timbs' is of English origin and is an occupational name derived from the Middle English word 'timber,' meaning a worker or seller of timber.

Geographic DistributionThe surname 'Timbs' is most commonly found in the United Kingdom, particularly in England. It is also found in smaller numbers in countries with British colonial and immigrant history, such as Australia and Canada.

VariationsVariations of the surname 'Timbs' include 'Tims,' 'Tibbs,' 'Timms,' 'Timbes,' 'Timbrell,' 'Timbsby,' 'Timball,' 'Timby,' and 'Timbrell.'
