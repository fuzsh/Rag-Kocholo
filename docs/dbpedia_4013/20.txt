Linda Susan Berry, 68 of Cave City (formerly of Evening Shade) died Friday, August 3, 2018. She was born July 4, 1950 at Houston, Texas to Wesley B. and Alice Melba Pulley. She was a member of Cave City Church of Christ.

She was preceded in death by her parents; husband, Charles Berry; brother, Craig Pulley.

Survivors include daughter, Jennifer Berry of Evening Shade; stepdaughter, Trina Taggart of Santa Fe, Texas; sister, Sherry Swain of Waco, Texas; and host of other relatives and friends.

Memorial services will be at a later date. Arrangements by Qualls Funeral Home of Cave City.
