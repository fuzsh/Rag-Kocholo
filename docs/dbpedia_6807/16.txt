Movie Moghul Dr D Ramanaidu was chosen for this year’s Padma Bhushan award. He received India’s third highest civilian honor, Padma Bhushan, from President Pranab Mukherjee during a ceremony at Rashtrapati Bhawan’s Darbar Hall in Delhi today. His grandson Rana Daggubati says that he is thrilled and excited that his grandpa is now called ‘Padma Bhushan Dr D Ramanaidu’. The actor took a flight straight to Mumbai for a day shoot after attending the award function in Delhi.

On the other hand, Athiloka Sundari’ Sridevi also receives national honor with India’s fourth highest civilian award Padma Sri. Ragalahari team congratulates Dr D Rama Naidu and Sridevi on receiving the prestigious awards.
