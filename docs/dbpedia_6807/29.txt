Legendary producer and Movie Moghul DR D Ramanaidu has been confirmed for the prestigious Padma Bhushan awards, says our sources. Though an official statement is yet to released, many film personalities have started congratulating the star producer on micro blogging websites. Ramanaidu has produced more than 130 film across many languages and made his way into Guinness Book of Records. In 2010, he was conferred with the Dadasaheb Phalke Award, India's highest award in cinema. Join your hands with us to congratulate DR D Ramanaidu garu for his achievement.
