WSLR-LP Station Manager

WSLR, INC

Sarasota , FL

Application Deadline: Friday, Apr 01, 2022

Description

The Station Manager is the essential coordinator of the operation and sound of WSLR, managing programming that entertains and informs our audience and brings in new listeners. The Station Manager will develop strategies and plans for the radio service and also work with other staff and volunteers to support the WSLR, Inc. mission and achieve success throughout the organization. The Station Manager will report to the WSLR+Fogartyville General Manager and will be the staff representative on the Radio Program Committee. The work requires the Station Manager to be on call and occasionally work nights, weekends and holidays. SFor complete details, visit: https://wslr.org/employment-opportunities-2/
