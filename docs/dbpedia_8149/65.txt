Mishti (born Indrani Chakraborty in West Bengal, India) is an Indian film actress. She made her Bollywood debut with Subhash Ghai's film Kaanchi: The Unbreakable and her Tollywood debut with Nithiin- A. Karunakaran film Chinnadana Nee Kosam.

Mishti's next Telugu film, Columbus, stars Sumanth Ashwin and is produced by MS Raju. She is returning to Bollywood with Indra Kumar's Great Grand Masti, the third film in Masti (film series).
