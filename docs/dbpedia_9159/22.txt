Mootaz Chehade Jounaidi (born on September, 1986) is a Lebanese professional football player who last played for Malaysian club FELDA United in the Malaysia Super League. He signed for Al-Ansar in 2001 as a youth player, he later grew from the academy and is now a squad player. He made his debut for Al-Ansar on 2/6/2001 in a 2-0 win against Safa Beirut SC.

Chehade has also represented Lebanon in international competition, appearing in qualifying matches for the 2010 and 2014 FIFA World Cup World Cups.
