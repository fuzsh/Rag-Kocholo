About São Tomé & Príncipe Dobra and Euro

STN is the currency code for São Tomé & Príncipe Dobra, which is the official currency of Sao Tome and Principe. The symbol for São Tomé & Príncipe Dobra is Db.

EUR is the currency code for Euro, which is the official currency of Andorra, Austria, Belgium, Croatia, Cyprus, Estonia, Finland, France, French Guiana, French Southern Territories, Germany, Greece, Guadeloupe, Ireland, Italy, Kosovo, Latvia, Lithuania, Luxembourg, Malta, Martinique, Mayotte, Monaco, Montenegro, Netherlands, Portugal, Réunion, San Marino, Slovakia, Slovenia, Spain, St. Barthélemy, St. Martin, St. Pierre & Miquelon, Vatican City and Åland Islands. The symbol for Euro is €.

How to use the currency converter

To change currency, press the flag or currency symbol.

You can also press $ or € on your keyboard, or start typing a currency name, currency code or country name to change currency.

You can even edit the URL directly. To convert São Tomé & Príncipe Dobra to US Dollar, type currency.world/convert/STN/USD.

If you type just one currency code into the URL, the target currency will be the currency of the country that the user is located in. Type currency.world/convert/STN_400 to convert 400 São Tomé & Príncipe Dobra to the user's currency.

This currency converter can convert between several currencies simultaneously. Press + to add a currency.
