The São Tomé and Príncipe dobra was established in 1977 as an official national currency and is used only in Sao Tome and Principe. It replaced the previously valid escudo. 1 Dobra is devided into 100 Centimes.

Since 2010, the São Tomé and Príncipe dobra is tied to the euro with a fixed exchange rate. Therefore, 1 dobra always equals to 0.0408 euro.
