In Sao Tome and Principe the following currency is used:

The São Tomé and Príncipe dobra (STD): this currency is devided in 100 Cêntimo. You can recognise the currency by the symbol Db.

You probably need to change money when traveling to Sao Tome and Principe. You might want to consider not to bring to much money. A credit card usualy is the easiest way to pay abroad. Withdrawing money at a local ATM is an other option. We strongly advise you to check the possibilities and international fees and charges of your bank or credit card company.
