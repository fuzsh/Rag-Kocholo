Manish Gupta is a film director and writer in the Indian film industry.

Manish has directed the movies One Friday Night (2023), 420 IPC (2021), Rahasya (2015), Hostel (2011), The Stoneman Murders (2009) and Darna Zaroori Hai (2006).

Manish has written the screenplay and dialogues for the movies Sarkar (2005) and Section 375 (2019).

Manish has mastered the craft of directing suspense films, he has developed his own unique genre wherein he takes a real-life case and weaves it into an intriguing suspense film.
