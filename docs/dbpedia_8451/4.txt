Zarangush (Persian: زرانگوش‎‎, also Romanized as Zarāngūsh and Zarangūsh) is a village in Hendmini Rural District, Badreh District, Darreh Shahr County, Ilam Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 1,605, in 307 families.
