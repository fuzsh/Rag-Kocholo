A topographic map titled 'Kroonstad' covering the area around the town of Kroonstad, Free State province, South Africa. Scale 1:2.347 english miles, temporary edition, Survey-General Office, Bloemfontein, 1901. Shows roads, rivers, hills, railways, farm boundaries and fences, divisional boundaries, populated places, cultivated land, and trees. Also includes an index to adjoining sheets. Printed on reverse is 'Kroonstad; Tempy Edition'.
