Bio:

Arnold Schwarzenegger (born Arnold Alois Schwarzenegger in Thal, Austria on July 30 1947) is an Austrian and American former professional bodybuilder, actor, producer, director, businessman, investor, and politician. He served two terms as the 38th Governor of California from 2003 until 2011. A former Mr. Universe and Mr. Olympia winner, Arnold made the move from bodybuilding to acting, and has starred in many action films, including The Terminator. He was sworn in on November 17, 2003, as Governor.
