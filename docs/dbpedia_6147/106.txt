Sentrigo is a leader in database security, provider of the Hedgehog line of products. Hedgehog is a real-time database activity monitoring (DAM), auditing and breach prevention solution that monitors all database activity for both external and insider threats, providing: - Real-time alerting and reporting of policy breaches - Session termination and quarantining of suspicious users - Virtual patching for DBMS vulnerabilities - End-user accountability for auditing purposes Sentrigo also offers two solutions for vulnerability assessment of databases, including Hedgehog DBscanner for enterprise deployments, and Repscan for auditors, consultants and pen testers. Sentrigo customers span all continents, and include universities, telecommunications operators, life sciences (pharmaceutical, biotech, and medical instruments), healthcare, and financial services, among others. Our products help these enterprises meet a broad range of compliance regulations, including PCI DSS, Sarbanes-Oxley, HIPAA, and more. Sentrigo was recently named a Red Herring 100 award winner for North America, winner of the Cloud Computing World Series Award for 'Best Security Solution', and received the 2010 Global Product Excellence Award for Database Security from Info Security Products Guide.
