Biography

Samuel Bass is Notable.

Samuel Bass A 19th-century American Old West train robber and outlaw.

Born July 21, 1851 Mitchell, Indiana, U.S.

Died July 21, 1878 (aged 27) Round Rock, Texas, U.S.

Occupation Armed robbery

He was the son of Daniel Bass and Elizabeth Sheeks.[1] He passed away in 1878. [2][3]

Sam Bass became a well known outlaw after he robbed the Union Pacific Gold Train in Nebraska. He was eventually hunted, double-crossed, and shot down in an ambush. [4]

A gun once owned or used by Sam Bass is in the Schwend Gun Collection[5]
