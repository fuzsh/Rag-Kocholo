Chandidas Mahavidyalaya, Birbhum West Bengal is established in the year 1972 as Government Educational Institute to provide higher education to build a career in the field of Humanities, Science and Commerce.

Chandidas Mahavidyalaya is having an Affiliation with reputed NAAC-C.

Management of Chandidas Mahavidyalaya, Birbhum is working under dr. Nursadh Ali (Principal).

Chandidas Mahavidyalaya, Khujutipara was established in 1972 named after the medieval Vaisnava poet of Humanism Chandidas.

The college is affiliated with the University of Burdwan and accredited with a ‘C’ Grade by NAAC in 2011.

It is located at the village Khujutipara under Nanoor Police Station, Birbhum district of West Bengal.

The college is aided by the state government which has been running with the motto of imparting higher education to the rural youth.

The mission of the college is to ensure value-based sustainable and quality education with a view to build self-confidence among the students and inject energy and vigor to help them to learn, grow and evolve.
