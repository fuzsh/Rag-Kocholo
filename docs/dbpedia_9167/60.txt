Biography

Adam Schlesinger is Notable.

Adam Lyons Schlesinger was an American singer-songwriter, record producer, bassist, guitarist, keyboardist and drummer. He was a founding member of the bands Fountains of Wayne, Ivy, and Tinted Windows.

Schlesinger won three Emmys[1][2] (2012, 2013, 2019), was nominated for an Oscar[1][2] for Best Original Song (1997), won a Grammy[1][2] in 2010, and was nominated for a Tony[1] award (2008) and for a Golden Globe[1][2] (1997).

Grandson of noted musician/producer Murray Bernthal.
