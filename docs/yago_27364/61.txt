Nancy Reagan (6 July 1921 — 6 March 2016) was a Human woman who lived on Earth during the 20th and 21st centuries. She was the wife of Ronald Reagan, the 40th President of the United States from 1981 to 1989. She had previously been a Hollywood film and television actress.

Biography[]

In December 1986, Reagan accompanied her husband to the diplomatic summit at the Hofoi House in Reykjavik, Iceland. While there, she and Raisa Gorbachova were entertained by the President of Iceland, Vigdís Finnbogadóttir while their husbands, President Reagan and Soviet General Secretary Mikhail Gorbachev.
