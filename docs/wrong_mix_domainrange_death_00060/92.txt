John C. Ford, 88, a beloved husband, father, grandfather, brother and friend passed away on June 29, 2024, in his hometown of Rockford. He was born on August 1, 1935, son to J. Clayton and Gertrude (Lane) Ford. John spent his over 40 year working career in the Rockford area as a real estate broker and retired from Dickerson Nieman in 2020. He also enjoyed his time with the Winnebago County Auxiliary serving for 20 years. He married the love of his life, Norma Moody on October 28, 1961, in Rockford, and they had two children. He loved his children, firstly being a father, but also enjoyed becoming their best friend too. John learned that his love and joy knew no bounds after his grandchildren were born, he made sure they knew he loved them to the moon and back. In his free time John enjoyed a good game of golf, antiquing with his sister and brother-in-law, or simply spending time in his yard.
