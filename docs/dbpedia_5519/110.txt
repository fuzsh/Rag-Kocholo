DIOCESE OF HONOLULU (Dioecesis Honoluluensis)

Country: United States

Denomination: Roman Catholic

Established: 1843 as Apostolic Vicariate of the Sandwich Islands

Renamed 1848 as Apostolic Vicariate of the Hawaiian Islands

Elevated to diocese of Honolulu in 1941
