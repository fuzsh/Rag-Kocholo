Maku, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran

Latitude and longitude coordinates are: 39.295620, 44.514187.

Maku is a town in West Azerbaijan Province of Iran, situated in the north westernmost part of the country, very close to the borders of Iran with Turkey and Azerbaijan. It is a small tourist spot, especially for those who like hiking or rock climbing, as well as swimming as fishing since there are some small lakes in the neighborhood. There are a few historic places in Maku, namely an old fort, a few historic mosques and mansions, etc.
