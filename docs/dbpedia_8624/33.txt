Biography

William Knowles was born about 1845 in Green Ponds, Colony of Tasmania, son of Francis Knowles (~1808–1890) and Mary McFarquhar (1816–1904)

He was christened at Greenponds, Tasmania, Australia in 1845 [1]

William married Ann Louisa Anderson (born on March 4, 1846 in Tasmania, Australia; daughter of Thomas Arthur Anderson and Christian Turnbull on 31 Aug 1866 in Hobart, Tasmania, Australia [2]

Their children were:

Christina Ada Louisa Knowles (1875– )

Joseph Anderson Knowles (1882–1960)

Alfred Edward Knowles (~1887–~1936)

William died on April 2, 1894 in Dunedin, Otago, New Zealand, aged about 48. [3]

He was buried in Southern Cemetery Block 144 Plot 41, Dunedin, Dunedin City, Otago, New Zealand [4]
