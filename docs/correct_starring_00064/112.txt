Austin Powers: The Spy Who Shagged Me is a 1999 English-language Action Adventure movie written by Mike Myers and Michael Mccullers. The movie is directed by Jay Roach and produced by Demi Moore, Eric Mcleod, Jennifer Todd, Mike Myers, Suzanne Todd and John Lyons. The film's title is a play on the James Bond film The Spy Who Loved Me . Austin powers: the spy who shagged me features Mike Myers, Heather Graham, Michael York, Robert Wagner, Seth Green, Elizabeth Hurley and Bryson Lang. The motion picture is based on Characters by Mike Myers. The music was composed by George S. Clinton. Cinematography was done by Ueli Steiger and editing by Debra Neil-Fisher and Jon Poll. The film has a running time of 95 minutes. It was released on 24th June 1999. The Film was successful at box office. Austin Powers: The Spy Who Shagged Me was made on a budget of $33 million and it was a super hit at box office gross of $312 million. Distribution rights for the motion picture were acquired by New Line Cinema. The film's title is a play on the James Bond film The Spy Who Loved Me .
