Remembering James- The Life and Music of James Brown Starring Dedrick Weathersby

Remembering James , a 60 minute concert show starring Dedrick Weathersby, takes you back in time to 1968. Following the assassination of Dr. Martin Luther King Jr., the Godfather of Soul, James Brown, performs live at the Boston Garden. Successfully restoring order and keeping the peace, James Brown gives an unforgettable show that honors Dr. King. Accompanied by a eight-piece band, songs include Pass The Peas", Papa's Got a Brand New Bag, Cold Sweat, I Feel Good, Say It Loud Im Black and Im Proud, and many more."

Come join us for an experience you don't want to miss.
