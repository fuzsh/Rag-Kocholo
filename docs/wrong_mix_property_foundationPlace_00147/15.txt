Mogilev, Belarus, September 2, 2009 - Awem studio, the developer of such hits as Star Defender 1 - 4, Cradle Of Rome and Cradle Of Persia, announced the exclusive release of its first and eagerly awaited hidden object game - Romance Of Rome which is now available at http://www.awem.com.

Romance of Rome is an enjoyable hidden object game which successfully merges elements of quest and puzzle into one title. Romance Of Rome offers a captivating storyline with notable and smart characters, sets engaging tasks and is filled with lots of pleasant surprises and twists of events. Players will have a great possibility to embark on a gripping journey through the lands of ancient Rome.

"Awem studio is proud of its hand-drawn graphics," said Oleg Rogovenko, Awem studio's CEO. "In Romance Of Rome you will be excited by a captivating story with humorous dialogues and unique interactions with characters. I believe players will enjoy the time they spend on finding cleverly hidden items and enjoy the game atmosphere we created."

Romance of Rome features 21 visually stunning locations and tons of inventory based puzzles. Earn over 30 ancient trophies and synchronize your achievements with your awem account.

A free trial version of Romance Of Rome can be downloaded from the developer's web site: http://www.awem.com/romance-of-rome.html

Pricing and Availability

Romance of Rome runs on Windows XP/VISTA and requires Pentium 800 MHz, 512 MB RAM. A single-user license costs $19.95 (USD). More information is available at http://www.awem.com/romance-of-rome.html
