Palm Desert is a city located in Riverside County California. Palm Desert has a 2024 population of 52,202. Palm Desert is currently growing at a rate of 0.48% annually and its population has increased by 1.96% since the most recent census, which recorded a population of 51,197 in 2020.

The average household income in Palm Desert is $115,908 with a poverty rate of 12.94% . The median age in Palm Desert is 55.2 years: 53 years for males, and 57.3 years for females.
