Grigoriy Andreyevich Rechkalov (February 9, 1920 - December 22, 1990) was a Soviet fighter pilot during World War II, credited with over 50 enemy aircraft shootdowns, making him one of the highest-scoring Soviet pilots of the war. He achieved his first enemy kill just six days after the German invasion of the Soviet Union. Rechkalov was awarded the title of Hero of the Soviet Union twice and achieved the rank of general after the war.
