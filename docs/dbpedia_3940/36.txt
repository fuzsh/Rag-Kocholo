Arthur Thomas "Tom" Grosvenor Origin/Culture/Country: British

Arthur Thomas "Tom" Grosvenor: was an English professional footballer who played as an inside right. His brother Percy Grosvenor played for West Bromwich Albion and Leicester City.

Thomas Grosvenor Origin/Culture/Country: British

Thomas Grosvenor: was a British Army officer and politician.Grosvenor was the third son of Thomas Grosvenor, MP, and his wife, Deborah, daughter and coheir of Stephen Skynner of Walthamstow, Essex. His father was the only brother of Richard Grosvenor, 1st Earl Grosvenor. Like his father, Grosvenor was educated at Westminster School (admitted November 1773).
