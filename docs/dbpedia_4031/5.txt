Qatar clinched their fourth gold medal at the 18th Asian Games in Indonesia, after Sherif Younis and Ahmad Tejan secured a win in the beach volleyball competition.

The Qatari duo defeated Indonesia's Ade Candra and Mohammad Ashfiya 2-0 in the final of the competition, finishing 26-24 and 21-17 in 46 minutes.

The medal is Qatar's eighth so far in the games.

Other medal winners are: Abderrahman Samba's 400m hurdles gold, Abdalelah Haroun's 400m gold, Ashraf Saifi's gold in the hammer-throwing competition, Yesir Salem Mubarak's silver in the 3000m race, Tosin Ogunode's silver in the 100m race, Fares Hassouna's silver in weightlifting (94kg), and Hammad al-Marri's bronze medal in shooting (double trap).
