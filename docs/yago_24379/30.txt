Georg Ludwig von Trapp and Maria von Trapp were married for 19 years before Georg Ludwig von Trapp died, leaving behind his partner and 3 children.

They had 3 children, Rosmarie von Trapp (95), Eleonore von Trapp (93) and Johannes von Trapp (85).

About

Austrian Military Serviceperson Georg Ludwig von Trapp was born Baron Georg Ludwig Ritter von Trapp on 4th April, 1880 in Zadar, Austria-Hungary and passed away on 30th May 1947 Stowe, Vermont, U.S aged 67. He is most remembered for Kommandant Georg Ritter von Trapp. His zodiac sign is Aries.
