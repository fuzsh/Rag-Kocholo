Pre-Queens of the Stone Age 'Gamma Ray' 7" Single - If Only Everything / Born to Hula

Man's Ruin Records Rare and Limited Coloured Vinyl

Pressed in 1996 in a limited run on the old record label of the artist Frank Kozik.

Mans Ruin went defunct in 2002 and started producing limited release records in 1994.

With a simple and respectable 50/50 licencing contract with the artists, Man's Ruin worked with such great punk/rock/stoner/metal bands as;

Fu Manchu, Electric Wizard, Orange Goblin, Earthlings?, Beaver, Kyuss, Queens of the Stone Age, Unsane, The Dwarves, The Melvins, Magic Dirt, Killdozer, The Heads, Acid King, Entombed , Greenmachine, Iron Monkey, Church of Misery, Nebula, Goatsnake, Cavity, Fatso Jetson, Unida, Los Natas, High on Fire and also the Josh Homme/Earthlings experimental stoner outfit The Desert Sessions.

Gamma Ray - If Only Everything/Born to Hula - 1996 Man's Ruin Records - MR 036

Tracklist:

A1. If Only Everything

B2. Born to Hula

Insert sheet stating: "This is the Gamma Ray featuring Josh Homme of Kyuss fame. It is NOT that guy from Helloween or whatever."

Edition of 2000 copies, all on clear vinyl. First 1000 have the "G with the ray coming out of it". Second 1000 have a pattern of squares over circles.
