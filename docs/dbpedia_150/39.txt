WUPA - CW69 Atlanta History, Coverage, Features

WUPA, also known as Atlanta’s CW69, is a regional US TV station affiliated to the CW network and licensed to Atlanta, Georgia. The English-language channel is owned by CBS Television Studios. The channel first aired on August 22, 1981. WUPA’s studios are based in the Upper Kirby district.

The channel is available on digital channel 36 (UHF) and on virtual channel 69 (PSIP). Viewers may additionally choose to watch WUPA via cable through the following TV providers in USA:

Comcast Xfinity: Channel 10

Charter Spectrum: Channel 9

In the past, WUPA has served as the official TV home of NFL side Atlanta Falcons. It has broadcast select Major League Soccer matches involving Atlanta United FC as well.
