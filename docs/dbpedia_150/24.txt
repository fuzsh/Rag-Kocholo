WUPA, virtual channel 69 (UHF digital channel 36), is a CW owned-and-operated television station licensed to Atlanta, Georgia, United States. The station is owned by the CBS Television Stations subsidiary of ViacomCBS. WUPA's studios are located on Northeast Expressway (I-85) in unincorporated DeKalb County (with an Atlanta mailing address), and its transmitter is located near Shepherds Lane and Arnold Avenue in the Woodland Hills section of northeastern Atlanta (near North Druid Hills).

On cable, WUPA is available on Comcast Xfinity channel 10 and Charter Spectrum channel 9.
