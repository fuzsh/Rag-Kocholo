NEW YORK: Actor Ryan Phillippe is defending girlfriend Abbie Cornish from reports that she came between him and Reese Witherspoon who were married for seven years.

Pagesix.com quoted Phillippe as saying, "I had difficulties in my relationship, and my marriage, long before I met her."

Phillippe sat down with a magazine and spoke about his marriage, children, career and his blossoming relationship with Cornish.

Speaking about his divorce from Witherspoon he said, "It was the darkest, saddest place I had ever been. There was a good four or five months of not being able to get out of bed. It was the worst time in my life."
