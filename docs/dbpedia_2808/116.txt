Popular actress, Mojirola Afolayan-Olayiwola also known as Moji Afolayan, is a popular figure in the Yoruba movie industry. Moji, who is one of the children of late veteran actor, Adeyemi Afolayan, better known as Ade Love, is older sister to popular actors, Kunle, Gabriel and Aremu Afolayan.

She is married to Rasaq Olasunkanmi Olayiwola, a Nigerian actor popularly known by his stage name, ‘Ojopagogo‘.

Today, November 3, 2015 is her birthday.
