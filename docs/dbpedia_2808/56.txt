Moji Afolayan is a Nigerian actress born on 5th February 1969 in Ibadan, Oyo state. She hails from Kwara State but grew up in Lagos state. Moji is from a lineage of actors and film makers, she is the daughter of the veteran actor Ade Love and the sister of the popular actor and producer, Kunle Afolayan.

Moji is not only an actress but also a producer and a director. She has directed and featured in so many Yoruba movies. This beautiful elegant woman is happily married.
