Remembering James Brown. James Joseph Brown was born on May 3, 1933. He was born in Barnwell, South Carolina. The singer kickstarted his career by joining the Famous Flames. The group had hits, “Please, Please, Please” and “Try Me.” The “Godfather of Soul” was known for his iconic funk sound. Brown and the Famous Flames later released the live album, 'Live at the Apollo.' The album featured popular singles, "I Got You (I Feel Good)" and "It's a Man's Man's Man's World".
