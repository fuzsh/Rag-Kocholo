Bektez, Kutjevo

Bektež is located in the City of Kutjevo, in Požega-Slavonia County at an elevation of 184 meters above sea level and it covers the area of 12.01 km² (4.64 sq. miles). According to the 2011 census report, there were 384 inhabitants. It is predominantly populated by Croats (99%), but also. Nearby places: Gradište, Vukojevica, Kula, Poreč, Nova Lipovica, Duboka, Kutjevo, Milanlug, Jasik and Ciglenik.
