Biography[]

Clark Haggans (Clark Cromwell Haggans) was born on January 10, 1977 in Torrance, California. After going to high school at Peninsula (Torrance, CA), Haggans attended Colorado State University. Haggans made his professional debut in the NFL in 2000 with the Pittsburgh Steelers. He has played for the Pittsburgh Steelers for his entire 7 year career.

Most people believe that 2005 was Clark Haggans's best year, as he recorded 9 sacks.
