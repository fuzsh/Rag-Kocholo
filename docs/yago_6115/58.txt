Elisabeth Elliot was born December 21, 1926 and passed to glory June 15, 2015. She is widely known as a Christian author and speaker. Her first husband, Jim Elliot, was killed in 1956 while attempting to make missionary contact with the Auca tribe of Ecuador. She was left with a 10 month old daughter, Valerie. She later spent two years as a missionary to that same tribe. She married Addison Lietch in 1969 who died in 1973. She then married Lars Gren in 1977. He continues to distribute her books, CDs and DVDs in the USA.
