Ole Miss has offered a preferred walk-on spot to 2024 Christ Presbyterian Academy linebacker Leroy Harris III. The offer was extended by Ole Miss special teams coordinator Jake Schoonover.

Harris is a 6-5.5, 210-pounder rated as a three-star prospect by 247Sports. He is ranked as the nation's No. 168 linebacker and the No. 62 player in the state of Tennessee by 247Sports. He does not have a Composite ranking at this time.

Harris holds offers from Memphis, Air Force, Navy and FCS members Furman and Eastern Kentucky. Ivy League members Penn and Princeton have also offered. His father, Leroy Harris Jr., played offensive line for N.C. State and seven years in the NFL with the Titans and Lions.

There are currently no predictions on his 247Sports Crystal Ball forecast.
