Freddie Cunliffe (born 1981) is a British television/film actor. He has had TV and film roles over the years, and is best known for his work on the BBC's one-off drama Bella and the Boys, in which he played the role of Martin opposite Billie Piper as Bella.

Another TV role was in The Bill, in which he played Martin Collins.

Cunliffe also appeared in Tim Roth's controversial 1999 film about child abuse, The War Zone, co-starring alongside Lara Belmont, Ray Winstone and Tilda Swinton.
