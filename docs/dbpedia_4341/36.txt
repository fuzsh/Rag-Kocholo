Modified Register for Iñigo López de Mendoza y Figueroa

First Generation

1. Iñigo López de Mendoza y Figueroa was born in 1415/1418 in

Guadalajara, España. He died in 1479. Iñigo married Elvira de

Quiñones daughter of Diego Fernández de Quiñones and María Alvarez

de Toledo.

They had the following children:

+ 2 i. Iñigo López de Mendoza y Quiñones was born in 1440. He died

in 1515.

3 ii. Diego Hurtado de Mendoza.

Second Generation

2. Iñigo López de Mendoza y Quiñones was born in 1440. He died in

1515. Iñigo married Francisca Pacheco Portocarrero daughter of Juan

Fernando Pacheco and María Portocarrero Enríquez.

They had the following children:

+4 i. Virrey Antonio de Mendoza y Pacheco was born about 1490/1491.

5 ii Luis Hurtado de Mendoza. Luis married Catalina de Mendoza.

6 iii. María de Pacheco. María married Juan de Padilla.

7 iv. María de Mendoza. María married Antonio de Mendoza.

8 v. Diego Hurtado de Mendoza.

9 vi. Francisco de Mendoza .

10 vii. Bernardino de Mendoza.

11 viii. Isabel de Mendoza.

Iñigo had outside of marriage with (2) Leonor de Beltrán the

following children:

+ 12 ix. María de Mendoza.

Third Generation

4. Virrey Antonio de Mendoza y Pacheco (Iñigo López de Mendoza y

Quiñones, Iñigo) was born about 1490/1491 in Granada, España.

Antonio married Catalina de Carvajal daughter of Francisco de Vargas

and Juana de Carvajal.

They had the following children:

13 i. Iñigo de Mendoza was born in 1518 in Granada, España. He died

in 1557 in San Quintín, España.

14 ii. Francisco de Mendoza died in 1563. Francisco married Catalina

de Mendoza .

+15 iii. Francisca de Mendoza.

12. María de Mendoza (Iñigo López de Mendoza y Quiñones, Iñigo).

María married Martín de Ircio son of Pedro Sánchez de Ircio and

María Jiménez de Ribafrecha.

They had the following children:

+16 i. María de Ircio y Mendoza.

+17 ii. Leonor de Ircio.

Fourth Generation

15. Francisca de Mendoza (Antonio de Mendoza y Pacheco, Iñigo López

de Mendoza y Quiñones, Iñigo). Francisca married Alonso Fernández de

Córdova y Velasco.

They had the following children:

18 i. Francisco Fernández de Córdova. Francisco married Ana

Pimentel daughter of Pedro Pimentel and María Manrique. Ana was born

in 1575.

16. María de Ircio y Mendoza (María de Mendoza, Iñigo López de

Mendoza y Quiñones, Iñigo). María married Luis de Velasco y Castilla

son of Luis de Velasco and Ana de Castilla y Mendoza.

They had the following children:

19 i. María De Ircio de Mendoza y Velasco. María married Juan de

Altamirano y Castilla son of Hernán Gutiérrez de Altamirano and

Francisca Osorio de Castilla.

20 ii. Luis de Velasco y de Ircio. Luis married Ana de Osorio y

Manrique .

21 iii. Francisco de Velasco y de Ircio. Francisco married Ana de

Castilla daughter of Diego de Ibarra and Ana de Castilla.

22 iv. Antonio de Velasco.

23 v. Martín de Velasco .

17. Leonor de Ircio (María de Mendoza, Iñigo López de Mendoza y

Quiñones, Iñigo). Leonor de Ircio married Carlos de Luna y Arellano .

They had the following children:

24 i. Tristán de Arellano.
