Actors Dina Merrill and Cliff Robertson smile following their wedding at the palatial estate of her mother, cereal heiress Marjorie Meriwether Post, in Washington, Dec. 21, 1966. It was the second marriage for both. The pair, both 41, posed briefly outside the doorway following the ceremony that was limited to family and close friends. (AP Photo/Henry Burroughs)
