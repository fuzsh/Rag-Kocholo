Daniel C. Miller is a politician and public servant from Harrisburg, Pennsylvania. He is current Harrisburg City Treasurer, and previously was the Harrisburg City Controller and was a member of the Harrisburg City Council. The latter two positions were elected at large. Miller is Harrisburg’s first openly gay city councillor.

Daniel C. Miller was born on January 1, 1956, in Harrisburg, Pennsylvania, United States. His birth geographical coordinates are 40° 16’ 25” North latitude and 76° 53’ 4” West longitude, with an altitude of 101 meters above sea level. Daniel C. Miller is currently 68 years old.

Astrologically, Daniel C. Miller falls under the Sun sign of Capricorn, with Aries as his Ascendant, Leo as his Moon sign, and Capricorn as his Black Moon Lilith sign.
