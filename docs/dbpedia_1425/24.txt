IN the words of the latest Honda ad, if the Big Brovaz' debut Nu Flow was What If?, then OK is probably just a little bit better than OK.

After the stage musical-style r'n'b smash which was Nu Flow, OK seems almost bland by comparison.

But that's a slightly unfair conclusion born more of the fact that Nu Flow was particularly quirky than any suggestion that this follow up isn't up to much. A return to standard form R'n' B/Garage led by a Ms Dynamite-clone female vocalist.
