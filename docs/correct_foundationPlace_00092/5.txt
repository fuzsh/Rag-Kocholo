Stacey Middle School, located in the vibrant city of Huntington Beach, California, has consistently held its place as a highly rated public school since its inception on July 1, 1980. With a firm commitment to providing exceptional education to its diverse student body, Stacey Middle School has become a cornerstone of academic excellence and personal growth.

Nestled in a picturesque neighborhood, Stacey Middle School caters to approximately 860 students from grades 6 through 8, ensuring a close-knit community that fosters individual attention and supportive relationships. The school's dedication to cultivating a culture of learning and achievement is reflected in its impressive track record and the numerous accolades it has received over the years.

One of the standout features of Stacey Middle School is its innovative Dual Language Program, which offers students the opportunity to learn Spanish alongside their core curriculum. This program not only enriches their linguistic abilities but also promotes cultural understanding and prepares them for an increasingly interconnected world.

With an overall Niche grade of A- in the areas of Academics, Teachers, and Diversity, Stacey Middle School consistently delivers an exceptional educational experience. The school prides itself on its highly skilled and dedicated faculty, who go above and beyond to ensure each student's success. These passionate educators utilize modern teaching methods, technology, and a personalized approach to meet the unique needs of every student.

Stacey Middle School places a strong emphasis on providing a safe and productive learning environment. Students are encouraged to take on responsibility, develop leadership skills, foster creativity, and engage in collaborative projects. This multifaceted approach to education instills values that extend beyond the classroom and prepares students for success in their future endeavors.

Recognition for achievements and successes is a hallmark of Stacey Middle School. The school has garnered numerous prestigious awards, including the coveted Pivotal Practice Award, which recognizes its exceptional instructional practices. Additionally, Stacey Middle School has been honored with the Distinguished School Award, the Gold Ribbon Award, and the PBIS (Positive Behavioral Interventions and Supports) Award, further highlighting its commitment to excellence.

Stacey Middle School stands as a testament to the power of education in transforming lives. By providing students with a solid foundation in academics, fostering their personal growth, and embracing diversity, the school sets them on a path towards lifelong success. With its unwavering dedication to learning, Stacey Middle School continues to empower and inspire its students, equipping them with the knowledge and skills they need to excel in an ever-changing world.
