On Friday, November 21, 2014, 15 San Diego State University (SDSU) MESA Program students will attend the Solar Turbines Training Academy hosted by Solar Turbines in San Diego.

Solar Turbines will provide SDSU MESA students the opportunity to:

Learn about the engineering technologies Solar Turbines applies to their Turbomachinery Products

Network with both recently hired Solar engineers and managers

Hone their Interview Skills through a mock interview

Showcase what they have learned in mini-project group presentation at the end of the day
