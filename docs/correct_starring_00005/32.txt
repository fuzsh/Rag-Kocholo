The Movie Geeks speak with acclaimed actor Michael Madsen (Reservoir Dogs, Kill Bill Vol. 2, Free Willy, Thelma and Louise) about many of the tremendous films and performances that populate his career, as well as his upcoming projects.

Writer/director Chris Fisher discusses his new film Meeting Evil starring Luke Wilson and Samuel L. Jackson. The film is now available On Demand and will release in theatres on May 4.

Plus, discussions on Anthony Hopkins as Alfred Hitchcock, the best and worst examples of director's cuts, the greatest horror films ever made, and the passing of actor William Finley.

This program contains occasional strong language.
