For a huge role and inarguable contribution to the strengthening and development of the Turkic creative world, Kyrgyz-Azerbaijani literature and culture the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of Azerbaijan to the KR Hidayat Orujov is elected an academician of the Academy of Public named after C.Aitmatov, the press service of the academy reported.

According to the vice-president of the National Academy of Sciences Abdyldazhan Akmataliyev, Hidayat Orujov - renowned poet, essayist, novelist, playwright. His works have been translated into many languages​​. He widely promotes culture and literature of the two countries.
