For the English player, see Mark Dempsey (English footballer).

Mark Anthony Peter Dempsey (born 10 December 1972) is a former Irish association football player during the 1990s and 2000s. He was a defender or midfielder who appeared for Gillingham, Leyton Orient, Shrewsbury Town, Barry Town, Bohemians (2 spells), Shelbourne and Drogheda United during his career.[1]
