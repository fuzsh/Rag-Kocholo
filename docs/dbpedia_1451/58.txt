Mark Dempsey was an actor who played the Air Force captain in the Star Trek episode “Tomorrow is Yesterday”.

Dempsey was born as Herbert L. Dempsey in West Hollywood, California and played regular roles in stage plays beside his on-screen appearances.

Among his acting resume are the films Valley of the Dragons (1961), Palm Springs Weekend (1963), Oh, Calcutta! (1972), The Prodigal (1983), and To Cross the Rubicon (1991).

He also guest starred in television series such as G.E. True (1962), Hawaiian Eye (1963), 77 Sunset Strip (1963), Perry Mason (1964), and Bergerac (1989).
