Ornella Barra

Ornella Barra, born in Italy, now of Monegasque nationality, graduated from the University of Genoa with a degree in pharmacy and embarked on a career in the healthcare sector. Throughout her career, she has held several roles and responsibilities, as member of the Boards of Directors of various companies (both listed and privately held) of the Group. These various transformation cycles led in 2014 to the creation of Walgreens Boots Alliance (Nasdaq WBA), the first global pharmacy-led, health and wellbeing enterprise.
