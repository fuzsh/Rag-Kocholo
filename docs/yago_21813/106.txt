My Village at Sunset is a 1992 Cambodian romance film. A handsome young doctor returns to his home village after studying in Paris. Most of the patients in his clinic are the victims of leftover land mines. He falls in love with a beautiful young nurse that works in his clinic. He must also fend off the advances of his cousin who is married to an amputee war veteran. To find love he must first find his way out of the triangle created by his situation.
