Hobgoblins is a 1988 English-language Comedy Horror film written and directed by Rick Sloane and produced by Rick Sloane. A group of hobgoblins, who allow you to live out your fantasies but kill you in the process, escape from a studio vault, and a security guard and his friends must stop them before dawn. Written by Brendan Dillon. Hobgoblins features Tom Bartlett, Paige Sullivan, Steven Boggs, Kelley Palmer, Billy Frank, Daran Norris and Cole Coonce. The music was composed by Alan Dermarderosian. Cinematography was done by Rick Sloane and editing by Rick Sloane. The film has a running time of 92 minutes. It was released on 14th July 1988. Hobgoblins was made on a budget of $0 million. Distribution rights for the motion picture were acquired by American Cinema Marketing.
