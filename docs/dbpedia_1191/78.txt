Dance music label Youth Control are celebrating a decade of dropping one tune after another. Finnish producer Rony Rex who made his Youth Control debut in 2017 decided to pay tribute to the label by naming his new compilation project, 10.

“Feeling Myself” featuring vocalist Rye Rye is one of the songs on this compilation. With an engaging bassline and sultry vocals, this song is so catchy. Listen to it and stream the whole project below via Spotify.
