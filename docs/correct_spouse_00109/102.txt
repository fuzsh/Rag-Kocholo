Vanessa Marcil has filed for divorce from CSI: NY actor Carmine Giovinazzo.

According to TMZ, the former 90210 star cited "irreconcilable differences" as the reason for the split.

Marcil and Giovinazzo married in 2010 and have no children together, but the couple had tried to expand the family. In December, Giovinazzo revealed that Marcil suffered at least two miscarriages during their marriage. Marcil does have a son Kassius, 10, with her ex and former 90210 co-star, Brian Austin Green.

Giovinazzo was Marcil's second husband. The actress was married to Corey Feldman for four years.
