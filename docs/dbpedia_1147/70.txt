Rygar: The Legendary Adventure, released in Japan as Argus no Senshi (アルゴスの戦士), is an action-adventure game for the PlayStation 2, released in November 2002. It is based on the original Rygar released for arcade and home consoles. It features 3D graphics and a destructible environment. The game is inspired by Greek and Roman mythology and has many enemies and worlds named after them.

It was released to generally positive reviews. The game was ported to the Wii in 2008, as Rygar: The Battle of Argus, released in Japan as Argus no Senshi: Muscle Impact (アルゴスの戦士 マッスルインパクト).
