Genre: Rock

Hometown: Las Vegas, NV

Raiding the Rock Vault brings the history of rock ‘n’ roll to life, featuring classic anthems by the biggest acts in music including The Rolling Stones, The Who, The Doors, Jimi Hendrix, Led Zeppelin, Deep Purple, Free, Eagles, Fleetwood Mac, Queen, Boston, Kansas, Aerosmith, Van Halen, AC/DC, Pat Benatar, Foreigner, Heart, Journey, Bon Jovi, Whitesnake and many more.

The rotating lineup for Raiding the Rock Vault is drawn from a “deep bench” of veteran rockstars chosen for their experience and commitment to the spirit of the show. Every night you can meet the band after the show for photos and autographs.

raidingtherockvault.com/tickets

Note: Due to touring schedules and recording committments, the lineup for the show changes often. Please note that specific performers are not guaranteed on specific nights and lineups can change without warning. Nevertheless, we will rock you.
