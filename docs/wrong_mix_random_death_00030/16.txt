Award-winning country singer Hank Williams Jr. is scheduled to perform at Mechanics Bank Arena on Saturday, September 25th at 8 p.m.

Williams has sold 70 million albums sold worldwide. Marking 52 years since his first album in 1964, Hank Jr. released 'It's About Time' on January 15.

He has won countless awards like ACM Entertainer of the Year, CMA Entertainer of the Year, and BMI Icon in addition to winning a GRAMMY and being inducted into the Nashville Songwriters Hall of Fame.
