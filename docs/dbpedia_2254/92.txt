Lais Ribeiro is a Brazilian model born on October 5, 1990 in Teresina. She is well-known for her work with some of the world's most prestigious fashion labels, such as Dolce & Gabbana, Louis Vuitton, Chanel, Versace, and Gucci. She gained further recognition for her runway work at the 2011 Sao Paulo Fashion Week.
