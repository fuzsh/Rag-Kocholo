Lais Ribeiro is a Brazilian fashion model most famous for being a Victoria’s Secret Angel. She has appeared on the cover of GQ Mexico, Vogue Brazil, Elle Brazil and 10 Magazine. Lais walked the VS Fashion Show in 2010, 2011, 2013, 2014, 2015 and 2016. In 2015, she became a Victoria’s Secret Angel. For campaigns, Lais appeared in advertisements for Tom Ford, American Eagle and Ralph Lauren. In 2017, she had the honor of wearing the Victoria’s Secret Fashion Bra valued at $2 million. Lais has one son.
