Research in progress in the territory of Sant'Antonio di Gallura for a man of about 60 who has been missing for hours.

It is a German tourist who, together with family members, rented a house in the countryside. This morning he went out, but he never came back . Relatives immediately informed 112.

The soldiers of the Tempio company arrived on the spot and asked for the intervention of the firefighters of the provincial command of Sassari for research.

The local command unit was immediately set up, with the involvement of the divers and the Sapr core of the firefighters who are flying over the area with drones .

A helicopter from the Alghero flight department is also in the sky. The scuba divers' searches focus on Lake Liscia.
