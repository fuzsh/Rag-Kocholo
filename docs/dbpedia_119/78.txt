Econophysics describes the application of tools from statistical physics to the study of problems in economics such as correlations in stock prices or the distribution of wealth in society. We present an analysis of financial data from stocks that belong to the London Stock Exchange, FTSE100, using the concept of random matrix theory and minimal spanning trees.
