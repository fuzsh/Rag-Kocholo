DIMAPUR — The Western Chakhesang Youth Organisation (WCYO), in collaboration with the Nagaland Tug of War Association, will be organising the first ethnic community tug of war (Nagaland open) on the theme ‘Oneness on rope for peace,’ on January 13, 2024, at DDSC Stadium, Dimapur.

The WCYO in an update informed that each team will consists of eight players with two substitutes and a coach-cum-manager. The tournament will be “free weight and free style” and the champions will receive cash prize of INR 1 lakh.
