JSC Olainfarm is the leading manufacturer in the chemical and pharmaceutical sector of the Baltic States with over 45 years of experience. We export to more than 60 countries worldwide; export share is near 75%.

Portfolio contains over 60 FDFs, 25 APIs and more than 20 Intermediates. The key areas of specialization in FDFs include neurology, cardiology, infectology and allergology. Olainfarm has a long-standing technological and know-how advantage in adamantane, quinuclidine and nitrofurane chemistry. The company owns the controlling stake in the leading Latvian food supplement company Silvanols, has its own chain of pharmacies Latvijas aptieka in Latvia, owns producer of elastic medical products Tonus Elast, producer of natural herbal medicines and food supplements NPK Biotest (Belarus), medical companies Klīnika DiaMed and Olainmed, eco-cosmetics company Kiwi Cosmetics.
