Olainfarm products are exported to more than 60 countries around the world – about 90% of production is placed on export markets.

840 employees perform daily work in the company, ensuring a continuous process of product production in 24/7 mode.

Olainfarm cooperates with globally and regionally known companies, including Chemo Group, Olon Spa, Dr Reddy’s, SUN Pharmaceuticals, Arotex AU, Almirall Ranke, UQUIFA, Hetero Drugs, Teva and Egis.

Since 2011, JSC Olainfarm’s anti-tuberculosis drugs have been prequalified for delivery by the World Health Organization.

The company has many years of experience in the production and development of pharmaceutical products such as the adamantane series, quinuclidine and nitrofuran.
