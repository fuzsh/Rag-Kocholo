Texans for Henry Cuellar is a campaign organization dedicated to supporting Congressman Henry Cuellar in his mission to fight for the interests of working families in South Texas. With a proven track record of 15 years of hard work and real results, Congressman Cuellar is committed to ensuring good schools, affordable healthcare, a strong economy, and opportunities for success for every family in the region.

As one of eight children born to migrant farm workers in Laredo, Texas, Congressman Cuellar's personal background fuels his passion for education and his unwavering work ethic. With the distinction of being the most degreed member of Congress, he credits his education for informing his public service to Texas. Texans for Henry Cuellar aims to keep the American Dream alive by supporting Congressman Cuellar's vision for the district and his dedication to improving the lives of the people he represents.
