At stake in this election:

The office of President of Cape Verde

Description of government structure:

Chief of State: President Pedro Verona Rodrigues PIRES *

Head of Government: Prime Minister Jose Maria Pereira Pereira NEVES

Assembly: Cape Verde has a unicameral National Assembly (Assembleia Nacional) with 72 seats.

* On December 8, 2005, President of the Parliament Aristides Raimundo LIMA became Acting President of the Republic per a constitutional requirement that Pedro PIRES suspend his duties of office while he runs in this election.

Description of electoral system:

The President is elected by popular vote to serve a 5-year term.

In the National Assembly (Assembleia Nacional) 72 members are elected by popular vote to serve 5-year terms.

Main Candidates in the Presidential race:

Pedro Verona Rodrigues PIRES

Party: African Party for the Independence of Cape Verde / (PAICV)

Carlos Alberto Wahnon de Carvalho VEIGA

Party: Movement for Democracy / (MPD)

Last Presidential election:

The last presidential election was held on February 11 and 25, 2001. Pedro PIRES (PAICV) was elected president with 49.43% of the vote against Carlos VEIGA (MPD) with 49.42%. The election was won by only 12 votes.
