These recycling efforts are available to members of the toy library only. Bring these items in to any of the branch locations. Ask a volunteer for help with where the collection boxes are located.

Recycling efforts at Toy Library locations:

Crayons via The Crayon Initiative

Broken crayons, whole crayons, wrappers on okay

Squeeze pouches via Terracycle and Go Go Squeeze

Squeeze pouches must be empty, clean, and dry and have caps. Any brand accepted.

**The Minneapolis Toy Library receives a kickback for pounds of squeeze pouches donated, so the more you collect, the more money the toy library receives!

1) Terracycle – Electronic toys, puzzle pieces, plastic & metal figurines, playing cards, remote control vehicles, board games, plush toys, flexible packaging accepted. Any toy broken beyond repair.

2) Hasbro Free Recycling Program – broken Hasbro toys and games

3) Spin Master Free Recycling Program – Spin Master toys, games, and packaging

4) V-tech and LeapFrog Toys Free Recycling Program – V-tech and LeapFrog electronic learning devices and electronic toys

5) Mattel PlayBack – Barbie®, Fisher-Price®, Matchbox® and MEGA™ toys for recycling.

Candy and Snack Wrappers via Rubicon Trash to Treasure Program Empty and clean candy and snack wrappers

Make sure to check out the bi-monthly swaps available at both locations.
