"Brewster McCloud" (1970)

*Monday* April 14 @ 5:30pm

*105 min., plus post-discussion

Directed by Robert Altman (M*A*S*H), about a young recluse (Bud Cort) who lives in a fallout shelter of the Houston Astrodome -- where he is building a pair of wings so he can fly. He is helped by his enigmatic "fairy godmother", played by Sally Kellerman. Co-starring Shelley Duvall.

One of the strangest Hollywood films ever made!

Not on Netflix. 105 min.
