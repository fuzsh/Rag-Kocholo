Louise of Hesse-Kassel and Christian IX of Denmark were married for 56 years before Louise of Hesse-Kassel died, leaving behind her partner and 6 children.

They had 6 children, Frederick (181), Alexandra (179), George (178), Dagmar (176), Thyra (170) and Valdemar (165).
