Maddox (also referred to as Sea Fury) is a racing plane who took part in the Lincoln Qualifier.

History[]

In Planes, Maddox can be seen in his stall at the Lincoln Qualifier for the Wings Around the Globe Rally while Dusty, Chug and Dottie are strolling past him and checking out the other racers.

Later, he hits a gate during his qualifying run.

Model[]

As mentioned by Dusty, he is a Hawker Sea Fury, a British late WW2 plane of the Fleet Air Arm.
