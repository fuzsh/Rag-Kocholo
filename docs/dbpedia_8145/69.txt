Meaning of Mishti

Mishti is a beautiful and unique name for baby girls. It originates from the Bengali language and is pronounced as "mish-tee" (/mɪʃ.ti/). Mishti has a sweet and melodious sound, perfectly reflecting its meaning, which translates to "sweet" or "sweetness" in English. This name carries a sense of warmth and affection, making it an ideal choice for parents who want to express their love for their little one. While Mishti may not be a widely used name in the United States, it has been steadily gaining popularity in recent years. In 2021, there were 6 newborns named Mishti, with slightly higher occurrences in previous years. Despite its rarity, Mishti exudes charm and uniqueness that will make your daughter stand out.
