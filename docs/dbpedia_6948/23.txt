The Oschelskopftour leads through all the villages of the Freinsheim holiday region. The "Radweg Deutsche Weinstrasse" (German Wine Route Cycle Route) and the "Kraut und Rueben-Radweg" (Cabbage and Turnip"Cycle Route) are stage cycle routes and run parallel to the German Wine Route. The "Salierradweg" (Salian Emperor Cycle Route) connects Bad Duerkheim and Speyer. The "Eckbachmuehlen" hiking path can also be explored by bike and leads you along Eckbach brook past several ancient mills.
