Biography

Maria was born in 1746 as daughter of Joannes Schwörer and Franziska Fürderer from Unterhöfenhof in Jostal. Her godparents were Anton Kaltenbach and Agatha Winterhalder.[1]

In 1788 she married Jakob Tröscher from Trescherhof in Eckbach (currently: Breitnau). Witnesses were Johannes Tröscher and Maria Tröscher.[2] The couple had at least 9 children.[3]

She passed away in 1788.[4]
