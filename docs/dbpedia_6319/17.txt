Boys in Blue is an American police procedural television series that aired on the RKO Network from September 5, 2003 to November 11, 2013. Based off the British series The Bill, the series took place in Baltimore, Maryland and focused on the lives of officers of the Baltimore Police Department. It is the first series in the Boys in Blue franchise. follow-up series, Boys in Blue: Baltimore, premiered in 2022.
