Lucas Grabeel is an actor known for playing the role of Ryan Evans on High School Musical and during the song High School Reunion.

Grabeel made a cameo appearance in the sixth episode of the first season of High School Musical: The Musical: The Series as himself. He appeared in a dream sequence and sang the song Role of a Lifetime together with Kate Reinders.
