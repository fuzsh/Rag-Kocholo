Wikipedia, December 23, 2014 (Auguste Marie François Beernaert (26 July 1829-6 October 1912) was the 14th Prime Minister of Belgium from October 1884 to March 1894. Born in Ostend. co-winner (with Paul d'Estournelles de Constant) of the Nobel Peace Prize in 1909 for his work at the Permanent Court of Arbitration. Chosen as president of the panel established under the rules of that organization in the Sarvarkar Case in 1911. A year later, he died in Lucerne, Switzerland; Prime Minister of Belgium in office 26 October 1884-26 March 1894)
