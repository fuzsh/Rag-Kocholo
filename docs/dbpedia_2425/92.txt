Gauhati University, situated in the vibrant city of Guwahati in Assam, India, is a renowned institution committed to providing quality education and fostering academic excellence. Established with a vision to promote higher education and research in the northeastern region of India, Gauhati University has been a beacon of knowledge and learning since its inception.

Accolades

Gauhati University has received numerous accolades and recognitions for its contributions to higher education and research. These accolades serve as a testament to the university's dedication to academic excellence and its significant impact on the region's educational landscape.

NIRF Ranking 2023

In the NIRF (National Institutional Ranking Framework) rankings of 2023, Gauhati University secured a notable position, reaffirming its status as one of the leading universities in India. This recognition highlights the university's commitment to academic rigor, research excellence, and holistic development of its students.

Global Focus

With a global outlook, Gauhati University emphasizes international collaborations, exchange programs, and research partnerships to provide students with exposure to global perspectives and opportunities. Through collaborations with universities and institutions worldwide, the university aims to foster a global learning environment that prepares students to thrive in a multicultural and interconnected world.

Placement Record (2023)

The placement record of 2023 reflects Gauhati University's strong industry interface and its ability to produce industry-ready professionals. Through comprehensive training programs, industry internships, and campus recruitment drives, the university equips its students with the necessary skills and knowledge to secure placements in top companies and organizations across various sectors.

Infrastructure

Gauhati University boasts modern infrastructure and state-of-the-art facilities to support teaching, research, and extracurricular activities. From well-equipped classrooms and laboratories to libraries, sports complexes, and student accommodation, the university provides a conducive environment for learning, research, and personal growth.

Courses/Programmes

The university offers a diverse range of courses and programs across various disciplines, including arts, sciences, commerce, management, and technology. From undergraduate and postgraduate degrees to doctoral research opportunities and specialized diploma programs, the curriculum is designed to cater to the diverse interests and career aspirations of students.

Admission Process

The admission process at Gauhati University is conducted through a standardized procedure, including entrance examinations, academic evaluation, and counseling sessions. Prospective students are selected based on merit and eligibility criteria, ensuring fairness and transparency in the admission process.

Fest/Events

Throughout the academic year, Gauhati University organizes a variety of festivals, events, and cultural celebrations to promote extracurricular participation and holistic development among students. From academic conferences and seminars to cultural festivals, sports tournaments, and alumni reunions, these events provide students with opportunities for intellectual engagement, social interaction, and personal development, fostering a vibrant and inclusive campus community.
