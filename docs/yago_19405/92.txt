71 year old Oludotun Jacobs popularly known as Olu Jacobs and wife Joke Silva are tagged as Nollywood’s most celebrated couple.

In an exclusive interview with theneg.ng,Olu Jacob narrated how he met his wife.

“I was having a meeting at National theatre and the door opened a young lady came in.I looked at her.I have never met her in my life And i said ‘Ladies and gentlemen,this is the lady i am going to marry’.Everybody laughed.She just looked at me up and down,hiss and left.Today,she is my wife and that was 27years ago”, he said.
