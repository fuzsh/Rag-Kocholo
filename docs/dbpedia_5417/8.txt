Gabriela M. Dehesa-Azuara is a project manager for the Board of Trustees at the Urban Institute. Before joining Urban, Gabriela worked at the International Baccalaureate, organizing global professional development conferences for international educators.

Gabriela holds a BA degree in political science from McGill University, a MSc in comparative politics from the London School of Economics and Political Science, and a MA in social science from the University of Chicago.
