Storyline: Sahasam (english:bravery) is a 2013 telugu adventure film directed by chandra sekhar yeleti and produced by b. v. s. n. prasad under sri venkateswara cine chitra starring gopichand and taapsee pannu, who share the screen for the second time after the 2011 film mogudu.
