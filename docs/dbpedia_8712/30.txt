Reshmi Ghosh, the winner of Femina Miss India Earth 2002, has carved a successful path in the entertainment industry, transitioning from the pageant stage to becoming a renowned television actress. Following her pageant victory, Reshmi ventured into films, debuting in the Bengali movie 'Aamar Mayer Shapath' in 2003. However, it was her foray into television that truly solidified her presence in the hearts of audiences.

Reshmi's television career boasts an impressive repertoire, with appearances in over 20 serials. She has graced popular shows like 'Kyunki Saas Bhi Kabhi Bahu Thi', 'Karam Apnaa Apnaa', and 'Mahakali - Anth Hi Aarambh'. Her talent and versatility have earned her recognition and admiration from fans and critics alike.

Apart from her television stints, Reshmi has starred in two movies, showcasing her acting prowess on the big screen. Her ability to portray diverse roles with finesse has made her a sought-after talent in the industry.
