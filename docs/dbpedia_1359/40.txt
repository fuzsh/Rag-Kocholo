Surname Nagaraj

Family name Nagaraj (Nagaraj , นาคราช ) , worldwide, is a common cognomen. The last name Nagaraj is habitual in Western Asia, particularly United Arab Emirates, where it is quite a rare surname, Bahrain, and Oman. Measured by absolute frequency, it is commonest in India, Thailand, and the United States. More frequently, Nagaraj is the forename as well as the last name.
