Heubuch, Otto

Date of birth:

October 17th, 1917 (Heuwang/Unterthingau, Germany)

Date of death:

May 13th, 1944 (Dnjestr near Griguripol, Soviet Union)

Nationality:

German

Biography

According to the notice on his death (deathcard), Otto Heubuch was killed on 13th May 1944. Several other sources state as date of his death 12th May.
