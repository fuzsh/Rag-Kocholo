Amanda Seyfried brought us beloved characters in Mean Girls and Mamma Mia! Coming up, the actress stars in an adaptation of Scooby-Doo and a horror film from screenwriter David Koepp. In the new Molner’s Table, Seyfried looks back at her career — and reveals what she’s been up to in quarantine. Highlights:

On a Mean Girls reunion: “There’s no time like the present.”

A recent book she loved: Untamed, by Glennon Doyle.

Her career regret: Turning down Guardians of the Galaxy.
