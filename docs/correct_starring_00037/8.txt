Roshon Fegan is an actor and rapper who goes by the name of 3inaRo a.k.a. Young Ro'. He also hosts the "Ro and Co Show", on YouTube, with Cody Linley.

He wrote and created a song called "Camp Rock" featured during "Camp Memories" bonus feature on the Camp Rock DVD, and took part in the 2008 Disney Channel Games.

He was portrayed as Rocky's older brother in the Disney Channel sitcom, "Shake it Up".
