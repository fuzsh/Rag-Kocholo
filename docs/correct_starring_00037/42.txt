Roshon Bernard Fegan (born October 6, 1991) is an American actor, dancer and musician. He is best known for his role as Sander Loya in the Disney Channel movie franchise Camp Rock and for his role as Ty Blue on the Disney Channel original series Shake It Up!.

Career[]

Fegan was born and raised in Los Angeles, the son of Roy Fegan, a well known television actor. He trained at the BK Acting Studio and USC 32nd Street Performing Arts and has had guest-starring roles on several television series including Monk. His feature film credits include Spiderman 2 and Drillbit Taylor. In 2008, he landed the role of Sander Loyer in the Disney Channel original movie Camp Rock which was the number one entertainment telecast on cable TV in 2008, and reprised his role in the 2010 sequel Camp Rock 2: The Final Jam. Fegan currently co-stars on the Disney Channel original series Shake It Up! as Ty Blue, the popular older brother of Rocky (Zendaya).

On February 29, 2012, it was announced by ABC that Roshon would be a contestant on season 14 of the reality dance show, Dancing With the Stars, and was partnered up with Chelsie Hightower. He is the fourth Disney Channel television star to compete in the series following the footsteps of Cody Linley, Kyle Massey, and Chelsea Kane. Season 14 premiered on March 19, 2012. He was eliminated on May 8, 2012 during a double-elimination show; he placed 6th in the overall competition

Personal life[]

Roshon Fegan was born on October 6, 1991 in Los Angeles, California. He was born to a Filipina mother and an African-American father. He attended Hollywood High School before leaving to be home-schooled in order to accommodate his busy work schedule. In his free time, Fegan is a music producer who creates his own music. He makes YouTube videos with fellow actor Cody Linley. The two call their show The Ro and Co Show, which mostly features videos of them freestyle rapping. Fegan has released a number of singles on iTunes and is finishing his first self produced album under his own label, called "3inaRo Entertainment." He has a passion for dance, acting and music. A drummer since the age of two, he has also learned to play piano, guitar, sing and write his own songs. He had been in a long-term relationship with model Camia-Marie Chaidez from August 2008 until 2015. He is now currently dating dancer Dytto.
