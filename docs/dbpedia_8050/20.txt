BIOGRAPHY

Tate Donovan began his career on television as a teenager in several TV films such as Not My Kid (1985), and in the mini series Nutcracker: Money, Madness & Murder . To get closer to cinema, he moved to Los Angeles and obtained roles in the films Clean and sober (1988) where he played a drug addict, Memphis Belle (1990), and Love Potion No. 9 (1992) in the lead role alongside Sandra Bullock .

After several small roles, and dubbing the character of Hercules in the Disney cartoon of the same name, Tate Donovan turned to television. He appeared in hit series like Ally McBeal (1997) and Friends (1998) where he played Joshua, Rachel’s ( Jennifer Aniston ) boyfriend . In 2003, the series Newport Beach offered him the regular role of Jimmy Cooper.
