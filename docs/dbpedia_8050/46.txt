Tate Donovan, who was raised in Tenafly, turns 52 on Sunday.

Donovan, an actor and director, was born Sept. 25, 1963.

An actor since his teenage years and a graduate of Englewood’s Dwight – Englewood School, Donovan has appeared in more than 75 films and television shows.

Among his most notable roles came in “Argo” and 1992’s “Love Potion No. 9” alongside Sandra Bullock.

Donovan has appeared in Broadway and off-Broadway productions. He has also done voice work for video games and radio advertisements, and released an album with his traditional Irish band “The Descendants.”
