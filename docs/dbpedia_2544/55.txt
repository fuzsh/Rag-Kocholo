Marie Magdeleine Real Del Sarte was a French artist who was born in 1853. Marie Magdeleine Real Del Sarte's work has been offered at auction multiple times, with realized prices ranging from 74 USD to 2,530 USD, depending on the size and medium of the artwork. Since 2018 the record price for this artist at auction is 2,530 USD for Élégante sur les quais, sold at Tessier & Sarrou in 2019. The artist died in 1927.

Artist's alternative names: Real del Sarte
