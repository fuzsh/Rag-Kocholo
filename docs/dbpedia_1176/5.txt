Rob Blumrick is the Director of Technical Servicea at the Moody where he oversees the technical needs of the facility alongside those of visiting artists, students, faculty, and the general public. A native Houstonian, he moves back home from Madisonville, Kentucky, where he served as Production Manager at The Glema Mahr Center for the Arts. Blumrick is a veteran of the U.S. Army, and has a Bachelor of Theatre Technology and a Master of Business Administration from Murray State University in Kentucky.
