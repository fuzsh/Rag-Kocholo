Khalid Khudair Mashaan Al-Khudair is the founder of Alargan International Real Estate Co., which was founded in 1994.

He held the title of Vice Chairman & Chief Executive Officer at the company.

Mr. Al-Khudair currently holds multiple positions.

He is the Director at the Kuwait Foundation for The Advancement of Science, Director at Injaz Kuwait, Director at Kuwait Green Building Council, and a Member of the Harvard Alumni Association.

In terms of education, Mr. Al-Khudair attended California State University-Los Angeles, where he completed his undergraduate degree.
