Alargan International Real Estate Co. engages in the development and marketing of real estate properties. Its project portfolio include dining destinations, residential housing, healthcare, and education. The firm also offers architectural design, construction and contracting, property management, project management, real estate sales and marketing, hospitality, and healthcare management services. It operates through the following segments: Real Estate Investment, Contracting, and Hotels and Resorts. The company was founded by Khalid Khudair Mashaan Al-Khudair in 1994 and is headquartered in Kuwait City.
