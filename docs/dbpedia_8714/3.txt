Reshmi Ghosh is a television actor who has starred in a number of serials such as Kyunki Saas Bhi Kabhi Bahu Thi, Kahe Naa Kahe, Karam Apnaa Apnaa, Kayamat, Maan Rahe Tera Pitaah, Tere Liye, Mata ki Chowki and currently Buddha. She started as a model and won a beauty pageant.
