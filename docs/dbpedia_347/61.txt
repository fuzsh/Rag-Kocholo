The shooting for Shahid Kapoor and Pooja Hegde’s action-thriller Deva has been wrapped, the makers announced on Monday. Pooja cut the cake along with the crew of the film to mark the shoot completion. The film also stars Pavail Gulatie in a pivotal role.

Sharing pictures of the wrap party, the makers wrote, “And that's a wrap on Deva! A thrilling, action-packed adventure coming your way real soon!” In the photos, producer Siddharth Roy Kapur can also be seen along with others. Shahid gave the celebration a miss.

Deva features high-octane action sequences performed by both Shahid and Pavail. The film, set against the picturesque landscapes of Mumbai, promises to deliver thrilling scenes and intense drama.
