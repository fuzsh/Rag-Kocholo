Shahid Kapoor and Kriti Sanon's untitled film is scheduled to release on December 7 this year. As per a report in Bollywood Hungama, Kapoor will soon be shooting for a special dance number that will feature in the film. A source close to the development told the portal, “A huge set has been put up in Mumbai where the song will be shot. It is obviously going to be a groovy dance track and we can’t wait to see Shahid’s dancing magic on the big screen soon.”

In addition to Kapoor and Sanon, the film also stars Dimple Kapadia and Dharmendra. The film is written and directed by Amit Joshi & Aradhana Sah and produced by Dinesh Vijan, Jyoti Deshpande & Laxman Utekar under the Maddock Films and Jio Studios banners.
