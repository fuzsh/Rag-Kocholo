He has won an NBA championship with the Lakers. He played with the Spanish basketball team at the 2004, 2008 and 2012 Summer Olympics, winning silver medals in 2008 and 2012. Before playing with the Lakers, he played in Spain and for the Memphis Grizzlies. He wears #16 for the Lakers, and plays both forward and center. He is 215 cm (7 ft 1 in) tall and weight 115 kg (254 lb) , and has a younger brother named Marc Gasol who plays for the Toronto Raptors.
