French Film Director Henri-Georges Clouzot was born on 20th November, 1907 in Niort, Deux-Sevres, Poitou-Charentes, France and passed away on 12th Jan 1977 Paris, France aged 69. He is most remembered for L`assassin habite au 21 / The murderer lives at Number 21. His zodiac sign is Scorpio.

Brazilian Actress Véra Clouzot was born Véra Gibson-Amado on 30th December, 1913 in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil and passed away on 15th Dec 1960 Paris, France aged 46. She is most remembered for Les Diaboliques (1955). Her zodiac sign is Capricorn.
