Written and directed by Jean-Pierre Bekolo, “Les Saignantes” is a 2005 erotic sci-fi thriller and political satire. It centers around two young women who use their sexuality to bring down their government. The film has often been praised for the fresh, futuristic perspective it offers viewers into a modern/futuristic Africa.
