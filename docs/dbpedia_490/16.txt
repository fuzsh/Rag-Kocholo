Tanya Blount has been played on NTS shows including DISE Records w/ NA DJ, with Through The Rain first played on 14 September 2019.

Tanya Blount is an African American actress, author and musician. She was born September 30, c. 1973 or 1974 in Washington, D.C. She studied at Potomac High School in Oxon Hill, Maryland, Duke Ellington School of the Arts and Morgan State University, where she majored in psychology and sang in the university choir.
