Stephanie Gibson is a Broadway, Television, and Film actress and writer residing in New York City. She is best known for playing step sister Gabrielle in Cinderella opposite Fran Drescher and Carly Rae Jepson, and Cherry Sunday in Charlie and the Chocolate Factory. She has also been seen on Broadway in Addams Family and Spamalot and around the county in multiple National Touring and Regional productions, including playing Maggie in Baltimore Center Stages production of Cat on a Hot Tin Roof directed by Judith Ivey and most recently, playing Sonya in the world premiere of Crime and Punishment: A Comedy at the Old Globe.

Steph was recently seen on “Run the World” on Starz and in the recurring role of Pixley on Syfy’s “Happy!” among others and has written and performed two original one woman comedy shows in NYC, “Glitter, Goblets, and Gatos”, and “Glitter, Goblets, and Gatos, Too-Electric Boogaloo” at Feinsteins/54 below.

She is a graduate of the Cincinnati College-Conservatory of Music and resides in New York City.
