The currency in Slovakia is the euro. Each euro is divided into 100 cents.

When you’re buying currency for Slovakia, look out for the currency code EUR. And once you’re in Slovakia, you’ll see the symbol € used to show prices.

You’ll find Euro banknotes in denominations of 5, 10, 20, 50, 100, 200, and 500 - although the 200 and 500 EUR notes are seldom used. There are also 1 and 2 euro coins.

Cents come in coins of 1, 2, 5, 10, 20 and 50.
