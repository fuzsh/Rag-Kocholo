The Slovak crown (Sk)

As of 1 January 2009 Euro (EUR) replaced former Slovak currency, Slovak crown (Sk), and is the official currency in use.

The official conversion rate is 1 EUR equals 30.126 Sk.

1 Euro is divided into 100 Cents.

Coins

Euro coins appear in denominations of 1, 2, 5, 10, 20, 50 Cents and 1, 2 EUR.

Notes

Euro notes appear in denominations of EUR 5, 10, 20, 50, 100, 200, 500.
