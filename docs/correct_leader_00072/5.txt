On the morning of June 14, the University of Finance - Marketing (Ministry of Finance) coordinated with the Finance College of Southern Laos to organize an international scientific conference: Ho Chi Minh's thought and Kaysone Phomvihane's thought on economic and financial development.

Associate Professor, Dr. Pham Tien Dat, Rector of University of Finance - Marketing and Mr. Inthapattha Somboun, Chief of Office of the Lao Ministry of Finance co-chaired the workshop.

The presentations at the conference affirmed that, 61 years since the two countries established friendly relations (September 9, 1962) and 9 years of signing the Treaty of Friendship and Cooperation (July 2023, 46, July 18, 7), the two countries of Vietnam and Laos have created a long history and great achievements. The presentations highlighted the role of President Ho Chi Minh and President Kaysone Phomvihane in the friendly relationship between the two countries, the profound influences of Ho Chi Minh's thought and Kaysone Phomvihane's thought when used to build and develop the Vietnam - Laos friendship relationship more and more sustainable.

Associate Professor, Dr. Pham Tien Dat, Rector of University of Finance - Marketing spoke at the seminar.

Dr. Lai Van Nam, Dean of the Faculty of Political Theory, University of Finance and Marketing, said: "President Ho Chi Minh attaches great importance to the long-term and sustainable friendship and solidarity between the two countries, considering it a vital issue for the upward development process of each country. At the same time, it is an issue that is both international and national, showing the profound human value of "helping you is helping yourself". The Vietnam-Laos friendship relationship will forever be an invaluable asset and an indispensable luggage of the two nations on the road to national construction and construction.

At the workshop, the presentations also outlined great achievements in many fields: political relations, defense cooperation, security, investment, trade, cooperation in the fields of transportation, agriculture, forestry and rural development, education and training, human resource development, multilateral cooperation... that the two countries have achieved from the Vietnam - Laos friendship relationship. In the current context, Ho Chi Minh's thought and Kaysone Phomvihane's thought have great theoretical and practical significance for each country of Vietnam and Laos, as well as for the friendly relationship of the two countries.

Delegates presented their presentations at the conference.

Presenting the presentation "Kaysone Phomvihane Thoughts on Innovation in the Current Economic Field", Master Doansouvanh Lamphoun, Director of the Kaysone Phomvihane Thought Research Center, National Academy of Politics and Administration, emphasized that: Kaysone Phomvihane's thought continues to be creatively applied in current practice. In which, focusing on solutions to economic structural reform, wage strategy reform, continuing to orient sustainable trade development, well implementing the monetary strategy, strengthening human resource training in economic management, opening up and international cooperation... International cooperation plays a very important role in current practice, including the Vietnam-Laos friendship relationship.

Associate Professor, Dr. Nguyen Thi My Linh, Deputy Dean, in charge of the Faculty of Finance and Banking, University of Finance and Marketing, also proposed many solutions to creatively apply Ho Chi Minh's thought in accordance with the current economic development context. In particular, it is necessary to focus on perfecting and improving the quality of the socialist-oriented market economy institution, better resolving the relationship between the State, the market and society, developing science, technology and innovation in order to create breakthroughs to improve the productivity, quality, efficiency and competitiveness of the economy. Along with that, speeding up industrialization, modernization and economic restructuring in association with innovating the growth model, ensuring the substance and efficiency, combined with the development of the digital economy to promote fast and sustainable growth.

University of Finance - Marketing presented souvenirs to the delegation attending the seminar of Laos.

Speaking at the seminar, Associate Professor, Dr. Pham Tien Dat, Rector of the University of Finance - Marketing affirmed: President Ho Chi Minh and President Kaysone Phomvihane are the two leaders who laid the first bricks for the two countries to officially establish diplomatic relations later. The conference organizers were very pleased when the panelists shared their views, comments, and proposed solutions with high theoretical and practical value, closely following the topic of the seminar. At the same time, the seminar also received many presentations from scientists, researchers, teachers, students from a number of universities and colleges in Vietnam and Laos. The issues and lessons learned at the seminar will be applied to the practical implementation of the educational and training tasks of the two universities, especially contributing to the training of financial and economic human resources to continue to foster the close relationship between the two countries.
