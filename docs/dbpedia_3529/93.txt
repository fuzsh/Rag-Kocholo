About

Swiss right winger who was chosen by the San Jose Sharks 9th overall in the 2015 NHL Draft.

Before Fame

On the Elite Junior A level, he played for Switzerland's Rapperswil-Jona Lakers ice hockey club. He went on to play several seasons for the Quebec Major Junior Hockey League's Halifax Mooseheads team.

Trivia

In 2013, he played for the Switzerland U18 national team.

Family Life

He spent his youth in Herisau, Switzerland, and later lived in both Canada and the United States.
