The Rapperswil-Jona Lakers are a professional ice hockey club from Rapperswil, Switzerland and are members of the Swiss National League A. The Lakers were founded in 1945 and were known as SC Rapperswil-Jona until 2005, when the club changed to its present name. They play their home games at Diners Club Arena.

They have survived relegation in every NLA season since last making the playoffs in 2007-08.
