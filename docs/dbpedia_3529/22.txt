Amanda Wick, wife of Jeremy Wick, who plays in Switzerland for the Rapperswil-Jona Lakers in the National League, and I sit down and have a conversation on all things traveling with kids. In this episode, we each share all the tips and advice to hopefully help you prepare as best as possible.

In this episode we cover:

Ages 0-5 years old. We break down what you need to bring with you and what you can leave behind

Where you should be sitting on the plane according to your kids age

What jet lag looks like and what you can do to stay sane

Whether you should purchase an extra seat

Whether you should bring a car seat on board

Kids 2 and over having to wear a mask on the plane

and more!
