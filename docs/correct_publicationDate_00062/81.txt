Leiber, Fritz

THE WANDERER

New York: Walker and Company, 1970. First hardcover edition. Hardcover. Fine / Fine. Item #34620

Inscribed by Leiber on the title page, "For Jan & George O'Nale, with all best wishes, Fritz Leiber, Oct 29, 1982." Jan and George O'Nale were publishers of Cheap Street Press. Their books were renowned as excellent examples of the book-making arts. In 2002, the O'Nales donated their collection of books and press materials to Tulane University, and then committed suicide in the spring of 2003. Hugo Award winner, 1965. "Superior Leiber." Barron, Anatomy of Wonder 1987, *3-253. Magill, Survey of Science Fiction Literature pp 2396 - 2401. Fine in fine and bright dust jacket with two tiny closed tears.
