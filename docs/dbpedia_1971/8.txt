Nusrat Fateh Ali Khan was born on 13 October 1948 in Lyallpur, Punjab, Pakistan [now Faisalabad, Pakistan]. He was a music artist and composer, known for 2012 - Das Ende der Welt (2009), Blood Diamond (2006) and Natural Born Killers (1994). He was married to Naheed Nusrat. He died on 16 August 1997 in London, England, UK.
