Sensenich Propeller manufactures and services fixed pitch wood, aluminum, and composite ground adjustable propellers for airboats, aircraft, and UAVs. Their product line includes high-performance airboat propellers designed for direct drive and reduction unit applications, certificated aircraft propellers made from aluminum, wood, and carbon fiber, and propellers engineered for small to medium size UAVs. The company offers custom propeller solutions for light aircraft, tactical and small UAVs, high-speed airboats, high-performance fans, and unique concepts, tailored to specific mission parameters. Sensenich Propeller has a rich history dating back over 80 years and is based in Plant City, Florida.
