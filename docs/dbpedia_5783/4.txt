Iban Language

The Iban are divided into different sub-ethnic groups. Each of them speak in different dialects. The most formal, and widely use dialect is the Saribas (mainly Betong and Saratok), others such as Balaus, Sebuyaus, Ulu Ai, or Rejangs, which are commonly use throughout Sarawak region.
