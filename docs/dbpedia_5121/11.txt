Irving Lambert: Sam’s boss and mentor, Lambert is the director of Third Echelon. He appears in most of the games in the series, providing guidance to Sam and overseeing his missions.

Story Line

The story of Tom Clancy’s Splinter Cell follows the adventures of Sam Fisher, a former Navy SEAL who becomes a highly trained covert operative working for a secret division of the NSA called Third Echelon. Sam’s main mission is to protect American interests and to neutralize any threats to national security.

Throughout the series, Sam faces a number of enemies and adversaries, including terrorist organizations, rogue governments, and criminal masterminds. He is equipped with the latest in high-tech gadgets and weaponry, and is trained in a variety of techniques, including stealth, close combat, and infiltration.

Over the course of the series, Sam must navigate complex political landscapes and work with a cast of recurring characters, such as his boss Irving Lambert and technical expert Anna Grímsdóttir, to complete his missions. Along the way, he must confront personal demons and grapple with the moral implications of his actions.

The series takes place in various locations around the world, from the streets of Washington D.C. to the jungles of Indonesia. As the series progresses, the stakes become higher and the threats become more dire, as Sam works to protect the United States and its citizens from various dangers.
