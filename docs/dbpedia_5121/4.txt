Tom Clancy's Splinter Cell® introduces a new type of game to the lineup: a third-person stealth action game with cyber-terrorism, shadow agencies and covert operations on the menu. Take control of Sam Fisher, a field operative of a secretive "black-ops" NSA sub-agency called THIRD ECHELON. Alone, yet armed with some of the best high-tech weapons on the market, Sam Fisher is geared up to infiltrate and neutralize the enemy, all without leaving a trace. Sam is sent into action when two CIA agents mysteriously disappear in Azerbaijan…
