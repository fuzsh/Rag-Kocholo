Shadeed is a 1982 horse that was born in 1982 and raced in 1985. He was sired by Nijinsky out of the Damascus mare Continual. He was trained by Sir Michael Stoute and raced for Sheikh Hamdan bin Rashid Al Maktoum, and was bred in Kentucky, United States by [Add Data]. Shadeed has raced and won at Aqueduct, Epsom-GB, Newmarket-GB. His has a stakes win at the 1985 English 2000 Guineas (G1).
