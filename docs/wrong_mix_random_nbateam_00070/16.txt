Mary J. Blige is a nine-time Grammy Award-winning singer-songwriter and actress. Since 1992, she has released 13 albums, four of which topped the Billboard 200 albums chart. As an actress, Blige has starred in the film I Can Do Bad All by Myself, guest starred on the series Empire and How to Get Away with Murder and played the Wicked Witch of the West on NBC's The Wiz Live! She next stars in the film Mudbound.
