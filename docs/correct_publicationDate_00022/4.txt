John Herbert Varley (b. 9 August 1947) is an American science fiction author.

Biography[]

In March 2021, Varley was rushed to the hospital for an emergency quadruple bypass. A successful GoFundMe campaign raised $42,028 USD to help cover the costs of recovery and other bills that would be encountered in the aftermath. Spider Robinson wrote an essay to help support Varley.[1]
