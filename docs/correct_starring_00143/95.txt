8. Square Pegs (1982)

The series captivated audiences with its candid discussions about sex and relationships, earning critical acclaim and a devoted fanbase. Sarah Jessica Parker’s portrayal of Carrie became synonymous with the show’s bold and glamorous appeal, solidifying its status as a cultural phenomenon.
