Biography

James was born in about 1615 (age 8 at the 1623 Wiltshire Visitation[1]), the son of Edward Goddard and Priscilla. [2]

He married Elinore Long, daughter of George Long a London merchant. [3] [4]

James and Elinor settled at South Marston. The had at least eight children baptised there:

Thomas (1657) [5]

James (1658/59) [6]

Anne (1659/60) [7]

Ellen (1661) [8]

Mary (1664) [9]

Judith (1666) [10]

Elizabeth (1669) [11]

Martha (1673) [12]

James died in Jun 1678 and was buried at South Marston, Wiltshire on 22 Jun 1678. [13]
