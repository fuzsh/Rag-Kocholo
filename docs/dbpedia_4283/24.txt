Heliotropium curassavicum is a broadleaf semi-evergreen perennial with green foliage. In summer white flowers emerge. Grows well with sun - mostly sun and regular water. Does well in average, gritty and well-drained soil. A good option if you're seeking something seaside / salt tolerant.

CHARACTERISTICS OF Heliotropium curassavicum

Plant type: perennial

Plant family: boraginaceae

Foliage: semi-evergreen green

Mature size: 12 IN - 18 IN - wide, 12 IN - 18 IN - tall

Flowers: white blooms in summer

GROWING CONDITIONS for Heliotropium curassavicum

USDA Zones: 6a - 10b

Sun exposure: sun - mostly sun

Watering frequency: regular

Resistant to: seaside / salt

Soil needs: average, gritty and well-drained
