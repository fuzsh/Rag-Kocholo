Al Argan International Real Estate Company - K.S.C.P. operates as a real estate development company in Kuwait and internationally. It operates through Real Estate Investment, Contracting, and Hotels and Resorts segments. The company engages in owning, buying, managing, and selling of real estates and lands; conducting real estate studies and consulting activities; performing maintenance work related to buildings and real estates, including maintenance work, execution of civil, mechanical, electrical, elevators work, and air conditioning; organizing real estate exhibitions; and import and export activities.
