About Contractor (Company)

Since ALARGAN International Real Estate Company was established in 2004, we’ve become one of the GCC region’s most innovative real estate developers, focused on sustainable residential and commercial buildings that support an economically and socially viable urban environment, and a shareholding company listed on the Kuwait Stock Exchange. ALARGAN started as a family-owned contracting company specializing in residential projects with a focus on the construction of middle income housing in Kuwait, and today, along with our subsidiaries and a solid business model, we significantly provide a wide range of real estate products and services in property management, contracting, landscaping, architecture, project management and hospitality.
