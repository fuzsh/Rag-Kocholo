The Holy Roman Empire is one of the 42 factions of the Medieval Conquest. It located on the center and top of Europe. Holy Roman Empire was ruled by Kaiser Richard of Cornwall. The capital of the Holy Roman Empire is Frankfurt. The claimant to the throne was Emperor Ferry of Upper Lorraine. Its bordered the Kingdom of France in the west, the Kingdom of Denmark in the north, the Polish Principalities and the Kingdom of Bohemia in the east, and the territories of Guelphs and Ghibellines. And in the southeast is the Kingdom of Hungary.

At the start of the game, the Holy Roman Empire is at peace, and soon in the near future, the Holy Roman Empire declared war random factions in Italy, and it's neighbors.

History[]

After the deposition of Frederick II by Pope Innocent IV in 1245, Henry Raspe, Landgrave of Thuringia was set up as anti-king to Frederick's son Conrad IV (d. 1254). Henry was killed in 1247 and succeeded as anti-king by William of Holland (died 1256). After 1257, the crown was contested between Richard of Cornwall, who was supported by the Guelph party, and Alfonso X of Castile, who was recognized by the Hohenstaufen party but never set foot on German soil.

While the Holy Roman Empire is height of the interregnum, however during the term Great Interregnum is occasionally used for the period between 1250 (death of Frederick II) and 1273 (accession of Rudolf I).
