NOVATO

A talk on protecting yourself from insurance fraud will be presented by the Marin County District Attorney’s Office’s insurance faud unit from 10 a.m. to noon Jan. 23 at the Margaret Todd Senior Center, 1560 Hill Road in Novato.

Inspectors Mike Miller and Jack Cooper will outline ways seniors can protect themselves against insurance fraud. Fraud awareness booklets will be provided by the state Department of Insurance, and there will be a question-and-answer session.

There is a $5 entrance fee payable at the door. Call 899-8290 to reserve a space or for more information.
