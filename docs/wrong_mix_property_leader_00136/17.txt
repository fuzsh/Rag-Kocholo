When Louis Botha Van Rooyen was born on 21 October 1900, in Natal, South Africa, his father, Johannes Jacobus Van Rooyen, was 46 and his mother, Jacoba Catharina Scheepers, was 44. He married Isabella Elizabeth Bosch about 1925, in Western Cape, South Africa. He died on 10 June 1965, in his hometown, at the age of 64.
