Jon Olav Fosse won the Nobel Prize in Literature 2023 for his innovative plays and prose which give voice to the unsayable.

The first Nobel Prize was awarded in the year 1901.

Nobel Prizes are awarded in six different categories Physics, Chemistry, Physiology or Science, Literature, Peace, and Economic Sciences.
