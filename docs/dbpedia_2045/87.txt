Tone Zgonc, also known by his partisan name Vasja, was a Slovenian military officer who held the rank of general of the Yugoslav People's Army. He was born on December 26, 1914, in Draga pri Šentrupertu, Slovenia. Zgonc played a significant role in the military during the Yugoslav period and is remembered for his contributions as a senior officer. He passed away on January 1, 2002, in Trebnje.
