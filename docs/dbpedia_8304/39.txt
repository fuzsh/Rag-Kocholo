William P. Lamson, 49, of Berlin, NH, formerly on Foxboro, MA, passed away on Tuesday November 12, 2013. He was a quiet man with a secret heart of gold who lived his life as he passed. He is survived by his parents George and Judith Lamson; his sister and brother-in-law Lori and Brian MacDonald; his brother and sister-in-law Brian and Sibyl Lamson; three nieces and one nephew. A Private Service will be held at a later date. There will be no calling hours. The Bryant Funeral Home is in charge of the arrangements.
