Heidi Dawn Fisher, 49, of New Lexington, Ohio died Monday, September 11, 2023

Born May 13, 1974 in Zanesville, Ohio to David and Marsha Spicer Potter.

Heidi was a 1992 graduate of New Lexington High School where she was a cheerleader. She loved her children and was very excited to be a grandmother. She was always willing to help with community fundraisers, worked concession stands for football, wrestling and baseball games, she enjoyed watching wrestling and she loved her dog.

In addition to her parents, she is survived by her husband, Brian Fisher; children, Tanner (Breanna) Walker and future grandson in April, Traven (Erika) Walker and Teryn Walker; step children, Katelynn Fisher, Shaley Fisher and Megan Fisher; step grandchildren, Kaden, Colten, Ryley and Camila; brother, Steven (Jenny) Garner; sister-in-law, Tara (Doug) Archer; nieces, Kessa Garner and Kelsey (Blain) Ruyf; Nephew, Alex Garner; grandma Debbie and several cousins.

Preceded in death by her first husband, Terry Walker; grandparents, Floyd and Wanda Spicer, John and Hazel Potter.

Calling hour will be held from 4-8 Pm on Friday, September 15, 2023 and from 10am-11am on Saturday at the Roberts-Winegardner Funeral Home, 304 Mill Street, New Lexington, Ohio.

Funeral service will be held at 11:00 on Saturday, September 16, 2023 at the funeral home with Pastor Jeff Gilkerson officiating.

Burial will follow in New Lexington Cemetery, New Lexington, Ohio.
