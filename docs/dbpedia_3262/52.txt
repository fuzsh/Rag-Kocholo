A man died following a two-vehicle crash in Pickaway County.

The crash happened in the area of US 23 and Tarlton Road just south of Circleville on Thursday, according to a statement from the Pickaway County Sheriff's Office.

The sheriff said 44-year-old Abigal Dirmeyer was driving north on US 23 when 65-year-old Brenda Fisher failed to yield at an intersection, striking Dirmeyer with her car.

Dirmeyer was rushed to Ohio Health Berger Hospital and Fisher was air-lifted to Grant Medical Center.

A passenger in Fisher's car was rushed toOhio Health Berger Hospital, where he later died, according to the statement.

Seventy-three-year-old David Fisher was identified as the passenger, according to the statement.
