In 1976 His Majesty King Fouad II of Egypt [‏الملك أحمد فؤاد الثاني‎ al-Malik Ahmad Fu’ad ath-thani, al-Malik Aḥmad Fuʾād aṯ-ṯānī] married Mlle Dominique-France Picard in a civil ceremony.

On 5th October 1977 the royal couple wed in a religious ceremony that took place in the Palace of H.S.H. Prince Rainier III of Monaco. He and his wife, H.S.H. Princess Grace Patricia attended the wedding. Equally present were the King's mother, H.M Queen Nariman, his sister, H.R.H. Princess Fawzia and the bride's mother.
