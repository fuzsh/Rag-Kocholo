Tenerife, Spain

Latitude and longitude coordinates are: 28.291565, -16.629129.

Tenerife is a very famous and popular resort island known also as Santa Cruz de Tenerife, one of seven Canary Islands, Spain. It is located right near the western coast of Africa and is considered one of the most modern and comfortable resort islands, as well as the largest island of Macaronesia. There are plenty of hotels and camps for various kinds of budgets. Local beaches, restaurants, entertainment facilities, and other attractions are among the best in Europe. The economy of the island is based on agriculture and tourism. The island is home to Hospital Universitario de Canarias, the University of La Laguna, etc.

Tenerife hosts the Carnival of Santa Cruz de Tenerife each February and attracts people all around the world. It is considered the second most popular carnival internationally. Tenerife North–Ciudad de La Laguna Airport is located in San Cristóbal de La Laguna. And Tenerife South Airport is the largest one on the island and situated in Granadilla de Abona. You can visit Teide National Park that is a UNESCO World Heritage Site situated in the center of Tenerife. Other places you can visit are Volcan El Teide, Siam Park, Playa del Duque, Parque Rural de Anaga, Playa de las Teresitas, and many more. The island is also a very popular filming location. Jason Bourne, Fast & Furious 6, Clash of the Titans, Wrath of the Titans are some of the films made in Tenerife.
