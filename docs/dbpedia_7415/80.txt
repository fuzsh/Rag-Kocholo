John Cowsill was born on 2 March 1956 in Newport, Rhode Island, USA. He is an actor, known for Tommy Tutone: Which Man Are You (1982), Tommy Tutone: 867-5309/Jenny (1981) and Gollum's American Cousin (2015). He has been married to Victoria Peterson since 25 October 2003. He was previously married to Valerie Ann Poulos and Jan Allison Rose.
