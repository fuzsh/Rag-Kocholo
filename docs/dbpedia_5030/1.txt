The Transports publics fribourgeois are the lead actor in mobility in the canton of Fribourg (Switzerland), operating a railway network, a regional bus network and two urban bus networks. This one company carries more than 80% of passengers throughout the canton of Fribourg. Consequently, mobility is the key point of the TPF group since 1868. Today it covers the entire canton of Friborg, with its trains, regional buses and urban lines. To be even closer to its users, the TPF group transforms its stations into places of residence and activity, and invests in innovation. It is organized as a holding company to ensure transparency on its activities and financing.
