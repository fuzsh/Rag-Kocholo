Jari Juha Tolsa (born April 20, 1981, in Gothenburg, Sweden) is a professional Swedish-Finnish ice hockey left winger currently playing for Linköpings HC in the Elitserien.

He was chosen 120th overall by the Detroit Red Wings in the 1999 NHL Entry Draft but never played for the team.
