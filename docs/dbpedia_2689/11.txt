Marcus Aurelius Cotta Maximus Messalinus was a Roman that lived in the 1st century. He served as a senator during the Julio-Claudian dynasty and became consul in 20. He was the great-uncle to brief Empress Lollia Paulina, who was Emperor Caligula’s third wife and her sister Lollia Saturnina. Messalinus was the brother to Paulina’s paternal grandmother.

Source

Tacitus, The Annals of Imperial Rome
