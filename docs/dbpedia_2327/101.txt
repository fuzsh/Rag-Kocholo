John Vesey served as archbishop of Tuam, Galway, Ireland, and Lord Justice of Ireland. Anne Muschamp was his second wife, with whom he had 5 sons and 5 daughters.

Most Rev. John Vesey was born in 1638.

He was the son of Venerable Thomas Vesey

He and Rebecca Wilson obtained a marriage license in 1662.

He married, secondly, Anne Muschamp, daughter of Colonel Agmondisham Muschamp

He died on 28 March 1716.

Most Rev. John Vesey graduated with a Doctor of Divinity (D.D.). He held the office of Archbishop of Tuam. He held the office of Lord Justice [Ireland]. He was invested as a Privy Counsellor (P.C.).
