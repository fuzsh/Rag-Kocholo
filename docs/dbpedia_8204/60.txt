Ajinomoto is like a salt which has its unique and special flavor. It appears to be like crystal. It is used in Chinese food. Ajinomoto is also known as monosodium glutamate or MSG. It is used as seasoning to enhance the flavor of dishes.

It is easily available with big glossary stores. There is a myth that pregnant women and children should not be allowed to have ajinomoto.
