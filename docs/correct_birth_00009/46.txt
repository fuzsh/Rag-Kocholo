The Eisenhower Birthplace State Historic Site is situated at 609 S. Lamar Avenue in Denison, Grayson County, in the U.S. state of Texas. This is the exact location where President Dwight D. Eisenhower was born, making it a significant historical site.

Historical Significance of the Birthplace

President Dwight D. Eisenhower, who was born in the house on October 14, 1890, holds the distinction of being the first United States President to be born in Texas. This fact adds to the historical significance of the site.

Establishment and Restoration of the Museum

The museum at the Eisenhower Birthplace State Historic Site was established in 1955. After Eisenhower was elected President, the Eisenhower Foundation was formed in 1953 to oversee the restoration of the site. This ensures that visitors can experience the site as it was during Eisenhower's time.
