Body fluids:

The liquids that are present inside the living body and are and place an important role in our bodies.

Types of body fluids:

Urine: is a liquid that is released from the excretory system of an organism and it eliminates chemicals and unwanted materials from the body.

Blood: contains plasma as well as formed elements.

The formed elements include red blood cells white blood cells and platelets.

The blood is responsible for the transport of materials and gases.

They are also responsible for carrying the waste away from the body tissue and transporting it to the excretory system.
