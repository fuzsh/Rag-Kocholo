Joe Jonas is singer-songwriter and actor who started his career as a member of the Jonas Brothers alongside brothers Nick and Kevin. The group starred in the Disney Channel movies Camp Rock and Camp Rock 2, as well on the Disney Channel series Jonas LA and Jonas Brothers: Living the Dream. They topped the Billboard 200 albums chart twice, with 2008's A Little Bit Longer and 2009's Lines, Vines and Trying Times and scored a Top 5 single on the Hot 100 with 2008's "Burnin' Up." In 2015, Jonas formed DNCE with Jack Lawless, JinJoo Lee and Cole Whittle. The band's debut single, "Cake by the Ocean," reached No. 9 on the Hot 100. Their self-titled album was released in 2016. In 2019, Jonas reunited with his brothers for the latest Jonas Brothers album, Happiness Begins, which debuted at No. 1 on the Billboard 200 chart. Most recently, the Jonas Brothers announced their Las Vegas residency. Additionally, Joe Jonas will star in the brand-new MTV music competition series Becoming a Popstar, set to premiere March 2022.
