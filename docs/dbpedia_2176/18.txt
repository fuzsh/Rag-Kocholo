The Italian Pagan folk band Daridel has released a video from their live acoustic performance in the woods for Aequanox Festival.

Marking the Spring Equinox, the event took place online in March 14, having the band playing their songs amidst nature.

The group is currently writing compositions for their next album, the follow-up to the release of PaganRoots in December 2019. You can order it from the band’s official web store.
