Random Cannesdom #3

An interesting film which has quite clearly faded into obscurity. Directed by none other than Serge Gainsbourg, Équateur presents the brutal reality of French colonial exploits in Africa - oh, but with a sex scene sprinkled in every ten minutes or so (I mean it is Serge Gainsbourg). It's a miracle this isn't worse than it is as it could easily come across as some bungled white-saviour bullshit or a misogynistic mess (I mean it is Serge Gainsbourg). But, in its unfaltering condemnation of those power-tripping white colonisers, Équateur feels rightfully angry and subtly radical.
