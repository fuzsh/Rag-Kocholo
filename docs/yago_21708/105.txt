Articles English: Charles Emmanuel III of Sardinia

Charles Emmanuel III Savoy of Sardinia, King of Sardinia, Duke of Savoy, was born 27 April 1701 in Turin, Italy to Victor Amadeus II of Sardinia (1666-1732) and Anne Marie d'Orléans (1669-1728) and died 20 March 1773 Turin, Italy of unspecified causes. He married Anna Christine Luise von Pfalz-Sulzbach (1704-1723) 15 March 1722 in Vercelli, Piedmont, Italy. He married Polyxene Christine Johannetta von Hessen-Rheinfels-Rotenburg (1706-1735) 20 August 1724 in Thonon-les-Bains, Auvergne-Rhône-Alpes, France. He married Elisabeth Therese von Lothringen (1711-1741) 1737 .
