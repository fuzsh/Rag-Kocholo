Biography of Charles Emmanuel III King Sardinia 1701-1773

On 27 Apr 1701 Charles Emmanuel III King Sardinia was born to Victor Amadeus King Sardinia (age 34) and Anne Marie Bourbon Queen Consort Sardinia (age 31) at Turin.

On 12 Jun 1675 [his grandfather] Charles Emmanuel II Duke of Savoy (age 40) died. His son [his father] Victor Amadeus King Sardinia (age 9) succeeded Duke Savoy.

On 10 Apr 1684 [his father] Victor Amadeus King Sardinia (age 17) and [his mother] Anne Marie Bourbon Queen Consort Sardinia (age 14) were married. They were second cousins. She a granddaughter of King Charles I of England, Scotland and Ireland 1600-1649. She by marriage Duchess Savoy. She the daughter of Philip Bourbon I Duke Orléans (age 43) and Princess Henrietta Stewart Duchess Orléans (age 39).

Before 1723 Charles Emmanuel III King Sardinia (age 21) and Anne Christine Wittelsbach (age 18) were married. He a great grandson of King Charles I of England, Scotland and Ireland 1600-1649. He the son of Victor Amadeus King Sardinia (age 56) and Anne Marie Bourbon Queen Consort Sardinia (age 53).

In 1723 [his son] Vittorio Amedeo Theodore Savoy was born to Charles Emmanuel III King Sardinia (age 21) and [his wife] Anne Christine Wittelsbach (age 18).

On 12 Mar 1723 [his wife] Anne Christine Wittelsbach (age 19) died.

On 23 Jul 1724 Charles Emmanuel III King Sardinia (age 23) and Polyxena Hesse Rotenburg Queen Consort Sardinia (age 17) were married. He a great grandson of King Charles I of England, Scotland and Ireland 1600-1649. She by marriage Queen Consort Sardinia. He the son of Victor Amadeus King Sardinia (age 58) and Anne Marie Bourbon Queen Consort Sardinia (age 54).

In 1725 [his son] Vittorio Amedeo Theodore Savoy (age 2) died.

In 1726 [his son] Victor Amadeus III King Sardinia 1726-1796 was born to Charles Emmanuel III King Sardinia (age 24) and [his wife] Polyxena Hesse Rotenburg Queen Consort Sardinia (age 19).

In 1728 [his daughter] Eleonora Maria Teresa Savoy was born to Charles Emmanuel III King Sardinia (age 26) and [his wife] Polyxena Hesse Rotenburg Queen Consort Sardinia (age 21).

On 23 Jul 1728 Louis Henri Bourbon Condé Duke Bourbon 1692-1740 (age 35) and [his sister-in-law] Landgravine Caroline Hesse Rotenburg (age 13) were married. He a great x 3 grandson of King James I of England and Ireland and VI of Scotland 1566-1625. The difference in their ages was 22 years.

On 26 Aug 1728 [his mother] Anne Marie Bourbon Queen Consort Sardinia (age 58) died.

In 1729 [his daughter] Maria Luisa Gabriella Savoy was born to Charles Emmanuel III King Sardinia (age 27) and [his wife] Polyxena Hesse Rotenburg Queen Consort Sardinia (age 22).

In 1730 [his daughter] Maria Felicita Savoy was born to Charles Emmanuel III King Sardinia (age 28) and [his wife] Polyxena Hesse Rotenburg Queen Consort Sardinia (age 23).

In 1731 [his son] Emanuele Filiberto Savoy was born to Charles Emmanuel III King Sardinia (age 29) and [his wife] Polyxena Hesse Rotenburg Queen Consort Sardinia (age 24).

On 31 Oct 1732 [his father] Victor Amadeus King Sardinia (age 66) died. His son Charles Emmanuel III King Sardinia (age 31) succeeded Duke Savoy, III King Sardinia. On 31 Oct 1732 His grandson [his son] Victor Amadeus III King Sardinia 1726-1796 (age 6) succeeded III King Sardinia.

In 1733 [his son] Carlo Francesco Romualdo Savoy was born to Charles Emmanuel III King Sardinia (age 31) and [his wife] Polyxena Hesse Rotenburg Queen Consort Sardinia (age 26).

In 1733 [his son] Carlo Francesco Romualdo Savoy died.

In 1735 [his son] Emanuele Filiberto Savoy (age 4) died.

On 13 Jan 1735 [his wife] Polyxena Hesse Rotenburg Queen Consort Sardinia (age 28) died.

On 05 Mar 1737 Charles Emmanuel III King Sardinia (age 35) and Elisabeth Therese Lorraine Queen Consort Sardinia (age 25) were married. They were half first cousins. He a great grandson of King Charles I of England, Scotland and Ireland 1600-1649. She a great x 3 granddaughter of King James I of England and Ireland and VI of Scotland 1566-1625. She by marriage Queen Consort Sardinia. She the daughter of Leopold Duke of Lorraine 1679-1729 and Élisabeth Charlotte Bourbon Duchess Lorraine (age 60). He the son of Victor Amadeus King Sardinia and Anne Marie Bourbon Queen Consort Sardinia.

In 1738 [his son] Carlo Francesco Savoy was born to Charles Emmanuel III King Sardinia (age 36) and [his wife] Elisabeth Therese Lorraine Queen Consort Sardinia (age 26).

In 1741 [his son] Benedetto Maria Maurizio Savoy was born to Charles Emmanuel III King Sardinia (age 39) and [his wife] Elisabeth Therese Lorraine Queen Consort Sardinia (age 29).

On 14 Jun 1741 [his former sister-in-law] Landgravine Caroline Hesse Rotenburg (age 26) died.

On 03 Jul 1741 [his wife] Elisabeth Therese Lorraine Queen Consort Sardinia (age 29) died.

In 1742 [his son] Maria Vittoria Margherita Savoy (age 2) died.

In 1745 [his son] Carlo Francesco Savoy (age 7) died.

On 31 May 1750 [his son] Victor Amadeus III King Sardinia 1726-1796 (age 24) and [his daughter-in-law] Infanta Maria Antonia Spain (age 20) were married. They were third cousins. He a great x 2 grandson of King Charles I of England, Scotland and Ireland 1600-1649. She the daughter of Philippe V King Spain and Elisabeth Farnese Queen Consort Spain (age 57). He the son of Charles Emmanuel III King Sardinia (age 49) and Polyxena Hesse Rotenburg Queen Consort Sardinia.

In 1767 [his daughter] Maria Luisa Gabriella Savoy (age 38) died.
