Thomas Lane is a '24 from Excelsior, Minnesota. He is an Economics and German Studies double major interested in health policy. He is a Healthcare Foundations Society Member at the Dartmouth Institute for Health Policy and Clinical Practice, an opinion columnist for The Dartmouth, and an associate editor at the Dartmouth Law Journal. He has written legislation concerning maternal health and health insurance regulation for the Minnesota Legislature and spent time working with Physicians for a National Health Program's Minnesota and New Hampshire branches. After graduation he hopes to pursue a master's in health economics in northern Europe and/or law school. Outside of class he enjoys cross country skiing, hiking, and spoiling his two cats.
