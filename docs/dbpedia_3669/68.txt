Trevor Thompson MS,ATC is a certified athletic trainer physician extender working alongside Dr. Vishal Khatri specializing in spine surgery as an orthopedic surgeon.

Trevor received his post-professional M.S. in athletic training from Old Dominion University in 2019. There, he served as a graduate assistant athletic trainer with the football and wrestling teams. He received his bachelor’s degree in athletic training from Ithaca College in 2017, where he also played baseball. Prior to Alliance, Trevor’s clinical experiences include working as an athletic trainer at Yale University, the New York Jets, Baltimore Ravens and Rugby United New York.

Trevor has additional certifications from the in First Aid/AED/CPR for the professional rescuer, bloodflow restriction training, myofascial decompression and mulligan concept therapy. Outside of work, Trevor is an avid golfer.
