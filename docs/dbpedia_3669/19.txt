Trevor Thompson

Biography

Trevor Thompson is a journalist with 25 years experience who is currently Executive Producer of ABC Radio News in Sydney. He reported on the National Soccer League for twelve years for the national Grandstand program on ABC Radio and on several Australian World Cup and World Youth Cup campaigns for radio and television news and the Triple J network. He has worked as an reporter and commentator on football issues in Australia for BBC Radio and commented on football history matters for SBS Television. He has written and produced documentaries on sport, media and money for Radio National and currently reports on Australian and Asian football for Radio Australia.
