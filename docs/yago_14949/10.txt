Martin Millar (born Martin Majella Sean Millar on 9 May 1979 in County Armagh, Northern Ireland) is an electronic musician, record producer and composer.

Work

Millar currently programs and produces soundtracks for the film and animation production company Syncretik, while continuing to release his own solo material.

His film credits include Room to Let and Murphy 771.

He has also composed music for commercials Sleep Right and Speed Junkie 4 and web, Interactive CDrom and radio soundtracks for the NHS, BAE Systems, Saville Audio Visual, Nestl√© and EMAP publishing.
