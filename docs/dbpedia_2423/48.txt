About Gauhati University

Name : Gauhati University

State : Assam

Type : State Government

Intro : Gauhati University is located in Jalukbari, Guwahati, is oldest and most renowned University in North East India. It was established in 1948. It is a teaching-cum-affiliating university.

At present there are approximately 326 Colleges/Institutes affiliated to it, which are mostly located in Lower Assam.
