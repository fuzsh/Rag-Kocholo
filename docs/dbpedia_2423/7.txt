ABOUT

Gauhati University is a prestigious institution of higher education located in Guwahati, Assam, India. It is one of the oldest and largest universities in the northeastern region of India. The university is known for its rigorous academic standards, state-of-the-art facilities, and experienced faculty members. Gauhati University also promotes research and innovation, actively engaging in collaborations with national and international institutions. It plays a significant role in the intellectual, cultural, and socio-economic development of the region, producing graduates who contribute to society's growth and progress.
