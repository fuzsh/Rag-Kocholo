About

English comedian known for his comedy show, The Charlie Chester Show, that ran from 1949 to 1960. He was also a presenter for the Sunday Soapbox radio show.

Before Fame

He won several talent competitions when he was young by singing and playing instruments.

Trivia

He enjoyed painting in his spare time.

Family Life

He married Dorita Langley in 1939, and they had one son.
