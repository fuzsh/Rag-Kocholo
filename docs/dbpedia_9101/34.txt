Escuintla Geographic coordinates

Escuintla is located at latitude 14.3009 and longitude -90.78581. It is part of America and the northern hemisphere.

The geographic coordinate system enables any place in the world to be located using its latitude and longitude. The latitude is the position relative to the equator, specifying the north-south position. The longitude specifies the east-west position measured from a reference meridian (usually the Greenwich Prime Meridian). The latitude and longitude of Escuintla have been calculated based on the geodetic datum WGS84.
