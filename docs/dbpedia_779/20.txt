Surname Menke

Cognomen Menke (Menke , Менке , Мэнкэ ) , all over the world, is a common last name. The surname Menke is habitual in Suriname, where it is quite a common surname, Germany, and Netherlands, where it is quite a rare surname. In absolute terms, it most frequently occurs in Germany, the United States, and Netherlands. Very seldom, Menke is the forename as well as the last name.
