Dominic Michael Catrambone is an American actor, voice actor, and producer best known for providing the voice of Leonardo in season 2 of Nickelodeon's 2012 Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles animated series.

He provides additional voices in Star vs. the Forces of Evil.

Career[]

Catrambone is best known for voicing Leonardo in some season 2 episodes for Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles on Nickelodeon, serving as Jason Biggs' temporary replacement before being replaced himself by Seth Green in season 3. Catrambone also voiced Leonardo in the 2013 Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles video game and Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles: Danger of the Ooze.

Other voiceover roles of Catrambone's include Commander Sazabi, Ashuramaru, and Zako Red in SD Gundam Force, Micro-Ice in Galactik Football, Jackson Stewart in several Hannah Montana video games, Theo/Turbo in Turbo: Super Stunt Squad, and Sulk and Kevin in Dawn of the Croods.
