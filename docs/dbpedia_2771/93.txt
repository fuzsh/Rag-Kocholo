The focus of the project was to take a content heavy site with 3+levels of hierarchical content structure and create a lateral interconnected and light experience. Built with ExpressionEngine, the site is featuring an event feed with recurring events with multiple locations. The sermon section also feeds as iTunes Podcast.

Client: Fifth Avenue Presbyterian Church

Scope: Design & UX, Information Architecture, HTML/CSS/JS, ExpressionEngine

Year: Sep 18, 2016
