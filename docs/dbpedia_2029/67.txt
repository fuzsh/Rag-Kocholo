Sergio Osmeña (September 9, 1878 - October 19, 1961) was the fourth President of the Philippines, and the second President of the Commonwealth of the Philippines.

This album features photographs of President Osmeña before and during his presidency, which lasted from August 1, 1944 to May 28, 1946.

For more information on President Osmeña, please visit our page on the Presidential Museum and Library website: malacanang.gov.ph/presidents/commonwealth/sergio-osmena/
