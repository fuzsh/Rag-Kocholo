Paul Haarhuis (born February 19, 1966 in Eindhoven) is a former professional male tennis player from The Netherlands.

In June 1995 he reached his highest singles ranking World No. 18. He is best remembered for his success in doubles with fellow Dutchman Jacco Eltingh. In his career he has won one singles title and 54 doubles titles.
