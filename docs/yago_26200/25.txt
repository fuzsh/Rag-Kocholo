Louise-Élisabeth de Croÿ de Tourzel (Louise Élisabeth Félicité Françoise Armande Anne Marie Jeanne Joséphine de Croÿ de Tourzel) 11 June 1749 – 15 May 1832), the Marquise de Tourzel (later Duchess) was a French noblewoman and courtier to the royal court of King Louis XVI. She was the last governess to the royal children of Louis and his wife, Queen Marie Antoinette. Decades after the French Revolution, de Tourzel published widely-read memoirs, which presented a unique perspective on the royal family.
