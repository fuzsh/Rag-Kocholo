Sreelekha Mitra (Bengali: শ্রীলেখা মিত্র) is an Indian actress who is known for her work in Bengali cinema. She is best known for her performance in Bappaditya Bandopadhyay's Kantatar (2006) which won her [email protected] BFJA Award and an Anandalok Award.
