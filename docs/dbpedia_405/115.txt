Smith College is a private, nonprofit college in the town of Northampton, Massachusetts. Smith College was founded in 1871. The college enrolls around 2,873 students per year, including 2,523 undergraduates.

Smith College Acceptance Rate

It is very difficult to get into Smith College. The acceptance rate at Smith College is 23%. In 2022, 7,269 students applied to Smith College and 1,662 were admitted.
