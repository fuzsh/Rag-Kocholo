Dorothy K.

(Phillips) Josefus

Funeral Mass for Dorothy K. (Phillips) Josefus, 83, of Willoughby Hills, will be 10 a.m. Saturday at Immaculate Conception Church, 37940 Euclid Ave., Willoughby.

Mrs. Josefus died Nov. 15, 2006.

Born April 23, 1923, in Cleveland, she had been a Lake County resident for the past 40 years, living in Willoughby and Willoughby Hills.

She was a member of Immaculate Conception Church in Willoughby and the Holden Arboretum. She was also a volunteer at the Browning Senior Center. She enjoyed nature and taking walks.

Survivors are her husband of 59 years, Richard P. Josefus; daughter, Gail M. (George) Polinsky of Mentor; and sister, Florence (James) Jeromson of Aurora.

Her son, Joseph P. Josefus; and brother, Norman Phillips, are deceased.

Friends may call 4 to 8 p.m. Friday at the McMahon-Coyne-Vitantonio Funeral Home, 38001 Euclid Ave., Willoughby. Father Michael Troha will officiate the service. Burial will be in All Souls Cemetery in Chardon Township.
