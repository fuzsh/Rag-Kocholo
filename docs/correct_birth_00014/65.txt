Stevland Hardaway Morris (born as Stevland Hardaway Judkins, May 13, 1950, Saginaw, MI), known by his stage name Stevie Wonder, is an American singer-songwriter, multi-instrumentalist, record producer and activist. Blind since shortly after birth, Wonder signed with Motown's Tamla label at the age of eleven, and continues to perform and record for Motown to this day.
