Engaging and Empowering All Learners

At Horizon School Division, students will gain the knowledge and skills to be contributing citizens and the desire to develop as life-long learners.

Land Acknowledgement

Horizon School Division is located on the traditional territory of the Siksika, Kainai, Piikani, Stoney-Nakoda, Tsuut'ina people and the Metis Nation of Alberta District 1. As an act of reconciliation, we honour the diverse Indigenous people whose ancestors have marked this territory for centuries.
