North Central Area

Marino is in Dublin City Council’s North Central Area, from where services are co-ordinated.

Parliamentary Representatives

Marino is in the parliamentary constituency of Dublin Bay North. The constituency has 5 deputies (Teachtaí Dála, commonly known as TDs), who represent the constituency in Dáil Éireann.

Local Representatives

Local Councillors are elected by the community to decide how the council should carry out its various activities. They represent public interest as well as individuals living within the ward in which they are elected.
