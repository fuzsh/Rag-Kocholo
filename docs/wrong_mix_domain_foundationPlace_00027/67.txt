Matching Gift Program

The process is simple: ask if your company has a Matching Gift program. If so, obtain a matching gift form from your personnel office or matching gift coordinator, fill it out completely and send it along with your contribution to: Wiregrass Habitat for Humanity, P. O. Box 7002, Dothan, AL 36302. We will complete the form and submit it to your employer. It’s as easy as that!

Your gift and your company’s matching contribution will help Wiregrass Habitat for Humanity reach our goal of eliminating poverty housing. You can help make a difference–one house, one family at a time!
