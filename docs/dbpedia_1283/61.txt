Eyemart Express is the second largest independent, value focused optical retailer in the U.S. and seventh-largest overall operating in more than 35 states. The company, founded in 1990 by Dr. Doug Barnes, provides a broad selection of high quality private label and branded frames with convenient one-hour service in all locations at a very attractive price point. Eyemart co-locates with entrepreneurial doctors who independently conduct eye exams and sell contact lenses.

Eyemart Express is headquartered in Farmers Branch, Texas and operates under the Eyemart Express, Vision4Less, Eyewear Express, VisionMart Express and 20/20 Express trade names. FFL invested in Eyemart alongside the Barnes Family in December 2014.
