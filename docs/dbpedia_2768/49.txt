Museum Mile Festival offers free admission to some of the world’s finest art collections during extended evening hours – 6- 9 pm. The stretch of Fifth Avenue is home to eight participating institutions – The Metropolitan Museum of Art; Museum of the City of New York; Neue Galerie New York; Solomon R. Guggenheim Museum; Cooper Hewitt, Smithsonian Design Museum; The Jewish Museum; El Museo del Barrio; and The Africa Center.
