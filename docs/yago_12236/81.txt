"DUST" directed by Max Jacoby and produced by Pol Cruchten (Red Lion) is competing in the new Official Selection "FLASH FORWARD" at the PUSAN International Film Festival which takes place from 8 to 16 October 2009. "FLASH FORWARD" is the only competition at the Pusan Film Festival reserved for emerging non-asian director's (first or second feature films). DUST is one of 11 films competing for the Flash Forward award and a cash price of 20.000$!

DUST premiere in Luxembourg will be on on 27th November 2009.
