Beasley Media Group signed-off Business “Money Talk Radio” 740 WSBR Boca Raton and Brokered Haitian 980 WHSR Pompano Beach FL at midnight on Sunday.

WSBR’s “Money Talk Radio” branding and programming has been merged into Beasley’s Brokered Talk 1470 WWNN Pompano Beach/95.3 W237BD Boca Raton. In addition, WSBR’s two translators 96.9 W245BC Lauderdale Lakes and 103.9 W280DU Boca Raton are now rebroadcasting WWNN. The stations serve northern Broward County in the Miami market including Fort Lauderdale and southern Palm Beach County around Boca Raton.
