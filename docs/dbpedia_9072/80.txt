DJ Jazzy Joyce is an American hip hop DJ and producer formerly on New York City's radio station Hot 97. She is best known for her collaboration with rapper Sweet Tee on the 1986 single "It's My Beat".[1] In 1994, she was featured on "9th Wonder (Blakitolism)", the first single from "Blowout Comb", the second album from Grammy winning rap group Digable Planets. Joyce was also a feature DJ on the BET Series "Master of The Mix". With over Thirty years experience as a Top Tier DJ Joyce Has The Ability To Play For any Crowd in Any Demographic with any genre of music.
