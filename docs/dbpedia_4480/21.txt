Key Highlights

NetSuite Inc (NetSuite), a subsidiary of Oracle Corp, is a provider of enterprise resource planning (ERP) and omni-channel commerce software suites. The company offers application software for financial management, customer relationship management (CRM), professional services automation (PSA), ecommerce and retail management and human capital management. NetSuite also offers customer support and professional services pertaining to support and implementation of its applications. The company offers solutions to manufacturing, wholesale distribution, transport logistics, nonprofit, media, professional services, retail and software companies. NetSuite markets its products through direct and channel partners. The company has operations in Europe, Asia-Pacific, and the North America. NetSuite is headquartered in Austin, Texas, the US.
