Erik Skog is a management consultant, author and lecturer. Following a career in marketing and sales he has since 1992 in an owner role funded, bought, run and sold ten companies. In addition, he has been the chairman and investor in another handful of young, fast growing companies. He has also been active as a business angel. He is a graduate from the Stockholm School of Economics and has been Program Director at SSE Executive Education and Career Director for the SSE Full Time MBA. He has also been lecturing in the SSE Executive MBA. Besides lecturing, he is operational as Founding Partner of management consultancy firm Sahlgren & Skog.
