Peter Düttmann, nicknamed 'Bonifazius', was a notable German Luftwaffe fighter ace born on May 23, 1923, and he passed away on January 9, 2001. During World War II, he achieved 152 aerial victories in 398 missions on the Eastern Front. He joined the Luftwaffe in 1940 and became a pilot in Jagdgeschwader 52 in 1943. Düttmann received the Knight's Cross of the Iron Cross for his combat achievements and led the 5th squadron until the war's end.
