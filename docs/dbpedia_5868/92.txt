The 2003 film "Seabiscuit" is about the famous 1930's California racehorse, his owner Charles S. Howard (Jeff Bridges) and main jockey John "Red" Pollard (Tobey Maguire). The film opens with a description of the Great Depression of the 1930's.

Young Red Pollard and his father Mr. Pollard are living at a migrant camp. Red Pollard brings his father two silver dollars that he has earned working at a nearby horse track.

The silver dollars are Peace dollars, minted from 1921 to 1935.

1. Red gives the silver dollars to his father

Mr. Pollard asks his son where he got the money and Red replies that he earned them by doing jobs at a nearby horse track.
