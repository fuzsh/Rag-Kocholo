Isaac Hayes died of a stroke, its been announced. The singer and star of South Park was found dead on Sunday at his Memphis home. His family physician Dr David Kraus revealed that he had been treating Hayes for high blood pressure and gave his cause of death as a stroke. Now Isaacs family are making funeral arrangements with a service planned for Monday August 18 in Cordova, Tennessee. They also released a statement saying they had been: overwhelmed with the outpouring of support and love from Isaac's dear friends, colleagues and fans from every corner of the world, and we thank each and every one of them for their kind thoughts and prayers. Meanwhile plans are being made for a memorial service for comedian Bernie Mac- who also died at the weekend. The event will take place this Saturday in Chicago at the 10,000 capacity House Of Hope. Strangely Isaac and Bernie were set to star in new film Soul Men together.
