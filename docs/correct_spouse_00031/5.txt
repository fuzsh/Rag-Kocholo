Actress Hiroko Hatano, 32, has got married to soccer player Keita Suzuki, 27, of Japan's national team. Hatano is currently 2 months pregnant.

Hatano divorced actor Takashi Kashiwabara in 2006 after two years of marriage.

Hatono said in a statement released to the press, “I want to have a happy family life with the baby and Keita.” Suzuki also announced the marriage on his official blog the same day.
