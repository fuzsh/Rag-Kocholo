Quad City Times newspaper coverage of the death of Cary Grant.

Cary Grant was in Davenport, Iowa, to give a talk at the Adler Theater when he was stricken ill with a stroke. He was taken from his room 907 in the Blackhawk Hotel about at about 9:15 p.m. Saturday, November 29, 1986 with his wife, Barbara Harris, at his side. Paramedics took the 82-year-old actor to St. Luke's Hospital, where he died.
