Who is Claudio Bieler?

Claudio Bieler is 40-years-old, born on 01 March 1984, and is a professional football player from Argentina. Born in Vera, Claudio Bieler has also played in Primera B Nacional for Rafaela, in Copa Pilsener Serie A for Independiente, in Liga Profesional de Fútbol for San Martín TU, in Liga Profesional de Fútbol for Belgrano, in Liga Profesional de Fútbol for Quilmes and in MLS for Sporting KC. He currently plays as a Striker in Primera B Nacional for club Agropecuario.
