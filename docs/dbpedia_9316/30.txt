Wayne Anthony Pilarski, 63, of Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania, passed away on August 5, 2023, surrounded by his loved ones. Wayne was born on September 29, 1959, in Pittsburgh, PA. He was a dedicated husband, father, grandfather, and brother.

Wayne had a fulfilling career as a long-time pipe fitter and welder, most recently employed by PPT. He was known for his exceptional skills and commitment to his work.

Outside of his profession, Wayne had a variety of hobbies that brought him joy and relaxation. He enjoyed camping, fishing, and spending time with his family in the great outdoors. Wayne also had a passion for model cars and trains, and he took pride in his collection. He had a green thumb and loved taking care of his aloe plants. Wayne also found peace and serenity in maintaining his fish tank and engaging in handy work.

Wayne will be deeply missed by his beloved wife, Jocelyn (Cecere), their children, Rebecca, Wayne, Nichole, and Bryan, as well as his grandsons, Kyle and Aiden. He is also survived by his sister, Carol. Wayne's loving presence and caring nature will forever be cherished by his family and friends.

A memorial service will be held to honor Wayne's life, and details will be announced to the family's closest friends and relatives.
