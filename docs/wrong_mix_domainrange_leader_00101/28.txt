Former Mauritian President Anerood Jugnauth presented Mauritius's arguments at the International Court of Justice on Monday regarding the legality of Britain's claim to the Chagos Islands in the Indian Ocean, which house a major U.S. military base. Jugnauth said the country was unfairly pressured to cede control of the islands during the negotiations, and had agreed only to their temporary use for military purposes.
