Boston Tag & Label designs and manufactures tags, lapels and specialty items such as envelopes, blank and variable data forms, folders, silkscreen decals, nameplates, DOT and hazardous waste compliance labels. It serves clients in the areas of manufacturing, health care and hospitals, education, seafood wholesalers, retailers as well as hazardous waste transportation and generation. It utilizes synthetic materials such as Tyvek and Mylar.
