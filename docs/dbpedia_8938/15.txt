Aoyama Gakuin University (Japanese: 青山学院大学 Hepburn: Aoyama Gakuin Daigaku, AGU) is a private university in Shibuya, Tokyo, Japan. Originally established in 1874 by missionaries from the Methodist Episcopal Church, it was reconfigured in its current form in 1949 as part of Aoyama Gakuin. AGU celebrated its 140-year anniversary in 2014 and is one of Japan’s oldest higher education facilities.
