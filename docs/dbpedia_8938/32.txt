Aoyama Gakuin School Corporation (AGU) is a school in Japan with two campuses, one in Aoyama and one in Kanagawa Prefecture. The School Corporation has a long-term mission of Christian education in Japan with its goals and objectives to foster Global Leaders and Servant Leaders. Since 1874, the founding spirit of Aoyama Gakuin, which is based on the Christian faith, has succeeded in becoming a living tradition. As a comprehensive educational institution comprising schools from the kindergarten through the graduate school, Aoyama continues to implement various types of educational reforms aimed at character building in the truest sense so that their graduates can contribute to modern day society.
