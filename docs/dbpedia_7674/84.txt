Mike Wallace was born on 9 May 1918 in Brookline, Massachusetts, USA. He was a producer and actor, known for 60 Minutes (1968), Ilja Muromez (1956) and The Big Surprise (1955). He was married to Mary Yates, Lorraine Perigord, Buff Cobb and Norma Kaphan. He died on 7 April 2012 in New Canaan, Connecticut, USA.
