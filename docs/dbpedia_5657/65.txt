William A. Wellman’s fact-based drama about US aviation pioneer John J. Montgomery who battled the odds to build a successful glider. Starring Glenn Ford, Janet Blair and Charlie Ruggles.

The story concentrates on flight pioneer, John Montgomery (Glenn Ford). In 1883, he made a practical glider regardless of the opposition of his pals, who thought he was irrational, and of his family, who were concerned that his ambitions of flying would destroy his father’s political dreams.
