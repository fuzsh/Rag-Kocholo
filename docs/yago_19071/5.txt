On October 18, 2013, the law firm of Roetzel & Andress LPA presented Toledo Mayor Michael P. Bell with the “Downtown Toledo Appreciation Award” at an event held at Georgio's.

The Downtown Toledo Appreciation Award, given to honorees in acknowledgement of their commitment to support the business community and further develop downtown Toledo, was presented to Mayor Bell by Tony Calamunci, Partner-in-Charge of the firm’s Toledo office. “Our law firm services the business community and recognizes Mayor Bell for his achievements and commitment to further support business growth and development in Toledo,” remarked Calamunci upon presenting the award. “Business is the heart of Northwest Ohio and we need leaders like Mayor Bell to support and grow the business community for a better tomorrow.”

Mayor Bell accepted the award and thanked both Roetzel & Andress and Toledo’s business community for their continued support.
