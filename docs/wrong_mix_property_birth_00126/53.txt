When President William McKinley was born on 29 January 1843, in Niles, Trumbull, Ohio, United States, his father, William McKinley Sr., was 35 and his mother, Nancy Campbell Allison, was 33. He married Ida Saxton on 25 January 1871, in Canton, Stark, Ohio, United States. They were the parents of at least 2 daughters. He lived in Canton, Stark, Ohio, United States in 1880 and Canton Township, Stark, Ohio, United States in 1900. He died on 14 September 1901, in Buffalo, Erie, New York, United States, at the age of 58, and was buried in West Lawn Cemetery, Canton, Stark, Ohio, United States.
