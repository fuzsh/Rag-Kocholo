The Chinese Pavilion, or Kina Slott in Swedish, located in the grounds of Drottningholm Palace Park. Drottningholm Palace is the private residence of the Swedish royal family. It is built on the island Lovön (in Ekerö municipality of Stockholm county). This is one the places where the music video for “Bewitched” by Candlemass was filmed by famous director Jonas Åkerlund. In the scene you can see a young Pelle “Dead” Yngve Ohlin from Mayhem.

ADDRESS:

KINA SLOTT 3, 178 93 Drottningholm, Sweden
