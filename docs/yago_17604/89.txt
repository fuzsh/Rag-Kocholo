Ola Rapace is a Swedish actor who has appeared in such films as Skyfall and as well as the Crimes of Passion television movie series. He is the former spouse of actress Noomi Rapace. The two of them chose to take the surname "Rapace" together when the two were married, and have kept it despite now being divorced.
