Written by Bert Berns, Jerry Goldstein, Bob Feldman and Richard Gottehrer Date 1965 Source The Strangeloves Publisher Sloopy II Music & Sony/ATV Songs LLC (BMI)

"I Want Candy" was a song originally recorded by the Strangeloves, and later covered by artists such as Bow Wow Wow and Aaron Carter.

Cookie Monster parodied the song with "Me Want Cookie" in Make a New Friend.
