I Want Candy is a song written and originally recorded by The Strangeloves in 1965 by Bow Wow Wow in 1982 and most recently by Melanie C, ex Spice Girl, Aaron Carter covered the song as well in 2000, for his second album Aaron's Party (Come Get It). Veruca Salt covers the song on their 2007 spring tour. Good Charlotte has also covered the song.

It was originally out in 1965. It was most famously covered by the group Bow Wow Wow in 1982
