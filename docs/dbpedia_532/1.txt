Richard C. Henifin, 85 passed away in his home surrounded by immediate family

on December 19 th , 2023.

He was born on July 1 st , 1938, in Atkinson, Nebraska. Richard later moved to and

was raised in Bellingham, Washington. Richard moved to Redlands, CA as a

teenager with his mother. He then moved to Riverside, CA where he married his

wife, Lana Henifin, and proudly called Riverside his home for the rest of his life.

Richard was a loving husband, father, grandfather, and uncle. He is survived by

his daughter Allison Davis, his son- in -law Patrick Davis, and his grandson Ryan

Davis. His niece Sheila Mangum, her husband Scott Mangum and great nephew

Dashiel Mangum. Nephews, Christopher Baumstark and Scott Lee.

Richard was preceded in death by his mother Elsie Henifin, his father Elba

Henifin, his five siblings, his son Steven Henifin, and his devoted wife Lana

Henifin.

Richard was a hard worker; he was a Lather by trade in the field of construction.

Richard loved his family; his favorite sound was the sound of his home filled with

the voices and laughter of his loved ones. Richard loved to go to Vegas and the

Casino. After he retired, he spent his time creating and constructing projects to

complement the back yard.

Richard was one of a kind and will be deeply missed by many.

Graveside service will be on Wednesday, January 3, 2023 at 1:00pm at Olivewood Memorial Park.

Arrangements entrusted to the care of Akes Family Funeral Home, Riverside, California.
