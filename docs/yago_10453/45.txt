Val Doonican and his wife Lynn in supporting roles at the christening of baby Russell Beamont White. The baby is the son of actor Arthur White who shortly goes into pantomime as an Ugly Sister at Bromley, Kent. Lynn Doonican is one of the godparents. The service is at Saint Mary the Blessed Virgin, Addington, Surrey. Pictured, left to right, Val Doonican, Jean Michelle (sister of the baby's mother), actor Arthur White (father), Joy White (mother), actor David Jason (Godfather and brother of Arthur White) and Lynn Doonican. 1st December 1968.
