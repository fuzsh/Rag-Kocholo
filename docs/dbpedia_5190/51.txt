Where is Horicon, New York ?

Horicon is a town in Warren County, situated in the northeastern part of the state of New York, USA. It lies within the Adirondack Park, north of the town of Lake George, and west of the Hudson River. Horicon is also near other key Adirondack communities such as Bolton and Chester.
