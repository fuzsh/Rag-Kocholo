Sony Xperia P mobile was launched in May 2012. The phone comes with a 4.00-inch touchscreen display offering a resolution of 540x960 pixels at a pixel density of 275 pixels per inch (ppi). Sony Xperia P is powered by a 1GHz dual-core ST-Ericsson NovaThor U8500 processor. It comes with 1GB of RAM. The Sony Xperia P runs Android 2.3 and is powered by a 1305mAh removable battery.

As far as the cameras are concerned, the Sony Xperia P on the rear packs 8-megapixel camera. It sports a 0.3-megapixel camera on the front for selfies.

Sony Xperia P is based on Android 2.3 and packs 16GB of inbuilt storage. The Sony Xperia P is a single SIM (GSM) mobile that accepts a Micro-SIM card. The Sony Xperia P measures 122.00 x 59.50 x 10.50mm (height x width x thickness) and weighs 126.00 grams. It was launched in Black, Silver, and Red colours.

Connectivity options on the Sony Xperia P include Wi-Fi 802.11 b/g/n, GPS, Bluetooth v2.10, NFC, FM radio, and 3G. Sensors on the phone include accelerometer, ambient light sensor, gyroscope, proximity sensor, and compass/ magnetometer.

As of 18th August 2024, Sony Xperia P price in India starts at Rs. 21,490.
