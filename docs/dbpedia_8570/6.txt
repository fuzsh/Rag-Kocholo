Moloya Goswami is an Indian actress who works in the Assamese films. She was born at Dibrugarh. In the 39th National Film Awards 1992, she won the Best Actress award for her performance in the Firingoti. Moloya married Pradip Goswami in 1981. The couple has two little girls, Nimisha Goswami and Nishita Goswami , who is an actress too. Some of the films of Moloya are Agnisnaan, Maa, Sarbajan, Siraj, Uttarkaal, Firingoti, Daman: A Victim of Marital Violence, Konikar Ramdhenu, Jeevan Baator Logori, Srimanta Sankardeva, Poley Poley Urey Mon, Bhal Pabo Najanilu and Calendar.
