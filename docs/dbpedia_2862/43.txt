About AVS Science and Technology Society

Founded in 1953, the American Vacuum Society (AVS) promotes, encourages, and supports the advancement of the science and technology of materials, interfaces, and processing. Field-specific resources, in-depth research publications, a dynamic schedule of meetings and events, and a proactive community of peers define the AVS experience. Deepen your knowledge, join the collaborative space of professionals, and propel your career in vacuum sciences to new heights.
