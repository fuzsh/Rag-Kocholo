Description of The King of Fighters '95

The King of Fighters '95 is a video game published in 1996 on SEGA Saturn by SNK Corporation. It's an action game, set in an anime / manga, arcade, fighting, martial arts and versus fighting themes, and was also released on Arcade, Neo Geo and Neo Geo CD.
