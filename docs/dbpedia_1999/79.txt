Heather Spohr is a writer and editor whose award-winning blog, The Spohrs Are Multiplying, has been visited by more than 55 million people since 2002. She is a nationally recognized fundraiser for the March of Dimes, and the President and Co-Founder of Friends Of Maddie, a charitable organization that supports families after the death of a child. She has spoken at numerous conferences, on CNN, CBS, and HLN, and before members of Congress. When she's not writing, Heather enjoys sewing, going to the beach, and discovering new and exciting things to do in Los Angeles with her husband and children.
