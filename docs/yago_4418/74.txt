Alan Keen (born 25 November 1937) is a politician in the United Kingdom. He is Labour and Co-operative member of Parliament for Feltham and Heston. He is married to Ann Keen MP, and his sister-in-law is Sylvia Heal. He entered Parliament in 1992.

Keen worked for Middlesbrough FC for 18 years before entering Parliament. He is a board member of the Co-operative Enterprise Board.
