The Data and Analytics Team (D&A) at NBCUniversal is looking for a passionate data and analytics expert who’s looking to participate in NBCU’s D&A journey to the cloud. Reporting to the Principal of Data Management, the Sr Data Governance Engineer role is right for you if you would like to drive product usability and innovation in the Data Analytics space.

The D&A team is focused on data and analytics strategies for the future. We support NBCU’s vast portfolio of brands - from broadcast, cable, news, streaming service, and sports networks to film studios, world-renowned theme parks and a diverse suite of digital properties. We take pride in supplying our business groups with data to advise and shape strategic business decisions related to our content.

In the Senior Data Governance Engineer role, you’ll be working with internal stakeholders, data engineers, visualization experts, data stewards, data scientists, and other technologists across the business. This position requires a passionate technologist – if you’re someone with expertise in data analytics and can maintain the fine balance of business acumen and deep technical knowledge, you’ve come to the right place. Here you can drive product transformation. Join us!

Responsibilities:

Design, develop and implement high value, reusable and reliable [meta]data governance functionalities inside our data governance tools

Design, configure and develop the user interface screens to display customized form and script (BPMN) workflows

Implement data quality workflows

Collaborate, support and interact closely and intensively with Data Citizens (data owners, data quality analysts, data custodians, …)

Identify, investigate and correct system bugs and performance issues to ensure platform user friendliness (UX) and stability

Coordinate implementation of data quality monitoring tools

Coordinate data quality stewardship with data product and business teams

Design and implement standards for master data and reference data

Implement tagging rules and policies on Cloud platforms and Cloud databases

Implement GDPR,CCPA and other privacy regulations on the data governance and data quality tools

Promote data governance and quality at design

Build data governance metrics and KPI dashboards

Engage in architecture design sessions, code reviews, and change management process for the team

Establish & document data governance guidelines, best practices, and standards for the team

Engage in evaluation & integration of other cataloging tools as needed

Bachelor's degree in computer science in related discipline or equivalent

Experience or familiarity with at least one or more data governance tools (Collibra, Alation, Atlan etc)

Strong verbal and written communications, and interaction with peers, internal and external customers at all levels
