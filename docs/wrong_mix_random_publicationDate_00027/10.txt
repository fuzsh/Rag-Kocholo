American Idol winner Ruben Studdard has filed for divorce from wife Surata Zuri McCants.

The season two champion cited "irreconcilable differences" in his legal papers, according to the Daily Mail.

Studdard's laywer said: "Ruben Studdard and Zuri McCants Studdard have separated, and they are in the process of seeking a dissolution of their marriage.

"They each request the public respects their privacy during this trying time in their lives."

Studdard and McCants married in a ceremony attended by around 200 guests - including 20 groomsmen - near the singer's hometown of Birmingham, Alabama in 2008 after dating for two years.

At the time, he said: "God has given me a beautiful wife. I am looking forward to having fun with her and enjoying all the blessings that have come my way so far."
