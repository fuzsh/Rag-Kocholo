Bio:

Tupac Shakur (born Tupac Amaru Shakur in New York City, New York, on June 16, 1971), was an American rapper and actor. During his career and since his death, he has sold over 75 million albums worldwide. His double disc album All Eyez on Me is one of the best selling hip hop albums of all time. He died on September 13 1996 after being shot in a drive by shooting.

Best Known For:

Tupac Shakur is best known for being a rapper, and for being murdered at the height of his fame.
