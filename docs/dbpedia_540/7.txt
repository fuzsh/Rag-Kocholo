John H. Helfrich, 74, passed away April 13, 2017 at Woodhaven Care Center in Ellinwood, KS. He was born August 1st, 1942 to Anthony G. Sr., and Rita M. Helfrich of Ellinwood. John attended Ellinwood High School & played football. He worked in the oil field & drove an over the road truck. He loved drag racing and was a great artist. He was lifetime Great Bend area resident where he attended St. Rose Catholic Church.

Survivors include son, James Helfrich, Newton, KS; brother, Butch Helfrich, Ellinwood, KS; 5 sisters, Patricia Donavon, Wichita, KS, Barbara Kieffer, Henderson, NV, Geraldine Helfrich, Rose Hill, KS, Rita Helfrich, Pawhuska, OK, Helen Burresss, Great Bend, KS; 2 grandchildren, 1 great grandchild and nieces & nephews. He was preceded in death by his parents, brother Jabo, niece, Michelle Donavon, nephews, Michael Donavon, George Kieffer, and Bryan Helfrich.

There will be a graveside service at Lakin-Comanche District Cemetery in Ellinwood on Monday April 24th at 11:00 am with Father Terrance Klein officiating. Memorials are suggested to “Donor’s Choice” in care of Minnis Chapel, PO Box 246, Ellinwood, KS 67526.
