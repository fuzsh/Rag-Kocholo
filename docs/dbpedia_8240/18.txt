Biography

Tomás Bartomeus (born 27 October 1982) is a Paraguayan international footballer who plays for Guaraní, as a defender.

Career

Bartomeus has played for Club Universal, Rubio Ñu and Guaraní.

He made his international debut for Paraguay in 2010.
