Together with his Ensemble Phoenix Munich the American bass-baritone and lutenist Joel Frederiksen has recorded Christmas music from 16th- and 17th-century Spain and Latin America. Their album Un niño nos es naçido (A child for us is born) takes listeners on a journey from contemplative sacred vocal music from Spain to some delightful examples of Christmas music from Latin America, including Bogotá and Peru.

Among the pieces featured in this recording are Spanish works by well-known composers such as Tomás Luis de Victoria and Mateo Flecha el Viejo, as well as lesser-known composers like Antonio de Cabezón, Diego Ortiz and Luys de Narváez. It is a beautiful Christmas CD with Spanish music that is eminently worth discovering.
