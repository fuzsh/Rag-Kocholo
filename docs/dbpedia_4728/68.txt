About BQ Aquaris E4.5 Ubuntu Edition

BQ Aquaris E4.5 Ubuntu Edition has been launched officially. The smartphone comes packed with a 4.5 inches (11.43 cm)IPS LCD Display and offers 540 x 960 pixels screen resolution. It is powered by MediaTek MT6582 processor. It is backed by a 2150 mAh battery. The phone runs on Ubuntu Touch.On the camera front, the smartphone comes with a Single camera setup consisting of a 8 MP primary sensor. On the front, the smartphone includes a 5 MP selfie shooter. The BQ Aquaris E4.5 Ubuntu Edition comes with up to 1 GB RAM and 8 GB of internal storage,2G,3G LTE.
