Heather Graham is an American actress whose career began in her teens, appearing in such films as License to Drive. She later gained acclaim for her role as Brandy/Rollergirl in the 1997 film Boogie Nights. Heather also has appeared in such films as Austin Powers: The Spy Who Shagged Me, Bowfinger, and two Hangover films. She also is the sister of actress Aimee Graham.
