Cidade Negra is a Brazilian reggae band formed in 1986 in Rio de Janeiro. Known for their smooth reggae rhythms and socially conscious lyrics, the band has been a staple in the Brazilian music scene. Over the years, they have released several successful albums and have garnered a dedicated fan base both in Brazil and internationally.

Cidade Negra's songs often explore deep emotional themes such as the irreplaceable experience of love, the longing for reconnection, and the regret of separation. Their lyrics also touch on life's challenging journey and the universal longing for love and unity, resonating deeply with their audience and reflecting the complexities of human relationships.
