This is the official site for Edwin O. Smith High School Crew. The team was established in 1992. We are the only public school rowing program operating out of the Northeast corner of the state. We have Fall and Spring competitive seasons and conditioning through the Winter months, with approximately 90 athletes participating in the Spring. The E.O Smith Panthers practice on Coventry lake, Coventry, CT at the Patriots Park boat house! This beautiful spot is also the home of the UCONN men's and women's Crew team!

Please browse the site for more information or use the form at the bottom of the page in order to contact the coaching staff for more information.
