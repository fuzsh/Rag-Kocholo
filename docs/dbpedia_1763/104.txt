About this school

Edwin O Smith High School is a suburban public school in Storrs, Connecticut that is part of Regional School District 19. It serves 1,033 students in 9 - 12 with a student/teacher ratio of 9.7:1. Its teachers have had 11 projects funded on DonorsChoose.
