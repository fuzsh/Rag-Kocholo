​Edwin O. Smith Hall of Fame

​Mission:

​​The purpose of the E.O. Smith Hall of Fame is to recognize alumni, staff, and community members whose outstanding achievements, contributions or character serve as an inspiration for current and former students.

Inductees have been selected based upon a record of outstanding achievement in one or more of the following:

​

Achievement or distinction, including but not limited to professional honors, awards, or memberships;

Citizenship, including but not limited to moral or ethical contributions;

Civic or community involvement or contributions;

Outstanding leadership or service;

Significant contributions to the E.O. Smith student body, community, and/or mission of E. O. Smith High School.
