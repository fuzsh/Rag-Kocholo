Lawrence John Miller is a comedian and actor. He guest-starred on Law & Order in the Season 5 episode "Coma" and the Season 6 episode "Encore" as Michael Dobson, a comedy club and later restaurant owner who arranges for two of his wives to be murdered. Despite appearing twice in this prominent guest role, he later guest-starred in the Season 13 episode "Smoke" as himself, discussing another in-universe standup comedian, Monty Bender.
