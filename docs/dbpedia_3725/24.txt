Crawley Town have signed Enzio Boldewijn on a two-year contract, following his departure from Dutch second-tier outfit Almere City.

Midfielder Boldewijn, 23, becomes head coach Dermot Drummy's first signing at the League Two club.

"We're delighted to have Enzio here and I think he's the sort of player who is going to excite our supporters," Drummy told the club website., external

"He's very quick, strong and technically very good."

Boldewijn previously had spells at Utrecht and FC Den Bosch prior to joining Almere City in 2013.
