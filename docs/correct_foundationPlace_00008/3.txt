About SAP Deutschland

The SAP Deutschland AG & Co. KG with its headquarters in Walldorf, Baden, was launched on 1 January 2001 as a legally independent subsidiary of the SAP AG.

Along with SAP’s change of the legal form – from an German AG (an incorporated company) to a European Company (Societas Europaea, SE) – in July of 2014, the company also changed its name to SAP Deutschland SE & Co. KG.

The business activities of the SAP Deutschland SE & Co. KG are focused on sales, consulting, training and marketing as to the product portfolio SAP SE in Germany.
