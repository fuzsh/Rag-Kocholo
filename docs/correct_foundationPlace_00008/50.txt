Mannheim is home to several well-known manufacturing companies, specializing in a variety of industries. From automotive to chemical processing, these companies have a strong presence in the city and contribute significantly to the local economy. Some of the prominent manufacturing companies in Mannheim include BASF SE, Daimler AG, John Deere, Roche Diagnostics GmbH, and Siemens AG. These companies employ thousands of people and have established themselves as leaders in their respective fields, both nationally and internationally. With a focus on innovation, sustainability, and quality, these manufacturing companies continue to drive growth and prosperity in Mannheim.
