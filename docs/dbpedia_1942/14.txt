Cecil Guy Turner, 78, of Clayton, GA passed away on March 5, 2020. He was born March 15, 1941 in Greenville, Tennessee the son of the late Lencil and Ruby Morgan Turner.

Cecil worked in the medical field for 44 years; he was a very familiar face at Ridge Crest Hospital for many years. He worked as a Medical Lab Technologist and was the Lab Manager. He was a member and attended Clayton Assembly of God. Cecil was a loving father, grandfather and great-grandfather.

In addition to his parents Cecil was preceded in death by his wife, Otha Mae (Sue) Dean Turner; and one brother Leonard Turner.

He is survived by a son Brian Turner (Elizabeth) of Long Creek, SC; two daughters, Michelle Munger (Jesse) of Highlands, NC, Janet Cummings (Thomas) of Clayton, three brothers, Charles Turner, Eddie Turner, David Turner all of East Tenn.; one sister, Betty Feezell of East Tenn.; fifteen grandchildren, 28 great-grandchildren and a number of nieces and nephews also survive.

The family will receive friends Saturday March 14, 2020 at Beck Funeral Home from 12:00 PM until 2:00 PM. A Memorial service will follow in the Chapel of Beck Funeral Home at 2:00 PM with Rev. Jeff Houston officiating.

The family will be at the home of Brian And Elizabeth Turner at 1163 Academy Road, Long Creek SC.
