Cecil Turner was born on

March 15, 1928, to parents Robert

Turner and Mattie Wilton, both of Megis,

GA. Cecil attended the Coolidge school system.

He later met Elizabeth Bostick who became

Elizabeth Turner. They lived as husband and

wife until her death. They had nine kids, three of

whom preceded them in death, Arthur Simpson,

Pricilla Williams, and Twinkle Turner Ivey.

Cecil found Christ at an early age and became

an active member of Mt. Sinai, where he was one

of the oldest members. Cecil worked for Thomas

County Electric Department for over 20 years.

He retired from there.

Cecil Turner leaves to cherish his memories: sons,

Odell Johnson (wife), Rex Turner, Robert Turner,

all of Thomasville, and Eddie Turner of Houston,

TX; daughters, Anne Mickens of Thomasville,

Vinnie Mae Crawford (Kenny) of Chicago, IL, and

Mattie Turner of San Diego, CA; brothers, Robert

Cooke Turner and Oscar Turner of Thomasville,

GA; sister, Justine Slaughter of Sylvester, GA; a

host of nephews, nieces, grandchildren, great-

grands, loved ones, and friends.
