Atlantic City is the eighth episode of the second season of How I Met Your Mother, and the thirtieth episode overall.

Starring: Josh Radnor (Ted Mosby), Jason Segel (Marshall Eriksen), Cobie Smulders (Robin Scherbatsky)

with Neil Patrick Harris (Barney Stinson)

and Alyson Hannigan (Lily Aldrin)

Guest Starring: Todd Stashwick (Steve), Patricia Belcher (Receptionist), Richard Gant (Judge)

Co-Starring: Boyuen (Big Chinese Gambler), Michael David Cheng (Paul), Monique Edwards (Producer), Mary Gillis (Old Woman), Jim Lau (Chinese Man), Kate Micucci (Registar), Ron Nicolosi (Mike)

Uncredited: Bob Saget (Ted of 2030 (voice))

Plot Overview

The whole gang is off to Atlantic City when Marshall and Lily finally decide to get married, but eloping just isn't as easy as it used to be!

Marshall and Lily, together again, decide to elope in Atlantic City, and they gather Barney, Ted and Robin to take part in the wedding.
