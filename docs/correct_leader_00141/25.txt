Sonexay Siphandone (Lao: ສອນ​ໄຊ ສີ​ພັນ​ດອນ; born 26 January 1966) is a Laotian politician and member of the Lao People's Revolutionary Party (LPRP). He is the son of former LPRP Chairman Khamtai Siphandon and brother of Viengthong Siphandone. He currently serves as Deputy Prime Minister of Laos. He was elected to the LPRP Central Committee at the 8th National Congress, and to the LPRP Politburo at the 10th National Congress. (en)
