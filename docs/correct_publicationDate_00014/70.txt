Rosenblum, Mary. SYNTHESIS & OTHER VIRTUAL REALITIES. [Sauk City, Wisconsin]: Arkham House Publishers, Inc., [1996]. Octavo, cloth. First edition. 3515 copies printed. The author's first collection of short fiction, nine stories first published in ISAAC ASIMOV'S SCIENCE FICTION MAGAZINE between 1990 and 1995. Several of the stories are set in the drought-stricken near future dystopian America depicted in her first novel, THE DRYLANDS (1993). Hartwell, 200 Significant SF Books by Women, 1984-2001. A fine copy in fine dust jacket. (#155112).
