Do you have an awesome idea and need some help getting it off the ground? The Portland chapter of the Awesome Foundation wants to hear from you!

We’ve funded over 60 projects in Portland (and surrounding areas) since 2012! Our grants have funded many things, including: farms in schoolyards, community radio stations, pianos everywhere, art hidden throughout the city, books for the homeless, training and classes for kids, growing cooperatives, and giant eyeballs, just to name a few…
