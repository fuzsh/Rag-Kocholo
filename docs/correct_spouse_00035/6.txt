About

Sebnem Dönmez is a 50 year old Turkish Actress. Born Sebnem Donmez on 17th May, 1974 in Salzgitter, Germany, she is famous for Size Baba Diyebilir Miyim? (2004) in a career that spans 1992–present. Her zodiac sign is Taurus.

Sebnem Dönmez has been in an on-screen matchup with Ali Aksöz in Medcezir (2013).

Sebnem Dönmez is a member of the following lists: 1974 births, Turkish actors and Turkish television presenters.
