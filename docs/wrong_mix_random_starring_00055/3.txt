Taylor,Thomas Madison, Jr. ‘Matt’of Boulder, CO died on May 24, 2008.Thomas was born on March 25, 1961 to Thomas Madison Taylor, Sr. and Reverend Glendora Boyce Taylor in Toledo, OH. He graduated with a degree in Bachelor of Science, with an emphasis in Computer Sciences, from the University of Denver. He was employed at Polycom in Boulder.Thomas was a classical cello and bass violin player. He was an avid reader and enjoyed scuba diving. He was a much loved son, brother, husband, uncle, father and friend that shared many happy experiences with those fortunate to know him.Thomas is survived by his former wife Kathleen Neeley; children Kayla Ann and Erik Matthew; sister Kathy Taylor Dusser and brother Greg Taylor; parents Mr. and Reverend Dr. Thomas M. Taylor of Venice, FL, and niece Elizabeth Paulette Dusser of Denver, CO.Interment will be at Montview Boulevard Presbyterian Church.
