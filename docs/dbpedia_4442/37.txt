The home of Shiner Beer, the Spoetzl Brewery was founded a hundred years ago, and for many years it was the only independent brewery in the state of Texas. Despite its nearly cult-like status within the Lone Star state, the brewery is still a comparatively tiny operation, commanding just a small percentage of the state's market. In addition to its namesake Shiner Bock, the brewery produces a number of specialty and seasonal brews, and offers tours daily.
