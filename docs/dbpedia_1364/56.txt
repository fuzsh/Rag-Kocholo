Surname Fake

Family name Fake (Fake , 发科 , Фейк ) , in the entire world, is quite a rare cognomen. The surname Fake is characteristic of U.S. Virgin Islands, where it is quite a common surname, Ireland, where it is a rare surname, and New Zealand. In absolute terms, it is commonest in the United States, China, and India. Also, Fake is the forename as well as the last name.
