Sumeet Raghvan ( born April 22, 1971 ) is an Indian film and television actor known for his roles in Shubh Mangal Savadhan], Hudd Kar Di, Sarabhai vs Sarabhai, Sajan Re Jhoot Mat Bolo and Badi Door Se Aaye Hai. He is currently appearing in the web series Sarabhai vs Sarabhai: Take 2 on Hotstar , a sequel to the cult tv show Sarabhai vs Sarabhai .
