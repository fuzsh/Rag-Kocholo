Westfield Middle School South is a reputable educational institution located in Westfield, MA, dedicated to providing quality education to its students. Led by Principal Jesse McMillan, the school offers a comprehensive curriculum and a supportive learning environment for students of diverse backgrounds and abilities.

Committed to inclusivity and equal opportunities, Westfield Middle School South ensures that all students are treated fairly and without discrimination based on race, color, religion, gender, or disability. With a team of dedicated staff and a focus on academic excellence, the school strives to prepare students for future success.
