Rajan Khosa is an Indian director, producer and writer. His movies include Dance of the Wind (Swara Mandal), Gattu and Wisdom Tree. His movie Dance of the Wind (1997) starring Kitu Gidwani, received a number of awards including the Best Director at the British Asian Film Festival 1998.
