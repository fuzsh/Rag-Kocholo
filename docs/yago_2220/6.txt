Queen Ingrid- daughter of King Gustav VI of Sweden, wife of King Frederick IX of Denmark, mother of Queen Margrethe II of Denmark, aunt of King Charles XVI of Sweden. died 2000. She also was a great-granddaughter of Queen Victoria of the United Kingdom. As descendants of Oscar I of Sweden, she and her husband were third cousins. Through Leopold, Grand Duke of Baden, they were third cousins. And finally through Paul I of Russia, Frederick was a fourth cousin of Ingrid's mother. She was also descended from the first German Emperor, William I.
