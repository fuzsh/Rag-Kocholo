News Center- Turkish prosecutors have issued summaries of proceedings against five members of the Turkish parliament and sent them to the Grand National Assembly of Turkey.

The summaries of proceedings have been issued against Green Left Party MP Meral Danış Beştaş and Green Left Party Van MP Pervin Buldan, Democrat Party Istanbul MP Cemal Enginyurt, Republican People's Party (CHP) Izmir MP Murat Bakan and CHP Diyarbakır MP Sezgin Tanrıkulu.
