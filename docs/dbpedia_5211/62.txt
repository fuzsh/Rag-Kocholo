In the 'hood, Burgersdorp as a destination

Described as the oldest town in the North Eastern Cape, Burgersdorp lies south in the tip of an inverted triangle between Aliwal North and Venterstad. It is not a town you'll simply happen upon unless you have a lot of time on your hands, as it doesn't lie on an obvious route, but it does make it onto lists of the most popular little towns in the Eastern Cape.
