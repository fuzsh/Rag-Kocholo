The charm of Gallura

The area of Gallura in the north east of Sardinia, is a popular destination for many Italian and foreign tourists as it contains many tourist resorts such as San Teodoro, Santa Teresa di Gallura, Badesi, the Maddalena archipelago with its splendid islands and islets.

The beauty of the Gallura is not only that for the crystalline sea and the white beach, but also for its hinterland, rich in vegetation and history, of breathtaking landscapes and villages like San Pantaleo and Sant'Antonio di Gallura.
