Actor

Arsha Aghdasi is a stunt man who was born in 1982 in Tehran, Iran. His most notable activities are “Murphy's Law” by Rambod Javan, “Hoosh-e Siah 2” by Masoud Abparvar, “We Are All Together” by Kamal Tabrizi, “Night of the Full Moon” by Narges Abyar, “Mina's Choice” by Kamal Tabrizi and “At the End of 8th Street” directed by Alireza Amini.
