Where is Centerville, Indiana ?

Centerville is a town located in Wayne County, within the state of Indiana, USA. It lies in the eastern part of Indiana, near the Ohio border. Centerville is situated just west of Richmond, Indiana, and is easily accessible via U.S. Route 40. The town is part of the Richmond Metropolitan Statistical Area.
