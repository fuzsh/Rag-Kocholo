Ron Silver appeared in numerous Hollywood blockbusters and smaller independent films, with such varied co-stars as Anne Bancroft and Vin Diesel.

He was the legendary boxing trainer Angelo Dundee in the biopic Ali (2001), in which Will Smith played Muhammad Ali, and the straight-talking political strategist Bruno Gianelli in the long-running American TV drama series The West Wing (2001-06). On Broadway he co-starred with Madonna and won a Tony Award for his performance in David Mamet’s Speed-the-Plow (1988).
