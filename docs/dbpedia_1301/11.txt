Mary L. Neal

Mary L. Neal, of Westminster, MD, formerly of Havre de Grace, MD, passed away on Friday, September 7, 2012. She was 40.

She was born in Baltimore, MD to Nelson William Neal, Jr., of Abingdon, MD, and Mary E. Neal (nee Clark) of Aberdeen, MD. Along with her parents, she is survived by her brother, Nelson William Neal, III, of Aberdeen, MD, sisters Christine Gagnon of Dover, New Hampshire, Jan Cullen of Spencer, West Virginia, and Thomasina Sanchez of Aberdeen, MD, her grandmother, Velma Neal of Westminster, MD, and her boyfriend Dan Sponaugle of Westminster. She was predeceased by a brother, Joseph “Joey” Kenneth Neal.

Mary’s family and friends were her life. She had a passion for helping others. She lived her life with purpose and had a strong relationship with God. Mary was currently employed at Global Payments.

A viewing will be held on Tuesday, September 11, 2012, at Zellman Funeral Home, P.A., 123 South Washington Street, Havre de Grace, MD, from 2-4 and 6-9 PM. Funeral services will be held on Wednesday, September 12, 2012 at 11:00 AM at the funeral home. Interment will be held at Lorraine Park Cemetery.
