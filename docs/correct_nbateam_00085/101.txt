Luke Ridnour wore this blue jersey while playing for the Minnesota Timberwolves during the 2012-13 National Basketball Association season.

Ridnour averaged 11.5 points and 3.8 assists per game over 82 starts during the 2012-13 season. The former Oregon Duck and 2003 Pac-10 Player of the Year averaged 9.3 points per game in 26.1 minutes per game over his NBA career, and retired after the 2014-15 season.

This Blue Set 1 jersey is photo-matched to 12 games. It includes a Timberwolves Letter of Authenticity stating it was worn in 17 games from November 10, 2012 to February 11, 2013.
