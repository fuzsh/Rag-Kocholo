Diana Hoddinott (born 1945) is an English actress. She was born to Winifred Doris, née Dibble (1909–2003) and Alan Hoddinott (1907–1985), who married in 1934 in Langport.

Hoddinott is best known for playing Annie Hacker, the wife of Jim Hacker, in Yes Minister and Yes, Prime Minister. She also had a role in the historical film, The Gathering Storm, as Mrs Landemare, the Churchills' housekeeper at Chartwell House.

Personal life

She was married to fellow actor Harry Towb from 1965 until his death in July 2009. They have three children: Emily, Daniel and Joshua.
