Acting Career

Mario Lopez‘s acting career began with his breakthrough role in Saved by the Bell. This role not only catapulted him to fame but also provided a substantial financial foundation.

His portrayal of A.C. Slater continues to generate residual income, especially with the show’s enduring popularity and subsequent revivals.

Hosting Roles

Transitioning from acting, he found a lucrative niche in hosting. His long tenure on Extra, from 2008 to 2019, significantly boosted his income.

The move to Access Hollywood in 2019 further increased his earnings, with a notable salary bump to $8 million per year. Additionally, hosting other shows like The X Factor and Dancing with the Stars contributed to his wealth.
