Actress (“Eve”) and former Miss USA Ali Landry wed in Mexico over the weekend, her manager said Monday.

Landry married director Alejandro Monteverde in San Miguel de Allende on Saturday, according to her manager Elissa Leeds.

No other details were released.

Landry appears in Monteverde’s upcoming film “Bella,” due out later this year.

Her brief marriage in 2004 to “Saved by the Bell” star Mario Lopez was annulled.
