About us

Murata Manufacturing Co., Ltd. is a Japanese manufacturer of electronic components, based in Nagaokakyo, Kyoto. Murata Manufacturing primarily manufactures ceramic passive electronic components, mainly capacitors, and has an overwhelming market share worldwide in ceramic filters, high-frequency parts, and sensors.

Smart City/Region Status: Murata recognizes that t the foundation of any form of smart city deployment will be the ongoing collection of large amounts of data. With IoT devices being placed in locations that are difficult to reach Murata offers a selection of component electronics, enabling customers to choose the best fit for their specific application requirements without having to make compromises. These are straightforward to install and deliver prolonged working lifespans.
