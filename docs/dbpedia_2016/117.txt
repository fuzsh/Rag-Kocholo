PRINT

As a free weekly newspaper, website and mobile platform, Cincinnati CityBeat has served the Greater Cincinnati area since 1994 with cultural and political reporting, entertainment previews, food and drink reviews, events coverage and much more. We do more than inform the community. We're a part of the community — we're advocates, consumers, influencers and entertainers as well as reporters, invested and involved in every aspect of local and regional life in both professional and personal roles. Our readers trust us to share the best Greater Cincinnati and beyond has to offer, and we strive to create meaningful conversations and relationships to the benefit of all.

In addition to our weekly print presence, Cincinnati CityBeat produces multiple lifestyle magazines throughout the year. From our Annual Best of Cincinnati Magazine, to our Annual Manual Magazine: A Locals Guide to Living in Cincinnati, we have the content our readers want and the audience you need.

EVENTS

Cincinnati CityBeat produces a variety of boutique events and themed weeks throughout the year, reaching a broad range of local consumers with an interest in culture, dining and nightlife. Our event portfolio includes some of the city's most beloved events, like The Best of Cincinnati®,Margarita Madness, Brunched!, Sugar Rush, Cincinnati Entertainment Awards, and Bourbon & Bacon.
