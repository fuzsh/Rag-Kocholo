Malabo is the capital city of Equatorial Guinea, located on the northern coast of Bioko Island. Situated in the Gulf of Guinea. Malabo is surrounded by lush rainforests, providing a picturesque backdrop to the city’s urban landscape. The city’s historic landmarks, including the Cathedral of Santa Isabel, showcase its colonial past and cultural heritage. Malabo’s vibrant markets, such as the Malabo Market, offer a glimpse into local life, with colorful stalls selling a variety of goods.
