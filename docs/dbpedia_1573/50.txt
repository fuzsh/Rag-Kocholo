Experience the unveiling of "Fable for Tomorrow Wendy Coburn" at the Book Launch event on April 13, 2024, from 2 p.m. to 4 p.m. Hosted at the prestigious Onsite Gallery, located at OCAD University in Toronto, this event showcases a captivating 96-page hardcover catalogue celebrating Wendy Coburn's remarkable survey exhibition. Delve into the depths of Coburn's artistry with insightful essays by Andrea Fatona, Caroline Seck Langill, Rebecca Garrett, and b.h. Yael. Featuring contributions from Jess Dobkin and Lisa Deanne Smith, this visually striking book, designed by Lisa Kiss Design, elevates Coburn's legacy in Canadian art history. The catalogue reveals the beauty of Coburn's work, including the exquisite bronze piece "Untitled (buck), 2007," now part of the esteemed Hart House Collection. Immerse yourself in this enriching experience, generously supported by The Delaney Family, at the heart of Toronto's vibrant art scene. Admission is free, offering a unique opportunity to explore the world of Wendy Coburn's artistry.
