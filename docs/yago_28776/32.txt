Archduchess Maria Christina of Austria (Maria Christina Henriette Desideria Felicitas Raineria 21 July 1858 - 6 February 1929) was Queen consort of Spain as the second wife of King Alfonso XII of Spain. She was regent of Spain during the minority of her son Alfonso XIII and the vacancy of the throne between her husband's death and her son's birth.
