Ron Fenton (September 21, 1940 - September 25, 2013) was a British football player and coach who played as a forward. He was known for his time with clubs such as Birmingham City, Brentford, Burnley, Notts County, and West Bromwich Albion. Fenton had a significant impact on the teams he played for during his career in football.
