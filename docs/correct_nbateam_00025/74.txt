Carlos Boozer is a 13-year NBA veteran, having played for the Cleveland Cavaliers, Utah Jazz, Chicago Bulls, and Los Angeles Lakers. A two-time NBA All-Star, Boozer played alongside legends like Kobe Bryant and LeBron James and clashed against greats like Michael Jordan and Shaquille O'Neal. As a member of Team USA, Boozer won an Olympic bronze medal at the 2004 Summer Olympics and an Olympic gold medal at the 2008 Summer Olympics. A Duke University graduate, Carlos provides commentary for the Atlantic Coast Conference Network (ACCN), ESPN, and NBA.tv. A real estate entrepreneur, Carlos owns multiple Popeye's franchise restaurants, with more properties in development. In his downtime, Carlos enjoys cheering on sons Carmani, Cayden and Cameron's budding sports careers, as well as his young daughter Bloom's artistic endeavors.
