Prabhleen Sandhu (born December 5, 1983) is a Punjabi film actress who rose to fame with the Indian soap on periodic freedom struggle on COLORS Mohe Rang De as Kranti, dons a different look and role in Zee's new show, Aapki Antara as Vidya, a middle-class wife, who has trouble adjusting with her husband's "illegitimate" child. She comes from a Jat Sikh family from Ferozepur, Punjab, India and has done post graduate in English.
