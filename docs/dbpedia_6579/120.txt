Ghost-Chasers was a mini-series of halloween themed videos that were released on ToyLabTV between 2016 and 2019 That follows paranormal investigators Ghost-Chaser Aaron and Ghost-Chaser LB. The videos are heavily inspired by the Ghostbusters. Ghost-Chasers is not part of the main T-Rex Ranch series timeline.
