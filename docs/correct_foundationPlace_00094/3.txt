Our Patient Financial Navigators work directly with patients and their families to help them reduce stress or hardship related to the cost of treatment. Our staff will:

Offer assistance to help patients with their health plans and address any areas of concern.

Help patients set up payment plans, find cost-saving methods for treatments, and improve access to healthcare services that the patient needs.

Deal with a patient's economic barriers to care and aid in providing any and all available resources such as:

Medicaid

Patient Assistance Applications

Social Security Disability

Affordable Care Act Plans

Offering additional assistance and information pertaining to Medicare and supplement plans.
