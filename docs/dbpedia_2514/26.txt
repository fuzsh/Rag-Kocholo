Episode 23 is an episode in the Yo-Kai Watch anime series.

Summary[]

Komasan tries to save the woman from the threat, but discovers she is simply a manga artist who was talking about her story when her manuscript is sent flying by mistake. Keita's mother prepares a large dinner, but the Yo-Kai Tsumamiguinosuke makes her eat from it without her knowing. The mechanical Yo-Kai Karakuri Benkei breaks the electronics in Keita's household looking for the "Hero's Screw" to make him more powerful.
