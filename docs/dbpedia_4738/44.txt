Mota-Engil is a multinational group with operations in engineering and construction, environment and services, transport concessions, energy and mining across 3 continents and 28 countries. Founded in 1946 in Portugal, it is now one of the top 30 construction groups in Europe and a leader in Portugal. With approximately 280 companies, Mota-Engil has a diversified portfolio and seeks to become a more international, innovative and competitive group through global expansion.
