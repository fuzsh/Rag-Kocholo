Roger Lemelin was born on 7 April 1919 in Québec City, Québec, Canada. He was a writer, known for Les Plouffe (1981), The Emperor of Peru (1982) and La famille Plouffe (1953). He died on 16 March 1992 in Québec City, Québec, Canada.

He was awarded the C.C. (Companion of the Order of Canada) on June 23, 1980 for his services to Canadian French Literature.
