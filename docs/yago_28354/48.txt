Biography

Sibilia married her first husband, Artal de Foces on an unknown date. Upon her widowhood, Sibila became a lady in waiting to Peter's third queen, Eleanor of Sicily. Eleanor died in 1375.

Sibila attracted the attention of the king, soon after Eleanor's death. Sibila was in her twenties and Peter was fifty six.

On 11 October 1377, in Barcelona, Sibila married Peter IV of Aragon, thus becoming the fourth wife of the sovereign Aragon.
