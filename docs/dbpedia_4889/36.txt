South China Athletic Association (Chinese: 南華體育會) is a Hongkongese professional sports club, whose football team currently competes in the Hongkongese Premier League, the top tier of Hongkongese football.

Founded in 1904 as the Chinese Football Club by Chinese students, which included Mok Hing (Chinese: 莫慶), South China is Asia's most decorated club and they are also Hong Kong's most successful club, having won more trophies than any other club. South China is considered as one of the most widely supported and valuable clubs in the world.

In September 2009, South China was awarded Best Asian Club of the 20th Century by the IFFHS. On 15 April 2024, the club celebrated its 120th anniversary.
