Frank “Pancho” Castillo Resident of Los Banos 71, went home to be with his Lord on Friday, June 10, 2011 after a courageous battle with cancer. He is a graduate of Los Banos High School class of 1958. He participated on the swim team through out high school. Frank was a member of the UAW and personal security for Caesar Chavez. He retired from Ford Motor Company in Milpitas, CA, and also from Lockheed Martin in Sunnyvale, CA. He enjoyed fishing, carpentry, reading, and being a die hard Steelers fan. His quality time spent with his great-grandchildren was precious to him. He was a lover of music and a devoted friend to all who know him especially his best friend George Castillo. “He did it His way.” He is survived by his loving and devoted wife Angelina of 48 yrs, his sons Frank, Michael, Richard, and daughter Cathy, 8 grandchildren and 5 great-grandchildren.

Visitation will be held Monday, June 20th beginning at 11am. at Whitehurst Funeral Chapel, Los Banos. Service will follow visitation with a reception immediately following.
