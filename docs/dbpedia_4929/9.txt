Frank Castillo, 62, of Clinton, MA passed away on Tuesday, June 7th at MetroWest Medical Center in Framingham, MA after a brief battle with Cancer. He was born on September 8, 1959 in Victoria, TX to the late Teodoro V. Castillo Sr. and Micaela Castillo.

He attended Victoria High School. He loved football, cooking, and volunteering in his community. He made a friend everywhere he went.

Frank was preceded in death by his parents Teodoro V. Castillo Sr. and Micaela Castillo, as well as his sister Andrea Blackwell. Frank is survived by his son, Alexander Castillo of Clinton, MA; his daughter Victoria McGovern and her husband Kyle McGovern of Fort Lee, VA., and his two beautiful grandchildren Lilly and Charlie McGovern.

He is also survived by many brothers and sisters. His sister Mary Delores Reyna; his brother Ted Castillo Jr.; his sister Gloria Morales; his sister Beatrice (Bea) Perez; his sister Mary Jane Corpus; his sister Alice Tamez; his brother Jessie Castillo Sr.; his sister Frances C. Martinez; his sister Esther Gomez; his brother Tony Castillo Sr.; his sister Nora Dominguez; his brother Jessie Patrick Castillo; his brother Freddie Castillo. He is also survived by numerous nieces, nephews, and cousins.

Private funeral arrangements have been entrusted to the Shaw-Majercik Funeral Home.
