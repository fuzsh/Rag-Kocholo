Sharjah, United Arab Emirates

Latitude and longitude coordinates are: 25.348766, 55.405403.

Sharjah (ٱلشَّارقَة) is the third-largest city in the United Arab Emirates, the capital of the same name Emirate. It is located near Dubai and is a part of the local metropolitan area known as one of the most developed and the most modern parts of the world. Sharjah is bordered by Ras Al Khaimah to the east, Dubai to the south, as well as Ajman, and Umm Al Quwain to the north. Sharjah International Airport is situated about 8.1 miles east-southeast of the city center. The city is also home to the University of Sharjah, Skyline College Sharjah, Westford School of Management, American University of Sharjah, and many educational institutions.

The city has plenty of amazing new buildings, shopping malls, business centers, other facilities. It is also a resort with plenty of hotels, amusement parks, and entertainment facilities. The landmarks and attractions in Sharjah are Rolla Square, Sharjah Fort, Mahattah Fort, Sharjah Heritage District, The Souq Al Markazi, or Gold Souq, 'Smile You're In Sharjah' Roundabout, Al Qasba Canal, Sharjah Heritage Museum, the Sharjah Art Museum, Sharjah National Park, Al Noor Island, Al Majaz Waterfront, Al-Noor Mosque, Saint Gregory the Illuminator Armenian Apostolic Church, St. Philip the Apostle Russian Orthodox Church, Meliha Archaeological Centre, the Sharjah Cricket Stadium, Sahara Center, etc.
