Well-known personalities have served Forman Christian College, every personality has played his role and are important part of our university and let me introduce DrNukhbah Langah is one of them. A unique personality who has been well-known for her role in Ewing English Society. She is an Associate Professor and Dean of Humanities Department.

“You were born with wings, why prefer to crawl throughlife”? is her famous quote.

Dr.Nukhbah Langah has a Phd. degree from University of Leeds ( UK), MA from University of Warwick (UK), BA(HONS) from University of Buckingham (UK).She has good interest in research as she has worked with several academic and literary forums such as “The poetry translation center” in London and “PakistanWorkshop” (UK). Her publications include Poetry as Resistance: Islam and Ethnicity in Postcolonial Pakistan and Noshi Gilani Poem( selected poems, co-translatedwith Lavina Greenlawn ( London: Enitharmon press 2008), the TranslatorPolitical activist and Social worker.She is also the chairperson in Pakistan Saraiki Party in Multan.
