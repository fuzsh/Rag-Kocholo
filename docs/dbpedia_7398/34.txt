‘Noshi Gilani Poetry’ is a a collection of remarkable and wonderful sad poetry from the Pakistani born American citizen Noshi Gillani. Unlike most literay figures, Noshhi Gilani is a living celebrity and literary legend of Urdu Poetry. Her poetic talent and creative imagination has also been translated into other languages.

Her language remains simple like Wasi Shah, Parveen Shakir, Munir Niazi(to some extent), Sahir Ludhyanvi and John Ailia etc. The youthful poetess has excelled in diction, style and the traditional inferences of love. The same inferences were expressed by Mirza Asadullah Khan Ghalib in a rich style and high diction. So, ‘Noshi Gilani Poetry’is appreciated and enjoyed. It can be easily understood without loosing charm and depth.
