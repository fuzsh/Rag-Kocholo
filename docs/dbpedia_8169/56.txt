Claudia Jordan is a TV personality and actress born on 12 April 1973 in Providence, Rhode Island. The American TV star started her career as a beauty pageant contestant, when she represented her home state, Rhode Island, as Miss Teen USA in 1991 and Miss USA in 1997.

As a gorgeous model, she made her breakthrough on the television screen modeling for U.S. game shows The Price is Right and Deal or No Deal. As well as a dancer in music videos for musical legends like Ludacris, Fabolous, and Backstreet Boys.
