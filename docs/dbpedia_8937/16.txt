Aoyama Gakuin University (AGU) is one of the top private universities in Asia, located in the heart of metropolitan Tokyo, Japan. The Strategic Management and Intellectual Property Rights Program (SMIPRP) - offered by the Graduate School of Business at Aoyama Gakuin University - is a 12-month master's degree program. It features cutting-edge classes and practical management skills for future leaders, particularly in Customs organizations around the world. All instruction on the SMIPRP is given in English, and the program is open to students worldwide. This master's degree program operates under the sponsorship of the World Customs Organization (WCO).
