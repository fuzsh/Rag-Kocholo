Dennis Arthur Wilkinson, loving father, grandfather, and brother, age 81, died peacefully on June 12, 2023. He is survived and loved by his son Russ (wife Tammy), grandsons DJ and Odin. Loved by his brothers, sister, and many family members.

Denny had a great love of all things cars and racing. He was a movie buff and had a great attention for details. He enjoyed spending time with his family and especially looked forward to big family get-togethers. He was a person who always looked on the bright side and kept a positive attitude.

A visitation will be held from 10-11am, with a funeral service at 11am on Friday, June 23, 2023 at Beaver Lake Lutheran Church, 2280 Stillwater Road, Maplewood, MN 55119. Memorials preferred to the Roseville Meals on Wheels in lieu of flowers.
