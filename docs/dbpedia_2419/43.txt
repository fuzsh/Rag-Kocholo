During the Second World War, Operation Flax was an Allied air operation designed to cut the air supply lines between Italy and the Axis troops in Tunis, in April, 1943 This interdiction operation led to the 18 April Palm Sunday Massacre in which the U.S. Ninth Air Force destroyed hundreds of Ju-52 tri-motored transports over Cape Bon loaded with soldiers escaping the Operation Vulcan attack. Over half the transports were destroyed. See Operation Retribution.
