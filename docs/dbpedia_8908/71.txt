Braceface is a Teen sitcom that first aired in 2001 on Teletoon. Braceface stopped airing in 2004.

Braceface aired for 3 seasons and 78 episodes. It features Alicia Silverstone doing voices, Pure West as theme composer, and Pure West as composer. Braceface is executive produced by Alicia Silverstone. Braceface is created by Melissa Clark.

Braceface is recorded in English and originally aired in Canada. Each episode of Braceface is 22 minutes long. Braceface is produced by Nelvana.

The cast includes: Alicia Silverstone as Sharon, Marnie McPhail as Maria, and Michael Cera as Josh.
