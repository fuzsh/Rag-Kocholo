NBCUniversal Media LLC (NBCUniversal), a subsidiary of Comcast Corp, is a media and entertainment company. It develops, produces and markets entertainment, news, and information. The company owns and operates a portfolio of news and entertainment television networks, television stations, theme parks, and interests in motion picture, television production operations and Internet-based businesses. NBCUniversal offers services under Universal Pictures, DreamWorks Animation, Focus Features, NBC, NBC News, MSNBC, CNBC, NBC Sports, Telemundo, NBC Local Stations, Bravo, USA Network, and Peacock brand names. The company produce and distribute premier filmed entertainment and programming through Universal Filmed Entertainment Group and Universal Studio Group. NBCUniversal is headquartered in New York, the US.
