Horam is a town dedicated to martial arts. Since it was founded, it has been a home to many dojos and students come from all over the world to train in Horam. It is located in the Western half of the Katarina Zone, near the Zen Zone (a martial arts tournament complex) and the Blue Dragon Cave.
