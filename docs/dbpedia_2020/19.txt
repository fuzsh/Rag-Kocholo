Lindsay Pollock is a seasoned art reporter and author who was named Chief Communications and Content Director at the Whitney Museum of American Art in 2018. She was previously editor of Art in America. She joined the magazine following a distinguished tenure as a contributor to Bloomberg News and the London-based Art Newspaper. Pollock has previously written for ARTnews, Art & Auction and The New York Sun. In 2009 she founded an influential blog, Art Market Views, which provides breaking news and analysis on the art industry. Her biography of pioneering art dealer Edith Halpert, The Girl with the Gallery, was published in 2006 by PublicAffairs.
