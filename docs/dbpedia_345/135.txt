Shahid Kapoor is back on the sets of ‘DEVA’ and has resumed shooting. The Bollywood actor informed about the same with his picture from the sets. The science-fiction stars Pooja Hegde alongside Shahid.

The new Mumbai schedule includes Pavail Gulati in a significant role. Both Shahid and Pavail will showcase high-octane action scenes, intensifying the film. On March 15, Shahid shared a monochrome photo on Instagram, featuring him facing away from the camera on a balcony with smoke swirling around him.
