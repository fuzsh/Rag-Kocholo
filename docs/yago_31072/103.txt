Karl Theodor, Duke in Bavaria, is most famous for his reign as the Elector of Bavaria and the Holy Roman Emperor. He was born on September 27, 1724, in Munich, Bavaria. He was the son of Maximilian III Joseph, Elector of Bavaria and Maria Amalia of Austria. He became the Elector of Bavaria in 1777. He became the Holy Roman Emperor in 1792. He died on February 12, 1799, in Munich, Bavaria.
