I’ll Be There is a 4-Track EP by Welsh singer-songwriter, actress, and television presenter Charlotte Church which was given as a special gift with the purchase of ‘Prelude…The Best Of Charlotte Church’. It contains songs featured in the movie ‘I’ll Be There’, a 2003 British-American comedy drama film directed and co-written by Craig Ferguson, who, in his directorial debut, also stars in the film with singer Charlotte Church in her film debut.
