Olá Manuel-

My paternal grandfather's family is from Graciosa, and I have information about some of his ancestors. His name was José Correia de Lima born 1 April 1873 in São Mateus (Praia), Santa Cruz, Graciosa. He immigrated to central California with an older brother, António. Another older brother, Tristão, immigrated to California where he met and married his first wife, had a child, then returned to Graciosa. He had two more daughters, before his wife died. Tristão married again and had three more children.

There's still a Lima family home in Fenais from which my dad's first cousin emigrated in 1960. His name was Manuel dos Reis Lima (son of Tristão and his second wife), and I was fortunate to have met with him a few times before he died in 2005.

As I said, I have more information about my Lima ancestors. A few other names: de São Joze, Bettencourt, Matos, Eiró, de Miranda, de Azevedo...and others. What names do you have? We can exchange information to see who we have in common.

Regards,
