Biography Of Drew Sidora

Drew Sidora is an American actress and singer best known for her roles in Step Up and The Game. She has been acting since she was a child, appearing in shows such as Smart Guy, One on One, ER, Strong Medicine and the Disney Channel Original Movie Twitches Too. In 2009, she released her first single “Say It”.

In addition to her acting career, Drew has pursued music with great success. Her song “Say It” reached number one on Radio Disney’s Top 30 Countdown in 2007 and gained traction on stations like MTV Jams and BET 106 & Park. Since then, Drew has continued to create new music that ranges from hip-hop to R&B ballads. Along with releasing several singles over the years, she also put out two EPs: Who Is Drew? (2009) and All That I Am (2013).

Today, Drew continues to focus both on her musical journey as well as other exciting projects. Just recently she co-founded the record label Born Unique Music Group with fellow artist Akon where they have collaborated together on multiple tracks.
