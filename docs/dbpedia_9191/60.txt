Deep Field is a 14 year old horse that was born in 2010 and raced from 2014 to 2015. He was sired by Northern Meteor out of the Elusive Quality mare Listen Here. He was trained by Michael, Wayne, and John Hawkes and raced for A. R. Bell, Kia Ora Stud Pty., Ltd., and was bred in [Add Data] by [Add Data]. Deep Field has raced and won at Flemington-AUS, Rosehill-AUS. His has a stakes win at the 2014 Tab.Com.Au Stakes (G2).
