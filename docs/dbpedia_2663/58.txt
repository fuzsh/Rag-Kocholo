Larenz Tate is an American film and television actor born on September 8, 1975. He is widely recognized for his roles in movies such as Menace II Society, Dead Presidents, Love Jones, A Man Apart, Crash, Waist Deep, and Ray. Tate is also known for his portrayal of Councilman Rashad Tate in the television series Power. Born in Chicago, Illinois, he is the youngest of three siblings, with his brothers Larron and Lahmard also being actors. Tate's acting career began in the 1980s when he and his brothers enrolled in a drama program. He made his small-screen debut in an episode of The Twilight Zone in 1985.
