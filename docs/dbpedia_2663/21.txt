Larenz Tate is a standout actor, director, producer and activist. Perhaps best known for groundbreaking role as ‘O-Dog’ in the cult classics “Menace II Society”, his feature film credits include “Love Jones,” “Dead Presidents,” and “A Man Apart” among others. He co-starred in the three-time Academy Award winning film “Crash” as well as the film “Ray” opposite the Oscar winner Jamie Foxx. He starred in the hit comedy “Girls Trip”. Currently, Larenz joined the cast of the gritty, Starz drama-series “Power”.
