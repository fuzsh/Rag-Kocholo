Biography

Tomasz Budzy?ski (born 1962 in Tarnobrzeg) is a Polish musician, painter and poet, the lead vocalist of the band Armia. He also worked with bands 2Tm2,3, Budzy i Trupia Czaszka, he was also the first vocalist of the punk band Siekiera.
