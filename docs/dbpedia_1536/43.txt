Piotr Sołtysik – born in 2002 in Warsaw. Graduate of Bartłomiej Budzyński’s Music School in Józefow in Grzegorz Skrobiński’s piano class. Since 2018 he’s studying piano in Karol Szymanowski Complex of State Music Schools No 4 in Warsaw, in Wilia Ochocka-Janusz’s class.

His more important achievemnts include among others:

• Grand Prix in the 3rd International Piano Competition „Primavera” in Riga (2019)

• First Prize in the 18th International Juliusz Zarębski Music Competition (2018)

• Second Prize in the 8th International Moritz Moszkowski Piano Competition in Kielce (2019)

• Laureate’s Prize in 26th International Chopin Festival In Mazovia (2018)

Piotr Sołtysik is also a scholarship holder of the Minister of Culture and National Heritage in Poland.

He took part in masterclasses run by among others Katarzyna Popowa-Zydroń, Elżbieta Tarnawska, Janusz Olejniczak, Wojciech Świtała, Paweł Zawadzki.

Piotr Sołtysik is also very interested in composition and improvisation.
