We had the pleasure to welcome on June 15th, 2020 Mr. Matteo Salvini at our HeadQuarter located in Castelli Calepio.

Welcomed by Guarniflon patron Mr. Giuseppe Mazza and together with some of his co-workers, he visited Guarniflon production departments showing authentic interest toward the international network and the main production processed at the Italian Headquarters. Later on he leaded a meeting with almost 50 local entrepreneurs at Villa Mazza.
