Guy Ritchie is an English film director, film producer, actor, screenwriter, and businessman. He is best known for his crime films, such as Lock, Stock and Two Smoking Barrels, Snatch, and RocknRolla. He also directed Sherlock Holmes, its sequel A Game of Shadows, and King Arthur: Legend of the Sword.

For Disney, he directed the 2019 live-action remake of Aladdin and will direct the live-action remake of Hercules.
