SageTV - About the company

What does SageTV do?

SageTV provided digital media and placeshifting software solutions. Provides a television interface for DVR, music, and photos on Windows and Linux and allows users to watch TV over LAN, stream YouTube videos, and stream from PVRs running SageTV's DVR software to a home theater setup. Users could "place-shift" both live and stored video content from a DVR or PC to either a local or remote playback location. cross-platform networked DVR and media management system.
