Project title: Installation of a green corridor in the municipality of Azuara (Zaragoza)

Location: Azuara, Zaragoza

Date of realisation: August 2022

Technical details: Creation of a green corridor in the vicinity of the municipality, for the revitalisation and greening of roads for pedestrians and sportsmen and women.

This is a closed route that begins and ends inside the park next to the town hall. Various elements have been installed along the route: calisthenics circuit, bicycle repair post, panels indicating points of interest, picnic areas, mural. In addition, the route is signposted with arrows and bollards.
