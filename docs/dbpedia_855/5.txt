University of Bristol - Historical Photographs of China reference number: Tr-m04. Signs on the building at the end of Pedder Street: BRITISH-AMERICAN TOBACCO COMPANY LTD and MADAME FLINT. On the left is an entrance to the Hongkong Hotel. Frederick George Trobridge stayed at the Hongkong Hotel in October 1919, perhaps on his arrival in the colony from England. A man is up a ladder by the hotel. See Tr02-017.
