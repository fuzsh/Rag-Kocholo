Biography

Italian stage and film actor who played the role of Father Costanzo in Alessandro Blasetti's 1934 historical film "1860." The same year he appeared in "The Old Guard" as Doctor Cardini, the father of a young blackshirt who is killed in street fighting.

He died on 29 November 1936 in Rome.
