Hitchhiker’s Guide to the Galaxy Series

Douglas Adams

The renowned Hitchhiker’s Guide centres on permanently bemused human protagonist Arthur Dent, who wanders the Universe after the destruction of Earth with alien travel writer Ford Prefect and a crew of oddities, including two-headed galactic president Zaphod Beeblebrox, an unhappy robot named Marvin, and Trillian, an astrophysicist.
