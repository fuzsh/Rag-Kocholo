Gauhati University is a public University previously known as Guwahati University. It is the first University formed in the North East India located in Guwahati, Assam. It was established in the year 1948. The University gives affiliations to various Colleges in the region and having 239 affiliated Colleges. The University motto is Vidyana Sadhayeta which means Achievement through Learning. The library of the University is the largest library in the State of Assam holding 850,000 books, magazines and journals under its shelter. The University offers Education in several disciplines of Study such commerce, medical sciences, Engineering and Technology. Okhil Kumar Medhi is the VICE Chancellor of the University.
