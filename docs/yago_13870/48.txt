Mohd Pierre Andre Bin Nazarul Andre (born 17 March 1985) or better known as Pierre Andre is a Malaysia actor and director. His role as Reza in the 2005 movie Gol &amp; Gincu was his first major role. Since then he has since appeared in other films and TV series, as well written a screenplay, and directed his first movie.
