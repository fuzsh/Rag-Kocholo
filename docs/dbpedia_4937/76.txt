Inge Morath was an Austrian photographer who was born in 1923. Numerous key galleries and museums such as Museum der Moderne Salzburg, Altstadt (Rupertinum) have featured Inge Morath's work in the past.Inge Morath's work has been offered at auction multiple times, with realized prices ranging from 145 USD to 13,800 USD, depending on the size and medium of the artwork. Since 2002 the record price for this artist at auction is 13,800 USD for Portfolio containing 15 original photographs, sold at Swann Auction Galleries in 2005. Inge Morath has been featured in articles for All About Photo , Hyperallergic and The Guardian. The most recent article is Magnum Photos and Granta Partner for a Unique Collaboration Across Photography and Literature written for ArtDaily in April 2024. The artist died in 2002.
