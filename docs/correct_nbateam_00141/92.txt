Shortly after hitting five three-pointers in the Rockets’ Game 2 playoff win over the Minnesota Timberwolves, Gerald Green wore a Warren Moon Houston Oilers throwback jersey to the podium and explained the significance of playing for his hometown team on this stage.

Now, he has a t-shirt to commemorate this run. Partnering with BreakingT and the National Basketball Players Association, Green is selling “Locked In on My Dream” shirts.
