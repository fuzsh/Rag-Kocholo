Abderrahmane Mssassi (born 24 March 1985) is a Moroccan footballer who plays for MAS Fez. He usually plays as defender.

Mssassi joined Wydad from FAR Rabat in the summer of 2010.

Mssassi played for Morocco at the 2005 FIFA World Youth Championship in the Netherlands.

In May 2011, Abderahmane Msassi was given a six match ban and fined US$15,000 by CAF for spitting at the referee during the game.
