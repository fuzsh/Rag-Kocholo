Aug. 31, 1957 - Laurence Harvey Arrives Home With His Actress Wife: Film star Laurence Harvey and his actress wife Margaret Leighton arrived back in London yesterday. The couple were married in Gibralter three weeks ago, but they were too busy to have a honeymoon. Harvey is working on a film in which he plays the late commander Crabb, the frogman. He resumes work on the film on Monday, then he is due to go to America for more work. Photo shows Laurence Harvey carries his actress wife Margaret Leighton into their Mayfair flat after their arrival in London yesterday.
