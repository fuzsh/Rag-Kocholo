Biography

Margaret (Leighton) Wilding CBE is Notable.

British actress: She was married to Michael Wilding from 1964 until her death of multiple sclerosis in 1976. She was married and divorced to Laurence Harvey (1957-1961) and Max Reinhardt (1947-1955). Her house still exists in Batchmere near Chichester and is called Ivy Cottage.

She was married to publisher, Max Reinhardt (1947–55); actor, Laurence Harvey (1957–61); and actor, Michael Wilding (1964–76)

Parents: Augustus George LEIGHTON and Doris Isabel EVANS
