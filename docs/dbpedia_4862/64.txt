James W. Appleby, 58 of Farmington, Pa., died suddenly Wednesday, January 4, 2012 as a result of an accident.

He was born June 8, 1953 in Uniontown, son of Joyce (Bradley) Appleby of Farmington and the late Thomas A. Appleby. In addition to his father, he is predeceased by two brothers and a sister.

Surviving in additon to his mother are his wife, Sandy (Gates) Appleby; three children: James Appleby, Jr. and wife Tracy of Yukon, Pa., Paul Appleby of Uniontown and Dorothy Appleby of Adah; two grandchildren: Gwen and Aiden Appleby; three brothers and three sisters, Harold Appleby and wife May of Hopwood, Dick Appleby and wife Ruth of Farmington, Mark Appleby and wife Anita of Farmington, Faye Wolfe and husband Charles of Clifton Mills, W. Va., Nancy Gregory of Uniontown and Carol Miller and husband Max of W. Va.

James was a member of Wharton Furnace Union Church and was employed with Utility Forestry Services.

Friends will be received in the DONALD R. CRAWFORD FUNERAL HOME, FARMINGTON on Friday from 2 to 4 and 6 to 8 p.m. Funeral services wil be held Saturday at 11:00 a.m. with Pastor James Handschumaker officiating. Interment will follow in Brown Cemetery, Elliottsville, Pa.
