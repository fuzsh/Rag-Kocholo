Octa Blue, Octavia and Octalux are stuffed animals weighing 2kg. They can be used when the child feels anxious, when he needs support to calm down, at meals by placing it on his lap, during homework, at school, at home and at daycare.

It is important to use weighted tools under adult supervision.

The weight of the octopus should not exceed 10% of the child's weight.

Do not place in microwave

For children aged 3 years and over
