Asking for Trouble is a red hot romance book that was released on May 12, 2020.

Summary[]

You're a billionaire heiress. Brent Mason is a rough and tumble scoundrel who knows how to push all your buttons. When the tension between you explodes into mind-blowing sex, are you just asking for trouble?

Hayden Winstead is a billionaire heiress who's used to the finer things in life. But when she's tempted by the rough and tumble Brent Mason, will she be willing to get her hands dirty?

Brent Mason is a charming scoundrel who's used to getting any woman he wants. When Hayden tries to ignore the spark between them, Brent will make damn sure he's hard for her to forget.
