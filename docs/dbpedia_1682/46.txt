Background Of The Song

The 2010 hit single “All I Do is Win” by DJ Khaled ft. Ludacris, Rick Ross, Snoop Dogg and T-Pain was an instant success in the hip-hop world. The track featured a sample from Heavy D & The Boyz’ 1990 song “Here We Go (Live at the Funhouse)”. An upbeat and infectious beat with heavy basslines backed up some memorable verses from each of the five rappers on the track. The chorus was catchy and simple yet powerful: “all I do is win no matter what/ got money on mind I can never get enough”.

The song achieved commercial success worldwide, peaking at number 24 on Billboard’s Hot 100 chart and becoming certified double platinum in Canada and Australia, as well as gold in France, Germany and Switzerland. It also received positive reviews from critics who praised its production value and energy. Furthermore, it has been used to soundtrack various sports teams such as NBA teams during their pregame warmups or celebrations of victories throughout different leagues around the world.

In short, “All I Do Is Win” remains one of DJ Khaled’s most iconic works due to its message about perseverance in the face of obstacles combined with its impressive list of features that make for an unforgettable listening experience.

Reception And Legacy

Upon its release, “All I Do Is Win” was met with widespread acclaim from both critics and fans alike. Rolling Stone called it a “bombastic club banger” that featured some of the best rhymes in rap at the time. Similarly, AllMusic praised it for its catchy chorus and infectious energy.

In addition to critical praise, the track also achieved commercial success around the world. It peaked at number 24 on Billboard’s Hot 100 chart as well as becoming certified double platinum in Canada and Australia, gold in France, Germany and Switzerland. Furthermore, it has been used to soundtrack various sports teams such as NBA teams during their pregame warmups or celebrations of victories throughout different leagues around the world.

The lasting legacy of this song lies largely in its message about perseverance and resilience even when faced with obstacles—which resonated strongly with listeners all over the globe. Additionally, its impressive list of features makes for an unforgettable listening experience that still holds up today.

Conclusion

In conclusion, the song “All I Do Is Win,” featuring Ludacris, Rick Ross, Snoop Dogg and T-Pain has become a timeless classic.
