UGO FOSCOLO was born in Greece, in the Isle of Zante, the 26th of January, 1778. He unfortunately lost his father, a Venetian by birth, when a child, and became the sole comfort of his widowed mother. The two left Greece for Venice, and then settled in Padua, where UGO finished his education and studied under CESAROTTI. The young man's taste for literature was now gradually developing.
