Biography

Simeon Saxe-Coburg-Gotha was the last reigning Tsar of Bulgarian and later served as Prime Minister of Bulgaria from 2001 to 2005.

Simeon was born 16 Jun 1937. He was the son of Boris Sachsen-Coburg und Gotha and Giovanna di Savoia. Simeon Borisov Saxe-Coburg-Gotha (Bulgarian: Симеон Борисов Сакскобургготски, (transliteration: Simeon Borisov Sakskoburggotski) or Цар Симеон II (Tsar Simeon II).

On 21 January 1962, Simeon married a Spanish aristocrat, Doña Margarita Gómez-Acebo y Cejuela. The couple have had five children – four sons and a daughter.
