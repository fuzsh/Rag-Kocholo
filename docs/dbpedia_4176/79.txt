Another HD collection is heading to a PC or console near you.

Devil May Cry HD Collection will hit PS4 and Xbox One as well as PC on March 13, 2018.

It features Devil May Cry, Devil May Cry 2, and Devil May Cry 3: Special Edition, and all will run in 60fps.

The first of the three games in the package was released in 2001, Devil May Cry 2 in 2003, and the special edition of DmC 3 arrived in 2006. This was a year after the original release of Devil May Cry 3: Dante's Awakening.

Devil May Cry HD Collection originally released for PS3 and Xbox 360 in 2012, after it was announced in 2011.

That concludes our history lesson for the day.

Devil May Cry HD Collection, will release both digitally and at retail for PC, PlayStation 4 and Xbox One on March 13. It will set you back $29.99.
