John William Ford was born April 14, 1945, in Guymon, Oklahoma to Nan and John Christopher Ford and passed away Saturday, July 29, 2023. He graduated from Claremore High School and attended Northeastern State College in Tahlequah where he met his wife to be. John and Harriett Barnett were united in marriage August 19, 1966, in Kinta, Oklahoma. The newlyweds transferred to Weatherford, and both graduated from Southwestern State College where John earned his degree in pharmacy.

John served in the United States National Guard for the next several years while employed as a hospital pharmacist in McAlester, Oklahoma. He left the practice of pharmacy and later became the vice president of sales and marketing for Unit Dose Laboratories in Rockford, Illinois from the late 1980s to 2005.

After retiring, the couple moved to Saddlebrooke, Missouri where John enjoyed time on Table Rock Lake, boating, fishing, and most of all, time with his two beloved daughters and four grandchildren.

John is survived by wife Harriett Barnett Ford, mother-in-law, Christine Barnett, daughter Johnnell Ramlow and her husband Darin, daughter Danae Schindele and her husband, John, granddaughters Cady Ramlow, Brynn Robison and her husband Christian Dean. Grandson Carson Colvin and granddaughter Carli Colvin. Nephew Kirk Barnett. Niece Tara Barnett and great niece and nephew, Maci and Adam Scott Chronister. John leaves behind an empty place at the Saddlebrooke card table and an emptier place in the hearts of his loved ones.
