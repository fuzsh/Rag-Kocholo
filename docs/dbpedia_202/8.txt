MGM and Dino De Laurentiis' "Body of Evidence," starring Madonna and Willem Dafoe, has received an NC-17 rating from the Motion Picture Producers Association, equivalent to the old X rating.

The association's classification and rating administration tagged the film with an NC-17 for what it said were explicit sexual scenes. It is scheduled for release next Jan-uary."Body of Evidence" involves a woman, accused of murdering her lover, who seduces her attorney during the course of her trial. The studio has the option of releasing the film as is or to make cuts necessary for an R rating.
