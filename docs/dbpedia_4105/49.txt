Goopy Gyne Bagha Byne is a series of Indian Bengalifantasy adventure comedy film. The series is based on a story by Satyajit Ray's grandfather Upendra Kishore Roychowdhury.Two films Goopy Gyne Bagha Byne and its sequel Hirak Rajar Deshe was directed by Satyajit Ray, and the third sequel Goopy Bagha Phire Elo was directed by his son Sandip Ray. The trilogy starring Tapen Chatterjee and Rabi Ghoshas Goopy and Bagha respectively. An animated film Goopi Gawaiya Bagha Bajaiya was made in Hindi, based on the story Goopy Gyne Bagha Byne. The film is directed by Shilpa Ranade.
