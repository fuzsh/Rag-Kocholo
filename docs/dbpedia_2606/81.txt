Roger O’Donnell is an English composer and keyboardist best known for his work with The Cure, and bands as varied as The Psychedelic Furs, Thompson Twins, and Berlin.

Roger gained his reputation as a world-class keyboardist and Moog synthesizer expert, and O’Donnell’s body of work includes several styles and genres, some collaborative and some solo, with many written for the piano and others conceived for strings and chamber orchestras.
