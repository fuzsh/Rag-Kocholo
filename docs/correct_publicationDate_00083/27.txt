Zebrowski, George

New York; (1979): Harper and Row. First Edition. Octavo. Item #022673

Signed by the author, and additionally inscribed by author Jack Dann to his mother, and with a laid in proof wrapper for the paperback edition. 284(1) pp., The book opens in the year 202`1. Inspired by the possibilities of space settlements, projections of biology and cosmology, and basic human longings, Macrolife is a visionary speculation of the long term future of human and natural history. Filled with haunting images and memorable characters, this is a vivid and brilliant work. Bound in 1/4 dark blue cloth over orange paper covered boards stamped in blind, spine lettering silver. a very nice, sharp cornered copy in unclipped pictorial dust jacket lettered in yellow and white. Near fine.
