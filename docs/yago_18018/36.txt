Karl, Prince of Hohenzollern-Sigmaringen (Karl Anton Friedrich Meinrad Fidelis; 20 February 1785 – 11 March 1853) was the reigning Prince of Hohenzollern-Sigmaringen from 1831 till 1848.

First marriage[edit]

On 4 February 1808, he married Marie Antoinette Murat, daughter of Pierre Murat (1748-1792), elder brother of Joachim Murat, King of Naples.

Issue[edit]

Princess Karoline of Hohenzollern-Sigmaringen (6 June 1810 – 21 June 1885) married Friedrich Franz, Prince of Hohenzollern-Hechingen.

Karl Anhon, Prince of Hohenzollern (7 September 1811 – 2 June 1885)

Princess Amalie of Hohenzollern-Sigmaringen (30 April 1815 – 14 January 1841) married Prince Eduard of Saxe-Altenburg.

Princess Friederike of Hohenzollern-Sigmaringen (24 March 1820 – 7 September 1906) married Gioacchino-Napoleone, Marquis of Pepoli.

Second marriage[edit]

On 14 March 1848, he married Princess Katharina of Hohenlohe-Waldenburg-Schillingsfürst. They had no issue.
