Robert Daley is an American film producer and former assistant director. He was Clint Eastwood's producing partner from 1971 to 1980.

As an Assistant director:

My Living Doll (1964–1965)

I Spy (1965–1966)

The Invaders (1967–1968)

The Doris Day Show (1968–1970)

Dirty Harry (1971)

Joe Kidd (1972)
