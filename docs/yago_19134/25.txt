Param Gill is an accomplished film-maker known for making diverse films. He won Best Director Award at Los Angeles International Film Festival and San Francisco Global Movie fest for his film "Going to America". The film was distributed by Sony Pictures. His next film, "Bad President" was a satire about Trump's meteoric rise to Presidency. The film was distributed by VMI Worldwide and received extreme reactions! He is also the winner of Platinum Award at Oregon Film Festival and won Award of merit at Indie Fest and Awards. He is the CEO of Young N Free Films, a film production and distribution company based in Silicon Valley in Northern California. He is active in Hollywood and Asian film industry.
