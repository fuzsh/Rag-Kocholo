Marie-Julie Clary, wife of Joseph Bonaparte; Queen of Spain, c1806-1808, (1896). Marie Julie Clary (1771-1845), was Queen consort of Spain and the Indies, Naples and Sicily as the spouse of Joseph Bonaparte. Engraving after a painting.
