Headquartered at Fort Sam Houston, the 232nd Medical Battalion is a subordinate unit of the 32nd Medical Brigade that is tasked with training Combat Medic Leaders. In addition to its Headquarters Company, which includes the Department of Combat Medic Training, it comprises six Companies providing training for the 68W MOS (Combat Medic) and a Reception/Recovery Company.
