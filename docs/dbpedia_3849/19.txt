The 32nd Medical Brigade Color Guard, dressed in World War II uniforms, lead more than 1800 Soldiers in the Pass in Review during the brigade's change of command ceremony held July 18 at MacArthur Parade Field, Joint Base San Antonio-Fort Sam Houston. Historically the Pass in Review is traced to the Middle Ages and demonstrates the glory and strength of the assembled troops. The Pass In Review is a ceremonial opportunity to let the soldiers march by so that all may witness their quality. (U.S. Air Force photo by Edward Dixon, 502nd Air Base Wing Public Affairs) /RELEASED
