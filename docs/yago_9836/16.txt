Adrianne Curry and her husband of five years, Christopher Knight, are heading to a divorce. On the five-year anniversary of their wedding, the first winner of "America's Next Top Model" and the Peter Brady of "The Brady Bunch" announced their separation through long-time friend and manager Phil Viardo.

"After starting a relationship with what seemed to be irreconcilable differences, the couple has reached a period where those differences are no longer appreciated," Phil explained in a statement released via Hollyscoop. "The decision was mutually reached after it became clear to both that some perspective was needed in order to assess their unique union."
