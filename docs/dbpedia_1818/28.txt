What Happened In Year 1214?

June 20, 1214 – The University of Oxford receives its charter.

July 27, 1214 – Battle of Bouvines: in France, Philip II of France defeats John of England.

Who Were Born In The Year 1214?

April 25, 1214 – King Louis IX of France (d. 1270)

Who Died In The Year 1214?

August 26, 1214 – Michael IV, Patriarch of Constantinople

September 14, 1214 – Albert Avogadro, Italian patriarch of Jerusalem (b. 1149)

October 5, 1214 – King Alfonso VIII of Castile (b. 1155)

October 31, 1214 – Leonora of England, queen of Alfonso VIII of Castile (b. 1162)

December 4, 1214 – William I of Scotland (a.k.a. William the Lion; b. abt.1143)
