Sporting Kansas City is moving on in the Major League Soccer playoffs.

Sporting KC beat the San Jose Earthquakes in penalty kicks on Wednesday night to win the MLS Western Conference Wild-Card Match at Children’s Mercy Park.

The match was scoreless through regulation and five minutes of stoppage time. In penalty kicks, Sporting KC’s Tim Melia stood tall with a couple of diving saves.

Johnny Russell, Erik Thommy, Gadi Kinda and Daniel Salloi made PKs for Sporting KC. Alan Pulido squibbed one that was saved by the Quakes’ keeper.
