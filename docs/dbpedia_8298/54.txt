Integra Lab welcomes Jefferson Bledsoe. Jefferson will be joining us as a research intern working across a range of projects including module development for Integra Live, investigation into possible uses of the Myo Armband for studio purposes using the JUCE framework, and research into new technologies for touch-free 3D interaction with virtual environments. As a current Music Technology student at Birmingham City University, Jefferson has a strong background in audio analysis and C++ development whilst also as a piano player. Jefferson will be with the Integra Lab until June 2018.
