Description

The ZUK Z1 is equipped with a 5.5-inch screen that features a 1080 x 1920 resolution. Under the hood is the Snapdragon 801 SoC. The chipset comes with a quad-core 2.5GHz CPU and the Adreno 330 GPU. 3GB of RAM is inside, along with 64GB of internal storage. The 13MP rear-facing camera comes with a Sony sensor (IMX214) and has OIS. An 8MP front-facing snapper uses an OmniVision sensor and is ready to snap selfies and handle video chat. A generous 4100mAh battery keeps the generator humming, and Android 5.1.1 is pre-installed. Other features include a fingerprint scanner, which is embedded in the "home button," and the USB Type-C 3.0 port which is on board.
