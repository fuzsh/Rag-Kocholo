Lenovo Zuk Z1 is a complete Android smartphone, which does not have much to envy the most advanced devices. It has a huge 5.5-inch Touchscreen display with a resolution of 1920x1080 pixels. In terms of functionality, this Lenovo Zuk Z1 does not lack anything. Starting with the 4G LTE module that allows data transfer and excellent internet browsing, passing through Wi-Fi connectivity and GPS.

This Lenovo Zuk Z1 is a product with few competitors for what concerns multimedia thanks to the 13-megapixel camera that allows you to take fantastic photos with a 4160x3120 pixel and to record videos in full-HD at the resolution of 1920x1080 pixels. The thickness of 8.9mm is contained and makes this Lenovo Zuk Z1 very interesting.
