Parris N. Glendening was born on 11 June 1942 in Bronx, New York City, New York, USA. He is an actor, known for Homicide (1993), Ghosts of 33rd Street (2020) and A River of Waste: The Hazardous Truth About Factory Farms (2009). He has been married to Jennifer Crawford since 25 January 2002. They have one child. He was previously married to Frances Hughes and Lynne V. Shaw.
