Larry Miller

Larry L. Miller, 87, Marietta, Ga., who retired from Skyline Mobile Home plant in Goshen, died Sunday morning, June 9, 2024.

He was born April 17, 1937. His wife, Betty Jean (Scarberry) Miller, whom he married Sept. 9, 1955, survives.

He also is survived by his sons, Jeffrey Miller, Marietta, Ga., and Greg Miller, Bradenton, Fla.; two grandchildren; two great-grandchildren; brothers, Rodney, Middlebury, Frederick, Indianapolis, and Marlin, Bradenton, Fla.; and sister, Sandra Coy, Fort Wayne.

Eastlund Funeral Home, Syracuse, is handling local arrangements.
