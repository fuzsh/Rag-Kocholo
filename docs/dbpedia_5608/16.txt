Larry Miller, 76, of Antwerp, passed away Monday, July 15, 2024 at Betz Nursing Home in Auburn.

Larry was born in Jefferson City, Tennessee on January 18, 1948, a son of the late Muriel (Bivens) & Cecil Reese.

Larry was a proud Vietnam War veteran who served in the US Army from 1966 to 1970. After his service, he dedicated many years to working at Spectemp Tempered Glass. On April 20, 1990, Larry married Maria (Lothamer), and they have spent 34 wonderful years together. Known for his kindness, Larry was a man everyone found a friend in. He cherished his role as a grandfather and brought joy to his family and friends.

Larry will be sadly missed by his wife, Maria; children, Larry (Tawnia) Fisher, Rich (Tonya) Fisher, Cecilia (Chester) Hamm, Danyale (Tom) Kinney, Ryan Miller, & Crystal Drake; siblings, Linda Shulzt & Ron (Gloria) Reese; 18 grandchildren; & 5 great grandchildren.

Larry will be laid to rest privately.

Fond memories may be shared at dooleyfuneralhome.com.
