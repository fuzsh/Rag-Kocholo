Sodagreen

(Traditional Chinese: 蘇打綠)is a Taiwanese independent folk-rock-pop

band formed by several students from the National Chengchi University. The

members of the band are: Wu Tsing-Fong (lead singer), Liou Jia-Kai (electric guitar), Ho Jing-Yang (

acoustic guitar) ,Gong Yu-Chi (keyboard, viola), Shie Shin-Yi (bass) and Shih

Jyun-Wei ( drums, percussion) .The band is especially prominent for its main

vocalist-songwriter Wu Tsing-Fong, who stands out with his unique literary and

performing style and wide vocal range.
