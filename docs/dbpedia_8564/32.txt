INDORE: Venerable Bhikkhu Sanghasena, the Founder of Mahabodhi International Meditation Centre (MIMC) Leh, Ladakh, India was honoured by Bharat Kirtiman Alankaran for matchless contribution in fields of spirituality and meditation awareness in an award function at Marriott Hotel in Indore (Central India). The ceremony was graced by international personalities and other prominent guests. This was informed by Shri Santosh Shukla, Supreme Court, Advocate (President, World Book of Records).
