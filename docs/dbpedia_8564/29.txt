Interview Santosh Shukla

Dynamic and charming personality in educational, cultural and philanthropic circles of India and abroad; Santosh Shukla, Supreme Court, Advocate (Cyber Security) has been founder president of International Brahmin Parliament (IBP). Apart from this, he is president of World Book of Records. He has been awarded honorable membership of international organizations. He is well-known personality and doing a lot of social work in promoting social causes. He involves in social and cultural activities across the world. He has always been dedicated in promoting social, cultural, education, environmental, positive journalism, economic development, human rights and humanity cause.
