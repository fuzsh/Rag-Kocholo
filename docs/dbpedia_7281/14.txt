About Viewbank College

Viewbank College is the outstanding government college of the northern region, and is viewed as the leading college in the City of Banyule. The college motto ‘Caring for Excellence’ encourages students to excel and achieve in a caring and supportive environment. Viewbank College is noted for its VCE excellence and this is a testimony to the strength of the curriculum and the expertise of its highly committed staff. The college offers an impressive Enhanced/Acceleration Program and Student Leadership Program. In order to achieve success and personal growth, students are encouraged to be involved in extensive cocurricular activities. The college is known for its exciting music and performance program, its dynamic German and Japanese programs and for offering students opportunities to excel through debating, public speaking, sporting and visual arts programs. It promotes a warm, caring environment that encourages and prepares students to become active, effective members of their community.
