About

Indonesian actor known for his work in film, television, and soap operas. He is best recognized for his roles in the series Splash, Singles, and The Story of the Five Suns.

Before Fame

He first made his acting debut in 1997. His feature film debut came later on in 2017 when he appeared in Nyai Ahmad Dahlan.

Trivia

He has won several awards for his acting including the Topguest Aneka award in 1997. She also has a YouTube channel under the name Malvino.

Family Life

He married actress Inne Azri in May of 2010 at the Danau Raya Sport Club.
