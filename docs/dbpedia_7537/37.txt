Surname Dahlan

Surname Dahlan (Dahlan , دحلان , דחלאן ) , around the globe, is a common cognomen. The cognomen Dahlan is characteristic of Palestinian Territory, where it is quite a common surname, Malaysia, and Brunei (explore the name in all countries). In absolute terms, the name is most common in Indonesia, Malaysia, and Palestinian Territory. Very seldom, Dahlan is the forename as well as the last name.
