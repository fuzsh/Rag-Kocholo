When Arthur Belchamber was born about 1874, in Guildford, Surrey, England, United Kingdom, his father, Alfred Belchamber, was 42 and his mother, Jane Furlonger, was 38. He married Elizabeth Isabel Sturt in 1896, in Guildford, Surrey, England. They were the parents of at least 1 son and 2 daughters. He lived in Andover, Hampshire, England in 1911 and Ringwood, Hampshire, England, United Kingdom in 1939. He died on 9 October 1966, at the age of 93.
