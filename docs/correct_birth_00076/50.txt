When Arthur Wellesley 1st Duke Of Wellington was born on 1 May 1769, in Dublin, Ireland, his father, Garret Wellesley 1st Earl of Mornington, was 33 and his mother, Anne Hill-Trevor, was 26. He married Hon. Catherine Sarah Dorothea Pakenham on 10 April 1806, in Dublin, County Dublin, Ireland. They were the parents of at least 2 sons. He died on 14 September 1852, in Walmer, Kent, England, United Kingdom, at the age of 83, and was buried in St Paul's Cathedral, London, England, United Kingdom.
