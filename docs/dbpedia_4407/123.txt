Founded in 1995 by Olympic athlete Dan Lyons, Team Concepts is a global team formation and leadership development company.

Through experiential training, we teach managers, leaders, and organizations how to create, lead, and maintain high-performance teams.

52,000 participants originating from 62 countries have attended our live programs - delivered at venues in 64 cities spread across 14 countries in North America, Europe, the Middle East, and Asia.
