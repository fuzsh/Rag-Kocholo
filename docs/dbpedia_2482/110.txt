Elizabeth "Elisa" Bauer

Born: March 5, 1918

Passed: March 1, 2014

Funeral Home: Eernisse Funeral Home

ELIZABETH "ELISA" BAUER Mrs. Elisa Bauer, of Crystal Springs Court in Fredonia, passed away at her residence Saturday, March 1, 2014. She was four days shy of her 96th birthday. Elisa was born on March 5, 1918 in the Town of Belgium, daughter of Engelbert and Mary Thomes Bares. She attended local schools through the 10th grade and later worked at Allen Edmonds Shoe Factory. Elisa was united in marriage with Elmer "Joe" Bauer at Holy Cross Catholic Church on May 5, 1945. The couple settled in the area, later moving to New Jersey in 1959. While in New Jersey, Elisa proudly finished her high school education and earned her diploma. They returned to Wisconsin in 1979 and settled in the Fredonia area. Joe preceded her in death in 1990. Mrs. Bauer was a member of Divine Savior Parish. She enjoyed gardening, painting and watching the birds in her backyard. She is survived by her son Michael (Daria) Bauer of Western Springs, IL and grandson Jason (Sarah) Bauer of Alexandria, VA . She is further survived by sister-in-law Janet Bares of Merrill, nieces, nephews, other relatives and friends.
