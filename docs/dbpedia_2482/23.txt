Mary (Barr) Bauer, 93, of Hopewell Twp., passed away Wednesday, May 16, 2012 in Beaver Elder Care.

Born June 25, 1918 in Aliquippa, she was a daughter of the late John and Theodora (Batz) Bur. Mrs. Bauer was the owner of the former Barr Hotel in Aliquippa, and was a member of SS. Peter and Paul Ukrainian Catholic Church in Aliquippa and the St. Helene's Guild. She was an avid golfer and one of the original members of the former Aliquippa Golf Club.

In addition to her parents, she was preceded in death by her husband, Joseph Bauer in 1993; a sister, Stella Lazar, and five brothers, Michael, Nicholas, John and Paul Barr and Andrew Bur.

Surviving are two daughters and sons-in-law, Paula and Michael Liptak, and Marilynn and Walter Postava, all of Hopewell Twp.; five grandchildren, Joe (Jennifer) Liptak, Michelle Liptak, David (Kim) Byrd, Joseph Byrd and Phillip (Jodi) Byrd, and seven beloved great-grandchildren, Adriana, Joshua, Joseph, Phillip, Nevadalyn, Mya and Abigail Lee.

Friends will be received today from 4 to 8 p.m. in the TATALOVICH FUNERAL HOME AND CREMATION SERVICES, INC., CENTER TOWNSHIP, 3475 Brodhead Road, Monaca, PA 15061, where a service will be held Friday at 9:15 a.m., followed by a Mass of Christian Burial at 10 a.m. in SS. Peter and Paul Ukrainian Catholic Church, Aliquippa, with Father Ihor Hohosha, her pastor, officiating.

Interment will follow in Mt. Olivet Catholic Cemetery, Hopewell Twp.

Parastas (blessing service) will be held today at 7 p.m. in the funeral home.
