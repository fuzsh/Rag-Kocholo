What is Gene Hackman's Biography?

Gene Hackman was born on January 30, 1930, in San Bernardino, California. He grew up in a working-class family and attended high school in Illinois. After serving in the United States Marine Corps, he pursued a career in acting, studying at the Pasadena Playhouse and later moving to New York City to establish himself in theater. Hackman's breakthrough came in the late 1960s, leading to a successful film career that would span over four decades.
