Bernd Dittert, East Germany (GDR) riding in the 4000m Individual Pursuit Semi Final at the Seoul Olympic Games in South Korea using their brand new bikes against Dean Woods of Australia. Woods went on to win and rode off for Gold in the Final against the Soviet Union rider (Gintautas Umaras, Gold Medal winner**), and won the Silver Medal.
