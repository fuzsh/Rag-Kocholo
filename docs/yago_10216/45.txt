Erin Hershey Presley first appeared on daytime on General Hospital where her character Alison Barrington was introduced before she made her way to spin-off Port Charles. Presley played the role until the series cancellation in 2003. Presley reprised the role of Alison on GH in early 2013.

Personal Life[]

Presley has been married to Brian Presley since 07/27/2002 and they have three children: Jackson Gunnar (b. 01/07/2007), Emma (b. 2010), and Ruby Rose (b. 06/2013).
