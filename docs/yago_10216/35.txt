Erin Hershey Presley (born Erin Alise Hershey on September 2, 1976) is an American former actress. She gained recognition for her portrayal of Alison Barrington on the American soap opera Port Charles from 2000 to 2003. Hershey Presley and actor Brian Gaskill formed the popular on-screen couple of Rafe Kovich and Alison Barrington. In 2010, she appeared in the film Once Fallen, alongside her husband Brian Presley.
