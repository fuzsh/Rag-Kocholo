Brian Presley was born on August 18, 1977, in Midland, Texas, USA. He is an actor and producer, known for Touchback (2011), Home of the Brave (2006) and Mechanic: Resurrection (2016). He has been married to Erin Hershey since July 27, 2002. They have three children.

Trivia (6)

Is engaged to Port Charles (1997) co-star Erin Hershey. He proposed to her at Disneyland on April 19, 2002.

He enjoys playing sports, such as golf, cycling, running and playing the guitar.

He often works with different outreach programs that help better the lives of disadvantaged children.

Worked with future Port Charles (1997) co-star Opal Anchel in a McDonald's commercial during the beginning of his career.

Most famous Presley is, of course, Elvis Presley. Brian Presley was born on the same day when Elvis was buried.

Son, Jackson Gunnar arrived on January 7, 2007, and weighed in at 7 lbs. 1 oz. Brian and Erin who have chosen to call their son "Jack".
