Jo Stafford Collection 1939-62 is an album by Jo Stafford, released in 2015. Jo Stafford Collection 1939-62 includes a.o. the following tracks: " Crazy Rhythm (the Pied Pipers)", " What Can I Say, After I've Said I'm Sorry (jo Stafford With", " Snooty Little Cutie (frank Sinatra & Jo Stafford With The T", " How Sweet You Are (jo Stafford With Paul Weston & His Orch)" and more. The album is a oldies 4-CD.
