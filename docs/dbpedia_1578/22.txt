Who is Andriy Hlushchenko? Biography, gossip, facts?

Andriy Hlushchenko (born 18 March 1974) is a Ukrainian football goalkeeper coach at Chornomorets Odessa of the Ukrainian Premier League. Prior to that he played at Torpedo Zaporizhia Zirka Kirovohrad Metalurh Zaporizhya llychivets Mariupol and Chornomorets Odessa and was also a goalkeeper coach at Metalurh Zaporizhya. Andriy announced his retirement as a professional goalkeeper in the spring of 2009.
