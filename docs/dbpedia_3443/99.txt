Search and pay for toll trips for the Sydney Harbour Bridge and Sydney Harbour Tunnel. For other toll roads, please refer to the toll notice issued for payment instructions.

To check the total amount of tolls owed on the Sydney Harbour Bridge and Sydney Harbour Tunnel you need to provide your licence plate number and state of registration. If you have a toll notice number, entering that will give you a breakdown of each Sydney Harbour Bridge and Sydney Harbour Tunnel toll owing for the vehicle registration.
