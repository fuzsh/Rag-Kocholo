Kevin Dunn is an American actor, who has appeared in supporting roles in a number of films and television series since the 1980s. His roles include Colonel Hicks in the 1998 version of Godzilla, Ron Witwicky in the Transformers film series, and Oscar Galvin, the primary antagonist in the 2010 action thriller Unstoppable. He also plays Ben Cafferty in the TV series, Veep.
