Cameron McAllister is a British screenwriter, who has written for both film and television. Cameron was the Series Producer for Series 1 of Primeval and an Executive Producer for Series 2. He also wrote Episode 2.4 and Episode 3.8 of Primeval. He is known primarily for his work on Emmerdale, the British long-running soap opera.
