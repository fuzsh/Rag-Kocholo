historical notes King Vidor was a filmmaker and art collector whose collection included work by Thomas Hart Benton, Diego Rivera, and Grant Wood.

Vidor was married and divorced three times, to Florence (Arto) Vidor and Eleanor Boardman, both actresses, and to Elizabeth Hill, a script writer. He had a daughter, Suzanne, by his first wife, and two daughters, Antonia and Belinda, by his second. He is also survived by eight grandchildren and nine great-grandchildren.
