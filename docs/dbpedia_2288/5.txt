Biography

Gundakar von Liechtenstein was born in 1580 in Eisgrub (now Lednice), Moravia, Holy Roman Empire. He was a son of Baron Hartmann II von Liechtenstein and Baroness Anna Maria (von Ortenburg) von Liechtenstein.

He was a court official of the Holy Roman Empire.

In 1603, he married Agnes von Ostfriesland (Agnes of East Frisia).
