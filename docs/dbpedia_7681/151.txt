Robert Downey Jr is an American actor. Born in 1965 in New York City, he began his career on the stage. His first lead role in a film was opposite Molly Ringwald in The Pick-up Artist. He won an Academy Award in 1992 for his portrayal of Charlie Chaplin in the film, Chaplin. He is known for his work in the Marvel Cinematic Universe, playing Iron Man. He has struggled with addiction throughout his life, and has had several stints in rehab. He has been drug free since 2003, which is when he met his now wife, film producer Susan Levin. They have two children together.
