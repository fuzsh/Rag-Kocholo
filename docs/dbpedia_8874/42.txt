Easton Corbin was born in Trenton, Florida, in 1982. He signed to Mercury Records Nashville in 2009 and released his self-titled debut album in March 2010. The album was a commercial success, spawning two number-one singles, "A Little More Country Than That" and "Roll with It". Corbin's music is a blend of traditional and modern country music. He has been praised for his baritone voice and his songwriting skills. He has released four studio albums, all of which have been certified gold or platinum. He has also had several Top 10 singles on the Billboard Hot Country Songs chart.
