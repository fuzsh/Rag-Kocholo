Formerly known as the Diskeeper Corporation, Condusiv Technologies was founded on July 22, 1981 by Craig Jensen. Condusiv Technologies is the developer of the Diskeeper software product that helps correct issues with disk fragmentation, undelete, and sitekeeper.
