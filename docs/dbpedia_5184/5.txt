Time Zones in Texas

Texas is generally in the Central Time Zone, and its west border corner region is in the Moutain Time Zone. Cities in Central Standard Time Zone include Houston, Lubbock, San Antonio, Abilene, Odessa and Amarillo. Cities in Moutain Standard Time Zone include El Paso and Hudspeth. Daylight Saving Times are used in Texas time zones during summer.

Currently the El Paso and Hudspeth regions observe Mountain Daylight Time, while most parts observe Central Daylight Time.
