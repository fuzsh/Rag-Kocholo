WXKB (103.9 FM) is a commercial radio station located in Cape Coral, Florida. It serves the Fort Myers-Naples radio market and Southwest Florida. The station is owned by the Beasley Broadcast Group and features a top 40/CHR radio format known as "B-103.9 All the Hits" or "The Killer B." During the afternoon drive time, it airs the syndicated show "On Air with Ryan Seacrest," which is provided by Premiere Networks. [*]
