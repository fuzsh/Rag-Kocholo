Town Without Pity (1961)

Directed by Gottfried Reinhardt.

With Kirk Douglas Christine Kaufmann E. G. Marshall

Town Without Pity is a 1961 American, Swiss, and West German international co-production drama film directed by Gottfried Reinhardt. Produced by The Mirisch Corporation, the film stars Kirk Douglas, Christine Kaufmann, and E. G. Marshall. Coincidentally, this movie came out the same year that John A. Bennett, to this day the last man executed by the U.S. Army, was hanged for raping an 11-year-old girl.
