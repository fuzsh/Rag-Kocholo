Focal, a Moog Inc. company, specialises in providing custom electrical slip rings, fibre optic rotary joints, fluid rotary unions, junction boxes as well as fibre optic, fluid and hybrid utility swivel and optical multiplexer solutions. All products designed and tested to operate effectively in the harshest maritime environments.

In our long-standing capacity as Moog Focal representatives with workshop facilities in Scandinavia, Europe, Gulf of Mexico and Asia Pacific, MacArtney facilitates sales and supplies of numerous Moog Focal marine products.

Throughout the years, we have joined forces in several projects. MacArtney therefore has an extensive track record as a Moog Focal supplier and provider of specialist engineering support and after-sales service.
