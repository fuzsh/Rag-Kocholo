Oscar-winning filmmaker Martin Charles Scorsese was born on November 17, 1942. Born in Queens, New York City, he was raised in the neighborhood of Little Italy, which later provided the inspiration for many of his films. Scorsese earned a BS degree in film communications in the year 1964 from the New York University's School of Film. he followed this with a Master's degree in the same field in the year 1966.
