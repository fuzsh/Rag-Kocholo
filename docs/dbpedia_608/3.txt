dbo:abstract

Prosperita Open je mužský profesionální tenisový turnaj hraný na otevřených dvorcích na červené antuce, který je součástí okruhu ATP Challenger Tour. Pořádán je každoročně v Ostravě od roku 2004, kdy nesl název Ispat Open. (cs)

Das ATP Challenger Ostrava (offizieller Name: Ostra Group Open, vormals Ostrava Open sowie vormals Prosperita Open) ist ein seit 2004 jährlich stattfindendes Tennisturnier in Ostrava, Tschechien. Es ist Teil der ATP Challenger Tour und wird im Freien auf Sand ausgetragen. (de)

El Prosperita Open es un torneo profesional de tenis. Pertenece al ATP Challenger Series. Se juega desde el año 2004 sobre pistas de polvo de ladrillo, en Ostrava, República Checa.​ (es)

The Prosperita Open is a professional tennis tournament played on outdoor red clay courts. It is currently part of the Association of Tennis Professionals (ATP) Challenger Tour. It is held annually in Ostrava, Czech Republic, since 2004. In 2004, the tournament was still called Ispat Open. As of 2022 still nobody has won two singles titles after 19 editions, while Pavel Šnobel and Sergiy Stakhovsky has won two doubles titles each. Stakhovsky won after 13 years from his first win. Jan Hájek, Stéphane Robert, and Andrey Kuznetsov won both singles and doubles title the same year. (en)

Prosperita Open adalah turnamen tenis yang dimainkan di permukaan tanah liat merah terbuka. Turnamen ini merupakan bagian dari Association of Tennis Professionals (ATP) . Turnamen ini diselenggarakan setiap tahunnya di Ostrava, Republik Ceko, sejak tahun 2004. Pada tahun 2004, turnamen ini masih bernama Ispat Open. (in)

L'Ostrava Open, conosciuto in passato anche come Prosperita Open per ragioni di sponsorizzazione, è un torneo professionistico maschile di tennis giocato sulla terra rossa, che fa parte dell'ATP Challenger Tour. Si gioca annualmente dal 2004 sui campi dell'SC Ostrava di Ostrava, in Repubblica Ceca. (it)
