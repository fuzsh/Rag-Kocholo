Garth Nix is an Australian Author, about whom this Wikia is written. He has three major International bestselling series:

The Old Kingdom series - Sabriel, Lirael and Abhorsen; The Keys to the Kingdom series - Mister Monday, Grim Tuesday, Drowned Wednesday, Sir Thursday, Lady Friday, Superior Saturday andLord Sunday; and The Seventh Tower series - The Fall, Castle, Aenir, Above the Veil, Into Battle, and The Violet Keystone. As well as a number of stand-alone novels.
