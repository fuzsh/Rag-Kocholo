Kevin Scott Buchanan, age 62 of Duluth, MN passed away peacefully on October 6, 2023. He was born in San Bernardino, CA on August 15, 1961 to parents, Bernard & Shirley (Moore) Buchanan. He moved to Duluth and married the love of his life, Lisa Barber. He is preceded in death by his parents, sister, Kathy Near; and mother-in-law, Mary Lou Pecht.

Kevin is survived by his wife of 23 years, Lisa; children kristine (Patrick) Defoe & Walter (Karen) Effinger; grandchildren, Mickey, Jeanette, Patrick Jr., Cassidy, Kaylin, and Zachiriah; sister, Cindy Buchanan, and many extended family members and friends.
