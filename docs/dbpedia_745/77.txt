The 3-star Sunflower B&B Magliano Vetere is situated about a 5-minute drive from Museo Paleontologico, which is an excellent place to improve cultural awareness.

This Magliano Vetere bed & breakfast is located approximately 25 minutes' walk from Chiesa Santa Maria Assunta Church and 25 minutes' stroll from Gole del Calore. Also, the property is 80 km from Positano.

The rooms feature such facilities as a mini-fridge-bar, along with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels for guests' entertainment.
