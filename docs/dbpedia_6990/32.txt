Kazi Abul Kasem

Kazi Abul Kasem, pronunciation (Bengali: কাজী আবুল কাসেম; 7 May 1913 – 7 August 2003) pseudonym Dopiaza, was a Bengali polymath, pathfinder creative professional who is known as the very first Muslim cartoonist in Indian subcontinent.
