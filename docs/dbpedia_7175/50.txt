ABOUT

Krishnakanta Handique Government Sanskrit College is affiliated to Gauhati University and offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in the field of Sanskrit. The college was established in 1947 with a mission to promote the study of ancient Indian languages like Sanskrit. It has an excellent faculty that provides quality education through various teaching methods such as lectures, seminars, workshops, etc. The college also organizes several cultural events throughout the year for students to participate in and gain knowledge about their culture.
