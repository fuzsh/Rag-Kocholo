Krishna Kanta Handique State Open University (KKHSOU) Study Centre

The college runs a study centre under Krishna Kanta Handique State Open University (KKHSOU). The persons, who fail to attend regular courses but are willing to continue study, may avail themselves of the various courses/programmes given below. The University permits them to attend in counseling classes on every Sunday from 9:30 AM for the following three- year- courses:

Bachelor Preparatory Programme

Bachelor of Computer Application

Bachelor of Mass Communication

Bachelor of General Degree courses: Assamese. English, Sociology, Economics, Pol. Science, History, Education, Mathematics, Journalism & mass communication, Assamese journalism

Master Degree in Pol. Sciences, Assamese, Education

Distance Education:

Post-graduate degrees in the following subjects are offered through D.D.E. (Directorate of Distance Education) Dibrugrah University. 1. Economics 2. Sociology 3. Political Science 4. Education 5. History and 6. Assamese
