by Staff Reporter

Former Nigeria youth internationals Sani Emmanuel and Harmony Ikande are undergoing trials with Bosnian club Sarajevo FC.

Both players are in team's training camp in Slovenia and Ikande featured in a friendly match against Borac, where he shone like a million stars in Sarajevo FC's 5-0 win.

Ikande played for Hapoel Tel Aviv in Israel last season.

The 22-year-old Emmanuel played for Swedish lower league team Oskarshamns AIK in 2015 where he played four games and scored a goal.

The former My Peoples Academy of Lagos ace has played for Lazio, U.S. Salernitana 1919 (Italy), FC Biel-Bienne (Switzerland) and Beitar Jerusalem (Israel).
