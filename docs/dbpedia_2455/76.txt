Stockdale High School (SHS) is a senior high school on Buena Vista Road. The school’s colors are black and silver, and its athletic teams go by the name Stockdale Mustangs. Stockdale High School opened its doors for the first time in 1991. It is now in its 28th year, with about 2,069 students registered. “Branded by Excellence” is the school’s motto. Stockdale was recognized in the top 6 percent of all American public schools by Newsweek in 2010.

Address: 2800 Buena Vista Rd, Bakersfield, CA 93311, US
