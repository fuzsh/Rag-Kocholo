Defending NA3HL League Champion, St. Louis Jr. Blues, have announced their roster for the 2011-12 season. Among the returners are the team's two leading scorers from last year, Jamie DeMarco and Ryan Hogland. In addition, the teams leading defensive scorer, Joe Bowler is back for another season and has been named the team's Captain for the upcoming season. 15 game winner, Mitch Fernsler will be back in net leading the goaltending tandem. Joining him is rookie, 1995 birth year, Clint Steinmeyer. Once again, the team will feature primarily St. Louis area players. In the early weeks of the season, three players seem to stand out. Forwards Trace Jablin, Daniel Rosse, and Kerry McGlynn have performed well to this point.
