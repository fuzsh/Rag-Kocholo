Elisabeth Wiedemann was born on April 8, 1926 in Bassum, Province of Hanover, Prussia [now Lower Saxony], Germany. She was an actress, known for Spätere Heirat erwünscht (1966), Ein Herz und eine Seele (1973) and Bürger Schippel (1964). She was married to Werner Mengedoht and Richard Lauffen. She died on May 27, 2015 in Marquartstein, Bavaria, Germany.
