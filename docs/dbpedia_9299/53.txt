Babyface Ray has announced his 2023 North American tour. The 25-date Courtesy Of The Mob tour will kick off Thursday, April 6th in Sacramento making stops in Los Angeles, Chicago, New York, DC, Atlanta and more before wrapping in Detroit for a hometown show. Veeze will be the special guest on all dates.

Tickets are available for presale starting tomorrow at 10:00 AM local and on sale this Friday, February 17th right here.
