Arthur Rankin Jr. isn't just a name in the film industry; he's a legend who has shaped much of what we cherish in holiday classics and animated entertainment. Alongside his partner Jules Bass, Rankin created a treasure trove of films that continue to enchant audiences around the globe.

This list rounds up the best movies by Arthur Rankin, highlighting those magical creations that have become almost synonymous with seasonal cheer and childhood nostalgia. From stop-motion marvels to captivating cartoons, each film on this list offers a unique glimpse into the imaginative prowess of this iconic filmmaker. Whether you're revisiting these gems or discovering them for the first time, prepare for a delightful cinematic journey.
