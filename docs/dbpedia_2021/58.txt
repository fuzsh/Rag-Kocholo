Lindsay Pollock is an Associate in Sklar Kirsh’s Real Estate Department. Lindsay’s experience includes representing companies in real estate and business transactions, such as acquisitions, dispositions, financing, joint ventures, leasing and construction transactions.

Prior to joining Sklar Kirsh, she worked as an attorney at the Los Angeles law firm Safarian Choi & Bolstad LLP.

Lindsay is a native Los Angeleno. She earned her J.D. from Loyola Law School. While in law school, Lindsay volunteered with the Legal Aid Foundation of Los Angeles in eviction defense, interned with the in-house counsel of a large international real estate investment fund, and was an active member of Loyola’s Real Estate Law Society.

Lindsay earned her undergraduate degree in sociology from UCLA, where she was a member of the varsity women’s rowing team.
