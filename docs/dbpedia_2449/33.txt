About Peter Shor

Peter Shor received his BFA degree from The School of Visual Arts in 1975. He has had a passion for drawing and painting since he was a child. With a love for color, he has played with many media including pastels, pencils, charcoal, acrylics, oils, water colors, and computer imaging. His creativity most often manifests as abstract art, but he has also produced a fair amount of realistic drawings and painting.

In addition to the creative arts Peter is a computer system\'s analyst, massage therapist, and Quantum-Touch Practioner. He also is available for neurofeedback, biofeedback, heartfullness training, and computer consulting.
