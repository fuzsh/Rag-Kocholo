Marie-Julie Clary, wife of Joseph Bonaparte; Queen of Spain', circa 1806-1808, (1896). Marie Julie Clary (1771-1845), was Queen consort of Spain and the Indies, Naples and Sicily as the spouse of Joseph Bonaparte. Engraving after a painting. From Life of Napoleon Bonaparte, Volume I, by William Milligan Sloane. [The Century Co., New York, 1896]. Artist Unknown. (Photo by The Print Collector/Getty Images)
