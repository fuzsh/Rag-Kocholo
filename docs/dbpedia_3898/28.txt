Ray Brassier

Ray Brassier is a member of the philosophy faculty at the American University of Beirut, Lebanon. He is translator of Alain Badiou’s Saint Paul (Stanford University Press, 2003), editor/Translator of Badiou’s Theoretical Writings (with Alberto Toscano, Continuum, 2004), and author of Nihil Unbound: Enlightenment and Extinction (Palgrave Macmillan, 2007).
