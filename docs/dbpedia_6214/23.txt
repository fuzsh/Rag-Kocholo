WIVM-LP (Channel 52) in Canton, Ohio and WIVN-LP (Channel 29) in Newcomerstown, Ohio are low power television stations serving Stark and Tuscarawas Counties in Ohio. WIVM and WIVN are owned by Image Video Teleproductions, Inc., and simulcast all programming on both channels.

The stations are America One network affiliates. Both stations are locally-owned and operated, and in addition to carrying America One programming, air a schedule of sports, local news and other local programming, movies, and family and educational programs. Every morning, the stations carry a television simulcast of the morning drive radio show of Canton station WHBC-AM 1480.

WIVM-LP is carried by Time Warner Cable on channel 365 in Stark County and on Massillon Cable, channel 21.WIVN-LP is carried by Time Warner Cable on channel 99 & channel 365 in Tuscarawas, Holmes & Carroll Counties.
