Age

Dodge Center's population is young compared to the rest of the country:

The median age in Dodge Center is 37.2, compared to 38.8 nationwide, according to the American Community Survey 2017 - 2021 5 Year estimates.

Dodge Center's population under 18 was 26.93%, compared to the 22.51% nationwide.

Dodge Center's elderly or the senior population (over 65 age) was 13.14% as compared to 16.04% nationwide.

Of the total population in Dodge Center, 23.29% were under the age of 15, 17.77% aged 15 to 29, 45.80% aged 30 to 64, 11.80% aged 65 to 84, and 1.34% were 85 years of age and older.

Race / Ethnicity

Dodge Center's population has become increasingly diverse:

Racial distribution of Dodge Center population: 85.80% are White, 0.25% are American Indian and Alaska Native, 2.51% are Asian, 9.54% are some other race and 1.91% are multiracial.

Dodge Center's racial breakdown clearly shows that there is an absolute majority of White population in the city.
