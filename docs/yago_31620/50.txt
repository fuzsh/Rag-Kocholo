Cornel West is an American philosopher, political activist, social critic, author, and public intellectual. He is Professor of the Practice of Public Philosophy at Harvard University and holds the title of Professor Emeritus at Princeton University. He has written 20 books and has edited 13. He is best known for his classics, Race Matters andDemocracy Matters.
