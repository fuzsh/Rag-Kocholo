Napoleon Solo is a fictional character portrayed by American actor Robert Vaughn in the popular 1960's spy series The Man from U.N.C.L.E.

Napoleon is U.N.C.L.E.'s Chief Enforcement Agent. He's commonly paired up with his partner, Illya Kuryakin. The two travel the world under the orders of Alexander Waverly to defend humanity against U.N.C.L.E.'s more sinister counterpart, THRUSH.
