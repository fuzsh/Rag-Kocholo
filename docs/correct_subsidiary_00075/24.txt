Avogadro's Instant Messaging Product to Augment Openwave Services OS

REDWOOD CITY, Calif. (May 10, 2001)-- Openwave Systems Inc. (Nasdaq:OPWV),

the worldwide leader of open, IP-based communications infrastructure

software and applications, today announced it has signed a definitive

agreement to acquire Avogadro, a telecommunications software infrastructure

developer. The closing of the acquisition is subject to closing conditions

customary for a transaction of this type.
