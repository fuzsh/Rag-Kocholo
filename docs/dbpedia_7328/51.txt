Universal HD, is a cable television network owned by NBC Universal designed for HDTV broadcasts. The channel exclusively broadcasts in high definition 1080i and shows a mix of unedited and uninterrupted films, dramas, TV series, sports, specials, and performance arts programming from the NBC Universal library. Most of the content is previously aired on one of other NBC Universal-owned networks, such as NBC, Bravo, USA Network, Sci Fi Channel, and Trio.
