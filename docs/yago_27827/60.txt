Adelaide of Saxe-Meiningen was previously married to King William Henry (1818 - 1837).

About

German Royal Adelaide of Saxe-Meiningen was born Adelaide Amelia Louise Theresa Caroline on 13th August, 1792 in Meiningen, Thuringia, Germany and passed away on 2nd Dec 1849 Bentley Priory, Middlesex, England aged 57. She is most remembered for Adelaide, the capital city of South Australia, is named after her. Her zodiac sign is Leo.
