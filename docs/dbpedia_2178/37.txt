About Dari

dARI is a band from Aosta, Italy that plays electro pop-punk music. They're quickly becoming popular with their first single, "Wale".

See more at their MySpace page.

Genres:

Alternative, Pop, Indie, Rock

Band Members:

Josh Kotras - Vocals, Adam Cunningham- Bass, Jake Widenhofer - Guitar, Sean McDonald - Drums, Chad Bell - Guitar

Hometown:

Decatur, Illinois
