マヌエル・アサーニャ

1. Manuel Azaña​Manuel Azaña Díaz (January 10, 1880 – November 3, 1940) was a Spanish politician. He was the first Prime Minister of the Second Spanish Republic (1931–1933), and later served again as Prime Minister (1936), and then as the second and last President of the Republic (1936–1939). The Spanish Civil War broke out while he was President. With the defeat of the Republic in 1939, he fled to France, resigned his office, and died in exile.
