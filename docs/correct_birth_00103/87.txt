Karviná

Karviná ranks 3,405th in number of biographies on Pantheon, behind Uttar Pradesh, Betzdorf Castle, and Lisburn. Memorable people born in Karviná include Radek Štěpánek, Petra Němcová, and Šárka Kašpárková. Karviná has been the birth place of many athletes, and tennis players. Karviná is located in Czechia.
