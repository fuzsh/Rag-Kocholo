BECKY HOBBS

B/w publicity photo from Joe Taylor Artist Agency and EMI America of Hobbs in light-colored blouse and teased hair, signed "with love" and "Happy New Year!" in red ink. Hobbs signed her O's with hearts on this photograph.

Photograph signed: "with love,/Becky Hobbs/Happy New Year!" in red ink. Hobbs signed her O's as hearts. B/w, 7¾x10 overall, image 6¾x8½ (one surface). Publicity photo from Taylor Artist Agency of Nashville, Tennessee and EMI America. Captioned: "Becky Hobbs". Hobbs is an American country singer and pianist. She began playing piano and writing songs at age nine. She recorded a Top 10 duet with Moe Brandy, Let's Get Over Them Together, shortly after moving to Nashville in 1981, and her 1990 version of Talk Back Tremblin' Lips peaked at no. 6 on CMT. She also co-wrote Conway Twitty's chart-topper I Want to Know You Before We Make Love. Hobbs is still releasing records, with her latest (as of this biography) being 2006's Best of the Beckaroo - Part 1. Lightly creased, otherwise fine condition.
