Maxime Chaya, first Lebanese to climb Mount Everest, will share stories from his daring adventures.

The Women's Auxiliary of AUBMC is organizing a presentation by Maxime Chaya about his daring adventures on Wednesday November 11, at 4:30 pm in West Hall, Bathish Auditorium.

Maxime Chaya was born in Beirut and he pursued his education overseas in Greece, France, Canada and the UK where he graduated with an Honors degree from the London School of Economics.
