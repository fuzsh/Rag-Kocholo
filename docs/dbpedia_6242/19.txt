The Maryland Space Business Roundtable (MSBR) and the Washington Space Business Roundtable (WSBR) will hold a joint event on February 1, 2023 from 6:00-8:00 pm ET. It will take place at the Zoca Mexican Restaurant, 319 Pennsylvania Ave., S.E., Washington DC (on Capitol Hill).

The speaker is Alicia Brown, NASA Associate Administrator for Legislative Affairs.

More information and registration instructions are on the MSBR website, which says:

Please join the Washington Space Business Roundtable and Maryland Space Business Roundtable for a special joint event featuring NASA’s Associate Administrator for the Office of Legislative and Intergovernmental Affairs, Alicia Brown. This pilot program will feature a networking reception near Capitol Hill and brief remarks from Ms. Brown and key committee staff from Capitol Hill regarding what’s to come for civil and commercial space policy in 2023.

The event will take place at Zoca Mexican Restaurant, located at 319 Pennsylvania Ave. SE, Washington, DC 20003 on Wednesday, February 1st at 6:00 p.m. ET. Doors open at 6:00 p.m. and the event will end at 8:00 p.m. Registration is required, and invitations are nontransferable.

Registration is $20 per person.
