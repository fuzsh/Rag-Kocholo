Welcome to Summitville ...

Summitville is located in Sullivan County<1>.

We aren't confident of the location of Summitville, so consider the above location as an estimate.<2>

While we don't have a date for the founding of Summitville, you might get a feel for it by knowing that the earliest mention that we've found (so far) was in a book dated 1895.

Summitville is 540 feet [165 m] above sea level.<3>.
