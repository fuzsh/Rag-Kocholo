GUADALCANAL DIARY (1943, b/w)

Channel 4, 1.15pm

Based on a nonfiction book by the news reporter Richard Tregaskis, Guadalcanal Diary re-creates the battle between US Marines and Japanese forces for a key airfield in the Solomon Islands that helped to turn the tide in the early days of the Pacific war. Preston Foster, Lloyd Nolan, William Bendix and Anthony Quinn are obliged to play patriotic ethnic stereotypes, although the director Lewis Seiler does give us a punchy soldier’s-eye view of foxhole gun battles and knife-edge courage. (93 min)
