James Cagney, Pat O’Brien, Gloria Stuart. A chronic troublemaker picks a fight and loses his girl and the fight to a naval officer. So he joins the Navy to get revenge against him for stealing his girl, only to discover she’s the petty officer’s sister. He makes lots of new enemies—until he’s called upon to stage a daring rescue. Directed by Lloyd Bacon. 1934/b&w/86 min/NR/fullscreen.
