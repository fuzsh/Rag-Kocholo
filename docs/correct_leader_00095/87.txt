At stake in this election:

The office of President of Sao Tome and Principe

Description of government structure:

Chief of State: President Fradique DE MENEZES

Head of Government: Prime Minister Joaquim Rafael BRANCO

Assembly: Sao Tome and Principe has a unicameral National Assembly (Assembleia Nacional) with 55 seats.

Description of electoral system:

The President is elected by absolute majority vote through a two-round system to serve a 5-year term.

The Prime Minister is elected by parliament.

In the National Assembly (Assembleia Nacional) 55 members are elected through a closed-list proportional representation system to serve 4-year terms.***

*** There are 7 multi-member constituencies.
