On September 18th, Chicago production duo The Hood Internet will leave behind the mashups and release their first album of all-original material. FEAT spans 10 tracks and features cameos from Astronautalis, The New Pornographers’ AC Newman, Class Actress, Cadence Weapon, Black Moth Super Rainbow’s Tobacco, Zambri, Hooray for Earth, and The Rosebuds. The pair have released a short album trailer, which is available at the bottom the page.

In support of the release, the duo will hit the road this summer/early fall for a chunk of shows. Check out those dates out just beneath the album tracklist.

02. One For The Record Books (feat. AC Newman and Sims)

08. Our Finest China (feat. The Rosebuds and Astronautalis)

09. Uzi Water Gun (feat. Millionyoung, Kenan Bell, and the Chain Gang of 1974)

10. These Things Are Nice (feat. Kid Static, Slow Witch, and Kleenex Girl Wonder)
