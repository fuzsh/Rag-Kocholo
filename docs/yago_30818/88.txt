Kainan Kelly Cramer, the president of Cramer & Sirras, a legal recruiting firm, and Mark Steven Bergman, an associate in the law firm of Paul, Weiss, Rifkind, Wharton & Garrison, both in New York, were married yesterday at the Army Navy Country Club in Arlington, Va. Rabbi Harold S. White officiated.

Mrs. Bergman, 40 years old and known as Kai, graduated from Russell Sage College. She is a daughter of Anne Supple Cramer of Newburgh, N.Y., and Alexandria, Va., and the late Capt. Harry Griffith Cramer 2d, U.S.A. Her mother retired as an elementary-school teacher in Newburgh. The bride's previous marriage ended in divorce.

Mr. Bergman, 33, is a son of Arlene Sterne of New York and Washington, and the late Paul M. Bergman. He graduated from the American International School in Vienna and magna cum laude from Bowdoin College. He received a master's degree in international affairs from the University of Virginia and a J.D. from American University.

His mother is an actress and playwright. His father was a Foreign Service officer, serving most recently as the First Secretary at the embassy in Ottawa.
