April 16, 2014 - WILL ARNETT is officially ending his marriage to AMY POEHLER. Arnett, 43, filed divorce papers on April 8th in Los Angeles Superior Court requesting joint legal and physical custody of their two young children, Archie and Abel, along with visitation rights. The couple married is August 2003 and separated in 2012.
