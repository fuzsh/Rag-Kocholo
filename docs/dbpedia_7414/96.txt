Tommy Tutone, originally formed in the late 1970s, is an American rock band best known for their 1981 hit single '867-5309/Jenny.' The group was led by Tommy Heath, who served as the band's lead vocalist. They gained significant popularity in the early 80s, blending power pop with rock influences, making a mark on the music scene of that era.

Their songs often explore themes of unrequited love and infatuation, capturing the emotional highs and lows of romantic pursuits. Additionally, their music delves into the thrill of anonymity, reflecting a sense of mystery and excitement. These themes resonate through their lyrics, making their songs relatable and timeless.
