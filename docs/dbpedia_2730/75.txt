Director Emir Kusturica directs a celebration of the extraordinary Diego Maradona, regarded by many as the world’s greatest modern soccer player, people’s champion, and inspiration to millions. In his 2008 film, Kusturica takes the viewer on an intimate journey through Maradona’s life, career and his ordinary day-to-day life.
