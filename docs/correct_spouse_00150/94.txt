Born Jun 25, 1954 in Barcelona, Catalonia, Spain

Born Rosa María Almirall Martínez, Lina Romay was a Spanish actress who often appeared in films directed by her long-time companion (and later husband) Jesús Franco. She appeared in approximately 109 Jesus Franco films made over a 30-year period, the majority of their films together were in the erotic film genre (including many X titles), but she was also featured in many of his horror, comedy and action/adventure films
