Ali is a 2001 American biographical sports drama film co-written, produced and directed by Michael Mann. The films stars Will Smith as Muhammad Ali, focusing on ten years of his life from 1964 to 1974.

Plot[]

With wit and athletic genius, with defiant rage and inner grace, Muhammad Ali (Will Smith) forever changed the American landscape. Fighting all comers, Ali took on the law, conventions, the status quo and the war, as well as the fists in front of him. Ali both ignited and mirrored the conflicts of his time and ours to become one of the most admired fighters in the world.
