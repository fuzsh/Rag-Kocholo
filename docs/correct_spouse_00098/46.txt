Actress has separated from husband Royston Langdon

After only five years of marriage it seems Liv Tyler and her Leeds born husband Royston Langdon are to split up. It was only three years ago they had their son Milo and seemed so happy but in a statement released last night a spokesperson said

'Liv Tyler and Royston Langdon have confirmed their separation. They remain good friends and devoted parents to their son Milo and are requesting that their family's privacy be respected at this time.'
