The Colorado based duo of Michaela Paetsch and Peter Biro create a uniquely personal recital experience, mixing classical favorites and new compositions.

Composer and keyboard artist Peter Biro is a graduate of the UCLA School of Fine Arts and currently resides in Colorado Springs. He has appeared as soloist and recitalist on piano, organ and harpsichord in the United States and Europe. As a composer he has had compositions premiered in both Los Angeles and Colorado. He enjoys woodworking and instrument building, and a number of his harpsichords are in use thought out the Rocky Mountain Region. He is organist and harpsichordist at St Mary's Church in Denver.
