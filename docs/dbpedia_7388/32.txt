Vita Construction Ltd is proud to be one of the Sponsors of Pavlos Kontides, Silver Olympic Medalist in Sailing Laser Class. In July 2012 Pavlos saw the culmination of all his efforts at the London 2012 Summer Olympics, he won the silver medal. Pavlos wrote his name with golden letters in Cyprus history, by giving his country its first ever and long-desired Olympic medal. Pavlos will be competing at the next Summer Olympic Games in Brazil.
