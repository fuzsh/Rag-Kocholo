PDM software provider and Auto-trol subsidiary Centra 2000 has announced the resignation of co-founder Susan Floyd, who leaves to join a major aerospace company at Kennedy Space Center as senior software manager. Floyd will continue her involvement with Centra 2000 product direction and marketing activities during the near term. Centra 2000 co-founder Steve Spradlin has been named as Floyd's replacement. "Steve's considerable experience as a computer scientist in the petroleum and aerospace industries, as well as being an early member of the Centra 2000 team, ensures the continued growth of Centra 2000's reputation in the marketplace," said Auto-trol president and CEO Howard Hillman.
