Go West is an American feature film of the western and comedy genres featuring The Marx Brothers. It was directed by Edward Buzzell with a screenplay written by Irving Brecher. It is based on the 1925 film of the same name starring Buster Keaton. The movie was produced by MGM and released on December 6th, 1940.
