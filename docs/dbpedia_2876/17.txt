In 2009, the Department of Health listed 721 public hospitals in the Philippines. 70 of these hospitals are managed by the Department of Health.

Under President Dutertes’ administration, all Filipino citizens are granted quality and affordable health care services through the UHC, Universal Health Care law.

Here is a list of public hospitals you can visit in Manila.
