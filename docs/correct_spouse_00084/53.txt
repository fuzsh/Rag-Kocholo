Milan hitman Alexandre Pato married renowned Brazilian actress Sthefany Brito last night in the church of Sao Francisco de Paulo in Rio de Janeiro. The ceremony took place at the Copacabana Palace and was attended by Milan director general Ariedo Braida.

“It’s official: Alexandre Pato and Sthefany Brito are husband and wife,” the Diavoli enthused on their official website. “Congratulations from the entire Rossoneri family. Representing the club and the team at Pato and Sthefany’s wedding was our technical director, Ariedo Braida.”

The groom wore a dashing white suit while the bride’s dress was reportedly designed by an Italian fashion house. [via futbolita.com and goal.com]
