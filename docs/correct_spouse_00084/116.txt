About Alexandre Pato

Alexandre Pato is a Brazilian football player. He plays as a forward for São Paulo and the national team of Brazil.

His Highlights/Legacy

He has won the FIFA Club World Cup, the Recopa Sudamericana, the Supercoppa Italiana, the Olympic Bronze Medal in 2008 and the Olympic Silver Medal in 2012 with Brazil.

His Personal Life

He was married for less than a year to actress Sthefany Brito. She filed for a divorce because of his constant partying. He has dated Miss Brazil Débora Lyra, Barbara Berlusconi and model Almeda Abazi in the past.

Fun Facts

Pato means "duck"!
