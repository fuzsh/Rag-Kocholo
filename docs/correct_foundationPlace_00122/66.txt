Intro and history: Temenos Group AG is a company specializing in software for financial services, with its headquarters in Geneva, Switzerland. The company was created in 1993, and has developed a solid reputation in the banking industry for its integrated banking software, particularly its core banking system known as T24. Temenos serves over 3,000 firms across the globe, including 41 of the top 50 banks. Industry: Software & Programming Product, Services and Operations: Temenos provides banking software systems to retail, corporate, universal, private, Islamic, microfinance and community banks. Its services include core banking, analytics, channels, payments, fund management, and others. Funding Details: As a publicly traded company, Temenos is funded through its shareholders. Its market capitalization is approximately $10 billion. Acquisitions: Temenos has made several acquisitions including Kony Inc, a digital banking SaaS company in 2019, and Avoka, a SaaS vendor of digital customer acquisition and onboarding solutions in 2018. Awards and Achievements: Temenos has received numerous awards, including the Best Digital Banking Solution award at the Asian Banker Technology Innovation Awards, and the Best Core Banking Product award from IBS Intelligence.
