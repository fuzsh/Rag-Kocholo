Born in Kharkiv, Hlushchenko began his career in the local Metalist Kharkiv academy, before transferring to the Shakhtar Donetsk academy in 2017.[1]

He played in the Ukrainian Premier League Reserves for several seasons and made his debut for the senior Shakhtar Donetsk squad in the Ukrainian Premier League as a second-half substitute in an away lost from Vorskla Poltava on 4 June 2023.[2]
