TAMIL FM is a Srilankan radio channel headquartered in Colombo. Constantly evolving and innovating its offerings, TAMIL FM 99.5 /99.7 draws a keen focus on its brand philosophy of ‘Radiola Oru Magic,’ forging an emotional connect with its listeners while continually creating offerings to suit local tastes.

TAMIL FM 99.5/99.7 is the first-ever radio station in Sri Lanka for Tamil Youths packed with passion, fuelled by Superhits, and driven by RJs with a style that is young, energetic, and classy
