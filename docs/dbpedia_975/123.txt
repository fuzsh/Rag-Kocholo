Saint James Episcopal Church in Monkton, MD is a vibrant and welcoming community that follows the teachings of Jesus Christ to address the challenges and opportunities of today. They believe in a balanced approach to Christian living through prayer, study, stewardship, and action. With a focus on spiritual nourishment, community, and inclusivity, Saint James offers worship services, Christian formation programs, outreach initiatives, and music opportunities for individuals of all ages.

At Saint James, individuals can find a place to worship, connect with others, and engage in meaningful activities that align with their faith. Whether seeking a church that welcomes children, returning to the church of their youth, or looking for a community that values the wisdom of scripture, Saint James provides a space for individuals to find spiritual fulfillment and a sense of belonging.
