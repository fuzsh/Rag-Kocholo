Come and experience the classic melodies of Duke Ellington’s Sacred Concerts at St. James’ Episcopal Church in Baltimore.

Part of St. James’ 200th Anniversary Jubilee celebration, the St. James’ Bicentennial Orchestra under the direction of Dr. John R. Lamkin II will render Ellington’s music supported by an all-star cast including Berklee School of Music Vocal Professor Gabrielle Goodman, the Baltimore City College Choir under the direction of Marcus D. Smith, and many noted musicians from the Baltimore/Washington area.

Don’t miss this opportunity to witness the magic of this jazz legend’s sacred music in the enchanting atmosphere of St. James’ Episcopal Church. This in-person event promises an unforgettable evening filled with powerful music and spiritual inspiration. Tickets are $45 per person.
