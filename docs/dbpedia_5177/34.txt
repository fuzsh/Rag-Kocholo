William R. Russell, 64, died peacefully on Thursday, June 2, 2022 at the Community Hospice House in Merrimack surrounded by his loving family. He is the beloved husband of Josephine (Lobrutto) Russell with whom he shared 35 years of marriage.

William was born in Presque Isle, Maine on October 12, 1957, and was the son of the late Richard and Lillian (Adams) Russell.

For many years, he worked as a sales representative for Advanced Metal Roofing.

In his free time, he enjoyed watching sports, wrestling and football were his favorites. He truly loved to travel. Traveling to many destinations with his wife, Josie; Hawaii was his number one destination. Above all, he cherished his time spent with his family.

In addition to his wife, Josephine, he is survived by his two daughters, Shawna Russell and her fiancé Shawn of Nashua and Lisa McDonald of New Hampshire; his brother, Andy Russell and his wife Debra of Portland, Maine as well as many nieces and nephews.

In addition to his parents, he was predeceased by his brother, Tom Russell and his sister Susan Russell.

Relatives and friends are invited to William’s visitation on MONDAY, June 6th from 3 until 5 PM at ROCHETTE FUNERAL HOME AND CREMATION SERVICES, 21 Kinsley Street, Nashua. His Funeral Service will follow in the Funeral Home at 5 PM. Interment to take place at the convenience of his family.
