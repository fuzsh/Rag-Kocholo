Jerry Lee Byers, 70, of Gardners, PA, passed away on Wednesday, October 12, 2022. He was born November 29, 1951, in Chambersburg, PA, to the late Dale Richard and Lois A. (Stache) Byers Sr.

He was a United States Navy Veteran and worked for the state of PA with 18 years of service. Jerry was a member of the Mt. Holly VFW post 7343.

Surviving are his siblings, Robin Louise Myers of Mt. Holly Springs, Dale Richard Byers Jr of Linglestown, and Rick Byers of Mt. Holly Springs. In addition to his parents, he was preceded in death by his sister, Patsy Byers and brother, Ray Byers.
