Adam W. Smith was born and raised in Pueblo and is a proud alumni of the East High School International Baccalaureate Program, where he graduated in 2002. Adam received BAs in International Business and Sport, Event, and Hospitality Management from The George Washington University in Washington DC in 2006. Adam enjoyed playing Division 1 soccer while at GW. After graduation, Adam worked in the hospitality industry until he moved to New Orleans, Louisiana to attend law school at Loyola University New Orleans College of Law. He received his J.D. and a Certificate of International Legal Studies at Loyola in 2019 where he graduated cum laude. Adam obtained his license to practice law in Louisiana before deciding to move back home to Pueblo to practice with his dad, Mickey W. Smith. Adam was licensed in Colorado in April of 2020.
