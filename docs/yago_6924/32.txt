Raquel Torres married to Stephen Ames aboard a liner . Raquel Torres , the Mexican screen actress , and Stephen Ames , former husband of Adrienne Ames , were married aboard the liner Lurline in Los Angeles harbour . After the ceremony the couple sailed aboard the same ship for a honeymoon in Honolulu . Photo shows , Raquel Torres and Stephen Ames after their wedding . 2 May 1934
