Ameritech and its five Middle Western Bell Telephone companies said they plan to re-enter the equipment market after they are separated from the American Telephone and Telegraph Company early next year. The Ameritech companies - Illinois Bell, Indiana Bell, Michigan Bell, Ohio Bell and Wisconsin Telephone - said they would provide state-of-the-art telecommunications equipment.
