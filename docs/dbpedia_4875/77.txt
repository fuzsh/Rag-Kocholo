A Vice President of the Samagi Jana Balawegaya P.Harrison said he has decided to extend support to President Ranil Wickremesinghe.

Mr Harrison said that in two months time a majority of the SJB members will join the United National Party.

Mr Harrison told the Lankadeepa newspaper that he was not satisfied about the progress made.

He said that the solution to the current problems would be a UNP President.

In a related devopment Badulla District MP Vadivel Suresh who is the General Secretary of the Lanka Jathika Estate Workers Union has said that he would extend support to MInister Harin Fernando who is now with the UNP.
