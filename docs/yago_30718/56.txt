The Wedding of Prince Antônio of Orléans-Braganza, son of Prince Pedro Henrique of Orléans-Braganza and Princess Maria Elisabeth of Bavaria, and Princess Christine de Ligne, daughter of the 13th Prince de Ligne and Princess Alix of Luxembourg, at the Château de Belœil on this day in 1981, with Royal Guests including her cousins Hereditary Grand Duke Henri, Hereditary Grand Duchess Maria Teresa of Luxembourg, Archduchess Marie Astrid of Austria and Princess Margaretha of Liechtenstein. The couple have four children and are the Heirs to the Headship of the Imperial House of Brazil.
