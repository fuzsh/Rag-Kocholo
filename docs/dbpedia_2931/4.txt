The Lord of the Rings: The Return of the King Developer(s) EA Redwood Shores Publisher(s) EA Games Series The Lord of the Rings Release Date(s) November 3, 2003 Genre(s) Action, hack and slash Mode(s) Single player, multiplayer Platform(s) Microsoft Windows, PlayStation 2, Game Boy Advance, GameCube, Xbox, Mobile, OS X

The Lord of the Rings: The Return of the King is a 2003 hack-and-slash video game based on the films The Lord of the Rings: The Two Towers and The Lord of the Rings: The Return of the King.
