The Lord of the Rings: The Return of the King is a 2003 action/hack and slash video game developed by EA Redwood Shores for the PlayStation 2 and Windows, by Hypnos Entertainment for the GameCube and Xbox, by Griptonite Games for the Game Boy Advance, by JAMDAT for mobile, and by Beenox for Mac. The game was published by Electronic Arts. The game was released on November 3, 2003 for Microsoft Windows, November 4, 2003 for PlayStation 2 and on November 5, 2003 for Game Boy Advance, Nintendo GameCube and Xbox.
