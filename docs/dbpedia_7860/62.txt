1979 - John Denver and the Ladies

The program features John Denver talking, singing, and dancing with five popular female celebrities of 1979. Cheryl Ladd, Valerie Harper, Cheryl Tiegs, Tina Turner and Erma Bombeck.

Director: Don Mischer

Producer: Jerry Weintraub

Together with John Denver Tina sings "Big Spender" and "Downhill Stuff".
