Whitworth University is a four-year, private (not-for-profit) school located in Spokane, WA. It is classified as Master's College and University with medium programs by Carnegie Classification

The Whitworth's 2024 tuition & fees is $50,920 for the academic year 2023-2024. 94% of the enrolled undergraduates have received grants or scholarships, with an average aid amount of $34,208.

For the academic year 2022-2023, the school has a total enrollment of 2,415 and the students to faculty ratio is 10 to 1 (10.00%).

Whitworth ranked 18th in Washington Colleges rankings. Whitworth University is accredited by Northwest Commission on Colleges and Universities.
