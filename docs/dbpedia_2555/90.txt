Niantic Inc, based in San Francisco, CA, is a leading augmented reality (AR) technology company that aims to enhance people's real-world experiences through innovative digital content. With a strong focus on community collaboration, Niantic is dedicated to creating a detailed map that allows developers to anchor digital content to the physical world, encouraging users to explore public spaces and connect with their neighbors.

Through their platform, Lightship, Niantic provides developers with the tools to build their own AR experiences, supporting hundreds of millions of users on billions of iOS and Android devices. With a commitment to using technology to engage people with the world around them, Niantic invites collaboration and offers opportunities for individuals and companies to join their network and contribute to the development of the real-world metaverse.
