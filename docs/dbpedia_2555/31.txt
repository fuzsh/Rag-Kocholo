Niantic Inc (Niantic) is a media company that provides gaming software solutions. The company offers augmented reality mobile games which include NBA All Worlds, Pikmin Bloom, pokemon GO and Ingress Prime. Its upcoming game project is Peridot. The company provides scaled augmented reality (AR) platform hardware. Niantic real-world AR platform enables users to interact with the virtual world blended with reality. The company operates offices in the US. Niantic is headquartered in San Francisco, California, the US.
