Where is Brookfield, New York ?

Brookfield is a town located in Madison County, situated in the central part of New York State, USA. Geographically, it lies southeast of the city of Syracuse and south of Utica. The town is surrounded by rural landscapes and is relatively close to both the Adirondack Mountains and the Finger Lakes region.
