Nashville-based musician Keegan DeWitt, who also spends time as a film-score composer (he’s studied under director David Mamet and actor Philip Seymour Hoffman), just released a new song called “Thunder Clatter.” DeWitt and band recently finished up Daytrotter’s fourth Barnstormer tour with Sondre Lerche, Guards and the Romany Rye, and they have a hometown show next month at the Mercy Lounge supporting Miami Horror.
