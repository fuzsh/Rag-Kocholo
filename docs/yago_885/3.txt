(1964, DCP, 118m, Swedish w/English subtitles) dir Mai Zetterling w/Harriet Andersson, Gunnel Lindblom, Gio Petré. In 1915, three pregnant women from different social backgrounds enter a maternity ward, revealing their interconnected lives. Zetterling’s debut film is an uncompromising take on gender, class, sex, and marriage. New Restoration!
