Who was Linda Ronstadt's now famous original stage band as a solo artist?

Linda was the lead vocalist of THE STONE PONEYS from 1964 until 1968. She was also backed by THE EAGLES during 1971 and 1972.

First what? I will assume you mean either hit single or album. Her first album was with the Stone Poneys called Stone Poneys Featuring Linda Ronstadt. Her first solo album was Hand Sown Home Grown.
