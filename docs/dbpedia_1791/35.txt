Bridal Veil, Oregon is located in the Pacific Time Zone, which means that the time is Pacific Standard Time (PST) during regular time and Pacific Daylight Time (PDT) when daylight savings is in effect.

Pacific Standard Time

When it is Pacific Standard Time, Bridal Veil, Oregon is UTC-08:00 which means that it is -8 hours offset from Coordinated Universal Time (UTC).
