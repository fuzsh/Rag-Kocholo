Pink Floyd Discography Books

Pink Floyd - Another Brick - The Illustrated Pink Floyd Story

Publisher: Omnibus Press, 1984, ISBN 0-7119-0521-5, 72 pages.

Synopsis: Reissue of the book, Pink Floyd - The Illustrated Discography.

Pink Floyd - A Discography

Author: compiled by Brendan James

Publisher: R. Lees, 2012, softcover, ISBN 978-1470131876, 136 pages.

Synopsis: A listing of Pink Floyd releases worldwide. Includes a picture of the the album cover followed by the track list, then text only listings of the release country with record company and catalog number.

Pink Floyd - Any Colour You Like - The Dark Side of the Moon on Vinyl

Author: by Stefano Tarquini

Publisher: The Bee Smart Book Co., NL, 2023, hardcover, 180 pages, ISBN: 978-90-831514-6-5, deluxe edition with signed and numbered card, plus tote bag. Limited edition of 500 copies.

Synopsis: A worldwide discography of the vinyl releases of Pink Floyd-The Dark Side of the Moon. Includes listings of the various issues by country, with catalog number, description, and color pictures of the labels.

Pink Floyd Discography - An International Floydography

Author: by Michael Soucy

Publisher: private publication, October 1996, 51 pages.

Synopsis: A listing of Pink Floyd and solo releases worldwide. Includes vinyl, CDs, 8-track tapes, laserdiscs and videotapes.

Pink Floyd - The Illustrated Discography

Author: Compiled by Miles

Publisher: Omnibus Press, ISBN 0-86001-783-4, reissued as Pink Floyd - Another Brick, 84 pages.

Synopsis: A 1981 discography of Pink Floyd. Covers British releases only. Includes some session work albums, and bootlegs.

Pink Floyd - Made in Japan - An Illustrated Discography from The Land of Rising Sun, Part 1: Analogic

Author: Lucilio Batini

Publisher: none given, U.S., hardcover, ISBN 9798411257687, 211 pages.

Synopsis: This book lists Pink Floyd releases from Japan, including LPs, bootlegs, 45s, cassettes, 8 tracks, video tapes, books and paper products. Includes color illustrations.

Pink Floyd on Forty-Five

Author: Charles Beterams

Publisher:

Aprilis, Netherlands, November 2008, ISBN 978-905994-245-5, 237 pages. (first edition)

Floydstuff Publishing, Netherlands, 2019, ISBN 978-90-826893-3-4, 256 pages, plus separate 16 page supplement. (second edition)

Synopsis: A book that documents all the vinyl singles released by Pink Floyd over the years. Includes full-color pictures of the labels and picture sleeves of singles from all over the world.

The first 100 copies of the first edition are numbered and include a copy of the Roger Waters picture sleeve single, Hello (I Love You), pressed in clear vinyl.

Pink Floyd - The Records

Author: Andreas Kraska

Publisher: Buchverlag Michael Schwinn, 1988, ISBN 3-925077-10-3, 201 pages.

Synopsis: An excellent German book, written in German, that includes discographies from Germany, U.K., Italy, U.S., and Japan of Pink Floyd singles, EPs, LPs, and CDs. Also includes a discography of vinyl bootlegs, plus a Pink Floyd biography, and lists of fanzines and tour dates.

Pink Floyd - Story & Discography

Publisher: Carlton Books Ltd., Japanese Exceed Press Pop Culture Series, 1999, ISBN 4-89369-739-0, 160 pages.

Synopsis: This Pink Floyd book, written in Japanese, includes the history and a discography of Pink Floyd. Although this is a Japanese book, the discography is of the British releases. Includes albums, singles, and session work, with pictures.

Bootleg Discography Books

Bootleg - The Secret History of the Other Recording Industry

Author: Clinton Heylin

Publisher: St. Martin's Press, 1995, hardcover, ISBN 0-312-13031-7, 441 pages.

Hot Wacks Book XV - The Last Wacks

Publisher: The Hot Wacks Press, 1996, ISBN 0-96980 80-3-8, 802 pages.

Synopsis: The sixth edition of Hot Wacks, the bootleg bible. This book lists both vinyl and CD bootlegs by title, with record company and catalog number, songs, sound ratings, source, and time. Includes 20 pages of Pink Floyd bootleg listings.

Hot Wacks Book - Supplement 1

Synopsis: First supplement book to Hot Wacks Book XV. Includes 3 pages of Pink Floyd bootleg listings.

Hot Wacks Book - Supplement 2

Publisher: The Hot Wacks Press, 1996, ISBN 0-9698080-0-3, 214 pages.

Synopsis: Second supplement book to Hot Wacks Book XV. Includes 3 pages of Pink Floyd bootleg listings.

Hot Wacks Book - Supplement 3

Publisher: The Hot Wacks Press, 1996, ISBN 0-96980 80-2-X, 238 pages.

Synopsis: Third supplement book to Hot Wacks Book XV. Includes 4 pages of Pink Floyd bootleg listings.

Hot Wacks Book - Supplement 4

Publisher: The Hot Wacks Press, 1996, ISBN 0-9698080-5-4, 256 pages.

Synopsis: Fourth supplement book to Hot Wacks Book XV. Includes 3 pages of Pink Floyd bootleg listings.

Hot Wacks Book - Supplement 5

Publisher: The Hot Wacks Press, 1997, ISBN 0-9698080-8-9, 248 pages.

Synopsis: Fifth supplement book to Hot Wacks Book XV. Includes 2 pages of Pink Floyd bootleg listings.

Pink Floyd Bootlegs

Author: Raymond Steeg

Synopsis: A private catalog of Pink Floyd bootlegs.

Pink Floyd - Guide des Compact Disc Pirates

Publisher: Pigs (Three Diferent Ones), France, edition 5.1, December 1995, 62 pages.

Synopsis: A French booklet, written in French, listing all the Pink Floyd bootleg CDs. Includes name of CD, length, label, matrix number, date, sources and song titles.

Record Companies

Fifty Years of Music - The Story of EMI Music Canada

Author: Nicholas Jennings

Publisher: Macmillan Canada, 2000, hardcover, ISBN 0-7715-7664-1, 168 pages.

Synopsis: A book about the history and releases of EMI Music Canada. Includes the story of Pink Floyd performing in Vancouver in 1973, the release of Dark Side of the Moon in Canada, and a brief history of Pink Floyd and their concerts in Canada. Also includes a complete discography of The 6000 Series of Capitol LP releases in Canada, including Pink Floyd releases, with release dates.

Great Record Labels

Author: Al Cimino

Publisher: Chartwell Books, 1992, hardcover, ISBN 1-55521-787-7, 112 pages.

Synopsis: An illustrated book covering the history of numerous record company labels. Includes EMI, Capitol, Virgin and others. Also, tells the story of Dick Asher's refusal to pay out on the Another Brick in the Wall (Part 2) single.

The Label - The Story of Columbia Records

Author: Gary Marmorstein

Publisher: Thunder's MouthPress, 2007, U.S, hardcover, ISBN 978-1-56025-707-3, 602 pages.

Synopsis: A book about Columbia Records. Includes a history of the label.

The Rise and Fall of EMI Records

Author: Brian Southall

Publisher: Omnibus Press, 2009, U.S., hardcover, ISBN 978-1-84772-244-7, 278 pages.

Synopsis: A detailed history of EMI Records by a former director of the company. Includes numerous mentions of Pink Floyd and their relationship with their former record company.

360 Sound - The Columbia Records Story

Author: Sean Wilentz

Publisher: Chronicle Books, 2012, U.S., hardcover, ISBN 978-1-4521-0756-1, 336 pages.

Synopsis: A history of Columbia Records, with coverage of many of the artists who appeared on the label. Includes a couple of pages on Pink Floyd.

Record Company Catalogs

January 1968 Tower Album Catalog

Publisher: Tower Records, U.S., 1968, 8 pages.

Synopsis: The official Tower Records catalog from 1968, with Tower Checklist. Came in a brown package with a January 1968 release sheet, and a Tower Demonstration Album for January. The Catalog includes a listing for the Pink Floyd album.

Records for All EMI 1969 Catalog

Publisher: EMI Records, U.K., 1969, 32 pages.

Synopsis: An EMI Records catalog from 1969, with currently available albums, catalog numbers and song listings. Features entries for the Pink Floyd albums More and A Saucerful of Secrets (stereo & mono).

Capitol Records 1971 Catalog

Publisher: Capitol Records, U.S., 1971.

Synopsis: The official Capitol Records catalog from 1971. Includes New Release Checklist, Complete Catalog Checklist, and Capitol Catalog with order forms, price lists, pictures, catalog numbers, etc. Sections include 1) Capitol LPs, 2) Apple, Blue Thumb, Chips, Harvest, Fame, Invictus, Shelter, Tower, and Zapple LPs, 3) Capitol of the World, 4) Angel (including Melodiya / Angel), 5) Seraphim, 6) Classics on Cassette and 8-track Tapes, 7) Capitol Cassette and 8-track Tapes, and 9) Singles- 45 rpm. Capitol LPs are listed in numerical order, illustrated alphabetically by artist, and gift sets are listed in numerical order. Included in the listing are the Pink Floyd albums Pink Floyd, A Saucerful of Secrets, More, Ummagumma, Atom Heart Mother, Relics and Meddle. Also in this book are order lists for Atlantic Records, Atco Records, and Cotillion /Embryo Records.

A Week of Capitol 1972 Catalog

Publisher: Capitol Records, U.S., 1972, 32 pages.

Synopsis: A Capitol Records promo catalog from 1972. Includes listing of Capitol albums for every week. Pink Floyd is listed in May 1972.

Pink Floyd-Bakkatalog, Switzerland 1987 Catalog

Publisher: EMI Switzerland, 1987, 4 pages.

Synopsis: An EMI Pink Floyd catalog from Switzerland in 1987. Includes the backkatalog of all albums. Also A Momentary Lapse of Reason flyer, with an order form that lists all titles, catalog numbers, MC-numbers, 8-spur-nummers, and codes.

Mobile Fidelity Sound Lab Original Master Recording Catalog 1991

Publisher: MFSL, U.S., April 15, 1991, 3 pages.

Synopsis: An Original Master Recording catalog from Mobile Fidelity Sound Labs. Comes in a glossy fold open cover with Pink Floyd The Wall Ultradisc on the front. Inside is a letter from MSFL dated April 15, 1991, informing customers that The Wall Ultradisc will only be available to order until September 1, 1991. Also included is a page showing all the Pink Floyd Original Master Record discs available, and an order form for The Wall Ultradisc and other Pink Floyd MFSL CDs.

Columbia Records - Pink Floyd MiniDisc Rollout Catalog 1994

Publisher: Columbia Records, U.S., 1994, 4 pages.

Synopsis: A Columbia Records catalog for the Pink Floyd MiniDisc rollout. Includes a complete listing of Pink Floyd MiniDiscs available, along with catalog numbers, bar codes, and selling points.

Mobile Fidelity Sound Lab Original Master Recording Catalog, Fall/Winter 1995

Publisher: MFSL, U.S., Fall/Winter 1995, 6 pages.

Synopsis: An Original Master Recording catalog from Mobile Fidelity Sound Labs. Comes in a glossy fold open cover. Has an explanation of the ANADISQ 200LP, and a listing of all the ANADISQ LPs available from Mobile Fidelity Sound Labs.

Pink Floyd U.K. EMI Trade Marketing Catalog 1998

Publisher: EMI Records, U.K., 1998, 4 pages.

Synopsis: An EMI Records Trade Marketng Catalog from 1998. Features the complete Pink Floyd catalog for both CD and vinyl, along with pictures, title, catalog number, bar code, and dealer price.

EMI Music Distribution Catalog, Volume 09, April 25, 2000

Publisher: EMI Records, U.S., 2000, 28 pages.

Synopsis: An EMI Records New Release Book from April 25, 2000. Features two pages of upcoming Pink Floyd releases, along with catalog numbers, reasons to buy, upcoming television & radio spots, pop merchandising (including poster and flatsters), and retail ads. Order form is in the back.

Pink Floyd / Roger Waters, Japanese Catalog 2005

Publisher: Sony Music Direct (Japan) Inc., Japan, 2005, 4 pages.

Synopsis: A catalog of Pink Floyd and Roger Waters releases in Japan on May 18, 2005. Includes pictures with catalog numbers and prices of all issues.
