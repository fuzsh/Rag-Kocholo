"Jamais Vu" is a song by Jin, J-Hope and Jungkook of BTS. It was released on April 12, 2019, and appears as the sixth track in their sixth mini album Map of the Soul: Persona and as the fourth track in their fourth studio album Map of the Soul: 7.

The song also appears as the fifth track on CD2 of BTS's first anthology album Proof.
