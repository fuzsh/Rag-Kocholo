Jamais plus is the fourth track on Alizée's third studio album, Psychédélices released on December 3, 2007. The entire song of 3 minutes and 26 seconds is in French except a part of the refrain where the name of the song, Jamais plus is also said in English (Never more). The song was written and composed by Daniel Darc and Frédéric Lo.
