An original costume from the production of Edward Scissorhands, the 1990 fantasy picture starring Johnny Depp under the direction of Tim Burton.

This shirt was custom made for the production and formed part of the wardrobe visioned by costume designer Colleen Atwood and worn by Johnny Depp as Edward Scissorhands. Bespoke made for Depp by Venice Custom Shirts of Studio City, California it features button cuffs with a pocket to the left breast, to the interior collar is a makers label which is tagged with the date 'MAR 90', shortly before the start of principal photography.

The shirt remains in excellent condition throughout with some minor soiling to the collar consistent with use during the production, very fine.
