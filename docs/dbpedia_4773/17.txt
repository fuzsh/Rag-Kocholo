Luis Alfonso Cruz, born on February 10, 1984, is a Mexican professional baseball shortstop and third baseman. He currently plays for the Generales de Durango in the Mexican League. Cruz has also had the opportunity to play in Major League Baseball (MLB) for teams like the Pittsburgh Pirates, Milwaukee Brewers, Los Angeles Dodgers, and New York Yankees. Additionally, he has played in Nippon Professional Baseball (NPB) for the Chiba Lotte Marines, Yomiuri Giants, and Tohoku Rakuten Golden Eagles. Cruz began his professional career with the Boston Red Sox organization in 2000 and later joined the San Diego Padres. Throughout his career, he has showcased his skills in various leagues and teams.
