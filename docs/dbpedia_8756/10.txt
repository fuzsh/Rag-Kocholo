In 1870-72, John Marius Wilson's Imperial Gazetteer of England and Wales described Horam like this:

HOREHAM, a hamlet in Sussex; 5 miles N by W of Hailsham. It has a post office, of the name of Horeham-Road, under Hurst-Green.
