Emily McLaughlin is Notable.

Biography

Emily[1] was born in 1928, the daughter of Frederick Chase McLaughlin and Vaughn Conley. She passed away in 1991.[2] and is buried in Glen Haven Memorial Park, Sylmar, Los Angeles Co, CA.[3]

She is best known for her role as Jessie Brewer on the soap opera General Hospital.

Emily McLaughlin married Robert Howell Brown, better known as the actor Robert Lansing, in 1956.[4] They had one child, Robert Frederick Orin Lansing born 1957 in New York.[5] The couple divorced in 1968.[6]

She married second to, Henry Herman McKinnies, Jr, who was also a well known actor whose screen name was Jeffrey Hunter. The couple eloped and were married in Ciudad Juarez, Chihuahua, Mexico on 4 Feb 1969, 4 months before his untimely death.[7]
