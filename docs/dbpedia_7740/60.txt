​From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Sebastian Junger (born January 17, 1962) is an American author, journalist, and documentarian, most famous for the best-selling book The Perfect Storm: A True Story of Men Against the Sea, his award-winning chronicle of the war in Afghanistan in his 2010 movie Restrepo, and his 2010 book War.
