Welcome to Gleaner

As an established supplier of bulk fuels, now in our 70th year, Gleaner possess a distinguished track record for supplying commercial fuels, agricultural fuels and heating oil.

As an authorised Shell Lubricants distributor for nearly 50 years, we have the capability and experience to cater to all of your oil needs.

Operating across Scotland ensures our customers benefit from our reliable delivery service and competitive pricing. Our dedicated team takes pride in providing an excellent customer service ensuring they learn the needs and requirements of our customers to build lasting relationships. Gleaner is proud to be an independent oil distributor and remaining a family owned company. This allows us to ensure our customers are at the centre of everything we do.
