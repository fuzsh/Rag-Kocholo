St. Louis Blues are a National Hockey League (NHL) team based out of St. Louis, Missouri. Presently playing in the Central division, the team was established in 1967. The official colors in the logo of the St. Louis Blues are royal blue, white, navy blue and gold and their mascot is Louie.
