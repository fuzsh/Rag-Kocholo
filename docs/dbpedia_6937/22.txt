St. Louis Blues Logo

The St. Louis Blues logo is a symbol of pride and tradition. It features a blue note, reflecting the city’s rich musical heritage. The iconic blue note is set against a background of navy blue and gold, representing the team’s primary colors.

Introduced in 1967, the logo has seen minor modifications over the years but has always retained its classic look. The blue and yellow color scheme is synonymous with the team’s identity. These colors symbolize strength, loyalty, and excellence on the ice.

The sleek, modern design of the current logo showcases a streamlined blue note with a slight shadow, giving it depth and movement. The use of navy blue adds a bold, powerful touch, while the gold accentuates the note’s elegant curves.

Overall, the St. Louis Blues logo is a timeless emblem that captures the spirit of the team and its deep connection to the city of St. Louis.
