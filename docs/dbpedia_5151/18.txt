Biography

Private David Marvin served with 8th Connecticut Regiment (1777), Continental Army during the American Revolution.

David Marvin is a DAR Patriot Ancestor, A074646.

David was born 2 October 1764 in Sharon, Litchfield Co., Connecticut Colony. [1]

Alternate DAR birth date is Dec. 2, 1764[2]

He married Deborah Baldwin. [3]

He died 19 June 1841 in Northampton, Fulton County, New York. USA [4]

Revolutionary War Service According to Sons of the American Revolution Membership Applications, 1889-1970 and DAR GRC search online, David served from Connecticut as a private under Captain Fitch. David Marvin was born 2 Dec 1764 in Sharon, Connecticut, British Colonial America and died 19 June 1841 in Northampton, New York.
