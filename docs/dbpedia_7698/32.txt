Chetan Korada is an Indian racing driver, born on 14 November 1986. He is notably the first driver in the world to win a race using synthetic feet and the first in Asia to compete in motorsport with a prosthetic leg. Despite being born with a deformity that led to bi-lateral amputation, he began his racing career in 2007 and achieved significant success including victories in the 2009 MMSC Summer Cup and participation in the JK Tyre FMSCI National Racing Championship.
