Taylor Jed Martin was born in Appleton Wisconsin. In his early teenage years he began writing radio spots and television commercials. Shortly he was recording voice-overs, became the on-camera personality for several regional television campaigns, along with producing and directing over a dozen commercial spots. Taylor Jed took off for Chicago in 2012 to pursue his entertainment career. Now residing in Los Angeles, he continues to train and audition booking numerous commercial, voice-over, and theatrical roles. His production company, Martin Broz, launched with his brother Reed Martin, drives forward with a passion in telling stories that change the way people think, feel and live their lives.
