Frances Lennon was an artist who was born in 1912. The artist's work has been offered at auction multiple times, with realized prices ranging from 31 USD to 926 USD, depending on the size and medium of the artwork. Since 2015 the record price for this artist at auction is 926 USD for Rags, Bones and Donkey Stones, sold at Capes Dunn in 2015. The artist died in 2015.
