Baldur von Schirach was born on 9 May 1907 in Berlin, Germany. He was a director, known for Napola - Elite für den Führer (2004), Nürnberg NSDAP Parteitag August 1-4, 1929 (1929) and Hitlerjunge Quex (1933). He was married to Henrietta von Schirach. He died on 8 August 1974 in Kröv, Rhineland-Palatinate, Germany.
