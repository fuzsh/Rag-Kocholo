Robert William "Bob" Hoskins (October 26, 1942 – April 29, 2014) was an English actor and director who portrayed Eddie Valiant in Who Framed Roger Rabbit.

His other work includes lead roles in The Long Good Friday (1980), Mona Lisa (1986), Mermaids (1990), and Super Mario Bros. (1993), and supporting performances in Brazil (1985), Hook (1991), Nixon (1995), Enemy at the Gates (2001), Mrs Henderson Presents (2005), A Christmas Carol (2009), Made in Dagenham (2010), and Snow White and the Huntsman (2012). He also directed two feature films.
