Peter Masterson was an American screenwriter, film producer, film director, theater director, and actor. Born in Houston, he had a multifaceted career in the entertainment industry, contributing significantly to film and theater. Masterson passed away at the age of 84 due to a fall. His work encompassed various roles, showcasing his versatility and dedication to storytelling through different mediums.
