Ice hockey player William Hodgson "Hod" Stuart (1879–1907) with the Pittsburgh Professionals of the International Hockey League (IHL) in 1905–06.

Hod Stuart played with the Pittsburgh Professionals in 1905–06 and for 4 games during the 1906–07 season. He also represented the Pittsburgh Bankers in the WPHL in 1902–03. His last club was the Montreal Wanderers.

Hod Stuart died in a diving incident in Bay of Quinte, Ontario on June 23, 1907.
