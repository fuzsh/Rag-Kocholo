Vodohod is a full-service tour operator and the largest cruise operator in Russia. For 17 years, we have been organizing cruises visiting the most interesting cities and locations of our country, providing the highest service and maintaining high quality standards as well as increasing the popularity of cruises as a comfortable and modern way of enjoying your holidays. Vodohod is the undisputed leader in the cruise industry in Russia, and one of the leaders in Europe in terms of the number of fleet units and passenger capacity. Every year about 110 thousand guests travel on our ships. We are proud that in 2021, Vodohod was deservedly recognized as the “Industry Leader” among passenger shipping companies in Russia.
