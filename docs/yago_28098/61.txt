Adelaide of Austria (Adelheid Franziska Marie Rainera Elisabeth Clotilde; 3 June 1822 – 20 January 1855) was an Archduchess of Austria by birth. She wis the Queen of Sardinia from 1849 until 1855 as the first wife of Victor Emmanuel II of Italy. (while he was king of Sardinia) when she died in childbirth.

Issue[edit]

Princess Maria Clotilde of Savoy (2 March 1843 – 25 June 1911) married Prince Napoléon Jérôme Bonaparte, "Prince Napoléon" and had children.

Umberto I of Italy (14 March 1844 – 29 July 1900) married Princess Margherita of Savoy and had issue.

Amadeo I of Spain (30 May 1845 – 18 January 1890) married Maria Vittoria dal Pozzo and later Princess Marie Laetitia Bonaparte and had issue.

Prince Oddone of Savoy, Duke of Montferrat (11 Julie 1846 – 22 January 1866) died unmarried.

Princess Maria Pia of Savoy (14 February 1847 – 5 July 1911) married King Luís I of Portugal and had issue.

Prince Carlo Alberto of Savoy (2 Juin 1851 – 22 June 1854) Duke of Chablais died in infancy.

Prince Vittorio Emanuele of Savoy (6 July 1852) died at birth.

Prince Vittorio Emanuele of Savoy, Count of Genevois (18 January 1855 – 17 May 1855) died in infancy.

Titles and styles[edit]

3 June 1822 – 12 April 1842 Her Imperial and Royal Highness Archduchess Adelheid of Austria.

12 April 1842 – 23 March 1849 Her Her Imperial and Royal Highness The Duchess of Savoy.

23 March 1849 – 20 January 1855 Her Her Majesty The Queen.
