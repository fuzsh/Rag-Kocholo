Marco Abbondanza is an Italian surgeon and ophthalmologist born on June 27, 1953, in Naples, Italy. He is renowned for his work in treating keratoconus, a progressive eye disease that affects the shape of the cornea. Abbondanza's contributions to the field of ophthalmology have made him a notable figure in medical treatment and eye care, particularly in addressing complex corneal conditions. His expertise has had a significant impact on patients suffering from vision issues related to keratoconus.
