Situated 400 metres from Lido Azzurro Beach, 600 metres from Lungomare San Marco and 2.6 km from Trentova Beach, La Dea dell'Abbondanza features accommodation located in Agropoli. There is a private entrance at the bed and breakfast for the convenience of those who stay. Free WiFi is available and private parking can be arranged at an extra charge. The bed and breakfast will provide guests with air-conditioned units offering a desk, a safety deposit box, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with a shower. The units are equipped with heating facilities. An Italian breakfast is available each morning at the bed and breakfast. At La Dea dell'Abbondanza, the family-friendly restaurant is open for dinner and lunch and specialises in Italian cuisine. Provincial Pinacotheca of Salerno is 48 km from the accommodation, while Salerno Cathedral is 49 km from the property.
