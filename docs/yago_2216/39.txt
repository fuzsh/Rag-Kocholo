About

Swedish Royal Ingrid of Sweden was born on 28th March, 1910 in The Royal Palace, Stockholm, Sweden and passed away on 7th Nov 2000 Fredensborg Palace, Fredensborg, Denmark aged 90. She is most remembered for Queen of Denmark from 1947 until 1972. Her zodiac sign is Aries.
