Mad Love is a 1995 teen romantic drama film directed by Antonia Bird and starring Chris O'Donnell, Drew Barrymore, Matthew Lillard, and Joan Allen.

Synopsis[]

High school student Matt Leland meets free-spirited Casey Roberts. When their romance is forbidden, the two of them decide to go on a road trip together where Matt learns that there is more to Casey than what she is showing.

Cast[]

Chris O'Donnell as Matt Leland

Drew Barrymore as Casey Roberts

Matthew Lillard as Eric

Joan Allen as Margaret Roberts

Jude Ciccolella as Richard Roberts

Amy Sakasitz as Joanna Leland

T.J. Lowther as Adam Leland

Kevin Dunn as Clifford Leland

Elaine Miles as Housekeeper

Liev Schreiber as Salesman

Trivia[]

Leonardo DiCaprio was considered for Matt Leland.

Drew Barrymore, Matthew Lillard, and Liev Schreiber would appear the next year together in Scream.

The darker material was toned down because Antonia Bird's previous film Priest was controversial for similar reasons.
