This tour includes visiting Serbia’s second biggest city Novi Sad and small baroque town of Sremski Karlovci, located in the northern province of Serbia - Vojvodina.

After hotel pick-up and 1,5 hour drive you will arrive to Sremski Karlovci - a small and historic town located 11 km south-east of Novi Sad.

Known for baroque architecture and wine production Sremski Karlovci represents the place of preservation of the Serbian culture in the 18th and 19th centuries. Most important building The Chapel of Peace, stands on a hill at the south end of the town. Wine tasting in one of the Karlovci’s wineries is also included.

The tour continues to Novi Sad, the second biggest city in Serbia and capital of Vojvodina. We will explore streets of the city and its most popular attraction - the Petrovaradin Fortress, where famous awarded music festival – EXIT is held every summer.

Return to Belgrade after free time in Novi Sad city center.
