ARCHDIOCESE OF KANSAS CITY IN KANSAS

Country: United States

Denomination: Roman Catholic

Established: 1850 as Apostolic Vicariate of Indian Territory East of the Rocky Mountains

Renamed in 1857 to Apostolic Vicariate of Kansas

Elevated in 1877 to Diocese of Leavenworth

Renamed in 1947 as the Diocese of Kansas City in Kansas

Elevated to Archdiocese in 1952
