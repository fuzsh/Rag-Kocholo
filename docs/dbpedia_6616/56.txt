Score Media and Gaming Inc (theScore), a subsidiary of PENN Entertainment Inc, is a media company that provides mobile sports applications including theScore, theScore eSports and theScore Bet. The company also offers various services through applications which include sports news, fantasy sports information, online and mobile sports news, video and editorial content, and schedules of leagues and sports. It offers its services through its subsidiaries. The company operates in various geographical regions including Canada and the US. theScore is headquartered in Toronto, Ontario, Canada.
