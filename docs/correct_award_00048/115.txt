Herbert A. Hauptman, 94, a Snyder resident who won the Nobel Prize in chemistry in 1985, died Sunday, Oct. 23, 2011. He was Western New York’s only Nobel Laureate. Dr. Hauptman won international recognition for developing mathematical methods for deducing the molecular structure of chemical compounds.
