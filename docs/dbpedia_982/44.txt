Thomas Lough

LOUGH---In Beaverton, May 24, Thomas Lough was found dead at his home by his son-in-law, Dr. Spencer. An inquest was held by Dr. Sturge and it was found that the deceased died of heart failure. Mr Lough was a soldier of the Civil War, in which he was much service. He was one of the first settlers of Beaverton. .
