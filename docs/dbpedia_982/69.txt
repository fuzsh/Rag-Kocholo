Thomas F. Lough Sr.

November 8, 1925 to February 19, 2022

On Saturday, February 19, 2022, Thomas F. Lough Sr. passed away peacefully. Beloved husband of the late Joan Lough. Loving father of William Lough, Shelley E. Asten, Thomas Lough Jr. and his wife Teresa, and Melinda Robbins. Cherished grandfather of Richard Robbins Jr., TW Robbins, Crystal Ebert, Tommy Lough, and Lisa Schikner. Devoted great-grandfather of Ruby Robbins, Rosie Ebert, Lilly Ebert, Freddy Ebert, and Ben Schikner.

Friends may call at the family owned Bruzdzinski Funeral Home P.A. 1407 Old Eastern Avenue, Essex at Route 702 (beltway exit 36) on Thursday, February 24, 2022 from 3 to 7 PM. Funeral service will be held at the funeral home on Friday, February 25, 2022 at 10 AM. Interment to follow at Holly Hill Memorial Gardens.
