Population

With 109,987 people, Santa Maria is the 61st most populated city in the state of California out of 1,573 cities. But watch out, Santa Maria, because San Buenaventura (Ventura) with 109,058 people and Downey with 108,816 people are right behind you.

Race & Ethnicity

The largest Santa Maria racial/ethnic groups are Hispanic (77.8%) followed by White (15.0%) and Asian (4.5%).

Median Income

In 2022, the median household income of Santa Maria households was $81,237. Santa Maria households made slightly more than Glendale households ($81,219) and Rancho Calaveras households ($81,144) . However, 9.5% of Santa Maria families live in poverty.

Median Age

The median age for Santa Maria residents is 29.3 years young.
