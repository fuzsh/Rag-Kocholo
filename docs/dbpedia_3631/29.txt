Santa Maria is located in south California. Santa Maria is between the Santa Barbara, San Luis Obispo county. Santa Maria has 22.76 square miles of land area and 0.64 square miles of water area. As of 2010-2014, the total Santa Maria population is 101,468, which has grown 31.06% since 2000. The population growth rate is much higher than the state average rate of 12.39% and is much higher than the national average rate of 11.61%. Santa Maria median household income is $50,753 in 2010-2014 and has grown by 38.89% since 2000. The income growth rate is higher than the state average rate of 29.47% and is higher than the national average rate of 27.36%. Santa Maria median house value is $252,200 in 2010-2014 and has grown by 73.21% since 2000. The house value growth rate is about the same as the state average rate of 75.60% and is much higher than the national average rate of 46.91%. As a reference, the national Consumer Price Index (CPI) inflation rate for the same period is 26.63%. On average, the public school district that covers Santa Maria is worse than the state average in quality. The Santa Maria area code is 805.
