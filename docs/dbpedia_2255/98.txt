Laís Ribeiro (born Laís Pereira de Oliveira on October 5, 1989) is a Brazilian model known for her work as a Victoria's Secret Angel. Prior to her modeling career, Ribeiro was training to become a nurse. After the birth of her son, she decided to pursue modeling upon the advice of a friend. In 2009, she walked for renowned designers such as Chanel, Gucci, Versace, and Dior, among others. Ribeiro has graced the pages of Vogue magazine in America, Brazil, Germany, and Italy, and has appeared in ad campaigns for Ralph Lauren, Christian Dior, and Tom Ford.
