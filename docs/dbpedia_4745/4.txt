Arizona Summit Law School (ASLS) completed its last day of instruction on August 17, 2018. The School commenced a Teach-Out, approved by the Council of the Section of Legal Education and Admissions to the Bar of the American Bar Association (ABA) in November 2018. The school will remain accredited by the ABA and licensed by the State of Arizona through the completion of the Teach-Out on May 31, 2020.
