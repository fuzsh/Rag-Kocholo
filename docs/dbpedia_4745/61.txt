The Phoenix School of Law is a private law school located in Phoenix, Arizona. Phoenix Law is the only law school in Arizona with both a part-time evening program and full-time program. The school's faculty includes former and sitting Arizona Superior Court judges. The school boasts a "student-centered" curriculum that both "serves the underserved" and produces "practice-ready" attorneys. The school is provisionally accredited by the American Bar Association. Phoenix Law School is operated by the for-profit InfiLaw Corporation.
