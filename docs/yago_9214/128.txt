Movie plot tags

A Mexican revolutionary and his band of men descend upon a small town and take it over. The revolutionary is a Robin Hood-type that forces the local businessmen to bend to his will, while the townspeople admire him for his cause. The revolutionary falls in love with the daughter of one of the businessmen and, despite her disdain for the man, pursues her romantically.
