Aim & Scope

Acta Archaeologica Lodziensia is the oldest archaeological journal in the Lodz research center. It has been published as a more than half a century. A rich variety of subjects, which are showing the possibility of archeology, characterizes published texts. Articles dedicated to issues between archeology and other disciplines (mainly history, history, science and technology) are often inserted. Chronological frameworks embrace a wide range of the Stone Age to the present day. [1]
