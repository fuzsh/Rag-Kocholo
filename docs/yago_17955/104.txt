All the Way Boys (1972) - (Terence Hill) Egyptian one-sheet

This isa 27" x39" Egyptian one-sheet designedby Moaty for the 1972 film All the Way Boys written and directed by Giuseppe Colizzi with additional writing credit for Barbara Alberti and Amadeo Pagani and starring Terence Hill as Plata. Plot summary: Salud [Bud Spencer] and Plata are making a meagre living by deliberately crashing aircraft in South America to defraud insurance companies. After one crash they find a diamond mining operation run by Mr. Ears [Reinhard Kolldehoff] and decide to compete with him as an airborne delivery service.

Cast and crew: Terence Hill, Bud Spencer, Rene Kolldehoff, Michel Antoine, Cyril Cusac, Giuseppe Colizzi, Barbara Alberti, Amedeo Pagani, Carlos Munoz, Riccardo Pizzuti, Marcello Verziera, Sergio Bruzzichini, Alexander Allerson, Ferdinando Murolo, Michel Antoine, Antoine Saint-John
