Lleyton Glynn Hewitt ( 24 February , 1981) (AM)[1] [1]is a retired Australian professional tennis player. To date, he is the most recent Australian player to have won a major men's singles title. In 2001 he won the US Open and in 2002 he won Wimbledon. From 2001 to 2022 he held the record for being the youngest ever World Number 1 player. He retired from competing in the singles tournaments in 2016 but continued playing doubles until 2020.

Lleyton married Rebecca Cartwright (Hayley Smith/Lawson) in 2005. In that same year he made a guest appearance on Home and Away.[2]
