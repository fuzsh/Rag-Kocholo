When Dwight David Eisenhower was born on 14 October 1890, in Denison, Grayson, Texas, United States, his father, David Jacob Eisenhower, was 27 and his mother, Ida Elizabeth Stover, was 28. He married Mamie Geneva Doud on 1 July 1916, in Denver, Colorado, United States. They were the parents of at least 2 sons. He lived in Fort Lewis Military Reservation, Pierce, Washington, United States in 1940 and New York City, New York, United States in 1950. He died on 28 March 1969, in Walter Reed Army Medical Center, Washington, District of Columbia, United States, at the age of 78, and was buried in Abilene, Dickinson, Kansas, United States.
