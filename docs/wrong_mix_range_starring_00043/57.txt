Jesse "The Body" Ventura (real name James George Janos born July 15, 1951) is an American actor and former politician and professional wrestler. After serving as a member of the U.S. Naval Underwater Demolition Team during the Vietnam War, he joined the WWF (now WWE) professional wrestling circuit. He also pursued a film acting career, with his best-known role in the film Predator as Blain.

Ventura held office as the mayor of Brooklyn Park, Minnesota from 1991-1994, and was the governor of the same state from 1999-2003.
