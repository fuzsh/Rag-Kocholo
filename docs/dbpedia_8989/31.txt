abt. 1810-1863

Lucy Ann Fry (Frye, Frey) Angell (Angel) (abt. 1810-1863) was born about 1810 in Moore Co., North Carolina. She was the daughter of Joseph Fry and Sarah Hartmann. She was sealed to Solomon Angell on 11 Sep. 1852 in Great Salt Lake City, Great Salt Lake Co., Utah Territory, and they had one child. She attended the Eagle Creek Branch in Humphreys Co., Tennessee when Wilford Woodruff served a mission there in 1835. She died 28 Aug. 1863 in Great Salt Lake City.
