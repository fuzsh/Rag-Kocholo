Aly Michalka Biography

Aly Michalka is an American actress and singer known for her roles in films like Easy A and television shows like Phil of the Future and iZombie. She rose to fame as a member of the pop duo Aly & AJ, alongside her sister AJ Michalka. As a musician, she has released several successful albums, including Insomniatic and Ten Years. Aly is also known for her philanthropic work and has been involved in various charitable organizations, including UNICEF and the Ryan Seacrest Foundation.

Aly Michalka Facts

Aly Michalka was born on March 25, 1989, in Torrance, California. She was raised in Seattle, Washington, and had an early interest in music and acting.

She began her career in show business with her younger sister, AJ Michalka, as a musical duo called Aly & AJ. The sisters released their debut album, "Into the Rush," in 2005, which went on to be certified gold by the RIAA.

Michalka's acting career took off when she was cast as Keely Teslow in the Disney Channel series "Phil of the Future" in 2004. She played the best friend of the show's main character and appeared in all 43 episodes of the series.

In addition to "Phil of the Future," Michalka has appeared in a number of television shows and movies over the years. Some of her most notable roles include Rhiannon Abernathy in "Easy A," Marti Perkins in "Hellcats," and Peyton Charles in "iZombie."

Michalka is also an accomplished musician and has continued to release music with her sister under the name Aly & AJ. In 2017, they released their first single in 10 years, "Take Me," and followed it up with an EP titled "Ten Years" later that year.

In 2019, Michalka was cast as Betsy in the comedy series "Schooled," a spin-off of "The Goldbergs." She appeared in 35 episodes of the show before it was cancelled in 2020.

Outside of her work in the entertainment industry, Michalka is also an advocate for animal rights and has worked with organizations such as PETA to promote the ethical treatment of animals.

Michalka married film producer Stephen Ringer in 2015 after dating for several years. The couple has one child together, a daughter named Keeva Jane, who was born in 2019.
