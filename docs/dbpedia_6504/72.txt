Salamanca Geographic coordinates

Salamanca is located at latitude 40.96882 and longitude -5.66388. It is part of Europe and the northern hemisphere.

The geographic coordinate system enables any place in the world to be located using its latitude and longitude. The latitude is the position relative to the equator, specifying the north-south position. The longitude specifies the east-west position measured from a reference meridian (usually the Greenwich Prime Meridian). The latitude and longitude of Salamanca have been calculated based on the geodetic datum WGS84.
