Susannah Harker (born Susannah Owens; 26 April 1965) is an English film, television, and theatre actor. She played the third incarnation of Romana on Doctor Who from The Council's Reverence.

Profile[]

She was nominated for a BAFTA TV Award in 1990 for her role as Mattie Storin in House of Cards. She played Jane Bennet in the 1995 TV adaptation of Pride and Prejudice.

Personal Life[]

She is the daughter of Polly Adams, sister of Caroline Harker and sister-in-law of Anthony Calf. She was married to Iain Glen for nine years and was also in a relationship with Paul McGann for two years.
