Susannah Harker

Susannah Harker is an English film, television, and theatre actor. She was nominated for a BAFTA TV Award in 1990 for her role as Mattie Storin in House of Cards. She is also known for her role as Jane Bennet in the 1995 BBC TV adaptation of Pride and Prejudice.

Harker has acted in both contemporary and classic works, on stage, in movies and in TV series. In 1990â€“91 she appeared alongside Clive Owen in Chancer. She later played Dinah Morris in the 1991 adaptation of Adam Bede. She is featured as Emma Fitzgerald, the love interest of Superintendent Tyburn (Trevor Eve) in the BBC TV series, Heat of the Sun (1998). She appeared in the play, On the Shore of the Wide World.

She played Sapphire in Big Finish Productions' audio revival of Sapphire & Steel. In 2003 she played Clare Keightley in the audio version of the Doctor Who adventure Shada, alongside Paul McGann.
