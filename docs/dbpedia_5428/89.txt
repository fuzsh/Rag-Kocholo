Timothy Dalton (born 21 March 1946) is a British actor. Dalton was in a relationship with actress Vanessa Redgrave (with whom he appeared in the 1979 film Agatha) between 1971 and 1986.[1]

Agatha Christie related acting roles[]

1979 - Agatha (1979 film) as Archie Christie

2006 - Agatha Christie's Poirot, The Sittaford Mystery as Clive Trevelyan
