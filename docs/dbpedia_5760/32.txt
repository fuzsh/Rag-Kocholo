Embark on an adventurous gravel route that takes you through various scenic landscapes and introduces you to some fascinating highlights. With a moderate difficulty level, this route is perfect for gravel enthusiasts seeking a mix of challenging terrain and breathtaking views. Explore the picturesque town of Thunstetten, conquer the wooded trails of Rehhubel, discover the historical city of Solothurn, admire the beauty of Wangen an der Aare, take in the panoramic vistas from Gensberg, and visit the charming town of Langenthal.
