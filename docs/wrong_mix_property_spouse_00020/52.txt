Yuri Komarov

Sebastian Koch (born May 31, 1962) is a German actor who portrayed the role of Yuri Komarov in A Good Day to Die Hard. Koch also appeared in films such as The Lives of Others, The Tunnel, Black Book, The Shell Seekers, Sea Wolf and Unknown (with Liam Neeson).
