Jun 26, 2002 - The Phoenix Suns selected Amar'e Stoudemire in Round 1 with Pick 9 in the 2002 NBA Draft. Jul 5, 2002 - Amar'e Stoudemire signed a multi-year contract with the Phoenix Suns. Oct 27, 2004 - The Phoenix Suns exercised their Team Option to extend the contract of Amar'e Stoudemire. Oct 3, 2005 - Amar'e Stoudemire signed a rookie scale extension with the Phoenix Suns. Jun 30, 2010 - Amar'e Stoudemire exercised an Early Termination Option to void the remaining seasons on his contract. Jul 1, 2010 - Amar'e Stoudemire, previously with the Phoenix Suns, became a free agent. Jul 8, 2010 - Amar'e Stoudemire signed a multi-year contract with the Phoenix Suns as part of an eventual sign and trade to New York Knicks. Jul 9, 2010 - Amar'e Stoudemire was acquired by the New York Knicks from the Phoenix Suns in exchange for a conditional 2012 2nd round pick (NYK own). Dec 18, 2012 - The New York Knicks assigned Amar'e Stoudemire to the Erie BayHawks (2008) of the G League. Dec 21, 2012 - The New York Knicks recalled Amar'e Stoudemire from the Erie BayHawks (2008) of the G League. Feb 16, 2015 - The New York Knicks placed the contract of Amar'e Stoudemire on waivers. Feb 18, 2015 - Amar'e Stoudemire, previously with the New York Knicks, became a free agent. Feb 18, 2015 - Amar'e Stoudemire signed a contract with the Dallas Mavericks. Jul 1, 2015 - Amar'e Stoudemire, previously with the Dallas Mavericks, became a free agent. Jul 10, 2015 - Amar'e Stoudemire signed a contract with the Miami Heat. Jul 1, 2016 - Amar'e Stoudemire, previously with the Miami Heat, became a free agent. Jul 10, 2016 - The Miami Heat renounced their free-agent exception rights to Amar'e Stoudemire. Jul 26, 2016 - Amar'e Stoudemire signed a contract with the New York Knicks. Jul 26, 2016 - The New York Knicks placed the contract of Amar'e Stoudemire on waivers.
