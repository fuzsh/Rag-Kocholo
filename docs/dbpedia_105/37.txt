Cardinal Ambrozic Catholic Secondary School is a secondary school in the Bram East neighbourhood of Brampton. Administered by the The Dufferin-Peel Catholic District School Board, the school has approximately 1320 students between 9 and 12. In the most recent year tests were administered, 2022 - 2023, the school scored an average of 92% in English and 50% in Math on their provincial standardized tests.
