Frederick, Duke of Saxe-Hildburghausen (29 April 1763 – 29 September 1834), was the sovereign duke of Saxe-Hildburghausen (1780–1826) then duke of Saxe-Altenburg (1826–1834).

Issue[edit]

Prince Joseph Georg Karl Friedrich of Saxe-Hildburghausen (12 June 1786 – 30 July 1786) died in infancy.

Princess Charlotte of Saxe-Hildburghausen (17 June 1787 – 12 December 1847) married Prince Paul of Württemberg and had issue.

Princess Caroline Auguste of Saxe-Hildburghausen (born and died 29 July 1788)

Joseph, Duke of Saxe-Altenburg (27 August 1789 – 25 November 1868) married Duchess Amelie of Württemberg and had issue.

Princess Friederike Luise Marie Caroline Auguste Christiane of Saxe-Hildburghausen (18 January 1791 – 25 March 1791) died in infancy.

Princess Therese of Saxe-Hildburghausen (8 July 1792 – 26 October 1854) married Ludwig I of Bavaria and had issue.

Princess Luise of Saxe-Hildburghausen (28 January 1794 – 6 April 1825) married William, Duke of Nassau and had issue.

Prince Franz Friedrich of Saxe-Hildburghausen (13 April 1795 – 28 May 1800) died in infancy.

Georg, Duke of Saxe-Altenburg (24 July 1796 – 3 August 1853)

Prince Fredrich Wilhelm Karl Joseph of Saxe-Hildburghausen (4 October 1801 – 1 July 1870) died in infancy.

Prince Maximilian Karl Adolf Heinrich of Saxe-Hildburghausen (19 February 1803 – 29 March 1803) died in infancy.

Prince Eduard of Saxe-Altenburg (3 July 1804 – 16 May 1852) married twice and had issue.
