Boxing Legends of the Ring is a sports simulation game for the Super Nintendo Entertainment System (SNES) focused on the world of professional boxing. Released in 1993, it falls under the fighting game genre. Players step into the ring as one of eight middleweight boxing legends, including Sugar Ray Leonard and Thomas Hearns, each with unique moves and fighting styles. The game features a career mode where players fight their way to the top, and a multiplayer mode for head-to-head bouts. Authentic boxing techniques and strategies are essential to mastering this title.
