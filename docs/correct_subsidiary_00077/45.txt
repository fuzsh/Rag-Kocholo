About Us

Addalot Consulting has its roots in Ericsson, Q-Labs and Det Norske Veritas dated back to 1989. During all these years we have been offering services with focus on process improvements in all software engineering related disciplines e.g. requirements engineering, architecture, coding, verification and validation. Addalot is a consulting company that helps companies to industrialize their software and systems processes. With robust processes, our customers are more competitive because they complete more projects on time, within budget, and with the appropriate level of quality. Addalot provides process improvement, quality verification and SW-safety services. Our expertise includes CMMI-DEV, ISO 26262, ISDS DNV OS-D203, ISO 15504-SPICE, COBIT, and Scrum. Our consulting approach covers all phases of a client´s improvement efforts: setting strategy, assisting with practical implementation, and measuring and evaluating the results. Our focus is on the Scandinavian market working were we address large and small clients in a variety of domains.
