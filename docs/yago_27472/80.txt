by Aaron Krach

New York author Paul Auster will publish the screenplay for his upcoming feature,

“Lulu On The Bridge” with Henry Holt and Faber and Faber. The book will include

the screenplay and essays by Auster and his creative collaborators on the film.

The film just completed principal photography in New York and Dublin, Ireland.

“Lulu” star Harvey Keitel, Mira Sorvino, Gina Gershon and Willem Dafoe, and is

being produced by Redeemable Features, who has an ongoing relationship with Henry

Holt to develop new projects. Auster is making his directorial debut with “Lulu”.

He was the co-director of “Blue In the Face,” with Wayne Wang.
