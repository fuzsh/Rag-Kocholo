name /gps/data/velocity/{station}/beta

URL: https://web-services.unavco.org/gps/data/velocity/WTCF/beta?analysisCenter=pbo&referenceFrame=igs08&report=short&solutionType=snaps

link function download

Description Outputs GPS crustal motion velocity field estimates. Results from daily GPS station position solutions are combined to generate long-term velocity estimate solutions of stations in IGS08 and NAM08 (North America fixed) reference frames. Station offsets due to earthquakes and equipment changes are estimated and low-quality outliers due to snow, for example, are removed from the velocity estimate solutions. More detailed descriptions are provided here: https://www.unavco.org/data/web-services/documentation/gps-velocity-documentation.html
