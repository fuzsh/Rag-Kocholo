Biography

Danny Lux is one of the most prolific composers in Hollywood, having composed music for shows such as Grey's Anatomy, Raising Hope, My Name is Earl and The Good Wife, among many others.

Lux has always been passionate about music and began his career straight out of high school with a dream and a pair of drumsticks in his pocket. He had the opportunity of a lifetime to work with the infamous Mike Post. After working with Post for nearly a decade, Lux set out on his own and is now in high demand for a variety of television shows.

Lux is known for his speed and masterful ability to manage multiple projects at once. He has won 3 BMI awards and was nominated for 2 Primetime Emmy Awards for his work on Ally McBeal, NYPD Blue, Profiler and Crisis.
