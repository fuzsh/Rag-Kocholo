Death of Fernando Felipe de Orleans. Ferdinand Philippe d'Orléans. (Palermo, September 3, 1810 - Sablonville, July 13, 1842) was the eldest of the sons of Luis Felipe I, king of the French (1830-1848) and María Amelia de Borbón Dos Sicilias. Duke of Chartres since his birth, and Duke of Orleans as well as the Royal Prince of France when his father was crowned king.
