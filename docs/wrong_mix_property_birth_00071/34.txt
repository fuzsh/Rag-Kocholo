Ringo Starr (born 1940) is a British musician, singer-songwriter and actor who achieved international fame as the drummer for The Beatles. He is a Rock and Roll Hall of Fame inductee who has released over 20 solo albums on various labels since 1970. Key albums include "Ringo" (1973), "Goodnight Vienna" (1974), "Ringo the 4th" (1977) and "Give More Love" (2017, Universal Music Group). He has won multiple Grammy Awards, an Academy Award, and a Golden Globe.
