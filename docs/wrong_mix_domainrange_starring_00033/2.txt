Tim Burns

Actor | Writer | Director

Tim Burns is a Canadian writer and producer for Canadian and American television; more popularly known as the show runnerexecutive producer of the Teletoon-Disney Channel shared, supernatural comedy-drama series, My Babysitter's a Vampire and for writing its TV pilot film. He was also supervising writer of the third season of Crank Yankers and was a composer on The Sunny Side Up Show.
