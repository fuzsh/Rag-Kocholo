KATHMANDU, Dec. 24, 2016 - Visiting Party Secretary of the Communist Party of China Chengdu Committee Tang Liangzhi (L) and Chief of Kathmandu Metropolitan City Rudra Singh Tamang sign an agreement in Kathmandu, Nepal, Dec. 23, 2016. Nepali capital city Kathmandu and Chinese city Chengdu have formally entered into the bond of sister cities.
