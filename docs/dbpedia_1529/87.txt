Asya's debut EP, "I Feel Eternity," is set to be released on December 29th! This EP represents Asya's first independent project, where she wears both the hats of a singer-songwriter and a producer, meticulously crafting every detail of the album. Additionally, this album marks her initial venture into the music scene in Norway.

With a total of five songs, featuring one in English and four in Turkish, the album aims to showcase the beauty and unique expressions of Asya's native language to her listeners. She hopes to highlight the richness of the Turkish language and invites audiences to appreciate its beauty. Stay tuned for more insights into Asya's music and journey!
