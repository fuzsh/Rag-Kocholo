Silapathar Population, Religion, Caste, Working Data Dhemaji, Assam - Census 2011

Silapathar is a Town Committee city situated in Sissibargaon circle of Dhemaji district. The Silapathar city is divided into 4 wards for which elections are held every 5 years. As per the Population Census 2011, there are a total 5,173 families residing in the Silapathar city. The total population of Silapathar is 25,662 out of which 13,350 are males and 12,312 are females thus the Average Sex Ratio of Silapathar is 922.

The population of Children aged 0-6 years in Silapathar city is 3086 which is 12% of the total population. There are 1600 male children and 1486 female children between the age 0-6 years. Thus as per the Census 2011 the Child Sex Ratio of Silapathar is 929 which is greater than Average Sex Ratio (922).

As per the Census 2011, the literacy rate of Silapathar is 83.2%. Thus Silapathar has a higher literacy rate compared to 72.7% of Dhemaji district. The male literacy rate is 87.72% and the female literacy rate is 78.39% in Silapathar.

Silapathar Town Committee has total administration over 5,173 houses to which it supplies basic amenities such as water and sewerage. It is also authorized to build roads within Town Committee limits and impose taxes on properties coming under its jurisdiction.

Working Population - Silapathar
