Bill Gates, born on October 28th in 1955, is a famous entrepreneur who is best known for starting Microsoft.

Microsoft is a leading software and hardware company known for its products such as Windows Office (Word, PowerPoint, Excel), XBox and WindowsPhone. Microsoft even has its own venture capital fund and accelarator.

After leaving Microsoft, Bill with his wife Melinda is active in helping the poor and the ill with their Bill & Melinda Gates foundation.
