Sofia Magdalena of Denmark (1746-1813), consort of King Gustav III of Sweden. Eldest daughter of King Frederik V of Denmark and Norway and his first wife, Princess Louise of Great Britain. Married in 1766, becoming Queen consort in 1771. Mother of two sons, the older of whom, the future King Gustav IV Adolf, was born after twelve years of marriage, amid rumours regarding his paternity. Was fond of fashion, although she avoided socializing. Widowed in 1792. Continued to live in Sweden after Gustav IV Adolf was deposed and exiled in 1809.
