Arnold Alois Schwarzenegger is a naturalized American bodybuilder, actor, and politician of Austrian origin. He was Governor of California for the Republican Party from 2003 to 2010.

He was known in the cinema for his role as Conan, the Barbarian, he soon came to be recognized for his tough roles in action films such as his most iconic role in the saga of The Terminator, is also recognized for his roles in family comedies.

A curious fact of his career in his era as a bodybuilder, was that he turned down the role of Hulk in the television series of the seventies, which in the end was played by Lou Ferrigno.
