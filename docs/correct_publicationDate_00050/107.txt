The Integral Trees is a science fiction novel by American writer Larry Niven. It was first published in 1968. The book tells the story of a group of people who travel to a strange planet, known as the Integral Tree, and find that it is a place where time and space are different.; 8vo; 240 pages; Signed by Author.
