Established in 1995, Geneva Business School is an international private Institution which is located in Geneva and has its main campuses in Geneva, Barcelona, and Madrid. Geneva Business School has more than 400 international students. The university has faculty from all over the world and the students of the university represent over 80 nationalities. Geneva Business School courses MBA, Fine Art International Management, DBA, and diverse continued education options which are 100% in English, and the university is committed to using new technology.

Eduniversal has given 3 Palmes of Excellence to the school and the CEO Magazine Best Business School in the World has placed the school among the Top 100. The city of Geneva is the home of the United Nations and the World Health Organization. The university is ideal for students with an international mindset.
