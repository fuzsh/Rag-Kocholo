Shame is a British drama movie directed by Steve McQueen, written by McQueen and Abi Morgan. Starring Michael Fassbender, Carey Mulligan, James Badge Dale, Lucy Walters, Nicole Beharie, Alex Manette, and Hannah Ware, the film follows the story of Brandon who is a bachelor executive, and his life falls apart after his lounge singer sister shows up. It was released on 13 January 2012 in the United Kingdom and nominated for the 2012 Golden Globes for "Best Performance by an Actor in a Motion Picture - Drama" (Michael Fassbender). Shame was mainly shot in Manhattan, New York City, NY, USA.

Where was Shame (2011) Filmed?

Shame (2011) was filmed in 28 St Station, 9 West 31st St (Sullivan's Apartment exterior), Essex St, New York City, Pier 54, The Standard Hotel and The Top of the Standard.
