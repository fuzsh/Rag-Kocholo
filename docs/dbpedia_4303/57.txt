Ben Moody was born on 22 January 1981 in Little Rock, Arkansas, USA. He is an actor and composer, known for Fantastic Four (2005), Daredevil (2003) and Terminator 5: Smert Gollivuda (2004).

Along with Zack Ward has begun a movie production company called Makeshift Productions, which will release their first feature film; a horror movie tentatively titled Zombies Ate My Mom, in 2005.
