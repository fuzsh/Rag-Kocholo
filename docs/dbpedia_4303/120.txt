Ben Moody is a writer-director based out of Los Angeles.

As a screenwriter, Ben has written nine screenplays in a variety of genres that have placed in such notable competitions as ISA’s “Table Read My Screenplay” and the Screenwriter's Network competition.

His fish-out-of-water crime comedy, THE OTHER SARATOGA, has received several 8's on the Blacklist and currently sits in the top 20 of all scripts with over 10 ratings.

As of May 2024, he is in post-production for DEEP CUTS, an 8-minute proof-of-concept for his horror comedy script of the same name.
