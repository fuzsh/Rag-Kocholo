Saruj (Persian: ساروج‎, also Romanized as Sārūj; also known as Sāvāj)[1] is a village in Derakhtengan Rural District, in the Central District of Kerman County, Kerman Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its existence was noted, but its population was not reported.[2] It is 500.90 miles away from the capital, Tehran which is equal to around 806.12 kilometers. Saruj is a small village, which is placed in mountains which the population is very small.
