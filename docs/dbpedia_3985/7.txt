Kerman Factory Housing (Persian: کارخانه خانه سازي کرمان‎ – Kārkhāneh Khāneh Sāzī-ye Kermān) is a company town and village in Sar Asiab-e Farsangi Rural District, in the Central District of Kerman County, Kerman Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 173, in 40 families.[1]
