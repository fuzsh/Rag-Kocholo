Village in Kerman, Iran

Deh Now-e Salar (Persian: ده نو سالار, also Romanized as Deh Now-e Sālār; also known as Deh Now)[1] is a village in Takab Rural District, Shahdad District, Kerman County, Kerman Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 284, in 63 families.[2]
