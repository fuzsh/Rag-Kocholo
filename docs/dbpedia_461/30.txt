Walid Abd-Rabo Al-Jahdali (Arabic: وليد عبدربه الجحدلي, born 1 June 1982) is a former Saudi Arabian footballer who played as a defender. He started his career with Al-Ahli and started in four of their 2005 AFC Champions League matches, before leading the Jeddah-based side to the Crown Prince Cup and the Saudi Federation Cup that same season. Walid made several appearances for the Saudi Arabia national football team, including five FIFA World Cup qualifying matches. He also represented Saudi Arabia at the 2007 AFC Asian Cup. (en)
