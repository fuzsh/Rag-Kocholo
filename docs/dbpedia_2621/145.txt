It Wasn’t God Who Made Honky Tonk Angels is a 1952 country song written by J. D. "Jay" Miller, and originally recorded by Kitty Wells. It was an answer song to the Hank Thompson hit "The Wild Side of Life." The song, became the first No. 1 Billboard country hit for a solo woman artist.
