Saovabha Phongsri

Saovabha Phongsri (Thai: เสาวภาผ่องศรี; RTGS: Saowapha Phongsi (Pronunciation)) birth title : Her Royal Highness Princess Saovabha Phongsri was an agnatic half-sister, royal wife and supreme chief queen of King Chulalongkorn (Rama V) of Thailand, and mother of both King Vajiravudh (Rama VI) and King Prajadhipok (Rama VII) .
