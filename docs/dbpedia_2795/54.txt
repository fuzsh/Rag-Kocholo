Oconee Fall Line Technical College (OFTC) is a technical college located in Louisville, GA, offering a wide range of credit and non-credit programs of study. With campuses throughout its 11-county service area, OFTC provides quality education and training to meet the needs of students and the community.

From credit programs of study to adult education and high school programs, OFTC offers a variety of educational opportunities to help students achieve their goals. With a focus on student success, OFTC provides resources such as advising, career services, and retention services to support students throughout their academic journey.
