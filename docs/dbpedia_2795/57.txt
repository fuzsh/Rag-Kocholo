Oconee Fall Line Technical College is a 2-4 year, public school located in Sandersville, GA. It is classified as special-use Associate's college by Carnegie Classification

The Oconee Fall Line Technical's 2024 tuition & fees is $3,201 for Georgia residents and $5,601 for out-of-state students for the academic year 2023-2024. 100% of the enrolled undergraduates have received grants or scholarships, with an average aid amount of $5,660.

For the academic year 2022-2023, the school has a total enrollment of 1,819 and the students to faculty ratio is 11 to 1 (9.09%).

Oconee Fall Line Technical College is accredited by Council on Occupational Education.
