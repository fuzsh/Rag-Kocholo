Ultra bilyonaryo Manny Villar and wife Senator Cynthia Villar have spent some quality time together abroad.

The power politiko couple found respite from the hustle and bustle of Manila, traveling to the enchanting land of the Rising Sun.

The former senator took to Instagram to share a glimpse of their delightful escape to a traditional Japanese garden nestled in Kagoshima, Japan.

“A beautiful day to explore and enjoy the stunning Japanese-style landscape gardens of Senganen Garden in Kagoshima, Japan,” Villar wrote on Instagram.

Manny and Cynthia have been married since 1975 and have three children — Mark, Paolo and Camille
