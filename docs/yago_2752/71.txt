Cassandra Harris was born on December 15, 1942 in Sydney, New South Wales, Australia. She was an actress, known for In tödlicher Mission (1981), Remington Steele (1982) and Der Löwe zeigt die Krallen (1980). She was married to Pierce Brosnan, Dermot Harris and William Firth. She died on December 28, 1991 in Los Angeles, California, USA.
