John Aurelius Addison is a businessperson who has been the head of 5 different companies. Currently, he holds the position of Chief Executive Officer at Addison Leadership Group LLC. Mr. Addison is also on the board of Pre-Paid Legal Services, Inc., Primerica, Inc. (former Co-Chief Executive Officer & Director) and National Monuments Foundation, Inc. Mr. Addison previously occupied the position of Co-Chief Executive Officer for Citigroup, Inc., Co-Chief Executive Officer of Primerica Financial Services LLC, Non-Executive Chairman of Primerica Life Insurance Co. (Investment Portfolio) and Senior Vice President for Primerica Life Insurance Co. Mr. Addison received an MBA from Georgia State University and an undergraduate degree from the University of Georgia.
