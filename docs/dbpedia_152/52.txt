V-103 International Launches

By

Radio Ink

-

Entercom announced the launch on Friday. The station will be heard on WVEE-HD2 in Atlanta. The station, will feature a wide range of genres including afro beats, dance hall, reggae, soca, hip hop, top 40 and big chune, and will serve as a complement to sister station V-103 (WVEE-FM).
