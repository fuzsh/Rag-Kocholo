WVEE is an FM radio station broadcasting at 103.3 MHz. The station is licensed to Atlanta, GA and is part of that radio market. The station broadcasts Urban programming and goes by the name "V-103" on the air. WVEE is owned by Audacy.

History:

With the merger of CBS Radio and Entercom completed on November 17, 2017, the station became part of the new combined company to go by the name Entercom.
