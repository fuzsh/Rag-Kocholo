People to Places provides a door-to-door transport service for local residents of the Royal Borough of Windsor & Maidenhead with mobility needs.

We provide a Dial-a-Ride minibus service to help individuals get out and about, whether it be a hospital appointment or a trip to the shops. People to Places can help you make that journey.

At Shopmobility we offer a hire service for mobility equipment, for both local residents of the Royal Borough of Windsor & Maidenhead and visitors.

You can hire various types of mobility equipment such as wheelchairs and mobility scooters, with the option of day hire or extended hire.

We have Shopmobility locations in Windsor and Maidenhead.

As a charity we value all the help we can get.

There are many ways that you can get involved and support us, including working for us, giving us time in a volunteering role, or making donations. There is a way for everyone to get involved.
