Michael John Wolff OBITUARY

Michael John Wolff Michael John Wolff, 61, a resident of San Francisco for the past ten years, died June 11, 2005. Born in Eureka July 1, 1944, he was the fifth of ten siblings.

Mike was a roofer by occupation, but after a roofing accident he worked for Jim Pastori, Senior, for several years at the Harris and K market. He loved to play poker and was a very good player. Many will remember Mike for the successful Little League teams he coached in the Eureka area.

His surviving siblings are brothers, Riley Wolff of Yuba City, Gerald Wolff, Stephan Wolff of Eureka, Ross Wolff of Fortuna; sisters, Virginia Breckner of Delaware, Marianne Poisall, Grace Hvratin, Kathleen Emery of Tacoma, WA, and their respective families. Anne Kehl-Wolff and Louis H. Wolff, his parents, and a brother Louis G. Wolff, precede Michael in death.
