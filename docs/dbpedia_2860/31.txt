Dr. Yue Kuo, professor and holder of the Dow Chemical Professorship in the Artie McFerrin Department of Chemical Engineering at Texas A&M University, has been elected a Fellow of the American Vacuum Society (AVS). The Fellow designation is the highest level of membership in AVS, and recognizes members who have made sustained and outstanding scientific and technical contributions in research, engineering, technical advancement, academic education or managerial leadership.

Founded in 1953, AVS is an interdisciplinary professional society that supports networking and collaboration between academic, industrial and governmental sectors. AVS members come from a wide variety of disciplines including chemistry, physics, biology, mathematics, engineering and business.
