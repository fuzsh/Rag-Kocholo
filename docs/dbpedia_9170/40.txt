David Rovics the infamous American Folk Singer/Songwriter/Musician/Storyteller is coming to Ireland & the UK in June, as part of his World Tour.

One of his most famous songs is ‘Saint Patrick’s Battalion’. He has an abundance of original music and songs. The acoustic sets he will be playing in Ireland are a must see.

He is also a YouTube indy music icon, with millions of views and he has Streamed Millions of downloads world wide.

David Rovics is known as a internationally acclaimed artist, whose arrangements are praised for their originality and emotional impact.

This is an opportunity for friends and families to bond over his music and songs of current issues & historic events in our society. Whether you like to sing along or relax and enjoy, this show is for you.
