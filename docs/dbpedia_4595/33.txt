Gabriella Zanna Vanessa Anstruther-Gough-Calthorpe, professionally known as Gabriella Wilde, is an accomplished English actress and model recognized for her notable contributions to film, television, and the fashion industry. She was born in Basingstoke, Hampshire, England, on April 8, 1989.

With an early start in the modeling world at the age of fourteen, Gabriella quickly captivated the attention of industry insiders with her striking looks and innate talent. In 2009, she made her impressive acting debut in the film St Trinian's 2: The Legend of Fritton's Gold, showcasing her versatility and natural on-screen presence. Her undeniable talent led to a recurring role in the widely acclaimed TV series Poldark, where she left a lasting impression on audiences with her captivating portrayal.
