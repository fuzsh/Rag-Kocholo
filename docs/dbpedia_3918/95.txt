'Neethaane En Ponvasantham' is a youthful and emotionally charged drama of the complex and emotionally-charged journey, a pair of childhood sweethearts go through, as they grow up and mature into adults.

Varun and Nithya are childhood friends from the age of eight. They fight and break up when in school and in college too they repeat the rigmarole of falling in love and then breaking up over sensitive and emotionally charged issues.

Finally, they meet once again at 25. And once more history repeats itself till the inevitable happens all over again. Even when they both secretly seem to know that they can never get over each other, reality has a way of creating the most challenging obstacles. Will the lovers ever managed to settle their differences and seal their relationship in the bond of matrimony?
