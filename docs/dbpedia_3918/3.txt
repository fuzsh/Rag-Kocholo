Neethaane en ponvasantham (english: you are my golden springtime) is a 2012 indian tamil romantic drama film co-written and directed by gautham menon, featuring jiiva and samantha in the lead roles. the film is based on a story written by reshma ghatala, who produced the film alongside elred kumar, jayaraman, venkat somasundaram and menon. the film's score and soundtrack were composed by ilaiyaraaja, while cinematography was handled by by m. s.
