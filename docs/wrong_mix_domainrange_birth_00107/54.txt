Mike Bryan (Rev. William Michael Bryan) was born and raised in the San Francisco Bay Area. He is a Christian minister and author with a wide range of ministry involvement. He received Jesus as Lord when he was 24 years old while employed as an Electro-Mechanical Designer for the Lawrence Livermore Laboratory. He has since served the Body of Christ as Youth Pastor, evangelist, teacher, Associate Pastor, Short-term missionary, artist and Art Director. Mike and his wife Sue have been Associate Pastors/Children's Pastors at The Rock Church and World Outreach Center in San Bernardino, California for since 1994. The Rock is a Southern California Mega Church of 22,000 active members. Together, they have 3 adult children and 2 grandchildren.
