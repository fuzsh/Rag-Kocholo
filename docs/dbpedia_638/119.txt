Emma Forbes, born on the 14th of May 1965 in London, England, is a renowned actress, known for her roles in films such as Scrooge: A Christmas Sarah (1990), Going Live! (1987), and International Velvet (1978). After graduating from RADA, she was cast in the 1953 film Wheel of Fate as a brassy blonde, and has since been in high demand for her acting. She has been married to Graham Clempson since 1987, and has two children. Bryan Forbes, born as John Theobald Clarke on the 22nd of July 1926 in Stratford, London, was an acclaimed actor, director, and writer, best known for his work in films such as The Guns of Navarone (1961), The Whisperers (1967), and Seance on a Wet Afternoon (1964). He was married to Nanette Newman and Constance Smith, and passed away on the 8th of May 2013 in Virginia Water, Surrey, England.
