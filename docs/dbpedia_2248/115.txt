Death Angel has been played on NTS shows including A Fist in the Face of God presents: Midnite Madness, with Voracious Souls first played on 25 April 2023.

Death Angel is an American thrash metal band from Daly City, California, initially active from 1982 to 1991 and again since 2001. Death Angel has released nine studio albums, two demo tapes, one box set and three live albums.
