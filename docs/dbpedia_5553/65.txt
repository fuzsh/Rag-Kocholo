Tour Diary: Tegan and Sara’s Backstage Photos from the Road

Indie-rock sweethearts Tegan and Sara Quin have shared a stage with Neil Young, Death Cab for Cutie, and the Black Keys since they began touring, in 1998. The identical twin sisters documented their latest tour with the Killers, answering VanityFair.com’s photo questionnaire about what life is really like on the road, from Bananagrams and practicing songs from their upcoming album, Heartthrob, to the endless search for sushi and making their tour bus smell like home.
