Dan Brown has always been a storyteller. Fascinated by rhythms and riddles since age 3, Brown loves to share stories through books, music and poetry for bookworms big and small. Dan Brown is the author of numerous No. 1 bestselling novels, including “The Da Vinci Code,” which has become one of the best-selling novels of all time. Brown’s novels are published in 56 languages around the world with over 200 million copies in print. In 2005, Brown was named one of the 100 Most Influential People in the World by Time magazine. His debut picture book is “Wild Symphony” (Rodale) with illustrator Susan Batori.
