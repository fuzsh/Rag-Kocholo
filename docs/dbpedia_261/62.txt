Espero is an Italian Belle available at the 1939 start of the game. She is one of four starter Belles for an Italian-aligned player (she is not the one exclusive to game backers).

Character Information[]

Personality and traits[]

Espero is shown as a lady who appreciates the finer things in life. She confesses to Mahan that she has a habit of stealing one thing from every country she visits. [1]

Trivia[]

The Espero was one of three Turbine-class destroyers used to transport Italy's anti-tank unit to South Africa during the Battle of the Espero Convoy in June of 1940. The three ships, including the flagship Espero were chosen for their high speed and loading capacity.
