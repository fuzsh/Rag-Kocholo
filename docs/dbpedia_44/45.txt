Wayland Village Court

Steuben county, NY

Wayland Village Court is located in Steuben county and is one of over 1300 justice courts located in New York State.

Justice courts are vested with the authority to handle misdemeanor criminal cases such as petit larceny, criminal contempt, assault, driving while intoxicated (DWI), traffic violations and misdemeanors, such as speeding, cell phone use and aggravated unlicensed operation.

Justice courts also have jurisdiction in civil cases up to $3,000.
