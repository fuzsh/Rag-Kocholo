Town of Wayland, New York

Wayland is a large and rural town in Steuben County, New York. It is located in the Finger Lakes region, approximately fifty miles directly south of the City of Rochester. Within the town, is a village of Wayland and the hamlets of Patchinsville and Perkinsville. The village is the main population area and the location of most town businesses. , the total population was estimated at 3,600.
