Biography

Elizabeth was born 15 Feb 1684 at Norwich, New London, CT, the daughter of Samuel & Mary (Brewster) Fitch. [1] [2]

Elizabeth Fitch was the first wife of Samuel Mason. They were married on 15 Apr 1712 in Stonington, Connecticut [3]

Their only child, a daughter Mehetable Mason was born on 15 Sep 1713 in Stonington, Connecticut. However, the child died on 6 Oct 1713. [4]

Elizabeth Fitch Mason died on 8 Feb 1715 in Stonington, Connecticut, [5]
