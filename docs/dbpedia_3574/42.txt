They made their home in Norwich, where no children were born to the second wife. Samuel was a farmer and innkeeper at Norwich.89

Samuel (15), b. 11 Sept., 1722, son of Jonathan Rudd and Joanna Gregory; married 25 Dec., 1750, Laura Fitch, b. 2 May, 1732, daughter of Jabez Fitch and Ann Knowlton. He was a farmer and inn-keeper at Norwich. She died 20 Jan., 1781. He then married Ann Bingham. He died 22 Sept., 1795, at Franklin.75, pgs 344-45
