Tamara Riley is the title protagonist turn villainess of the 2005 horror movie Tamara. Tamara was a shy girl form a abusive home who was bullied constantly at school. One unfaithful night she accidentally died form a a prank gone wrong. Unknown to anyone was that she was practicing witchcraft and came back form the dead for revenge.

The Empire of a New Age[]

she was brought back once agin after her second demise by the Knight Reaper to become his second in command and in which her task is to recruit as many villians as possible to his cause.
