Synopsis

Tell Me Lies season 2’s filming may begin in early 2024. The official cast confirmation is still pending.

Hulu’s drama, Tell Me Lies, stirred critics but left viewers split. Adapted from Carola Lovering’s novel, it follows Lucy (Grace Van Patten) navigating college and a tumultuous relationship with Stephen (Jackson White). The narrative unfolds four years post-graduation, then flashes back eight years.

Tell Me Lies Season 2: Release Date, Filming

Despite mixed audience reactions, a second season was confirmed in late November 2022. Unfortunately, the release date for Tell Me Lies season 2 remains uncertain. While 2023 is ruled out, early 2024 is also unlikely. Filming might commence in early 2024, targeting a mid to late-2024 release.

Tell Me Lies Season 2: Cast

While the official cast confirmation is pending, Grace Van Patten and Jackson White are expected to return. Natalie Lines, Catherine Missal, and Branden Cook's comebacks seem probable.

Tell Me Lies Season 2: Storyline, Where to Watch

Tell Me Lies season 2 is anticipated to continue the intricate storyline, addressing lingering questions from the first season. The writer, Meaghan Oppenheimer, hinted at a messier narrative with a shift towards revenge. The first season is currently available for streaming on Hulu.
