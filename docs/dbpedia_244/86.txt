Yorkton

Yorkton is a city located in south-eastern Saskatchewan, Canada, about 600 km north-west of Winnipeg and 300 km south-east of Saskatoon.

CICC-TV

CICC-TV (branded on-air as CTV Yorkton) is the CTV owned-and-operated station in Yorkton, Saskatchewan, Canada.

Yorkton Municipal Airport

Yorkton Municipal Airport, (IATA: YQV, ICAO: CYQV), is located 2.8 nautical miles (5.2 km; 3.2 mi) north of Yorkton, Saskatchewan, Canada.

CKOS-TV

CKOS-TV was a television station in Yorkton, Saskatchewan.

Lady Lake, Saskatchewan

Lady Lake is a hamlet in the Canadian province of Saskatchewan.

Yorkton (provincial electoral district)

Yorkton is a provincial electoral district for the Legislative Assembly of Saskatchewan, Canada.

Willowbrook, Saskatchewan

Willowbrook is a special service area in Orkney Rural Municipality No. 244, Saskatchewan, Canada.
