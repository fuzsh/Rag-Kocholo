The day of the week January 15th, 1815 fell on was a Sunday.

William Bickerton the English-American religious leader, 3rd President of the Church of Jesus Christ (d. 1905) was born on 15 January in 1815.

Emma the Lady Hamilton, English mistress of Horatio Nelson, 1st Viscount Nelson (b. 1761) died on this day in 1815.
