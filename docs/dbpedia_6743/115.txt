At Brooks School, we seek to provide the most meaningful educational experience our students will have in their lives. Established in 1926 in the town of North Andover, Massachusetts, Brooks School provides an all gender, college preparatory program for 350 students on a 270-acre campus overlooking Lake Cochichewick.
