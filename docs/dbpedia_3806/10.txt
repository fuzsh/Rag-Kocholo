Villa Rica, GA, USA

Latitude and longitude coordinates are: 33.732067, -84.919395.

A small town of Villa Rica is western Georgia is situated near US Route 20 and State Highway 78. The town has the population close to 13,000 people, and it is located in a famous resort area, where plenty of villas and hotel resorts can be found. People who live in Atalanta (which is about 50 miles east from the town) enjoy going to the area and spending time on a wonderful local natural setting. It is an absolutely amazing town to spend a day with kids since there are plenty of playgrounds, some picnic areas, etc.
