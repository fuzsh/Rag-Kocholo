Reds is a historical drama film released in 1981, directed by Warren Beatty, who also stars in the lead role. The film depicts the life and career of journalist and writer John Reed, who was a key figure in the American Communist Party and a witness to the Russian Revolution. Reds garnered critical acclaim and received numerous accolades, including three Academy Awards.
