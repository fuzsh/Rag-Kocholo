Jennifer Bassey graduated from the Royal Academy of Dramatic Art in London and then moved back to the United States to begin her professional acting career. She has appeared in several plays and many soap operas including "Love of Life" and "The Edge of Night".

In 1983 she took on the role of bad-girl Marian Colby on All My Children and has remained on the show on a recurring basis ever since.

Jennifer is widowed; she was married to her longtime companion, Luther Davis, from 2004 until his death in 2008.
