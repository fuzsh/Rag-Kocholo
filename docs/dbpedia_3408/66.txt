Tokheim was founded in 1898 in Cedar Rapids, Iowa and now its renowned Quantium dispenser line and technology solutions are sold in over 160 countries worldwide. Over the years, Tokheim has made history by revolutionizing the industry, building a legacy of unrelenting innovation and unparalleled quality. A legacy that now continues to thrive through Dover Fueling Solutions and the Dover Corporation (NYSE: DOV).
