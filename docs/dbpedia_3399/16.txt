Biography

Elisabeth was born on 2 February 1847 in Mecklenburg, Germany. She was the daughter of Johann Georg Wiedeman and Sophia Dorothea Zorn.[1]

Elisabeth arrived in the USA in 1869.[1]

When Elisabeth was 22 she married Gustav August Johann Friederich Evert on 20 December 1869. The couple had 7 children: William Frederick Johan Evert, August John Theodore Evert, Albert Rudolph Evert, Louis Magnus Evert, Rudolph Christ Evert, Edward John Evert and Otto William Carl Evert.[1]

In the 1900 census Elisabeth (age 53) was living in Pewaukee village, Waukesha, Wisconsin.[1]

Elisabeth was buried after 12 August 1932 in Forest Hill Cemetery, Pewaukee, WI.
