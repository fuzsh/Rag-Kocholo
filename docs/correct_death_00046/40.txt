Münster

Münster ranks 577th in number of biographies on Pantheon, behind Regensburg, Alkmaar, and Hamilton, Ontario. Memorable people born in Münster include Heinrich Brüning, Kurt Gerstein, and Carl Schuhmann. Memorable people who died in Münster include Raisa Gorbacheva, Johann Georg Hamann, and Clemens August Graf von Galen. Münster has been the birth place of many athletes, and writers and the death place of many politicians, and mathematicians. Münster is located in Germany.
