Sodagreen are a very popular indie rock band from Taiwan. Sodagreen are a sextet, including Qing Feng (lead vocal and songwriter), Wei (drums and songwriter), A Fu (acoustic guitar), A Gong (keyboards and orchestral arrangements), Claire (bass), and Kay (electric guitar). Formed in 2001, since then Sodagreen have released 9 critically acclaimed albums and received many music awards, which earned them a loyal following in China, Taiwan, Hong Kong and the Chinese young people around the world.
