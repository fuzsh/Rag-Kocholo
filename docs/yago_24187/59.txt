Reshma Aur Shera is a 1971 Indian movie directed by Sunil Dutt starring Sunil Dutt, Waheeda Rehman, Rakhee and Amitabh Bachchan. The feature film is produced by Sunil Dutt and the music composed by JAIDEV.
