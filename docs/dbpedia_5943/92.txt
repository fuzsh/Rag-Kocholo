Oando Plc (Oando), is an integrated energy solution provider. The company explores and produces oil and gas through the acquisition. It carries out the trading of crude oil, liquefied natural gas, refined oil products, and petroleum products ranging from naphtha, gasoline, fuel oil, gas oil and kerosene. Oando provides terminal services, distribution services, oilfield services and exploration services. It serves individual, commercial and industrial customers. The company has its operations in Africa, Europe, and the Middle East. Oando is headquartered in Lagos, Nigeria.
