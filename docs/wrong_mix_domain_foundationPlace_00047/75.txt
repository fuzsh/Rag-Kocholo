Adobe Systems is a software company headquartered in San Jose, California, USA. It is best known for the technology behind PDF, Photoshop, and Flash.

Adobe Inc, formally known as Adobe Systems Incorporated is a software company headquartered in San Jose, California, USA. It is best known for invent pdf (Which is now an International Standard: ISO 32000-1:2008) and its creative products such as photoshop, adobe-illustrator, adobe-indesign, adobe-premiere, adobe-xd, after-effects, dreamweaver and flash.
