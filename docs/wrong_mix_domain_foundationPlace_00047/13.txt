California was the home state of Adobe Inc., which is headquartered in the city of San Jose.

Statistics[]

California is the 3rd largest state by geographical area in the United States and has been the most populous U.S. state since Adobe Systems was founded in 1982.[1]

Macromedia and Adobe in California[]

The state contains several of the most populous U.S. cities, such as San Jose and San Francisco, where Macromedia was based. Adobe acquired Macromedia in December 2005 and now also occupies Macromedia's former offices in San Francisco.[2][3]
