Discovered when asked to sing in a crowd at the bustling Jazz Cafe in London, Charlene has since been on a whirlwind journey, which has seen the release of her debut album "Good Day", international performances and collaborations, and her songs featured in film and on the Netflix show, Seven Seconds. To date her journey has brought her on stage with Omar, The Floacist, Jon B, Saint Motel and Emeli Sande. She has recorded live sessions for BBC Radio London, 1Xtra and London Live, and performed at Vogue and Duvetica events in Italy. She is currently recording new material.
