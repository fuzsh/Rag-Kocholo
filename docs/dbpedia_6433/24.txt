ATLANTA, Ga. (Atlanta News First) - How would you settle a dispute with a friend or family member? Judge Cristyl Kimbrough serves as host and judge on PeachtreeTV’s original show “Bury the Hatchet.”

This courtroom brings two warring parties together to help them settle their beef.

The Atlanta lawyer and influencer stopped by Atlanta News First to talk about her favorite moments from season one and play a little game of “Quit or Bury the Hatchet!”
