Carol Jackson, 68, of Strafford, left this life to meet our Lord, Savior and Creator on April 10, 2013.Carol was born on April 27, 1944 in Kansas City, MO. Carol married John Jackson on June 21, 1963 in Springfield. She is survived by her husband John; two sons, Dwayne and Vance; and four grandchildren. Funeral service will be 1:00PM on April 13, 2013, at Greenlawn Funeral Home North, with burial in Greenlawn Memorial Gardens. Visitation will be 6 – 8 p.m.,Friday, in the funeral home. Memorial contributions may be made to the Charity of the donor’s choice in memory of Carol.
