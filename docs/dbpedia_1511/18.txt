Biography

Rebecca (Call) Gorham was born on 14 May 1744 in Charlestown, Massachusetts. Her parents were Caleb Call (1718 - 1780) and Rebecca (Stimson) Stimpson (1718 - 1765).

Her sister was Elizabeth (Call) Bartlett (1759 - 1833).

Rebecca married Nathaniel Gorham (1738 - 1796) on 6 Sep 1763 in Charleston, Suffolk County.

Their children were...

Mary (Gorham) Bartlett (1767 - 1832)

Ann (Gorham) Brooks (1771 - 1850)

Lydia (Gorham) Phillips (~1778 - 1856).

Rebecca died on 18 Nov 1812 in Charlestown aged 68.
