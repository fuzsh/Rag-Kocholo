Singapore’s first on-screen music festival!

A new screening concept by Sirens Network, Encore is a 3 night musical showcase featuring the cult musical movies Mamma Mia!, ‘A hard days night’ from the Beatles, and the always popular Grease! But much more than a just a movie screening, the night continues after the credits role with a DJ set and the party really starts!

Fort Canning provides the beautiful location for this party spectacular with a full cutting-edge cinema experience created on the lawns, complete with theatre quality giant screen and a full DJ sound system for the after party.

The gates open a 5pm with a sunset music session to get the crowds ready for the features…

No cinema experience is complete without popcorn and at Encore the corn is free-flow all night! There’s also a full bar with soft drinks, wine, beers and more and a variety of great food selections to ensure youre well stocked for the party.

DJ Deepak (Ku De Ta SG) will be spinning the tunes after the movie features, providing a chance to cut loose beneath the stars.

The Films

28th March 2014

Mamma Mia! – The Movie!

The highest grossing musical worldwide to date! Mamma Mia! Is the star-studded musical rom-com film adapted from the 1999 West End/2001 Broadway musical of the same name, based on the songs of successful pop group ABBA. Meryl Streep heads the cast, playing the role of single mother Donna Sheridan. Pierce Brosnan, Colin Firth (Harry Bright), and Stellan Skarsgård (Bill Anderson) play the three possible fathers to Donna’s daughter, Sophie played by Amanda Seyfried. This is one of the few modern musicals to have spawned it’s own cult following.

29th March 2014

“A Hard Days Night” The Beatles’s very first movie!

A Hard Day’s Night is a 1964 British black-and-white comedy film starring The Beatles’s Fab Four  John, Paul, George and Ringo  during the height of Beatlemania.

Considered to be one of the best and most influential musical films of all-time the film portrays the spectacular events of a few days in the lives of the group.

30th March 2014

“Grease” is the word!

Grease is based on the Warren Casey’s and Jim Jacobs’s 1971 musical of the same name about the two lovers in a 1950s high school. The film stars John Travolta, Olivia Newton-John, Stockard Channing, and Jeff Conaway. It was successful both critically and at the box office, its soundtrack album ended 1978 as the second-best selling album of the year in the US, and of course went on to become one of the most popular films ever since and a true cult classic.
