Rebecca Lynn Howard is a two-time Grammy Award winner and a two-time Academy of Country Music Awards (ACM) nominee. Rebecca’s songs have been recorded by Martina McBride, Trisha Yearwood, John Michael Montgomery and many more! Her voice and lyrics won over critics fans alike with her smash hit “Forgive.”

Rebecca plays bass guitar in the Nashville band Loving Mary. Steve Tyler from Aerosmith often performs shows with Loving Mary.

Make sure you go check them out and hear Rebecca Lynn Howard and her Hofner bass!
