Andrew Jackson Montague (3 October 1862-24 January 1937) was the Democratic Governor of Virginia from 1 January 1902 to 1 February 1906 (succeeding James Hoge Tyler and preceding Claude A. Swanson) and a member of the US House of Representatives (D-VA 3) from 4 March 1913 to 24 January 1937 (succeeding John Lamb and preceding Dave E. Satterfield Jr.).
