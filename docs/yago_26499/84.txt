Archduchess Gisela of Austria (12 July 1856 – 27 July 1932) was the second daughter and eldest surviving child of Emperor Franz Joseph I and Empress Elisabeth of Austria. She became a Princess of Bavaria through her marriage to her second cousin, Leopold.

Marriage[edit]

On 20 April 1873, she married Prince Leopold of Bavaria in Vienna,Leopold was a son of Prince Luitpold of Bavaria and Archduchess Auguste Ferdinande of Austria.

Issue[edit]

Princess Elisabeth Marie of Bavaria (8 January 1874 – 4 March 1957) married Count Otto of Seefried and Buttenheim and had issue.

Princess Auguste of Bavaria (28 April 1875 – 25 June 1964) married Archduke Joseph August of Austria and had issue.

Prince Georg of Bavaria (2 April 1880 – 31 May 1943) married Archduchess Isabella of Austria but had no issue.

Prince Konrad of Bavaria 22 November 1883 – 6 September 1969) married Princess Bona Margherita of Savoy and had issue.
