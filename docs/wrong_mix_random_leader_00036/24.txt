Application Lifecycle Management (ALM) software is a set of tools and processes used to manage the entire lifecycle of an application, from its conception to retirement. The purpose of ALM software is to streamline the development process, improve collaboration between teams, and ensure that applications are delivered on time, within budget, and with high quality.
