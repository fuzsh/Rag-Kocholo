Patrick J. Sullivan, 17, of Rockland, passed away suddenly on January 1, 2015. He was born in Weymouth, beloved Son of Kevin T. Sullivan and his wife Suzanne M. (Stec) Sullivan, and cherished Brother of Meghann M. Sullivan of Rockland. Patrick was raised and educated in Rockland, and was a member of the senior class at Rockland High School. Patrick was a well-rounded and hardworking student. He was an active member of Student Government, a devoted athlete, a member of the football, wrestling, and baseball teams. Patrick also was employed at Stop & Shop in Abington. He enjoyed traveling, listening to music, and spending time with his family and friends whom he loved so much. In addition to his parents and sister, Patrick is survived by his loving grandparents John S. Sullivan and his wife, Mary A. (Foley) of Rockland and Francis "Ron" Stec and his wife, Mary A. (Donovan) of Quincy. Also survived by many treasured Aunts, Uncles, Cousins, and many dear Friends. The Sullivan Family welcomes friends and loved ones to celebrate his life at the Magoun-Biggins Funeral Home, 135 Union St. Rockland on Wednesday January 7, 2015, from 5:00-8:00 PM. A Funeral Mass will be held on Thursday January 8, 2015 at 9:00 AM in Holy Family Church in Rockland. Burial will follow at Blue Hill Cemetery in Braintree. Donations in his memory may be made to the Patrick J. Sullivan Memorial Scholarship Fund or the Rockland Athletic Department c/o Rockland High School 52 MacKinley Way Rockland, MA 02370.
