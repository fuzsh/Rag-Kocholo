Joseph Patrick Sullivan II of Walpole and Clearwater Beach, FL, formerly of Brighton, and Dennisport passed away on April 13, 2023 after a short illness surrounded by his loving family. Loving son of the late Joseph Patrick and Margaret (Butler) Sullivan. Devoted father of Carole Sullivan, Joseph Sullivan III and his wife Denise all of Walpole, Joanne Sullivan of Medfield, Scott Sullivan and his wife Jeni of Woburn. Grandfather of Catherine, Patrick, Camden and Ava Sullivan. Brother of Vincent Sullivan and his late wife Marie of Peabody, William Sullivan of Peabody, Paula Frawley and her late husband William of Brighton. Former husband of Joan (Donahue) Sullivan. Also survived by many nieces and nephews.

Funeral from the Lehman Reen & McNamara Funeral Home 63 Chestnut Hill Ave (nr Brighton Courthouse) Brighton on Thursday April 20th at 9:30am. Followed by Funeral Mass in St Columbkille Church 321 Market Street Brighton at 10:30am. Relatives and friends are kindly invited to attend. Interment St Joseph Cemetery West Roxbury. Visiting Hours on Wednesday April 19th from 4-8pm in the funeral home.

He was an active member of the St Columbkille Class of 1957, Army Veteran, member of the K of C Brighton Council #121, past president of Longell Road Beach Association Dennisport, and Clearwater Beach Condo Association, founding member of the Combustion Engineering Association and longtime business owner in Brighton.

In lieu of flowers donations in memory of Mr Sullivan may be made to St Columbkille Church 321 Market Street Brighton MA 02135, or Old Colony Hospice 321 Manley Street West Bridgewater MA 02379.
