Wiki content for Mahesh Bhupathi

Mahesh Bhupathi - Mahesh Shrinivas Bhupathi (born 7 June 1974) is a retired Indian professional tennis player. In 1997, he became the first Indian to win a Grand Slam tournament (with Rika Hiraki).

Mahesh Bhupathi Tennis Academy - Mahesh Bhupathi Tennis Academy is a tennis training facility started by Indian tennis player Mahesh Bhupathi.
