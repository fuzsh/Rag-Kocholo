Born Oct 1, 1950 in Houston, Texas, USA

Randy Randall Rudy Quaid has undoubtedly lived one of the craziest lives of any Hollywood actor. He is a genuinely talented actor, his early roles were in classics like The Last Picture Show, Paper Moon, The Last Detail, and later in Vacation film series, Kingpin and Independence Day. Near the end of his film career he held a supporting role in Brokeback Mountain.
