Martin M. Foster OBITUARY

MARTIN M. FOSTER In Oakland, on April 19, 2006 at the age of 84, and after a long illness with Alzheimer’s disease at his home in Montclair. Born in the Bronx, NY in 1922. Beloved and devoted husband of Helena R. Foster for 41 years. Loving brother of Arnold Foster of San Rafael and Ed Fessler of Phoenix, Arizona. Uncle of Bonnie Radest and Norman, Lindsay and Joanne Fessler. Great-uncle of Mark and Joey Radest, and cousin of Paul Taussig. Martin was the owner of Martin Foster Enterprises theatre chain in the East Bay for 30 years prior to his retirement in 1988. His sense of showmanship and love for foreign films expanded the audiences for specialized cinema in the East Bay. His passion was the movie business, the theatre, symphony and opera.
