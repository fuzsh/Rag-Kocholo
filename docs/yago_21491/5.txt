Warren Beatty is an American actor/director whose five decade career includes leading roles in such films as Bonnie and Clyde, Heaven Can Wait, and Bugsy. He has since directed several films including Dick Tracy and Reds, the 1981 film for which he received the Academy Award for Best Director. He is the brother of fellow Academy Award winner Shirley MacLaine.
