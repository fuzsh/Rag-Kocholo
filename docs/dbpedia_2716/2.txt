E-mail: muzeumkn@nextra.sk Abstract – Thirteen species of Microlepidoptera, Coleophora calycotomella STAINTON, 1869, Coleophora kyffhusana PETRY, 1899, Coleophora ononidella MILLIÈRE, 1879, Coleophora subula (FALKOVITSH, 1993), Coleophora succursella HERRICH-SCHÄFFER, 1854, Dichrorampha plumbagana (TREITSCHKE, 1830), Elachista gormella NIELSEN et TRAUGOTT-OLSEN, 1987, Phyllonorycter eugregori LAŠTÙVKA et LAŠTÙVKA, 2006, Phyllonorycter platanoidella (JOANNIS, 1920), Syncopacma suecicella (WOLFF, 1958), Syncopacma wormiella (WOLFF, 1958), Trifurcula bleonella (CHRÉTIEN, 1904), Trifurcula magna LAŠTÙVKA et LAŠTÙVKA, 1997 are recorded as new to the fauna of Hungary. Five species are deleted from the Hungarian list. Key words – Microlepidoptera, Hungary, new records. NEW AND CONFIRMED RECORDS FOR HUNGARY Coleophora calycotomella STAINTON, 1869 (Coleophoridae) – A new record of the species to the Hungarian fauna, it is distributed in the Netherlands, Germany, South Europe, North Africa (Tunisia, Morocco), Turkey, Lebanon, Syria (BALDIZZONE 1994, 1997, BALDIZZONE et al. 2006). Its host plants are Calicotome spinosa, Sarothamnus and Adenocarpus (Fabaceae). Data of the specimens in coll. Z. TOKÁR &G.PASTORÁLIS: Csákberény, Bucka-hegy, 21. V. 2005, eight females, leg. Z. TOKÁR &G.PASTORÁLIS (gen. prep. Z. TOKÁR, No. 9071). It is very closely related to Coleophora obtectella ZELLER, 1849, which was already recorded from Hungary (e.g. BALDIZZONE 1978). We think all historical records need to be examined again with regard to the external similarity of these two coleophorids. Its proposed Hungarian name: rekettye-zsákosmoly.
