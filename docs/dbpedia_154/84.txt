TERRY BOWDEN

Born: Feb. 24, 1956, in Douglas, Ga.

Hometown: Morgantown, W.Va.

Alma mater: West Virginia.

Assistant coach: Florida State graduate assistant, 1982; Akron assistant, 1986.

Head coach: 19-13 at Salem, 1983-85; 45-23-1 at Samford, 1987-92; 47-17-1 at Auburn, 1993-98; 29-9 at North Alabama, 2009-11; 11-25 at Akron, 2012-present.
