NC State football will host a Mega Football Clinic on Saturday, April 20, along with the coaching staffs from Appalachian State and Coastal Carolina. Wolfpack alum and 17-year NFL veteran Philip Rivers will be the keynote speaker.

The clinic, which will run from 8 am to 6 pm, will be held at the Close-King Indoor Practice Facility adjacent to Carter-Finley Stadium. All high school coaches are welcome to attend.
