Walker Interactive Systems is based out of San Francisco. Walker Interactive Systems Inc. designs, develops, markets and supports high performance financial and control application software products and provides custom software development services. Elevon, Inc. engages in designing, developing, marketing, and supporting a family of enterprise financial, operational, and analytical software products. Elevon Collaborative Synergies solutions are organized into key operational areas: e-finance, e-procurement, e-revenue, shared transformation enterprise processes, and Portals. Elevon 2 e-finance includes the general ledger, asset management, and project cost management modules. Elevon 2 e-procurement includes the accounts payable, inventory management, and purchase order modules. Elevon 2 e-revenue includes the accounts receivable, billing, and collection modules. The company offers analytical applications, which include planning and forecasting, financial consolidation, and active financial planning. The company markets its products to large and medium-sized organizations; higher education institutions; and federal, state, and government agencies. Its competitors include SAP AG; Oracle Corporation; PeopleSoft, Inc; Hyperion Solutions Corporation; Cognos, Inc; and Comshare, Inc. The firm has no submitted 13F filings and does not appear to be an investment advisor.
