Career

He currently plays the point guard position at Tadamon Zouk. Hassan was selected by Lebanese scouts to be in the national team reserve roster after his performance in the 2013-2014 season. Mahmoud was born on May 11, 1991 in Beirut, Lebanon.

Before starting his professional career Hassan had been training in the United States of America at various camps and showed an interest to enter in the 2013 National Basketball Association Draft, but went in undrafted.

Hassan moved back to Lebanon sign for First Rate (at Lloyd's) Mouttahed Tripoli for one season before moving to Tadamon Zouk in the 2013-2014 season. Hassan is best known for his assists topping the league in the highest average assists per game in front of fellow local star Rodrigue Akl.
