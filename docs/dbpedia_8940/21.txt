Career

Mahmoud was born on May 11, 1991 in Beirut, Lebanon. Before starting his professional career Hassan had been training in the United States at various camps and showed an interest to enter in the 2013 NBA Draft, but went in undrafted. Hassan moved back to Lebanon sign for Al Mouttahed Tripoli for one season before moving to Tadamon Zouk in the 2013-14 season. Hassan is best known for his assists topping the league in the highest average assists per game in front of fellow local star Rodrigue Akl.
