Panic & Action Records has announced that is will no longer release records, ceasing as a company. The label was founded in 2006 after the dissolution of Burning Heart Records and the owners have recently reacquired the trademark for their former label and will thus resurrect the company in place of Panic & Action.

Artists who will release on Burning Heart include Adept, Asta Kask, Bombshell Rocks, Her Bright Skies, and Walking With Strangers.
