Minister of Foreign Affairs of Turkey, Mr. Ahmet Davutoğlu has begun his official program on the sidelines of 68th UN General Assembly meetings.

On the first day of his contacts, Foreign Minister Davutoğlu met with Deputy Prime Minister and Foreign Minister of Montenegro Igor Luksic, Foreign Minister of Barbados, Maxine Pamela Ometa McClean, Foreign Minister of Trinidad and Tobago, Winston Dookeran, Foreign Minister of Jordan, Nasser Judeh, Foreign Minister of Malta, George Vella, Foreign Minister of Kosovo, Enver Hoxhaj, U.S. Secretary of State, John Kerry, Foreign Minister of Iran, Javad Zarif, Deputy Prime Minister and Foreign Minister of Greece, Evangelos Venizelos and Secretary-General of CARICOM. Irwin LaRocque.
