ORLANDO, Fla. - Former Arthur Hill star Jason Richardson was held scoreless in the Orlando Magic's 103-93 win Wednesday over the Phoenix Suns.

Richardson was 0-for-7 from the floor, including 0-for-3 from the 3-point line. Richardson did grab six rebounds and led the team with five assists.

Orlando's Ryan Anderson made seven 3-pointers and finished with 29 points, while Dwight Howard added 28 points and 16 rebounds.

The win snapped a two-game losing streak.
