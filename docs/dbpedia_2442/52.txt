Rossford High School, Rossford, OH, USA

Latitude and longitude coordinates are: 41.608952, -83.558723.

Rossford High School is a higher educational establishment situated at 701 Superior St, in the town of Rossford, Ohio. It is an old public high school built at the place of the previous school in 1923. It serves not only to the town but also to a few neighboring villages. Jonathan Bennett, an American actor, is one of the graduates of this high school.
