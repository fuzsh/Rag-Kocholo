Vesta Harris, 84, of Bryan, passed away Wednesday, June 8, 2011 Magnified Health and Rehab in College Station.

Visitation will be at 1 p.m. Saturday, June 11, 2011 at Callaway-Jones Funeral Home Chapel. Funeral services will follow at 2 p.m. in the Callaway-Jones Chapel. Interment will be at Mt. Pleasant Cemetery in Robertson County.

Vesta was born May 11, 1927 in Robertson County to Roy and Pearl McBride. She worked in the food service department for Bryan Independent School District for ten years. Vesta loved to dance and loved her family, especially all of her grandchildren. She was a member of Calvary Baptist Church.

She was preceded in death by her husband; John Harris; son, Arlen Lane Favors; brothers, A.W. McBride, Raymond McBride; sisters, Pebble Lewis and Becky Junek.

Vesta is survived by her daughters and son-in-law, Peggy and Charles Langdon of Bryan; Judy McNatt of Frankston, Texas; sister, Christine Russell and husband, Birl, of Bryan; seven grandchildren, ten great-grandchildren and two great-great-grandchildren.

Memorials maybe made to Texas Home Health Hospice, 7676 Hillmont St., Ste. 300, Houston, Texas 77040
