Who is Danilo Carando?

Danilo Carando is 36-years-old, born on 05 August 1988, and is a right-footed professional football player from Argentina. Born in Córdoba, Córdoba, Danilo Carando has also played in Copa Sudamericana for Cienciano, in Bundesliga for Altach, in Primera División for Cusco, in Playoffs 1/2 for Fujairah, in Copa Pilsener Serie A for Uni Católica, in Liga Profesional de Fútbol for Unión SF, in Qatar Stars League for Qatar SC and in Qatar Stars League for Al Ahli. He currently plays as a Striker (Centre) in for club Garcilaso.
