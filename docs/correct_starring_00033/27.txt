Kevin Jonas is singer-songwriter and actor who began his career as a member of the band Jonas Brothers along with his brothers Joe and Nick. The Jonas Brothers starred in the Disney Channel movies Camp Rock and Camp Rock 2: The Final Jam, as well on the Disney Channel series Jonas L.A. and Jonas Brothers: Living the Dream. The band topped the Billboard 200 albums chart in 2008 and 2009 and landed a Top 5 single on the Hot 100 with "Burnin’ Up" in 2008. The Jonas Brothers split in 2013, and Kevin Jonas went on to star in his own E! reality series, Married to Jonas, and on Season 7 of The Celebrity Apprentice. In 2019, Jonas reunited with his brothers to release the Jonas Brothers’ next album, Happiness Begins, debuting at No. 1 on the Billboard 200 chart. Jonas can next be seen hosting Claim to Fame with brother Frankie Jonas, set to premiere on July 11.
