Kevin Jonas, born on November 5, 1987, is an American musician and television personality. He gained fame as the lead guitarist of the popular pop rock band Jonas Brothers, which also included his younger brothers Joe and Nick. In the late 2000s, Kevin rose to prominence through his appearances on the Disney Channel. He starred in the successful musical television film, Camp Rock (2008), and its sequel, Camp Rock 2: The Final Jam (2010). Kevin also featured in two other series, Jonas Brothers: Living the Dream (2008–2010) and Jonas (2009–2010). Following the band's hiatus in 2012, Kevin starred in his own reality series, Married to Jonas, with his wife Danielle. He later appeared on The Celebrity Apprentice in 2014, founded the construction company Jonas Werner, and became co-CEO of The Blu Market, an influencer marketing company.
