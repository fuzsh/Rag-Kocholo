The Jonas Brothers are a pop-rock band comprised of brothers Nick, Kevin and Joe. The band gained popularity after starring in the Disney Channel movies Camp Rock and Camp Rock 2: The Final Jam. The band also starred on their own TV show, Jonas LA, which lasted two seasons on Disney Channel. The group released four albums, two of which topped the Billboard 200 albums chart, before splitting in 2013. They reunited in 2019 and released the hit single "Sucker," which debuted at No. 1 on the Billboard Hot 100 - a first for the band. Their most recent album, Happiness Begins, debuted at No. 1 on the Billboard 200 chart. The Jonas Brothers’ Olympics special, Olympic Dreams Featuring Jonas Brothers, came out in July 2021. Their most recent single, Wings, was released in February 2023. The Jonas Brothers’ newest album, The Album, is set to release in May 2023.
