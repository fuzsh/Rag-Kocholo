Jun 28, 2005 - The New York Knicks selected David Lee in Round 1 with Pick 30 in the 2005 NBA Draft. Jul 1, 2005 - David Lee signed a multi-year contract with the New York Knicks. Oct 25, 2006 - The New York Knicks exercised their Team Option to extend the contract of David Lee. Oct 27, 2007 - The New York Knicks exercised their Team Option to extend the contract of David Lee. Jun 23, 2009 - The New York Knicks tendered a Qualifying Offer to make David Lee a Restricted FA. Jul 1, 2009 - David Lee, previously with the New York Knicks, became a free agent. Sep 25, 2009 - David Lee signed a contract with the New York Knicks. Jul 1, 2010 - David Lee, previously with the New York Knicks, became a free agent. Jul 9, 2010 - David Lee was acquired by the Golden State Warriors from the New York Knicks in exchange for Kelenna Azubuike, Anthony Randolph, Ronny Turiaf, a 2012 2nd round pick (GOS own) and a 2013 2nd round pick (GOS own). Jul 9, 2010 - David Lee signed a multi-year contract with the New York Knicks as part of an eventual sign and trade to Golden State Warriors. Jul 27, 2015 - David Lee was acquired by the Boston Celtics from the Golden State Warriors in exchange for Chris Babb and Gerald Wallace. Feb 19, 2016 - The Boston Celtics placed the contract of David Lee on waivers. Feb 21, 2016 - David Lee, previously with the Boston Celtics, became a free agent. Feb 22, 2016 - David Lee signed a contract with the Dallas Mavericks. Jul 1, 2016 - David Lee, previously with the Dallas Mavericks, became a free agent. Jul 7, 2016 - The Dallas Mavericks renounced their free-agent exception rights to David Lee. Aug 2, 2016 - David Lee signed a multi-year contract with the San Antonio Spurs. Jul 1, 2017 - David Lee, previously with the San Antonio Spurs, became a free agent.
