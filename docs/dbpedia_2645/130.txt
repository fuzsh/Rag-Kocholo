After impressing one and all with his performance as the shrewd politician Ramadhir Singh in Anurag Kashyap's Gangs of Wasseypur, actor-filmmaker Tigmanshu Dhulia will be seen in Hansal Mehta's Shahid.

The film starring Rajkumar Yadav is based on the true story of slain human rights activist and lawyer Shahid Azmi. Tigmanshu will be playing a lawyer and a mentor of sorts to Rajkumar Yadav in this film. Shahid is set to be screened at the prestigious Toronto International Film Festival.
