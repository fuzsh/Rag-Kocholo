Presley Hart (born December 1, 1988) is the stage name of an American pornographic actress. Hart was born in Anaheim, California and she is of Italian descent. Her mum worked in real estate and her dad was in the Navy. She has one older girl and one younger girl. She moved around often and spent most of her younghood in Coronado, California. Hart attended college and was majoring in architecture and engineering, but she did not graduate.
