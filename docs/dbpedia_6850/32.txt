Star saxophonist Archie Shepp recorded in a duo setting with Danish bassist Niels-Henning Ørsted Pedersen, on 180g audiophile vinyl. The album consists of duets on compositions written by, or associated with, Charlie Parker and was recorded on February 7, 1980 at Sweet Silence Studios, Copenhagen.

Personnel: Archie Shepp (tenor ans soprano saxophone), Niels-Henning Ørsted Pedersen (bass)
