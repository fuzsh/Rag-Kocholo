refnum=86001161cite web|url=http://www.nr.nps.gov/|title=National Register Information System|date=2008-04-15|work=National Register of Historic Places|publisher=National Park Service] Lovin & Withers Investment House is at 722 Beale Street in Kingman, Arizona. The house was built around c. 1914. The house is in the Bungalow/Craftsman style. Lovin & Withers built the house as contractors. They used native stone. The house closely resembles the two houses on Pine Street. The house was a rental property during a growth period. Today it is office for local lawyer. The house is on the National Register of Historic Places and the number is 86001161.
