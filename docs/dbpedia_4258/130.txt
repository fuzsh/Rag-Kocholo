Bob Hoskins was an English actor and comedian, born on October 26, 1942, and died on April 29, 2014. He gained acclaim for his performances in films such as 'The Long Good Friday', 'Mona Lisa', and 'Who Framed Roger Rabbit'. Hoskins received a BAFTA and a Golden Globe Award for his role as George in 'Mona Lisa'. His diverse career in film and television showcased his extensive talent. Known for his charismatic screen presence, Hoskins left a lasting impact on the entertainment industry.
