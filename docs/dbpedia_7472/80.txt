Janet Thompson, age 75 of Knox (Ober), passed away Tuesday, April 14, 2020 at her home. Janet was born November 19, 1944 in New Britain, Connecticut to Stanley Januszewski and Albina Dawidowicz Januszewski, both deceased. She was a veteran having served in the United States Marine Corps, where she met her husband David J. Thompson Sr. They were married on May 1, 1965. She had an avid love for art along with incredible talent for painting and photography. She was a member of the Heartland Artists and won multiple awards for her works. She had served multiple terms as a public official, including Starke County Assessor, Washington Township Trustee and precinct committee person.
