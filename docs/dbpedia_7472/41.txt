New York City, New York Mar 20, 2024 (Issuewire.com) - Janet is an accomplished realtor who works alongside her daughter, Kirsty Daviau. They are partners and the owners of Thompson Daviau Realty, an independently owned-real estate agency in Boulder, Colorado.

The mother-daughter duo have sold millions of dollars worth of real estate, with their professional focus extending across Boulder County and neighboring areas such as Broomfield, Adams, Jefferson, and Weld Counties. They are committed to working together with their clients to give them the best service and support in the business.

Originally from London, England, Janet relocated 5000 miles to Colorado with 3 teenage children in the spring of 1991. This relocation experience provided her with firsthand insight into the complexities of moving, whether locally or across vast distances.

Establishing roots in Boulder County, Janet has been a cherished member of the community for over three decades, while her real estate career commenced in 2002. She boasts an array of impressive credentials, including Certified Residential Specialist (CRS), Accredited Buyer’s Representative (ABR), Certified Negotiation Expert (CNE), and Cendant Mobility Relocation Specialist (CMRS).

Additionally, she actively contributes to professional associations such as the National Association of Realtors, the Colorado Association of Realtors, and the Longmont Association of Realtors.

Renowned for her reliability and credibility, Janet adheres to ethical standards and transparency in her dealings. To remain abreast of advancements in the industry and maintain a competitive advantage, she actively participates in real estate seminars and educational programs.

Attributing her success to her personable demeanor, extensive knowledge, and meticulous organization skills, Janet finds fulfillment in both marketing properties for sale and assisting prospective buyers in finding their dream homes.

Beyond her real estate endeavors, she has a passion for travel. Family and friends play a huge part in her life. Whenever she has any free time she loves to entertain, take Pilate classes, and play Bridge.
