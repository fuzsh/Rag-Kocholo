Population Details ...

Taken from the 2019 Census Estimates, Auburn Hills had an population of 24,755 people. This is an increase of 15.61% since the 2010 Census (or an increase of 24.79% since the 2000 Census).

At the time of the 2010 Census, Auburn Hills had a population of 21,412 people. This makes Auburn Hills the county's 10th most populous community.

The population of Auburn Hills represents 0.22% of the population of Michigan (which has 9,883,640 people), making it the state's 50th most populous community.

With a 2010 count of 21,412 people, the population of Auburn Hills increased 7.94% from the 2000 Census (with a count of 19,837 people).
