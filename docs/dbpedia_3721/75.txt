77, of Davison, passed away Saturday, December 3, 2011, at Genesys Convalescent Center. Jim was born in Flint on September 26, 1934, son of the late Godfrey and Louise (Merkle) Wehrli. He was one of eleven children. Jim married Sandra Ragland on February 15, 1958.

He proudly served his country with the U.S. Army. Jim was a member of Central Church of the Nazarene. He was employed by AC as a skilled tradesman for 30 years. Jim loved to build furniture.

Jim is survived by his wife, Sandra “Sandy”; children, Dawn (Richard) Kotsko and Dru (David) Kostelic; grandchildren, Adam and Hannah Kotsko, Tyler and Tanner Kostelic; brothers, Robert (Betty) and Fred Wehrli; sisters, Elaine (Dave) Ostrander, Margaret (Conrad) Herald, Dorothy (Fred) Buhler and Nancy (Ross) Geiger; sisters-in-law, Michelle (Eckhard) Gollan and Esther Wehrli; brothers-in-law, Mark Ragland and Walt Ratzlaff; many nieces, nephews and close friends.

In lieu of flowers, memorial contributions may be made to Loving Hands Ministries.

He was preceded in death by his brothers, Eric (Idadell) and Richard; sisters, Louise (Don) Owen and Selma Ratzlaff; and sister-in-law, Millie Wehrli.

A Memorial Service will be held 6 p.m. Saturday, December 10, 2011 at Central Church of the Nazarene, 1261 W. Bristol Rd., Flint, MI 48507. Pastor Glen Gardner will officiate. Cremation has taken place.
