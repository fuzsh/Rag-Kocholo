2009/10 was Manchester City's 108th season of competitive football - its 81st in the top division of English football, its 13th (total) and its 8th consecutive season in the English Premier League. The leading goalscorer this season was Carlos Tevez who scored 29 goals from just 42 appearances. The player who made the most appearances for the club this season was Shay Given who played 44 times. The club had 21 different goalscorers and used 33 different players including 16 debutants. Debutants this season were Abdisalam Ibrahim, Adam Johnson, Alex Nimely, Carlos Tevez, Dedryk Boyata, Emmanuel Adebayor, Gareth Barry, Greg Cunningham, Gunnar Nielsen, Joleon Lescott, Kolo Toure, Marton Fulop, Patrick Vieira, Roque Santa Cruz, Stuart Taylor and Sylvinho.
