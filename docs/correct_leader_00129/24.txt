Abstract: Mintoff, Dominic [Dom] (1916–2012), prime minister of Malta, was born in Cospicua, Malta, on 6 August 1916, the son of Lorenzo Mintoff and his wife, Concetta, née Farrugia. His father was from the island of Gozo but his mother was from Cospicua, an old bastion-fortified dockyard town on the Grand Harbour, where the family had a small grocery business.
