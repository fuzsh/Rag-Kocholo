N.p. Mirisch Corporation, 1961. Two vintage studio still photographs from the 1961 film. Nominated for an Academy Award.

Released in conjunction with the execution of John A. Bennett, a soldier convicted for molesting an eleven year old girl. The film follows the trial of four American soldiers in Germany accused of raping a young woman. Dimitri Tiomkin composed the film score, including the Academy Award nominated song "Town Without Pity."

Shot on location in Vienna, Austria and Bavaria, Germany.

8 x 10 inches. Near Fine, with pin holes to the corners and light wrinkling overall.
