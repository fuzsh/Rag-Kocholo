Original British 11 inch x 8 1/2 inch Trade Advertisement for the 1961 Gottfried Reinhardt Drama TOWN WITHOUT PITY, based on the novel ‘Das Urteil’ by Manfred Gregor and starring Kirk Douglas, E.G. Marshall, Robert Blake, Richard Jaeckel, Frank Sutton, Mal Sondock, Alan Gifford, Barbara Rutting and Christine Kaufmann.

Story of a trial of four American soldiers by an Army court martial for the rape of a German girl.

The Advertisement is in very good condition.
