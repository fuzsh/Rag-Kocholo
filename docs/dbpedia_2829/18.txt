About the project

In Gulskogen in Drammen municipality, right next to Gulskogen station, SPG is developing a modern housing destination with a focus on active seniors, first-time buyers and families with children. Gulskogen will be a neighborhood that is adapted and accessible to everyone, regardless of life situation, finances and purchasing power.

The property is 16,000 square meters and has been owned by the Halmrast family for over 70 years. At present, the property is leased for warehousing and logistics. Expected sales start is 2025.
