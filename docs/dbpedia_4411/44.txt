Percy is a small, green tank engine, and the deuteragonist of Thomas the Tank Engine Tales. He is engine number 6. Percy is best friends with Thomas, and his favorite job is pulling the Sodor mail. He is the junior member of the principal team of engines. Percy is voiced by Linda Ballantyne.
