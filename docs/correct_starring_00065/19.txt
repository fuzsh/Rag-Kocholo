Austin Powers: The Spy Who Shagged Me is an action game based on the movie of the same name. The game was developed by Eurocom and published by Electronic Arts. It was released for the PlayStation, Nintendo 64, and Game Boy Color two months after the film's release. Versions for the SEGA Dreamcast and PC were released the following year in 2000.
