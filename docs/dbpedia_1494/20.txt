Photo Upload Standards

Pictures that contain material of pornographic, racist, or extremely violent nature will not be allowed.

Pictures that are taken from book covers, magazines, or newspapers will not be allowed.

Pictures that contain watermarks will not be allowed.

Low-quality pictures or digitally-modified pictures will not be allowed.

Pictures of people must not have any obstruction that covers their faces, such as masks or glasses.

Pictures must have an accurate photo contrast.

Pictures must be no less than 1000 pixels in length or width. Pictures over 1000 pixels are preferred.

Public property pictures are preferred over copyrighted pictures or private property pictures.

elcinema.com maintains the right to reject or remove any picture that violates its terms and conditions.
