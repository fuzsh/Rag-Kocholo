Gauri Pradhan Tejwani is a model turned noted television actress best known for playing the role of Gauri Mittal in Kutumb and Nandini Virani in Kyunki Saas Bhi Kabhi Bahu Thi. She is married to actor Hiten Tejwani whome she fell in love with during the shooting of Kutumb. They have twins, son Nevaan and daughter Katya. Post motherhood Gauri took a break from acting. She is currently playing the character of Falguni Harshad Parekh (mother of the female lead Ishaani) in the tele-series Meri Aashiqui Tumse Hi which is aired on Colors.
