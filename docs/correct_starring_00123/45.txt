William Henry "Bill" Duke, Jr. (born February 26, 1943) is an American actor, writer, director, producer, cinematographer and editor who played Mac in the 1987 film Predator. Other notable films Duke has appeared in include Commando (1985), Menace II Society (1993), The Limey (1999) and Red Dragon (2002). As a director he has helmed Deep Cover (1992). Aside from his work in film, Duke has had an extensive career in television.
