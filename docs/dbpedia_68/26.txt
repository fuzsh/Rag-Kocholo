Housing Department

Darlington Borough Council - Housing Department

Services offered

Assessment of entitlement to housing for people who are homeless, threatened with homelessness. Provision of housing for people considered in priority need of housing. Temporary accommodation may be available while eligibility enquiries made.

Area served

Darlington Borough Council area.

Who is the project for?

Who do we help?

General public, including people who are or may be priority homeless.
