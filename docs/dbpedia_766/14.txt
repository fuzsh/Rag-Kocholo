Universidad Técnica Estatal de Quevedo (UTEQ): A Leading Ecuadorian Higher Education Institution

About Universidad Técnica Estatal de Quevedo

Founded in 1984, Universidad Técnica Estatal de Quevedo (Technical State University of Quevedo), commonly known as UTEQ, is a distinguished non-profit public higher-education institution located in the suburbs of Quevedo, within the population range of 50,000-249,999 residents in Los Rios, Ecuador. UTEQ extends its educational presence to various areas, including Mocache, La Represa, and Murocumba, through branch campuses.

Recognitions and Rankings

The Universidad Técnica Estatal de Quevedo (UTEQ) is steadily climbing the ranks in the world of higher education. With a commitment to academic excellence and innovation, UTEQ is making its mark on the global stage. Through continuous dedication to research, teaching, and community engagement, this institution is on a trajectory towards higher rankings and international recognition. It is officially recognized by the Consejo de Aseguramiento de la Calidad de la Educación Superior del Ecuador (Council for Quality Assurance of Higher Education of Ecuador). This recognition underscores the institution’s commitment to maintaining high educational standards and academic quality.

Admission Process and Acceptance Rate

With a rich history spanning 36 years, UTEQ maintains a rigorous admission process. Prospective students are evaluated based on entrance exams and their academic records and ratings. This selective approach results in an acceptance rate ranging from 50% to 60%. While the admission standards are high, students from diverse backgrounds and locations worldwide are encouraged to apply for admission to experience the educational excellence UTEQ offers.
