Patricia Arquette is an Academy, Emmy and Golden Globe award-winning American actress most known for her role in True Romance. Some of her other film credits include Ed Wood, Bringing Out the Dead, Holes and Boyhood. In addition to her roles on the big screen, she has also starred on TV shows such as Medium, The Act and CSI: Cyber. Most recently, Arquette stars on the new Apple TV+ series Severance, which is out now.
