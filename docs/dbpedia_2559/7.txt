Get down to business. Get a taste of the business world with Virginia Peninsula’s Business Administration program.

The Associate of Science curriculum is designed to prepare you for business programs at a four-year institution. Enjoy core classes in English, history and math as well as specialized classes in accounting and economics. Choose electives that will give you an understanding of business needs and practices and allow for a seamless transfer to the four-year institution of your choice.

Virginia Peninsula’s Business Administration program is the first step to pursuing a baccalaureate degree in Business. If you complete Virginia Peninsula’s program with a qualifying grade point average, you’ll benefit from special admissions agreements that Virginia Peninsula has with more than 20 Virginia colleges and universities. Pursue careers in accounting, economics, business administration, international business, health services management, management information and more.
