From 1960 to 2023 the population of South Sudan increased from 2.84 million to 11.09 million people. This is a growth of 290.1 percent in 63 years. The highest increase in South Sudan was recorded in 2015 with 6.06 percent. The biggest decrease in 2017 with -3.69 percent.

In the same period, the total population of all countries worldwide increased by 164.7 percent.

The average age in South Sudan rose by 0.18 years from 2012 to 2024 from 18.52 to 18.70 years (median value).

Around 21 percent of the inhabitants live in the country's larger cities. This growing trend of urbanization is increasing by 3.3 percent annually.
