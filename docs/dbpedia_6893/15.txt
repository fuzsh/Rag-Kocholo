Paulo Moura has been played on NTS in shows including Donna Leake, featured first on 1 June 2015. Songs played include El Negro Del Blanco, Fibra and Bicho Papão.

Brazil's best keeper of the gafieira tradition (popular ballrooms historically linked to the Carioca folklore tradition of highly artistic and swinging dancing and playing) and one of the best choro players ever, Paulo Moura is an internationally awarded musician whose high standards make it easy for him to cross boundaries between classical and popular music, both performing and arranging in small ensembles or large symphonic orchestras.
