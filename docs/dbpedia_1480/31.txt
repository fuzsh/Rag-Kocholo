Mulligan Stew Plays the Fifth Province Pub

Beloved since 1978, Mulligan Stew is a fixture in Chicago’s Irish/Celtic Music Scene. They have played many times at the Fifth Province Pub as well as at the Chicago Irish Fest, Gaelic Park Irish Festival, Iowa Irish Fest, and Milwaukee Irish Fest. They play traditional Irish music, Irish drinking songs, folk, rock, bluegrass, and country music. Come see why they are one of the area’s favorite bands!

Doors/Pub – 6PM, Music – 8PM

This is a free show
