Farewell…

Roberta Taylor 1948-2024

Best known for… Roberta starred in EastEnders from 1997 to 2000, playing Irene Raymond (above), who married Tiffany’s dad Terry. Irene famously had an affair with young lodger Troy. Other achievements… From 2002 to 2008, Roberta played Inspector Gina Gold in ITV police drama The Bill. She also appeared in Bleak House, Inspector Morse, Silent Witness and Shakespeare & Hathaway. Family life… Roberta died on 6 July, aged 76, from an infection resulting from a fall. She leaves behind her actor husband Peter Guinness, son Elliott and a granddaughter. Where to watch Roberta… Some EastEnders episodes featuring Roberta, such as the 1999 Christmas special, are on Prime Video for a fee.
