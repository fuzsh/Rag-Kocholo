EyeMart Express is driven by a singular focus: to make quality eyewear that is affordable, quick, and stylish. As a doctor-founded company, they understand that their success and innovation stems from maintaining great relationships with optometrists and optical experts across the country. They also believe in building a strong relationship with customers and use their feedback to develop top-selling private label brands from lab to shelf. Through state-of-the-art lab technology, merchandising and online marketing, EyeMart brings excitement back to the eye care industry.
