Ratatat is an American electronic rock band based in Brooklyn, New York. Formed in 2001, the band consists of Evan Mast and Mike Stroud.

Known for their experimental and psychedelically-tinged rocktronica, Ratatat have continued to be one of the US's most unique bands past the break of the millennium.

Having released their latest and fifth album, 'Magnifique', in July 2015, Ratatat are now set to play a one-off headline show at Electric Brixton in October - get your tickets below!
