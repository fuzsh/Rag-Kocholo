Nineveh (or Ninevah) is an Assyrian city in Civilization V: Brave New World and the Assyrian capital and a Babylonian and Persian city in other Civilization and Call to Power games and C-evo. In real life, it is a ruined city on the outskirts of Mosul in present-day Iraq that served as the capital of the Neo-Assyrian Empire.
