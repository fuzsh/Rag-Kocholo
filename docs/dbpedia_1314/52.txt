In this fondly remembered animated series produced by Hanna-Barbera Studios, Bo, Luke and Daisy Duke take their high-wheelin country cruisin around the world in a race against Boss Hogg for the family farm. No joshin  the Dukes speed through France, Italy, Austria, Greece, England  you name it, the General Lee made tracks there! En route, Boss Hogg pulls the nastiest tricks ever, like blinding the boys with a fog machine, having them kidnapped and tricking them into a jewel heist. Why, that Hoggs as crooked as a dogs hind leg! But that wont stop the Dukes from fighting a fair fight and teaching that cheat a few tricks along the way. (less)
