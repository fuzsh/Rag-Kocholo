Joan M. Bennett, 86, of Jordan died January 7, 2020, peacefully at home. She was born July 20, 1933 in Port Chester, NY to George and Carolyn Ficken. Joan is survived by her former husband Donald Bennett, and their children Colleen Bennett, Eireann (Patrick) Govern, Michael (Allison) Bennett and grandchildren Samantha, Paige, and Lindsay.

A funeral Mass will be held 10 am Tuesday, January 14th in St. Patrick’s Church, 28 N. Main St. Jordan. Burial will be private. In lieu of flowers, contributions in memory of Joan may be made to St. Patrick’s Church cemetery fund, P.O. Box 567, Jordan, NY 13080
