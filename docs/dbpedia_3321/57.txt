Alma Macrorie was born on 7 December 1904 in Davenport, Iowa, USA. She was an editor and actress, known for Immer Ärger mit Harry (1955), Die Brücken von Toko-Ri (1954) and Mutterherz (1946). She died on 28 June 1970 in Woodland Hills, Los Angeles, California, USA.

American editor, under contract at Paramount from 1937 to 1962. Worked on several films by directors Mitchell Leisen and George Stevens. Edited her last few films for Universal (1963-66).
