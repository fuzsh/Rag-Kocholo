Nepali actress Manisha Koirala is now married to businessman Samrat Dahal. After a traditional ceremony on Friday, Ms. Koirala completed her grand marital rituals here on Saturday.

The marriage ceremony, conducted in a traditional Brahmin style, was held at the Gokarna Forest Resort.

Bollywood actors Jackie Shroff, Gulshan Grover, Vivek Mushran (her co-star in her debut movie Saudaagar ), Suman Ranganathan and Dipti Nawal attended Saturday's ceremony.

The actress's wedding reception will be hosted on Sunday at the Soaltee Hotel in Kathmandu. It is expected to be attended by Prime Minister Madhav Kumar Nepal, President Ram Baran Yadav and former king Gyanendra Shah.
