INRI studio is the creative company founded by filmmaker Tzang Merwyn Tong in 1999. The studio has produced 4 award-winning films, including FAERYVILLE - the US-Singapore Co-Production with Film Mall and Eleven Arts that made its world premiere in Los Angeles, CA in 2015, and e’Tzaintes which won the Bronze Remi Award at the Houston WorldFest in 2003 and was the Opening Night Film of the Berlin-Asia-Pacific Film Festival. The films of INRI has enjoyed success in the underground film scene and among subculture groups due to its ‘radical and distinctive’ style. Bootleg copies of films like A Wicked Tale (2005) and e’Tzaintes (2003) are still in circulation and frequently screened at grind house cinemas, clubs and late night theatres in Montreal and Berlin.
