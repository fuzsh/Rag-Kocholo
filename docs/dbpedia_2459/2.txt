KENNETH GILLESPIE, SR., 51, of Imlay City, passed away Tuesday, October 28, 2014 at home.

Kenneth Wade Gillespie was born on November 13, 1962, in Highland Park, the son of Robert and Jean Gillespie. He moved with his family to Imlay City when he was 9 years old. Kenneth graduated from Imlay City High School with the Class of 1981.

He was a CNC operator and ran the surface grinder for C.L.A.S. Carbide in Oxford. Kenneth was involved with the chain gang for the Imlay City High School football team. He enjoyed hunting and fishing.

Kenneth is survived by his longtime companion, Teri Younge of Imlay City; sons, Kenneth (Jana) Gillespie of Flushing, Michael Gillespie of Lenox, IA, and Robert Gillespie of Lenox, IA; grandchildren, Owen Gillespie, Isabella Gillespie, Elden Gillespie, Ulston Gillespie, Ava Gillespie, Alba Rose Gillespie, Tysen Gillespie and Maggie Gillespie; Teri’s children, Amy (Adam) Dhooghe, Ashley Younge, Joe Younge, and Amber Younge; Teri’s grandchildren, Alivia Dhooghe, Alayna Dhooghe, Lucas Younge, Gavin Younge, Brooke Younge, Mallory Stout and Leah Stout; his parents, Robert and Jean Gillespie of Imlay City; brother, Mark Gillespie of Marlette; niece, Markie Gillespie.

He was preceded in death by a grandchild, Daniel Guy.

A Memorial Service was held Saturday, November 1, 2014 at the Imlay City Eagles Hall. Jerry Schriber officiated.

Funeral arrangements were made by Muir Brothers Funeral Home of Imlay City.
