Q. In which year was the Polish poet Wisława Szymborska awarded the Nobel Prize in Literature?

Answer: 1996

Notes: Wisława Szymborska, a renowned Polish poet known for her thought-provoking and accessible poetry, was awarded the Nobel Prize in Literature in 1996. The Nobel Committee recognized her for poetry that with ironic precision allows the historical and biological context to come to light in fragments of human reality.
