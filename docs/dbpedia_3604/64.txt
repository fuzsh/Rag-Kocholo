Rural District in Fars, Iran

Sofla Rural District (Persian: دهستان سفلي)[3] is in Korbal District of Kharameh County, Fars province, Iran.[4] Its capital is the village of Sofla.[5]

Demographics

[edit]

Population

[edit]

At the time of the 2006 National Census, the rural district's population (as a part of Shiraz County) was 6,618 in 1,611 households.[6] There were 8,317 inhabitants in 2,495 households at the following census of 2011,[7] by which time the district had been separated from the county in the establishment of Kharameh County.[4] The 2016 census measured the population of the rural district as 7,023 in 2,287 households. The most populous of its 17 villages was Sofla, with 888 people.[2]
