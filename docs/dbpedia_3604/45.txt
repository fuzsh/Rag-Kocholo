Ab Barik Industrial Estate (Persian: شهرك صنعتي آبباريك‎ – Shahrak-e Şanʿatī-ye Āb Bārīk) is a village and company town in Zarqan Rural District, Zarqan District, Shiraz County, Fars Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 47 in 16 families.[1]
