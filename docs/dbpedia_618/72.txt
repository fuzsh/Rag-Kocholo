Nick Greiner was Premier and Treasurer of New South Wales from 1988-1992. Since his retirement from politics he has been heavily involved in the corporate world.

He is currently Chairman of Bradken, Bilfinger Berger Australia, Citigroup Australia, The Nuance Group, United Utilities, PMI Mortgage Insurance, Healthcare Australia and Blue Star Print Group; Deputy Chairman of Stockland Trust and CHAMP Private Equity; and a Director of Australian Vintage Ltd and Blue Freeway.

Nick is Chairman of The Australian Subscription Television and Radio Association (ASTRA); a Trustee, Sydney Theatre Company Foundation; and a Member of the Board of Governors, Committee for Economic Development of Australia (CEDA).

In the Queen's Birthday Honours List of 1994 he was awarded a Companion of the Order of Australia for public sector reform and management and services to the community.

Nick holds an Honours Degree in Economics from Sydney University and a Master of Business Administration with High Distinction from Harvard Business School.
