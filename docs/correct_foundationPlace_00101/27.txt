GRAND RAPIDS ? Microsoft System Integrator NuSoft Solutions has created a new line of business, called Kinetic IG, which uses multi-service technology to create web and desktop applications.

Positioning itself as a separate division of NuSoft Solutions, Kinetic IG?s goal is to ?create a moving experience? for its clients. The experienced creative team and technology group does this by offering to their customers, websites and broad-reach applications that are designed with web standards, follow good usability practices, possess engaging visual design and are successfully built with a solid technology foundation.

?Kinetic IG was born from many years of experience building broad-reach applications where the user experience is absolutely critical.? says Brian Anderson, Director of Kinetic IG. ?We recognize that the right blend of great creative design, software development and an understanding of marketing fundamentals is the best way to build world class solutions for our clients. Kinetic IG makes it possible for innovators to turn their vision into reality.?

Kinetic IG clients include National City Bank, Houghton Mifflin Harcourt Learning Technology, Spectrum Health, Indistr.com and Spout.com. The company employs 20 in NuSoft?s Grand Rapids office.
