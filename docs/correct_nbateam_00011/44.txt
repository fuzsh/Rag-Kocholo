Rasheed Wallace officially signed with the New York Knicks. The last time the 15-year veteran played was in 2010 with the Celtics. (Ronald Martinez/Getty Images)

Rasheed Wallace officially signed with the Knicks, the team announced on Wednesday afternoon. Terms of the deal were not confirmed.

Wallace, who last played for the Celtics in 2009-'10, announced he was coming out of retirement and practiced with the Knicks last week. The 15-year veteran joins a New York team that also has 39-year-old Jason Kidd, 38-year-old Marcus Camby and 39-year-old Kurt Thomas.
