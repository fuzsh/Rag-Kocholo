Frank Miller, Ph.D., age 85 years, of Lebanon Township, NJ, died on Tuesday, March 13, 2018 (26 Adar 5778) at Hunterdon Hills Assisted Living, Lebanon Township, NJ, under the caring watch of Ascend Hospice.

Born in Brooklyn, NY, March 28, 1932, son of the late Abraham and Anita Bellin Miller, he was raised in Brooklyn, NY and had lived in Lawrence, KS, Wilmington and Newark, DE, Medford, WI, Bridgewater, NJ, Cherry Hill, NJ and Hillsborough, NJ prior to moving to Lebanon Township.

A humble, hard-working man, Frank will be remembered for his dry humor, intelligence and love of family and friends.

He earned a Ph.D. in Organic Chemistry from the University of Delaware and was the Chief Environmental Health Official for many years at Columbia University, New York City, NY.

In addition to his parents, he was preceded in death by his wife, Shelia Sasson Miller.

Surviving are his daughters and sons-in-law, Fern and Sam Dille and Stefanie and James Allen; three granddaughters, Samantha Dille, Kaya Allen and Avra Anagnostis; his sister, Beatrice Smith; a nephew, Jaques Smith; his wonderful caregivers at Hunterdon Hills; and many friends.

Graveside services officiated by Rabbi Mendy Lubin will take place on Wednesday, March 14, 2018 at 12:30 p.m. in Wellwood Cemetery, 1400 Wellwood Avenue, Wyandanch, NY 11798 under the care and direction of Wright & Ford Family Funeral Home and Cremation Services, 38 State Highway 31, Flemington, NJ.

Shiva will be held at the Dille residence. Please contact the funeral home for more information.

Please visit Frank’s permanent memorial site at www.wrightfamily.com where one can light a memorial candle and leave a message of condolence and words of comfort, as well as share stories and photographs of his life.

As is custom in the Jewish faith, no flowers please.

In lieu of flowers and in appreciation of the exceptional care they took of Frank and his family, memorial contribution’s to Ascend Hospice, 1600 St. George Avenue, Rahway, NJ 07065, would be greatly appreciated.
