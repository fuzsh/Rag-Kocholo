Biography

Frank Tyler Miller was born on November 27, 1904 in Red House, Cattaraugus, New York to Myrtle Brown and Martin Miller. In 1905, Frank and his sister, Mary, and brother, Julius, lived with their parents in Salamanca, Cattaraugus, New York. [1]

Frank married Rose Ahles, daughter of Lucy Cullen and John Ahles, on September 15, 1923 in Salamanca, Cattaraugus, New York. [2]

Frank died on November 15, 1962 in Portville, Cattaraugus, New York and was buried in Evergreen Cemetery, McKean, Pennsylvania. [3][4]
