Your Niantic ID avatar photo is the profile image that represents you and your Niantic ID. It will be visible through Niantic Chat, the Niantic Profile web portal, and Wayfarer. Please use good judgment when uploading an avatar photo. We want our games and experiences to be fun and safe for all players. Visit our web page to see our Niantic Player Guidelines for more information on what might be considered as inappropriate.

Changing your avatar photo

Click 'Edit Profile' from the main menu page, then click the x button to add a new avatar photo from the Settings page.
