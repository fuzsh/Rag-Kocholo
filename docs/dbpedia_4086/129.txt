Welcome to the Manchester Museum Meetup Group! This group is perfect for museum enthusiasts who want to explore everything the Manchester Museum has to offer. From exhibitions on natural history to cultural artifacts, we'll delve into a wide range of topics to satisfy all our interests. Whether you're a history buff, art lover, or science geek, join us for fun and educational meetups where we can share our knowledge and passion for learning. Let's come together to appreciate, discuss, and enjoy the wonderful world of museums in Manchester!
