Sebastian Arcelus played the role of Fiyero in the first national tour of Wicked: The Untold Story of the Witches of Oz from January 3 until October 3, 2006. He then went to the Broadway production from January 9th and left on December 16, 2007. He is also the husband of Stephanie J. Block, and starred alongside her during the national tour, and Broadway.
