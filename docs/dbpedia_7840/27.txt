Located in Buffalo's Elmwood Village, Nardin Academy is the oldest independent, Catholic school in Western New York. Founded in 1857 by the Daughters of the Heart of Mary, Nardin consists of four schools: a coed Montessori school educating toddlers through Kindergarten, coed Lower School serving Kindergarten through 4th grade, coed Middle School educating students grades 5-8 and an all-girls college preparatory high school serving grades 9-12. Named Niche's Number One private K-12 school in the region, its graduates are creative, curious learners who lead with confidence, strength and integrity, grow through faith and spirituality, champion equity and justice and serve others with humility.
