Biography

Albert Michelson is Notable.

Albert Abraham Michelson was born at Strelno, Prussia on December 19, 1852, son of Samuel Michelson and Rozalia Przylubska.[1][2]

Field: electromagnetism, optical physics.

He received the Nobel Prize for Physics for his work on finding the speed of light,[3] becoming the first American to win the Nobel Prize in a science.

His first wife was Margaret Hemingway (m. 1877-1897).

They had:

Albert Heminway

Truman

Elsa Elizabeth

His second spouse was Edna Stanton (m. 1899–1931).

They had:

Beatrice Michelson.

Dorothy Michelson.

Madeleine Michelson.

He passed away on May 9, 1931 at Pasadena, California.[4]
