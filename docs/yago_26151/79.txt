Archduchess Maria Clementina of Austria, Princess of Tuscany (Marie Klementine Josepha Johanna Fidelis; 24 April 1777 – 15 November 1801) was the tenth child and third daughter of Leopold II, Holy Roman Emperor and Maria Luisa of Spain. On 26 June 1797, she married her double first-cousin Prince Francis, Duke of Calabria, heir to the kingdoms of Naples and Sicily.

Issue[edit]

Princess Maria Carolina of Naples and Sicily (5 November 1798 – 17 April 1870) married Charles Ferdinand, Duke of Berry and had issue.

Prince Ferdinando Ferdinando Francesco d'Assisi of Naples and Sicily, Duke of Noto (27 August 1800 – 1 July 1801) died in infancy.
