Thom Mathews (born 1958) is an American actor best known as a star of low budget Horror, Action and Sci-Fi films. He began his career in the early 1980s, appearing in small parts in movies and TV.

His first starring role was in the 1985 zombie horror film The Return of the Living Dead. The following year, he portrayed the male lead in Friday the 13th Part VI: Jason Lives. He then began working with notorious director Albert Pyun, appearing in many of his films. Throughout the 1990s, Thom continued to work as an actor. He left show business in the 2000s to start a family and pursue other personal interests.
