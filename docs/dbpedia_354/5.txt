Novar - About the company

What does Novar do?

Novar is one of the oldest companies working in energy management space. They started the technology to control and manage HVAC and lighting systems for businesses with multiple locations. Their Opus Building Automation System offers a suite of cloud-based software and hardware products designed to centrally manage and reduce the energy consumption of HVAC, lighting and refrigeration systems for multi-site facilities. Their solutions are deployed in retail, theatres and grocery stores.

Which sectors and market segments does Novar operate in?
