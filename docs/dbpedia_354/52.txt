IQ3xcite: Setting You Free New publication demonstrates benefits of IT based technology

Copley, Ohio - 2003 - Read about IQ3xcite, the latest product addition at Novar® Controls. This controller is designed to facilitate the integration of Building Management Systems (BMS) with IT and other services.

Using Ethernet® connectivity, Novar Controls has incorporated a Web server within the controller. This connection enables the IQ3xcite to operate at very high data transmission speeds (10 million bits per second).

The publication outlines the performance capabilities of IQ3xcite, including daily adjustments and monitoring without a proprietary supervisor package, and its potential for system expansion as the controller can be extended from 16 to 96 points.

To receive this brochure or for more information on the IQ3xcite, contact Novar Controls Corporation email info@novarcontrols.com.
