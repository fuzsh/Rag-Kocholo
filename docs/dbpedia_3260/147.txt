David A. Fisher, 54, of Palmyra, passed away on Wednesday, January 12, 2022 at Hershey Medical Center. Born Friday, December 22, 1967 in Hershey, he was the son of Linda L. (Wagner) Fisher of Elizabethtown and the late Daniel E. Fisher Sr. He was married almost 29 years to Stacy L. (Shertzer) Fisher.

Always quick witted and fun, David loved his family and made friends wherever he went. He was an avid hunter. He bowled and played softball in multiple leagues over the years. He was extremely proud of the Middletown Marksmen BB Gun team he started 5 years ago that was sponsored by the Middletown Anglers and Hunters. David loved going to the cabin. He enjoyed baseball, football and NASCAR, as well as going to the Little League World Series, where he made many friends while standing in line for pins. David was employed for the past ten years with Paramount Contracting, Inc., working as a construction foreman.

Surviving in addition to his wife Stacy and mother Linda, are two children: Daniel E. Fisher of Palmyra and Rachel L. Fisher of Hershey. He was predeceased by a sister Diane M. Fisher and a brother Daniel E. Fisher, Jr.
