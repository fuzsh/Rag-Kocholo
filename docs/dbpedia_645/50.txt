GENEVA, July 7—Investors Overseas Services announced today another Internal shakeup with the ousting of Erich Mende, former West German Vice Chancellor, from the Mu tual‐fund empire.

An announcement by I.O.S. said the 53‐year‐old Dr. Mende had been “relieved of his re sponsibilities within the I.O.S. organization.”

The function of “official rep resentative” of I.O.S. funds in West Germany that the Ger man political figure held is being transferred to the Orbis Bank in Munich, an I.O.S. sub sidiary, the announcement said.

The former leader of West Germany's Free Democratic party was sharply critical of the I.O.S. management during West German television inter view recently when the mutual fund complex was in the throes of a crisis.
