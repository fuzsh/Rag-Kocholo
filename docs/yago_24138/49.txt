Amalie Auguste of Bavaria was the daughter of King Maximilian I Joseph of Bavaria and Queen Therese of Saxony. She was a member of the House of Wittelsbach. Amalie married King Ludwig I of Bavaria and became the Queen consort of Bavaria. She was most famous for being the mother of King Otto I of Bavaria.
