Dorothy Appleby (born January 6, 1906 - August 9, 1990) (age 84) was an actress, known for 7 Three Stooges episodes from 1940 to 1942. She died of natural causes.

Appearances with the Three Stooges:[]

Rockin' Thru the Rockies (1940), From Nurse to Worse (1940), Cookoo Cavaliers (1940), So Long Mr. Chumps (1941), In the Sweet Pie and Pie (1941), Loco Boy Makes Good (1942), and What's the Matador? (1942).
