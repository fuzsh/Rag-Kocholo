Biography

Dorothy Mitchum was the wife of the Hollywood film actor Robert Mitchum. She was one of the founding members of Share Happily and Reap Endlessly (SHARE), a nonprofit organization to benefit people with severe developmental disabilities.

Dorothy was born 2 May 1919 in Camden, Delaware, the daughter of William Spence and Ina Clements[1][2]

She married the Hollywood actor Robert Mitchum 16 Mar 1940 in Dover, Delaware.[3][4] They were the parents of three children: James, an actor-writer, Chris, and actor-politician, and Petrine, a writer. Dorothy and Robert were married until Robert's death in 1997.[5]

Dorothy died 12 April 2014 in Montecito, Santa Barbara, California at the age of 94. Her ashes were scattered at sea.[6]
