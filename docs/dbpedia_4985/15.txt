Nathan Douglas Thomas, 42, of Randleman, passed away Saturday, August 13, 2022, at Randolph Hospice House.

Born January 27, 1980, in Davidson County, he was the son of Teresa Hooker Smith of Thomasville and the late Terry Lee Thomas. Nathan was an automotive technician with Crown Dodge.

In addition to his mother, he is survived by his wife, Caitlin Post Thomas of the home; sons, Ayden Thomas of Browns Summit, Sawyer Thomas of East Bend, and Carter Fargason of Asheboro; daughters, Jada Thomas of Browns Summit, and Nataleigh Thomas of Browns Summit; brother, Jordan Thomas and wife Cherish of Tennessee, sister, Terri Brown and husband Jeremy of Thomasville; and many nieces and nephews.

Services will be at a later date.

Wright Funerals-Cremations, Archdale is in charge of arrangements.
