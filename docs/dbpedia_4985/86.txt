Nathan Douglas Miller, 58, of Bainbridge, passed away Monday, Oct. 9, 2023 at home.

He was born March 4, 1965 in Greenfield, the son of Wendell E. Miller, Sr. and Frances N. (Tolle) Miller.

He worked at AMVC Broadview Barn and was a former employee of C-Mold of Greenfield and was a member of the F.O.E. #1161 of Hillsboro.

He is survived by his wife, Danette Miller; two daughters, Heather (Craig) Bond of Greenfield and Emilee (Jennifer) Vail of Hillsboro; three sons, Heath Stratton of Greenfield, Kyler (Gentry) Willis of Greenfield and Landon Willis of Mandeville, La.; seven grandchildren, Taylor, Brooklyn and Leiland Bond, Hayden Pitzer and Cassidy, Catlin and Remy Willis; two sisters, Dixie L. Rogers of Greenfield and Gena (Lafe) Arthur of Jackson; one brother, Joe (Kaye) Miller of Greenfield; several nieces and nephews; a special granddaughter, Lyndi Lovett; mother-in-law, Shirley Lovett of Greenfield; two sisters-in-law, Dar (Mike) Brown of South Salem and Michelle Miller of Greenfield; brother-in-law, David (Nicole) Lovett of Bainbridge; and special friends, Bobby Hampton of Leesburg and Rodney Johnson of Greenfield.

He was preceded in death by two brothers, Fred A. Miller and Wendell E. Miller Jr.; one brother-in-law, Bruce T. Rogers; one sister-in-law, Barbara A. Miller; stepmother, Carolyn R. Miller; and both parents.

Funeral service will be Thursday, Oct. 12 at 1 p.m. at the Murray-Fettro Funeral Home in Greenfield with Pastor Greg Trent officiating. Burial will follow at the Greenfield Cemetery.

Visitation will be Thursday, Oct. 12 from 11 a.m.-1 p.m. at the funeral home.

In lieu of flowers, memorial contributions may be made to assist the family with expenses at the Murray-Fettro Funeral Home in Greenfield.

Condolences may be sent to www.murrayfettro.com.
