Where is Heber, Utah ?

Heber is a municipality located in Wasatch County, in the north-central region of the United States. It is situated in Utah, approximately 43 miles southeast of Salt Lake City and near the eastern end of the Wasatch Range. Heber is also close to the popular recreational area of Park City.
