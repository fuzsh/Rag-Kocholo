Morihiro Hosokawa (born 14 January 1938) was Prime Minister of Japan from 9 August 1993 to 28 April 1994, succeeding Kiichi Miyazawa and preceding Tsutomu Hata. He was a member of the Japan New Party during his premiership, having changed party affiliation several times; he was the first non-Jiminto prime minister since 1955.
