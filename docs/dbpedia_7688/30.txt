Meaning of Zygmunt

Zygmunt is a strong and distinctive name for a baby boy. It has its origins in Poland and carries with it a rich history and cultural significance. Pronounced as ZIG-muhnt in Polish (/ˈzɪɡmʊnt/), this name exudes strength and character. The variation Zigmund can also be used, providing an alternative spelling for this unique name. In terms of popularity, Zygmunt was moderately common in the early 20th century in Pennsylvania, with occurrences ranging from 6 to 11 per year during that time. Despite not being as widely used today, Zygmunt remains a standout choice for parents looking to bestow upon their child a name with historical roots and a touch of international flair.
