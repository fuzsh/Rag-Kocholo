Voice of Asia Network (Pvt) Ltd is the Largest Media Network in Sri Lanka, currently operating over 2 Television Stations, 4 Radio Stations, 3 Visual Radio Stations & the most prominent News Property in the country. These include the 1st & the only 4K ULTRA HD Television Station in Sri Lanka named ``Siyatha TV`` alongside the tamil language entertainment television station named ``Star Tamil Television``, The most popular Sinhalese radio station ``Siyatha FM``, Tamil lanuage music station ``Star Tamil Radio``, English retro music station ``Real Radio`` & the only english Dance music station ``Kiss FM Sri Lanka``. These also include 3 visual radio stations as well as the most reliable News property ``Siyatha News``.

Voice of Asia Network (Pvt) Ltd was started in 2005 with a vision to revolutionize the Sri Lankan Media Industry. In 2006 with the launch of the Radio station Siyatha FM Voice of Asia Network kept the first steps into the Media industry capturing audiences all across Sri Lanka being the first Radio station to launch with Island wide coverage from the inception.

his was soon Followed by 2 more Radio stations in English and Tamil languages namely “Real Radio” & “Vettri FM`` (Now rebranded as ``Star Tamil Radio”) captivating different target audience segments in the market in a short span of time. Further to the existing stations Voice of Asia Network launched “Kiss FM” the first English dance music station to enter the Sri Lankan Radio industry giving the listeners a chance to experience the latest music with international standards.

Moving on from the Radio supremacy in the industry Voice of Asia Network soon moved onto Television industry by Launching 2 terrestrial Television channels in Sinhala & Tamil languages simultaneously for the first time in Sri Lanka with terrestrial island wide coverage as well as Satellite DTH platform namely “Siyatha TV”, “Vettri TV” (Now rebranded as ``Star Tamil Television``), making a revolutionizing impact in the Sri Lankan mass market television viewing audience becoming highest rated televisions in the country in a span of few months.

Being the innovators in the Television & Radio industry in Sri Lanka, Voice of Asia Network moved onto evolve the radio industry for the first time in Asia by launching 3 Visual Radio stations named “Kiss TV”, “Real TV”, “Vettri TV”, making their audience experience the future of Radio & Television convergence.

On 15 February 2017, Voice of Asia Network (Pvt) Ltd had a network relaunch to showcase all of the media brands revamped to suit the new day and age with cutting edge technology, Siyatha TV, revamped their entire line-up of programs and the look and feel of the TV station along with novel concepts and styles becoming the First and the only Ultra HD 4K Television Channel in Sri Lanka.
