Percy is Thomas' best friend and a small, yet hard-working, light-green tank engine. He will soon be voiced by Keith Wickham in the UK and Christopher Ragland in the US in the upcoming live action/CGI movie: Thomas and Friends the Magical Railroad Adventures.

Best friend for life: Thomas

In Thomas The Slender Engine[]

Percy is an Antagonist in the Game. He was the Last Engine to be Infected by Thomas.
