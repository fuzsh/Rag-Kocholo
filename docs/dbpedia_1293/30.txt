NRK Sport is a radio channel based in Oslo, Norway, catering to sports enthusiasts across the region. The channel broadcasts a variety of sports programming, including live coverage, highlights, analysis, and expert commentary. From football and ice hockey to skiing and athletics, NRK Sport covers a wide range of sports and events, both local and international. The schedule includes regular live broadcasts of major sports events and tournaments, along with daily news updates and analysis. Sports fans can listen to NRK Sport on the radio or online at https://www.nrk.no/sport/. The channel is a great source of sports news and entertainment, keeping listeners informed and engaged with the latest developments in the world of sports.
