About us

London based interior design studio, Emily Penrose Design, are specialists in transforming offices across the UK into exciting and thoughtfully designed workplaces.

Our mission is to create your perfect space, whether you are a landlord looking to attract tenants, a start-up enterprise of 10 or an established organisation of 200, we are here to help realise your workplace vision.

We can find the right solution for all budgets, delivering unique and bespoke schemes relevant to your specific brief, needs and brand identity.

More emphasis than ever is placed on the office environment in attracting and retaining the best talent and setting your business apart from the competition. You know your staff and we know how to get the best out of them by enhancing their working world through ergonomic, functional and creative design with productivity, safety and wellbeing at their heart.

Utilising long standing relationships with leading consultants, suppliers, architects and contractors, we deliver a range of commercial projects across Greater London and major UK business centres including Birmingham, Manchester, Bristol and Reading.

Our core services include:

– Cat A +/Plug & Play

– Cat B

– Common Parts Refurbishments

– Styling spaces for launch events/ tenant viewings
