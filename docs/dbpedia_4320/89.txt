Dame Emily Penrose is a work by British artist Philip Alexius de László (1869 - 1937). It's one portrait oil on canvas painting created in 1907. The painting is owned by the museum of University of London - Royal Holloway, and is accessible to the general public. Philip Alexius de László made most paintings about portrait and figure and tableau.
