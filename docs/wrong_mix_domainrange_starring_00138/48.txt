Charles Dance is an actor who portrays Dr. Ben Wilson on Paramount+'s Rabbit Hole.

Profile[]

Charles Dance is an actor of stage, television and film. He is perhaps most known for his role as Tywin Lannister in the hit series Game of Thrones. His other roles have included series such as Bleak House and the Crown, as well as films including For Your Eyes Only, Dracula Untold and Godzilla: King of the Monsters.
