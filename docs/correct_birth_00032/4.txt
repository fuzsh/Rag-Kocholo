Kylie Minogue was born on May 28, 1968.

Kylie Minogue, the Australian singer, songwriter, and actress, was born on May 28, 1968, in Melbourne, Australia. She started her career as a child actress in Australian television shows before venturing into the music industry.

She rose to fame with her hit song “The Loco-Motion”.

In 1987, Kylie Minogue released a cover of “The Loco-Motion,” originally sung by Little Eva, which became a massive hit worldwide. The song topped the charts in Australia, the UK, and several other countries, launching her into international stardom.
