Name – Kylie Ann Minogue

Stage Name – Kylie Minogue

Date Of Birth – May 28, 1968

Birthplace – Melbourne, Australia

Net Worth – $120 Million

Source Of Wealth: Singer, Songwriter, Actress

Kylie Minogue, born on May 28, 1968, is an Australian singer, songwriter, and actress. She gained fame in the late 1980s with her hit singles like “The Loco-Motion” and “I Should Be So Lucky.” Minogue’s music career has spanned several decades, and she has released numerous successful albums, earning her the title of “Princess of Pop.”

Aside from her music, Kylie Minogue has also ventured into acting, starring in various films and television shows. One of her notable acting roles was in the Australian soap opera “Neighbours.”

Over the years, Minogue has received numerous awards and accolades for her contributions to the music industry, including Grammy and Brit Awards. Her iconic fashion sense and stage presence have made her a style icon, and she continues to be a prominent figure in the global entertainment scene.
