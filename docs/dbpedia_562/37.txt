Biography

JUNIOR (2009-10): Played in 28 games, starting 15 ... Averaged 7.6 points and 3.9 rebounds ... Shot 57.2 percent from the field ... Scored in double-figures eight times, including a season-high 22 points in 77-68 win at Florida Memorial on Dec. 9 ... Had one-double-double with 11 points, 11 rebounds on Dec. 5 against Florida Southern ... Also had a season-high four blocks against the Mocs ... Had five games in which he shot perfect from the field, including a 5-of-5 performance against Rollins in the NCAA Regional.

COLLEGE (SANTA FE CC): Played two seasons under Chris Mowry ... Earned Mid-Florida Conference First Team honors ... Grabbed 134 boards as a sophomore while avergaing 8.3 points per game ... As a freshman, was named to the Mid-Florida Conference First Team … Averaged 9.1 points per game in 27 games … Scored in double figures in 10 games, including a career high 22 vs. Cape Fear CC on 12/20/07 …Averaged 6.0 rebounds per game … Had a career high 15 rebounds vs. Gulf Coast CC on 12/16/07 ... Had six double-doubles on the year … Shot 56.8 percent (104-for-183) from the field and 53.3 percent (40-for-75) from the free throw line … Had three blocked shots vs. Palm Beach CC on 12/15/07 ... Averaged 8.3 points and 4.9 rebounds as a sophomore ... Shot 57 percent (94-for-165) from the field ... added 1.1 steals per game.

HIGH SCHOOL & CLUB: Played under coach James Hawley at Verchlilo High ... Averaged 25 points per game and 10 rebounds per game for the Hitters ... Added six assists and two steals ... Earned Leeward Island Tournament MVP award in 2003 ...Played for the St. Kitts junior national team from 2000-2004.

PERSONAL: Son of Averton and Wilma Davis ... Majoring in criminology.
