Robert Lowry was born on March 28, 1919 in Cincinnati, Ohio, USA. He was a writer, known for So etwas von Frau (1959), Starlight Theatre (1950) and Actor's Studio (1948). He was married to Mary Louise O'Neill, Antoinette LoBianco, Frankie Abbe and Bella Alice Cohen. He died on December 5, 1994 in Cincinnati, Ohio, USA.
