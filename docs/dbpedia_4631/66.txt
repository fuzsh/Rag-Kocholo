About Sanaya Irani

Sanaya Irani is an Indian actress and model.

Her Highlights/Legacy

TV: "Iss Pyaar Ko Kya Naam Doon", "Rangrasiya", "Chhanchhan".

Her Awards

She has been nominated for 5 Star Parivaar Awards (2 wins) and 3 Indian Television Academy Awards (1 win).

Her Personal Life

Though she grew up in India, she wasn't fluent in Hindi and that became an obstacle in her early career.

Fun Facts

She has won 3 "Face of The Year" awards, while she has been titled the 3rd Sexiest Asian Women Alive. She is regarded as a sex symbol in Asia.
