Grant Show will soon follow in his Melrose Place character's footsteps and become a divorcee.

The actor's wife, actress Pollyanna McIntosh, filed for divorce citing irreconcilable differences, People reports.

Grant Show Goes from Big Love to Burn Notice

In the Los Angeles Superior Court documents, McIntosh lists her assets as a diamond wedding ring, her diamond and sapphire engagement ring, a 1968 Mustang convertible, a 1978 Corvette Stingray, furniture and appliances.

Show, 49, and McIntosh, 32, met in 2003 at a Lane Bryant photo shoot in which they posed together for an ad. They tied the knot in 2004 and have no children.
