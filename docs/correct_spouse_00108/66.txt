Grant Show is getting divorced.

Pollyanna McIntosh, wife of the original “Melrose Place” star, filed for divorce from the actor last week, TMZ was first to report.

McIntosh, an actress and model, filed papers in Los Angeles Superior Court and cited irreconcilable differences, People reported.

The pair married in 2004. They met on a Lane Bryant ad shoot in 2003.

Show most recently appeared in “Burn Notice” on USA as Max.
