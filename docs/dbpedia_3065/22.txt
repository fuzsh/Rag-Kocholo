Harold Peary, Jane Darwell, Nancy Gates. Peary brings his pompous radio show character, Throckmorton P. Gildersleeve, to the screen in this 5-film collection. Includes "The Great Gildersleeve" (1942/62 min.), "Gildersleeve’s Bad Day" (1943/62 min.), "Gildersleeve on Broadway" (1943/65 min.), "Gildersleeve’s Ghost" (1944/63 min.) and the Lucille Ball and Victor Mature comedy, "Seven Days’ Leave" (1942/87 min.). 2 DVDs. B&w/NR/fullscreen.
