Arthur Rankin Jr. was born on 19 July 1924 in Baltimore, Maryland, USA. He was a producer and director, known for Das letzte Einhorn (1982), Willy McBean and His Magic Machine (1965) and Der Hobbit (1977). He was married to Olga Karlatos and Elizabeth Deland. He died on 30 January 2014 in Harrington Sound, Bermuda.
