Benjamin Moody is a Youth Ocean Advocate and marine life lover.

Ben graduated from University of Birmingham after studying Biological Sciences and conducting his research project on the effect of plastic on marine ecosystems. He has a background in multiple startups and always had a focus on sustainability throughout his time at university.

After joining SCS in July 2019, Ben has helped Tom drive SCS to be leaders in the market of plastic offsetting and key players within the market of sustainability.

Ben has represented Seven Clean Seas in a number of panel discussions and educational talks focusing on inspiring the more Ocean stewards and driving others to help their cause.
