Brussels, 4 November - Prime Minister Anton Rop welcomed the revamped European Commission presented by its incoming President Jose Manuel Barroso in Brussels on Thursday. The outgoing PM is confident that the reshuffled team will get the green light of the European Parliament as early as this month.
