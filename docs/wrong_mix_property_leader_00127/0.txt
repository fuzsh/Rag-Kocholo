Brussels, 15 October - Three topics will be in the focus of the 16 and 17 October Brussels summit of heads of EU member states and acceding countries - Slovenia will be represented by PM Anton Rop. The event will be centred on inter-governmental negotiations on the EU institutional reform, new economic initiatives, and EU external borders and immigration policy. Before the summit, PM Rop is to attend a meeting of European liberal party leaders.
