Columbia Pictures Original U.S. Half Sheet Poster (22x28) Folded Fine Plus Condition

Columbia PIctures Original U.S. Half Sheet Poster for the Jerry Lewis comedy DON'T RAISE THE BRIDGE, LOWER THE RIVER (1968). The film sets Jerry down in London, where, as the tagline suggests "the fun never sets on the British Empire". The film also stars Bernard Cribbins, Michael Bates, and British comic Terry-Thomas. The film says it was directed by Jerry Paris. Paris worked extensively in television. MovieArt Austin will sell no reproductions. We guarantee that this poster is authentic.
