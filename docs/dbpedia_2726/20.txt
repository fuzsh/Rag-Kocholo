Amy J. Berg (born 1970 in Los Angeles, California, USA) is an American film maker, known best for her Academy Award-nominated document film Deliver Us from Evil (2006) about the sex abuse cases in Roman Catholic Church and an abusive native Irish priest Oliver O'Grady. She has directed also a 10-minute-long document film Polarized (2007).

Born in Jewish family, Berg was grown up in San Fernando Valley and lives currently in Santa Monica, California.
