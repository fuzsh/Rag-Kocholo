bio notes:

born: 7/6/1859

died: 5/20/1940

born as: Carl Gustaf Verner von Heidenstamm

nationality: Sweden

Poet and prose writer who led the literary reaction to the naturalistic movement in Sweden, calling for a renaissance of the literature of fantasy, beauty, and nationalism. He won the Nobel Prize for Literature in 1916. - Merriam-Webster's Encyclopedia of Literature
