News Michael C. Hall Marries at City Hall This is the third marriage for the stage and screen star.

Michael C. Hall, who was most recently on Broadway in the title role of Hedwig and the Angry Inch, has remarried, according to the Associated Press.

The stage and screen star wed writer Morgan Macgregor February 29 at New York City Hall.

Hall, who was previously married to Amy Spanger and Jennifer Carpenter, is best known for his TV roles on Dexter and Six Feet Under. His other Broadway credits include Skylight, Chicago, Cabaret and The Realistic Joneses.
