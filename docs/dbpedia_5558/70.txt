By the Order of President Ilham Aliyev, 68-year-old Hidayat Orujov was appointed as Ambassador of Azerbaijan to Kyrgyzstan.

By another decree of the head of state, Orujov was given the title of Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary.

Orujov in 1992-2006 served as state adviser on national policy.

From 2006 to June 2012 Orujov supervised the State Committee for Work with Religious Organizations. -06D-
