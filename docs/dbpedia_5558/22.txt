Azerbaijan, Baku, March 14 /Trend, K.Zarbaliyeva/

Chairman of the State Committee for Work with Religious Organizations of Azerbaijan Hidayat Orujov received the representatives of the U.S. Department of State, who are on visit to Azerbaijan.

During the meeting with he U.S. Department of State's special representative to Muslim communities Farah Pandit, special envoy to monitor and combat anti-Semitism Hannah Rosenthal and other representatives accompanying them, Orujov touched upon the question of religion in contemporary world and questions related to youth.

Discussions were held on various national, religious differences, to prevent religious extremism.
