Biography

Arthur was born in 1919. He was the son of Abraham Wagner and Bessie Addis.[1] He married Judith Schutzman before 1943.[2]

Arthur died in 2007.[1][3]

Obituary

ARTHUR P. WAGNER (Age 87)[4]

On Sunday, April 22, 2007, ARTHUR P. WAGNER of Leisure World Silver Spring, MD. Beloved husband of 65 years to Judith S. Wagner; devoted and loving father of Paula (Art) Nerret and Roberta (Joe) Warren; loving grandfather of Amanda Michelle Nerret. Graveside services will be held on Wednesday, May 2, 2 p.m. at Arlington National Cemetery, Arlington, VA. In lieu of flowers, expressions of sympathy in his memory may be made to the American Heart Association, www.americanheart.org. Arrangements entrusted to DANZANSKY-GOLDBERG MEMORIAL CHAPELS, INC., 301-340-1400.
