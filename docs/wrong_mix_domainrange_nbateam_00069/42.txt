ORLANDO — Chris Kaman, a former Central Michigan standout, started for the Dallas Mavericks on Sunday, Jan. 20 against the Orlando Magic and scored 13 points while grabbing eight rebounds in a 111-105 win.

Kaman, who is averaging 12.6 points and 6.3 rebounds, shot 5 of 11 from the field and 3 of 4 from the free throw line.

The Mavericks led 57-48 at half and by 10 points heading to the fourth where the Magic drew within six before falling short.

Seven players for the Mavericks scored in double figures with Shawn Marion’s 20 points leading the way.

Orlando’s Glenn Davis led all scorers with 24 points.

Dallas improved to 18-24, while Orlando fell to 14-26.
