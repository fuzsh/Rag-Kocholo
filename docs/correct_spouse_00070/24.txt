Who is Kris Humphries?

Kristopher Nathan Humphries was born in Minnesota on February 6, 1985, to William and Debra Humphries. His father, William, played football at the University of Minnesota. Kris has two siblings, Kaela and Krystal.

He attended Hopkins High School and then received a scholarship to Duke University but chose to attend the University of Minnesota.

Kim Humphries career

On September 25, 2017, he signed with the NBA's Philadelphia 76ers but was dropped on October 14, 2017, as one of the final roster cuts. He officially announced his retirement from professional basketball on March 26, 2019.

What is Kris Humphries doing today?

In 2023, Kris Humphries and his family opened a restaurant in Minnesota under the franchise Dave's Hot Chicken. The Dave’s Hot Chicken was founded in 2017 by Gary Rubenyan, Tommy, Chef Dave Kopushyan and Arman Oganesyan.

He posted some of the restaurant's menu choices on his Instagram, including fries, chicken, milkshakes, and mac and cheese. Kris and his family also own several Five Guys restaurants in Minnesota.

How rich is Kris Humphries?

According to Celebrity Net Worth and Clutch Points, Kris Humphries's net worth is between $35 million and $40 million. He has earned this from his basketball career, lucrative endorsements, and investments.

Kris Humphries and Kim Kardashian relationship

Humphries and reality TV personality Kim Kardashian began dating in October 2010 and got engaged in May 2011. The pair had a lavish wedding on August 20, 2011, and it was aired on E! in early October 2011.

On October 31, 2011, after 72 days of marriage, Kim Kardashian surprisingly filed for divorce from Humphries. This was so unexpected that several media houses reported that Humphries and Kardashian's marriage was a plot by E! Entertainment and Kim to promote the Kardashian family brand and TV ventures. However, they didn't support these claims.

After a lengthy legal battle, Humphries and Kim reached a divorce settlement in April 2023, and it was finalised on June 3, 2013.
