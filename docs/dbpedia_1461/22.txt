Olympia Jean Snowe is currently the Chairman & Chief Executive Officer of Olympia Snowe LLC, an Independent Director at The Price Group LLC, a Director at Bipartisan Policy Center, a Director at Synchrony Bank, and a Member-Bipartisan Congressional Fiscal Leadership at Fix The Debt.

She previously worked as a Director at Aetna, Inc., an Independent Director at T.

Rowe Price Group, Inc., an Independent Director at Synchrony Financial, and a Member of the U.S.

House of Representatives from 1979 to 1995 and the United States Senate from 1995 to 2013.

She received an undergraduate degree from the University of Maine.
