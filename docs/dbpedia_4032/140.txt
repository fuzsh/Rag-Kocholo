How common is the last name Younis in the United States?

Based on the Decennial U.S. Census, the surname Younis has seen a notable increase in popularity between 2000 and 2010. In 2000, the surname ranked 31,126 but improved to 23,899 in 2010, marking a positive change of around 23.22%. The count of people with this surname also increased from 705 in 2000 to 1,056 in 2010, indicating a substantial growth of approximately 49.79%. The amount of individuals carrying the Younis surname per 100k people likewise rose by 38.46%, from 0.26 in 2000 to 0.36 in 2010.

Race and Ethnicity of people with the last name Younis
