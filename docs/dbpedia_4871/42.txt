Wyckoff â€” Joseph Patrick Sullivan, 80, died June 5.

He was born in New York City in 1935 and attended Our Lady of Lourdes grammar school and Cardinal Hayes High School, Class of 1953.

After earning a business degree from Manhattan College, he joined the U.S. military as a Marine Corps infantry officer and completed Naval Justice School. After discharge he dedicated 25 years to the FBI as an agent specializing in Cuban affairs. Upon his government retirement, he went to work for Nabisco as senior director of corporate security.

He had lived in Wyckoff since 1972. He enjoyed camping trips, world travel, playing tennis and golfing.

Funeral arrangements were by Vander Plaat Funeral Home, Wyckoff.
