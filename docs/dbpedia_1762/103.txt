Ralph Hanan was a New Zealand politician and lawyer, born on June 13, 1909, in Invercargill, New Zealand. He was active in the National Party in New Zealand. His political career and legal background contributed to his prominence in New Zealand's political landscape during his time. Hanan passed away on July 24, 1969, in Cairns, Australia.
