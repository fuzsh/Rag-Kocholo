Important ZIP Code 48326 Information

ZIP Code 48326 is located in the city of Auburn Hills, Michigan and covers 16.95 square miles of land area. It is also located within Oakland County.

According to the 2020 U.S. Census, there are 22,228 people in 10,361 households. ZIP-Codes.com estimates that the current population is 23,351. It has a population density of 1,311.4 people per square mile.

48326 is classified as a "non-unique", also known as a Standard ZIP Code. Its primary purpose is for normal street, apartment, and business mailbox delivery. Carriers will deliver mail to this region through vehicles, walking routes, and other means. It may also have PO Boxes located at the USPS facility.

48326 is in the Eastern (GMT -05:00) timezone and does observe daylight saving time. It has 2 Area Codes providing telephone service, including 248 and 947.

A Core Based Statistical Area is the official term for a functional region based around an urban center of at least 10,000 people, based on standards published by the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) in 2000. These standards are used to replace the definitions of metropolitan areas that were defined in 1990.

A CBSA is broken into two sub categories, Metro and Micro, and are detailed in the CBSA Type Field. These are defined by their population densities.
