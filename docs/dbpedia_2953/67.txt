About Her Recent Hit Zara Hatke Zara Bachke

ZHZB had garnered attention for its unique storyline and engaging performances. The movie's box office performance has been notable, with a strong opening weekend earning it a spot among the top earners. Its blend of comedy, romance, and suspense has resonated with audiences, leading to positive word-of-mouth and steady footfalls in theaters.

According to the reports, the film went on to earn Rs 115.89 Cr globally and was success, considering its smaller budget.
