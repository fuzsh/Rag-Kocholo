Gwyneth Strong (born 2 December 1959) is an English actress. She is best known for her role as Cassandra, the love interest and, later, wife of Rodney (Nicholas Lyndhurst) in the BBC Comedy, Only Fools and Horses.

Personal Life[]

Strong was born in Greenwich, London on 2nd December 1959. When she got older, she married former Footballers' Wives and Eldorado star Jesse Birdsall on 15th July 2000. Now they live in Bexhill, East Sussex and have two children, a son called Oscar (born 1988) and a daughter called Lottie (born 1991).
