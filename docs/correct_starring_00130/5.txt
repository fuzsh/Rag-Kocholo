Superbad is a 2007 comedy film. The film stars Jonah Hill and Michael Cera as Seth and Evan (named after the film's lead writers, Seth Rogen and Evan Goldberg), two best friends about to graduate from their senior year who aim to attend a house party with their friend Fogell (Christopher Mintz-Plasse). While trying to purchase liquor with Fogell's fake ID, the three friends navigate between girls, the police, and another party as they attempt to have fun one last time before they leave each other for college.
