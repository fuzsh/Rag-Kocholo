This print takes us back to the bustling streets of San Francisco in 1852, capturing a significant landmark of that time - the Wells Fargo Bank. The image is an oil painting layered over a daguerreotype, creating a unique blend of artistic techniques from that era. The scene showcases the grandeur and architectural beauty of this historic bank building. Standing proudly amidst the city's evolving landscape, it exudes an air of confidence and prosperity. The brick exterior stands tall with its intricate detailing, while a charming balcony overlooks the vibrant street below. Their hats and attire reflect middle-class fashion trends prevalent during this period. This photograph not only captures a moment frozen in time but also serves as a testament to America's growing financial sector during the mid-19th century.
