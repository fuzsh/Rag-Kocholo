Welcome to Summitville ...

The village of Summitville is located in Columbiana County<1>.

The location of Summitville has been provided by the Geographic Names Information System (ie- the GNIS).<2>

The pronunciation of Summitville: SUH-miht-vil<3>

While we don't have a date for the founding of Summitville, you might consider that their post office opened in 1853.

From the Census Estimates for 2019, Summitville has a population of 123 people<4> (see below for details).

Summitville is 1,110 feet [338 m] above sea level.<5>.

Time Zone: Summitville lies in the Eastern Time Zone (EST/EDT) and observes daylight saving time

Telephone area codes for Summitville: (330) and (234)<6>

Population Details ...

Taken from the 2019 Census Estimates, Summitville had an population of 123 people. This is a decrease of 8.89% since the 2010 Census (or an increase of 13.89% since the 2000 Census).

At the time of the 2010 Census, Summitville had a population of 135 people. This makes Summitville the county's 13th most populous community.

With a 2010 count of 135 people, the population of Summitville increased 25% from the 2000 Census (with a count of 108 people).

More information can be found on the Census Page for Summitville.
