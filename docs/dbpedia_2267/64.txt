Clarissa Brock was born on June 7, 1952, in Anniston, Alabama. She departed this life surrounded by her family on July 29, 2020.

She graduated from Parker High School in 1970. She was a Seamstress at Bush Hills Alterations in Birmingham, Alabama for many years. She was a member of True-Life Missionary Baptist Church under the leadership of Pastor Steve Small, Jr.

She was preceded in death by her husband Edward Brock and sister Jewell Watts.

Those left to cherish her loving mother: Billie Jean Ransom; uncle: John Carson Snow; two children: Dexter Keith Brock and April Jeanell Brock; two sisters: Jackie Mitchell (Renee) and Mia Parnell; brother-in-law: Ernest Watts; four grandchildren: Raven Nicole Layfield, Jalen Montez Brock, Lexcia Jemece Brock and JaKobe Edward Godbolt; one great-grandchild: Marko Williams;and a host of other relatives and friends.
