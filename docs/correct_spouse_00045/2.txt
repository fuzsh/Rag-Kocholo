About

Award-winning English songwriter and record producer who has written songs for a number of major recording artists and feature films. He has worked with artists like Mary J. Blige, Bryan Adams, Donny Osmond and Spice Girls. He contributed songs to films like Spice World, The Princess Diaries 2: Royal Engagement and Bridge to Terabithia.

Before Fame

He began writing songs at the age of 13.

Trivia

He is a co-owner of Steelworks Studios in Sheffield, England.

Family Life

He was married to Australian actress Natalie Mendoza from 2006 to 2010. He has a dog named Charlie Parker.
