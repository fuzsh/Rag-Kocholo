Mura

old town

Mura is a small, picturesque village located in the province of Barcelona, in the autonomous community of Catalonia, Spain. Nestled within the Sant Llorenç del Munt i l'Obac Natural Park, Mura is known for its stunning natural landscapes, medieval architecture, and tranquil ambiance. The village features charming stone houses, narrow winding streets, and historical sites such as the Church of Sant Martí, which dates back to the Romanesque period.
