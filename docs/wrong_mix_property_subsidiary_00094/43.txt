Notification number(s)

N94669

Summary

Snapfish is a website that supplies photo processing and printing products and services. It is owned by Hewlett-Packard Australia.

Nestle Australia Limited proposes to run a promotion whereby minor prizes offered would be supplied to winners by Snapfish.

Hewlett-Packard Australia proposes to supply or offer to supply credit prizes through the Snapfish website to winners of the Nestle promotion on condition that the winner has purchased three of the nominated Nestle products, complies with the terms and conditions of the promotion and creates an account with Snapfish.

The notification was allowed to stand on 13 May 2010.
