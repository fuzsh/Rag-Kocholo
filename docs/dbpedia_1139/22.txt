WSJR (93.7 MHz) is a commercial FM radio station licensed to Dallas, Pennsylvania, and serving the Wilkes-Barre - Scranton radio market. It is owned by Cumulus Media and has a country music radio format, known as "Nash FM 93.7" with the slogan "Northeast PA's Best Country." (en)
