CHARLOTTE, N.C. - Former Arthur Hill star Jason Richardson scored nine points in the Orlando Magic's 100-84 loss Tuesday to the Charlotte Bobcats.

Richardson was 3 of 10 from the floor, including 1 of 5 from the 3-point line. He was also 2-for-3 from the free throw line and had one two assists and one rebound.

Orland led by 20 points in the first half, but watched Charlotte go on a 22-1 to take the lead in the third quarter.

Corey Maggette led the Bobcats with 29 points. Dwight Howard led Orlando with 15 points and 17 rebounds.
