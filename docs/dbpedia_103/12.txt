This session is for educators who are interested in using the Virtual Learning Environment (vLE) for blended learning in their classrooms. No previous experience is necessary. We will provide an experiential learning model and guide you through the usage of the most important tools and resources in D2L. Blended learning uses the tools of the provincial Learning Management System (LMS) to teach and support learning in a face-to-face class. Through blended learning, students can access high-quality course materials, course calendars, and assignments during and outside school hours. This introductory session will focus on the following: overview of blended learning, Announcement tool, new lessons Content tool, new Activity Feed, Classlist tool, Discussion tool, Assignment (Drop box) tool, Calendar tool, HTML Editor, ordering courses, resources, use of the Quiz tool to help prepare students for the OSSLT and EQAO assessments, and best practices.
