David Burtka was born on 29 May 1975 in Dearborn, Michigan, USA. He is an actor and writer, known for Harold & Kumar - Alle Jahre wieder (2011), Neil's Puppet Dreams (2012) and CSI: New York (2004). He has been married to Neil Patrick Harris since 6 September 2014. They have two children.

Won the Clarence Derwent Award as Best Male Newcomer to the New York Theatre for his performance as "Boy" in Edward Albee's "The Play About the Baby". Has also been seen on Broadway in Albee's "The Goat or Who Is Sylvia?", off-Broadway in "Beautiful Thing" and in the national tour of "Beauty and the Beast".
