Jelena Jankovic, born on February 28, 1985, in Belgrade, Serbia, is a retired professional tennis player renowned for her dynamic playing style and tenacity on the court. Jankovic's career peaked in 2008 when she reached the World No. 1 ranking in women's singles. Known for her powerful baseline game, impressive footwork, and remarkable defensive skills, she amassed numerous accolades throughout her career, including 15 WTA singles titles. Jankovic's spirited performances in Grand Slam tournaments, particularly her runner-up finish at the 2008 US Open, solidified her status as one of Serbia's most accomplished athletes. After retiring from professional tennis, she has remained involved in the sport, contributing to its growth and development.
