Accomplishments

Victories in singles

Winner in Hong Kong, China by beating Angelique Kerber: 2015.

Winner in Guangzhou, China by beating Denisa Allertová: 2015.

Winner in Bogotá, Colombia by beating Paula Ormaechea: 2013.

Winner in Indian Wells, United States by beating Caroline Wozniacki: 2010.

Winner in Cincinnati, United States by beating Dinara Safina: 2009.

Winner in Marbella, Spain by beating Carla Suárez Navarro: 2009.

Winner in Moscow, Russia by beating Vera Zvonareva: 2008.

Winner in Stuttgart, Germany by beating Nadia Petrova: 2008.

Winner in Beijing, China by beating Svetlana Kuznetsova: 2008.

Winner in Birmingham, United Kingdom by beating Maria Sharapova: 2007.

Winner in Rome, Italy by beating Svetlana Kuznetsova: 2007.

Winner in Auckland, New Zealand by beating Vera Zvonareva: 2007.

Winner in Budapest, Hungary by beating Martina Suchá: 2004.

Winner in Dubai, United Arab Emirates (ITF) by beating Henrieta Nagyová: 2003.

Victories in doubles

Winner with her Slovakian partner Katarina Srebotnik in Toronto, Canada: 2013.

Winner with her Chinese partner Li Na in Birmingham, United Kingdom: 2006.

Victory in mixed doubles

Winner with British partner Jamie Murray in Wimbledon, London, United Kingdom: 2007.

Olympic Games

She participated in one Olympic Games in Athens, Greece (2004) by representing Serbia and Montenegro.

Fed Cup

She participated 12 times in Fed Cup (2001 to 2005, 2007 to 2012, and 2016).

Title

Best tennis player of Serbia: 2004.

World ranking

During the interview (November 1, 2006), she was ranked 12th in the world in singles according to the Women's Tennis Association (WTA).

Her best WTA world ranking in singles is the 1st place on August 11, 2008.
