Mr. Barker is responsible for leading and directing Beacon Hill Technologies in the Northern California markets, including San Francisco and Sacramento. With over 11 years in the IT staffing industry, he is known for building and developing highly successful sales and recruiting teams in some of the largest IT markets in the country. Prior to joining Beacon Hill, John led one of the top performing divisions for one of the largest IT staffing firms in the US. He started in the industry as a recruiter, and advanced into sales and leadership roles. He received his Bachelor of Science degree from Georgia Tech where he majored in management.
