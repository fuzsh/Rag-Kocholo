Arthur Wilkinson was a British artist who was born in 1860. Arthur Wilkinson's work has been offered at auction multiple times, with realized prices ranging from 13 USD to 1,020 USD, depending on the size and medium of the artwork. Since 1998 the record price for this artist at auction is 1,020 USD for Picking flowers, sold at Christie's New York in 2007. The artist died in 1930.
