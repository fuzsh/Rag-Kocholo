Biography

Stanley Kubrick is Notable.

Stanley Kubrick has Jewish Roots.

Stanley Kubrick was an American filmmaker and photographer. He is considered one of the greatest filmmakers of his era, directing such classics as 2001: A Space Odyssey and Full Metal Jacket.

Stanley Kubrick was born on 26 Jul 1928 in The Bronx, New York City, USA.[1] son of Jacob Leonard Kubrick and Sadie Gertrude Perveler. Stanley died of a heart attack on 7 March 1999 in England a few days after hosting a private screening of his latest film, Eyes Wide Shut.[2][3] He was buried in Childwickbury Manor.[4] He was married to German painter and actress Christiane Susanne Harlan at the time of his death.
