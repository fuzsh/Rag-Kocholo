New Castle, Delaware, is a city in New Castle County, Delaware, United States, with a latitude of 39.6620600 and longitude of -75.5663100. It is located on the Delaware River, across from Wilmington, Delaware, and has a humid subtropical climate with hot, humid summers and mild winters. New Castle was founded in 1651 by Dutch settlers and was the capital of the Delaware Colony until 1777. It is known for its rich history and heritage, including the New Castle Historic District, which is listed on the National Register of Historic Places.
