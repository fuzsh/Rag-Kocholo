Mrs. Mary Bryan, age 81, of Covington passed away Wednesday, May 24, 2023. She was born in Oxford on October 6, 1941 to Alvin and Susie Boyd Hodges who have preceded her in death. Mrs. Bryan grew up in Newton County and graduated from Newton County High School. She worked for Main Street Bank and enjoyed working in her yard and sewing. Along with her parents she was preceded in death by her grandson Dustin Bryan; her brothers James Hodges, Thomas Hodges, and Ernest Hodges.

Mrs. Bryan is survived by her husband David Bryan; her sons Mike Bryan of Covington, Lynn Bryan (Tammy) of New Zealand, Mark Bryan (Gina) of Covington, and Doug Bryan (Missy) of Cleveland, Georgia. She is also survived by 7 grandchildren, 4 great grandchildren, and her sisters Liz Allgood of Walnut Grove and Myrtle Beasley of Covington, and numerous other family members and many close friends.
