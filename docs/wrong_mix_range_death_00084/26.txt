What are the features of Bo Diddley drum beats?

The Bo Diddley drum beat is a distinctive rhythm present in musician Ellas Bates's (AKA Bo Diddley) music. The characteristics of the Bo Diddley drum beat are almost identical to that of the clave rhythmetic pattern.
