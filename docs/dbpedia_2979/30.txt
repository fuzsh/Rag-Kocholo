PRIVATE C. T. GROWL.

Private Claude Terrell Crowl killed

in action. At Gallipoli on May 21. was

the youngest son of Mr. R. T.Crowl.

of Poowong. He was born at the Na-

tional Bank, Stratford. and was 23

years of age. He was formerly a

scholar of the Caulfield Grammar

School and a member of the Trinity

Church choir, and was a nephew of

Mrs. Brear and Miss Orme, Mepunga

East State school.
