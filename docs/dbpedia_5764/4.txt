The Langenthal Zoo is situated in the city of Langenthal, within the canton of Bern in Switzerland. This location is easily accessible and offers a unique opportunity to explore the diverse wildlife in a serene environment.

Animal Species at Langenthal Zoo

The Langenthal Zoo houses approximately 80 animals from eight different species. Visitors can see a variety of animals including fallow deer, sika deer, red deer, and wild boar. Additionally, the zoo is home to dwarf goats, donkeys, and ponies. This diversity of species makes the zoo a great place for animal lovers and those interested in wildlife.
