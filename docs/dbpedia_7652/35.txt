Full Name: RuPaul Andre Charles

Profession: Actor, Singer and Drag Queen

Biography: RuPaul is America's most successful drag artist. He has a successful career as a model, recording artist, actor and TV host. His TV show "RuPaul's Drag Race" has won two Emmys.

RuPaul is considered a prominent figure in the Gay and Lesbian community and one who has helped increase visibility and understanding of LGBTQ issues through his strong media presence.

Born: November 17, 1960

Birthplace: San Diego, California, USA

Age: 63 years old
