Rolo to the Rescue Genre Platform Platform Sega Mega Drive/Sega Genesis Developer Vectordean Country United Kingdom Release date 1992

Rolo to the Rescue is a platform video game initially released in 1992. The player controls Rolo, a young elephant who travels to save his animal friends and mother, who have been captured by a circus. Each rescued animal has a special ability that can be used to get through the levels.

When Rolo picks up a helium tank, he will inflate and float around, allowing him to reach certain areas.
