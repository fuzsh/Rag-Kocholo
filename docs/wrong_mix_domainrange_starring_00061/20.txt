Profession: Actress

Biography: Amanda Peet is an American actress, best known for a number of television and film roles. She first came to widespread recognition with her performance alongside Bruce Willis and Matthew Perry in The Whole Nine Yards (2000).

She has since starred in a variety of roles, and has also written and produced a Netflix series titled The Chair (2021).

Peet married David Benioff, the showrunner for Game of Thrones, in 2006, and the couple have three children. She is also friends with Peter Dinklage, who she became close with while he was performing on Game of Thrones.

Born: January 11, 1972

Birthplace: New York City, New York, USA

Age: 52 years old
