Turn 10 Studios is a developer of video games in Redmond, Washington, United States. The company was founded in 2001 by Microsoft Studios, specifically for developing Forza Motorsport game series. The company released the first game, Forza Motorsport in May 2005, and continued working on feature releases, with the Forza Motorsport 2 released in May 2007. Turn 10 Studios continued working on the game series, releasing so far nine installments, see below.
