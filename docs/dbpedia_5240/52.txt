For four decades Ellery Queen was absolutely everywhere yet even though his Mystery Magazine is still being published if you asked an ordinary 20- or even 30-something about him nowadays, you'll likely only get a confused "Who's she?" back.

I am always fascinated by cultural phenomena that have then fairly quickly run out of favour. Safe to say: THE MANDARIN MYSTERY will definitely not be a production that will help the character to become rediscovered again.

I find it virtually impossible to rate films like these as they seem to have totally lost any link to our current reality.

Is it a good movie? No!

Would I recommend it to anyone? No!

Did I enjoy it? Define "enjoy"!
