Corbin Bleu Reivers (born February 21, 1989) is an American actor who plays Chad Danforth in High School Musical, High School Musical 2, and High School Musical 3: Senior Year. Bleu also appeared in "High School Musical: The Concert". He was also the runner-up on Season 17 of ABC's "Dancing with the Stars".
