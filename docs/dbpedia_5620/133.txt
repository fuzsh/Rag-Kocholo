Offered is a Joe DiMaggio signed Baseball Hall of Fame "Induction Day" print. These prints celebrate the July 25, 1955, induction ceremony when DiMaggion joined the hall. The print lists Joe's stats during his 16-year career with the New York Yankees. DiMaggio has signed boldly in blue Sharpie. Accompanied by an LOA from Beckett Authentication. The print measures 11 x 8 1/2 inches. Near Mint condition.
