Chhatna Chandidas Mahavidyalaya, Bankura is located in the state of West Bengal. Chhatna Chandidas Mahavidyalaya, Bankura was established in the year 2007. It is one of the leading college in Arts, Humanities and Social Sciences. Chhatna Chandidas Mahavidyalaya, Bankura is situated in Bankura, West Bengal. 5 courses in various specialities are taught in this institution. It provides UG course only.
