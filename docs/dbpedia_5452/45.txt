David Park is the Executive Vice President of Commercial Banking and Treasury Management, responsible for strategic development, client acquisition and retention of Specialty Deposit verticals, C&I client verticals and Treasury Management services for Axos Bank.

Prior to joining Axos Bank, Mr. Park was Executive Vice President and Head of Commercial Banking at Banc of California. There he was responsible for the development and operations of C&I business verticals, treasury management sales, leveraged lending, ABL and SBA lending.

Mr. Park held various roles during his 10 years at City National Bank, including Senior Vice President and Head of Business Banking and SBA Lending.

He holds an MBA in Finance from the University of Southern California, B.S. in Economics from UC Irvine and a Graduate Certificate from Pacific Coast Banking School, where he instructs a Leadership course.
