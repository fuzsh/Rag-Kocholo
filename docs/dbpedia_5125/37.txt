Tom Clancy's Splinter Cell: Essentials is an action-adventure video game with an emphasis on stealth. It is part of the Splinter Cell series and was released for the PlayStation Portable handheld system. It was developed by Ubisoft Montreal and released by Ubisoft in North America on March 21, 2006. It is the fourth installment in the series and was developed with the Unreal Engine 2.
