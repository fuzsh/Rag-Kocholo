Eduard Karl Wilhelm Prinz von Sachsen-Altenburg was born on 3 July 1804 at Hildburghausen, Germany .1 He was the son of Friedrich Herzog von Sachsen-Altenburg and Charlotte Georgine Luise Herzogin von Mecklenburg-Strelitz.1 He married Amalie Antoinette Prinzessin von Hohenzollern-Sigmaringen, daughter of Karl Anton Friedrich Fürst von Hohenzollern-Sigmaringen and Antoinette Marie Murat, Princesse Murat, on 25 July 1835 at Sigmaringen, Baden-Württemburg, Germany.1 He married Luise Caroline Prinzessin Reuss zu Greiz, daughter of Heinrich XIX Fürst Reuss zu Greiz and Gasparine Rohan-Rochefort, Princess Rohan-Rochefort, on 8 March 1842 at Greiz, Sachsen, Germany .1 He died on 16 May 1852 at age 47.1

He gained the title of Prinz von Sachsen-Altenburg.1
