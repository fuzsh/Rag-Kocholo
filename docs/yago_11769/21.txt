First marriage[edit]

On 25 July 1835, he married Princess Amalie of Hohenzollern-Sigmaringen.

Issue[edit]

Princess Therese of Saxe-Altenburg (21 December 1836 - 9 November 1914) married Prince August of Sweden, Duke of Dalarna.

Princess Antoinette of Saxe-Altenburg (17 April 1838 - 13 October 1908) married Frederick I, Duke of Anhalt.

Ludwig Joseph of Saxe-Altenburg (24 September 1839 - 13 February 1844)

Johann Friedrich of Saxe-Altenburg (8 January 1841 - 25 February 1844)

Second marriage[edit]

On 8 March 1842, he married Princess Luise Caroline Reuss of Greiz, daughter of Heinrich XIX.

Issue[edit]

Prince Albert of Saxe-Altenburg (14 April 1843 - 22 May 1902) married (1) Princess Marie of Prussia and (2) Duchess Helene of Mecklenburg-Strelitz.

Princess Marie Gasparine of Saxe-Altenburg (28 June 1845 - 5 July 1930) married Charles Gonthier, Prince of Schwarzburg-Sondershausen.
