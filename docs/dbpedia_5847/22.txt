Oliver Parker was born on 6 September 1960 in London, England, UK. He is a director and actor, known for Johnny English - Jetzt erst recht! (2011), Othello (1995) and Ernst sein ist alles (2002).

He acted in Pierre De Beaumarchais' play, "The Barber of Seville", at the Palace Theatre in Watford, London, England with Lee Cornes, Helena Bonham Carter and Barry Jackson in the cast. Lou Stein was the director.
