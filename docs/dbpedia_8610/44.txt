(D. R. on the list WAH - 1922-01-18). Thesis in the CVC - "Awarded to the teacher" rating - excellent. The training took place at the picturesque faculty, qualification of the artist painting.

Born in 1922 in Leningrad.

In 1950 Nina Veselova graduated from the Repin Institute of arts in Repin, Boris Ioganson workshop.

He studied of Boris Fogel, Leonid Ovsyannikov, Semion Abugov, Genrikh Pavlovsky, and Alexander Zaytsev.

Participant of exhibitions since 1939.

Member of the Leningrad Union of artists since 1950.

He painted portraits, genre and thematic compositions, landscapes, still lifes.
