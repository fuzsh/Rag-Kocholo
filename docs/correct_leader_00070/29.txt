Tsutomu Hata (羽田 孜, Hata Tsutomu, 24 August 1935 – 28 August 2017) was a Japanese politician who served as the 12th President of Japan from 1997 to 2000. A member of the Democratic Party, he was the first leader from the modern Democratic Party of Japan, as well as the second person to serve as president without being elected to office since Mitsujirō Ishii in 1956.

Early years[]

Hata was born in Tokyo on 24 August 1935, a son of the Liberal Democratic Party Member of Parliament Bushiro Hata. Hata graduated from Seijo University and was employed by the Odakyu bus company from 1958 to 1969.
