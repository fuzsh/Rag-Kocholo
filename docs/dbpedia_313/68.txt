Original British 30 inch x 40 inch Quad Poster for the 1983 Raoul Ruiz Fantasy THREE CROWNS OF THE SAILOR (LES TROIS COURONNES DU MATELOT) starring Jean-Bernard Guillard, Philippe Deplanche, Jean Badin, Nadege Clair, Lisa Lyon, and Claude Derepp and Frank Oger.

A drunken sailor recounts the surrealistic odyssey of his life story to a murderous student.

The poster art is by Yves Prince. Born in Paris in 1952, Yves Prince is a painter, photographer, poster designer and author of some three hundred posters for the cinema.

The poster is in fine unfolded (rolled) condition.
