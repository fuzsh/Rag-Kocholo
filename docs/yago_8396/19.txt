The Magnificent vision for this week features the public screening of the film L’udienza (Papal audience) by Marco Ferreri (Italy/France 1972, 111’, DCP, col.), restored by the Museum and by the Bologna Film Archive thanks to the MakingOf.it crowdfunding campaign. Repeat screening on Wednesday 5 November at 4.00 p.m. Admission 6.00/4.00/3.00 euro.
