Death at a Funeral is a 2010 American ensemble black comedy film directed by Neil LaBute. It premiered in Hollywood on April 12, 2010 and was released in the United States on April 16, 2010 by Screen Gems.

The film stars: Keith David, Loretta Devine, Peter Dinklage, Ron Glass, Danny Glover, Regina Hall, Kevin Hart, Martin Lawrence, James Marsden, Tracy Morgan, Chris Rock, Zoë Saldaña, Columbus Short, Luke Wilson.
