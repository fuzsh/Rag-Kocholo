By Antigoni Zachari / info@eurohoops.net

Bayern Munich and Danilo Barthel have agreed to extend their co-operation for the next two seasons, the team announced Thursday.

26-year-old Barthel has spent the previous two seasons with the German club and tapped an average of 8.5 points and 4.1 rebounds in 16 7DAYS EuroCup games this past campaign, as Bayern made it to the semifinals.

Barthel was also named German League MVP as Bayern seized the 2017-18 championship.
