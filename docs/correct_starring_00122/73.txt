Synopsis

Arnold Schwarzenegger joins Alan Ritchson in Amazon MGM Studios' Christmas comedy 'The Man With The Bag.' Directed by Adam Shankman, the film revolves around a heist to save Christmas. Schwarzenegger's last holiday comedy was in 1996, and Ritchson is known for his roles in 'Reacher' and 'Titans.'

In a delightful holiday surprise, Arnold Schwarzenegger is returning to the big screen in a new Christmas comedy, "The Man With The Bag," alongside Alan Ritchson, a Deadline report said. Directed by Adam Shankman, the film is set to be a heartwarming family adventure.

Schwarzenegger's Return to the Big Screen

Arnold Schwarzenegger, the legendary action star and former Governor, is making a highly anticipated return to the silver screen. Known for his iconic roles in films like "Terminator" and "Predator," Schwarzenegger's last movie appearance was in 2019's "Terminator: Dark Fate." Now, he is set to bring his unique charm to the role of Santa Claus in "The Man With The Bag."

'The Man With The Bag': Plot and Production

The film, produced by Amazon MGM Studios, follows the story of Santa Claus, whose magical bag is stolen. To retrieve it, Santa enlists the help of Vance, a former thief played by Alan Ritchson. Together with Vance's daughter and a group of misfit elves, they embark on a daring heist to save Christmas.

Cast and Crew

Joining Schwarzenegger and Ritchson are producers Lawrence Grey, Ben Everard, Reg Tigerman, Dan Spilo, and Ritchson himself. The film is directed by Adam Shankman, known for his work on hit comedies like "Hairspray" and "The Wedding Planner."
