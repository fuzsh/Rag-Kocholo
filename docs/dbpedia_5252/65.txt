The Porsche 911 GT1 (993) is a German GT1 class racing car manufactured by Porsche. The 911 GT1 (993) competed in the 1996 FIA GT1 championship season, which required the manufacturer to build a minimum of 25 road-going homologation specials to comply with the regulations of the series. Porsche built a total of 3 race cars in 1996.

Games[]

Test Drive Le Mans[]

The Porsche 911 GT1 (993) appears in the Eutechnyx's 1999 PlayStation and first PC rendition of Test Drive Le Mans as a GT1 Class vehicle.
