The 1996 Porsche 911 GT1 Strassenversion (German: Stra√üenversion) is a German performance car manufactured by Porsche. It is the street-legal version of the Porsche 911 GT1 race car and was assembled in Stuttgart, Germany. Strassenversion is German for "Street version". It was succeded by the Carrera GT in 2003.

Under the hood, the 911 GT1 Strassenversion is powered by a 3.2L twin-turbocharged flat-6 engine capable of developing 536 horsepower at 7,200 revolutions per minute (RPM).

The Crew 2[]

The Porsche 911 GT1 Strassenversion made its first appearance in The Crew 2 where the vehicle was added to the game on February 3, 2021 alongside with the Jeep MOAB Quicksand Concept and the Land Rover Defender Works V8 70th Edition as part of the Vault Enforcer Academy LIVE Summit. The vehicle is listed as a Hypercar and is only available as such. It can be purchased with either 1.050.000 or with 150,000.
