Edmund Gerald Dease1

M, #277772, b. 6 September 1829, d. 17 July 1904

Last Edited=30 Mar 2008

Edmund Gerald Dease was born on 6 September 1829.1 He was the son of Gerald Dease and Elizabeth O'Callaghan.1 He married Mary Grattan, daughter of Henry Grattan, on 21 June 1859.1 He died on 17 July 1904 at age 74.1

He graduated from Queen's University of Ireland, Dublin, County Dublin, Ireland , with a Master of Arts (M.A.)1 He held the office of High Sheriff in 1859.1 He held the office of Member of Parliament (M.P.) for Queen's County between 1870 and 1880.1 He held the office of Deputy Lieutenant (D.L.)1 He held the office of Justice of the Peace (J.P.)1 He held the office of Commissioner of National Education.1 He was a member of the Senate at Royal University of Ireland, Dublin, County Dublin, Ireland .1 He lived at Rath House, Ballybrittas, County Laois, Ireland .1
