Goopy Gyne Bagha Byne is a 1969 Bengali-language Adventure Comedy motion picture written and directed by Satyajit Ray and produced by Purnima Pictures. Goopy gyne bagha byne stars Tapen Chatterjee in the lead role along with an ensemble cast including Rabi Ghosh, Santosh Dutta, Harindranath Chattopadhyay, Jahor Roy, Santi Chatterjee and Chinmoy Roy. The movie is based on Goopy Gyne Bagha Byne by Upendrakishore Ray Chowdhury. The music was composed by National Award winner Satyajit Ray. Cinematography was done by Soumendu Roy and editing by Dulal Dutta. The motion picture has a running time of 120 minutes. It was released on 8th May 1969.
