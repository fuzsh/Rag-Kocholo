Amanda Michalka, known professionally as AJ Michalka, is an American actress, singer, and songwriter. Born on April 10, 1991, in Torrance, California, she began her career in music with her sister Aly as part of the duo Aly & AJ. Together, they garnered fame with hits like 'Potential Breakup Song'. Her solo career also reflects her diverse talents in both music and acting.

Amanda's music often delves into themes of unwavering spiritual faith and divine reassurance. Her songs convey a sense of future hope and optimism, exploring the journey of spiritual path redemption and the quest for true belonging. These themes resonate deeply with her audience, offering comfort and inspiration through her heartfelt lyrics.
