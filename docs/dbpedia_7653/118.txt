RuPaul, born RuPaul Andre Charles, is an iconic American drag queen, singer, and television personality. Born in San Diego, California, RuPaul gained international fame with the release of the hit song 'Supermodel (You Better Work)' in the early 1990s. They are also known for hosting the popular reality competition show 'RuPaul's Drag Race.'

RuPaul's music and public persona often explore themes of personal empowerment and self-expression. Their songs celebrate individuality, the cultural influence of drag queens, and the importance of being true to oneself. Through both music and television, RuPaul has become a symbol of artistic freedom and the celebration of unique identities.
