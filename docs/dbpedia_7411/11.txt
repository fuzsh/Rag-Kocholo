GCstar is an application that can be used tomanage collections of different types. Users canenter all the items they own, along withassociated information and later perform searchesusing filtering criteria. A plugin system allowssome information to be downloaded automaticallyfrom Internet sites. Collections can be exportedto many formats, including HTML, XML, CSV, orTellico file format, or imported from Ant MovieCatalog or Tellico, among others. It currentlymanages collections of movies, video games, books,music, numismatic, wines, board games, and lets users definetheir own types of collections.License: GNU General Public License (GPL)Changes:
