Frank Stephen Miller OBITUARY

FRANK STEPHEN MILLER Of San Mateo died at his home on April 28, 2003 at the age of 84. He was Secretary of the Elks Lodge of South San Francisco #2091, member of SEI Boku Bonsai Club, a World War II Veteran and worked for many years at Byron Shoe Stores. Frank is survived by his two sons, Jeffrey Miller of Tacoma, WA and Greg Miller of Toronto Canada. He had six grandchildren and two great grandchildren.
