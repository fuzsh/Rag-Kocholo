Naum Lvovich Aronson was a Russian sculptor who was born in 1872. Petach Tikva Museum of Art featured Naum Lvovich Aronson's work in the past.Naum Lvovich Aronson's work has been offered at auction multiple times, with realized prices ranging from 172 USD to 128,879 USD, depending on the size and medium of the artwork. Since 2006 the record price for this artist at auction is 128,879 USD for A marble figure of a nude, sold at Christie's London in 2009. The artist died in 1943.

Artist's alternative names: Naoum Aronson, Naum Aronson, Naum Lvovic Aronson
