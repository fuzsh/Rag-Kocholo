Biography:Chris Sarandon (born July 24, 1942) is an American actor of Greek descent. He was married to the actress Susan Sarandon, whom he met as a student at The Catholic University of America, and who retained her married name after their divorce. He has appeared in a large number of smaller budget genre films and distinguishes himself in most of his roles due to his intensity and dedication to the craft of acting. He is currently married to Joanna Gleason and has a 21-year-old daughter, Stephanie.
