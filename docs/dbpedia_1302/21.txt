Mary Marie Neal, 90, of Madill, passed away Monday, Feb. 20, 2012, at Brookside Nursing Center in Madill. Funeral services will be 10 a.m. Monday at Cumberland Baptist Church in Cumberland. Virgil Hill and Roy Vinson will officiate the service. Music will be arranged by Cumberland Baptist Church. She was born Nov. 1, 1921, to the late Joseph E. Each and Ethel Cox Each in Thorsby, Ala. Mary was raised in Alabama and later lived in Florida. She married Gaylan Woodrow Neal on July 17, 1941, in Newkirk. He preceded her in death on Feb. 20, 1994. She moved to Noble, where she lived for 40 years. Mary was a homemaker and also worked at the cafeteria at the Noble School. In 1983, she moved to Cumberland, where she lived the rest of her life. Mary was a member of the Cumberland Baptist Church, the Red Hat Society and the Eastern Star. She enjoyed cooking, playing the piano, gardening and mowing her lawn.
