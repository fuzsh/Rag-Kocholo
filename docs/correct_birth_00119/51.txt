Banja Luka ranks 585th in number of biographies on Pantheon, behind Troyes, Hangzhou, and Kingdom of Portugal. Memorable people born in Banja Luka include Milorad Dodik, Mustafa Nadarević, and Vinko Puljić. Memorable people who died in Banja Luka include Momčilo Krajišnik, Jadranka Stojaković, and Marijan Beneš. Banja Luka has been the birth place of many soccer players, and handball players and the death place of many politicians, and musicians. Banja Luka is located in Bosnia and Herzegovina.
