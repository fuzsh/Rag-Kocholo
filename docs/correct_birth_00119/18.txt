The talisman behind Croatia's run to the Davis Cup final is also one of the players of the 2005 ATP season, winning three titles from nine finals on tour.

Ivan Ljubicic has won all his singles and doubles rubbers in this year's Davis Cup campaign and is looking to emulate John McEnroe's clean sweep in 1982.

The 26-year-old made his debut for Croatia in the 1998 Euro-African zone against Finland, losing all three rubbers. Ljubicic has won 29 of 43 rubbers in total.
