About

Television and film actress who is recognized for her performance as Marta on Queen of Swords from 2000-2001. In 2015, she was cast on the Telemundo telenovela Dueños del Paraíso.

Before Fame

She began her career in entertainment as a flamenco dancer.

Trivia

She was nominated for the Best Actress award at the 2004 APES Award ceremony.

Family Life

She was married to Alexis Valdés from 2007-2009.
