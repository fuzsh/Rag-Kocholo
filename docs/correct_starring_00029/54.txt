Anna Maria Perez de Tagle is best known to TV audiences as Hannah Montana mean girl Ashley Dewitt. She also starred in the Disney Channel films Camp Rock and Camp Rock 2: The Final Jam opposite the Jonas Brothers and Demi Lovato. Perez de Tagle joined the Jonases on their North American tour and appeared in the 2009 Fame film remake before making her Broadway debut in the 2011 revival of Godspell.
