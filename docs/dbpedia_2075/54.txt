Baron Louis Gerhard De Geer af Finspång (18 July 1818 – 24 September 1896) was a Swedish politician, statesman, and author. De Geer pursued governmental reform and introduced the Riksdag. De Geer served as the first Prime Minister of Sweden from 20 March 1876 – 19 April 1880. Failure to resolve the armaments issue led him to resign.
