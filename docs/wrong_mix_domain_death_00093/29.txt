Teeters

Timothy Charles

Our beloved Tim, age 82, received his Angel wings on Saturday, December 23, 2023, at his Ingram home with his family by his side. He was the favorite handyman for his family and many friends and was a proud U.S. Army veteran. He was the beloved husband of Shannon J. (Carlin) Teeters for 48 years. Loving father of Tammi Lynn (William) Teeters-McDade, Stacie (the late Kristopher) Wilson, Shannon Ciganik and George (April) Ciganik. Grandfather to 8 children and Great Grandfather to 3. Dear brother of Mary “Patricia” Schnars, Margaret “Marge” Hill Conners, Owen “Mickey” Kirby, Judith Kirby, Regis Kirby and the late John Henry “Buddy”, George, Howard "Buckey" and his twin Thomas Teeters Sr. Friends received at the Hershberger-Stover Inc. Funeral Home and Cremation Services, 170 Noble Avenue, Crafton, on Thursday from 2 - 7 PM. Mass of Christian Burial in Ascension Church of St. Philip Parish, 114 Berry St., Ingram on Friday at 8:30 AM. Interment, with military honors, will follow in the National Cemetery of the Alleghenies.
