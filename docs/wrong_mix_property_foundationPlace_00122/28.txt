About Temenos

Temenos AG (SWX: TEMN), headquartered in Geneva, is a world leader in banking software. We collaborate with banks and other financial institutions to transform their businesses and stay ahead of the curve in a changing market.

More than 3,000 banks worldwide, including 41 of the top 50, rely on Temenos to process both the most day-to-day operations and transactions between customers of more than 500 million users.

Temenos offers front office and core banking systems, both cloud-native and interoperable, payments, fund and wealth management, that enable banks to consistently and frictionlessly track their customers, and achieve operational excellence.

Temenos software has proven to enable its highest performing customers to achieve efficiency ratios of 26.8% and a return on capital of 29%, 3 times better than the industry average. These customers also invest more than 51% of their IT (Information Technology) budget in growth and innovation versus maintenance, 2 times the industry average, demonstrating that banks' IT investment is adding tangible value to their businesses.
