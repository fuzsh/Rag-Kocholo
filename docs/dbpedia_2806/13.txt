Makhdom Bilawal bin Jam Hassan (b. 1451 AD / 856 AH) was a sufi saint, philosopher and poet of Persian and Sindhi languages; pulverized in a seed grinder by the Arghoon rulers under accusation of dishonouring the Quran. His shrine situated at left side of Indus Highway spread through Dadu City.
