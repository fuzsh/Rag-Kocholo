What is Shamari Fears' Net Worth?

Shamari Fears is an American R&B singer, dancer, and actress who has a net worth of $3 million. Shamari Fears was born in Detroit, Michigan in February 1980. She is best known for being the lead singer of the group Blaque. The group formed in 1996 and released their self-titled debut studio album which reached #23 on the US R&B chart and #53 on the Billboard 200 chart. Their singles "808" and "Bring It All to Me" both reached the Top 10 on the Billboard Hot 100 chart.
