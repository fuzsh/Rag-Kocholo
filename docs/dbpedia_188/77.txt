Ahmed Izzet Pasha (1864 - 31 March 1937), known as Ahmet Izzet Furgac after the Turkish Surname Law of 1934, was an Ottoman general during World War I. He was also one of the last Grand Viziers of the Ottoman Empire (14 October 1918 - 8 November 1918) and its last Minister of Foreign Affairs
