Telenet Selection (テレネット・セレクション), catalog number TOCT-5617, is a original, composed and arrangement soundtrack by Shinobu Ogawa, Hisao Inoue, Tenpei Sato and Jizou Club, and labeled by INVICTUS on December 13, 1989 in Japan. The soundtrack contains musics of 9 Telenet games, including Valis II, featured in Track 2: Medley I.
