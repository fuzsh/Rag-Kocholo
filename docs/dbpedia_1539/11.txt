Susan E. Dietter (pronounced "Deeter"), credited as Susie Dietter, is a former director on Futurama's original run. She started as a director on The Critic and The Simpsons, but went on to work on Futurama. When the show was cancelled, she went back to work for The Simpsons, which she still works on.
