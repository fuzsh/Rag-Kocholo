Jozsef Kerekes (born May 19th, 1962) is a Hungarian actor and dub actor, most known for dubbing Jim Carrey, Dan Aykroyd, Will Ferrell, Sylvester Stallone, Bobcat Goldthwait, Sean Astin, and Oliver Platt. He's also known for dubbing Peter Griffin in "Family Guy", Donkey in "Shrek", and Garfield.
