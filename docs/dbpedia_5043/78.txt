Biography

Paul Mullen (b. 1982) is an American composer, music educator, and tubist.

Paul Mullen is a 2009 graduate in music composition from the University of Missouri - Kansas City, as well as Stetson University in 2004. His initial influences as a composer came from 20th century classical music (including from film and video games), and were soon followed by jazz and rock from his years as a bass guitarist. He currently teaches elementary general music and choir in Rossville, Indiana, and performs with the Lafayette Citizens Band as a tubaist. He has also performed with the First Coast Wind Symphony and the Southern Winds from Central Florida.
