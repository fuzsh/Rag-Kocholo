Robert J. Miller Sr.andnbsp; 82, formerly of 1508 E. Grand Ave. Tower CIty, died Friday afternoon at his daughters home in Williamstown with whom he resided.

Born in Tower City, Sept. 7, 1923 a son of the late Harry and Myrtle Jones Miller.

Mr. Miller was a baker all of his life working for the former Coopers Bakery and then he and his wife owned and operated the former Tower City Bake Shop He retired as a supv. from PennDot - Harrisburg.

He was of the Protestant Faith and belonged to the American Legion .

He was a W.W. II Airforce Veteran and of the Navy Reserve.

He was pre-deceased by his wife Juanita Coleman Miller in 1990 a Step-son Charles Russell. 5 Brothers, Allen, Paul, Joe, Gene and Henry Miller 1 Sister Joyce Ponti and 2 Step-Sisters Eleanore Laing and Naomi Zerby

Surviving are his daughter - Shirley C. Malick and her husband Robert - with whom he resided 3 Sons - James R. and his wife Myra of Williamstown, John R. and his wife Lynn of CA. and Robert J., Jr. "Fritz" and his wife Janet of Muir; 6 Grandchildren - Robbie and Michael Malick, Daniel, Tammy, Jason and Heidiandnbsp;Miller 4 Great Grandchildren.
