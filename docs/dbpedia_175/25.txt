Burnet County

Burnet county is located close to the center of Texas. Burnet county has 994.26 square miles of land area and 27.00 square miles of water area. As of 2010-2014, the total Burnet county population is 43,911, which has grown 28.59% since 2000. The population growth rate is higher than the state average rate of 25.13% and is much higher than the national average rate of 11.61%. Burnet county median household income is $50,712 in 2010-2014 and has grown by 33.73% since 2000. The income growth rate is higher than the state average rate of 31.68% and is higher than the national average rate of 27.36%. Burnet county median house value is $153,000 in 2010-2014 and has grown by 63.46% since 2000. The house value growth rate is higher than the state average rate of 59.27% and is higher than the national average rate of 46.91%. As a reference, the national Consumer Price Index (CPI) inflation rate for the same period is 26.63%. On average, the public school district that covers Burnet county is close to the state average in quality. The Burnet county area codes are 512, 830.
