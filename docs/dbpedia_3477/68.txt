Lottie Mae Williams-Hardnett of Crossett, AR was born to the late Richard and Lottie Guy-Williams on February 20, 1957, in Little Rock, AR. On Saturday, April 4, 2020 surrounded by love ones Lottie transitioned from her earthly home to her eternal home. She was preceded in death by her parents, one brother: Willie James Williams.

Lottie loved her family and she loved singing. When she was younger she loved dancing but as she got older she began to take up the love for drawing and fishing.

She leaves to cherish her memories one son: Gregory Williams wife Latisha of Denver CO; two daughters: Rosalind Perry husband Joe of Jacksonville, AR and Melody Hawkins of Dallas, TX; Seven grandchildren: Isaiah Perry, Travis Perry, Mercedes Williams, Gregory Williams Jr., Jayland Williams, Jasmine Hawkins and Timberly Hawkins. Three great-grandchildren: Jeremiah, Gabriel, and Noah; five brothers: Robert Williams, Jessie Williams and Thomas Williams all of Crossett Arkansas, Elikai Ben Israel of Chicago, IL and Bobby Earl Williams of Germany; Two: Sisters Dorothy Tucker of Crossett, AR and Shirley Williams of Little Rock, AR and a host of nieces, nephews, cousins, friends, and loved ones.

A private graveside service will be held on Saturday, April 11, 2020, at 11:00 A.M.

Visitation will be held on Friday, April 10, 2020, from 11 until 7 at the chapel, under the direction of Mitchell Memorial Funeral Service of Crossett, AR.
