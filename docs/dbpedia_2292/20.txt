BIOGRAPHY

Milo Andrus was born March 6, 1814.

As of January 9th, 1875, he lived in St. George.

Milo Andrus was Superintendent of a United Order Company at Price, six miles from St. George, from the time of its organization until its dissolution and the winding up of its business (this organization existed during the years 1876-1877.

In 1879, Milo Andrus bought a house at 272 South 100 East in St. George from Miles P. Romney. He paid $2,000 for it. Then he sold it to Moses T. Farnsworth in 1882.

Andrus died June 19, 1893.
