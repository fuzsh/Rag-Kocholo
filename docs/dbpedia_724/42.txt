Masonic Temple - Aurora, Illinois

Posted by: BruceS

N 41° 45.217 W 088° 18.769

16T E 390856 N 4623253

Historic former Masonic Temple in Aurora, Illinois.

Waymark Code: WM1ARN

Location: Illinois, United States

Views: 15

The Masonic Temple was built in 1922 and served as home for 10 Masonic organizations. The building was designed by architect William Q. Bendus in a Neoclassical style. The building is now used as a banquet facility and is currently undergoing renovations.

Street address:

104 S. Lincoln Ave.
