About

Camille Keaton is a 76 year old American Actress. Born Camille Norma Alice Constance Keaton on 20th July, 1947 in Pine Bluff, Arkansas, USA, she is famous for her roles as Solange Beauregard in the 1972 giallo film What Have You Done to Solange?, Guendalina Bacherozza De Porcaris in the 1973 comedy film Il gatto di Brooklyn aspirante detective, Madeleine in the 1974 psychological horror film Madeleine: Anatomy of a Nightmare, Jennifer Hills in the 1978 rape-and-revenge horror film I Spit on Your Grave as well as in the 1993 rape-and-revenge horror film Savage Vengeance and the 2019 rape-and-revenge horror direct sequel film I Spit on Your Grave: Deja Vu, Rita Newman in the 1982 women in prison film The Concrete Jungle, Doris Von Fux (uncredited) in the 2012 supernatural horror film The Lords of Salem, and Marsha Kane in the 2019 home-invasion horror thriller film Cry for the Bad Man in a career that spans 1972-present and 1972–present and 1972–present and 1972 - present.
