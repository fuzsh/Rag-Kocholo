About Michael Gray

Michael Gray is a british DJ and house music producer who is signed to independent dance record label Eye Industries. He is also one half of the popular dance music production/remixing duo Full Intention. His first big hit was a house tune in the mutant disco style, called The Weekend. The record, which similar to Junior Jack's Room 5 owed a debt to Oliver Cheatham, became a Top 10 hit in the United Kingdom when licenced through UMTV and also notched up over 20,000 plays on the radio. In the United States, "The Weekend" was also a top 10 hit on Billboard's Hot Dance Airplay chart in 2005, where it was released on Ultra Records. The follow-up to The Weekend is called Borderline and features vocals from Shelly Poole, formally of the Top 20 recording act Alisha's Attic. Released on 7th August 2006.
