Michael Gray is a British DJ and music producer, best known for his work in the house music genre. He gained international fame with his hit single 'The Weekend,' which became a dance anthem in the mid-2000s. Gray has continued to influence the electronic music scene with his energetic and uplifting tracks.

Gray's music often revolves around themes of escaping everyday monotony and the exhilarating joy of weekend celebrations. His lyrics capture the anticipation and excitement for freedom, resonating with listeners who seek a temporary escape from their routine lives. Recent works continue to embody these themes, reinforcing his reputation as a master of feel-good dance music.
