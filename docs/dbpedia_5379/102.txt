Arnon Milchan is an Israeli film producer and owner of New Regency Films. Milchan produced numerous critical and financial successes. After co-producing Pretty Woman (1990), he formed Regency Enterprises. Regency's film credits include Oscar winners and nominees, as well as popular financial hits. He is considered one of the most prolific independent producers in Hollywood history. His film credits include such titles as; Brazil (1985), Legend (1986), JFK (1991), The Mambo Kings (1992), Under Siege (1992), Six Degrees of Separation (1993), The Client (1994), The Mirror Has Two Faces (1996), Fight Club (1999), Unfaithful (2002), Fantastic Mr. Fox (2009), 12 Years a Slave (2013), Birdman or (The Unexpected Virtue of Ignorance) (2014), Gone Girl (2014), and The Big Short (2015).
