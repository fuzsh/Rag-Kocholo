10th August 2022 – (Hong Kong) Legislative Councillors James Tien, Selina Chow, Miriam Lau said today (10th) that they plan to terminate their membership from the Liberal Party. The source said that the three were dissatisfied with the current leadership’s cancellation of the honorary chairman title without sufficient consultation. The chairman of the Liberal Party, Tommy Cheung, said that the Liberal Party leadership deeply regrets that the Liberal Party has received letters of notice of resignation from the three members. The party chairman is now trying to convince them to remain in the party.
