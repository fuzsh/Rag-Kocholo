At Duke University we strive to provide the best programming, facilities and training environment for our student-athletes as we expect optimal performance in return. We are fortunate to work out of 3 weight rooms to accommodate our 27 Division 1 sports programs.

Our mission is to provide the best student-athlete experience while pushing individuals outside of normal comfort zones to prepare for the demands of high-level, successful, Division 1 athletics.
