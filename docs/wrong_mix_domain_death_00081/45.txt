When William Mckinley Stephenson was born on 1 November 1896, in St. Louis, Missouri, United States, his father, Guido "Guy" Stephenson, was 37 and his mother, Lillian Vivian McMahon, was 32. He married Helen Hobart on 10 December 1921. They were the parents of at least 1 son and 1 daughter. He lived in San Diego, San Diego, California, United States in 1935 and Los Angeles Judicial Township, Los Angeles, California, United States in 1940. He registered for military service in 1919. He died on 21 November 1966, in Long Beach, Los Angeles, California, United States, at the age of 70, and was buried in San Bruno, San Mateo, California, United States.
