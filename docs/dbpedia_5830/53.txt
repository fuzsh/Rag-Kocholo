Ramat HaShofet (Template:Lang-he, lit. The Judge Heights) is a kibbutz in northern Israel. Located in the Menashe Heights, it falls under the jurisdiction of Megiddo Regional Council. In 2006 it had a population of 578.

The village was established on 2 November 1941 by two gar'ins, Mitzpe HaSharon and Shihrur, which included immigrants from Bulgaria, Hungary, Lithuania and Poland.
