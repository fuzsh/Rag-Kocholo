Emmy Rossum has given birth to her first child!

The American actress made the surprise announcement on Instagram, revealing that she and her husband Sam Esmail welcomed a baby girl on Monday, May 24.

“5.24.21 On a sunny Monday morning, at 8:13AM, we welcomed our daughter into the world,” Rossum said in the caption.

Emmy Rossum is known for her roles in “Phantom of the Opera,” “The Day After Tomorrow” and the award-winning comedy-drama “Shameless.”

Her husband, Sam Esmail, is the acclaimed director and producer behind the hit drama-thriller series “Mr. Robot.”
