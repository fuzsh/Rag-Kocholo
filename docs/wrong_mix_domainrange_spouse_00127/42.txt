The husband of actress Emmy Rossum, 23, has filed for divorce. Justin Siegel cited irreconcilable differences after a marriage lasting 18 months. The divorce filing, made at the Los Angeles Superior Court, contains no separation date but the pair is said to have split in August. Rossum played the part of Sean Penn's murdered daughter Katie in Mystic River and has already been nominated for a Golden Globe. Rossum was recently linked to Adam Duritz, 45, of band Counting Crows. They were seen canoodling in a New York restaurant over Labor Day weekend.
