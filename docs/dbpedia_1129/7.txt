THE Undertones have announced concerts celebrating the 45th anniversary of their classic single Teenage Kicks.

The Derry pop punk veterans will headline a hometown gig at The Millennium Forum on Friday November 24 before playing The Telegraph Building in Belfast on Saturday November 25.

The shows will see the band joined by special guests Buzzcocks (Belfast date only), The Outcasts and Specials man Neville Staple, plus DJ-ing by Good Vibrations founder Terri Hooley - the man who released Teenage Kicks all those years ago.
