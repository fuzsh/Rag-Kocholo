Beirut, also known as Beyrouth, is a vibrant and historic city located on the Mediterranean coast of Lebanon, at the latitude of 33.8889400 and longitude of 35.4944200. With a temperate climate, Beirut has a rich history dating back to the Phoenician era, and is renowned for its cultural heritage, blending ancient ruins with modern architecture.
