Phillip Marion Dickey, 67, of French Lick, formerly Paoli, passed away, Friday, November 9, 2018 at Springs Valley Meadows of French Lick. He was born November 3, 1951 to parents, Quinten "Lotus" & Dorothy M. (Johnson) Dickey. He is survived by: daughter- Jamie Marie Dickey of Salem; sisters- Linda Manship of French Lick & Dinah Bowles of Paoli; brothers- Stephen B. Dickey of Paoli & Eric Dickey of French Lick & grandchildren- Asia Marie Burns & Dayton Isiac Burns. He is preceded in death by parents & brothers- David Dickey, Terry Dickey & Bobby Dickey.

Phillip Marion Dickey was formerly employed as a security guard. He was a member of the Christian faith.

Visitation will be from 11am-1pm Monday, November 12 at McAdams Mortuary. Funeral will follow Monday at 1pm at the funeral home. Bro Harvey McAdams will officiate. Burial will be in Lick Creek Cemetery, Paoli.
