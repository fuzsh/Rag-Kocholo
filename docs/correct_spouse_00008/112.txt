SYDNEY - Australian actor Heath Ledger has split from his actress partner Michelle Williams, US Weekly has reported.

The couple who met on the set of Brokeback Mountain in 2005 have a daughter, Matilda, aged 23 months.

A source close to the couple was quoted in US Weekly as saying that Ledger and Williams, who is American, "quietly and amicably split a few weeks ago".

"The relationship had been rocky recently. They tried very hard to make it work but finally decided to separate. They just grew apart."
