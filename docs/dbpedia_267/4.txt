WXEI-LP (95.3 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a News/Talk radio format. Licensed to Crestview, Florida, USA, the station serves the Crestview/Fort Walton Beach area. The station is currently owned by X-Static Enterprises Inc. The station is home to the Nationally Syndicated Radio Show "Jon Arthur Live!". The show airs Monday-Friday live at 9 a.m.
