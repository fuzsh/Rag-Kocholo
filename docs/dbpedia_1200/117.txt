Le Tigre is an American electronic rock band formed in 1998 by Kathleen Hanna, Johanna Fateman, and Sadie Benning. Known for their eclectic mix of punk rock, feminism, and electronic music, the band has been influential in the riot grrrl and electroclash scenes. Le Tigre's energetic sound and politically charged lyrics have resonated with fans worldwide.

Their songs often explore themes of punk rock rebellion, searching for authenticity, and critiquing superficiality in society. Through their music, Le Tigre challenges conventional norms and encourages listeners to question societal structures and embrace individuality. Their continued relevance in modern music underscores the enduring power of their message.
