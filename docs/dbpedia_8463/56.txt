Eddie Peng(彭于晏) was born on March 24, 1982, in Penghu, Taiwan, China. He is an actor and singer of Canadian Chinese descent.

In 2005, he gained attention for his role as Tang Yu in the immortal drama "Chinese Paladin".

Main works include Fleet of Time, Operation Mekong, The Rescue, Wu Kong, etc.
