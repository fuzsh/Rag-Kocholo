Kathryn Cramer has had a career as a science fiction editor, and after graduating with a Master of Design in Strategic Foresight & Innovation, she is exploring what it means to be a designer. She is also an artist and writer. She has devoted her quarantine time to learning about mail art, 3D printing, & stencil art using spraypaint. Her studio is at The Juncyards on DuPont & Bartlett and The Juncyards are also her artistic representatives; her art is available for sale on their website. She has an orchard in upstate NY which she hopes to return to after the pandemic.
