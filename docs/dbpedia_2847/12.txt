Giles; a nephew Gavin Giles; a niece Gabriella Giles; his maternal grandmother, Carol Zurawski; his paternal grandfather, George Brown, Sr; and an aunt, Laurie Zurawski. He was the grandson of the late Charles Zurawski and Sherry Brown.
