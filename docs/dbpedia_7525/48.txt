Hans Grundig was a German artist who was born in 1901. Numerous key galleries and museums such as Kunstmuseum Stuttgart have featured Hans Grundig's work in the past.Hans Grundig's work has been offered at auction multiple times, with realized prices ranging from 108 USD to 522,564 USD, depending on the size and medium of the artwork. Since 2004 the record price for this artist at auction is 522,564 USD for Schüler mit roter Mütze, sold at Grisebach in 2020. Hans Grundig has been featured in articles for ArtDaily and ARTFORUM. The most recent article is Kunsthalle Mannheim Opens Major Exhibition 'Constructing the World: Art and Economy' written for ArtDaily in December 2018. The artist died in 1958.
