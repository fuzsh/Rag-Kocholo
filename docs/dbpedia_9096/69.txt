IST representations, usage and related time zones

Email/RFC-2822: Internet Message Format Date Standard, typically used for timestamps in email headers

+0330 - sign character (+) followed by a four digit time providing hours (03) and minutes (30) of the offset. Indicates three hour and thirty minutes time differences to the east of the zero meridian.
