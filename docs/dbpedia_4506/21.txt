Luna Amarilla is a 7 year old mare and has raced from 2021 to 2024. She was sired by Capo Bastone out of the City Zip mare Playinthecity. She was trained by Nestor Marrero and has raced for Alberto Orellana, and was bred in Florida, United States by Alberto Orellana & Sandra P. Velasquez. Luna Amarilla has raced at Colonial Downs, Gulfstream Park, Tampa Bay Downs.
