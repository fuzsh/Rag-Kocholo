MOUNTAIN VIEW--Infinity, the California-based trading and risk management software company, is to have its name changed to that of its parent company SunGard.

The new name for Infinity--which has recently released Treasury Trader, its first internet based foreign exchange and treasury system--is undisclosed.

However, analysts say that as Infinity is already branded "a SunGard company" the name change is likely to include the SunGard tag.
