Shah Mohammed Reza Pahlavi Good will visit. Afterwards visited New York City, Princeton (New Jersey), West Point (New York), Detroit (Michigan), Fort Knox (Kentucky), the Grand Canyon (Arizona), Las Vegas (Nevada), Phoenix (Arizona), San Diego, Los Angeles, and Sun Valley (Idaho). Departed U.S. December 13. November 16–20, 1949 Prime Minister Mossadeq Discussed the Anglo-Iranian oil dispute after appearing at the UN Arrived in U.S. October 8. October 23–November 18, 1951 Shah Mohammed Reza Pahlavi Unofficial visit. Afterwards visited San Francisco, Sun Valley (Idaho), and New York City. Departed U.S. February 11, 1955. December 13–15, 1954 Shah Mohammad Reza Pahlavi Informal visit. In U.S. June 1–July 3. Also visited Hawaii, San Francisco, Minneapolis (Minnesota), Springfield (Massachusetts), and New York City. June 30–July 2, 1958 Prime Minister Eghbal Attended CENTO Ministerial Meeting. October 7–9, 1959 Shah Mohammed Reza Pahlavi State visit at Washington and Onslow Beach (North Carolina). In U.S. April 10–18, visiting Cape Canaveral (Florida), Fort Bragg (North Carolina), and New York City. April 11–14, 1962 Shah Mohammed Reza Pahlavi Private visit. In U.S. June 4–14; visited New York City and Los Angeles. June 5, 1964 Shah Mohammad Reza Pahlavi Official visit. Arrived in New York City August 21. August 22–24, 1967 Shah Mohammad Reza Pahlavi Private visit. In U.S. June 10–16; visited New York City, St. Louis, Cambridge (Mass.), and Chicago. June 12, 1968 Prime Minister Amir Abbas Hoveyda Official visit. In U.S. December 3–16; visited New York City, Cape Kennedy, Palm Beach, Los Angeles, and Honolulu.
