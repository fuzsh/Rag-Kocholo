Aurora, IL, USA

Latitude and longitude coordinates are: 41.763889, -88.290001.

Aurora is a large city in the state of Illinois, USA, situated in the northern part of the state, in the south western neighborhood of Chicago. A century ago it used to be a small manufacturing settlement which has now grown into the second largest city in Illinois (after Chicago itself). Historically, the city has a nikname "The City of Lights" as it was one of the first cities in the region provided all the households and buildings with electricity. The current population of the city is estimated to be close to 200,000 people. The downtown area is popular for its very interesting stylish architecture and there are streets known for the architecture made in special styles like Lustron Style or Searas Style. There are also a lot of museums and theaters in the city.
