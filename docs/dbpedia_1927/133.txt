He began his career at IF Brommapojkarna, where he progressed through the youth ranks before making his first-team debut in the Superettan (Swedish second division) on July 9, 2022 in a 1-1 draw with Örgryte IS. On October 4, 2022, he scored his first professional goal against Östers IF. In total, he played 11 games and scored one goal for Brommapojkarna in the 2022 season.

On February 1, 2023, he signed for Djurgardens IF, one of the biggest and most successful clubs in Sweden, for a figure close to 900 thousand euros. He debuted as a starter in the Allsvenskan (Swedish first division) on May 24, 2023 in a 1-0 win over reigning league champions BK Häcken. After a great performance, he was chosen as the best man of the match by the Aftonbladet newspaper. So far, he has made 12 appearances for Djurgarden in the 2023 season.

He has also been international with the Swedish youth teams. He has played 8 games and scored 3 goals with the under-17 team, with which he participated in the 2023 European Under-17 Championship. He is currently part of the under-19 team, with which he has played 3 games without scoring goals.

Technical quality to control, pass and dribble the ball with solvency and elegance.

Vision of the game to distribute and create scoring chances with precise and creative passes.

Arrival in the rival area to finish off and score goals with his good shot from long distance.

Versatility to play in various positions in midfield.

Youth and projection, he is considered one of the best projects in Swedish football.

Lack of experience in elite football, having only played in the Swedish league and youth tournaments.

Resistance to prolonged efforts and physical strength to compete with guarantees against senior rivals in body to body duels.

Overconfidence, since sometimes he risks the ball too much and can lose it in dangerous areas.

Lucas Bergvall is a player with a lot of talent, personality and a future, who can become a great player if he continues to progress, continues to acquire skills and improve physically. He has the conditions to succeed in a team in the best European leagues and be an important player for the Swedish team in the future.
