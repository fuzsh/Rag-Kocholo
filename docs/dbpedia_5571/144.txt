Rolo Tomassi is an English band formed in 2005, known for their experimental approach to hardcore and progressive rock. The band has released several critically acclaimed albums and is praised for their dynamic live performances. Their unique sound blends elements of mathcore, jazz, and ambient music, showcasing their versatility and innovation.

Their lyrics often delve into profound themes such as illuminating moments, the nature of cyclical existence, and personal emotional transformation. These themes reflect a deep introspection and exploration of human experiences, resonating with listeners on an emotional and intellectual level. Their recent work continues to push boundaries, both musically and thematically.
