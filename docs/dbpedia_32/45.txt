On August 4, 2003, “Kling Klang” label released “Tour de France Soundtracks”, the eleventh and final Kraftwerk studio album. It was recorded in 2003, at “Kling Klang” in Düsseldorf, Germany, and was produced by Ralf Hütter and Florian Schneider.The album was recorded for the 100th anniversary of the first “Tour de France” bicycle race.

Personnel:

Ralf Hütter – vocals, software synthesizers, sequencing

Henning Schmitz – software synthesizers, electronic percussion

Fritz Hilpert – software synthesizers, electronic percussion, engineer

Florian Schneider – additional vocals, software synthesizers, sequencing

Tour de France Étape 3 – lyrics by Ralf Hütter, Maxime Schmitt, music by Ralf Hütter, Florian Schneider, Fritz Hilpert

Chrono – lyrics by Ralf Hütter, Maxime Schmitt, music by Ralf Hütter, Florian Schneider, Fritz Hilpert

Vitamin – lyrics by Ralf Hütter, music by Ralf Hütter, Florian Schneider, Fritz Hilpert

Titanium – lyrics by Ralf Hütter, Maxime Schmitt, music by Ralf Hütter, Fritz Hilpert

Elektro Kardiogramm – lyrics by Ralf Hütter, music by Ralf Hütter, Fritz Hilpert

La Forme – lyrics by Ralf Hütter, Maxime Schmitt, music by Ralf Hütter

Régéneration – lyrics by Ralf Hütter, Maxime Schmitt, music by Ralf Hütter

Tour de France – lyrics by Ralf Hütter, Maxime Schmitt, music by Ralf Hütter, Florian Schneider, Karl Bartos
