LinkExchange (ILE or Internet Link Exchange) was a popular internet advertising cooperative founded in 1996 by Tony Hsieh, Sanjay Madan and Ali Partovi. The company was acquired by Microsoft for $265 million.

LinkExchange was popular before it caught Microsoft's attention. its Banner Network had reached over a hald of internet-based households every months. The company that had 100 employees was a successful that it expanded its reach by acquiring MerchantPlanet, an early shopping cart and credit card application, Submit It! Inc., ClickTrade, and ListBot.
