Where is Westham?

Westham is located in the county of Somerset, South West England, five miles south of the town of Axbridge, ten miles south-east of the major town of Weston-Super-Mare, 23 miles south-east of Cardiff, and 120 miles west of London. Westham falls within the unitary authority of Somerset. It is in the BS28 postcode district. The post town for Westham is Wedmore.
