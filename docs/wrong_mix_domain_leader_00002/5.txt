Ljubljana, 12 April - The process of Slovenia's accession to the EU is coming to an end. The European Parliament confirmed the entry of the ten newcomers in 2004, paving the way for the signing of the Accession Treaty on 16 April. As the war in Iraq draws to a close too, Slovenia has welcomed the end to the dictatorship in Iraq, allocated funds for the reconstruction of the country and said it would help in the rebuilding of the country.
