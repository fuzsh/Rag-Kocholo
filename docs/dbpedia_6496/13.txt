Rural District in Mazandaran, Iran

Gatab-e Shomali Rural District (Persian: دهستان گتاب شمالی)[3][a] is in Gatab District of Babol County, Mazandaran province, Iran.[4] It is administered from the city of Gatab.[5]

Demographics

Population

At the time of the 2006 National Census, the rural district's population was 23,405 in 5,909 households.[6] There were 23,844 inhabitants in 7,076 households at the following census of 2011.[7] The 2016 census measured the population of the rural district as 24,650 in 8,240 households. The most populous of its 30 villages was Darzikola-ye Akhundi-ye Pain, with 2,153 people.[2]
