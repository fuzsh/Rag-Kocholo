I Am Curious (Blue) (1968) Jag är nyfiken - en film i blått (original title) Language: Swedish Synopsis / Plot The same movie with the same characters, cast and crew as I am Curious (Yellow), but with some different scenes and a different political slant. The political focus in Blue is personal relationships, religion, prisons and sex. Blue omits much of the class consciousness and non-violence interviews of the first version. Yellow and Blue are the colors of the Swedish flag. Awards 1968: Guldbagge Awards (Sweden): Best Actress (Lena Nyman)
