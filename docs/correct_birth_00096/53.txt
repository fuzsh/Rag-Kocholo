Victoria Azarenka was born July 31, 1989 in Minsk, Belarus. She was introduced to tennis at the age of 7 by her mother, Alla. She has one brother, Max, and she speaks Belarussian, Russian, and English. She also loves music, particularly hip-hop and R&B. Her favorite shot is her backhand and her best surface is hard courts. Her first Grand Slam title was in 2012 at the Australian Open. Her second came in 2013 at the Australian Open and she was also a US Open Finalist that year. Azarenka spends most of her time training and living in Monaco, and she's a great admirer of Roger Federer.
