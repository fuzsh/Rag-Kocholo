Martrel Reeves (born June 26, 1990), better known by his stage name Fat Trel, is an American hip hop recording artist. He is currently signed to DTLA Records with distribution via BERYL MEDIA, and was signed Rick Ross's label Maybach Music Group and Atlantic Records. From 2009 to 2016, Fat Trel released a solo mixtape every year.
