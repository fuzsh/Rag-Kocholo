Selected Italian sport-related comedies from late 1940s and 1950s.

Two major sport events impressed the post-war Italian public opinion:

the challenges between legendary Italian cycling champions Gino Bartali (left) and Fausto Coppi (aka il Campionissimo);

the victories of Turin's football team Torino FC (aka il Grande Torino) and its tragic end in the Superga's airplane crash on 4th May 1949.

Front page of the main Italian sport newspaper, Milan's La Gazzetta dello Sport:

FC Torino's airplane coming back from Lisbon crashes down and catches fire before arriving in Mirafiori (Turin's airport in those years) - All the players, the managers and three journalists deceased.
