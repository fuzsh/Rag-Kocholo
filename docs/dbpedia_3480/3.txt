A funeral service for Lottie Langley Williams will be held 11:00 a.m. Friday, March 26, at Oliver Funeral Home in Winona, MS, with Rev. Randy Bridges officiating. Visitation will be Friday from 10:00 am until service time. Burial to follow at2:00 p.m. at Greencrest Memorial Cemetery in Belzoni, MS.

Mrs. Lottie Langley Williams, 85, of Stewart passed away at her home Monday, March 22, 2021. She was born in Hollandale, MS on June 16, 1935 and was the daughter of the late Edd L. and Fannie Grey Hudson Langley. She was a cafeteria manager before retiring and a member of Stewart Baptist Church in Stewart, MS. In addition to her parents, she was preceded in death by her husband, Leslie Marvin Williams, Jr, her sisters, Jackie Blakely and Modene Russell and two brothers, Robert and Charles Edward Langley.

Survivors include one daughter, Leslie Williams Kelly (Emmitt) of Carrollton, MS, one son, Rueben Doyle Foster, Jr of Florence, MS, one brother, Art Langley of Carrollton; two grandchildren, Destiny May Kelly and Misty Foster and two great-grandchildren, Austin and Cody McCrory.
