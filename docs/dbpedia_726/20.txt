Herbert George is a British artist who was born in 1863. Numerous key galleries and museums such as Brauer Museum of Art have featured Herbert George's work in the past.Herbert George's work has been offered at auction multiple times, with realized prices ranging from 16 USD to 5,240 USD, depending on the size and medium of the artwork. Since 1999 the record price for this artist at auction is 5,240 USD for Captain Scott Writing his Diary', October 1911, printed 1930s, sold at Bonhams Knightsbridge in 2024.
