Offenbach am Main, located on the southern bank of the Main River, is a city known for its industrial history and cultural diversity. With a mix of contemporary art and historic landmarks, it’s a dynamic urban center. The Offenbacher Wochenmarkt is a bustling weekly market with local produce. The Klingspor Museum celebrates the city’s tradition of typography and printing. The Isenburger Schloss is a historic castle surrounded by gardens and walking paths.
