Daniel Lawrence Whitney, better known by his stage name "Larry the Cable Guy", is an American stand-up comedian, actor, voice actor, producer, country music artist, and former radio personality, known for his distinctive Southern drawl voice. He is mostly well-known for being a member of the Blue Collar Comedy Tour (along with Jeff Foxworthy, Bill Engvall, and Ron White).

For Disney, he is best known as the voice of Mater in the Disney/Pixar Cars franchise and its related media.

Outside of Disney, Larry the Cable Guy is well-known for portraying himself in most films and television shows.
