Caracas

Caracas ranks 145th in number of biographies on Pantheon, behind Odessa, Indianapolis, and British Hong Kong. Memorable people born in Caracas include Simón Bolívar, Nicolás Maduro, and Francisco de Miranda. Memorable people who died in Caracas include Hugo Chávez, Juan Pujol García, and Rafael Caldera. Caracas has been the birth place of many politicians, and soccer players and the death place of many politicians, and writers. Caracas is located in Venezuela.
