Hugo Chávez

(1954-2013)

President of Venezuela, 1999–2002 and 2002–2013 – Hugo Chávez was born in Sabaneta (Town in Venezuela's Barinas State) on July 28th, 1954 and died in Hospital Militar de Caracas on March 5th, 2013 at the age of 58. Today Hugo Chávez would be 69 years old.
