Abdul Qadir Bajamal (Abd-al-Qadir Abd-al-Rahman Ba-Jammal) (born 1946) has been the Prime Minister of Yemen since 31 March 2001. He is a member of the General People's Congress party and was appointed by President Ali Abdullah Saleh. Before Bajamal's appointment as Prime Minister, he served as Foreign Minister.
