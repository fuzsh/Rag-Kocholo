Taruni Sachdev Model - Born May 14, 1998 in Mumbai, Maharashtra, India

Died May 14, 2012 in Jomsom, Nepal (airplane crash)

Mini Bio (1) Taruni Sachdeva was born on May 14, 1998 in Mumbai, Maharashtra, India. She was an actress, known for Paa (2009), Sathyam (2004) and Vellinakshatram (2004). She died on May 14, 2012 in Jomsom, Nepal.
