Another 48 Hrs. is a 1990 American action comedy film & sequel to the 1982 film, "48 Hrs.", directed by Walter Hill, which was released on June 8, 1990 by Paramount Pictures.

Plot[]

San Francisco police officer Jack Cates (Nick Nolte) has 48 hours to clear his name from a manslaughter charge. In order to do so, he again needs the help of Reggie Hammond (Eddie Murphy), who is a newly released convict.

Meanwhile, at the same time, a mastermind known only as the Iceman has hired a biker gang to kill Reggie.
