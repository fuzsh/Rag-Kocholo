Biography

2023 (Sophomore): Played in 25 matches, starting 22 and in 89 total sets as a sophomore…named CUSA Setter of the Week on Oct. 23, 2023…led FIU with 808 assists for an average of 9.08 assists per set…posted a career-high 50 assists in four sets vs. Stetson on Sept. 15, 2023…racked up a career-best 45 assists in five sets on Oct. 4, 2023…amassed a career-best 40 assists in a three-set match vs. La Tech on Sept. 23, 2023…added 142 digs, 1.6 digs per set, fourth-most on the Panthers…had a season-most 12 digs and three service aces on Sept. 8, 2023 vs. Memphis

2022 (North Florida): In her freshman season at UNF, Cook started in 15 matches, playing in 22 while appearing in 78 sets, posting 665 assists, 15 aces, 121 digs and 31 blocks…Emerged as UNF’s starting setter in late September and went on to earn ASUN All-Freshman accolades... ASUN Freshman of the Week twice, earning the honor Oct. 24 and Nov. 7... Just the second two-time ASUN Freshman of the Week among setters in UNF history... Paced all ASUN freshman setters in assists/set during league play... Seventh-most assists in conference matches among setters...Second among all ASUN freshmen in assists/set for all matches... Posted three double-doubles... Recorded five aces in a 3-1 win against Stetson Nov. 11, the most by a UNF a student-athlete since September of 2019... Had nine matches of at least 40 assists, including a pair of 50-plus assist matches... Had 52 assists in a 3-2 win against Jacksonville State and 47 in a defeat of ASUN front runner Kennesaw State... Her 54-assist match in a 3-2 win against North Alabama also featured 12 digs... Had six blocks in a sweep of Bellarmine

HIGH SCHOOL: Under Armour All-American... First-team all-conference and second team all-area as a junior... As a senior, earned MEC Player of the Year, first team-all conference, and AVCA All-Region Team... Led the league in kills her senior year with 304... Other notable stats during senior year: 357 assists, 223 digs, 68 blocks, 5 triple-doubles and 15 double-doubles

PERSONAL: Daughter of Tiffany and Jon Cook... Has two siblings, Marlee and Bella... Father played baseball at Pfeiffer University
