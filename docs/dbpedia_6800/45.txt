Profile of Roger Daltrey

Roger Daltrey is an English singer-songwriter, actor and film producer who is best known for being the founder and lead singer of the rock band the Who. As a solo artist, Daltrey has released nine studio albums and some of his most popular songs include "Giving It All Away", "Thinking", "Walking the Dog", "Free Me", "Walking in My Sleep", "After the Fire", "Under a Raging Moon", "Rock and Roll", and more.
