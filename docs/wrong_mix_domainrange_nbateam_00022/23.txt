Jun 27, 2001 - The Los Angeles Clippers selected Tyson Chandler in Round 1 with Pick 2 in the 2001 NBA Draft. Jun 27, 2001 - The draft rights to Tyson Chandler was acquired by the Chicago Bulls, along with Brian Skinner, from the Los Angeles Clippers in exchange for Elton Brand. Oct 1, 2001 - Tyson Chandler signed a multi-year contract with the Chicago Bulls. Oct 24, 2003 - The Chicago Bulls exercised their Team Option to extend the contract of Tyson Chandler. Jun 30, 2005 - The Chicago Bulls tendered a Qualifying Offer to make Tyson Chandler a Restricted FA. Jul 1, 2005 - Tyson Chandler, previously with the Chicago Bulls, became a free agent. Sep 20, 2005 - Tyson Chandler signed a multi-year contract with the Chicago Bulls. Jul 14, 2006 - Tyson Chandler was acquired by the New Orleans Pelicans from the Chicago Bulls in exchange for P.J. Brown and J.R. Smith. Feb 17, 2009 - Tyson Chandler was acquired by the Oklahoma City Thunder from the New Orleans Pelicans in exchange for Joe Smith, Chris Wilcox and the draft rights to DeVon Hardin. Feb 18, 2009 - The trade that sent Tyson Chandler to the Oklahoma City Thunder from the New Orleans Hornets was voided. Tyson Chandler is now back on the New Orleans Hornets. Jul 28, 2009 - Tyson Chandler was acquired by the Charlotte Hornets from the New Orleans Pelicans in exchange for Emeka Okafor. Jul 13, 2010 - Tyson Chandler was acquired by the Dallas Mavericks, along with Alexis Ajinca and multiple TPEs, from the Charlotte Hornets in exchange for Matt Carroll, Erick Dampier, Eduardo Najera and cash. Jul 1, 2011 - Tyson Chandler, previously with the Dallas Mavericks, became a free agent. Dec 10, 2011 - Tyson Chandler was acquired by the New York Knicks from the Dallas Mavericks in exchange for Andy Rautins, Ronny Turiaf, a 2013 2nd round pick (NYK own) and cash. Dec 10, 2011 - Tyson Chandler signed a multi-year contract with the Dallas Mavericks as part of an eventual sign and trade to New York Knicks. Jun 25, 2014 - Tyson Chandler was acquired by the Dallas Mavericks, along with Raymond Felton and a TPE, from the New York Knicks in exchange for Jose Calderon, Samuel Dalembert, Wayne Ellington, Shane Larkin, a 2014 2nd round pick (DAL own), a 2014 2nd round pick (BOS own) and cash. Jul 1, 2015 - Tyson Chandler, previously with the Dallas Mavericks, became a free agent. Jul 9, 2015 - Tyson Chandler signed a multi-year contract with the Phoenix Suns. Nov 4, 2018 - The Phoenix Suns placed the contract of Tyson Chandler on waivers. Nov 6, 2018 - Tyson Chandler, previously with the Phoenix Suns, became a free agent. Nov 6, 2018 - Tyson Chandler signed a contract with the Los Angeles Lakers. Jul 1, 2019 - Tyson Chandler, previously with the Los Angeles Lakers, became a free agent. Jul 6, 2019 - The Los Angeles Lakers renounced their free-agent exception rights to Tyson Chandler. Jul 18, 2019 - Tyson Chandler signed a contract with the Houston Rockets. Nov 21, 2020 - Tyson Chandler, previously with the Houston Rockets, became a free agent.
