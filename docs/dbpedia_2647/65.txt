Apurva Asrani is an Indian filmmaker. He has written the human rights drama Aligarh, the courtroom drama Criminal Justice: Behind Closed Doors, has co-written& edited the film Shahid, has edited the popular gangster film Satya& also the webseries Made in Heaven. He also the director of the Tera Mera Pyar music videos for Sony Music India. Apurva made his webseries writing debut with the 2021 Disney Hotstar show Criminal Justice: Behind Closed Doors.
