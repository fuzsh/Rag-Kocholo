Wiki content for Yedida

Yedida - Yedida (Hebrew: יְדִידָה, lit. Friend) is a special education institute and village in central Israel.

Yedida Kameswara Rao - "Balabarathi" Yedida Kameswara Rao, better known as Radio Annayya, was a freedom fighter and personal secretary for "Andhra Kesari" Tanguturi Prakasam Pantulu.

Yedi Daraq - Yedi Daraq (Persian: يدي درق‎, also Romanized as Yedī Daraq) is a village in Ojarud-e Sharqi Rural District, Muran District, Germi County, Ardabil Province, Iran.

Yedid Nefesh - Yedid Nefesh (Hebrew: יְדִיד נֶפֶש y'did nefesh) is the title of a piyyut. It is usually sung on the Jewish Sabbath.
