BQ Aquaris E4.5 Ubuntu Edition The phone comes with a 4.50-inch touchscreen display offering a resolution of 540x960 pixels at a pixel density of 240 pixels per inch (ppi). BQ Aquaris E4.5 Ubuntu Edition is powered by a 1.3GHz quad-core MediaTek processor. It comes with 1GB of RAM. The BQ Aquaris E4.5 Ubuntu Edition runs Ubuntu Touch and is powered by a 2150mAh non-removable battery.

As far as the cameras are concerned, the BQ Aquaris E4.5 Ubuntu Edition on the rear packs 8-megapixel camera. It sports a 5-megapixel camera on the front for selfies.

BQ Aquaris E4.5 Ubuntu Edition is based on Ubuntu Touch and packs 8GB of inbuilt storage that can be expanded via microSD card (up to 32GB). The BQ Aquaris E4.5 Ubuntu Edition is a dual-SIM (GSM and GSM) mobile that accepts Micro-SIM and Micro-SIM cards. The BQ Aquaris E4.5 Ubuntu Edition measures 137.00 x 67.00 x 9.00mm (height x width x thickness) and weighs 123.00 grams. It was launched in Black colour.

Connectivity options on the BQ Aquaris E4.5 Ubuntu Edition include Wi-Fi 802.11 b/g/n, GPS, Bluetooth v4.00, FM radio, and 3G. Sensors on the phone include accelerometer, ambient light sensor, gyroscope, and proximity sensor.
