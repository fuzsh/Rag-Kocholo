SmarTone Telecommunications Holdings Limited, an investment holding company, provides telecommunication services in Hong Kong and Macau. The company offers voice, multimedia, and mobile and fixed fiber broadband services, as well as fixed fibre broadband services for the consumer and corporate markets. It also engages in the sale of handsets and accessories; issuance of guaranteed notes; and provision of customer support and telemarketing services.
