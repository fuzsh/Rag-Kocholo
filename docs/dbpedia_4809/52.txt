Overview

SiteGround is a privately held, Bulgaria, Europe based web hosting company. This company is founded in 2004 and today hosting 2,000,000 domains worldwide. They have 400+ employees that offer around the clock support and serves the businesses of all sizes.

With 1-click WordPress installation, 99.9% uptime guarantee, and 24/7 support, it’s a smart choice for every website hosting.

SiteGround is one of the largest and most popular hosting companies in the world. They offer shared hosting, VPS hosting, dedicated servers, cloud hosting, and managed WordPress hosting plans.

Uptime & Reliability

Amazing Loading Time

SiteGround uses Linux Containers technology. This technique is extremely resource-efficient, highly elastic and provides great stability in times of unexpected traffic spikes.

They have created in-house server monitoring. With Pro-Server Monitoring, the DevOps team quickly fixes the server issues.

They have developed an extremely effective Artificial Intelligence solution that stops brute-force attempts per hour across all their servers before they reached customers’ sites.

SiteGround uses the Secure Account Isolation Technique. This means a single account cannot affect other accounts in a shared server. They claim that they have one of the best security isolations in the industry.

Features

WordPress feature-rich & cPanel hosting

Shared Hosting

The list of features by SiteGround is mainly focused on WordPress site hosting and is perfect for small businesses. With a limited no. of SSD space and monthly user visits, they offer Free SSL and Cloudflare CDN. All shared plans are with cPanel interface and have 30 days money-back guarantee. PHP7 and http/2 are supported by SiteGround.

Speaking of SiteGround hosting, there are 3 shared hosting plans with different specifications. The basic plan, StartUp allows single domain hosting with 10GB space, email address, daily backups, website builders and allows up to 10,000 visitors per month.

The GrowBig plan comes with 20GB of space and has a 25,000 monthly visitor limit. In this plan, you can host multiple websites and get priority technical support and Super Cache for more site speed.

Siteground GoGeek plan has all the features of StartUp & GrowBig plans. 30Gb space, multiple websites and 100,000 monthly visits included in it. But you will get some advanced features in the GoGeek plan. The sites with this plan will get hosted on the PCI compliant server that has fewer accounts.

WordPress and Joomla site staging is available only in the GoGeek plan.

Cloud Hosting

There are 5 cloud hosting plans are available on the site. The entry plan starts at the cost of $80 per month that includes 40GB SSD Space, 5TB Data Transfer, and 4 GB RAM. WordPress, Joomla, Drupal can be installed with one click. WHM & cPanel, free SSL and dedicated IP, Softaculous Autoinstaller and SSH access are the features that are included in cloud hosting plans

Dedicated Servers

Siteground offers 3 high-end class dedicated server plans. Entry server starts from $269 per month including 16 GB RAM, 480 GB SSD space, 10 TB bandwidth, and 4 core CPU. There are 3 different locations to choose from.

All the dedicated server plans are fully managed and come with cPanel/WHM, SSH access, 5 Free Dedicated IP addresses and much more.

Pricing

Competitive prices for the features you get

SiteGround offers discounted pricing for the first time purchase. The startup plan costs with an introductory price of $6.99 per month with a one-year billing contract term. For monthly billing, it costs $19.99. StartUp plan renews at the cost of $19.99 per month.

GrowBig plan costs $9.99 per month for the first time purchase. This plan renews at the regular cost of $24.99 per month with a one-year billing term.

The GoGeek plan is the most advanced plan that costs $14.99 for the first time purchase. This plan renews at the cost of $39.99 per month with one-year billing.
