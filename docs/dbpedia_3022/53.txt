Definitions:

Japanese name: Self-explanatory.

Romaji: English transliteration of the Japanese name.

NOJ (Nintendo of Japan) name: From the official site and in-game.

Fan name: Derived by English Fire Emblem fans. There are other fan names besides the one listed; the fan names listed here are the ones commonly used on this site.

NOA name: From the North American (NA) version of Awakening.

NOE name: From the European version of Awakening (English language option).
