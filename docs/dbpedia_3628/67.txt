Kairana is one of the 403 Uttar Pradesh Legislative Assembly Constituencies that comes under the Muzaffarnagar district. Uttar Pradesh Assembly Election 2022 for Kairana was held on February 10 and the result was declared on March 10.

In 2017, the Kairana assembly seat had 3,00,654 registered voters. Of the, 1,63,915 were male and 1,36,739 were female voters. 0.3% of votes were casted for NOTA.

What happened last time

In the Uttar Pradesh Legislative Assembly Election 2017, Kairana constituency had 11 candidates for assembly seat contention. Kairana was one of 47 seats won by the Samajwadi Party. Samajwadi Party's Nahid Hasan won the election by upstaging Bharatiya Janata Party's Mriganka Singh by a margin of 21,162 votes.

The top two candidates split 84.8% of votes between them. Samajwadi Party's Nahid Hasan got a total of 98,830 votes, while Bharatiya Janata Party's Mriganka Singh secured 77,668 votes. Rashtriya Lok Dal's candidate Anil Kumar secured 19,992 votes to finish at the third place. The top three parties got 47.5%, 37.3% and 9.6% respectively.

In Uttar Pradesh Assembly election 2012, Bharatiya Janata Party's Hukum Singh beat Bahujan Samaj Party's Anwar Hasan by 19,543 votes.
