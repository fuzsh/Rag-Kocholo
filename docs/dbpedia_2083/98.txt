About 56th IBCT

56th Infantry Brigade Combat Team

The 56th Infantry Brigade Combat Team is a modular Brigade of the United States Army National Guard, headquartered in Fort Worth, Texas. The 56th Infantry Brigade Combat Team has a long and proud history of service to our state and nation.

The 56th Infantry Brigade Combat Team has deployed soldiers around the world - participating in the global war on terrorism, and serving our local and regional communities in times of natural disaster.
