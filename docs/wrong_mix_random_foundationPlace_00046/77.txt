El Centro, Ca. – March 1, 2024 – The El Centro Library is hosting Dr. Seuss Family Night from 4 p.m. to 6 p.m. on Wednesday, March 6, in honor of Dr. Seuss’s birthday and Read Across America Day.

Theodor Seuss Geisel is an American children’s author and cartoonist who wrote more than 60 books under the pen name Dr. Seuss. He is also known for writing the famous book “The Cat in the Hat” in 1957.

“This will be a wholesome family event that pays tribute to one of the best American authors in our nation’s history,” said El Centro Mayor Sylvia Marroquin. “There is no better place to celebrate Dr. Seuss’s birthday and Read Across America Day than at our beautiful library.”

Read Across America Day is March 2 and was established in 1998 by the National Education Association to get kids excited about reading.

The event will include crafts, games, refreshments and music. Look for more information at the El Centro Public Library Facebook page.
