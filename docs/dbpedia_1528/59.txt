Asya Shein is the Founder and Publisher of Fusicology, a renowned lifestyle marketing platform and boutique agency, with curated calendar listings and news for the Global Soul Music Community.

Since 2003, the hub has served the niche markets serving the RnB, house, hip hop, and jazz scenes in over 20 markets, having commemorated its 20th Anniversary in 2023.

As a digital marketing entrepreneur, cultural curator and event producer, Asya has been a part of the Prince Tribute, Miles Davis' Birthday Celebration, is the Co-Producer of the J Dilla Fest and various Grammy productions alongside being a 3-time executive event producer for LA Department of Cultural Affairs.
