Makati

Makati ranks 7,916th in number of biographies on Pantheon, behind Grants Pass, Oregon, Anzoátegui, and Montélimar. Memorable people born in Makati include Tim Tebow, and Sayaka Akimoto. Memorable people who died in Makati include Corazon Aquino, Diosdado Macapagal, and Fidel Ramos. Makati has been the birth place of many american football players, and actors and the death place of many politicians, and comedians. Makati is located in Philippines.
