Deborah Orr was an award-winning journalist who in 1993 became the first female editor of the Guardian’s Weekend magazine, and then a newspaper columnist. She had two children with the novelist Will Self, whom she married in 1997, separating from him acrimoniously 20 years later. She was diagnosed with stage four cancer last year, a decade after she was first treated for breast cancer. She died in October, aged 57.
