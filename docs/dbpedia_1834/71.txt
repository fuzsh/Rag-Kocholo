Travel Guide to Pha Taem National Park, Issan, Thailand

Pha Taem National Park is a stunning destination located in the Ubon Ratchathani Province in Eastern Thailand. Known for its ancient rock paintings, breathtaking cliff views over the Mekong river, and beautiful natural scenery, the park is a must-visit for nature and history enthusiasts. Here's how to reach Pha Taem National Park:

By Air

The quickest way to reach the area is by flying to Ubon Ratchathani Airport (UBP). Several domestic airlines operate flights from Bangkok's Suvarnabhumi Airport (BKK) and Don Muang Airport (DMK) to Ubon Ratchathani, including Thai Airways, Nok Air, and Thai AirAsia.

By Bus or Train

An economical way to reach Ubon Ratchathani is by bus or train from Bangkok. The journey by bus takes about 8-10 hours and trains can take approximately 9-12 hours, depending on the service chosen. Buses depart from Bangkok's Eastern Bus Terminal (Ekkamai) and Mo Chit Northern Bus Terminal, while trains leave from Hua Lamphong Railway Station.

By Car

Renting a car or hiring a taxi from Ubon Ratchathani allows for flexibility and is a good option if you plan to explore the region extensively. The drive to Pha Taem National Park from the city takes around 1.5 to 2 hours, covering a distance of about 100 kilometers.

Directions:

From Ubon Ratchathani, take Highway 24 eastwards.

Continue on Highway 24 and follow the signs leading to Khong Chiam district.

Once in Khong Chiam, take the rural roads following the signs to Pha Taem National Park.

Local Transportation

To get to Pha Taem National Park from Ubon Ratchathani without your own vehicle, you can take a local bus or songthaew to Khong Chiam, and from there take a tuk-tuk or arrange for a local guide to take you to the park.
