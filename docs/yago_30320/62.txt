Alexandra Feodorovna (Friederike Luise Charlotte Wilhelmine ; born Princess Charlotte of Prussia (13 July 1798 – 1 November 1860), was Empress of Russia as the wife of Nicholas I of Russia.

Issue[edit]

Alexander II of Russia (29 April 1818 – 13 March 1881) married Princess Marie of Hesse and by Rhine and had issue.

Grand Duchess Maria Nikolaevna of Russia (18 August 1819 – 21 February 1876) married Maximilian de Beauharnais, 3rd Duke of Leuchtenberg and had issue.

Stillborn daughter (1820)

Grand Duchess Olga Nikolaevna of Russia (11 September 1822 – 30 October 1892) married Karl, Crown Prince of Württemberg but had no issue.

Grand Duke Konstantin Nikolayevich of Russia ( 21 September 1827 – 25 January 1892) married Princess Alexandra of Saxe-Altenburg and had issue.

Grand Duke Nicholas Nikolaevich of Russia (8 August 1831 – 25 April 1891) married Duchess Alexandra of Oldenburg and had issue.

Grand Duke Michael Nikolaevich of Russia (25 October 1832 – 18 December 1909) married Princess Cecilie of Baden and had issue.

Titles and styles[edit]

13 July 1798 – June 24 1817 Her Royal Highness Princess Charlotte of Prussia.

June 24 1817 – 1 December 1825 Her Imperial Highness Grand Duchess Alexandra Feodorovna of Russia.

1 December 1825 – 2 March 1855 Her Imperial Majesty The Empress.

2 March 1855 – 1 November 1860 Her Imperial Majesty The Dowager Empress.
