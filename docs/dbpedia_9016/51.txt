Pretoria, South Africa

Latitude and longitude coordinates are: -25.731340, 28.218370.

Pretoria is a city in South Africa, located in the northeastern part of the country. It is one of the satellite cities of Johannesburg and can be found about 35 miles north of Johannesburg's city center. Pretoria is considered to be the administrative capital of the country, with Cape Town being the legislative capital city. Pretoria is an inland city that is situated on a small plateau between the Bushveld woodlands and the Highveld highlands of the northeast of South Africa. The city is located quite high above sea level, with an elevation estimated to be about 44 hundred square feet, and Pretoria has a beautiful setting, surrounded by fertile lands and low hills of the southern and western parts of the Magaliesberg mountains.

The border between South Africa and Botswana is located about 180 miles west of Pretoria and the distance to the city of Gaborone is close to 210 miles. To the east of Pretoria, a small country of Eswaniti is located, and a large city and a port of Mozambique, the city of Maputo, is situated about 300 miles east. The district of Central Pretoria is located in the northwestern part of the metropolitan area of the city, and some of the largest outskirts districts of the city include Pretoria North, Montana Park, Mamelodi, Moreleta Park, Roodeplaat, Atteridgeville, and many more. Pretoria is a known educational center of the country, with a number of large universities like the University of South Africa (UNISA), the University of Pretoria (UP), the Tshwane University of Technology (TUT), and others located in the city.
