Hardcore Henry, a new action flick from STX Entertainment, could be the first real-world test case for how virtual reality could enhance a non-interactive entertainment experience. The movie wasn’t made for VR headsets, but it was filmed entirely from a first-person perspective. It looks more like a videogame than a typical summer blockbuster, so it’s not hard to imagine how a VR version (or VR-specific sequel) could be developed.
