This is my eleventh entry for Jez Braithwaite’s Fan Of Challenge. I’m a fan of Indian Television Actress Pooja Sharma. Pooja Sharma is an Indian model and actress known for the role of Draupadi in Star Plus’s Mahabharat and as Mahakali / Parvati in Colors TV’s Mahakali — Anth Hi Aarambh Hai. She also worked in shows RadhaKrishn, Ram Siya Ke Luv Kush as narrator. In 2012, Sharma made her acting debut with Star Plus’s Teri Meri Love Stories. In 2013, she portrayed the role of Queen/Empress Draupadi on Star Plus’s Mahabharat. In 2017, Sharma played the role of Mahakali / Parvati on Colors TV’s Mahakali — Anth Hi Aarambh Hai. In 2018 and 2019 she voiced over for one show and narrated three shows. Pooja Sharma was listed 17th in Times of India’s Top 20 Most Desirable Women on Indian Television 2017. Pooja Sharma has a big following in Indonesia and other countries due to her acting as Draupadi in Mahabharat.
