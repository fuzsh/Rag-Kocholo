J. Robert (“Bob”) Andrews has over 50 years’ experience in the Firm. With Mullen and Henzell, Bob handles the business, corporate, real estate and estate planning needs of family-owned businesses and their owners, particularly those with significant real estate holdings. Working with others in the Firm having expertise in specific areas, he coordinates the planning, design, negotiation and implementation of transactional solutions to complex business and real estate issues. Typically, Bob works with his clients on acquisition, finance, operations, land use, disposition, and environmental matters, together with estate and succession planning.

He served as Managing Partner of the Firm for 16 years.
