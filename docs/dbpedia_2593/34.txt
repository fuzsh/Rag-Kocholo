Notes for Baronet Sir Charles De Carteret Sieg 5e Sark

Charles if the records are correct held a commission as cornet from the 20 th June 1685 in the Independent Troop of Horse,Non Resident Seigneur of Sark .question of who was living in Le Manoir at this time,property was passed to Lord John Carteret,his Cousin on his death in 1715.John held the Seigneur till he sold to Colonel John Johnson, 1720.Lost to the Family,

It appears that some arrangement was made to apease the family in Sark as the Estate known as le Manoir was broken up in to lots,La Seigneurie de le Manoir original size was some 95 vergees 16 perch,this also included La Perronerie,which had gone to Issac Le Gros in the “Quarantaine”list of property desent to the present day,the sell off started in 1691 and the last parcels of land in 1719

Supposedly had ason James born 1694 died before his father in 1715 Fief passed to Lord John Carteret who dropped the( de ) from his name
