Cambridge Footlights graduate Emma Thompson, daughter of Magic Round About writer and narrator Eric Thompson, has starred in a multitude of films and television series. From Love Actually to Sense and Sensibilty, Howard's End and Nanny McPhee, she has proved that she can do serious drama and brilliant comedy. Married to actor/director Kenneth Branagh in the 90s, she is now married Greg Wise who starred alongside her in Sense and Sensibility (1995) - the film which won her an Oscar for her writing.
