Prime Minister Nikol Pashinyan received the founding chairman of the international political consulting organization "Rasmussen Global" and former Secretary General of NATO Anders Fogh Rasmussen, and the delegation led by him.

Nikol Pashinyan and Anders Fogh Rasmussen warmly recalled the meeting held on February 18 this year in the fsidelines of the Munich Security Conference.

The interlocutors exchanged ideas on regional developments and security challenges. Issues related to security and stability in the South Caucasus were discussed. The sides emphasized continuous efforts in this direction.
