Oracle Corporation is a multinational computer technology corporation founded in 1977. The company is known worldwide by its Oracle Database product.

The company develops database (DB) software, enterprise resource planning (ERP) software, Human Capital Management (HCM) software, customer relationship management (CRM) software, and supply chain management (SCM) software.

Oracle Corporation headquarters in Redwood Shores, California. Company employes more than 137 000 persons.
