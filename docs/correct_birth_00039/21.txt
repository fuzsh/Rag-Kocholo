Novak Djokovic was born on May 22, 1987, in Belgrade, Serbia. He began playing tennis at the age of four. To date, he has won 53 ATP singles titles, including 8 Grand Slam trophies and 24 ATP Masters Series 1000 crowns. He was also a Bronze medalist at the 2008 Beijing Olympics and a winner of the Davis Cup championship with Team Serbia in 2010. Djokovic is currently ranked World No. 1.
