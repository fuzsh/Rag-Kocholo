The Memphis Chicks of the Southern Association ended the 1954 season with a record of 80 wins and 74 losses, finishing fourth in the SA.

The Chicks scored 822 runs and yielded 785 runs. Ed White topped all regular batters by hitting .314. Bill Fischer led Memphis with 14 wins, while Kelly Swift recorded a 3.73 ERA, best among hurlers with significant time on the mound.
