Cheryl Ann Tweedy, known as Cheryl ((previously Cole and Fernandez-Versini) born 30 June 1983) is an English pop singer and formerly a member of the band Girls Aloud. She was a judge on The X Factor UK until 2010 before returning in 2014 and 2015. She was formerly a judge on The X Factor USA for the Los Angeles and Chicago auditions.
