The world’s most popular film noir festival returns to SIFF Cinema Egyptian for the 13th year, kicking off with Argentine director Román Viñoly Barreto’s THE BEAST MUST DIE (La bestia debe morir) in a brand new 35mm restoration from the Film Noir Foundation!

Before the film, enjoy sultry tango music performed live by the Ben Thomas Trio beginning at 5:45 PM.

35mm restoration by UCLA Film & Television Archive, funded by the Film Noir Foundation, with support from the Hollywood Foreign Press Charitable Trust (The HFPA Trust). Special thanks to Fernando Martín Peña, Luis Scallela, and Argentina Sono Films.
