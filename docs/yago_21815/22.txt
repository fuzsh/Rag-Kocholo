My village at Sunset (1992)

NEARI is a young nurse at Siem Reap-Angkor provincial hospital. She recalls a recent memory. She seems to see her SEIHA again. He had been a young doctor, just returned from France. The two of them performed surgical operations on patients caused by land mines because Cambodia is full of mines. SIEHA stays in a house with DARA who is a teacher in the village. Her husband SOK is disabled because of the war. DARA tries to court SEIHA but then he decides to marry NEARI. After relieving her anger, DARA moves to Phnom Penh. NEARI and her sister who work in a factory producing prostheses agree to take care of SOK. The suicide of SOK makes SIEHA and NEARI feel sorrowful. SIEHA decides to follow thede-mining team. Finally, he dies in his mission there.
