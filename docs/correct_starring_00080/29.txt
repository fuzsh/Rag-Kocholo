Remembering Sterling Holloway, born January 4, 1905 and passed away on November 22, 1992.

Sterling Price Holloway, Jr. was an American actor who appeared in over 100 films and 40 television shows. He was also a voice actor for The Walt Disney Company, well known for his distinctive tenor voice and is perhaps best remembered as the original voice of Mr. Stork in Dumbo, Adult Flower in Bambi, Cheshire Cat in Alice in Wonderland, the eponymous character in Winnie the Pooh, Kaa in The Jungle Book and Roquefort in The Aristocats.
