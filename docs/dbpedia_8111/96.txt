Sharkey has been played on NTS in shows including If Music Presents You Need This w/ Jean-Claude, featured first on 23 February 2020. Songs played include Snobird (Dirty).

Sharkey is a popular British DJ/MC/producer and one of the key originators of the freeform genre of hardcore dance music.

Sharkey initially became well known as an MC at hardcore rave events in the UK in 1993, with a popular and charismatic style.
