LeRoy Harris graciously known as Pooh was born on December 24, 1952, In Kansas City, Missouri to the union of Leroy Sr. and Helen Harris. He Is the first of six children. Pooh was educated in the Kansas City school district. If you ever had the pleasure of spending time with him, you would know that he was an exceptional storyteller and an amazing artist.

LeRoy departed this life on December 17, 2022.

Preceding him in death are his parents, two brothers: Allen Harris and Edward Harris.

He leaves to cherish his memory, one uncle: Rayford Williams (Saundra) of California; two brothers: Raymond Harris and Lemuel Harris; one sister: Audrey Harris all of Kansas City; Children: Cortez Taylor (Adrienne), Chante Harris, LaTosha Harris, Latrice Harris, Markus Brinkley and Sparkle Brlnkley;(and more) Special Nieces: Sharae Bandy (Shayla and William) and Tiffany Harris (Aarion, Avery and Amari) all of Kansas City; and longtime companion: Cindy Lovell, along with a host of other grandkids, great grandkids, nieces, nephews, cousins, family and friends.
