Ulania

Ulania is an Union of Mehendiganj Upazila in the District and Division of Barisal, Bangladesh. Ulania is best known for its culture, tradition, education, sports etc. Some of well known Bengali writers, politicians came from this union. The well-known Chowdhury family can be found in the centre of Ulania. In Ulania town there are the ancient ruins of the palaces, mosques of a local Lord (called Jamidar in Bengali).

Haridaspur, Bangladesh

Haridaspur is a border town in Bangladesh, on its border with India.
