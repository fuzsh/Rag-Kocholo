Where They’re From:

Newark, New Jersey

Who They Are:

Gloria Gaynor is a popular American singer who is most known for her contributions to the disco era, and leading earlier jazz and R&B groups. Her breakthrough into the mainstream came after her hit single “I Will Survive,” and persisted throughout the 80s and 90s in the disco revival period. Gaynor wrote a number of Billboard hits, recorded 16 studio albums, and is still active today.

Years Active:

1965-present

Essential Albums:

Never Can Say Goodbye (1975), Love Tracks (1978), I Am Gloria Gaynor (1984)
