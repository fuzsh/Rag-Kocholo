Jamal Aman Jones (born April 24, 1981) is a former American football wide receiver. He was originally signed by the St. Louis Rams as an undrafted free agent in 2004. He played college football at North Carolina A&T. Jones is currently pursuing a Masters in Public Administration from the Harvard Kennedy School.

Early years[]

Jones attended DeMatha Catholic High School in Hyattsville, Maryland and was a letterman in football. Jones graduated from DeMatha in 1999.
