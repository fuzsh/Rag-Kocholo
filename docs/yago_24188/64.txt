Storyline: Reshma aur shera is a 1972 hindi film starring waheeda rehman as reshma and sunil dutt as shera. dutt produced and directed the film. it also stars vinod khanna, amitabh bachchan, raakhee and amrish puri. sunil dutt's son sanjay dutt who was 13 years at the time appears briefly as a qawali singer in his first film appearance. reshma aur shera was well received by the critics internationally and was nominated for the golden bear at the 22nd berlin international film festival.
