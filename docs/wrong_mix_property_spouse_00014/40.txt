Krista Allen – Born in 1971

Krista Allen is an actress who is known for her roles in Days of Our Lives and Baywatch. She later starred in TV movie Party Mom. Allen started her own line of T-shirts called Superexellent. She was married to Iranian rapper Mams Taylor, but the two called it quits in 2011.

Sandahl Bergman – Born in 1951

Sandahl Bergman is best known her role in the film Conan the Barbarian alongside Arnold Schwarzenegger, in which she played Valeria. The role earned her a Golden Globe award. She appeared in many films after that, including Ice Cream Man and The Singing Detective.
