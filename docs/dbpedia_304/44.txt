Posted Thursday 19 Aug 2004 at 3:20pm , updated Thursday 19 Aug 2004 at 3:30pm

Australia's Lauryn Mark has finished fourth in the women's skeet shooting at the Athens Olympics.

Diana Igaly of Hungary won the gold medal with 97 points.

Silver went to China's Wei Ning, who finished with 93 points.

Zemfira Meftakhetdinova of Azerbaijan (93) edged out Mark (92) for bronze.
