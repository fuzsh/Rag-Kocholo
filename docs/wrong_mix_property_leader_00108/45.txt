Young members of the volunteers’ union Yerkrapah gathered in Yerevan’s Yerablur pantheon on Wednesday morning to mark the birthday of Vazgen Sargsyan, a former minister of defense and prime minister, and a national hero of Armenia.

High-ranking officials (including Prime Minister Tigran Sargsyan, Minister of Defense Seyran Ohanyan, Yerevan Mayor Taron Margaryan, cabinet ministers and parliament members), veterans of the Nagorno-Karabakh liberation war and lots of other people visited Sargsyan’s grave since morning to commemorate him.
