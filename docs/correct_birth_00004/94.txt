There are two visitor centers at Abraham Lincoln Birthplace National Historical Park. The main visitor center is the Birthplace Visitor Center located at 2995 Lincoln Farm Road in Hodgenville, Kentucky. It is open year-round.

The Knob Creek Tavern Visitor Center is located in the Knob Creek Unit of the park at 7120 Bardstown Road in Hodgenville. This is approximately 10 miles from the Birthplace Unit. It is open seasonally.
