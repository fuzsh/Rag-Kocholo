Released: 2nd December 2022. Chet Baker and Art Pepper continued their fruitful collaboration with the 1956 Pacific Jazz album 'Picture of Heath' (originally released as 'Playboys'). The sextet for the session was loaded with top talent from the West Coast Jazz scene including Phil Urso, Carl Perkins, Curtis Counce, and Larance Marable. This mono Tone Poet Vinyl Edition was produced by Joe Harley, mastered by Kevin Gray from the original analog master tapes, pressed on 180g vinyl at RTI, and packaged in a deluxe tip-on jacket.
