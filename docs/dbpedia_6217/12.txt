Ali Tansu Turhan was born in 1991 in Ankara, and now lives in Istanbul, Turkey. He graduated from the Film and Television Department of Bahcesehir University in 2017. Between 2009 and 2012, he worked in PTOT production company in various positions. He wrote and directed his first short film Portrait (2010), selected in Cannes Short Film Corner. He worked as director of photography on the feature film Devir (2013). He made five more short films during the years 2012-2020. He is on the pre-production of his first feature film Diyalog (2021).
