OneRepublic is an American pop rock band consists of six members, Ryan Tedder (vocalist), Zach Filkins (guitarist), Drew Brown (guitarist), Brent Kutzle (bassist & cellist), Eddie Fisher (drummer) and Brian Willett (keyboardist). Their genres are pop-rock, pop, alternative-rock. Their debut album was 'Dreaming Out Loud' that got out November 2007 by Interscope Records and it was produced by Greg Wells.
