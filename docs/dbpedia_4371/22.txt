Steven James "Steve" Zahn (born November 13, 1967) is an American actor and comedian. He is best known for playing Wayne in Happy, Texas and Frank Heffley in Diary of a Wimpy Kid. For Phineas and Ferb, he voiced Sherman in "Dude, We're Getting the Band Back Together" and "Delivery of Destiny".
