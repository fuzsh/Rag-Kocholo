On April 26th, 1989, Lucille Ball passed away in a Los Angeles hospital, devastating fans worldwide. Her iconic career, highlighted by the groundbreaking show 'I Love Lucy,' made her a global sensation. Despite being in great health just weeks prior, the 77-year-old actress succumbed to a rare aortic rupture. Forensic pathologist Dr. Michael Hunter delves into Ball's medical records, death certificate, and personal accounts to unravel the mystery behind her sudden and tragic death.
