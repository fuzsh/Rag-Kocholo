Bryan Lee Cranston (born March 7, 1956) is an American actor, voice actor, writer and director, known for his work as Hal in the FOX situation comedy, Malcolm in the Middle, and Walter White in the AMC drama series, Breaking Bad, for which he has won several Primetime Emmy Awards. Other notable show appearances include Seinfeld, The King of Queens, From the Earth to the Moon and How I Met Your Mother.

He voices his character Hal Wilkerson from Malcolm in the Middle in "I Take Thee Quagmire".

In addition to other guest voices on Family Guy, Bryan makes a live action appearance when Stewie and Rupert watch Bryan Cranston Sneezes in "Herpe, the Love Sore". Following a simple sneeze, Bryan is handed an Emmy.
