Bryan Greenberg and Jamie Chung are the stars of the new movie "Already Tomorrow In Hong Kong" and they aren’t just romantic on screen, they got married in real life last Halloween. The film is about two ex-pats meeting in Hong Kong and sharing an impromptu romance. Greenberg and Chung speak to TODAY’s Al Roker, Tamron Hall, Willie Geist, and Natalie Morales about the film and what it was like working together.Feb. 11, 2016
