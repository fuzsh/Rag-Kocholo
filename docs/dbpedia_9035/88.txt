Garland Wilson was born on 30 July 1908 in Martinsburg, West Virginia, USA. He was an actor, known for On Velvet (1938), The Pursuit of Love (2021) and Burnt Sepia (1936). He died on 31 May 1954 in Paris, France.

In 1939, he returned to the United States, where he remained until 1951, when he moved to Paris, France. The artist remained there until he died in 1954.
