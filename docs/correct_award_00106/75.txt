A matted, black and white, 3.5" x 4.5" Photograph of Thomas Mann (1875-1955).

An attractive younger photograph of Thomas Mann, the German-born novelist, who was awarded the 1929 Nobel Prize in Literature.

Signed and inscribed by Thomas Mann to the matte, below the Photograph, to Robert W. Smith (1926-2011).

An interesting and noteworthy association. Robert W. Smith was a Martial Arts Master who penned many books and essays on Asian martial arts and their masters.

In fine condition.
