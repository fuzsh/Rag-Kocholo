WAYF-DT2 is a Fox affiliated station serving the Myrtle Beach, South Carolina broadcast area. It is a second digital subchannel of CBS affiliate WAYF and is owned by Berfield/Willis Broadcast Corporation. Over-the-air, the station broadcasts a high definition digital signal on UHF channel 43.2 (or virtual channel 5.2 PSIP). WAYF-DT2 can also be seen on cable channel 5 on most cable providers.
