A treatise on the decadence and shallowness of the 70s upper class, Welcome to L.A. boasts an impressive ensemble cast of characters navigating romantic despair in the city of angels. Sexual misadventures, failed affairs, and a womanizing singer-songwriter comprise Altman protégé and undersung hero of the New Hollywood Alan Rudolph’s “extraordinary debut” (Newsweek), featuring stand-out performances by Sissy Spacek, Richard Baskin, Harvey Keitel, and others.
