Giovanna of Italy (Giovanna Elisabetta Antonia Romana Maria) (13 November 1907 – 26 February 2000) was an Italian princess of the House of Savoy who later became the Tsaritsa of Bulgaria by marriage to Boris III of Bulgaria.

Issue[edit]

Princess Marie Louise of Bulgaria born 13 January 1933) married (1) Prince Karl of Leiningen and had issue. Married (2) Bronislaw Chrobok and had issue.

Simeon Saxe-Coburg-Gotha (born 16 June 1937) married Margarita Gómez-Acebo y Cejuela and had issue.

Titles and styles[edit]

November 13, 1907 – October 25, 1930 Her Royal Highness Princess Joan of Savoy, Princess of Italy

October 25, 1930 – August 28, 1943 Her Majesty the Tsarina of Bulgaria

August 28, 1943 – February 26, 2000 Her Majesty Tsarina Joan of Bulgaria.
