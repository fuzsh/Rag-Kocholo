The Prime Minister of Belgium Paul Van Zeeland, centre, along with the Belgian Foreign Minister Paul-Henri Spaak, right, are met at Liverpool Street Station, London, on July 23, 1936, by the Belgian Ambassador to London, Baron De Cartier De Marchienne. Van Zeeland is to take part in the three-power Locarno Conference being held in London.(AP Photo/Illingworth)
