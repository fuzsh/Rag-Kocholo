About

He played roles on Friends and Seinfeld and appeared with Clint Eastwood in Escape from Alcatraz as Charlie Butts the weak-willed inmate. He has appeared in popular TV series like Barry and Breaking Bad. He has amassed over 190 credits throughout his long career.

Before Fame

He attended Syracuse University.

Trivia

He has appeared on the television shows The Music Scene and Claude's Crib, and was nominated for an Academy Award for Best Live Action Short Film. He appeared in the popular Netflix movie El Camino.

Family Life

His middle name is Michael. He was college friends with screenwriter Carl Gottlieb.
