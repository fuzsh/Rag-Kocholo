EARLY LIFE

Born in New York City, Hankin attended Syracuse University with fellow artists Carl Gottlieb and Frank Langella. He studied acting and designing. He stepped into the entertainment world as an illustrator and later landed roles in films and television. Known for his deadpan voice and slender frame, Hankin has emerged as a successful actor. He is also a talented screenwriter, singer, director, and producer.

PERSONAL LIFE

Larry Hankin was born on August 31, 1940, in New York City, New York, USA. After graduating from high school, he attended Syracuse University.
