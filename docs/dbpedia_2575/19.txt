Dale Brown (born November 2, 1956) is an American author and aviator, most famous for his aviation techno-thriller novels, with thirteen New York Times best sellers to his name.

He was the author of both novels Act of War and the Edge of Battle, which where based on the video games Act of War: Direct Action and Act of War: High Treason, respectively. He was also the creator of the story for Act of War: Direct Action.
