2nd Earl of Courtown.

Son of James Stopford, 1st Earl of Courtown and Elizabeth Frances (née Smith) Stopford

Educated at Trinity College, Dublin.

Held the office of Member of Parliament (M.P.) for Taghmon [Ireland] between 1761 and 1768. Member of Parliament (M.P.) (Tory) for Bedwyn in 1774.

Appointed Privy Counsellor (P.C.) [Ireland] on 28 December 1775. Held the office of Lord of the Bedchamber to HRH The Prince of Wales between 1780 and 1784. MP for Marlborough between 1780 and 1793.

Treasurer of the Royal Household between 1784 and 1793. Privy Counsellor 1784.

Married Mary Powys on 19 April 1762 at St. George's Church, Hanover Square, London.

Children:

James George Stopford, 3rd Earl of Courtown, 1765-1835

Lieut. Gen. Hon. Sir Edward Stopford 1766-1837

Admiral Hon. Sir Robert Stopford 1768-1847

Revd. Hon. Richard Bruce Stopford 1774-1844

Buried on 12 April 1810.
