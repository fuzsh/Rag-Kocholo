About Nino Porzio

Nino Porzio born Antonino Porzio, 4 June 1972, is an Italian singer and actor. He has released five albums and his songs have been included in various

The first two albums collection with the song Ti amo are Messaggio d´amore with Milva and Adriano Celentano and Super Hits Sun & Fun with Gigi D'Agostino

has sold 30,000.

Born in Palermo in 1972, the Sicily|Sicilian-born but Naples Neapolitan artistically, since childhood is dedicated to singing and acting.

The late seventies he moved with his younger brother and his family in Germany.

In the eighties, the small Antonino began working in his father's grocery store in between studying singing and acting.

From 1983 to 1989 he began studying drums formed his first band, playing drums in various street festivals.

In the nineties, he began his career as a soloist in Germany, performing in other parts of Europe.

In 1995 he recorded his first album titled Rosa The album was presented to United States America by City News Service

In 1998 Porzio composed the song Sogno. The song is selected and included in a compilation of HDN Music label.

On 20 July 2000 he recorded his second album Mille Pazzie distributed in Italy by Musicanapoletana

In 2001, he participated in the singing competition “Voci Nuove Per La Discografia” in Rome organised by CBS Italia Edizioni Musicale e Discografiche Girofestival della canzone italiana of Rai Tre Italy.

In 2009 he signed his first contract with the record company Warner Music Group.

On 16 January 2009, he released his first single Ti amo produced by Akasa Records and distributed by Warner Music Group.

The Ti amo Song is very popular in Italy, Switzerland, Germany, France, Belgium, Austria, Egypt and Brazil has sold over 250,000 copies

The following year he triumphed in the final standings, getting first place in DDO-Chartbreaker Hitparade Wins Gold Record with Ti amo

April 21, 2011 he released his new album titled Caruso (album). The historic song from Lucio Dalla produced by Mint Records and distributed by ZYX Music.

Later comes out in 2011 the cd Made in Italy the cd contains 8 cover, including Nel blu dipinto di blu, Volare (song)|Volare by Domenico Modugno, Ancora Tu by Lucio Battisti, and Quando Quando Quando by Tony Renis, and many other.

.The June 14, 2013 organizes a concert at Lanxess Arena in Cologne, Germany, entitled Nino Porzio Live

accommodates 9000 people.

On 19 August 2013, the song Ti amo is placed in a box set of four CDs on the Italian song distributed by Mondadori Editore Mondadori.

Also in December 2013 comes out the new single Io penso a te The results are discrete for 4 weeks in the charts reaching the 38th place in the megacharts, Mallorca in Spain.

In June 2014 he released his new single Attimi, which anticipates the fifth album Brividi nel cuore, released September 17, 2014, recorded live and produced and distributed by Seamusica.

On 28 February 2015 he was named in the novel on the life of Lucio Dalla Caruso The Song Lucio Dalla and Sorrento" by Raffaele Lauro.
