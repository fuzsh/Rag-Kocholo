Education Resources

Photo Museum Ireland is committed to making photography education accessible to all.

We offer free educational resources available online. Our educational guides for exhibitions and our "How to Read an Image" worksheet enhance understanding and engagement with photography.

Free exhibition tour for school groups, tours and in-person workshops that explore our printed educational materials. We invite educators to explore these tools and take advantage of the diverse learning opportunities we offer to enrich their educational experience.
