Stockdale High School serves grades 9-12 in the Stockdale Independent School District. It is among the few public high schools in Texas to receive a distinguished GreatSchools Rating of 8 out of 10.

Stockdale High School is a public school in the Stockdale Independent School District located in Stockdale, TX. The principal of Stockdale High School is Sandra Lynn. As of 2015, the students are 56.4% White, non-Hispanic; and 40.4% Hispanic. 43.1% of students are participating in a free or reduced-price lunch program.
