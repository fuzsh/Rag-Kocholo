When Abraham Lincoln Hudson was born on 18 January 1865, in Fillmore, Minnesota, United States, his father, George Babcock Hudson, was 42 and his mother, Pamelia Ann Whitfield, was 37. He married Laura Mabel Casterton on 27 June 1894, in Fremont Township, Winneshiek, Iowa, United States. They were the parents of at least 2 sons and 1 daughter. He lived in Canton, Fillmore, Minnesota, United States for about 80 years. He died on 5 September 1954, in Canton Township, Fillmore, Minnesota, United States, at the age of 89, and was buried in Elliota, Fillmore, Minnesota, United States.
