Biography:Will Patton (born June 14, 1954 in Charleston, South Carolina) is an American actor. The eldest son of a Lutheran minister, he attended the North Carolina School of the Arts and The Actors Studio and won two Obie Awards for best actor for his performances in Sam Shepard's play Fool for Love and the Public Theatre production of What Did He See?. He was nominated for the Saturn Award for Best Supporting Actor for his portrayal of General Bethlehem in The Postman. He is also the proud owner of a Beagle.
