Maseru District Hospital and Eye Clinic is a state-of-the-art medical facility committed to delivering high-quality healthcare to the Basotho people. Equipped with advanced medical technology and offering services such as dialysis, ICU, CT-scan, dermatology, and eye care, we strive to meet the healthcare needs of our community. Our hospital also includes training facilities for aspiring doctors and nurses, reinforcing our dedication to improving healthcare accessibility and quality in Lesotho.
