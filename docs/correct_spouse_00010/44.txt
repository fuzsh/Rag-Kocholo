Emmy Rossum was secretly married to music mogul Justin Siegel before their recent split, according to a new report.

It was reported in tabloids earlier this week (07Aug09) that the Phantom Of The Opera star and producer Siegel had parted ways after calling off their long-term relationship.But the couple is actually filing for divorce after they wed in a secret ceremony two years ago, according to gossip blogger Perez Hilton.A source tells the website, Emmy and Justin decided to keep that part of their relationship a secret and out of the media spotlight. They wanted to keep it private in order to prevent their marriage from coming under scrutiny.
