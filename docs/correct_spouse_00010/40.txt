(17 February 2008 - 28 December 2010) (divorced)

Emmy Rossum secretly married music producer Justin Siegel, but he filed for divorce after they'd been married only a year and a half. The public didn't even know they were married until news of the split hit the Web.

Rossum married music executive Justin Siegel in February 2008. He filed for divorce on September 25, 2009, in Los Angeles, citing irreconcilable differences.
