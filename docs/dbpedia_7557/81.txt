Description

Massena, general of division, and deputy general in chief of the armee of italy. Caption below. André Masséna, Duke of Rivoli, Prince of Essling (1758–1817) was a French military commander during the Revolutionary and Napoleonic Wars. He is considered by military historians as one of the greatest field commanders in history and is often ranked among generals of his generation second only to Napoleon himself.
