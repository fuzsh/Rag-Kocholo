Roy (Popsy) Lawrence Webb, 89, passed away on February 10. He was born on September 14, 1929 to Joseph H. and Clara (Calkina) Webb in McKeesport, PA. Roy served in the Air Force during WW2 and lived at the Armed Forces Retirement Home for the last 30 years. He owned Webb Secretarial Service and DC Duplicators during his working years. He was an avid golfer, a passionate poker player and loved playing the slot machines. Popsy spent the last 29 years "working" at Southern Maryland Auto Repair with son Ralph. He made friends with the customers, delivery people and employees. He was known for his quick wit and humor and always had a smile for everyone.

He was divorced from Rose Marie (Waitlevertch) Webb with whom he had four children: Roy Webb, Jr. of Waldorf, MD, William (Barbara) Webb of Angier, NC, Ralph (Kelly) Webb of Waldorf, MD, and Sharon (Benjamin) Walsh of La Plata, MD. Additionally, he is survived by eight grandchildren: Ralph Webb, Jr., Jordan (Katherine) Hoffman, Benjamin Webb, Stephanie Webb, Nicholas Webb, Matthew Webb, Benjamin Walsh and Joseph Walsh and five great grandchildren: Emily Webb, Hailey Webb, Riley Webb, Sawyer Webb and Greyson Hoffman.

Friends received on Saturday, February 23, 2019 from 2PM until time of Funeral Service at 3PM at Raymond Funeral Service Chapel, 5635 Washington Avenue, La Plata, MD 20646. Interment will be at a later date at the Armed Forces Retirement Home Cemetery in Washington, DC.
