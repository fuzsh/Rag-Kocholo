Roy Webb, an industry veteran and top sales executive at Mark Andy for many years, has been named vice president of global and strategic accounts at the narrow web press manufacturing company, based in Chesterfield, MO, USA.

In his new position, Webb will focus on support for multi-national converters, says Greg Palm, VP of sales and marketing. “Roy will manage and strengthen the global cultivation of key strategic accounts for both Comco and Mark Andy equipment brands, with the aim to continue building strong cross-continental partnerships,” Palm says. “This new position is part of Mark Andy’s focus and commitment to supporting the expansion of converters into global markets.”

Acknowledging Webb’s “knowledge and leadership”, Palm said that the company is “excited to have an industry champion like Roy Webb leading the development of this multi-national customer network.”
