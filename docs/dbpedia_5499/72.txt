Regent University, located in Virginia Beach, Virginia, is a private Christian institution with a relatively modest campus and student body size. The university enrolls around 10,000 students, consisting of a mix of undergraduate and graduate students. Regarding the campus, it covers approximately 70 acres, providing a cozy environment for students to live, study, and participate in activities.

Regent's academic offerings span eight schools and colleges, and the university has a strong commitment to a Christian-centered education. Although the campus and student body are not as large as those of some other universities, the size allows for a more intimate learning environment and better opportunities to foster close connections with your peers and professors.
