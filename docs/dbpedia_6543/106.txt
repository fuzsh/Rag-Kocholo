1. Record label research Roc Nation

2. Context Roc Nation is an American entertainment company founded by jay-Z. the label is operated by the Sony Music Entertainment and distributed by Universal music group. The label was founded in April 2008 when live nation partnered with rapper jay-Z. In 2008 Jay-Z and Norwegian producers Mikkel S. Eriksen and Tor Erik Hermansen of the production duo Stargate formed the label StarRoc. The label is a 50/50 partnership between Stargate and Jay-Z's Roc Nation entertainment company, which he signed in the same year to Live Nation. In early April 2013, Roc Nation formed a new sports management division, Roc Nation Sports.

3. It is home to a diverse roster comprised of critically acclaimed recording artists, writers and producers. These include Rihanna, Shakira, J Cole, Rita Ora, Calvin Harris, NO ID, Timbaland, M.I.A., Santigold, deadmau5, Wale, etc. Roc Nation has a partnership with global management company, Three Six Zero.
