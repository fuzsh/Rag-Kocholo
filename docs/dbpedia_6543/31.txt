Roc Nation is an American record label founded in 2008 by Jay-Z as a partnership between him and Norwegian producers Mikkel S. Eriksen and Tor Erik Hermansen. Originally distributed by Sony Music Entertainment, it is now distributed by Universal Music Group. Roc Nation signs around 51 artists across music and sports, including major names like Jay-Z, Rihanna, J.Cole, Shakira, and Big Sean. Some of its top artists have amassed large net worths, with Jay-Z's being around $600 million and Rihanna's around $120 million.
