The ENnie award winning Nightfall Games is owned by Dave Allsop, Jared Earle, Benn Graybeaton, Garry Harper and Mark Rapson. The company was founded in 1993 in Scotland and is responsible for the 'SLA Industries RPG', 'SINS RPG' and the official 'The Terminator RPG'. We are known for high production values, evocative writing and some of the best art in the industry.
