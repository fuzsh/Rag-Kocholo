Lakehead University, commonly referred to as Lakehead U, is a publicly funded research university with campuses located in Thunder Bay and Orillia, Ontario, Canada. Notably, Lakehead University is non-denominational and receives provincial support. The university offers a wide range of programs, including undergraduate and graduate studies, the esteemed Bora Laskin Faculty of Law, the sole business school in northern Ontario accredited by AACSB (Association to Advance Collegiate Schools of Business), and serves as the western campus for the Northern Ontario School of Medicine.

With a strong network of alumni, Lakehead University’s main campus is in Thunder Bay, and it has expanded with a permanent extension campus in Orillia, situated roughly 150 kilometres (93 miles) north of Toronto.
