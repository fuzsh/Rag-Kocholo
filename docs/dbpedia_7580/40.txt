The PFBiH is the bicameral Parliament of the Federation of Bosnia and Herzegovina that is the larger entity covering 51% of the territory of BiH and is mainly populated by Bosniaks and Croats.

PFBIH is situated in Sarajevo and is composed of the House of Representatives and the House of Peoples. The Constitution allocates a complex system of exclusive and joint competences between the FBiH and its cantons.
