Sidney Luft was born on November 2, 1915 in New York City, New York, USA. He was a producer, known for A Star Is Born (1954), Kilroy Was Here (1947) and French Leave (1948). He was previously married to Camille Keaton, Patti Hemingway, Judy Garland, Lynn Bari and MaryLou Simpson. He died on September 15, 2005 in Santa Monica, California, USA.
