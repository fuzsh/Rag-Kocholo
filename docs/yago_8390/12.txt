This sometimes confusing erotic drama about the incestuous relationship of a mother and daughter is based on the autobiography of Italian theater actress Piera Degli Esposti though it focuses more on her mother Eugenia.

Director: Marco Ferreri. AKA The Story of Piera

Stars: Isabelle Huppert, Marcello Mastroianni, Hanna Schygulla, Angelo Infanti, Tanya Lopert, Bettina Grühn, Loredana Bertè, Rita Calderoni, Renato Cecchetto, Serena Bennato, Silvia Nebbia, Maurizio Donadoni, Aïché Nana, Girolamo Marzano, Lidia Montanari, Laura Trotter.

1983 Cannes Film Festival – Nominated for the Palme d’Or.
