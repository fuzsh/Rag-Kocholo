About College

Cotton College State University (CCSU) was established by the Govt. of Assam, with Cotton College as a constituent College of this new University. The vision of the University is to be a world-class one, excelling in developing knowledge, both research and teaching and skills with an emphasis on this part of our country. It provides broad-based education to students to take on the challenges of tomorrow via learning and assessment methods, modern curricula, upholding the highest ethical standards, and provide a clean, efficient and transparent administration.
