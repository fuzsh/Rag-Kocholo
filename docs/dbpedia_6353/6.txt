Overview

Cotton College State University is a state public university located in Panbazar in Guwahati. CCSU was established in 2011 through an Act of the state government of Assam. The cotton College founded in 1901 became its constituent college. The university focuses on teaching and research. This multidisciplinary university offers undergraduate, postgraduate and doctoral courses in areas such as physical, chemical and mathematical sciences, life sciences, earth sciences, language and literature, humanities and social sciences.
