Jun 28, 2005 - The New York Knicks selected Channing Frye in Round 1 with Pick 8 in the 2005 NBA Draft. Jul 2, 2005 - Channing Frye signed a multi-year contract with the New York Knicks. Oct 25, 2006 - The New York Knicks exercised their Team Option to extend the contract of Channing Frye. Jun 28, 2007 - Channing Frye was acquired by the Portland Trail Blazers, along with Steve Francis, a 2008 2nd round pick (NYK own) and multiple TPEs, from the New York Knicks in exchange for Dan Dickau, Fred Jones, Zach Randolph and the draft rights to Demetris Nichols. Oct 29, 2007 - The Portland Trail Blazers exercised their Team Option to extend the contract of Channing Frye. Jul 1, 2009 - Channing Frye, previously with the Portland Trail Blazers, became a free agent. Jul 10, 2009 - The Portland Trail Blazers renounced their free-agent exception rights to Channing Frye. Jul 14, 2009 - Channing Frye signed a multi-year contract with the Phoenix Suns. Jul 1, 2010 - Channing Frye, previously with the Phoenix Suns, became a free agent. Jul 8, 2010 - Channing Frye signed a multi-year contract with the Phoenix Suns. Jul 1, 2014 - Channing Frye, previously with the Phoenix Suns, became a free agent. Jul 14, 2014 - Channing Frye signed a multi-year contract with the Orlando Magic. Feb 18, 2016 - Channing Frye was acquired by the Cleveland Cavaliers, along with a TPE, from the Orlando Magic in exchange for Jared Cunningham, a 2020 2nd round pick (CLE own) and a TPE. Feb 8, 2018 - Channing Frye was acquired by the Los Angeles Lakers, along with Isaiah Thomas, a conditional 2018 1st round pick (CLE own) and a TPE, from the Cleveland Cavaliers in exchange for Jordan Clarkson and Larry Nance, Jr.. Jul 1, 2018 - Channing Frye, previously with the Los Angeles Lakers, became a free agent. Jul 2, 2018 - The Los Angeles Lakers renounced their free-agent exception rights to Channing Frye. Jul 19, 2018 - Channing Frye signed a contract with the Cleveland Cavaliers. Jul 1, 2019 - Channing Frye, previously with the Cleveland Cavaliers, became a free agent.
