Adrian Păunescu

Biography Adrian Păunescu (1943-2010)

Romanian poet, journalist and politician

Was born on 20 jul 1943.

Died on 05 nov 2010, at 67 years old.

Origin country România

Adrian Paunescu is a Romanian poet, journalist and politician. Paunescu is best known as a poet and promoter of literary circle The Flame. It is one of the most prolific contemporary Romanian poets. His books have sold over 1 million copies, holding a record in this regard.

Among his best known poems are:
