Sarah Silverman (born December 1, 1970, in New Hampshire, U.S.A.) is a Comedian, actress, and writer who got started as a writer and cast member on Saturday Night Live before starring in her show, “The Sarah Silverman Program.” She has since acted in television series and films such as “Mr. Show,“”School of Rock,” and “Wreck-It Ralph.” She even adapted her autobiography, “The Bedwetter,” into an off-Broadway musical.

As a stand-up, she made her television debut on the “Late Show with David Letterman,” and has since made four comedy specials, including the Golden Globes nominated “Someone You Love.”
