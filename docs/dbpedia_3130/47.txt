The Sea Girt Surf Lifesaving Association is a not for profit association formed in 1998 to support the additional needs of our Lifeguards, Lifeguard Athletes and Water Safety Campaign. We are thankful over the years to receive private donations helping fund our additional equipment needs and national and international competition teams and support of their efforts. We have also utilized donations to support public information flyers, bulletins and lectures in regards to beach and ocean safety.
