The 1993 film was the first version of The Secret Garden I saw, but fortunately discovered this 1987 version soon after. As a kid, I had both versions on VHS, but this one had plenty more replays and remains my favorite.

All the kids in here are delightful. Gennie James plays Mary as such a little spitfire, but one who comes into her own and matures into a kind young woman. She does better in the role than whoever the random kid from 1993 was. Barret Oliver's been a childhood favorite thanks to The NeverEnding Story and D.A.R.Y.L., even if his Yorkshire accent is practically non-existent here.
