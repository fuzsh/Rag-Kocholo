Constance of Austria (23 March 1560 - 27 October 1609) was, by marriage to Richard V, Duke of Veneria, Duchess of Veneria. She served as regent for her daughter Isabella of Veneria when she became Duchess. Constance, as a daughter of Ferdinand I, Holy Roman Emperor and Constance V of Genovia, was born an Archduchess of Austria and a Genovian Princess. She was the half-sister of Maximilian II, Holy Roman Emperor.
