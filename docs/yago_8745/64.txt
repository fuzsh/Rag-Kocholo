Françoise Rosay was born on 19 April 1891 in Paris, France. She was an actress and writer, known for La kermesse héroïque (1935), The Halfway House (1944) and Mutterliebe - Mutterleid (1951). She was married to Jacques Feyder. She died on 28 March 1974 in Montgeron, Essonne, France.

Often played stern, authoritarian family matriarchs who adopted extreme measures for her family's sake, usually to nefarious consequences
