“Bypass Giurgiu” project: 3TI has been commissioned the feasibility study, technical project, DTAC (technical authorization for construction) and executive details of bypass Giurgiu by the COMPANIA NATIONALA DE ADMINISTRARE A INFRASTRUCTURII RUTIERE S.A. in Romania.

The bypass will ensure the connection between roads DN5-DN8 and DNC in the Giurgiu district for an estimated length of 11 km.
