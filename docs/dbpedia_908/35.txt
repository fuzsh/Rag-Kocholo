Chris Heyring, a sculptor and lecturer in fine arts, devised a suspension system that has brought cash from Detroit.

Build a better mousetrap and the world will beat a path to your door, even if the door is in Dunsborough and the pathway from Detroit is 20,000 kilometres long. Tenneco Inc, one of the world's biggest makers of car parts, is undeterred by the distance between its United States headquarters and the south-west of Western Australia. It has just paid $52 million for a better vehicle suspension system and is turning its new Dunsborough acquisition into a private "skunkworks" to continue developing smart ideas.
