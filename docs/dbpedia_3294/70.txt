DELMARVA PUBLIC MEDIA IS A COLLABORATION OF PUBLIC RADIO STATIONS OWNED BY SALISBURY UNIVERSITY (WSCL 89.5 AND WSDL 90.7) AND THE UNIVERSITY OF MARYLAND EASTERN SHORE (WESM 91.3). IT COMBINES THE TALENT, EXPERTISE AND EXPERIENCE OF BOTH STAFFS TO LEAD A THREE- STATION OPERATION, FEATURING THE WIDEST VARIETY OF PUBLIC RADIO PROGRAMMING ON THE DELMARVA PENINSULA.
