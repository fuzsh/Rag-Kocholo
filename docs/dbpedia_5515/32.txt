At 3:00 p.m. on May 23, on the initiative of the Shirak for the Homeland movement, Archbishop Mikael Ajapahyan will meet in the rectory of the Diocese of Shirak with the members of local self-government bodies of Shirak Province who disapprove of the situation in the country and are ready to look for ways out of the current situation, the press service of the movement said.

“Further details of the meeting will be provided later,” the movement stated.
