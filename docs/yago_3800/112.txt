Marriage of the Princess Beatrice and Prince Henry of Battenberg, 23 July 1885, (1900)

Marriage of the Princess Beatrice Mary Victoria Feodora Wettin and Prince Henry of Battenberg, 23 July 1885, (1900). Beatrice, daughter of Queen Victoria, married Prince Henry at at St Mildreds Church, Whippingham, on the Isle of Wight. Illustration from The Life and Times of Queen Victoria by Robert Wilson, Volume IV, (Cassell and Company, London, Paris, New York, Melbourne, 1900).
