Cancellation Policy: To cancel a course, please email ce@laniertech.edu. All cancellations must be in writing via email. NO EXCEPTIONS. You may cancel or reschedule up to three business days prior to the course start date. All cancellations, transfers, and student substitutions will be charged a $20 processing fee. All cancellations received less than three business days in advance will NOT be refunded.

Low Enrollment Policy: If low enrollment requires that classes be rescheduled, you will be contacted and will receive a full refund or will be rescheduled at no additional charge.
