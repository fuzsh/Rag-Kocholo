Robert Drake Ford, age 76, resident of Eads, Tennessee and husband of Ann Ford, departed this life Sunday morning, February 18, 2024 at Baptist Memorial Hospital in Memphis, Tennessee.

Robert was born August 3, 1947 in Ottawa, Illinois, the son of the late James A. Ford and Jan Shank Ford. He was the owner of American Consulting, a computer consulting company.

Mr. Ford is survived by his wife, Ann Ford; his daughter, Katherine Breedlove (Scott) of Union, MS; his sister, Susan Ford Lester (Bob) of Reno, NV; and two grandchildren, William Breedlove and Abbey Breedlove, both of Union, MS. In addition to his parents, he was preceded in death by two brothers, John Ford and Mike Ford.
