Prior Scientific is a global leader in precision positioning devices, optical systems, automation solutions, and components. With a focus on custom OEM solutions, they offer a wide range of motorized stages, illumination solutions, and sample loaders to meet the advanced needs of microscopists. Their expertise in nanopositioning devices and sensors ensures the highest performance for demanding applications.

With a commitment to research and development, Prior Scientific continuously adds new products to their portfolio, including custom microscopes and optical systems, laser autofocus systems, and nanopositioning piezo scanners. They provide excellent customer support through telephone and email, along with a comprehensive collection of manuals and installation instructions for immediate download.
