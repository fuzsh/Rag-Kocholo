Background

She was born in 1884 in Mecca, Ottoman Empire. She was the elder twin daughter of Amir Nasser Pasha and his wife Dilber Khanum.

Career

In 1904 Musbah married Sayyid Abdullah bin al-Husayn later King Abdullah I of Jordan at Stinia Palace, İstinye, Istanbul, Ottoman Empire. His Royal Highness Princess Haya (1907–1990). Married Abdul-Karim Ja"afar Zeid Dhaoui.

King Talal I (26 February 1909 – 7 July 1972).

His Royal Highness Princess Munira (1915–1987). Never married. Abdullah went on to take two more wives.

Queen Musbah died on 15 March 1961 in Irbid, Jordan.
