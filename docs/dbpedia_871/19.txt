Doha: Joyalukkas has reopened one of its five showrooms in Qatar after a complete upgrade and revamp. The newly refurbished showroom is meant to enhance the shopping experience for all jewellery lovers. The showroom is located at Al Watan Centre, and is now open to customers for shopping.

The redesigned interiors feature luxurious and modern décor and is as per the global standards set by Joyalukkas. The showroom offers a welcoming feel with a comfortable ambience that is designed keeping in mind a shopper's convenience, both the shopping space and parking space has also been increased to add to the convenience. This re-opening is timed to meet the demand of the festive season and bring the best offers and jewellery designs to jewellery lovers in Qatar.

On the occasion of the reopening, John Paul Alukkas, Managing Director at Joyalukkas Group, said,“We have received a lot of love from our patrons in Qatar, and we felt it's now time to upgrade their shopping experience and convenience. We are happy to offer our customers an unparalleled global jewellery experience with the best facilities and services.

'I welcome everyone to visit our refurbished showroom in Qatar for a world-class jewellery shopping experience. Also we have attractive offers planned for Eid and Akshaya Tritiya which they can take advantage of.”
