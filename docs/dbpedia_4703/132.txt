K1 Speed has opened its eighth family-friendly, Southern California location at K1 Speed Thousand Oaks, open daily at 770 Lawrence Drive, Newbury Park.

Adults and teens can drive the all-electric Adult Karts, which can reach speeds of up to 45 mph on the professionally designed circuit.

Kids 4 feet and taller can get behind the wheel of electric Junior Karts, which can reach 20 mph.

The off-track gaming area has over 30 video games from the past and present, pool tables, air hockey, basketball hoops and prize machines.

The Paddock Lounge serves hamburgers, pizza, nachos, wings and an assortment of nonalcoholic beverages.

A private room for birthdays, corporate events, bachelor parties and other group events is available.

The entire venue may be rented by the hour, half-day or full day.
