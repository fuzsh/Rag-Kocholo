Available for the first time on vinyl in celebration of its 25th anniversary, My Own Prison is the debut album from hard rock titans Creed. First released in 1997, it's the album that began the band’s rapid ascent to becoming one of the biggest-selling artists of all time. Featuring four Billboard Hot Mainstream Rock #1 singles “My Own Prison,” “Torn,” “What’s This Life For” and “One.”
