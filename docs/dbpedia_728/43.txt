Mr. Herbert Scott, 74, of Ridgeland, SC passed away Monday April 26, 2021. Mr. Scott was born June 16, 1946 to Robert and Priscilla Mervin Scott in Ridgeland, SC.

Mr. Scott served in the US Army and later worked as a carpenter. He was a friend to all, and everyone who knew him loved him. He always enjoyed a good joke.

He leaves to cherish his memory a host of nieces and nephews. In addition to his parents, he was preceded in death by six brothers and one sister.

A funeral service will be at 10:00 am, on Friday, May 28, 2021 at Beaufort National Cemetery, 1601 Boundary St., Beaufort, SC 29902.
