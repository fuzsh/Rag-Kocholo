Happy birthday Chris Sarandon, born July 24, 1942.

Christopher Sarandon Jr. is an American actor. He is known best for playing Prince Humperdinck in the movie The Princess Bride, the vampire Jerry Dandrige in Fright Night, Detective Mike Norris in Child’s Play (1988), and for providing the speaking voice of Jack Skellington in The Nightmare Before Christmas. He was nominated for an Academy Award for Best Supporting Actor for his performance as Leon Shermer in Dog Day Afternoon.

He played Martus Mazur in the Star Trek: Deep Space Nine episode “Rivals”.
