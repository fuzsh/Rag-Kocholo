100.7 The Bay (WZBA) is owned by Times-Shamrock Communications and broadcasts a classic rock format. From legendary bands like Led Zeppelin and Guns N' Roses to contemporary artists like Royal Blood and Highly Suspect, there's something for everyone on this station. The station markets itself as the only station in the Baltimore market dedicated to the classic rock format. It is also dedicated to providing its listeners with the latest news and updates from the world of rock.
