Margaret Hoffmann (Jan 24, 1875 - Oct 12, 1939) was born January 24, 1875 to Francis Hoffmann and Susanna Heiter in St. Donatus, Iowa.

She married Frank Kraus on November 29, 1892. Together they had two children: Susanna and Elizabeth.

After Frank died, Margaret married Frank Noesen on January 27, 1899. Together they had six children, including: Anna, Katie, Frank, and Joseph.

She died October 12, 1939 in Dubuque, Iowa.
