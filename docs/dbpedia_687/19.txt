Silapathar is a city in Dhemaji district in the Indian state of Assam. The city is situated on the northern bank of the Brahmaputra River, and is located 470 kilometres from the city of Guwahati and just 6 km from border of Arunachal Pradesh.The longest rail cum road bridge in India (Bogibeel bridge)connects silapathar to Dibrugarh.Historical Malinithan mandir is located on its subareas.

It is the commercial hub of Dhemaji district and Arunachal Pradesh, the place has a heady mix of Assamese Mishings Nepalis andBengalis.
