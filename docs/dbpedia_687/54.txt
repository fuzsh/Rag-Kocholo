Silapathar Climate Summary

Located at an elevation of 117.34 meters (384.97 feet) above sea level, Silapathar has a Humid subtropical, dry winter climate (Classification: Cwa). The district’s yearly temperature is 25.49ºC (77.88ºF) and it is -0.48% lower than India’s averages. Silapathar typically receives about 119.04 millimeters (4.69 inches) of precipitation and has 65.09 rainy days (17.83% of the time) annually.
