Liesel Matthews (Born on March 14, 1984) is an American former child actress. She starred as Sara Crewe in A Little Princess, a 1995 adaptation of the Frances Hodgson Burnett classic which was her only Warner Bros role.

She is a member of the wealthy Pritzker family and currently she is an heiress to the Hyatt Hotels fortune, and Philanthropist. She is married to Ian Simmons and resides with her husband in Boston Massachuetts.
