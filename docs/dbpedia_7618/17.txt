Blind Pig Records, based in San Francisco, CA, is a renowned music label that specializes in blues and roots music. With a roster of talented artists, Blind Pig Records continues to captivate audiences with their soulful tunes and authentic sound.

Established in 1977, Blind Pig Records has been a driving force in the blues music industry, consistently delivering high-quality albums and showcasing the best of the genre. Their dedication to preserving the rich heritage of blues music has earned them a loyal following and a reputation as one of the leading record labels in the genre.
