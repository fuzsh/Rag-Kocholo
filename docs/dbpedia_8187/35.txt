Biography

German stage and film actress, cabaret artist, chanson singer, soubrette and operetta singer as well as the founder and director of a cabaret. At the beginning of her vocal training she changed her first name from Gertrud to Gertrude because it had such a poetic sound. She appeared in 89 films between 1917 and 1964. Trude Hesterberg died on 31 August 1967, aged 75, in Munich.
