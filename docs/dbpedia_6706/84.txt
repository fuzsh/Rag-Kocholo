Zeenat Aman

Birth Date: 19 November 1951

About Me

Zeenat Aman (born 19 November 1951) is an Indian actress who has appeared in Hindi films since the 1970s. She was the second runner up in the Miss India Contest and went on to win the Miss Asia Pacific in 1970. Upon making her debut in Bollywood, Zeenat Aman, along with Parveen Babi was credited with making a lasting impact on the image of its leading actresses by bringing the western heroine look to Hindi cinema and throughout her career she has been considered a sex symbol.
