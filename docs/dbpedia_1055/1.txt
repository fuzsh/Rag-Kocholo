Laza Ristovski has been played on NTS in shows including Donna Leake, featured first on 25 June 2016. Songs played include In The Sky, Rumba Balcana and Pulsing Star.

Laza Ristovski was born in 1956 and his first band was BEZIMENI, where he played from 1970-74. As a young and talented keyboard player, during the 1970s and 1980s he played with two mega rock bands in former Yugoslavia - SMAK and BIJELO DUGME.
