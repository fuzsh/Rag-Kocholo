The Trusts Arena is a multi-purpose indoor facility that can host a range of sporting, cultural, trade and community events located just 15 minutes from Auckland. The indoor arena can hosts sports such as basketball and netball with a seating capacity of just under 5,000. Located adjacent to the arena, is the 'Douglas Track and Field' (also known as Waitakere Stadium) which has a seating capacity of 3,000 and hosts athletics, rugby union, rugby league and football.
