Freiburger Münster, Freiburg im Breisgau, Germany

Latitude and longitude coordinates are: 47.995609, 7.852736.

Freiburger Münsteris an old catholic church located at Münsterplatz, in the central part of Freiburg im Breisgau, southwestern Germany. It was built in the late 14th century and for a few centuries it used to be one of the tallest churches in Germany. Today's tower height is close to 380 meters, and its famous with its bells, in total number of 16, with a huge bell names "Hosanna" dated back to the 14th century.
