Kip Christian Moore was born on April 1, 1980 in Tifton, Tift County, Georgia USA to Stanley Moore and Bonnie Mann.

Kip is known for "Mary Was the Marrying Kind", "Somethin' 'Bout a Truck", "Beer Money" and "Pretty Girl" just to name a few.

Kip also co-wrote with fellow artists Thompson Square on their songs "All the Way" and "Let's Fight", James Wesley "Walking Contradiction" and Frankie Ballard "Cigarette"

Kip lost his father Stanley on September 3, 2011.
