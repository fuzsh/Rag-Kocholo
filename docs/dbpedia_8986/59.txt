What is Eric Brittingham's Net Worth?

Eric Brittingham is an American bass guitarist who has a net worth of $6 million. Eric Brittingham was born in Salisbury, Maryland in May 1960. He is best known for being a member of the band Cinderella. The band formed in 1982 and broke up in 2017 and Brittingham was one of two members to stay with the group the entire time. Cinderella released their debut studio album Night Songs in 1986 which reached #3 on the Billboard 200 chart.
