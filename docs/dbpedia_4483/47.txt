About Business Network

NetSuite Inc. is an American cloud computing company founded in 1998 with headquarters in San Mateo, California, that provides software services to help manage business finances, operations, and customer relations. Its services suit both medium and large-sized businesses with consolidated ERP, CRM and e-commerce features.[3] Oracle Corporation offered to purchase NetSuite for approximately $9.3 billion USD in July 2016, with the deal finalizing in November 2016.
