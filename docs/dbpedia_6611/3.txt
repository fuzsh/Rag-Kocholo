Sabine Peters is a German writer born on January 17, 1961, in Neuwied. She studied literature, political science, and philosophy at the University of Hamburg. Since 1989, she was married to writer Christian Geissler until his death. A free writer residing in Hamburg, she is known for her prose and radio plays. Peters has been an active literary critic since 1995 and is a member of the PEN Center Germany. She teaches at Hamburg University of Applied Sciences and has received numerous literary awards.
