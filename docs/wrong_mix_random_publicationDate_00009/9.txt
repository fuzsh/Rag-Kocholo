Early Life and Background

Kamal Haasan was born on November 7, 1954, in Paramakudi, Tamil Nadu, India. He was born into a Tamil Brahmin family and was the youngest of four children. Kamal’s father, D. Srinivasan, was a lawyer and a freedom fighter, while his mother, Rajalakshmi Srinivasan, was a homemaker. Growing up in an intellectually stimulating environment, Kamal developed a keen interest in the arts at a young age.

The Budding Artist

Kamal’s foray into the world of cinema began as a child artist at the tender age of four with the film “Kalathur Kannamma” in 1960. His performance earned him the President’s Gold Medal, a prestigious recognition that set the stage for his illustrious career.

Age and Height

As of 2024, Kamal Haasan is 69 years old. Despite his advancing age, he continues to be a dynamic presence in the entertainment industry. Kamal stands at a height of 5 feet 5 inches (165 cm), proving that stature is defined by talent and achievement rather than physical height.

Family

Kamal Haasan comes from a close-knit family. He has three siblings: two brothers, Charuhasan and Chandrahasan, and a sister, Nalini Raghu. Both of his brothers have also made their mark in the film industry, with Charuhasan being a National Award-winning actor and Chandrahasan serving as a producer and lawyer.

A Legacy of Talent

The Haasan family is often referred to as a family of artists, with each member contributing significantly to Indian cinema and performing arts. Their collective achievements are a testament to their dedication and passion for the arts.

Wife and Relationships

Kamal Haasan’s personal life has been as eventful as his professional career. He has been married twice and has had high-profile relationships that have garnered much public attention.

Vani Ganapathy

Kamal’s first marriage was to classical dancer Vani Ganapathy in 1978. The couple was together for ten years before parting ways in 1988. During their marriage, Vani played a significant role in Kamal’s life, even working as his costume designer for several films.

Sarika Thakur

After his divorce from Vani, Kamal married actress Sarika Thakur in 1988. The couple had two daughters, Shruti Haasan and Akshara Haasan. Unfortunately, this marriage also ended in divorce in 2004. Despite their separation, Kamal and Sarika have remained supportive of each other and their children.

Daughters

Kamal Haasan is the proud father of two talented daughters, Shruti Haasan and Akshara Haasan.

Shruti Haasan

Shruti Haasan, born on January 28, 1986, is a renowned actress and singer in the Indian film industry. She has worked in various Tamil, Telugu, and Hindi films and has earned a reputation for her versatility and talent. Shruti has also made a name for herself in the music industry, showcasing her singing prowess in multiple languages.

Akshara Haasan

Akshara Haasan, born on October 12, 1991, is an actress and assistant director. She made her acting debut in the Hindi film “Shamitabh” in 2015. Akshara has also worked behind the camera, assisting in film production and direction, proving that talent runs deep in the Haasan family.

Movies

Kamal Haasan’s filmography is a testament to his versatility and dedication to the craft of acting. With over 200 films to his credit, he has portrayed a wide range of characters, leaving an indelible mark on Indian cinema.

Early Career

Kamal’s early career saw him taking on diverse roles in Tamil cinema. Some of his notable films from this period include “Apoorva Raagangal” (1975), “Moondram Pirai” (1982), and “Nayakan” (1987), which was listed among TIME magazine’s “All-Time 100 Best Films.”

Iconic Roles

Kamal Haasan is known for his ability to immerse himself in his characters, often going to great lengths to ensure authenticity. Films like “Indian” (1996), “Hey Ram” (2000), “Virumaandi” (2004), and “Vishwaroopam” (2013) showcase his commitment to pushing the boundaries of conventional cinema.

Multilingual Star

Kamal’s proficiency in multiple languages has allowed him to work across various regional film industries in India. He has acted in Tamil, Telugu, Hindi, Malayalam, and Kannada films, earning accolades and a massive fan following in each language.

TV Shows

Awards and Achievements

Kamal Haasan’s contributions to cinema have been recognized with numerous awards and honors. His accolades span across various categories, showcasing his multifaceted talent.

1. National Film Awards

Kamal has won the National Film Award for Best Actor three times for his performances in “Moondram Pirai” (1982), “Nayakan” (1987), and “Indian” (1996). These awards are a testament to his exceptional acting skills and dedication to his craft.

2. Padma Awards

The Government of India has honored Kamal Haasan with the Padma Shri in 1990 and the Padma Bhushan in 2014 for his contributions to Indian cinema. These prestigious awards highlight his impact on the cultural and artistic landscape of the country.

3. Other Notable Awards

Kamal has also received several Filmfare Awards, Tamil Nadu State Film Awards, and international accolades. His film “Hey Ram” was India’s official entry for the Best Foreign Language Film at the Oscars in 2000, showcasing his global appeal.
