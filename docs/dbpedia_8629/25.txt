Who is Al Murray?

Al Murray is one of the UK’s most popular comedians.

What are Al Murray’s main achievements?

From local comedy clubs to some of the nation’s biggest venues, Al Murray is one of the country’s most famous comedians. Whether it is selling out huge arenas or performing for royalty at the Royal Variety Performance, his career has seen him reach highs many comedy acts can only dream of.

To date, he has fronted eight nationwide tours and continues to leave audiences in stitches wherever he performs.

What is Al Murray best known for?

Al Murray is best known for being one of the UK’s greatest comedians. His pub landlord character in particular has become instantly recognisable and has made regular appearances on many of the nation’s biggest comedy programmes, as well as his own show Al Murray’s Happy Hour.
