Percy Gender: Male Number: NWR 6 Basis: Avonside 0-4-0ST Railway: North Western Railway UK Voice Actor: Keith Wickham (2009-2015), Nigel Pilkington (2015-2021), Christopher Ragland (2016; singing voice) US Voice Actor: Martin Sherman (2009-2015), Christopher Ragland (2015-2021) Other Voice Actors: Linda Ballantyne (2000; Thomas and the Magic Railroad)

Percy is a green 0-4-0ST engine who works on the North Western Railway. He was first brought to Sodor to help run the railway while Gordon, James and Henry went on strike and later became station pilot at Knapford. Following the renovation of New Harbour, Percy was moved permanently to Thomas' branch line, where he now works as a goods engine. His favourite job is pulling the mail train and he is Thomas' best friend.
