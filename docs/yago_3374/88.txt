Frederick William IV of Prussia (15 October 1795 – 2 January 1861), the eldest son and successor of Frederick William III of Prussia, was king of Prussia from 7 June 1840 until his death on 2 January 1861.

Marriage[edit]

On 29 November 1823, he married Princess Elisabeth Ludovika of Bavaria, daughter of Maximilian I Joseph of Bavaria and Caroline of Baden. They had no issue.

Titles and styles[edit]

16 November 1797 – 7 June 1840 His Royal Highness The Crown Prince of Prussia.

7 June 1840 – 2 January 1861 His Majesty The King.
