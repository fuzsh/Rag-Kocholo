Surname Marlene

Cognomen Marlene (Marlene , مارلين , Марлен , along with others...) , in all the world, is a common surname. The family name Marlene is characteristic of Latin America and the Caribbean, particularly Nicaragua, where it is a common surname, Venezuela, where it is quite a common surname, and Mexico (explore the name in all countries). More prevalently, Marlene is the forename as well as the last name.
