Marzieh Meshkini (Persian: مرضیه مشکینی‎‎) (born 1969 in Tehran) is an Iranian cinematographer, film director and writer. She is married to film director Mohsen Makhmalbaf who wrote the script for her début film The Day I Became a Woman.
