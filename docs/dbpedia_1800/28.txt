The qualifying match of the world Cup-2022, which will be held on November 14, 2019, in Ashgabat between the national teams of Turkmenistan and the DPRK, will be judged by a team of referees from Thailand.

As reported from the headquarters of the Asian Football Confederation in Kuala Lumpur, 31-year-old referee Sivakorn PU-Udom was appointed as the main referee for this game. Help him to be compatriots of Rawat Renting and Thanet Chuchen. Reserve judge – Mongolica of Petri. Inspector referee – Heat Mombeni (Iran), and the Commissioner of the match – Dylan Jayasuriya Liyanage of Rukantha De Silva (Sri Lanka).
