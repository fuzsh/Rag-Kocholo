Beasley Media has sold Brokered Business 1470 WWNN Pompano Beach FL and translators 95.3 W237BD Boca Raton and 96.9 W245BC Lauderdale Lakes/Fort Lauderdale FL to Marc Paskin’s Marco Broadcasting for $1.25 million.

Paskin most recently operated 104.5 KXXP White Salmon WA/Portland OR via LMA as Soft Oldies “104.5 MeTV-FM” from November 2019 until April 2021. He previously owned 1550 KBUD/94.1 K231BQ Golden/Denver CO in 2015 as a mix of Rock music, Marijuana news, and the Bubba The Love Sponge morning show branded as “Smokin 94.1“.
