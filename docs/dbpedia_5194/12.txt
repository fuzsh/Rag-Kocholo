Weather in Ljungby in June

Temperatures on an average day in Ljungby in June

The average temperature in Ljungby in June for a typical day ranges from a high of 67°F (19°C) to a low of 49°F (10°C). Some would describe it as mildly cool with a gentle breeze.

For comparison, the hottest month in Ljungby, July, has days with highs of 70°F (21°C) and lows of 54°F (12°C). The coldest month, January has days with highs of 31°F (-0°C) and lows of 23°F (-5°C). This graph shows how an average day looks like in Ljungby in June based on historical data.
