Warner Music Group

Founded in 1958, Warner Music Group is an entertainment and record label company. Warner Music is home to several record labels including Asylum, Atlantic, Big Beat, Canvasback, East West, Elektra, Erato, FFRR, Fueled by Ramen, Nonesuch, Parlophone, Reprise, Rhino, Roadrunner, Sire, Warner Bros., Warner Classics and Warner Music Nashville, as well as Warner/Chappell Music. The company is headquartered in New York City, New York.
