I. Quick Facts about SmarTone Macau

SmarTone is one of the largest mobile network operators in Macau, providing both residential and enterprise telecommunication services. As a subsidiary of the renowned SmarTone Telecommunications Holdings Limited from Hong Kong, SmarTone Macau has made a significant impact in the market since its establishment in 2001.

With a strong presence in Macau, SmarTone Macau strives to provide extensive network coverage across the region. Its dedication to quality service and customer satisfaction has earned it a solid reputation in the telecommunications industry in Macau.

V. How to Register SmarTone SIM Cards?

Here are the steps to register your prepaid SmarTone SIM cards on the real name registration system:

Go to the registration website https://www.smartone.com/jsp/mo/english/real_name_registration.jsp ↗ or scan the QR code provided.

If you are a new user, insert or scan the 15-digit number found on your prepaid SIM card. If you are an existing user who has registered before, insert your prepaid card mobile number and the SMS verification code sent to that number.

Upload a clear copy of your valid government-issued photo ID such as passport or ID card. You will also need to take and upload a selfie photo for identity verification purposes.

Fill in the required personal information fields such as name, ID number, date of birth etc. Read and agree to the Terms and Conditions of the registration.

Once you have submitted all the required information and documents, your registration will be processed. You will get a confirmation message if registration is successful.

Your prepaid SIM card will now be properly activated for use upon successful registration.

You can contact SmarTone customer service at 1628 if you face any issues or have additional questions during the registration process.

IX. Where to Buy SmarTone SIM Cards?

Important Note: Regarding the implementation of the Cyber Security Law in Macau SAR, individual customers can only register a maximum of 10 prepaid cards, while associations can register up to 25 prepaid cards.

Here are a few options for where you can buy SmarTone SIM cards in Macau:

SmarTone Stores – SmarTone has their own retail stores located throughout Macau where you can purchase SIM cards. Some locations include Shoppes @ Four Seasons, Macau Forum shopping mall, and Venetian Macao.

Major Carriers Stores – While SmarTone-branded, SIM cards are also sold at major carrier stores run by other companies. Try stores operated by China Telecom or China Mobile.

Convenience Stores – Convenience stores like 7-Eleven often carry a selection of prepaid SIM cards from all the major carriers, including SmarTone. Check larger branches.

Mobile phone shops – Independent mobile phone shops throughout Macau will usually stock Smartone and other carrier SIM cards for sale. Try shops in Senado Square or near hotel casinos.

Airport – Upon arrival at Macau International Airport, you can purchase a SmarTone SIM from vending machines located past customs and baggage claim.

Online – SmarTone’s website ships SIM cards to a Macau address, but in-person purchase is easier. Consider asking your hotel concierge to assist if ordering online.
