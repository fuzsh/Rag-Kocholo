Recognition, .

The United States recognized the Republic of Korea, with its capital at Seoul, as an independent state on January 1, 1949, in a White House statement.

Establishment of Diplomatic Relations, .

Diplomatic relations between the United States and the Republic of Korea (South Korea) were established on March 25, 1949, when Ambassador Dr. John Myun Chang, presented his credentials to President Truman.
