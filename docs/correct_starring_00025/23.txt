High School Musical 2 is the soundtrack album for the 2007 Disney Channel Original Movie of the same name. It serves as the follow-up to the highly successful "High School Musical" soundtrack. The album features songs performed by the cast of the movie, including Zac Efron, Vanessa Hudgens, Ashley Tisdale, and others.

The album contains a mix of catchy pop songs and energetic musical numbers that reflect the film's storyline. It explores themes of summer adventures, friendship, and romance, capturing the spirit of the high school characters as they navigate their summer vacation at a country club.

Upon its release, the "High School Musical 2" album achieved significant commercial success. It debuted at the top position on the Billboard 200 chart, making it the first soundtrack album from a television movie to achieve such a feat since 2006. The album's success was fueled by the popularity of the movie and the strong fan base that had developed around the franchise.

Several songs from the album also charted on the Billboard Hot 100, including "What Time Is It," "Fabulous," "You Are the Music in Me," and "Gotta Go My Own Way." The soundtrack's lead single, "What Time Is It," peaked at number 6 on the Hot 100, becoming one of the highest-charting songs from the "High School Musical" series.
