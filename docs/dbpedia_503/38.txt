Escuintla

Maps, pictures and statistics of Escuintla, a beautiful city of 10,624 inhabitants of the State of Chiapas

The town of Escuintla belongs to the municipality of Escuintla (State of Chiapas). There are 10,624 inhabitants and it is 77 meters altitude. In the list of the most populated towns of the whole municipality, it is the number #1 of the ranking.
