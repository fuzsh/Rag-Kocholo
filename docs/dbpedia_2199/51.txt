The song "Miracle" by Whitney Houston is about the power of love in our lives. It suggests that when we have love inside us, nothing else should matter. The lyrics are written from the perspective of someone who is struggling with pain and regret, but still holds on to hope for a better future. Whitney sings about how she made a choice that led her to feel immense pain, but she believes that there is still a miracle in store for her. She encourages us to hold on to our miracles and not let them slip away. The song suggests that miracles can come in the form of love, and that we all have the power to create our own miracles. Overall, "Miracle" is an uplifting and inspiring song that reminds us of the power of love and the importance of holding on to hope, even in difficult times. It encourages us to believe that there is always a miracle waiting for us, if we are willing to open our hearts and let love in.
