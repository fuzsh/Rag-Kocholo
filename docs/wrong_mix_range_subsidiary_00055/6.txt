The Southeastern Pennsylvania Transportation Authority (SEPTA) operates subway and elevated rail, commuter rail, trolley and trackless trolley service in five counties in the Philadelphia, Pennsylvania region.

The subway system consist of 3 heavy rail lines, 1 light rail lines system and 2 light rail-trolly lines system.

SEPTA's commuter rail service is run by the SEPTA Regional Rail, operates 13 lines serving more than 150 stations covering most of the five county southeastern Pennsylvania region.
