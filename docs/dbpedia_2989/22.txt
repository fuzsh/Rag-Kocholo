Günter Zöller is a German figure skating coach and former competitor for East Germany. He is the 1970 World bronze medalist, the 1970 European bronze medalist, a five-time Blue Swords champion, and a five-time East German national champion.

Günter Zöller was born on May 21, 1948, in Chemnitz, Saxony, Germany. His birth geographical coordinates are 50° 50’ 9” North latitude and 12° 55’ 45” East longitude. Günter Zöller is currently 76 years old.

Astrologically, Günter Zöller falls under the Sun sign of Gemini, with Leo as his Ascendant, Scorpio as his Moon sign, and Aquarius as his Black Moon Lilith sign.
