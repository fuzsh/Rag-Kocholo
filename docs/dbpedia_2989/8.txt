Gertrud Caspari was one of the most important German children’s book illustrators in the first half of the 20th century, and is recognised as the creator of a “modern toddler” or “Caspari” style.

Gertrud Caspari was born on March 22, 1873, in Chemnitz, Saxony, Germany. Her birth geographical coordinates are 50° 50’ 9” North latitude and 12° 55’ 45” East longitude. Gertrud Caspari passed away at the age of 75 in 1948.

Astrologically, Gertrud Caspari falls under the Sun sign of Aries, with Cancer as her Ascendant, Capricorn as her Moon sign, and Leo as her Black Moon Lilith sign.
