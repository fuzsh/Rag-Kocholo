Rudolf Lebius

(1868-1946)

German journalist (1868–1946) – Rudolf Lebius was born in Sovetsk (City in Kaliningrad Oblast, Russia) on January 4th, 1868 and died in Berlin (Federated state, capital and largest city of Germany) on April 4th, 1946 at the age of 78. Today Rudolf Lebius would be 156 years old.
