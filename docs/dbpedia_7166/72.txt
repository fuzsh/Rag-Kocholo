Per Wahlöö was born on 5 August 1926 in Gothenburg, Västra Götalands län, Sweden. He was a writer, known for Der Schlafwagenmörder (1967), Der Mann auf dem Dach (1976) and Nattmara - Der Killer von Stockholm (1965). He was married to Sylvia Nilsson and Inger Andersson. He died on 22 June 1975 in Malmö, Skåne län, Sweden.
