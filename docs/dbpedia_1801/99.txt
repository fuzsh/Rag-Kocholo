Kathryn Helena Irvine was born on September 29, 1894. She married Frank Lillard in 1919. Kathryn died on March 2, 1920, four days after giving birth to Kathryn Anita ‘Katie.’

Frank Zachary Lillard, b. March 16, 1889 d. August 24, 1967.

In 1916 he was a lawyer in Los Angeles and his office was located in the historic Hellman Building. His office address was listed as 417 H. W. Hellman Building, Los Angeles. He was also registered as a member of the Delta Chi Fraternity. He was at USC as a freshman in 1909/10.

On December 13, 1917 he enlisted in the Navy reaching the rank of Yeoman 2/C in the Navy. When he returned from WWI, he married Kathryn Irvine 1919 and resided in Orange , CA.

On September 12, 1967 he was interned in the Los Angeles National Cemetery (also known as Sawtelle Soldiers Cemetery). He is buried in section 276 row C site 22.
