Sivakorn Lertchuchot, also known as Guy, is a Thai actor born on August 27, 1992. He is signed under GMMTV and is recognized for his roles in popular television series such as SOTUS S: The Series (2017), 2gether: The Series (2020), and Mama Gogo (2022). His work in these series has contributed to his fame in the Thai entertainment industry.

Meaning of the name - Sivakorn

The name Sivakorn is a combination of two Thai words: 'Siva' meaning 'mountain' and 'korn' meaning 'seed' or 'grain'. Together, it translates to 'mountain seed' or 'mountain grain', symbolizing strength, resilience, and the potential for growth.
