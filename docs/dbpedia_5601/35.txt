Wise Genealogy

OriginsThe surname 'Wise' is of English origin and is an occupational name for a wise or knowledgeable person. It derives from the Old English word 'wis', meaning 'wise' or 'knowing'.

Geographic DistributionThe surname 'Wise' is most commonly found in the United States, followed by the United Kingdom and Australia. It is also present in Canada, Germany, South Africa, and other English-speaking countries.
