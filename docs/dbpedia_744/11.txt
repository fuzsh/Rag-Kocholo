This distinctive route takes you on a thrilling gravel adventure through the beautiful countryside of Campania near Roccadaspide. With a total distance of 87 kilometers and an ascent of 2946 meters, it offers a challenging ride for experienced cyclists. The route explores various highlights along the way, including Castel San Lorenzo, Laurino, Monte Calvello, Tempa della Lanza, Cannalonga, Rupa Rossa, Magliano Vetere, and Monte Varco Cervone. These attractions offer stunning views, historical landmarks, and natural beauty, making the journey even more rewarding. Overall, this gravel circuit provides an epic and exciting cycling experience.
