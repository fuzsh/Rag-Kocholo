3rd Degree is an extremely versatile silicone product that can either resurface skin, or create small out-of-kit effects, and can even be poured into molds. It can be thinned with 99% Alcohol when uncured to blend into the skin. Perfect for prosthetics and Special FX.

What it is:

3rd Degree is a two-part platinum-based silicone that can be applied directly on skin or ran through a mold. Used to a multitude of effects such as cuts, bullet wounds, burns, bumps and much more. Use silicone or universal tints to color silicone.

Ingredients include a proprietary blend of Platinum Silicone and Curing Agent.

Clear / Light / Medium / Dark / Blood Red
