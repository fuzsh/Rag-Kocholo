Home Details

This lovely and spacious mobile home presents an inviting open concept floor plan, featuring a great room open to the kitchen and dining area. Located in Casa Del Rio Mobile Estates, a 55+ senior park, this residence features 2 bedrooms, 2 baths, and an additional office/craft room with laundry area. The primary bedroom offers ample space, while its ensuite primary bathroom offers a double vanity, soaking tub, and a separate shower. Step outside to the expansive yard with decking, providing an ideal setting for weekend gatherings. Residents of Casa Del Rio Mobile Estates community enjoy access to various amenities including a clubhouse, swimming pool, spa, parks, and more. Conveniently located near walking and biking trails, shopping, and with easy access to the 101 freeway and Vandenberg Space Force Base.
