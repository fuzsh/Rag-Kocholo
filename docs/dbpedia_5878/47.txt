What is Mike Herrera's Net Worth?

Mike Herrera is an American singer, songwriter, and musician who has a net worth of $1 million. Mike Herrera was born in Bremerton, Washington in November 1976. He is best known for being the lead vocalist and bassist for the punk rock band MxPx. He has also been the frontman of the band Tumbledown and the bass player for the band Goldfinger. MxPx formed in 1992 and released their debut studio album Pokinatcha in 1994.

They released the albums Teenage Politics in 1995 and Life in General in 1996. Their album Slowly Going the Way of the Buffalo reached #2 on the US Christian chart in 1998 and their album The Ever Passing Moment reached #1 on the same chart in 2000. The band released the album Before Everything & After in 2003 and their albums Panic in 2005 and Secret Weapon in 2007 both reached #1 on the US Christian chart. They have also released the albums On the Cover II in 2009, Punk Raw Christmas in 2009, Plans Within Plans in 2012, Life in General 2.0 in 2016, and MxPx in 2018.
