Slam-bang martial-arts Western follows in the bootsteps of "Billy Jack" and "Walking Tall." Dolph Lundgren wrote, directed and stars as a mysterious biker who preaches to Native-American children when not dispensing his own brand of frontier justice to redneck locals who want to turn the tribe's reservation into a casino and will stop at nothing to get their way. John Enos III, Matthew Tompkins, Brad Imes, Jeff Krebs.
