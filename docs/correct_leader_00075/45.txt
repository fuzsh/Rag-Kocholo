Former Finnish Prime Minister, Centre Party MP Anneli Jäätteenmäki may make a political comeback at the European elections.

"If she is available, I believe that the attitude towards her is very positive", said Centre Party secretary Eero Lankia according to Helsingin Sanomat.

On 26 February the Centre Party is to nominate its candidates for the elections, which will be held on 13 June.

Anneli Jäätteenmäki resigned as PM in June 2003 after only two months in office.
