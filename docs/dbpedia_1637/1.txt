(2002)

(A book in the Doctor Who series)

In the London of 2012, the Doctor and Ace are called through time to south-east Scotland where they find themselves at Graystairs, an Alzheimer's clinic and place of healing. The patients there seem to be gaining a new lease of life. But whose life is it?
