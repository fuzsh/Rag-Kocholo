SAN ANTONIO - San Antonio Spurs 2005 draft pick, Ian Mahinmi, has announced his retirement during a chat with NBAextra.

Mahinmi spent 12 years in the NBA and three seasons with the Spurs. He'd miss the full 2008-09 season due to an ankle injury.

In two playing seasons with San Antonio, he'd average 3.8 points per game including 1.8 rebounds, and 61% field-goal shooting. He'd go on to play with Dallas, Indiana, and Washington before his retirement. He won an NBA title in 2011 with the Mavericks.
