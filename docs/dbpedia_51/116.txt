Nicholas Le Prevost was born on 18 March 1947 in Wiltshire, England, UK. He is an actor, known for Shakespeare in Love (1998), Generazione perduta (2014) and Up the Garden Path (1990). He has been married to Aviva Goldkorn since July 1984. They have two children.

He was nominated for a 2002 Laurence Olivier Theatre Award for Best Performance in a Supporting Role in a Musical or Entertainment of 2001 for his performance in "My Fair Lady" performed at the Royal National Theatre: Lyttelton and Theatre Royal: Drury Lane.
