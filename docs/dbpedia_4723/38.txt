Vodohod Limited Liability Company is a shipping company, the largest Russian operator of river cruises and a full-cycle tourist operator, combining under its management all links of the service chain - transportation, catering, excursion services, entertainment programs, staff training.

The company organizes cruises from Moscow, St. Petersburg, Samara, Nizhny Novgorod and Kazan along the main rivers of the European part of Russia and popular tourist routes, as well as expedition cruises along the Yenisei River.
