Tyumen, Tyumen Province, Russia

Latitude and longitude coordinates are: 57.161297, 65.525017.

Tyumen is a large city and the capital of Tyumen Province in central Russia, located about 16 hundred miles east of Moscow. The city was established in the 16th century and is considered to be one of the first and the fastest developing cities in Siberia, along with Novosibirsk and Irkutsk. Tyumen is an industrial and business center of the region, with plenty of factories and industrial corporations. Located near the border with Kazakhstan, the city is an important transportation spot.
