Petroglyph - About the company

What does Petroglyph do?

Petroglyph Games is a game development studio based in Las Vegas, US. The studio focuses on the development of AAA quality games for PC and console platforms and has a portfolio of over 10 games as on January 2017. Popular games from the studio include 'Battle Battallions' - a strategy battle game for PC platforms and 'Gre Goo' - a real-time strategy game.
