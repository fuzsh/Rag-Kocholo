Jane Wall is an English actress. She is best known in the U.K for her four seasons on ITV's BAFTA award winning show The Bill. Jane was born in Nigeria and raised in London, United Kingdom. She trained at Mountview Academy of Theatre Arts and with Sam Kogan at The School of the Science of Acting. Television includes Ghost Whisperer (CBS), Holby City (BBC), Little Napoleans (CH4) and Touch of Frost (ITV).

Film Includes Cloud Cuckoo Land (with Sir Derek Jacobi), Beneath the Blue (Paradise Films), Killing Boyfriends, A Year and a Day, and Bliss.
