Bill Johnson is Co-Chairman of Lotus Entertainment, a top tier production, finance and international sales company which seeks to finance and produce 4 to 5 feature films per year in addition to handling international sales on other productions.

Johnson's film passion is to create commercially viable films which are positive, inspirational and raise consciousness.

Johnson founded and is majority shareholder of a successful online insurance venture called Clickinsure.

On the philanthropic side, Johnson founded a public charity called The Enlightenment Network which has the mission of supporting the arts within disadvantaged youth communities.

Johnson is a native of Chicago and was an All-American tennis player in college.

He currently lives on the beach in Venice, California with his wife Leah. He has three children, Skyler, Jett and Jade.

After years of in-depth study and practice of yoga and meditation, Johnson has taught yoga in recent years.
