Helmstedt is a town in Lower Saxony, Germany, located at the confluence of the rivers Aller and Mittelland Canal. The town has a rich history dating back to the Middle Ages, when it was a member of the Hanseatic League. Today, Helmstedt is known for its university and its many historical buildings. The weather in Helmstedt is temperate, with warm summers and cool winters.
