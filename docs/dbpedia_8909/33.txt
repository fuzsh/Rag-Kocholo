Braceface is an American-Canadian-Chinese animated television series that aired on Teletoon in Canada and the Fox Family Channel, Disney Channel & Toon Disney in the United States. The series was produced by Nelvana and Jade Animation, with actress Alicia Silverstone of Clueless fame (who also voiced the titular character for the first two seasons) serving as executive producer.

Plot[]

13 year-old Sharon Spits navigates life with braces.
