Courteney Bass Cox (born June 15, 1964) is an American actress, producer, and director. She gained worldwide recognition for her starring role as Monica Geller on the NBC sitcom Friends (1994–2004) and as Gale Weathers in the horror film series Scream (1996–present). Her notable role is the woman from the audience from Bruce Springsteen's music video Dancing In The Dark.

For Nickelodeon, she voiced Daisy in Barnyard.
