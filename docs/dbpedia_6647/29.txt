Boxing Legends of the Ring is a boxing video game developed and published by Sculptured Software, and released in 1993 for the Super Nintendo Entertainment System (SNES). The game features several modes, including Exhibition, Career, and Championship, where players can create their own boxer and work their way up the ranks to become a champion.

The game also includes a training mode where players can practice their moves and combos. The graphics and sound are considered to be of high quality for its time, and the game is well-regarded by boxing fans and gamers alike for its accurate portrayal of the sport.
