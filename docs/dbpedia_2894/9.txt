Privilege Ibiza is one of Ibiza’s most popular clubs. It is a superclub with a capacity of 10.000 people, a total space of 69.968 sq. ft. (6.500 m2) and two rooms: the Main Room and the Vista Club. Located close to the village of San Rafael in Ibiza, Privilege Ibiza is a nightclub that offers massive parties with spectacular shows of dancers, acrobats, and the best international DJ’s of the Electronic Dance Music scenario.
