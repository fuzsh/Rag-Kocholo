This photograph shows Chateaugay, with Hall of Fame rider Braulio Baeza aboard, holding off Never Bend and Candy Spots to win the 1963 Kentucky Derby at historic Churchill Downs. The "Run For The Roses" is considered America's premier horse race, always drawing crowds in excess of 100,000. This horse race has earned the nickname of being the "Fastest Two Minutes In Sports".
