Directors: Michael Dowse, 2002

Stars: David Lawrence, Paul Spence, Gordon Skilling

Out to buy and rent: On DVD

FUBAR stands for “F***** Up Beyond All Recognition”, which is a good description of the two Canadian headbangers whose lives are documented in what could be the funniest rock’n’roll movie since This is Spinal Tap.

There’s not much to do in Calgary, but Dean (Spence) and Terry (Lawrence) have simple ambitions: drinking (they’re never further than an arm’s length from their next can of beer), fighting, music (heavy metal, of course) and breaking stuff. For the young film-maker Farrel Mitchener (Skilling), whose self-belief far outshines his talent, they are also the perfect documentary subjects.
