Lottie Mae Williams, 81, of Chesapeake, VA entered into eternal rest on April 27, 2019. She was born January 6, 1938 in North Carolina to the late Archie and Irene Hill. Lottie graduated from Booker T. Washington High School, Norfolk, VA, in May of 1956. She participated in plays and enjoyed dancing while attending school.

On December 26, 1957 she married Joseph B. Williams and to that union five children were born. Lottie was a Certified Nurse at the Veterans Hospital in Hampton, VA for many years. She later changed careers and worked as a packer for the US Navy. She enjoyed shopping and tending to her flower garden.

Besides her parents Lottie was predeceased by her sister, Sarah Hill.
