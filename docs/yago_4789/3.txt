Destroy All Monsters (怪獣総進撃 Kaijū Sōshingeki, lit. "Monster Total Advancement") is a 1968 tokusatsu kaiju film directed by Ishiro Honda and written by Honda with Kaoru Mabuchi, with special effects by Sadamasa Arikawa. Produced by Toho, it is the ninth installment in the Godzilla series as well as the Showa series. It stars Akira Kubo, Yukiko Kobayashi, Kyoko Ai, Jun Tazaki, Yoshio Tsuchiya, and Kenji Sahara. The film was released to Japanese theaters on August 1, 1968. American International Pictures released an edited English-dubbed version of the film to American theaters on May 28, 1969.
