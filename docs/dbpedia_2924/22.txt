Avex Trax (エイベックス トラックス Eibekkusu Torakkusu) is a Japanese music label set up in 1990. It is owned by Avex Group, a Japanese entertainment group. Some artists under the Avex Group include Tokyo Girls' Style (東京女子流 Tōkyō Joshi Ryū), Girls Generation (少女時代 Shojo Jidai), 2NE1, After School (アフタースクール), U-KISS (ユーキス), and Acid Black Cherry (アシッドブラックチェリー).

They handle the Japanese DVD releases of Transformers: Prime.
