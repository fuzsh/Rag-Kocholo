Avex Inc. (エイベックス株式会社) is a Japanese entertainment conglomerate established in 1988.

Several of Avex's labels have either published Sega-related music or have been featured in video games. Avex Trax is the group's oldest record label, debuting in 1990, while Avex Mode was typically associated with animation.
