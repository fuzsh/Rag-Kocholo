Down Under From the episode: Long John Peter Singers: Peter Griffin Voices: Seth MacFarlane

"Down Under", also known as "Land Down Under", is a pop song recorded by Men at Work for their debut album Business as Usual. The song went to #1 on American, British, and Australian charts.

In "Long John Peter", Peter Griffin is the musical entertainment for the date he set up between his son Chris and his friend, Barbara, singing a song by Men at Work.
