Original U.S. One-Sheet Poster (27x41) Folded Theater Used Fine Condition

United Artists Original U.S. One-Sheet Poster, theater-used and absolutely authentic, for the Ted Kotcheff directed drama BILLY TWO HATS (1974). The film stars Gregory Peck and Desi Arnaz, Jr. They were supported by a very good cast including Jack Warden, David Huddleton, Dawn Little Sky and Henry Medicine Hat. After a bank robbery, runaway Scottish outlaw Arch Deans and his young half breed Kiowa partner Billy Two Hats develop a father-son relationship but Sheriff Henry Gifford is determined to capture or kill them.
