YEADON, PA – A teenage girl is missing, and she was last seen in Delaware County, according to Philadelphia Police.

Police said Talia Mayhew, 16, was last seen on Tuesday, Jan. 1 on the 1900 block of Heather Grove in Yeadon Borough.

Mayhew is 5 feet 1 inch tall and weighs 110 pounds.

She has a thin build, medium complexion, with brown hair braided below the shoulder.

When she was last seen she was wearing a blue coat, blue jacket, pink sneakers.

Anyone with any information on Mayhew’s whereabouts is asked to please contact Southwest Detective Division at (215) 686-3183 or 911.
