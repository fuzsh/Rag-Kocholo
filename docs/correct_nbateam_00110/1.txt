Jun 24, 2004 - The New Orleans Hornets selected J.R. Smith in Round 1 with Pick 18 in the 2004 NBA Draft. Jul 6, 2004 - J.R. Smith signed a multi-year contract with the New Orleans Hornets. Jul 14, 2006 - J.R. Smith was acquired by the Chicago Bulls, along with P.J. Brown, from the New Orleans Pelicans in exchange for Tyson Chandler. Jul 20, 2006 - J.R. Smith was acquired by the Denver Nuggets from the Chicago Bulls in exchange for Howard Eisley, a 2007 2nd round pick (DEN own) and a Least Favorable 2007 2nd round pick. Oct 28, 2006 - The Denver Nuggets exercised their Team Option to extend the contract of J.R. Smith. Jun 27, 2008 - The Denver Nuggets tendered a Qualifying Offer to make J.R. Smith a Restricted FA. Jul 1, 2008 - J.R. Smith, previously with the Denver Nuggets, became a free agent. Aug 21, 2008 - J.R. Smith signed a multi-year contract with the Denver Nuggets. Jul 1, 2011 - J.R. Smith, previously with the Denver Nuggets, became a free agent. Feb 17, 2012 - J.R. Smith signed a multi-year contract with the New York Knicks. Jul 1, 2012 - J.R. Smith, previously with the New York Knicks, became a free agent. Jul 11, 2012 - J.R. Smith signed a multi-year contract with the New York Knicks. Jul 1, 2013 - J.R. Smith, previously with the New York Knicks, became a free agent. Jul 10, 2013 - J.R. Smith signed a multi-year contract with the New York Knicks. Jan 5, 2015 - J.R. Smith was acquired by the Cleveland Cavaliers, along with Iman Shumpert and a conditional 2015 1st round pick (OKC own), from the New York Knicks in exchange for Lou Amundson, Alex Kirk, Dion Waiters, a 2019 2nd round pick (CLE own) and a DPE. Jul 1, 2015 - J.R. Smith, previously with the Cleveland Cavaliers, became a free agent. Sep 2, 2015 - J.R. Smith signed a multi-year contract with the Cleveland Cavaliers. Jul 1, 2016 - J.R. Smith, previously with the Cleveland Cavaliers, became a free agent. Oct 15, 2016 - J.R. Smith signed a multi-year contract with the Cleveland Cavaliers. Jul 15, 2019 - The Cleveland Cavaliers placed the contract of J.R. Smith on waivers. Jul 17, 2019 - J.R. Smith, previously with the Cleveland Cavaliers, became a free agent.
