Twitter HQ, San Francisco, CA, USA

Latitude and longitude coordinates are: 37.776463, -122.417213.

Twitter's San Francisco headquarters is located at 1355 Market Street, Downtown San Francisco, California, United States. Twitter was founded by Jack Dorsey, Noah Glass, Biz Stone, and Evan Williams on March 21, 2006. Twitter, Inc. is based in San Francisco and has more than 25 offices around the globe. Twitter's San Francisco HQ is close to many attractions in the city such as Bill Graham Civic Auditorium, San Francisco Symphony, Rickshaw Stop, San Francisco Opera, San Francisco War Memorial & Performing Arts Center, Sydney Goldstein Theater, Civic Center Plaza, San Francisco City Hall, Herbst Theatre, SFJAZZ, San Francisco Public Library, and so on.

* Jack Dorsey, the co-founder of Twitter and Square, Inc., resigned from Twitter on Monday, 29 November 2021. He will be replaced by the chief executive officer of Twitter, Parag Agrawal.
