Baghdad, October 12, 2005 - On Tuesday, 11 October 2005, the Special Representative of the Secretary General met the Iraqi Prime Minster, Ibrahim Al-Jafari at the Prime Minister's Office. The meeting, focused on the latest political and constitutional developments, as well as UNAMI's role in Iraq.

The Iraqi Prime Minister reiterated his appreciation for United Nations' presence in Iraq at a critical moment, and for the work it was doing. He urged the UN to continue playing its role in promoting peace and conflict resolution.

On the referendum, the SRSG expressed the importance of ensuring an atmosphere in which the people of Iraq could exercise their choice free from fear and intimidation.

The SRSG also discussed the status issue of Arab Nationals, particularly those of Palestinian refugees living in Iraq, as well as some of the difficulties they face.
