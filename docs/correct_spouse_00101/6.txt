A terrible loss.

It is with sadness that Soaps.com reports the passing of Jennie Lee Enos, wife of actor John Enos, who played the popular character, Bobby Marsino, on “The Young and the Restless” from 2003 – 2005. John’s Facebook wall filled with condolences from fans, friends, and loved ones after he posted that he’d lost the love of his life. Jennie, who married John in 2006, died on February 18 following a long illness. She is survived by a sister and her mother, as well as her husband and their beloved pet dogs, Duke and Bruno. A funeral service is planned for tomorrow.

Soaps.com extends sincere condolences to John, and to his and Jennie’s loved ones.
