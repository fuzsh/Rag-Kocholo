About Dallon Weekes

Dallon James Weekes is an American musician, singer-songwriter and record producer best recognized as a bassist, songwriter and keyboardist for the rock band, Panic! At the Disco (2009-2017).

In 2010, he released the single titled, Skid Row.

Family Life

He tied the knot with Breezy Douglas in 2006 and welcomed a daughter known as Amelie Olivia in 2008 and a son named Knox Oliver in 2010.

Achievements

Weekes earned a nomination at the 2015 Alternative Press Music Awards for Best Bassist.

Trivia

As at 2020, Weekes is a member of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints.
