Washington-on-the-Brazos is a historic site located on the banks of the Brazos River in Texas. This site holds significant importance as it was here that the Republic of Texas was proclaimed in 1836, marking a pivotal moment in the history of Texas. Visitors to this site can learn about the events that led to the formation of the Republic of Texas and the role this location played in shaping the state's history.

Located between Brenham and Navasota, off Texas State Route 105, the site also includes a museum and a library, offering tourists a comprehensive understanding of the history of the area.
