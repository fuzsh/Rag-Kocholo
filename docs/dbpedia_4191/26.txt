Based on the gamepad included with the European version of the Atari 7800 released in 1987, the CX78+ Gamepad features two fire buttons and a small thumbstick (included) that can be optionally screwed into the d-pad, offering more flexibility when using this controller with your favorite games.

The CX78+ features:

- Two buttons, allowing you to play 7800 games

- Removable thumbstick, allowing you to play in two different ways

- Five (5) foot cord

- Finished plastic that looks and feels authentic

Compatible with the new Atari 2600+ and all original Atari systems.
