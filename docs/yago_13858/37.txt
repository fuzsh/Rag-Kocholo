Bryce Dallas Howard, who raised an army of baby vampires in the third Twilight film Eclipse, is adding to her own -- human -- brood.

Howard, 30, and her husband, Fringe actor Seth Gabel, are expecting their second child, Us Weekly reports.

The couple were married in 2006 and welcomed their first son, Theo in February 2007. Following her son's birth, Howard detailed her postpartum depression on Gwyneth Paltrow's GOOP blog.
