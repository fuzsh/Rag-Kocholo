Taruni Sachdev was born in Mumbai to a city-based industrialist. Taruni acted in several television advertisements for products such as Rasna, Colgate, ICICI Bank, Reliance Mobile, LG, Coffee Bite, etc. As a child actor, she has acted along with Amitabh Bachchan, Abhishek Bachchan and Vidya Balan in the Hindi film Paa . She was the highest paid child artist of her time with over 50 commercials to her credit and is most remembered for her appearance as the Rasna girl with Karishma Kapoor. Taruni Sachdev died in the plane crash in Nepal, on 14 May 2012, Taruni's mother Geetha Sachdev who accompanied her on the flight also died.
