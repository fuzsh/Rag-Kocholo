Arizona Christian University is a private, religious university in the suburb of Glendale, Arizona. Arizona Christian University was founded in 1960. The university enrolls around 934 students a year, including 961 undergraduates.

Arizona Christian University Acceptance Rate

Arizona Christian University admitted 74% of all applicants in 2022. In 2022, 749 students applied to Arizona Christian University and 557 were admitted.

Arizona Christian University Campus Tours

Arizona Christian University is located in the town of Glendale, Arizona, near Phoenix.

If you would like to see Arizona Christian University for yourself, be sure to plan a visit! The best way to reach campus is to take Arizona State Route 101 to Glendale, or book a flight to the nearest airport, Phoenix Sky Harbor International.
