Chris Penn (1965-2006) was an American actor known for his roles in movies such as The Wild Life, Reservoir Dogs, and Rush Hour. He was the brother of actor Sean Penn and musician Michael Penn. Penn was a skilled character actor and often portrayed tough characters, villains, or working-class thugs, as well as comedic roles. He won the Volpi Cup for Best Actor for his performance in The Funeral. Additionally, Penn provided the voice of corrupt cop Edward 'Eddie' Pulaski in the video game Grand Theft Auto: San Andreas. Sadly, Penn passed away at the age of 40 due to nonspecific cardiomyopathy (heart disease).
