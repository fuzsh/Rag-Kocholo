Christopher Shannon "Chris" Penn was an American film and television actor known for his roles in such iconic films as To Wong Foo, Thanks for Everything, Julie Newmar with Patrick Swayze and Wesley Snipes, Footloose with Kevin Bacon and John Lithgow, All the Right Moves with Tom Cruise, Reservoir Dogs, True Romance, and Pale Rider.

Chris is the younger brother of actor Sean Penn and musician Michael Penn, and the former brother-in-law of singer Madonna. He never married, but from 1993 to 1999, he dated and lived with Filipino-American model Steffiana de la Cruz.

A lovable actor, Chris was found deceased in his Santa Monica California home; his death was ruled an accident by complications with an enlarged heart and his medications.
