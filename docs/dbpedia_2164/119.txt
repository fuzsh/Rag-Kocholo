Synopsis

After a seven-year wait since the captivating visuals and mesmerizing narratives of "Planet Earth II" graced our screens in 2016, and more than 17 years since the original "Planet Earth" wowed audiences in 2006, the beloved British nature documentary series is making a triumphant return with "Planet Earth III."

It's been a patient seven years since the enchanting visuals and mesmerizing narratives of "Planet Earth II" graced our screens in 2016, and over 17 years since the original "Planet Earth" wowed audiences in 2006. Now, the beloved British nature documentary series is making a triumphant return with "Planet Earth III." This stunning documentary is set to air on BBC America and stream online, promising a captivating journey through the natural world narrated by the legendary Sir David Attenborough.

Where and How to Watch "Planet Earth III"

On TV: For traditional television viewing, 'Planet Earth III' will be broadcast on the cable network BBC America. It's as simple as tuning in through your cable TV provider or visiting BBCAmerica.com, where you can watch the mesmerizing docuseries by logging in with your cable TV account details.

Without Cable: If you've cut the cord, worry not. "Planet Earth III" will be available for streaming on BBC America and AMC+. The series is scheduled to air every Saturday, commencing on November 4, at 8 p.m. ET/PT.
