Tajana - Tajana (also called Tajana Mandal) is a village, situated in Bahawalnagar District, Punjab, Pakistan. It consists 500 homes.

Tajanak, Amol - Tajanak (Persian: تجنك‎; also known as Tajnīk) is a village in Pain Khiyaban-e Litkuh Rural District, in the Central District of Amol County, Mazandaran Province, Iran.

Tajanak-e Olya - Tajanak-e Olya (Persian: تجنك عليا‎, also Romanized as Tajanak-e ‘Olyā) is a village in Mazkureh Rural District, in the Central District of Sari County, Mazandaran Province, Iran.

Tajanak-e Sofla - Tajanak-e Sofla (Persian: تجنك سفلي‎, also Romanized as Tajanak-e Soflá) is a village in Mazkureh Rural District, in the Central District of Sari County, Mazandaran Province, Iran.

Tajanak, Babol - Tajanak (Persian: تجنك‎) is a village in Karipey Rural District, Lalehabad District, Babol County, Mazandaran Province, Iran.
