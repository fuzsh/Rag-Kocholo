Kastamonu is a city full of historical texture, natural beauties and cultural richness. The city has many places worth seeing that appeal to both history buffs and nature lovers.

Kastamonu Castle, one of the symbols of the city, dates back to prehistoric times and is located on the hill on which the city was founded. The castle bears traces of the Byzantine, Seljuk and Ottoman periods and is famous for offering a panoramic view of the city. Excavations in and around the castle reveal the rich history of the region.

Inebolu is a district of Kastamonu and played an important role during Turkey's War of Independence. The coastal part of Inebolu attracts attention with its old Greek houses and historical buildings. In addition, the Independence Road starting from Inebolu is the historical route where weapons and ammunition were transported to Ankara during the national struggle.

Ilgaz Mountain National Park, one of the natural beauties of Kastamonu, is located about 45 kilometers from the city center. Ilgaz Mountain is known for its snow-capped peaks, diverse wildlife, flora and fauna for most of the year. It is an ideal destination for mountaineering, trekking and winter sports. Various hiking trails within the national park offer visitors the opportunity to get in touch with nature.

Another important cultural building in Kastamonu is Nasrullah Mosque. Built in 1506, the mosque is one of the beautiful examples of Ottoman architecture and has an important place in the religious and cultural life of the city.

Another important place that reflects the cultural heritage of the city is the Liva Pasha Mansion Museum. This museum is located in a mansion built in the 19th century and exhibits artifacts about the traditional lifestyle, handicrafts and history of Kastamonu. The architecture and interior decoration of the mansion are enriched with details reflecting the lifestyle of the period.

Finally, districts such as Cide and Azdavay stand out for their natural beauty and quiet beaches. Cide is located on the Black Sea coast and welcomes visitors with its cool waters and green nature, especially in summer. Azdavay is known for its location in the valley and historical stone bridges. These districts reveal the natural and cultural diversity of Kastamonu. Kastamonu is a city that offers unforgettable experiences to visitors with its historical richness and natural beauties.

Azdavay is one of the hidden paradises of Kastamonu with its valleys that host every shade of green, lively stream sounds and lush forests. This district has the potential to develop especially in terms of nature tourism. Its mild climate and rich vegetation offer ideal conditions for activities such as hiking, picnicking and camping. In addition, the historical stone bridges in Azdavay represent the region's architectural heritage from the Ottoman period and offer beautiful shots for photography enthusiasts.

Another important cultural richness of Kastamonu is its traditional handicrafts. Especially woven products produced around Kastamonu and known as "Kastamonu Cloth" in Turkey are prepared with motifs and techniques unique to the region. Handcrafted products made from these cloths are not only one of the livelihoods of the local people, but also a popular souvenir for tourists.

The respect for art and cultural heritage in the city is also manifested in various art festivals and events organized every year. These events aim to support local artists and handicrafts, as well as to introduce Kastamonu's rich cultural heritage to a wider audience. Workshops, exhibitions and live performances organized during the events offer visitors the opportunity to experience the local culture.

In short, Kastamonu is a remarkable city with its historical and natural beauties and has gained the appreciation of both locals and visitors with its cultural richness and calm lifestyle. These features make Kastamonu an attractive place to both live and visit. Offering a variety of experiences for everyone from history lovers to nature lovers, art enthusiasts to adventure seekers, Kastamonu is a rich destination with values waiting to be discovered.
