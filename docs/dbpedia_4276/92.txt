North Light is a 23 year old horse that was born in 2001 and raced from 2003 to 2005. He was sired by Danehill out of the Rainbow Quest mare Sought Out. He was trained by Sir Michael Stoute and raced for , and was bred in [Add Data] by [Add Data]. North Light (IRE) has raced at Curragh-IRE, Epsom-GB, Goodwood-GB, ParisLongchamp-FR, Sandown-GB, York-GB with wins at Epsom-GB, Goodwood-GB, York-GB. His stakes wins include wins in the 2004 Epsom Derby (G1), 2004 Dante Stakes (G2), and the 2003 Maiden Stakes (RS).
