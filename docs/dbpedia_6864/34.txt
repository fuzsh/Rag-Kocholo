Horizon Christian Elementary and Middle School (7400 SW Sagert Street, Tualatin, OR) will host an Open House on Tues. Nov. 8, 2011, from 6:30 - 8:00 p.m. for prospective students in kindergarten through 8th grades.

Come for an informal evening to find out how Horizon Christian Schools creates high-achieving, well-rounded students with excellent programs in the areas of academics, arts, athletics and club activities. Visit with the teachers and staff and learn about the curriculum and culture at Horizon.

For more information, call 503- 692-9312 or visit horizonchristianschools.org.
