Alan Walsh General Information

Biography

Mr. Alan Walsh was the Founder and served as Chief Executive Officer at Amido (London). Launching his IT career as a Systems Engineer, becoming an Infrastructure Architect and then a Delivery Manager, Alan had experienced enough of the development lifecycle to spot an opportunity. Inspired to build a consultancy that put outcomes first, using technology in a smarter way and collaborating with clients to make projects successful, Alan launched Amido in 2010.
