Souvenir programme for a theatrical performance during the First World War. This program is for a performance of the play 'The Better 'Ole' or 'The Romance of Old Bill'. This was written by war cartoonist Bruce Bairnsfather and Arthur Eliot. The program is illustrated with three Bairnsfather cartoons and includes details of the performance inset into pages of advertisements.
