Robert Moritz has been a member of the local theatre community since 2011, performing on many stages in both San Antonio and the surrounding area. He is currently on the board of governors at Boerne Community Theatre and SATCO (San Antonio Theatre Coalition). He has been designing buildings, like the Scobee Education Center at San Antonio College and the Phipps Building on the San Antonio Riverwalk, with DHR Architects since 2004.
