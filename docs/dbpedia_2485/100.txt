Michael Bauer is a German Postwar & Contemporary painter who was born in 1973. Numerous key galleries and museums such as Miles McEnery Gallery (520 W 21st Str.) have featured Michael Bauer's work in the past.Michael Bauer's work has been offered at auction multiple times, with realized prices ranging from 354 USD to 25,000 USD, depending on the size and medium of the artwork. Since 2008 the record price for this artist at auction is 25,000 USD for Basho Mob, sold at Sotheby's New York in 2008. Michael Bauer has been featured in articles for ARTnews, San Francisco Chronicle and Art in America. The most recent article is A Look Inside Nino Mier Gallery's "Paper (And One on Wood)" Online Viewing Room written for HYPEBEAST in April 2020.
