Guns N' Roses - Short Biography

Guns N' Roses is an American hard rock band from Los Angeles, formed in 1985 and signed to Geffen Records in 1986. The original lineup, consisting of vocalist Axl Rose, guitarist Slash, rhythm guitarist Izzy Stradlin, bassist Duff McKagan and drummer Steven Adler, has expanded to include current members Richard Fortus, Frank Ferrer, Dizzy Reed and Melissa Reese. The band has released six studio albums and numerous chart-topping singles, establishing the band as one of the most popular hard rock bands of all time.

"You Could Be Mine" is a song by American rock band Guns N' Roses from their fourth studio album, Use Your Illusion II. The song was released on June 21, 1991 as the first single from the album Use Your Illusion. It was also used in the 1991 film Terminator 2: Judgment Day by director James Cameron. The single reached number 29 on the US Billboard Hot 100, number 3 on the UK Singles Chart, number 1 in Finland and Spain, and was a top 5 hit in over 10 other countries.
