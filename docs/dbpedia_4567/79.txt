Niklas Luhmann

(1927-1998)

German sociologist, administration expert, and social systems theorist (1927-1998) – Niklas Luhmann was born in Lüneburg (Town and capital of the Lüneburg district in Lower Saxony, Germany) on December 8th, 1927 and died in Oerlinghausen (Town in Northrhine-Westfalia, Germany) on November 6th, 1998 at the age of 70. Today Niklas Luhmann would be 96 years old.
