Niklas Luhmann was a German sociologist born on December 8, 1927, in Lüneburg, Germany, and passed away on November 6, 1998, in Oerlinghausen, Germany. He is regarded as one of the founders of social systems theory, a branch of sociology that aligns with general systems theory. Alongside the American sociologist Talcott Parsons, Luhmann's work has significantly influenced the way social systems are understood and analyzed within the field of sociology.
