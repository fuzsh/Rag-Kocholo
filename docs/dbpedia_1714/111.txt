Tang Dynasty Band

Founded in 1988,Heavy metal rock band in mainland China

In 1988, the Tang Dynasty band started.In December 17, 1994, the band participated in the rock and roll music concert in Hung Hom stadium, Hongkong.In 1999, the album 《Historical Romance》 was released.In December 13, 2003, the band served as a guest of the second-hand rose band.In June 13, 2008, the album 《Romantic Knight》 was released.In January 23, 2010, the band won the seventh gold record Award "Rock Band Award".In November 8, 2013, the album 《Thorn》 was released.In January 11, 2014, the band won the Golden Melody Awards in China.“Hall level Band Award”.In February, the band held the "Sun and Thorn Overseas Tour Concert" in New Zealand and Fiji.

Acting career

In 2000, he formally joined the Tang Dynasty, one of the most famous rock bands in China.

In August 2002, he participated in the Guitar China Society held in Tai Temple, Beijing.

In October 2003, for the first time, he participated in the "MIDI Music Festival", the music festival is known as "China's Woodstock".

In November 2003, he acted as a guest player for the Megadeth band's Guitarist Marty's concert in Beijing.

In August 2004, he performed as a guest for the guitar hero Steve Vai Beijing concert.In the same year, works were included in the first guitar album of China, guitar China 1.

In July 2005,have a improvisational performance with Guitar Hero Paul Gilbert together

In May 2006, he again participated in the MIDI music festival with his “Eight Hand”Band.

In March 2007, released the first solo album 《Love In The Six Strings》.

In May 2007, he went to the MIDI Music Festival for the third time with “Eight Hand”Band

In 2008, second solo album 《Shore》 was launched.

August 2011, attending the opening ceremony of the 2011 Fifth China guitar culture festival

In December 2015, endorsement of ALP headless silent electric guitar.

In July 2016, participated in Alibaba Taobao "Creation Festival".

In October 2016, he participated in ALP Shanghai Musical Instrument Exhibition.

In May 5, 2017, he participated in ALP Beijing music life exhibition.

Up to now, Chen lei has participated in more than 100 performances of the tang dynasty band.His band of “Eight Hands” attended almost all the important performances of guitar in China.And become a performance guest of STEVE VAI, MARTY FRIEDMAN, PAUL GILBERT Beijing concert.Also participated in the ALP guitar special activities many times.Chen Lei and “Eight Hand” band have gradually become China's most live rock guitar band.
