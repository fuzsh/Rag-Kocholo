The Total column shows the total number of people in that county or town with this surname. For example, there were 162,421 people called JOSEPH in Kensington London at the time of the 1881 census.

The Frequency column shows the percentage of people in this county or town with this surname. For example, a frequency of 5.7715 in Kensington London means that 5.7715% of the people in Kensington London on census day were called JOSEPH.
