Rio Tinto is a metal and mining company. Its primary activities include mining and processing of commodities such as iron ore, copper, aluminum, and other essential minerals. The company's products range from iron ore, aluminum, bauxite, alumina, copper, diamonds, titanium dioxide, lithium, salt, and borates. The company operates port and rail facilities, smelters, refineries, processing plants, shipping, and commercial and research facilities. Rio Tinto also carries out the exploration, technology, and innovation business through research centers. The company has operations in Europe, Africa, Asia-Pacific, the Middle East, and the Americas. Rio Tinto is headquartered in London, Greater London, the UK.
