Mishti is an Indian film actress who made her Bollywood debut with Subhash Ghai's Kaanchi: The Unbreakable, Tollywood debut with the film Chinnadana Nee Kosam, and her Mollywood debut with Jinu Abraham's Ada Joan.

Born on: 20 December, 1987
