Plains friends greet Nobel-winner Carter: Former President and the newest Nobel Peace Prize recipient Jimmy Carter makes his way through reporters following a press conference, Friday, Oct. 11, in his hometown of Plains, Ga. The 78-year-old Carter had been notified at 4:30 that morning that he was to receive the prize.”I am delighted, humbled and very grateful that the Nobel Prize committee has given me this,” he told a crowd of about 200 gathered in front of the Plains antique mall.
