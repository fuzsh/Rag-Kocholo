NOBEL PEACE PRIZE LECTURE, SIGNED BY PRESIDENT JIMMY CARTER

CARTER, Jimmy. The Nobel Peace Prize Lecture. New York: Simon & Schuster, (2002). Small octavo, original blind-stamped beige cloth, original dust jacket. $850.

First edition, boldly signed on a preliminary page by America's 39th President.

Jimmy Carter won the 2002 Nobel Prize for Peace "for his decades of untiring effort to find peaceful solutions to international conflicts, to advance democracy and human rights, and to promote economic and social development" (Nobel Citation). This lecture was delivered on December 10, 2002 when he received the Prize.

Fine condition.
