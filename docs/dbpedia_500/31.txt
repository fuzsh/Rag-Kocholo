Nestled on the vast North China Plain, Xingtai is a city that echoes with ancient tales, serving as a crossroads where history, culture, and modern development converge. Xingtai’s Guangfu Ancient City is a historical treasure trove, showcasing relics from various dynasties. The city’s Dingzhou Pagoda, dating back over a thousand years, adds a touch of architectural splendor to its skyline. Xingtai’s strategic location and historical landmarks make it a city where the past and present dance in harmony on the expansive canvas of the North China Plain.
