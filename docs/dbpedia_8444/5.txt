From 1960 to 2023 the population of Burkina Faso increased from 4.83 million to 23.25 million people. This is a growth of 381.5 percent in 63 years. The highest increase in Burkina Faso was recorded in 2015 with 6.44 percent. The smallest increase in 1962 with 1.34 percent.

In the same period, the total population of all countries worldwide increased by 164.7 percent.

The average age in Burkina Faso rose by 1.69 years from 2012 to 2024 from 17.01 to 18.70 years (median value).

Around 32 percent of the inhabitants live in the country's larger cities. This growing trend of urbanization is increasing by 4.5 percent annually.
