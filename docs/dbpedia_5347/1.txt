A Special Correspondent for NBC News, Chelsea Clinton is currently pursuing a doctorate at Oxford, working at New York University, the Clinton Foundation and the Clinton Global Initiative. She currently serves on the boards of the Clinton Foundation, the Clinton Global Initiative, the School of American Ballet, IAC, Common Sense Media and the Weill Cornell Medical College. Clinton has worked at McKinsey & Company and Avenue Capital. She studied at Stanford, Oxford and Columbia Universities. Clinton and her husband, Marc, live in New York City.
