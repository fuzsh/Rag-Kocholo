Where is LaGrange, Georgia ?

LaGrange lies in Troup County, located in the western part of the state of Georgia, USA. It is situated near the Alabama-Georgia border. LaGrange is approximately 60 miles southwest of Atlanta and 90 miles northeast of Montgomery, making it strategically positioned between these significant cities.
