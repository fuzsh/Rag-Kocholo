Bank of America Plaza is a 72-story, 280.7 m (921 ft) late-modernist skyscraper in Dallas, Texas, United States. It is the tallest skyscraper in the city, the 3rd tallest in Texas and the 40th tallest in the United States.

Construction began in 1983 and the tower was completed in 1985.
