# Rock# Blues# Southern Rock

Formed in 1984, the Black Crowes are an American Hard Rock/Blues Rock band signed to Silver Arrow Records. Notable albums include "Shake Your Money Maker" (1990), "The Southern Harmony and Musical Companion" (1992), and "Warpaint" (2008). Their final album "Croweology" was released in 2010. They have won multiple Grammy nominations and an MTV Music Award.
