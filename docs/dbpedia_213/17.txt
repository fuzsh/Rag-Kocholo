The Black Crowes are an American retro / blues / rock band formed in Atlanta, Georgia, in 1984. Their discography includes eight studio albums, four live albums and several charting singles.

They released their debut album, Shake Your Money Maker, in 1990. Their follow-up, The Southern Harmony and Musical Companion, reached the top of the Billboard 200 in 1992.

They were labeled by Melody Maker as "The Most Rock 'n' Roll Rock 'n' Roll Band in the World".
