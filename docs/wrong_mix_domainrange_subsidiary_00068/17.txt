DNV GL is an independent expert in assurance and risk management since 1864 with offices in 100 countries.

In 2013, Det Norske Veritas (DNV) and the german classification society Germanisher Lloyd merged in the DNV GL Group.

On 1 March 2021 DNV GL became DNV.
