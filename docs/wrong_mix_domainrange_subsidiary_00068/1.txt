Key Highlights

DNV Group AS (DNV), a subsidiary of Det Norske Veritas Holding AS, is a provider of risk management and quality assurance services. The company offers advisory, testing, certification, cyber security, classification, data and analytics, inspection, energy management, training, and verification services. It also provides digital solutions for managing risk and improving safety and asset performance of ships, pipelines, processing plants, offshore structures, electric grids, smart cities, and others. DNV serves maritime, power, and renewables, automotive and aerospace, food and beverage, digital services, healthcare, and other industries. The company has operations in Europe, the Americas, Africa, the Middle East, Asia, Australia, and New Zealand. DNV is headquartered in Hovik, Oslo, Norway.
