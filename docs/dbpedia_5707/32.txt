The Carpet Frogs are a Toronto based rock band. They are known for their work with private events, Randy Bachman and Burton Cummings. Formed in Toronto in 1993, the pop-revival sextet the Carpet Frogs comprised lead vocalist Nick Sinopoli, vocalist/guitarist Greg Godovitz, lead guitarist Steve Jenson, guitarist David Love, bassist Leo Valvassori and drummer Lawrence Greech.

Their debut LP Frog Curry was issued in 1995. This group offers a professional quality service aimed at making your event run as smooth as possible and guaranteed to get your guests up out of their seat.
