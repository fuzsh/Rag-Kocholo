Jerzy Jurandot was born on March 19, 1911 in Warsaw, Poland, Russian Empire [now Warsaw, Mazowieckie, Poland]. He was a writer, known for Przygoda z piosenka (1969), Love Gets a Room (2021) and Pani minister tanczy (1937). He was married to Stefania Grodzienska. He died on August 16, 1979 in Warsaw, Mazowieckie, Poland.
