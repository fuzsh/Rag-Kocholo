Jerzy Jurandot was born on 19 March 1911 in Warsaw, Poland, Russian Empire [now Warsaw, Mazowieckie, Poland]. He was a writer, known for Przygoda z piosenka (1969), El amor en su lugar (2021) and Pani minister tanczy (1937). He was married to Stefania Grodzienska. He died on 16 August 1979 in Warsaw, Mazowieckie, Poland.
