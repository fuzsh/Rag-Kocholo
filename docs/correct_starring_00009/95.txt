Morgan Freeman (born June 1, 1937) is an American actor, film director, and narrator who portrayed Allan Trumbull in Olympus Has Fallen, London Has Fallen and Angel Has Fallen. Freeman and Gerard Butler are the only actors who've appeared in all three films of the series.

Accolades and Career[]

Freeman has received Academy Award nominations for his performances in Street Smart, Driving Miss Daisy, The Shawshank Redemption and Invictus and won in 2005 for Million Dollar Baby.

Freeman has appeared in many other box office hits, including: Glory, Robin Hood: Prince of Thieves, Unforgiven, Se7en, Kiss the Girls, Along Came a Spider, Deep Impact, The Sum of All Fears, High Crimes, Bruce Almighty, Dolphin Tale, The Dark Knight trilogy, Oblivion, Now You See Me and Dolphin Tale 2. He also narrated the documentary film March of the Penguins and appeared in Now You See Me 2, the sequel to Now You See Me.

He has frequently collaborated with co-star Ashley Judd in Kiss the Girls, High Crimes, Dolphin Tale and Dolphin Tale 2. He had previously played a U.S. President in Deep Impact and a government official in The Sum of All Fears.
