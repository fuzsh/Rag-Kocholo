James Blunt is an English singer-songwriter and musician. He was born on February 22, 1974, in Tidworth, Wiltshire, England. Blunt first gained international attention with the release of his debut album "Back to Bedlam" in 2004, which featured the hit singles "You're Beautiful" and "Goodbye My Lover." The album was a commercial success and has sold over 11 million copies worldwide. Blunt's music is characterized by his distinctive tenor voice, acoustic guitar playing, and introspective lyrics. His songs often deal with themes of love, heartbreak, and personal reflection. In addition to his music career, Blunt served in the British Army before pursuing music full-time. He has also worked as a television presenter and has appeared on several shows, including "Top Gear" and "The X Factor." Blunt has been recognized for his music with several awards, including two Brit Awards and two Ivor Novello Awards. He has sold over 20 million records worldwide and continues to record and tour.
