Gothic revival (architecture), Grade II* Listed (UK), redundant church

St John the Baptist's Church, Avon Dassett, is a redundant Anglican church in the village of Avon Dassett, Warwickshire, England. It is designated by English Heritage as a Grade II* listed building, and is under the care of the Churches Conservation Trust. A Victorian church, built in 14th-century Gothic style.
