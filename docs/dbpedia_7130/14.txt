Freelance Lifestyle Journalist and Presenter with a love for crafts, style and travel. Momtaz is Founder of London craft night The Make Escape, has published three books and was a Finalist at The Asian Media Awards 2015 as TV Presenter of the Year. She has written for The Guardian, The i Paper, Mollie Makes, Asian Culture Vulture and Asiana Magazine. She is on a mission to travel the world learning about local crafts and records her discoveries on her blog Craft and Travel.
