Bio

John L. McLaughlin was born and raised in Saint John, NB, and misses the Atlantic Ocean, fog and dulse. He earned a BA (Philosophy Honours and English Major) from St. Thomas University, Fredericton, NB, and an MA in Philosophy from the University of Toronto. He studied Theology and Scripture at the University of St. Michael’s College, where he earned an MDiv and PhD. After teaching at Wheeling [Jesuit] University in Wheeling, West Virginia for seven years, Professor McLaughlin joined the University of St. Michael’s College in July 2002. He is Professor of Old Testament / Hebrew Bible in the Regis St. Michael’s Faculty of Theology and an Associate Member of the Graduate Faculty, Dept. of Near and Middle Eastern Civilizations, University of Toronto. He served nine years as the Director of Advanced Degree Programs and two terms as Interim Dean in the USMC Faculty of Theology, and one term as the Acting TST Graduate Coordinator. He is a past President of the Canadian Society of Biblical Studies and serves on the Editorial Board of the Journal of Hebrew Scriptures.
