SUBWAY LINES: IRT 2 or 3 to 135th Street and Lenox Avenue Station.

BUS ROUTES: M7, M102, BX33, to 135th Street and Lenox Avenue.

CAR: FDR/HARLEM RIVER DRIVE NORTHBOUND

Exit 135th Street. Proceed West bound, straight to third traffic light at Lenox Avenue and 135th Street. Make right turn onto Lenox Avenue and Harlem Campus.

HARLEM RIVER DRIVE SOUTHBOUND

Exit on 142nd Street. Proceed Southbound, straight on fifth Avenue to fourth light at 135th Street and Fifth Avenue. Make a right turn onto 135th Street. Go to first traffic light at Lenox Avenue. Make right turn onto Lenox Avenue and Hospital Campus.

TRIBORO BRIDGE WESTBOUND

Manhattan bound to 125th Street exit. Proceed westbound straight on 125th Street (Dr Martin Luther King Jr Boulevard) to ninth traffic light at Lenox Avenue and 125th Street. Make a right turn on to Lenox Avenue, proceed Northbound to 135th Street and Lenox Avenue and Hospital Campus.

GEORGE WASHINGTON BRIDGE EAST BOUND

Harlem River Drive South to 142nd Street exit. Proceed Southbound straight onto Fifth Avenue to fourth traffic light at 135th Street Fifth Avenue. Make right turn on to 135th Street follow straight to second traffic light at Lenox Avenue and 135th Street. Make right turn on to Lenox Avenue and Hospital Campus.

MAJOR DEEGAN EXPRESSWAY SOUTHBOUND

Manhattan bound to 138th Street exit. Turn right on to Madison Avenue Bridge. Make a left turn onto Fifth Avenue and a right turn at 135th Street. Proceed west to Lenox Avenue and turn right onto Lenox Avenue and the Hospital Campus.

BRUCKNER EXPRESSWAY

Manhattan bound Major Deegan North to 138th Street exit. Make a left onto Fifth Avenue Bridge and cross. Make a left turn onto Fifth Avenue and a right turn at the 135th Street. Proceed West to Lenox Avenue and turn right onto Lenox Avenue and the Hospital Campus.
