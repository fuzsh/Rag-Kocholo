Colin Greenwood was born on 26 June 1969 in Oxford, Oxfordshire, England, UK. He is a composer and actor, known for Children of Men (2006), Vanilla Sky (2001) and I Origins - Im Auge des Ursprungs (2014). He has been married to Molly McGrann since December 1998. They have three children.
