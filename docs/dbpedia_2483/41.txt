Dr. Michael Bauer, 44, compassionate father and friend as well as a gifted physician who loved poetry and nature, passed away on Saturday, June 24, 2017. Michael was known for his ability to warmly welcome any person into a conversation whether he had known them for two minutes or 20 years. He could make anyone smile. Michael is survived by the love of his life, Dr. Andrea Sherwood, and his children, Eli and Ava Bauer. He is also survived by his mother, Linda Bauer; brother, William Bauer and sisters, Michele Bean and Jeanne Bauer. He leaves many cherished aunts, uncles, nieces, nephews, cousins and countless friends.

A celebration of Michael’s life will be held Saturday, July 1, 2017 with a welcome at 11:00 a.m. and a service at 11:30 a.m. at the University of New Mexico Alumni Chapel. A reception will follow at the University Club on campus from 1:00-3:00 p.m.

For those who wish, memorial contributions may be made to the Kewa Medical Clinic – Santo Domingo Pueblo or the Brain and Behavior Research Foundation at www.bbrfoundation.org (please designate for depression studies).
