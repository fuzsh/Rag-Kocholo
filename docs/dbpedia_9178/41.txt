Savannah Guthrie is an American broadcast journalist and attorney. She is currently co-anchor of the NBC morning show The Today Show, a position she has held since 2012. She has served as a White House correspondent and co-anchor on The Daily Rundown. Guthrie was born in Melbourne, Australia, and moved to Tucson as a child. She received a B.A. in Journalism from the University of Arizona in 1993.
