Her Royal Highness Princess Beatrice of Bourbon Two Sicilies was born in Saint-Raphaël on 16 June 1950. In 1978 Princess Beatrice married His Imperial Highness Prince Charles Napoleon (divorced 1989) and has two children by this marriage. Her Imperial and Royal Highness Princess Caroline (born 1980) and His Imperial and Royal Highness Prince Jean Christophe (born 1986).

Princess Beatrice is a Dame Grand Cross of Justice of the Sacred Military Constantinian Order of Saint George.

Her Royal Highness Princess Anna of Bourbon Two Sicilies was born in Saint-Raphaël on 24 April 1957. Princess Anna married Jacques Cochin of the Barons Cochin in 1977 (now divorced) and has two children by this marriage. Nicolas (born 1979) and Dorothée (born 1985).

Princess Anna is a Dame of Grand Cross of Justice of the Sacred Military Constantinian Order of Saint George.
