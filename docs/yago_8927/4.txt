Proxima was the second feature-length film from Spanish director Carlos Atanes. Carlos Atanes had previously appeared with FAQ: Frequently Asked Questions, a dystopian science-fiction film that sat just between a profusion of interesting imagery, a satirical sense of humour and having inhaled a little too much of the 1960s French Nouvelle Vague. Atanes would subsequently go onto make Codex Atanicus, a collection of his short films.

Proxima is certainly a quantum leap outwards from the things that Carlos Atanes was starting to explore in FAQ. It is difficult to sum up what Proxima is in a nutshell.
