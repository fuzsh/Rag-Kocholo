Simon Templeman came to Los Angeles in 1986 with the Royal Shakespeare Company production of Nicholas Nickleby. Met his wife, Rosalind Chao and has lived and worked in the US ever since. Theatre in the UK includes performances ranging from Harry the Horse to Hamlet in Repertory, Bristol Old Vic, Nottingham Playhouse, the RSC, and The National. Many TV appearances in the US including, The Neighbors, Just Shoot Me, Northern Exposure, and most recently, Acapulco, Almost Paradise and The Orville. Simon is fortunate enough to have been involved with many LATW productions over the years.
