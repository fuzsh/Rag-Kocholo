Emily McLaughlin's daytime career began in 1959 as Dr. Eileen Seaton on Young Dr. Malone but it would be her role as Jessie Brewer on General Hospital that she became best known to audiences. McLaughlin was an original cast member on GH appearing in the very first episode in 1963 and continued in the role until her death in 1991.

Personal Life[]

McLaughlin married Jeffrey Hunter in February 1969 but he died just four months later in May. McLaughlin was also married to Robert Lansing (1956-1968) and had a son Bobby. Emily also had a daughter Mary Ann.
