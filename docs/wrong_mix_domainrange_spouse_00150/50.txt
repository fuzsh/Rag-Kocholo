Demi Moore is divorcing husband Ashton Kutcher after six years of marriage. The actress told ET the breakup comes with "great sadness and a heavy heart." She added: "As a woman, a mother and a wife, there are certain values and vows that I hold sacred, and it is in this spirit that I have chosen to move forward with my life."

After two years of dating, Ashton married Demi, 16 years his senior, on September 24, 2005. The couple have been inseparable since then however in recent months rumors that Ashton was unfaithful persisted in the media. Take a look at the May/December couple through the years.
