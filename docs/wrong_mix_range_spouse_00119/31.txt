Biography

Sir Ben Kingsley, CBE (born Krishna Pandit Bhanji) is an English actor.

Krishna Pandit Bhanji was born on December 31, 1943 in Snainton, England. [1] [2] His parents were Rahimtulla Harji Banjhi and Anna Lyna Mary Goodman. [3]

He married Angela Morant in 1966 in Chichester, Sussex, England. [4] They had two children, Thomas and Jasmine. [5] They divorced in 1972. [5]

He married Alison Sutcliffe in 1978 in Gloucester, Gloucestershire, England. [6] They had two children, Edmund and Ferdinand. [5] They divorced in 1993. [5]

He married Alexandra Christmann in 2003. [5] They divorced in 2005.

He married Daniela Barbosa de Carneiro in 2007. [5]
