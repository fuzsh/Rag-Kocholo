Sir Ben Kingsley, CBE is an English actor. In a career spanning over 40 years, he has won an Oscar, Grammy, BAFTA, two Golden Globes and Screen Actors Guild awards. Kingsley was named a Commander of the Order of the British Empire. He was made a knight bachelor. His demand to be called 'Sir' was documented by the BBC, to some criticism. Since then, Kingsley appears to have altered his stance; credits for his latest films refer to him as Ben Kingsley. He won a Grammy Award for Best Spoken Word or Nonmusical Recording for The Words of Gandhi. He was awarded the Indian civilian honour Padma Shri Kingsley was awarded a star on the Hollywood Walk of Fame. Kingsley has been married four times and has four children. He divorced German-born Alexandra Christmann, having been "deeply, deeply shocked" after pictures of her kissing another man surfaced on the Internet. Kingsley married Daniela Lavender,a Brazilian actress, at Eynsham Hall, in North Leigh Oxfordshire.
