Lucy Liu is an Emmy-nominated actress who garnered acclaim for portraying Ling Woo on Ally McBeal from 1998 to 2002. Following Ally McBeal, Liu starred on the short-lived series Cashmere Mafia and Dirty Sexy Money before joining the cast of Southland. From 2012 to 2019, Liu portrayed Watson on CBS' Sherlock Holmes-inspired series, Elementary. The actress's notable film credits include the Charlie's Angels and Kill Bill films as well as voicing Viper in the Kung Fu Panda films. Liu can next be seen in the film Shazam! Fury of the Gods, which is set to premiere in theaters in March 2023.
