Jan Montijn was a Dutch artist who was born in 1924. Kunsthal Rotterdam featured Jan Montijn's work in the past.Jan Montijn's work has been offered at auction multiple times, with realized prices ranging from 12 USD to 5,663 USD, depending on the size and medium of the artwork. Since 1998 the record price for this artist at auction is 5,663 USD for paradis terrestre, sold at Sotheby's Amsterdam in 2007. The artist died in 2015.

Artist's alternative names: Jan Montyn
