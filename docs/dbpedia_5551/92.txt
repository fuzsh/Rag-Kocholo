This is the discography of Tegan and Sara, a Canadian indie rock band composed of twins Tegan and Sara Quin. They have released six studio albums and thirteen singles. They released their first album Under Feet Like Ours independently in 1999. Their first major release was This Business of Art , released in 2000 on Vapor Records. This album contained their debut single, "The First ".Tegan and Sara have three Gold certified album by Music Canada, formerly the Canadian Recording Industry Association. In addition, two of their six studio albums have charted in the Top 5 of the Canadian Albums Chart, both peaking at number 4. There most recent record Love You To Death has sold 50,000 copies worldwide.
