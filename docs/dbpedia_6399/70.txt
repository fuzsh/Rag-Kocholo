Dipps Bhamrah brings listeners another Behind the Bhangra as he hears from legendary Punjabi vocalist, A.S. Kang, who talks about his life, career and music.

Avtar Singh Kang, universally known as A.S. Kang and one of the founding members of the global Punjabi Folk and Bhangra uprising, recently stepped back into the music arena after a hiatus of almost a decade with his brand new album, The Maestro. He is a world-renowned singer, an artist of the highest calibre with a fan following transcending the full circumference of the globe. He is the only Punjabi artist in history to have had hits in five consecutive decades.
