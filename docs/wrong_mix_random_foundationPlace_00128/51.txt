Mann

Mann is a romantic film starring Manisha Koirala and Aamir Khan together. The two fall in love and have to overcome several problems before coming together.

Bombay

Bombay stars Kamal Haasan and Manisha, two interreligious people who fall in love with each other and have to face the community`s wrath.

Akele Hum Akele Tum

Manisha Koirala is yet again paired with Aamir Khan in this film. The duo is a pair of struggling artists who go through troubles in their married bliss.

1942: A Love Story

The film is set in the pre-independence period and pairs Anil Kapoor with Manisha. Anil is a politician`s son who falls for a freedom fighter`s daughter.
