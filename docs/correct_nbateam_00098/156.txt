On Sunday, the Chicago Bulls announced the signing of free agent guards Jannero Pargo and John Lucas III, terms of the contracts were not released.

This will be Pargo's fourth stint with the Bulls. The seven-year veteran played for the Bulls during the 2003-06, 2009-10 seasons. In 379 regular season games in the NBA, Pargo has averaged 6.6 points, 2.0 assist and 1.5 rebounds per game and averaged 15.7 minutes.

Lucas III has already played two games for the Bulls this season, averaging 1.0 ppg and 0.5 apg in 5.0 mpg.
