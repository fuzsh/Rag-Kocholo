89 year old Albion, Illinois resident, Amy L. Leslie passed away Friday, December 8, 2023 at Oakview Nursing and Rehab Center in Mt. Carmel. IL

Amy was born on May 31, 1934 in Albion, Illinois to John and Mabel (Anniss) Fewkes. Amy married Jack Leslie in 1955 at Albion, Illinois. Amy was a home maker and she attended the Albion Presbyterian Church.

Survivors include several nieces, nephews and cousins.

Preceding her in death were her parents, her husband and son Kevin.

Graveside services for Amy L. Leslie will be held at 10:00 a.m. Saturday, December 16, 2023 at Graceland Cemetery. Friends and family are asked to go directly to the cemetery. Nale’s Funeral Home of Albion is assisting the family.

Memorials may be made to the Albion Presbyterian Church and will be accepted at Nale’s Funeral Home

Fond memories and expressions of sympathy may be left for the family at www. nalesfuneralhome.com
