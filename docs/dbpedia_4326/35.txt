Getting to SGEO

From JR Hamamatsu Station

Take a #40 bus from the loading zone 15 at Kita-guchi Bus Terminal (about forty-five minutes ride). Get off at “Seirei Mikatabara Byoin” (Seirei Mikatabara General Hospital). Three minutes walk from the bus stop to the east.

From Central Japan International Airport (Centair), Nagoya

Take a shuttle bus heading to Hamamatsu/Iwata/Kakegawa that leaves from the loading zone 1 at ground level (About ninety minutes ride). Get off at “Hamamatsu Nishi Interchange”. Take a taxi to SGEO (About ten minutes ride).
