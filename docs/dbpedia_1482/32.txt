Gilbert Sylvester Strzelczyk entered eternal rest on Saturday, Aug. 20, 2022, at the age of 76. Gilbert was born Dec. 31, 1945, in San Antonio, Texas, to Joseph and Clara (Ploch) Strzelczyk.

He was preceded in death by his parents; brothers Joe Strzelczyk Jr. and Theo Strzelczyk; and sisters Lollie Bednorz and Rose Mary Appelt.

Gilbert is survived by his loving wife, Frances (Cielencki) Strzelczyk; son Todd Strzelczyk (Heide); daughter Nan Koehler Curlee (Robert); grandchildren Blade and Maddie Strzelczyk and Shannon and Ryan Rast; great-grandchildren Eleanor and Charles; brother Anton Buster Strzelczyk (Betty); numerous nieces and nephews; and many friends.

Visitation will be held Tuesday, Sept. 6, from 6-8 p.m. in the Finch Funeral Chapel in La Vernia.

A memorial service will be held on Wednesday at 10 a.m. in the funeral chapel.

Inurnment will follow at 1 p.m. in the Fort Sam National Cemetery in San Antonio.

Memorial contributions may be made in Gilbert’s memory to a charity of one’s choice.
