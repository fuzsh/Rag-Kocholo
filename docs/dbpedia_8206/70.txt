Blue Racer is a Rare figure part of the Wreck-It Ralph collection.

Appearance[]

Blue Racer's skin is white and has yellow eyes and teeth. He wears a blue and white helmet and a blue jumpsuit with black stripes.

Behaviour During Gameplay[]

Blue Racer adds no difference to the Wreck-It Ralph world.

How To Unlock[]

Play the third Wreck-It Ralph Weekend Challenge until the second bar is filled. If you don't do this in time, then you can unlock him through the Ticket Machine.
