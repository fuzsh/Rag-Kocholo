Kill Bill: Vol. 1

111 mins | Rated R (for strong bloody violence, language and some sexual content) | Action

Directed by Quentin Tarantino | Starring Daryl Hannah, Michael Parks, Vivica A Fox, Chiaki Kuriyama, Gordon Liu, Uma Thurman, Lucy Liu, David Carradine, Sonny Chiba, Michael Madsen

The Bride wakens from a four-year coma. The child she carried in her womb is gone. Now she must wreak vengeance on the team of assassins who betrayed her - a team she was once part of.
