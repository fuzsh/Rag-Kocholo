The surname "Azuara" is of Spanish origin. It is a habitational name derived from the town of Azuara in the province of Zaragoza in northern Spain. People who were originally from this town or lived near it may have adopted Azuara as their surname. The name Azuara is believed to have originated from the Arabic word "az-zawra," meaning "the valley" or "the lowland."
