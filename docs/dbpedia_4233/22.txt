Rokunin no Ansatsusha (六人の暗殺者, Rokunin no Ansatsusha) is a 1955 black-and-white Japanese film drama directed by Eisuke Takizawa.

The film won 1956 Blue Ribbon Awards for best screenplay by Ryuzo Kikushima.
