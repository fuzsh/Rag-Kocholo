Biography

Geoffrey was born in 1891[1]. He was the son of George Albert Bromet and Ellen Isabel Robinson.

He had an illustrious career in the Royal Navy from 1904, then the Royal Naval Air Service and finally the Royal Air Force until 1945. [2]

He married Ida Margaret Ratliffe in 1917[3]

He married Jean Lena Annette Conan Doyle, daughter of Sir Arthur Ignatius Conan Doyle and Jean Leckie, in 1965.[4]

He passed away in 1983.[5]
