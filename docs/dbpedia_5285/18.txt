Thomas Mansfield. AKA Silly Grampa,Tom or Tommy Died at the age of 83. He died at the Golden Nugget, Cripple Creek , Colorado. He passed on April 19th, 2024.

Tom was born in Chicago, raised in the Virginia area. His parents were Lyman and Catherine Mansfield. Tom had 6 brothers and 5 sisters. He was married to Judith Shelton and bore him 5 children: Thomas. Doug. Kirk, Dean and Tracy who passed in 2006 from Breast Cancer and daughter Beth. Tom has been a member of the Asbestos Workers for over 55 years. Out of the 12 children with Tom's passing his sister Sharon is the last remaining member of the family.

Tom is survived by Gerry Mansfield his spouse of 42 years and has 4 bonus children,Lori, Kathy, James and Tim..We have 20 Grandchildren, 28 great Grandchildren.

A Memorial date has not been estabiished, and will be announced when it is.
