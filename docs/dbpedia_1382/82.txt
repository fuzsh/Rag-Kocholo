Daniel Wass reached against Villarreal one hundred league matches with RC Celta. The Danish international will shortly ‘ hunt down ‘ his fellow national Krohn-Dehli, who between 2012 and 2015 played 103 clashes in first division with the skyblue jersey.

Wass arrived in Vigo in the summer of 2015. Since then he has played a total of 133 games with the Celta jersey (100 League, 19 Cup, 14 Europa League), becoming the Danish footballer with the most matches played in the history of the Vigo team.
