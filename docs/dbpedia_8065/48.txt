Forename Snježana

Around the globe, Snježana (Снежана , Snježana , Snjezana ) is a common girly given name. The given name Snježana is habitual in Southern Europe, particularly Croatia, where it is a widespread female name, Bosnia and Herzegovina, where it is quite a common girly name, and Serbia (explore the name in all countries). In absolute terms, it most frequently occurs in Croatia, Russia, and Bosnia and Herzegovina. Much less frequently, Snježana is the last name as well as the forename.
