Turai Yar'Adua, front right, wife of late Nigerian President Umaru Yar'Adua, stands with Patience Jonathan, wife of Nigeria's newly-sworn in President Goodluck Jonathan, as her husband's body is loaded into a plane to be transported for burial in his home state of Katsina, at the airport in Abuja, Nigeria, Thursday, May 6, 2010. Goodluck Jonathan was sworn in Thursday as the president of Nigeria, Africa's most populous nation, just hours after the death of the oil-rich country's elected leader whose long illness had sparked a leadership crisis.
