A century ago, Elkin's downtown was home to a large tobacco warehouse at the East end of Main Street. Today that warehouse has been renovated into the prestigious venue called The Liberty. Unknown to owner Cicely McCulloch, by purchasing the historic Liberty tobacco warehouse, she was buying a bit of family history.

Found among papers from the 1930s was the deed of the building listing her own grandmother as a past owner. Coley Hall provides a spacious venue that is easily customized to your taste and is the perfect wedding venue for wedding receptions, rehearsal dinners, banquets and events.
