Rising Signs: A Reggae band born of local area musicians who have been rocking the Northern California sector in one configuration or another for several decades.

Over the last 10 years Rising Signs has had the honor to share the stage with legendary Reggae musicians including Don Carlos, Pablo Moses, Israel Vibration, Prezident Brown, Mykal Rose, The Itals, Pato Bantan and Everton Blender.

With 2 albums of interesting and engaging originals and sets filled with impressive blends of classic Reggae hits and timeless covers, Rising Signs will please any crowd with their Irie Vibes.

Bass, Drums, Guitar, Keys and Ukulele!
