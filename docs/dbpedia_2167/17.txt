Signs is the14th band recording that mostly includes songs written by band members and friends of the band. Several selections received extensive airplay and climbed the bluegrass charts.

A Pinecastle Records release, this features band members Greg Cahill, banjo; Justin Carbone, guitar; Ashby Frank, mandolin and David Thomas, bass with special guests Randy Kohrs, dobro; Tim Crouch, fiddle and Sonya Issacs singing on the song Footprints.
