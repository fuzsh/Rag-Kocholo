Joachim Fernandez was born on December 6, 1972, in Ziguinchor, Senegal, and died on January 19, 2016.

He was a French-Senegalese former footballer.

He played as a defender.

He stood 1.88 m (6 ft 2 in).

Joachim Fernandez played for Sedan, Angers, Bordeaux, Caen, Udinese, Monza, Toulouse, Dundee United and Persma Manado.

Joachim Fernandez passed away at 43 yrs old.
