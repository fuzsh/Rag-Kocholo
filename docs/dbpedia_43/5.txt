Where is Wayland, New York ?

Wayland lies in Steuben County in the western part of New York State, USA. It's situated near the intersection of Interstate 390 and State Route 21. Geographically, it is south of Rochester and west of the Finger Lakes region, making it a connector between multiple key locations.
