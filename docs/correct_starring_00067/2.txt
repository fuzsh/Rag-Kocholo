Feast your eyes on Om Shanti Om

After the huge success of Main Hoon Na in 2004, Farah Khan is back with Om Shanti Om starring her close friend Shah Rukh Khan. Arjun Rampal, Shreyas Talpade, Malaika Arora Khan and newcomer Deepika Padukone make up the rest of the cast.
