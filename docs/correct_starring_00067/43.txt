Arjun Rampal played the first negative character in his Bollywood career in the 2005 action thriller Ek Ajnabee co-starring Amitabh Bachchan.

Arjun Rampal played the evil producer Mukesh Mehra who burns down Deepika Padukone's Shanti in SRK-starrer Om Shanti Om in 2007.

Pitted against Shah Rukh Khan's G.One in Ra.One, Arjun Rampal aced the titular antagonist role in the 2011 superhero film.

Portraying gangster-turned-politician Arun Gawli, Arjun Rampal played the grey-shaded role in the 2017 crime drama Daddy.

Arjun Rampal played Rudraveer, leader of the international human and arms trafficking gang, in Kangana Ranaut's Dhaakad in 2022.

Arjun Rampal played the evil businessman Rahul Sanghvi in Nandamuri Balakrishna's Telugu film Bhagavanth Kesari in 2023.

Arjun Rampal plays the main antagonist Dev in the upcoming sports action film Crakk.

Vidyut Jammwal plays the protagonist Siddhu in Crakk. He has also produced the movie under his banner Action Hero films.
