Arthur W. Wagner, 88, of Countryside Village, Selinsgrove, passed away on Sunday morning, April 15, 2018, at Evangelical Community Hospital, Lewisburg.

He was born April 30, 1929, in Winfield, a son of the late Milton E. Sr. and Carrie H. (Bennage) Wagner. On July 30, 1955, he married the former Vernetta M. Mitchell who preceded him in death on March 3, 2018. They shared a marital union of 62 years.

Arthur was a 1948 graduate of Lewisburg High School and served in the Army from 1951 to 1953.

His early employment was with JG Ott Packaging, Selinsgrove, and later at Wood Mode, Kreamer, retiring in 1992.

He was a member of Lafayette Lodge 194 Free and Accepted Masons, Selinsgrove, Loyal Order of Moose 1173, Selinsgrove where he was very active holding most of the Lodge offices and receiving the Pilgrim Degree Class of 1991, a member of the New Berlin American Legion, the Kreamer Sportsmen Club, and a life member of the DH&L Fire Company, Selinsgrove.

Art enjoyed hunting, and particularly wood working.

He is survived by two daughters and one son-in-law, Karen L. Facer of Winfield and Charlyn M. and Robert Brouse of Hummels Wharf; four granddaughters, Cara Facer Powers and husband Brian of Bedford, Mass., Kris Kelly of Bloomsburg, Dr. Meghan Facer of Winfield and her significant other, Brad Crebs, and Lindsey Brouse of Selinsgrove and fiance, Nicholas Ney; and one brother, Milton E. Wagner Jr. of Selinsgrove; and two nephews and one niece.

He was preceded in death by one sister, Shirley M. Wagner; one brother, Robert L. Wagner; and one son-in-law, Richard G. Facer.

Friends and family will be received from 10 to 11 a.m. Thursday at the V.L. Seebold Funeral Home, 601 N. High St., Selinsgrove, where Loyal Order of Moose will conduct a Pilgrim memorial service at 11, followed by the funeral with Pastor Larry Siirkanen officiating.

Burial with military honors by American Legion Post 25 and V.F. W. Post 6631, both of Selinsgrove, will be in Chestnut Hills Cemetery, Winfield.

Contributions to honor Arthur's memory may be made to Mooseheart Child City and School, Mooseheart, IL 60539.
