Lake Semyonovskoye

Rosta River

Rosta River (Russian: Роста) is a river in the north of the Kola Peninsula in Murmansk Oblast, Russia. It is 10 km in length. The Rosta River originates in the Lake Rogozero and flows into the Kola Bay.
