St Mary's Church, Fordwich (set of 2 images)

The main fabric of the church is early 13th century. The windows in the south aisle are early 14th century. The attractive interior is unrestored with box pews and other 18th century fittings. The church is Grade I Listed and is under the care of the Churches Conservation Trust. This view looks from the chancel towards the nave. See https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Church_of_St_Mary_the_Virgin,_Fordwich for more information.
