Prevalence

Only a few people with faimily name Janik live in Croatia today, in one household only. There were 0 of them in the middle of the past century, and their number remains constant.

They are located in one Croatian city only, in Zagreb (<5).

Outside Croatia

Family name Janik (including: Yanik , Yanick , Janick , Ianik , Jannick , Janic , Jannik , Janiek and Janieck ) is present in 86 countries worldwide. The family name "Janik " is used by some 40000 people in Poland , about 5000 in the United States , and some 5000 people in Germany. "Yanik " is used by some 40000 people in Turkey , about 1000 in the United States , and some 1000 people in Germany. "Yanick " is used by some 700 people in Cameroon , about 500 in the United States , and some 400 people in Haiti.
