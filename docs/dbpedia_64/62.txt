Brig. Gen. Stephen E. Slade is the Mobilization Assistant to the Director of Operations (J3), United States Space Command, Peterson Space Force Base, Colorado. He is responsible for assisting in executing full-spectrum space operations, maintaining credible space deterrence capabilities, providing analytical planning support, combatant command’s participation in joint and combined exercises, and for the education, training, and assessments of assigned and attached space forces.

Prior to assuming his current position, Brig. Gen. Slade served as the Mobilization Assistant to the Combined Force Space Component Commander, U.S. Space Command; and Deputy Commander, Space Operations Command, U.S. Space Force, Vandenberg Space Force Base, California.

Brig. Gen. Slade commissioned in August 1993 through the Reserve Officer Training Corps program at Utah State University. He has served in a variety of active-duty space and missile operations and staff positions, including Minuteman III ICBM and Milstar operations squadrons, a test squadron and United States Strategic Command. Transitioning to the Air Force Reserve in 2005, Brig. Gen. Slade has served in multiple joint commands and on the Air Staff. Recalled to active duty in 2009, he deployed in support of Operation Enduring Freedom as the Expeditionary Cyber Support Element officer in charge, Deputy Chief of Staff Communication, International Security Assistance Force in Afghanistan.
