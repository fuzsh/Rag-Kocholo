This book provides detailed biographical sketches of Thomas B. Walker and his wife, Harriet G. Walker. The couple were prominent figures in Minneapolis at the turn of the 20th century, known for their philanthropy and cultural contributions to the city. The book includes descriptions of the couple’s personal lives, as well as their many accomplishments. It’s a valuable resource for anyone interested in the history of Minneapolis or the Walker family.
